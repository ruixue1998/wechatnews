DeepSeek 完成 500 亿融资

According to The Information, DeepSeek has completed its first round of financing, raising a total of over 50 billion yuan, setting a new record for the highest single-round financing to date for domestic large models and even the entire Chinese AI industry. 据 The Information 报道，DeepSeek 已经完成了其首轮融资，募资总额超过 500 亿元人民币，刷新了国产大模型甚至整个中国 AI 行业迄今为止的单轮融资最高纪录。

Among the publicly disclosed investors, Liang Wenfeng himself subscribed for the largest share, contributing 20 billion yuan; Tencent followed with 10 billion yuan; CATL invested 5 billion yuan; JD, NetEase, and IDG Capital each contributed 3 billion yuan. The remaining investors and amounts have not yet been fully disclosed. 在公开披露的出资方中，梁文峰本人认购份额最大，出资 200 亿元人民币；腾讯跟投 100 亿元人民币；宁德时代出资 50 亿元人民币；京东、网易和 IDG 资本各出资 30 亿元人民币。其余投资方及金额尚未全部披露。

It is worth noting that this round of financing adopted an unusual transaction structure: investors must inject funds into a limited partnership managed by DeepSeek CEO Liang Wenfeng, rather than investing directly in DeepSeek itself. 值得注意的是，本轮融资采用了一种不同寻常的交易结构：投资者须将资金注入一个由 DeepSeek CEO 梁文峰管理的有限合伙企业，而非直接投给 DeepSeek 本身。

According to informed sources, this arrangement is to ensure Liang Wenfeng maintains absolute control over the company, and all external investors' shares have a five-year lock-up period during which they cannot be transferred. 据知情人士透露，这是梁文峰确保自己对公司保持绝对控制的安排，同时所有外部投资者的股份设有五年禁售期，期间不得转让。

微软：典型 AI 查询耗电不超过微波炉运行 2 秒，用水低于一滴

Microsoft recently published a blog post, citing research from the journal Joule, which indicates that sending a typical text query to a large language model consumes approximately 0.16 to 0.60 watt-hours of electricity. 微软日前发布博文，援引《Joule》期刊研究指出，向大型语言模型发送一次典型文本查询，耗电量约为 0.16 至 0.60 瓦时。

Converted to a daily reference, this is equivalent to a 1000-watt microwave oven running for 0.6 to 2 seconds, or a 40-watt computer running for 15 to 60 seconds. Compared to figures cited in previous literature and media, this result is 4 to 20 times lower. 换算为日常参照，相当于 1000 瓦微波炉运行 0.6 至 2 秒，或一台 40 瓦电脑运行 15 至 60 秒。与此前文献和媒体所引数字相比，这一结果低出 4 至 20 倍。

Regarding water usage, the study, under conservative assumptions, estimated that the cooling water for each typical query is approximately 0.0 to 0.067 milliliters, with a median equivalent to about 1/100 of a teaspoon, less than the volume of a single drop of water. Microsoft stated that this figure is expected to decrease further with the continued promotion of zero-water data center designs. 用水方面，研究在保守假设下估算，每次典型查询的冷却用水约为 0.0 至 0.067 毫升，中位数约相当于 1/100 茶匙，低于一滴水的体积。微软表示，随着零用水数据中心设计的持续推广，该数字预计还将进一步下降。

The study suggests that the main reason for previous overestimations of AI energy consumption by external parties is that related measurements failed to reflect the overall operational efficiency of systems under large-scale deployment. The research was jointly conducted by Microsoft AI for Good Lab, Microsoft's sustainability department, and the Azure team. 研究认为，外界此前高估 AI 能耗的主要原因在于，相关测量未能体现大规模部署下系统的整体运行效率。该研究由 Microsoft AI for Good Lab、微软可持续发展部门与 Azure 团队联合开展。

7 月起电动车强制一键物理断电

According to Sina Auto, starting July 1st, two mandatory national standards related to electric vehicle safety will officially come into effect. The "Electric Vehicle Safety Requirements" (GB18384—2025) and "Safety Requirements for Power Batteries for Electric Vehicles" (GB38031—2025) will take effect simultaneously. 据新浪汽车报道，7 月 1 日起，两项涉及电动汽车安全的强制性国家标准将正式实施。《电动汽车安全要求》（GB18384—2025）和《电动汽车用动力蓄电池安全要求》（GB38031—2025）届时将同步生效。

The new national standard for electric vehicle safety for the first time explicitly defines the "one-button power-off" device as a physical power-off device, requiring the physical disconnection of the high-voltage circuit between the entire vehicle's high-voltage circuit or drive system and the rechargeable energy storage system, completely replacing the previous software-controlled power-off method. 电动汽车安全新国标首次将「一键断电」装置明确定义为物理断电装置，要求实现整车高压回路或驱动系统与可充电储能系统之间高压回路的物理断开，彻底取代此前依赖软件控制的断电方式。

The standard also stipulates that when the vehicle is stationary and not charging or discharging, the driver must be able to trigger this function with a single action (tap or long press) to improve the reliability and timeliness of accident rescue. 标准还规定，车辆在静止非充放电工况下，驾驶员须能通过单一动作（点触或长按）触发该功能，以提升事故救援的可靠性和时效性。

The adjustments to the new national battery standard focus on upgrading test conditions and safety judgment criteria.
