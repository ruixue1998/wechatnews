China Mobile, China Telecom, and China Unicom will provide eSIM support for this product, with the specific timing depending on regulatory approval. 中国移动、中国电信和中国联通将为本产品提供 eSIM 支持，具体时间依监管部门审批情况而定。

小鹏召回 4.7 万台 P7+：转向助力失效

Yesterday, according to information disclosed on the website of the State Administration for Market Regulation, Zhaoqing XPeng New Energy Investment Co., Ltd. recently filed a recall plan with the State Administration for Market Regulation: 昨天，据市场监管总局网站披露，肇庆小鹏新能源投资有限公司近日向国家市场监督管理总局备案了召回计划：

The recall number is S2025M0142V and will be implemented starting September 15, 2025, involving some XPeng P7+ vehicles produced between August 20, 2024, and April 27, 2025, totaling 47,490 units. 此次召回编号为 S2025M0142V，将自 2025 年 9 月 15 日起实施，涉及 2024 年 8 月 20 日至 2025 年 4 月 27 日期间生产的部分小鹏 P7+ 汽车，共计 47490 辆。

The official announcement stated that some vehicles within the scope of this recall may have poor contact in the steering assist motor sensor harness, which could lead to fluctuations in the steering gear sensor signal, triggering the steering fault warning light to illuminate, and causing steering assist failure, posing a safety hazard. 官方通报称，本次召回范围内部分车辆因转向助力电机传感器线束接触不良，可能导致转向机传感器信号波动，触发转向故障警告灯点亮，并造成转向助力失效，存在安全隐患。

The company will replace the improved steering gear assembly for free for vehicles within the recall scope to eliminate the risk. Owners will be informed of the recall information via mobile app push notifications, phone calls, etc., and can log in to the XPeng Motors APP or call the service hotline '400-783-6688' for details. 企业将为召回范围内车辆免费更换改进后的转向机总成，以消除风险。车主将通过手机 APP 推送、电话等方式获知召回信息，并可登录小鹏汽车 APP 或拨打服务热线「400-783-6688」获取详情。

According to reports, "gPass" is developed around three core capabilities: "security, interaction, and connection," aiming to achieve secure, trustworthy, and instant information interaction between AI glasses and intelligent agents. Chen Liang, CTO of Ant Group's Greater Security, stated that "gPass" has opened up Ant's financial-grade security capabilities, which will support the evolution of AI glasses from "basic tools" to "personal intelligent agents." 据介绍，「gPass」围绕「安全、交互、连接」三大核心能力展开，致力于实现 AI 眼镜与智能体之间的安全、可信、即时信息交互。蚂蚁集团大安全 CTO 陈亮表示，「gPass」开放了蚂蚁金融级安全能力，将支撑 AI 眼镜从「基础工具」演进为「个人智能体」。

  • At the security level, "gPass" provides technologies such as trusted identity circulation, end-to-end encryption, and device authentication to ensure "the device belongs to the user," and has adapted Android and RTOS dual-system chip-level security solutions. 在安全层面，「gPass」提供可信身份流通、端到端加密、设备认证等技术，确保「设备是本人」，并已适配 Android 与 RTOS 双系统芯片级安全方案。

买书不读指南｜《制造消费者》

Since Thorstein Veblen introduced 'conspicuous consumption' in 'The Theory of the Leisure Class' in 1899, consumption has gradually permeated the social fabric. Entering the 20th century, it even replaced production to become the core of people's lives. Baudrillard pointed out in 'The Consumer Society' that people no longer consume the goods themselves, but rather the symbols and cultural meanings they carry. 自 1899 年范伯伦在《有闲阶级论》中提出「炫耀性消费」以来，消费逐渐渗透进社会肌理。进入 20 世纪，它甚至取代生产，成为人们生活的核心。鲍德里亚在《消费社会》中指出，人们消费的不再是商品本身，而是其承载的符号与文化意义。

In this book, "Manufacturing Consumers," French scholar Antoine Galluzzo views the history of consumption as a history of accelerated circulation of goods and images, and also a process where markets and media mutually shape each other. 而在这本《制造消费者》中，法国学者安东尼·加卢佐将消费史视为商品与图像流通加速的历史，也是市场与媒体相互塑造的过程。

The book starts by examining the development of transportation and the increasing complexity of global division of labor in the 19th century, revealing how modern people, while distanced from production, developed a fetishistic worship of commodities. The proliferation of advertising images not only reinforced this worship but also reshaped people's identity. As Baudrillard said, consumption is a "permanent violence," and this brief history spanning two centuries outlines the accelerating trajectory of this force. 书中从 19 世纪交通运输的发展与全球分工的复杂化切入，揭示现代人在远离生产的同时，如何形成了对商品的拜物崇拜。广告图像的扩散不仅强化了这种崇拜，也重塑了人们的身份认同。正如鲍德里亚所言，消费是一种「永久性的暴力」，而这部跨越两个世纪的简史，勾勒出这种力量不断加速的轨迹。
