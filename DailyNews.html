NeurIPS 道歉：禁止中国机构投稿系「沟通误解」，已更新规则

Yesterday, the official NeurIPS academic conference issued a statement, formally apologizing for the controversy surrounding its submission rules, admitting that the root cause of the problem was "a communication misunderstanding between the NeurIPS Foundation and its legal team," and stating that it "never intended to restrict submissions beyond mandatory compliance obligations." 昨天，学术会议 NeurIPS 官方发布声明，正式就征稿规则风波道歉，承认问题根源在于「NeurIPS 基金会与法律团队之间的沟通误解」，并表示「从未打算在强制性合规义务之外限制投稿」。

NeurIPS simultaneously announced that it has updated its submission guidelines to align with the submission rules of ACM, IEEE, other international conferences, and previous NeurIPS events, reiterating that it "welcomes submissions from all institutions and individuals who meet compliance requirements." NeurIPS 同步宣布已更新投稿手册，与 ACM、IEEE 及其他国际会议和往届 NeurIPS 的投稿规则保持一致，重申「欢迎所有符合合规要求的机构和个人提交论文」。

The incident originated when NeurIPS, in this year's call for papers, prohibited institutions listed on relevant U.S. lists from submitting papers, participating in reviews, or serving as editors, citing "compliance with U.S. sanctions and trade control regulations." 事件起因于 NeurIPS 在今年征稿通知中，以「遵守美国制裁与贸易管制规定」为由，禁止被列入美国相关名单的机构投稿、参与评审及担任编辑。

The affected institutions include hundreds of Chinese organizations, including Huawei, numerous universities, research institutes, key laboratories, and the three major operators. Consequently, relevant researchers have been deprived of the right to present and publish their findings at this top conference. 受影响机构涵盖华为、多所高校院所及重点实验室、三大运营商在内的数百家中国机构，相关科研人员因此被剥夺在该顶会展示与发表成果的权利。

After the news broke, the China Computer Federation (CCF) was the first to issue a strong statement, advocating that all Chinese researchers in the computer field refuse to submit papers to NeurIPS and decline to provide any academic services such as reviewing, editing, or organizing for it. 消息传出后，中国计算机学会（CCF）率先强硬表态，倡议全体中国计算机领域科研工作者拒绝向 NeurIPS 投稿，并拒绝为其提供审稿、编辑、组织等任何学术服务。

Yesterday, the China Association for Science and Technology issued an official statement, announcing that it would stop accepting funding applications for scholars attending this year's NeurIPS conference, and would not recognize papers accepted by this NeurIPS as representative works for project applications. Related funding will be redirected to domestic academic conferences or other eligible international conferences. 昨天，中国科学技术协会发布官方声明，宣布停止受理学者参加今年 NeurIPS 会议的资助申请，不予认可以本届 NeurIPS 中稿论文作为代表作的项目申请，相关资助将转向国内学术会议或其他符合条件的国际会议。

防止人才被挖，苹果向设计师发放高额奖金

According to Bloomberg, Apple this week issued a rare batch of additional bonuses to iPhone hardware designers, primarily targeting the iPhone product design team, independent of Apple's regular quarterly and annual performance bonuses. 据彭博社报道，苹果本周向 iPhone 硬件设计师发放了一批罕见的额外奖金，发放对象主要集中于 iPhone 产品设计团队，与苹果常规的季度及年度绩效奖金相互独立。

The bonuses were issued in the form of Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) with a four-year vesting period, meaning employees must remain at Apple for four years to receive the full benefits. It is reported that individual bonuses are valued between approximately $200,000 and $400,000, with the final amount also fluctuating with Apple's stock performance. 此次奖金以限制性股票单位（RSU）形式发放，归属期为四年，意味着员工须在苹果任职满四年方可获得全部收益。据悉，单人奖金价值约在 20 万至 40 万美元之间，最终金额还将随苹果股价表现而浮动。

Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman, citing informed sources, stated that employees generally view this pay raise as a direct response to the recent surge in large-scale hiring by startups. 彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 引援知情人士表示，员工普遍将这次薪酬提升视为对近期初创企业大规模招聘热潮的直接回应。

However, compared to the offers from competitors, these bonuses still appear modest. OpenAI offers annual stock incentives of up to $1 million to Apple engineers interested in switching jobs. 不过，相较于竞争对手开出的条件，此次奖金仍显逊色。OpenAI 向有意跳槽的苹果工程师提供的年度股票激励最高可达 100 万美元。

OpenAI's hardware division is co-led by former Apple executive Tang Tan. Tang Tan was long responsible for the iPhone product design team, which is precisely the core group that received these bonuses. OpenAI 的硬件部门由前苹果高管唐·坦（Tang Tan）联合主导。唐·坦曾长期负责 iPhone 产品设计团队，正是此次获发奖金的核心群体。

Reportedly, his team has poached dozens of engineers from Apple, covering multiple product lines including iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Vision Pro. Additionally, OpenAI has collaborated with former Apple Chief Design Officer Jony Ive to jointly develop new generation AI hardware products, with plans for a launch next year. 据报道，其团队已从苹果挖走数十名工程师，涵盖 iPhone、iPad、Apple Watch 及 Vision Pro 等多条产品线。此外，OpenAI 还与前苹果首席设计官乔尼·艾维（Jony Ive）展开合作，共同开发新一代 AI 硬件产品，并计划于明年推出。

Gemini 推出记忆导入功能

Yesterday, Gemini launched two new features, "Import Memories" and "Import Chat History," allowing users to directly migrate personal preferences and conversation history accumulated in other AI applications to Gemini, eliminating the need to re-"train" the assistant from scratch. 昨天，Gemini 推出「导入记忆」和「导入聊天记录」两项新功能，允许用户将在其他 AI 应用中积累的个人偏好与对话历史直接迁移至 Gemini，无需从零开始重新「训练」助手。

  • Import Memories: After entering the Gemini settings page, the system will provide a prompt. Copy and paste it into your existing AI application. Once that application generates a preference summary, paste the content back into Gemini to "move" your memories to Gemini; 导入记忆：进入 Gemini 设置页面后，系统会提供一段 prompt，将其复制并粘贴至原有 AI 应用，待该应用生成一份偏好摘要后，再将内容粘贴回 Gemini，即可将记忆「搬家」到 Gemini；
  • Import Chat History: Export the complete chat history from your original AI assistant, upload it to Gemini as a .zip archive no larger than 5GB, and then you can continue previous conversations within Gemini. 导入聊天记录：从原 AI 助手导出完整的聊天记录，以不超过 5GB 的 .zip 压缩包形式上传至 Gemini，完成后即可在 Gemini 内继续此前的对话。

Claude 新模型曝光，官方称「将带来前所未有的安全风险」

According to Fortune, a new AI model being tested by Anthropic has been revealed, codenamed "Claude Mythos," which significantly outperforms the current strongest version, Claude Opus 4.6, in multiple tests including programming, academic reasoning, and cybersecurity. 据《财富》报道，Anthropic 正在测试的一款全新 AI 模型曝光，代号为「Claude Mythos」，在编程、学术推理和网络安全等多项测试中的得分均大幅超越现有最强版本 Claude Opus 4.6。

Leaked documents show that Claude Mythos is also internally referred to as "Capybara," representing an entirely new model tier—larger in scale and more capable than the existing top-tier Opus series, but also with higher operating costs. 泄露文件显示，Claude Mythos 在内部也被称为「卡皮巴拉」（Capybara），代表一个全新的模型层级——比现有最高档的 Opus 系列体量更大、能力更强，但运行成本也更高。

Compared to our previous strongest model, Claude Opus 4.6, Capybara showed significant score improvements in multiple tests, including software programming, academic reasoning, and cybersecurity. 与我们此前最强的模型 Claude Opus 4.6 相比，Capybara 在软件编程、学术推理和网络安全等多项测试中的得分均大幅提升。

Notably, Anthropic expressed an unusually high degree of vigilance regarding the cybersecurity risks of Claude Mythos in the leaked documents. The files state that it "heralds an impending wave of AI-driven exploits, whose speed will far outpace defenders' ability to respond." 值得关注的是，Anthropic 在泄露的文档中对 Claude Mythos 的网络安全风险表达了异乎寻常的高度警惕。文件称其「预示着即将到来的一波 AI 驱动漏洞利用浪潮，其速度将远超防御者的应对能力」。

The company also stated that the model's computational cost is extremely high, and it is not yet ready for public release. Access will be gradually expanded through the Claude API in the coming weeks. 公司同时表示，该模型计算成本极高，尚未准备好面向大众发布，将在未来数周内通过 Claude API 逐步扩大访问范围。

乐道重启轿车和 SUV 研发，定位 20 万级市场

According to LatePost Auto, Ledao is developing a sedan and an SUV, both positioned in the 200,000-yuan market segment. The sedan targets small family users while also considering commercial scenarios. 据晚点 Auto 报道，乐道正在开发一款轿车和一款 SUV，两款车型均定位于 20 万级市场，其中轿车面向小家庭用户，同时兼顾商用场景。

In fact, these two models had been planned previously but were temporarily suspended when Nio implemented its CBU (Core Business Unit) reform early last year, resuming development in late August. Ledao does not rule out further expanding its mass-market models based on market conditions. 事实上，这两款车型此前已有规划，但在去年初蔚来推行 CBU（基本经营单元）改革时一度暂停、去年 8 月底恢复开发。乐道并不排除根据市场情况进一步扩充大众市场车型。

The direct reason for the project's restart was the impressive results delivered consecutively by the Ledao L90 and the new ES8. 促成项目重启的直接原因，是乐道 L90 与全新 ES8 接连交出的亮眼成绩。

  • The L90 was launched at the end of July last year, with sales exceeding 10,000 in its first month, driving Nio's overall sales to grow by 55.2% year-on-year for that month; L90 于去年 7 月底上市，首月销量破万，带动蔚来当月整体销量同比增长 55.2%；
  • The new ES8 began pre-sales in late August last year, and after its official launch on NIO Day on September 20, its full-year production capacity for last year sold out within two days. 全新 ES8 于去年 8 月下旬开启预售，在 9 月 20 日 NIO Day 正式发布后，去年全年产能在两天内售罄。

Nio internally believes that relying solely on two or three models to boost sales is unrealistic, especially in the fiercely competitive mainstream price segment; furthermore, sales of high-end models like the ES8 often have cyclical patterns and are difficult to maintain at high levels long-term. Therefore, quickly completing the Ledao product line while the current momentum is strong is considered an exceptionally urgent strategic task. 蔚来内部目前认为，仅靠两三款车型拉升销量并不现实，尤其在竞争激烈的主流价格带；而 ES8 这类高端车型的销量往往具有周期性，难以长期维持高位。趁当前势头尽快补齐乐道产品线，因此被视为格外紧迫的战略任务。

智谱 CEO 张鹏：大模型低价竞争不可持续，提价是「回归正常商业价值」

At the open-source themed roundtable of the Zhongguancun Forum Annual Conference held yesterday, Yang Zhilin, founder of Moonshot AI (Kimi), hosted an open-source themed roundtable, with Zhang Peng, CEO of Zhipu AI, Xia Lixue, co-founder and CEO of Wuwenshinqiong, Luo Fuli, head of Xiaomi MiMo large model, and Huang Chao, assistant professor at the University of Hong Kong, participating. 在昨天举行的中关村论坛年会开源主题圆桌上，月之暗面（Kimi）创始人杨植麟主持了一场开源主题圆桌，智谱 AI CEO 张鹏、无问芯穹联合创始人兼 CEO 夏立雪、小米 MiMo 大模型负责人罗福莉、香港大学助理教授黄超共同参与。

🔗 Related reading: On-site record | Yang Zhilin asked around, and AI leaders are all worried about Tokens 🔗 相关阅读： 现场实录｜杨植麟问了一圈，AI 掌门人都在为 Token 发愁

The conversation started with OpenClaw (Lobster), the hottest topic in the AI community this year, and extended to model pricing logic, inference infrastructure bottlenecks, model architecture innovation, and a collective judgment on the industry's direction over the next 12 months. 对话以今年 AI 圈最热的 OpenClaw（龙虾）为引，延伸至模型定价逻辑、推理基础设施瓶颈、模型架构创新，以及对未来 12 个月行业走向的集体判断。

At the meeting, Zhang Peng, CEO of Zhipu AI, responded to the price increase of its newly released GLM-5-Turbo model, characterizing it as an inevitable choice driven by changes in the industry's cost structure, rather than a commercial strategy adjustment. 会上，智谱 AI CEO 张鹏就旗下最新发布的 GLM-5-Turbo 模型提价一事作出回应，将其定性为行业成本结构变化下的必然选择，而非商业策略调整。

Zhang Peng characterized this price increase as a "return to normal commercial value," and frankly stated that long-term low-price competition is detrimental to the overall development of the industry. 张鹏将此次提价定性为「回归正常商业价值」，并直言长期低价竞争不利于行业整体发展。

We need a healthy business closed-loop to continuously optimize model capabilities. 我们需要一个良性的商业闭环，才能持续优化模型能力。

Zhang Peng stated that with the rapid popularization of Agent frameworks represented by OpenClaw, the usage paradigm of large models has shifted from "dialogue" to "getting things done." To complete a complex Agent task, the model needs to autonomously perform long-range task planning, repeated debugging, context compression, and process multimodal information. 张鹏表示，随着以 OpenClaw 为代表的 Agent 框架快速普及，大模型的使用范式已从「对话」转向「干活」。完成一个复杂 Agent 任务，模型需要自主进行长程任务规划、反复 Debug、压缩上下文，并处理多模态信息。

He pointed out that under this paradigm, the Token consumption for a single task could be ten or even a hundred times that of previous simple Q&A. 他指出，在这一范式下，单次任务的 Token 消耗量可能是此前简单问答的十倍甚至百倍。

Most people might not realize it, but they will see the money on their bills continuously dropping. 一般人可能体会不出来，但会看到自己账单上的钱在不停地往下掉。

Xia Lixue, CEO of Wuwenshinqiong, also pointed out that since the end of January this year, Wuwenshinqiong's Token usage has basically doubled every two weeks, and has now increased tenfold. "The last time I saw this growth rate was when mobile data was just becoming popular in the 3G era." 无问芯穹 CEO 夏立雪也指出，无问芯穹今年 1 月底以来，Token 用量基本每两周翻一番，目前已翻了十倍，「上次见到这个增速，还是 3G 时代手机流量刚普及的感觉」。

He believes that existing resources are far from sufficient to support this growth rate, and the entire industry is accelerating its transition from the training era to the inference era. Structural tension in computing power supply will be one of the most core contradictions in the coming year. 他认为，现有资源远不足以支撑这一增速，整个行业正在从训练时代加速转入推理时代，算力供给的结构性紧张将是未来一年最核心的矛盾之一。

Regarding clear time standards, the new regulations stipulate: if a motor vehicle driver drives continuously for more than 4 hours without stopping for rest, or rests for less than 20 minutes, it shall be deemed as fatigue driving. 在明确的时长标准上，新规规定：机动车驾驶人连续驾驶超过 4 小时未停车休息，或停车休息时间少于 20 分钟的，应认定为疲劳驾驶。

For passenger vehicles, the new regulations make stricter provisions: if a passenger motor vehicle driver drives continuously for more than 2 hours between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM the next day without stopping for rest or resting for less than 20 minutes, or if the cumulative driving time within 24 hours exceeds 8 hours, it shall also be deemed as fatigue driving. 对于客运车辆，新规作出更为严格的规定：客运机动车驾驶人在晚 22:00 至次日凌晨 6:00 连续驾驶超过 2 小时未停车休息或休息不足 20 分钟，以及在 24 小时内累计驾驶时间超过 8 小时的，同样认定为疲劳驾驶。

The new regulations also introduce two types of auxiliary determination methods: "physiological state" and "background investigation": 新规还引入了「生理状态」与「背景调查」两类辅助认定路径：

  • For drivers who had conditions to avoid an accident but failed to take timely measures, if monitoring equipment (including video surveillance, EEG measurement devices, etc.) shows "physiological fatigue with closed eyes" within 10 minutes before the accident, it can be determined as fatigue driving; 对于在事故中具备避险条件但未及时采取措施的驾驶人，若监测设备（包括视频监控、脑电测量设备等）显示其在事故发生前 10 分钟内出现「生理疲劳闭眼」，可认定为疲劳驾驶；
  • If interrogation or questioning results confirm that the driver was in a dazed or drowsy state while still driving, or if investigations into their sleep, diet, medication, work, and living conditions before departure confirm the existence of relevant risk factors, these can also serve as a basis for determination. 若询问或讯问结果证实驾驶人处于精神恍惚、困倦状态仍驾驶车辆，或对其出发前睡眠、饮食、用药、工作及生活情况的调查证实存在相关风险因素，同样可作为认定依据。
