Sora 首周 iOS 下载量达 62.7 万，超越 ChatGPT 首发成绩

According to the latest data from app intelligence firm Appfigures, OpenAI's new app Sora garnered approximately 627,000 downloads in its first week on the iOS platform, surpassing ChatGPT's performance in its launch week. 应用情报公司 Appfigures 最新数据显示，OpenAI 推出的新应用 Sora 在 iOS 平台上线首周共获得约 62.7 万次下载，超过了 ChatGPT 推出时的首周表现。

Sora was officially released on September 30th, recording 56,000 downloads on that day and quickly entering the top three in the overall download rankings of the US App Store. Sora 于 9 月 30 日正式发布，当日即录得 5.6 万次下载，并迅速跻身美国应用商店综合类下载榜前三。

By October 3rd, its ranking had risen to the top. In comparison, ChatGPT's iOS downloads in its first week were 606,000, but it was only available in the US market at the time, whereas Sora covered both the US and Canadian markets. If only the US region is considered, Sora's first-week downloads also reached approximately 96% of ChatGPT's. 到 10 月 3 日，其排名已升至榜首。相比之下，ChatGPT 在首周的 iOS 下载量为 60.6 万次，但当时仅在美国市场开放，而 Sora 同时覆盖了美国和加拿大市场。若仅计算美国地区，Sora 的首周下载量也达到 ChatGPT 的约 96%。

It is worth noting that Sora currently still operates on an invitation-only basis, while ChatGPT was open to a wider public upon its release. 值得注意的是，Sora 目前仍采取邀请制，而 ChatGPT 在发布时则面向更广泛的公众开放。

This means that even with higher thresholds, Sora still demonstrates strong user appeal. 这意味着，在更高门槛下，Sora 依然展现出强劲的用户吸引力。

Appfigures data also shows that Sora's daily downloads peaked at 107,800 on October 1st, and although there have been fluctuations since then, the overall trend remains stable. Appfigures 数据还显示，Sora 在 10 月 1 日的日下载量达到峰值 10.78 万次，此后虽有波动，但整体保持稳定。

Mark Gurman 质疑「天线门」修复：真正解决在 iPhone 4s

In 2010, Apple released the iPhone 4, but users soon discovered that when holding the phone in a common posture, the signal bars would drop sharply. 2010 年，苹果发布 iPhone 4，但很快用户发现，当以常见姿势握持手机时，信号栏会骤降。

At that time, Apple co-founder Steve Jobs even responded by saying "users are holding it wrong." However, Apple eventually had to provide free protective cases to users and face class-action lawsuits. 彼时，苹果联合创始人史蒂夫·乔布斯甚至回应称「用户握持方式不对」。然而，最终苹果不得不为用户提供免费保护壳，并面对集体诉讼。

Recently, the topic has resurfaced, and more details have been disclosed. 最近，该话题再度浮出水面，更多细节也被披露。

9to5Mac's latest report points out that the real key to "Antennagate" was not a hardware defect, but a problem with the signal display algorithm: Apple's calculation formula at the time was too "optimistic," leading to 4 to 5 bars of signal still being displayed in weak signal environments. 9to5Mac 的最新报道指出，「天线门」真正的关键并非硬件缺陷，而是信号显示算法存在问题：苹果当时的计算公式过于「乐观」，导致弱信号环境下依然显示 4 至 5 格信号。

And once holding the phone caused attenuation, the signal bars would instantly drop from "full" to 2 bars, creating a "catastrophic" drop. 而一旦握持导致衰减，信号栏会从「满格」瞬间跌至 2 格，形成「灾难性」落差。

In the report, software engineer Sam Henri Gold found after comparing the firmware that Apple only modified a lookup table in the CommCenter framework in iOS 4.0.1, totaling 20 bytes of data. 报道中，软件工程师 Sam Henri Gold 对比固件后发现，苹果在 iOS 4.0.1 中仅修改了 CommCenter 框架中的一张查找表，总计 20 字节数据。

After the adjustment, the signal bar drop curve became smoother, making it harder for users to see "instant drops." At the same time, Apple also quietly increased the display height of the signal bars, making 1 and 2 bars of signal look "not too bad." 调整后，信号栏下降曲线更加平滑，用户更难看到「瞬间掉格」的情况。同时，苹果还悄然加高了信号栏的显示高度，让 1 格和 2 格信号看起来「不至于过于糟糕」。

However, Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman bluntly stated on X that "this is complete nonsense," believing that the update did not truly fix the problem, but merely "masked the signal bar display," and the real solution was the redesigned antenna system on the iPhone 4s. 不过，彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 在 X 上直言「这完全是胡说八道」，他认为该更新并未真正修复问题，只是「掩盖了信号栏的显示」，真正的解决方案是 iPhone 4s 上重新设计的天线系统。
