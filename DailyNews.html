「iOS 变得更像安卓」，苹果发长文反对欧盟《数字市场法》

Yesterday, Apple issued a statement on its official website, strongly opposing the EU's Digital Markets Act (DMA), stating that the law is forcing iOS to become 'more like Android' and severely harming user experience and privacy security. 昨天，苹果在官网发布声明，对欧盟《数字市场法》（DMA）提出强烈反对，称该法正在迫使 iOS 变得「更像安卓」，并严重损害用户体验与隐私安全。

Apple stated that since the launch of the App Store in 2008, the company has been committed to providing users with a safe and unified app ecosystem. 苹果表示，自 2008 年 App Store 上线以来，公司一直致力于为用户提供安全、统一的应用生态。

However, the DMA requires Apple to open up sideloading, third-party app stores, and alternative payment systems in the EU, which will lead to 'a riskier, less intuitive App experience.' Apple warned that users might face risks such as fraud, malware, pornography, and gambling apps. 但 DMA 要求苹果在欧盟开放侧载、第三方应用商店及替代支付系统，这将导致「风险更高、更不直观的 App 体验」。苹果警告称，用户可能面临诈骗、恶意软件、色情与赌博应用等风险。

Furthermore, Apple also pointed out that the DMA requires it to provide some features on non-Apple devices, leading to new features such as AirPods real-time translation, iPhone mirroring, and Maps 'places visited' being forced to delay their launch in the EU. 此外，苹果还指出，DMA 要求其在非苹果设备上提供部分功能，导致 AirPods 实时翻译、iPhone 镜像、地图「曾去地点」等新特性在欧盟被迫延迟上线。

Apple stated that this has caused the device experience for European users to gradually fall behind other regions globally. 苹果称，这使得欧洲用户的设备体验逐渐落后于全球其他地区。

What worries Apple even more is that the DMA allows other companies to request access to iPhone users' sensitive data, including notification content and Wi-Fi connection history. Apple believes this will pose serious privacy and security threats. 更令苹果担忧的是，DMA 允许其他公司请求访问 iPhone 用户的敏感数据，包括通知内容和 Wi-Fi 连接历史。苹果认为，这将带来严重的隐私与安全威胁。

Apple stated bluntly in its declaration that the DMA has not achieved its goals of 'promoting competition and increasing choice,' but rather 'makes iOS look more like Android,' weakening its differentiated advantages. 'EU users have fewer choices, a worse experience, and higher security risks.' Apple called on regulators to re-examine the actual impact of the law. 苹果在声明中直言，DMA 并未实现「促进竞争、增加选择」的目标，反而「使得 iOS 看起来更像 Android」，削弱了差异化优势。「欧盟用户的选择更少，体验更差，安全风险更高。」苹果呼吁监管机构重新审视该法的实际影响。

机构：第四季度闪存价格将上涨 5-10%

A new research report released yesterday by TrendForce indicates that, influenced by the spillover effect of rapidly increasing demand for QLC Enterprise SSDs, the NAND Flash market will see a comprehensive price increase in the fourth quarter, with an average increase expected to be between 5-10%. 昨天发布的 TrendForce 集邦咨询最新研报指出，受 QLC Enterprise SSD 需求快速攀升的外溢效应影响，NAND Flash 市场在第四季度将迎来全面涨价，平均涨幅预计在 5-10% 之间。

The report shows that due to HDD supply shortages and extended delivery times, CSPs (Cloud Service Providers) have recently shifted a significant portion of their storage demand to QLC products, with a surge of urgent orders in the short term, driving the market sentiment from conservative to active. 报告显示，由于 HDD 供给短缺与交期过长，CSP（云端服务供应商）近期将储存需求大幅转向 QLC 产品，短期急单涌入，推动市场氛围由保守转向积极。

Currently, SanDisk has already announced a 10% price increase, and Micron has also suspended quoting due to capacity and pricing considerations. 目前，闪迪已率先宣布调涨 10%，美光也因产能与价格考量暂停报价。

From the supply side, original manufacturers significantly improved the supply-demand balance in the first half of the year through production cuts and inventory reduction, and concentrated capacity on high-margin products, reducing price competition. QLC, with its cost advantage, is becoming a key focus in the SSD market, especially with the rise of generative AI driving continuous growth in demand for massive data storage. 从供给端来看，原厂在上半年通过减产与去化库存，已显著改善供需平衡，并将产能集中于高毛利产品，减少价格竞争；QLC 因具备成本优势，正成为 SSD 市场布局重点，尤其在生成式 AI 带动下，海量数据储存需求持续上升。

On the demand side, although consumer market and OEM procurement have slowed, Server OEM and CSP vendors' inventories were cleared in the first half of the year. Coupled with NVIDIA's new generation Blackwell chips entering mass shipment, and the tight supply of HDDs, Enterprise SSD demand has significantly increased, becoming the main driver for price hikes. 在需求端，尽管消费市场与 OEM 采购趋缓，但 Server OEM 与 CSP 厂商库存已在上半年清理完毕，加上 NVIDIA 新一代 Blackwell 芯片进入放量出货阶段，叠加 HDD 紧缺，Enterprise SSD 需求大幅增长，成为价格上涨的主要推力。

Furthermore, eMMC/UFS have limited room for price increases due to weak demand and competition from Chinese manufacturers; while NAND Flash Wafer supply remains tight due to process transitions and capacity prioritization for high-margin products, also supporting higher prices. 此外，eMMC/UFS 受制于需求疲软与中系厂商竞争，涨幅空间有限；而 NAND Flash Wafer 因制程转换与产能优先投向高毛利产品，供给持续紧俏，同样支撑价格走高。

TrendForce expects that as AI investment momentum continues, the NAND Flash market will maintain a positive development trend in the fourth quarter. 集邦咨询预计，随着 AI 投资动能延续，NAND Flash 市场在第四季度将维持正向发展态势。

X 光扫描显示 8% 的廉价电池样本存在短路与起火风险

Recently, Lumafield, a provider of industrial X-ray CT scanners and cloud analysis software, released a 'Battery Quality Report,' which scanned and analyzed 1054 18650 batteries using industrial X-ray CT technology. 近日，工业级 X 光 CT 扫描仪与云端分析软件提供商 Lumafield 发布了一份《电池质量报告》，利用工业级 X 光 CT 技术对 1054 节 18650 电池进行了扫描分析。

The report shows significant differences in the safety and reliability of batteries from different sources. 报告显示，不同来源的电池在安全性和可靠性上的巨大差异。

The report indicates that batteries from reputable brands (such as Panasonic, Samsung, Murata) performed stably on key quality indicators, with no serious defects found. 报告显示，正规品牌（如松下、三星、Murata）的电池在关键质量指标上表现稳定，没有发现严重缺陷。

In contrast, cheap or counterfeit batteries frequently showed problems, with 8% of samples having 'electrode overhang' defects . This structural issue can lead to the formation of lithium dendrites, causing short circuits and thermal runaway, with the defect rate for some brands even reaching as high as 15% . 反观廉价或假冒电池，问题频出，其中 8% 的样本存在「极片悬垂」缺陷 ，这一结构性问题可能导致锂枝晶形成，引发短路和热失控，部分品牌的缺陷率甚至高达 15% 。

  • OEM batteries: Stable electrode sheet overhang distribution, good electrode alignment, and capacity is 76% – 89% of the nominal value. OEM 电池：极片悬垂分布稳定，电极对齐良好，容量为标称值的 76% – 89%。
  • Counterfeit batteries: Poor process control, electrode sheet overhang deviation is 7 times greater than OEM; severe false labeling, with a battery nominally rated at 9900 mAh actually measuring only about 1200 mAh. 假冒电池：工艺控制差，极片悬垂偏差比 OEM 大 7 倍；虚标严重，标称 9900 mAh 的电池实测仅约 1200 mAh。
  • Supply chain risk: Counterfeit batteries penetrate the market using complex global supply chains, making them almost impossible to trace. 供应链风险：假冒电池利用复杂的全球供应链渗透市场，几乎无法溯源。

The report points out that unregulated battery supply chains introduce dangerous products into the consumer market, increasing the risk of fires and explosions. 报告指出，未经管控的电池供应链将危险产品带入消费市场，增加火灾、爆炸风险。

Lumafield recommends that manufacturers strengthen quality inspection through means such as CT scanning and enhance supply chain management. Lumafield 建议制造商通过 CT 扫描等手段强化质量检测，并加强供应链管理。

For consumers, Lumafield advises avoiding the purchase of cheap batteries from unknown sources and paying attention to warning signs such as battery heating, swelling, or unusual odors. 对于消费者，Lumafield 则建议应避免购买来源不明的廉价电池，并注意电池发热、膨胀、异味等预警信号。

Lumafield emphasizes: "The few dollars saved are far from enough to offset potential performance degradation and safety hazards." Lumafield 强调：「省下的几美元远不足以抵消潜在的性能下降和安全隐患。」
