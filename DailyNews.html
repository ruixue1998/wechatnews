苹果智能眼镜曝光：四款镜框、多种配色，还有库克同款

According to information revealed by Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman yesterday in his PowerOn column, Apple is developing its first smart glasses product, internally codenamed N50, with plans for release by the end of this year or early next year, and an official market launch set for next year. 据彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 昨天在 PowerOn 专栏中曝光的信息，苹果正在开发其首款智能眼镜产品，内部代号为 N50，计划于今年底或明年初发布，正式上市时间定于明年。

Gurman pointed out that the functionality of these glasses is similar to the Meta Ray-Ban series, focusing on practical daily use cases: taking photos and videos, answering calls, receiving notifications, playing music, and performing daily operations via voice assistant, and will feature AI Siri, launched alongside iOS 27 . Gurman 指出，这款眼镜的功能定位与 Meta Ray-Ban 系列相似，主打日常实用场景：拍照录像、接听电话、收听通知、播放音乐，以及通过语音助手实现日常操作， 并搭载与 iOS 27 一同推出的 AI Siri 。

From a design perspective, unlike Meta, which primarily relies on Essilor for frames, and Google and Samsung, which entered the market with Warby Parker, Apple plans to design its frames entirely in-house, aiming to create a recognizable "iconic" look. 设计层面，与 Meta 主要依赖依视路提供镜框、Google 和三星借助 Warby Parker 入局不同，苹果计划完全自主设计，以期打造出具有辨识度的「标志性」外观。

Apple plans to launch at least four frame styles and offer a variety of color options. Currently, the tested designs include: large rectangular frames (similar in style to Ray-Ban Wayfarers), slender rectangular frames (similar to those Apple CEO Tim Cook wears daily), large oval or round frames, and smaller, more delicate oval or round frames. 苹果计划推出至少四种镜框样式，并提供多种配色选择，目前测试中的方案包括：大尺寸矩形框（风格近似 Ray-Ban Wayfarers）、细长矩形框（与苹果 CEO 库克日常佩戴款式相近）、大号椭圆或圆形框，以及更小巧精致的椭圆或圆形款。

Regarding colors, Apple is exploring options such as black, ocean blue, and light brown. The frame material uses high-end acetate fiber, offering superior durability and texture compared to the ordinary plastic used by most brands on the market. 配色方面，苹果正在探索黑色、海洋蓝和浅棕等选项，镜框材质采用高端醋酸纤维，耐用性和质感均优于市面上大多数品牌所用的普通塑料。

Camera design is also a key differentiation point for Apple. It is reported that Apple is considering a design solution with vertically arranged oval lenses accompanied by surrounding lights, differing from the circular cameras on Meta products. 摄像头设计同样是苹果的差异化着力点。据悉，苹果正在考虑采用竖向排列的椭圆形镜头配合环绕灯光的设计方案，有别于 Meta 产品上的圆形摄像头。

曝特斯拉引入欣旺达动力电池

According to 36Kr, Sunwoda Power, a subsidiary of Sunwoda focusing on power battery business, has officially entered Tesla's global supply chain, becoming its fifth power battery supplier. The relevant batteries have been shipped from Sunwoda's Yiwu factory in Zhejiang and are installed in export models produced at Tesla's Shanghai factory. 据 36 氪报道，欣旺达旗下专注动力电池业务的子公司欣旺达动力，已正式进入特斯拉全球供应链，成为其第五家动力电池供应商。相关电池已从欣旺达浙江义乌工厂出货，搭载于特斯拉上海工厂生产的出口车型之上。

This cooperation adopts a new model. Tesla directly procures battery cells from Sunwoda, while modules and PACKs (battery packs) are manufactured by Tesla itself. This differs from the previous method of purchasing complete modules from CATL, and it also means that Tesla will gain more pricing power and customization rights to achieve deep control over battery costs. 此次合作采用了新模式。特斯拉直接向欣旺达采购电芯，模组与 PACK（电池包）则由特斯拉自行制造。这与此前从宁德时代采购完整模组的方式不同，也意味着特斯拉将掌握更多定价权与定制权，以实现对电池成本的深度管控。

Insiders revealed that the power battery cells supplied by Sunwoda to Tesla use third-generation lithium iron phosphate material, supporting a charging rate increase to 3C. 知情人士透露，欣旺达为特斯拉供应的动力电芯采用第三代磷酸铁锂材料，支持充电倍率提升至 3C。

Currently, there is no definitive conclusion on when Sunwoda batteries will be installed in Tesla models sold domestically. Battery industry insiders analyze that Tesla usually has an observation period of nearly a year for new components, but other decision-making factors cannot be ruled out. 目前，欣旺达电池何时搭载于特斯拉国内销售车型尚无定论。有电池行业人士分析，特斯拉对新零部件通常有将近一年的观察期，但不排除其他决策因素介入。

小米汽车：XLA 和其他家的 VLA 的区别在「X」能力

Recently, Xiaomi Auto released "Xiaomi Auto Q&A with Netizens" Episode 234. It mentioned that XLA is an extension of the industry's mainstream VLA technology, with the core difference being the arbitrary modality capability represented by "X". 近日，小米汽车发布「小米汽车答网友问」第 234 集。其中提到，XLA 是对行业主流 VLA 技术的扩展，核心差异在于「X」所代表的任意模态能力。

Compared to common industry solutions, XLA natively supports more modalities of information input—in addition to video, radar, and navigation, it also incorporates sound and even robot data, aiming for a more comprehensive perception of complex traffic environments. 相比业内常见方案，XLA 原生支持更多模态的信息输入——除视频、雷达、导航外，还引入了声音乃至机器人数据，以期更全面地感知复杂交通环境。

In terms of inference mechanisms, XLA performs high-speed logical deduction in the latent space using machine language, without the need to translate the thought process into human language. The official statement claims this approach balances low latency and interpretability. Additionally, the system continues to apply reinforcement learning and world model technologies to ensure safe and controllable behavior. 在推理机制上，XLA 在潜空间内以机器语言进行高速逻辑推演，无需将思考过程转译为人类语言，官方称此举兼顾了低时延性与可解释性。此外，该系统延续应用了强化学习与世界模型技术，以确保行为安全可控。

蔚来李斌：智能电动时代迭代太快，一款车浪费几个亿是常态

According to IT Home, at the High-Level Forum on the Development of Smart Electric Vehicles, William Li, founder and chairman of Nio, stated that the rapid product iteration in the era of smart electric vehicles is causing significant resource waste. He pointed out that a car model often needs to start a new round of iteration just after its production capacity ramp-up is completed, "it's normal for a car to waste several hundred million [yuan]." 据 IT 之家报道，在智能电动汽车发展高层论坛上，蔚来创始人、董事长李斌表示，智能电动时代的快速产品迭代正在造成大量资源浪费。他指出，一款车型往往在产能爬坡刚完成后便需启动新一轮迭代，「一款车浪费几个亿很正常」。

Li Bin summarized this phenomenon as "no flower stays red for a hundred days" – initial orders for new cars are strong, and companies expand production accordingly, but by the time capacity catches up, market enthusiasm often has already faded. 李斌将这一现象概括为「花无百日红」——新车上市初期订单旺盛，企业随即扩产，但等到产能跟上，市场热度往往已经消退。

He believes that the accelerated iteration of electric vehicle products makes it increasingly difficult to balance supply and demand in the industry, and the core pressure facing the entire industry remains profitability. In response, Li Bin called for promoting the standardization of battery cells and the unification of chips, believing this would bring cost reduction opportunities exceeding hundreds of billions of yuan to the industry. 他认为，电动汽车产品迭代提速，使得行业供需平衡的难度持续加大，当前整个行业面临的核心压力依然是盈利压力。对此，李斌呼吁推动电芯标准化与芯片统一化，认为这将为行业带来超千亿元的降本机会。

At the chip strategy level, Nio is currently advancing the "normalization" and "standardization" of automotive chips. Li Bin expects that by next year, Nio's localization rate for automotive semiconductors will reach 35%~40%. 在芯片战略层面，蔚来目前正推进车用芯片的「归一化」与「标准化」工作，李斌预计，到明年蔚来车用半导体国产化率将达到 35%~40%。

一汽高管：卖一辆车亏两三万，本土车企不能只顾内卷

According to Sina Finance, at the High-Level Forum on the Development of Smart Electric Vehicles (2026) held from April 11th to 12th, Zhou Shiying, Deputy General Manager of China FAW's Strategy and Cooperation Department, pointed out that the current situation for local Chinese car companies is quite severe. 据新浪财经报道，在 4 月 11 日至 12 日召开的智能电动汽车发展高层论坛（2026）上，中国一汽战略与合作部副总经理周时莹指出，当前中国本土车企的处境已相当严峻。

"Selling a car means losing twenty to thirty thousand yuan," and many high-quality component enterprises are also struggling to operate. She further asked, "Are we destined to forever compete against each other only in the domestic market?" 「卖一辆车就要亏损两三万块钱」，众多优质零部件企业的经营同样举步维艰。她追问道：「难道我们未来永远只能在国内市场互相厮杀吗？」

In her view, the root cause of this predicament lies in the industry's internal redundant construction and resource depletion. She cited examples of vehicle manufacturers establishing their own chip companies and computing centers, pointing out that this "each fighting their own battle" model is extremely inefficient. 在她看来，这一困局的根源在于行业内部的重复建设与资源内耗。她以整车企业纷纷自建芯片公司、算力中心为例，指出这种各自为战的模式效率极低。

If this continues, they might even have to develop controllable nuclear fusion to power computing centers, doing too many unnecessary things. 如果这样下去，甚至要去搞可控核聚变为算力中心供能，做的事情太多，完全没有必要。

She called on the entire industry to formulate unified standardized interfaces around the integration of artificial intelligence and automobiles, promoting reasonable division of labor: computing power companies focusing on computing services, high-process electronic component companies focusing on core component R&D, OS software companies focusing on operating system development, and vehicle manufacturers returning to their core responsibilities of brand building, safety assurance, and the integration of power chassis and intelligence. 她呼吁整个行业围绕人工智能与汽车的结合，制定统一的标准化接口，推动合理分工：算力公司专注算力服务，高制程电子元器件公司专注核心元器件研发，OS 软件公司专注操作系统开发，整车企业则回归品牌建设、安全保障及动力底盘与智能化的整合本职。

She emphasized that if the industry cannot support each other and allow specialized companies to leverage their advantages under an open technical architecture, "in the long run, the entire industry will struggle to have good development prospects." 她强调，若行业无法相互扶持、在开放技术架构下让专业公司发挥优势，「长期来看，整个行业很难有好的发展前景」。

According to IGN, Taeseok Jang, head of PUBG Studios, recently expressed a relatively tolerant stance on the application of generative AI in game development, believing that as long as the gameplay is excellent enough, there is nothing wrong with developers using AI to generate art assets. 据 IGN 报道，PUBG Studios 负责人 Taeseok Jang 近日就生成式 AI 在游戏开发中的应用表达了相对宽容的立场，认为只要游戏玩法足够出色，开发者使用 AI 生成美术资产并无不妥。
