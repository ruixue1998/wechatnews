英伟达千亿美元投资 OpenAI

Early this morning, Nvidia announced a strategic partnership with OpenAI and will invest up to $100 billion in OpenAI to support the latter's AI data center plan. 今日凌晨，英伟达宣布与 OpenAI 结成战略合作伙伴关系，并向 OpenAI 投资至多 1000 亿美元，用以支持后者的 AI 数据中心计划。

Specifically, the investment will be gradually paid to OpenAI as GPU deployment and usage increase. 具体来说，该笔投资将随着 GPU 的部署使用量提升，逐渐向 OpenAI 支付。

OpenAI will use millions of Nvidia GPU systems to deploy AI data centers, with energy consumption reaching at least 10 gigawatts (a million kilowatts). OpenAI 将使用数以百万计的英伟达 GPU 系统来部署 AI 数据中心，能耗达到至少 10 吉瓦（Gigawatt 百万千瓦）。

The system will adopt Nvidia's Vera Rubin platform as the core of OpenAI's next-generation AI infrastructure. This system will be used to train and run OpenAI's next-generation models, thereby accelerating the realization of OpenAI's anticipated superintelligence (superintelligence). 该系统将采用英伟达的 Vera Rubin 平台，作为 OpenAI 的下一代 AI 基础设施的核心。该系统将用于训练和运行 OpenAI 的下一代模型，从而加速 OpenAI 期待中的超级智能 (superintelligence) 的实现。

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang mentioned in a statement the story of OpenAI being the first "beta user" of Nvidia's DGX supercomputer system, stating that the two companies have been pushing each other for ten years. 英伟达 CEO 黄仁勋在声明中提到了 OpenAI 作为英伟达 DGX 超级计算机系统首个「内测用户」的故事，表示两家公司十年来一直互相推动。

Press releases from both sides show that OpenAI will become NVIDIA's preferred 'computing' and 'networking' strategic partner for its AI factory growth plan, ensuring that NVIDIA systems can always achieve the earliest and best optimization effects for OpenAI models and infrastructure software. 双方新闻稿显示，OpenAI 将成为英伟达 AI 工厂增长计划的首选「计算」和「网络」战略合作伙伴，确保英伟达系统总是能够面向 OpenAI 模型和基础架构软件实现最先、最好的优化效果。

The two companies plan to finalize the specific details of their strategic cooperation in the coming weeks. 两家公司计划在未来几周内敲定战略合作的具体细节。

微信回应「存储空间不足」提示 Bug：已定位原因并将尽快修复

Recently, some users complained on social media that WeChat displays a 'storage space insufficient' prompt during data migration or file reception, even when the device has ample remaining capacity, preventing normal operation. 近日，有用户在社交平台吐槽称，微信在数据迁移或文件接收过程中，会出现「存储空间不足」的提示，即便设备剩余容量充足，依然无法正常操作。

In response, WeChat employee 'Kecun Xiao Jiang' stated that the problem stems from the caching mechanism of an internal general interface: when the interval for obtaining remaining space is less than 5 minutes, the interface does not request the latest data from the system but directly returns a cached value. 对此，微信员工「客村小蒋」回应称，问题源于内部通用接口的缓存机制：当获取剩余空间的间隔不超过 5 分钟时，接口不会向系统请求最新数据，而是直接返回缓存值。

On some new devices, this logic might cause the remaining space data returned within 5 minutes of booting up to be 0, thus triggering an error message. 在部分新设备上，该逻辑可能导致开机 5 分钟内返回的剩余空间数据为 0，从而触发错误提示。

The official statement indicates that under normal circumstances, trying again 5 minutes after booting up should restore normal use, but this bug will be fixed in a subsequent version. 官方表示，正常情况下，开机 5 分钟后再次尝试即可恢复正常使用，但该 Bug 将在后续版本中修复。

Furthermore, the team emphasized that this issue is unrelated to device capacity and does not align with external speculations of a 'unit conversion error'. 此外，团队强调，该问题与设备容量大小无关，与外界猜测的「单位换算错误」也并不相符。

欧盟拟改革隐私法规，或终结网页「同意 cookies」弹窗

According to POLITICO, the European Commission is planning significant adjustments to the ePrivacy Directive, revised in 2009, aiming to reduce the proliferation of "accept cookies" pop-ups on webpages. 据 POLITICO 报道，欧盟委员会正计划对 2009 年修订的《电子隐私指令》进行重大调整，意在减少网页上泛滥的「同意 cookies」弹窗。

The directive requires websites to obtain consent before writing cookies to a user's device, unless these cookies are "strictly necessary for providing the service". 该指令要求网站在向用户设备写入 cookies 前必须征得同意，除非这些 cookies 属于「提供服务所必需」。

For years, this regulation has led users to frequently encounter pop-ups, habitually clicking 'agree,' which has paradoxically weakened the actual effectiveness of privacy protection. 多年来，这一规定导致用户频繁面对弹窗，习惯性点击同意，反而削弱了隐私保护的实际效果。

The EU is considering allowing users to set cookie preferences once in a unified location, such as a browser, or exempting "technically necessary" and "simple statistics" cookies from consent requirements. 欧盟方面正在考虑允许用户在浏览器等统一位置一次性设定 cookies 偏好，或为「技术必要」及「简单统计」类 cookies 免除同意要求。

Industry insiders suggest incorporating cookie regulation into the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) to adopt a more flexible 'risk-based' approach; privacy groups, however, warn that expanding the scope of 'necessity' could open backdoors for advertising tracking. 业内人士建议将 cookies 监管纳入《通用数据保护条例》（GDPR），以采用更灵活的「基于风险」方式；而隐私团体则警告，扩大「必要」范围可能为广告追踪开后门。

The relevant reform proposals are expected to be presented as a 'comprehensive bill' in December this year, and along with the Digital Fairness Act in 2026, will trigger a new round of contention between privacy and industry interests. 预计相关改革方案将于今年 12 月以「综合法案」形式提出，并在 2026 年与《数字公平法案》一同引发新一轮隐私与产业利益的博弈。

丑化理想 MEGA 案终审宣判：赔 12 万元并道歉

Li Auto's legal department today issued a statement saying that it recently received a final court judgment confirming that Zhang Moumou, the holder of Weibo account 'A5W 工', and his affiliated MCN, "maliciously defamed the Li MEGA model to gain improper online traffic" on the day before the new Li MEGA car launch. 理想汽车法务部今日发布声明称，近日已收到法院终审判决，确认微博账号「A5W 工」持有人张某某及其所属 MCN 在理想 MEGA 新车发布前一天，「恶意丑化理想 MEGA 车型，以谋取不正当网络流量」。

多车主起诉特斯拉 FSD 功能涉嫌欺诈

According to a recent report by The Beijing News, seven Tesla owners who purchased the FSD (Full Self-Driving) feature have sued Tesla Motors Sales and Service (Beijing) Co., Ltd. on grounds of sales contract disputes. The cases have been collectively filed at the Daxing District People's Court in Beijing and are awaiting the first hearing. 据新京报日前报道，7 名购买了 FSD（完全自动驾驶能力）功能的特斯拉车主，以买卖合同纠纷为由，将特斯拉汽车销售服务（北京）有限公司告上法庭，案件已在北京市大兴区人民法院批量立案，正等待一审开庭。

The plaintiffs claim that they paid a FSD prepayment of up to 56,000 yuan when purchasing their vehicles, but in actual use, only limited functions such as automatic follow-car lane changes on highways could be realized. Core capabilities advertised, such as automatic summoning in open-air parking lots and full self-driving, have not yet been delivered. 原告称，他们在购车时支付了高达 5.6 万元的 FSD 预付费，但实际使用中仅能实现高速公路自动跟车变道等有限功能，宣传中的露天停车场自动召唤、完全自动驾驶等核心能力至今未兑现。

What further dissatisfied the owners was that in February this year, Tesla renamed the feature from "Full Self-Driving Capability" to "FSD Smart Assisted Driving Function," and some models equipped with HW3.0 hardware were excluded from the rollout. 更令车主不满的是，今年 2 月，特斯拉将该功能从「完全自动驾驶能力」更名为「FSD 智能辅助驾驶功能」，且部分搭载 HW3.0 硬件的车型被排除在推送范围之外。

The owners believe that Tesla engaged in misleading promotion despite knowing the hardware limitations and the inability to implement the functions, constituting fraud, and are demanding "a refund plus three times the compensation." 车主认为，特斯拉在明知硬件限制及功能无法落地的情况下，仍进行误导性宣传，构成欺诈，要求「退一赔三」。

The attorney pointed out that according to the "Consumer Rights Protection Law," business operators who provide false or misleading publicity that leads consumers to make incorrect purchasing decisions should bear three times the compensation liability. 代理律师指出，根据《消费者权益保护法》，经营者提供虚假或引人误解的宣传并导致消费者作出错误购买决定的，应承担三倍赔偿责任。

Currently, this series of cases has not yet been heard in court. The industry is watching closely, as this case may become the first consumer class action lawsuit in China specifically targeting the FSD feature. 目前，该系列案件尚未开庭审理，业内关注此案或将成为国内首例针对 FSD 功能的消费者共同诉讼。

北京市监局：电动自行车充电严禁「暗扣费」

Today, the Beijing Municipal Market Supervision Department issued a reminder and warning letter regarding charging fees for electric bicycles, requiring all operating units, property management services, and related entities to strictly implement regulations such as price-fee separation and clear pricing, and to eliminate the practice of collecting unlisted fees. 北京市市场监管部门今日发布针对电动自行车充电收费行为的提醒告诫书，要求各运营单位、物业服务及相关单位严格执行价费分离、明码标价等规定，杜绝收取未标明费用的行为。

  • Implement price-fee separation and strictly enforce clear pricing. 实行价费分离，严格明码标价
  • Strictly implement relevant electricity pricing policies. 严格落实相关电价政策
  • Reasonably formulate charging service fee standards. 合理制定充电服务费标准
  • Advocate for reducing charging service fees. 倡导降低充电服务费

The document clarifies that electric bicycle charging fees should comprise two parts: "charging electricity fee" and "service fee," which should be separately indicated and priced; electricity used by charging facilities within residential communities should be charged according to the "residential electricity" category. 文件明确，电动自行车充电费用应包含「充电电费」与「服务费」两部分，并分别标示、分别计价；居民住宅小区内的充电设施用电应按「居民生活用电」类别执行电价。
