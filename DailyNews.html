消息称折叠屏版 iPad 或 2029 年才亮相

Image from 9to5Mac 图来自 9to5Mac

Early this morning, Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman published an article stating that Apple is currently attempting to redesign the iPad by adding a giant foldable screen, but this plan has encountered multiple issues, leading to a later release date for the product than originally intended. 今天凌晨，彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 发文称，苹果目前正在试图通过新增巨型可折叠屏来重新设计 iPad，但这一计划遇到了多重问题，导致相关产品发布时间将会比原定目标更晚。

Gurman stated that Apple has been developing a foldable screen iPad for several years, with an estimated price of $3000 (approximately RMB 21367), and the latest plan is to release it in 2028. Gurman 称， 苹果目前已研发折叠屏版 iPad 数年，定价预计在 3000 美元（约合人民币 21367 元），目前最新计划是于 2028 年发布。

However, according to informed sources, due to engineering challenges related to weight, functionality, display technology, and other factors, the debut of the foldable screen iPad has currently been postponed to 2029 or even later. 但据知情人士透露， 由于重量、功能、显示技术等相关工程挑战影响，折叠屏 iPad 的首次亮相目前已被推迟至 2029 年甚至更晚。

The report states that the screen portion of the foldable iPad will be co-developed with Samsung, measuring approximately 18 inches, and the crease optimization will use the same technology as the rumored foldable iPhone. In terms of size, the foldable iPad, when folded, will be about the size of a 13-inch laptop and will feature an aluminum alloy casing. 报道称，折叠屏 iPad 的屏幕部分将与三星合作开发，约为 18 英寸，折痕优化将与传闻中的折叠屏 iPhone 共用同样的技术。尺寸方面，折叠屏 iPad 折叠后约为一台 13 英寸的笔记本电脑的大小，并且采用铝合金外壳。

At the same time, the report also pointed out that engineers are not satisfied with the weight of the foldable iPad: the product will weigh 3.5 pounds (approximately 1.59 kilograms), which is close to or even exceeds the weight of a 14-inch MacBook Pro (the M5 MacBook Pro 14 weighs 1.55 kilograms). 同时，报道还指出，工程师对折叠屏 iPad 的重量不够满意：该产品重量将达到 3.5 磅（约 1.59 千克），而这一表现接近甚至超过一台 14 英寸的 MacBook Pro 的重量（M5 MacBook Pro 14 重量为 1.55 千克）。

Additionally, Apple's new iPad Pro officially went on sale today. The new device is equipped with the latest M5 chip, bringing dual improvements in performance and AI capabilities. 另外，苹果全新 iPad Pro 今日正式开售，新机搭载最新的 M5 芯片，带来性能与 AI 能力双提升。

The new iPad Pro's screen can reach a peak brightness of 1600 nits, a minimum brightness as low as 1 nit, and supports an external display refresh rate of up to 120Hz. 新 iPad Pro 屏幕峰值亮度可达 1600 尼特，最低亮度低至 1 尼特，并支持最高 120Hz 外接显示器刷新率。

In terms of network and connectivity, the new device is equipped with Apple's self-developed N1 wireless chip for the first time, supporting Wi-Fi 7 and Bluetooth 6 for more stable peripheral connections. The cellular network version uses the C1X baseband, with data transfer speeds increased by approximately 50% compared to the previous generation, and supports eSIM. 网络与连接方面，新机首次搭载苹果自研 N1 无线芯片，支持 Wi-Fi 7 与蓝牙 6，外设连接更稳定；蜂窝网络版本则采用 C1X 基带，数据传输速度较前代提升约 50%，支持 eSIM。

It is worth noting that the iPad eSIM setup process is simpler than iPhone Air; you can directly download the carrier app and follow the instructions, without needing to visit a physical store. 值得注意的是，iPad eSIM 设置流程相比 iPhone Air 更加简便，直接下载运营商应用根据指引即可，无需前往营业厅。

In terms of storage and memory configuration, the entry-level model's RAM has been upgraded from the previous generation's 8GB to 12GB (1TB and above versions have 16GB), and SSD read/write speeds have also improved. 存储与内存配置方面，入门款起步从上代的 8G RAM 升级为 12GB（1TB 及以上版本为 16GB），SSD 读写速度也有所提升。

制糖工厂首创 OTW 云端升级服务，让「出厂定型」的充电头实现持续更新进化

On October 21st, Sugarcane Factory officially launched the internal beta of its OTW (Over The Wire & Wireless) custom charging solution service. 10 月 21 日，制糖工厂正式开启 OTW（Over The Wire & Wireless）自定义充电方案服务内测。

Unlike common OTA (Over The Air, wireless upgrades), OTW allows even non-networked chargers to receive continuous updates through a "wired + wireless" relay. 不同于常见的 OTA（Over The Air，无线升级），OTW 让即便不会联网的充电器，也能通过「有线 + 无线」的接力持续更新。

At the same time, OTW is also a long-term online cloud service, with a built-in "Own The Wish" mode, providing customized charging solutions for different devices. 同时，OTW 还是一项长期在线的云端服务，内置 Own The Wish 模式，为不同设备提供定制化的充电方案。

Through OTW, users can push configuration updates to charging heads with the assistance of AI Xiaodianpin, and customize exclusive charging solutions according to their device needs. This innovation transforms what were once standardized, generic components into personalized, self-evolving devices. 通过 OTW，用户可在 AI 小电拼的协助下，为充电头推送配置更新，并根据自身设备需求定制专属充电方案。这一创新，让原本千人一面的标准件，变成能够自我成长的个性化设备。

The Hard Candy A Charger (40W) has become the first non-smart AVS charging head that users can self-upgrade. With the help of AI Xiaodianpin's Smart Charging Agent function, users can obtain the latest configuration files via cloud OTA and upgrade the Hard Candy A Charger through a wired connection. 硬糖 A 充（40W）成为首个用户能够自升级的非智能 AVS 充电头，借助 AI 小电拼的智能助理功能（Smart Charging Agent），用户从云端 OTA 获取最新配置文件，并通过有线的方式硬糖 A 充进行升级。

After upgrading, the Hard Candy A Charger will gain continuously optimized protocol support and charging strategies, maintaining long-term compatibility and efficient performance with new devices and standards. 升级后，硬糖 A 充将获得持续优化的协议支持与充电策略，保持与新设备、新标准的长期兼容与高效表现。

OTW also provides on-demand upgrade modes for different models. Currently, users can configure the Hard Candy A Charger with three modes: Huawei mode, Android mode, and native mode. OTW 还为不同机型提供了按需升级模式。目前，用户可为硬糖 A 充配置三种模式：华为模式、安卓模式和原生模式。

Among them, Huawei mode prioritizes the Huawei protocol and cancels the PD protocol, while Android mode defaults to two PPS levels. Subsequently, Sugarcane Factory will gradually introduce other modes to meet users' demands for personalized charging. 其中，华为模式优先握手华为协议，取消 PD 协议，而安卓模式默认两档 PPS。后续，制糖工厂将陆续推出其他模式，满足用户对个性化充电的需求。

OpenAI stated that Atlas is a browser built around ChatGPT, rather than simply stuffing ChatGPT into it. Its core philosophy is comprehensive integration: OpenAI 表示，Atlas 是围绕 ChatGPT 构建的浏览器，而不是简单地把 ChatGPT 塞进去，其核心理念是全方位整合：

「智能眼镜 + 视网膜植入」临床试验成功，视障患者重获阅读能力

According to The Verge, a new study from The New England Journal of Medicine shows that an experimental technology combining smart glasses with a retinal implant has helped some patients severely blinded by age-related macular degeneration (AMD) regain partial central vision. 据 The Verge 报道，来自《新英格兰医学杂志》的最新研究显示，一项结合智能眼镜与视网膜植入物的实验性技术，帮助部分因老年性黄斑变性（AMD）而严重失明的患者恢复了部分中心视力。

The research team implanted a 2×2 mm photovoltaic device, composed of miniature solar panels, beneath the retina in 38 patients over 60 years old. 研究团队在 38 名 60 岁以上患者中植入了一枚 2×2 毫米的光伏装置，该装置由微型太阳能电池板组成，植入于视网膜下方。

The smart glasses worn by patients have a built-in camera that captures external images and transmits them to the implanted device via near-infrared light. The implanted device then sends weak electrical signals to the optic nerve, simulating the function of retinal cells. 患者佩戴的智能眼镜内置摄像头，可捕捉外部画面并通过近红外光传输至植入设备，随后由植入设备向视神经发出微弱电信号，模拟视网膜细胞的功能。

In the one-year clinical trial, 26 out of 32 patients who completed follow-up showed significant improvement in vision compared to baseline levels, accounting for approximately 80%. 在为期一年的临床试验中，32 名坚持完成随访的患者中有 26 名视力较基线水平显著改善，占比约 80%。

Some patients were able to read ordinary books and complete crossword puzzles again. Researchers noted that the current visual effects are still blurry and limited to black and white, but have been described by external experts as "astonishing progress." 部分患者能够重新阅读普通书籍、完成填字游戏。研究人员指出，目前的视觉效果仍然模糊且仅限黑白，但已被外部专家评价为「令人惊叹的进展」。

This technology is being advanced by the brain-computer interface company Science Corporation, whose founder and CEO, Max Hodak, co-founded Neuralink with Elon Musk. Science Corporation acquired relevant technology from the French medical device company Pixium Vision in 2024, allowing this clinical trial to continue. 该技术由脑机接口公司 Science Corporation 推动，其创始人兼 CEO Max Hodak 曾与马斯克共同创立 Neuralink。Science Corporation 于 2024 年收购了法国医疗设备公司 Pixium Vision 的相关技术，使得这项临床试验得以延续。

最新研究：持续喂养低质文本会对 AI 造成不可逆「脑损伤」

Recently, in a paper titled "Large Language Models May Suffer from 'Brain Rot'!" jointly published by multiple universities, the research team pointed out that large language models exhibit a phenomenon similar to human "cognitive degradation" after continuous exposure to low-quality text from online social platforms. 近日，在多所高校联合发布的题为《大语言模型可能会得「脑腐化」！》的论文中，研究团队指出，大型语言模型在持续接触低质量网络社交平台文本后，会出现类似人类「认知退化」的现象。

Researchers conducted continuous pre-training experiments on various models by constructing "junk data" and "control data" from the X platform. 研究人员通过从 X 平台构建「垃圾数据」与「对照数据」，并对多种模型进行持续预训练实验。

The results show that when the model is exposed to a high proportion of "junk data", it experiences a significant decline in reasoning, long-text comprehension, safety, and personality traits. 结果显示，当模型暴露于高比例「垃圾数据」时，其在推理、长文本理解、安全性以及人格倾向等方面均出现显著下降。

Error analysis shows that the model's main problem is "thought skipping": an increasing tendency to truncate or bypass critical reasoning chains required to solve problems. 错误分析显示，模型的主要问题是「思维跳跃」：越来越倾向于截断或跳过解决问题所需的关键推理链条。

Researchers further compared different types of social media posts and found that "engagement" is the strongest toxicity indicator — the more viral content is, the more likely it is to lead to model cognitive degradation. 研究者进一步比较了不同类型的社交媒体帖子，发现「参与度」是最强的毒性指标 —— 越容易病毒式传播的内容，越容易导致模型认知退化。

More worryingly, this decline is persistent. Even if high-quality data is supplemented in subsequent stages for instruction fine-tuning or continued pre-training, the model can only partially recover, and representation drift still exists. 更令人担忧的是，这种衰退具有持久性。即便在后续阶段补充高质量数据进行指令微调或继续预训练，模型也只能部分恢复，仍存在表征漂移。

The research team pointed out that this phenomenon is similar to "brain rot" in humans when exposed to fragmented, low-nutrient information for a long time, emphasizing the critical role of data quality in the continuous training of large models. The research team called on the industry to incorporate "cognitive health checks" into model maintenance processes to avoid long-term capability degradation. 研究团队指出，这一现象与人类在长期接触碎片化、低营养信息时的「脑腐化」类似，强调了数据质量在大模型持续训练中的关键作用。研究团队呼吁业界将「认知健康检查」纳入模型维护流程，以避免能力长期退化。

分析称 Anthropic 实际成本被严重低估

Recently, British tech writer Ed Zitron pointed out in his latest analysis that Anthropic's actual operating costs are significantly higher than its publicly disclosed figures. 近日，英国科技作家 Ed Zitron 在其最新分析中指出，Anthropic 的实际运营成本远高于其对外披露的数据。

According to public information and industry insiders, Anthropic's spending on Amazon Web Services (AWS) alone in 2024 reached $1.359 billion, while its revenue for the same period was only about $400 million to $600 million, meaning its cloud computing expenses have more than doubled its income. 根据公开资料与业内人士透露，Anthropic 在 2024 年仅在 Amazon Web Services（AWS）上的支出就高达 13.59 亿美元，而同期营收仅约 4 亿至 6 亿美元，意味着其云计算开销已超过收入的两倍。

After entering 2025, the situation did not improve. Data shows that Anthropic's AWS spending accumulated to $2.66 billion between January and September, while revenue during the same period was approximately $2.55 billion, with cloud costs exceeding 100% of revenue. This means that for every dollar of revenue, almost all of it was consumed by cloud computing fees. 进入 2025 年后，情况并未改善。数据显示，Anthropic 在 1 月至 9 月期间的 AWS 支出累计达到 26.6 亿美元，而同期营收约为 25.5 亿美元，云成本占比超过 100%。这意味着公司在每一美元收入中，几乎全部被云计算费用吞噬。

Analysis points out that Anthropic's cost structure shows a linear trend of "higher revenue, higher expenditure," and in addition to AWS, the company also has significant investments in Google Cloud, meaning the actual total cost may far exceed the disclosed figures. As models iterate and user scale expands, its operating expenses continue to climb. 分析指出，Anthropic 的成本结构呈现「收入越高、支出越高」的线性趋势，且除了 AWS，该公司还在 Google Cloud 上有大额投入，实际总成本可能远超已披露的数字。随着模型迭代和用户规模扩大，其运营开销正持续攀升。

Zitron believes that if costs cannot be reduced through price increases or architectural optimization, Anthropic's business model will face huge challenges, potentially even leading to a "subprime AI crisis," which could have a ripple effect on startups relying on large models. Zitron 认为，若无法通过提价或优化架构降低成本，Anthropic 的商业模式将面临巨大挑战，甚至将导致「次贷式 AI 危机」，这可能对依赖大模型的初创公司带来连锁冲击。
