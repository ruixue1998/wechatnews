库克：iPhone 17 产能跟不上需求

According to 9to5Mac, yesterday, Apple CEO Tim Cook stated during the Q4 2025 earnings call that demand for the iPhone 17 series is strong, but production capacity still cannot fully meet market demand. 据 9to5Mac 报道，昨天，苹果 CEO Tim Cook 在 2025 财年第四财季财报电话会上表示，iPhone 17 系列需求强劲，但产能仍未能完全满足市场需求。

Cook pointed out that sales performance of the iPhone 17 and iPhone 17 Pro exceeded expectations, and some consumers may therefore face longer delivery waiting times. 库克指出，iPhone 17 与 iPhone 17 Pro 的销售表现超出预期，部分消费者可能因此面临更长的交付等待时间。

He also mentioned that before the iPhone 17 launch, the iPhone 16 was still in short supply, and the company "could have sold more" devices. The financial report shows that quarterly revenue as of September 27 was $102.47 billion, an 8% year-over-year increase, which only includes the first few days after the iPhone 17's market launch. 他同时提到，在 iPhone 17 发布前，iPhone 16 依旧供不应求，公司「本可以卖出更多」设备。财报数据显示，截至 9 月 27 日的季度收入为 1024.7 亿美元，同比增长 8%，其中仅包含 iPhone 17 上市后的最初几天。

It is worth noting that the iPhone Air was barely mentioned during the call, and its production has been adjusted to "end-of-life" levels, indicating its market performance was below expectations . Cook also explicitly stated that Apple would not disclose specific sales data by model. 值得注意的是，iPhone Air 在电话会上几乎未被提及，产量也已被调整至「生命周期末期」水平，显示其市场表现不及预期 。库克也明确表示，苹果不会按机型披露具体销量数据。

Additionally, Cook also mentioned the progress of AI Siri during this conference call. He stated that Apple plans to launch an upgraded version of Siri next year. 此外，库克还在本次电话会中提到 AI Siri 的进展。他表示，苹果正计划在明年推出升级版的 Siri。

We are making good progress with Siri... If it can advance the company's roadmap, Apple would still consider acquiring other companies. 我们在 Siri 方面进展良好 …… 若能够推动公司的发展路线图，苹果仍然会考虑收购其他公司。

奥特曼晒特斯拉订单：七年了还没交付

Yesterday, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman posted on X, revealing a 7-and-a-half-year long experience of ordering a Tesla Roadster. He showed a screenshot of an email from July 2018, confirming a $45,000 deposit payment, and revealed that when he recently tried to cancel the order and request a refund of $50,000, the email was returned as undeliverable. 昨天，OpenAI CEO Sam Altman 在 X 发帖，公开了一段长达 7 年半的特斯拉 Roadster 订车经历。他展示了 2018 年 7 月支付 45000 美元订金的邮件截图，并透露近日尝试取消订单、要求退回五万美元时，邮件被退回未送达。

Altman stated bluntly in the post: "I'm really looking forward to this car! I understand there will be delays. But 7 and a half years of waiting is just too long." 奥尔特曼在帖子中直言：「我真的很期待这辆车！我理解会有延迟。但 7 年半的等待实在太漫长了。」

The new Roadster was once one of Tesla's most anticipated models. In 2017, Tesla CEO Elon Musk claimed: "The Roadster will be the fastest production car ever," emphasizing its 0-96km/h acceleration could break the two-second record. 新款 Roadster 曾是特斯拉最受期待的车型之一。2017 年，特斯拉 CEO Elon Musk 曾宣称：「Roadster 将成为有史以来最快的量产车」，并强调其 0 – 96km/h 加速可突破两秒纪录。

However, the model, originally scheduled for mass production in 2020, has been repeatedly delayed. Musk stated in the October 2024 earnings call that the company is finalizing the Roadster's design, but the priority remains advancing the core mission of a "sustainable energy future." He also thanked customers who have been waiting with deposits for a long time. 然而，该车型原定 2020 年量产，却一再跳票。马斯克在 2024 年 10 月财报电话会议中表示，公司正在完成 Roadster 的设计，但优先事项仍是推进「可持续能源未来」的核心使命。他同时感谢长期等待的订金用户。

Musk was an early funder of OpenAI but left the board in 2018 and has since repeatedly criticized Altman's leadership. In 2024, Musk sued OpenAI twice and filed an injunction in November, attempting to prevent it from becoming a for-profit company. An OpenAI spokesperson responded by stating that the allegations were "completely unfounded." 马斯克曾是 OpenAI 的早期资助者，但 2018 年退出董事会，此后多次批评 Altman 的领导。2024 年，马斯克先后两次对 OpenAI 提起诉讼，并在 11 月申请禁令，试图阻止其转为盈利公司。OpenAI 发言人则回应称相关指控「完全没有依据」。

存在电池热失控隐患，理想召回万辆 MEGA

Yesterday, the State Administration for Market Regulation announced that Beijing Li Auto Co., Ltd. will recall a total of 11,411 units of its Li MEGA 2024 electric vehicles, starting from November 7, 2025. 国家市场监督管理总局昨日发布公告，北京理想汽车有限公司将自 2025 年 11 月 7 日起召回部分理想 MEGA 2024 款电动汽车，共计 11411 辆。

It is understood that this recall is a proactive recall, involving vehicles produced between February 18, 2024, and December 27, 2024. 据悉，此次召回为主动召回，涉及生产日期为 2024 年 2 月 18 日至 2024 年 12 月 27 日的车辆。

Official statements indicate that the coolant in this batch of vehicles has insufficient anti-corrosion properties, which under specific conditions, may lead to corrosion and leakage in the power battery and front motor controller cooling aluminum plates, subsequently causing fault lights to illuminate, power limitations, or even inability to power on. In extreme cases, there is a safety risk of thermal runaway in the power battery . 官方说明显示，该批次车辆冷却液防腐性能不足，在特定条件下可能导致动力电池和前电机控制器冷却铝板腐蚀渗漏，进而引发故障灯点亮、动力受限甚至无法上电。 极端情况下，存在动力电池热失控的安全隐患 。

Li Auto stated that it will free of charge replace the coolant, power battery, and front motor controller for vehicles within the recall scope. Regarding the risk of coolant leakage, the vehicle's cloud-based early warning program can provide advance alerts, and users will be contacted and rescue arranged through channels such as 400 customer service and in-car Ecall. If a vehicle experiences a fault, the system will alert the user to pull over and await rescue via fault lights, text, and alarm sounds. 理想汽车表示，将为召回范围内车辆免费更换冷却液、动力电池及前电机控制器。针对冷却液渗漏风险，车辆云端预警程序可提前提示，并通过 400 客服、车机 Ecall 等渠道联系用户并安排救援。若车辆出现故障，系统会通过故障灯、文字及报警音提醒用户靠边停车等待救援。

Previously, multiple fire incidents involving the Li MEGA model have occurred. On the night of October 23 this year, a Li MEGA caught fire while driving in Shanghai; fortunately, people escaped in time with no casualties. Earlier in August, another MEGA spontaneously combusted on an elevated bridge in Hangzhou, causing a brief traffic disruption. 此前，理想 MEGA 车型已发生多起起火事故。今年 10 月 23 日夜间，上海一辆理想 MEGA 行驶中起火，所幸人员及时逃离未造成人员伤亡；更早前的 8 月，杭州亦有一辆 MEGA 在高架桥上自燃，导致交通短时中断。

On the day the recall news broke, Li Auto CEO Li Xiang responded on Weibo, stating that this recall was a proactive decision, and the company chose not to wait for accident investigation conclusions after discovering potential hazards, but instead took immediate action. He emphasized that even if the risk probability is extremely low, user safety must be prioritized. 召回消息传出当天，理想汽车 CEO 李想在微博回应称，此次召回属于主动决策，公司在发现潜在隐患后选择不等待事故调查结论，而是立即采取措施。他强调，即便风险概率极低，也必须优先保障用户安全。

美的回应人车家融合战略：不是效仿小米

According to Sina Tech, yesterday, at Midea Group's 30th Technology Month and 3rd Visionaries Conference, Shang Zhe, General Manager of Midea Group's Whole-House Intelligence, proposed the concept of "human-car-home integration" to meet users' cross-space and cross-scenario connection needs. 据新浪科技报道，昨天，在美的集团第 30 届科技月暨第 3 届远见者大会上，美的集团全屋智能总经理尚喆提出「人车家融合」理念，以满足用户跨空间、跨场景的连接需求。

Shang Zhe emphasized in the interview that Midea is not imitating Xiaomi, but rather, based on user research, found that the development of home intelligence is closely related to the popularization of smartphones and smart cars, and users require a unified smart experience across different spaces. 尚喆在采访中强调，美的并非效仿小米，而是基于用户调研发现，家庭智能化的发展与智能手机和智能汽车的普及密切相关，用户在不同空间中需要统一的智能体验。

He pointed out: "In my opinion, an empty 'human-car-home' ecosystem has little value or meaning. We will not make phones or cars; what we want to create is user value." 他指出：「在我看来空洞的『人车家』生态没有太大的价值和意义，我们不会去做手机和汽车，我们要做的是用户的价值。」

According to Midea's official website, Midea Group unveiled its new "Whole-House Intelligence" strategy on August 1st at its Shanghai Global Innovation Park, building a three-in-one intelligent blueprint comprising AI Agent capabilities, an integrated smart home and appliance matrix, and a "human-car-home ecosystem." 根据美的官网信息，美的集团 8 月 1 日在上海全球创新园区发布全新「全屋智能」战略，构建 AI Agent 能力、智能家居·家电融合矩阵及「人·车·家生态」三位一体的智能版图。

This strategy includes refreshing the "Midea Miju" App, launching the Xiaomei AI Home Assistant, supporting remote voice control, scene generation, appliance service appointments, and health management, among other functions. 该战略包括焕新「美的美居」App，推出小美 AI 家庭助手，支持远程语音操控、场景生成、家电服务预约及健康管理等功能。

手机市场迎来旺季，苹果小米表现突出

According to analytics firm Counterpoint Research, the Chinese smartphone market started strong in Q4 2025. 据分析机构 Counterpoint Research 报道，2025 年第四季度中国智能手机市场开局表现强劲。

Data shows that from September 29 to October 12, China's smartphone sales increased by 11% year-on-year, with Apple's iPhone 17 series being the main driver, achieving a 29% year-on-year increase in overall sales, leading the market significantly during the same period . 数据显示，9 月 29 日至 10 月 12 日期间，中国智能手机销量同比增长 11%， 其中苹果 iPhone 17 系列成为主要推动力，整体销量同比增长 29%，在同期市场中遥遥领先 。

Analysis indicates that sales of the iPhone 17 standard version in the first two weeks after launch were almost double that of the iPhone 16 standard version, and the year-on-year increase for the iPhone 17 Pro was nearly two-thirds higher than last year's iPhone 16 Pro. 分析指出，iPhone 17 标准版在上市前两周的销量几乎是 iPhone 16 标准版的两倍，而 iPhone 17 Pro 的同比增幅更是超过去年的 iPhone 16 Pro 近三分之二。

All three models — standard, Pro, and Pro Max — performed remarkably, leading to a 40% overall sales growth for Apple within the aforementioned two weeks. The agency believes that the combination of Mid-Autumn and Golden Week holidays, Double Eleven pre-sales, and user upgrade cycles collectively boosted Apple's sales performance. 三款机型 —— 标准版、Pro 和 Pro Max 均表现亮眼，使得苹果在上述两周内整体销量增长 40%。该机构认为，中秋与黄金周假期叠加、双十一预热以及用户换机周期共同助推了苹果的销售表现。

Following closely behind Apple, Xiaomi also achieved significant growth, with sales increasing by 21% year-on-year . The Xiaomi 17 series gained market favor with its rear screen design and large-capacity battery. Analysts pointed out that Xiaomi's early release strategy caught competitors off guard, becoming a crucial factor in its growth. 紧随苹果后的小米同样实现显著增长，销量同比提升 21% 。小米 17 系列凭借后置屏幕设计和大容量电池获得市场青睐，分析师指出，小米的提前发布策略让竞争对手措手不及，成为其增长的重要因素。

Furthermore, yesterday, Zhou Yibao, product head of OPPO Find series, posted on Weibo stating that the OPPO Find X9 series officially rolled off the production line with 1 million units in just 10 days. 此外，昨天 OPPO Find 系列产品负责人周意保在微博发文表示，OPPO Find X9 系列仅在 10 天内已正式下线 100 万台。

亚马逊裁员 14000 人，CEO：与成本和 AI 无关

According to Business Insider, Amazon laid off 14,000 people this week. CEO Andy Jassy responded for the first time on yesterday's earnings call, stating that the layoffs were not due to cost control or AI-driven factors, but rather considerations of corporate culture. 据《商业内幕》报道，亚马逊本周裁员 14000 人。CEO Andy Jassy 在昨日的财报电话会议上首次回应称，此次裁员并非出于成本控制或人工智能驱动，而是源于企业文化的考量。

Jassy stated: 'The layoffs we announced a few days ago were not driven by financial factors, nor were they driven by AI, at least not yet. The real reason is culture.' He emphasized that Amazon's rapid expansion in recent years led to increased hierarchies and decreased decision-making efficiency, thus requiring streamlining to improve execution. Jassy 表示：「几天前我们宣布的裁员并不是由财务因素推动的，也不是由 AI 推动的，至少目前不是。真正的原因是文化。」他强调，亚马逊近年来的快速扩张导致层级增加、决策效率下降，因此需要通过精简来提升执行力。

Beth Galetti, Amazon's Senior Vice President of People Experience and Technology, noted in a Tuesday blog post that despite the company's solid performance, organizational structures must be adjusted in the context of AI rapidly reshaping the industry. 亚马逊人力体验与技术高级副总裁 Beth Galetti 在周二的博客文章中指出，尽管公司业绩稳健，但在 AI 快速重塑行业的背景下，组织结构必须调整。

This generation of AI is the most transformative technology since the internet, enabling companies to innovate at an unprecedented pace. 这一代 AI 是自互联网以来最具变革性的技术，使企业能够以前所未有的速度创新。

These layoffs are Amazon's largest since cutting 27,000 jobs in 2022. The company expects related severance costs to be approximately $1.8 billion. Jassy emphasized that during the critical period of AI transformation, Amazon must remain lean and efficient to prevent bureaucracy from slowing down the pace of innovation. 此次裁员是亚马逊自 2022 年裁减 27000 个岗位以来规模最大的一次。公司预计相关遣散费用约为 18 亿美元。Jassy 强调，在 AI 转型的关键时期，亚马逊必须保持精简和高效，以避免官僚化拖慢创新步伐。

Google：安卓用户收到的垃圾短信比 iOS 少 58%

According to 9to5Google, Google, in a new study conducted in partnership with research firm YouGov, pointed out that Android users significantly outperform Apple iOS users in preventing spam and scam messages. 据 9to5Google 报道，Google 在与调研机构 YouGov 合作的一项最新研究中指出，安卓用户在防范垃圾短信和诈骗短信方面的表现显著优于苹果 iOS 用户。

The survey covered over 5,000 smartphone users in the United States, India, and Brazil. Results showed that in the week prior to the survey, Android users were 58% more likely to avoid receiving scam messages than iOS users. 该调查覆盖美国、印度和巴西超过 5000 名智能手机用户，结果显示，在调查前一周内，安卓用户避免收到诈骗短信的概率比 iOS 用户高出 58%。

The study further indicated that Pixel users showed even more prominent protection, being 96% more likely than iPhone users to not receive any spam or scam messages in a week. Relatively, iOS users were 65% more likely than Android users to report receiving 3 or more scam messages per week. 研究进一步指出，Pixel 用户的防护效果更为突出，其在一周内未收到任何垃圾或诈骗短信的可能性比 iPhone 用户高出 96%。相对而言，iOS 用户报告每周收到 3 条及以上诈骗短信的概率比 Android 用户高出 65%。
