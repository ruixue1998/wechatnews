郭明錤：苹果扶持 Intel 制衡台积电，良率目标 50% 至 60%

Tianfeng International Securities analyst Ming-Chi Kuo disclosed yesterday that Apple has initiated projects for low-end and older iPhone, iPad, and Mac processors using Intel's 18A-P series process, with iPhones accounting for approximately 80% of the orders. 天风国际证券分析师郭明錤昨日披露，苹果已在 Intel 18A-P 系列制程上为低阶及旧款 iPhone、iPad 与 Mac 处理器立项，订单中 iPhone 约占 80%。

The tape-out plan shows that Apple expects to ramp up production at Intel in 2027, continue to increase in 2028, and enter a decline phase in 2029. For Intel, the yield target for 2027 is to stably reach over 50% to 60%, but the mass production schedule and shipment scale are currently unclear. 流片规划显示，苹果预计 2027 年在 Intel 放量，2028 年续增，2029 年进入衰退期。Intel 方面，2027 年良率目标为稳定达到 50% 至 60% 以上，量产时程与出货规模目前仍未明朗。

However, Ming-Chi Kuo also pointed out that even if Intel successfully ships, TSMC will still account for more than 90% of Apple's supply chain. 不过，郭明錤也指出，即便 Intel 顺利出货，台积电在苹果供应体系中仍将占 90% 以上比重。

Ming-Chi Kuo believes that Apple's move is intended to cultivate Intel as a long-term alternative supplier. As AI's demand for TSMC's advanced process resources continues to expand, Apple needs to plan ahead while it still has bargaining power. At the same time, Apple is also evaluating other advanced process technologies from Intel. 郭明錤认为，苹果此举是有意将 Intel 培养为长期备选供应商。AI 对台积电先进制程资源的占用将持续扩大，苹果需要在议价能力尚存时提前布局。与此同时，苹果也在同步评估 Intel 其他先进制程技术。

According to WIRED, OpenAI announced a new round of organizational adjustments to employees yesterday, with co-founder and president Greg Brockman officially taking over product strategy. 据 WIRED 报道，OpenAI 昨日向员工宣布新一轮组织调整，联合创始人兼总裁 Greg Brockman 正式接管产品战略。

微信捐赠第一行代码和测试用机，入藏 CCF 计算机博物馆

The CCF Computer Museum (CCM) yesterday received a batch of early historical artifacts donated by the Tencent WeChat team, including the original archive of WeChat's first line of backend code, early development equipment such as an iMac ME086CH/21-inch all-in-one and a Dell tower workstation, as well as early testing terminals like the Apple iPod Touch 3 and Nokia 5800XM. CCF 计算机博物馆（CCM）昨日收到腾讯微信团队捐赠的一批早期历史实物，包括微信第一行后台代码原始存档、早期开发设备 iMac ME086CH/21 寸一体机与戴尔塔式工作站，以及 Apple iPod Touch 3、诺基亚 5800XM 等早期测试终端。

CCM stated that due to the extremely fast iteration speed of mobile products and the ease with which digital data can be overwritten and deleted, a large number of physical artifacts witnessing the dawn of the mobile internet have not been systematically protected. This donation showcases WeChat's R&D chain from 0 to 1, serving as a core physical testament to the early development of China's mobile internet. CCM 表示，由于移动产品迭代速度极快、数字资料易被覆盖删除，大量见证移动互联网起步的实物未能得到系统保护。此次捐赠呈现了微信从 0 到 1 的研发链条，是中国移动互联网早期发展的核心实物见证。

The main building of CCM has been fully completed and is scheduled to open in September this year. It is currently continuously soliciting computer-related collections from all periods from the public. CCM 主体建筑已全面落成，拟于今年 9 月开馆，目前持续面向社会征集各时期计算机相关藏品。

These remarks immediately sparked discussion. Some netizens pointed out that he committed the logical fallacy of "appeal to hypocrisy," to which Luo Yonghao strongly retorted, stating that he had clearly admitted that plagiarists are "also rogues," and was not excusing plagiarism. 此番言论随即引发讨论。有网友指其犯了「诉诸伪善」的逻辑错误，罗永浩强硬回怼，称自己已明确承认抄袭者「也是流氓」，并非为抄袭行为开脱。

微信发布「全球青少年 AI+ 小程序」洞察报告

Yesterday, Tencent Research Institute, in conjunction with WeChat, released the "Global Youth AI+ Mini Program Insight Report," disclosing key data on how primary and secondary education groups worldwide utilize AI tools and mini-programs for application development. 昨天，腾讯研究院联合微信发布《全球青少年 AI+小程序洞察报告》，披露了全球基础教育阶段群体利用 AI 工具与小程序进行应用开发的各项核心数据。

Regarding developer profiles, 62% of projects were completed independently by individuals, with the youngest award-winning developer being only 5 years old.
