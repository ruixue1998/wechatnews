17999 元起，华为 Mate XTs 正式发布

Yesterday, Huawei released the world's second mass-produced "tri-fold" phone, the Mate XTs Ultimate Design. The chip has been upgraded to Kirin 9020, and the price is 2000 yuan lower than the first-generation Mate XT. 昨日，华为发布了世界上第二台量产的「三折叠」手机 Mate XTs非凡大师。芯片提升到麒麟 9020，价格比第一代 Mate XT 还降了 2000 元。

It is worth mentioning that Richard Yu, Huawei's Executive Director and CEO of Consumer BG, once again publicly discussed chips at a Huawei press conference: Huawei Mate XTs officially announced to be equipped with Kirin 9020 processor. Industry insiders point out that this sends a clear signal to the outside world: the domestic chip supply chain has achieved full-link independent control. 值得一提的是，华为常务董事、终端 BG 董事长余承东再次在华为发布会上公开谈到了芯片： 华为 Mate XTs 官宣搭载麒麟 9020 处理器。 有业内人士指出，这向外界释放了一个很明确的信号：国产芯片供应链，已经实现全链路自主可控。

In terms of appearance, the new generation Mate XTs Ultimate Design, in addition to adding "Hao Bai" (Bright White) and "Jin Zi" (Hibiscus Purple) color options, has not seen significant changes in the phone's appearance and the overall tri-fold solution compared to the previous generation. Updates mainly focus on some parameters, hinge mechanism, and back cover craftsmanship. 外观方面，全新一代 Mate XTs非凡大师，除了新增「皓白」和「槿紫」两个配色，手机外观和整个三折叠的方案对比上一代都没有太大变动，更新主要集中在一些参数、铰链机制、后盖工艺上。

On the Mate XTs, this time it incorporates the Mate 70's Red Maple Primary Color camera, as well as imaging experiences such as ultra-clear panoramic, personalized color cards, and AI-assisted composition, which were first introduced in the Pura 80 series. 在 Mate XTs 上，这次加入了 Mate 70 的红枫原色摄像头，以及之前首发于 Pura 80 系列的超清全景、个性色卡、AI 辅助构图等影像体验。

The imaging hardware has remained largely unchanged: the previous generation's 13-megapixel wide-angle camera has been upgraded to a 40-megapixel RYYB sensor, while the main camera and telephoto lens remain the same. 影像硬件基本没有改变：前代的 1300 万像素广角摄像头升级 4000 万像素 RYYB 传感器，主摄和长焦保持不变。

In terms of software, Huawei has brought many "PC-level" productivity applications, such as WPS, Wondershare Edraw, and Wind Financial Terminal, to the Mate XTs. It also supports flexible multi-window arrangement, just like a computer. 软件上，华为将不少「PC 级」的生产力应用，如 WPS、万兴绘图、Wind 金融终端等，都下放给 Mate XTs，还支持像电脑一样多窗口灵活排布。

Additionally, the Huawei Mate XTs is equipped with a new M-Pen 3 stylus, priced at 599 yuan. It supports global annotation within the phone, allowing users to write, draw, and quickly activate voice recognition for text transcription. It can even transform into a "remote control": capable of air gestures to turn pages on the Mate XTs, acting as a "laser pointer." 另外，华为 Mate XTs 配备了一支全新手写笔 M-Pen 3，售价 599 元。其支持手机内全局批注，不仅能写能画，还能快速唤起语音识别转文字记录。甚至还可以化身「遥控器」：可以隔空操作 Mate XTs 进行翻页，当做「激光笔」。

苹果自研 AI 搜索曝光

According to Bloomberg's report yesterday, Apple is internally developing an AI search engine codenamed World Knowledge Answers, which it plans to integrate into Siri. 据彭博社昨日报道， 苹果内部正在研发一套代号为 World Knowledge Answers（世界知识问答）的 AI 搜索引擎，准备整合进 Siri 里。

Reportedly, this plan extends beyond Siri; Safari browser and Spotlight features will also utilize this technology in the future. Notably, Apple executives have given this project an impressive name—"Answer Engine"—and it is expected to be released next spring alongside a major Siri upgrade. 据报道，该计划不只是 Siri，Safari 浏览器和 Spotlight 功能以后也都要用上这套技术。值得一提的是，苹果高管们给这个项目起了个挺唬人的名字——「答案引擎」，预计明年春天就要和 Siri 的重大升级一起发布。

Specifically, Apple's proposed AI search engine aims to give Siri not only full web search capabilities but also, similar to ChatGPT and Google's AI Overviews, directly integrate text, images, videos, and local information, along with an AI summarization system. 具体来看，苹果所推的这一 AI 搜索引擎要让 Siri 不仅具备全网搜索能力，同时跟 ChatGPT、Google 的 AI 概览一个路数，直接整合文字、图片、视频和本地信息，还配个 AI 总结系统。

According to sources, some of the underlying technology supporting the new Siri may come from Apple's long-standing partner, Google. The two companies reached a formal agreement this week for Apple to evaluate and test AI models developed by Google to enhance Siri. 据消息人士称， 支撑新版 Siri 的底层技术，部分可能来自苹果的老合作伙伴 Google。 两家公司本周达成了正式协议，苹果将评估并测试 Google 开发的 AI 模型来增强 Siri。

The report mentioned that Apple's internal technical overhaul of Siri has two codenames: Linwood and LLM Siri. Craig Federighi, who is in charge of software engineering, recently stated in an internal meeting that this upgrade will exceed expectations: "This end-to-end Siri transformation has given us the desired results, not only fulfilling previous promises but also bringing about more significant improvements." 报道中提到，苹果内部给 Siri 的技术改造起了两个代号：Linwood 和 LLM Siri。负责软件工程的 Craig Federighi 最近在内部会议上表示，这次升级会超出预期：「这次端到端的 Siri 改造让我们拿到了想要的成果，不仅能兑现之前的承诺，还能带来更大幅度的提升。」

In addition, Apple is also preparing to completely reconstruct Siri, expected to be based on three core components: a planner, a search system, and a summarizer. Unlike previous complete in-house development, Apple is also beginning to consider introducing third-party models this time, with the project codename Glenwood. 另外， 苹果还准备完全重构 Siri，预计基于三个核心组件： 规划器、搜索系统和总结器。与以往完全自研不同，这次苹果同样开始考虑引入第三方模型，项目代号叫 Glenwood。

Early this morning, Xiao Hong, founder of Butterfly Effect / Manus, posted on 'Jike' commenting on the above event: "Dia / Arc has been acquired by Atlassian, which might be its best outcome." 今日凌晨，蝴蝶效应 / Manus 创始人肖弘在「即刻」发文评价了上述事件：「Dia / Arc 被 Atlassian 收购了，这可能是它最好的结局了。」

Xiao Hong stated that new technologies are better suited for defense when applied to old categories. He pointed out the problems with old categories: 肖弘表示，新技术用在老品类更适合做防御。其指出老品类的问题：

If the innovation is not significant, users' motivation to switch is not high enough, and simple innovations are easily integrated by large companies or covered by the plugin ecosystem. However, if the innovation is very significant, the user's switching cost becomes very high. 如果创新不大，用户切换的动力不够高，简单的创新也容易被大公司集成或者被插件生态覆盖。而如果创新很大，用户的切换成本就很高。

In this regard, he believes that if one wants to be a browser company, "either be very good at marketing, like Arc making videos, or Perplexity attracting free attention by acquiring Chrome as a gimmick." "Or do To B (business-to-business)." 对此他认为，若想做一家浏览器公司，「要么很会营销，Arc 是拍视频，Perplexity 通过收购 Chrome 当噱头吸引免费关注。」「要么做 To B（面向企业）」。

At the same time, Xiao Hong also stated, "We (Butterfly Effect) will not do marketing, nor will we do 'To B', and we were not founded to be acquired." Figure 的机器人又学新技能：整理餐具

Recently, robot company Figure, after demonstrating 'robots can fold clothes' in August, once again announced a new skill for its humanoid robot VLA (Vision-Language-Action) model 'Helix': neatly placing dishes and glasses into a dishwasher. 近期，机器人公司 Figure 继 8 月展示了「机器人能叠衣服」后，再一次公布了其人形机器人 VLA（视觉-语言-行动）模型「Helix」的一项新技能：将碗碟、玻璃杯整齐放进洗碗机中。

Similar to the 'folding clothes' period, Figure also stated that 'placing dishes into a dishwasher' is not an easy task for robots: 与「叠衣服」时期一样，Figure 同样表示「将碗碟放入洗碗机」对于机器人来说并非易事：

Utensils usually need to be separated from messy stacks and repositioned, or transferred between two simultaneously operating robotic arms; furthermore, the slipperiness and fragility of utensils require fingertip-level precision; placing them into the dishwasher is also not simple: the machine rack only provides a centimeter-level tolerance range. 餐具通常要从杂乱的堆叠中分离，并且进行重新定位，亦或者需要在两个同时运作的机械臂之间传递餐具；并且餐具的湿滑、易碎需要指尖级别的精准操作；放进洗碗机也不是简单事：机器架子只提供了厘米级别的容错范围。

Additionally, the robot might pick up something new each time. 另外，机器人每次拿取的东西都有可能是新的。

For robots equipped with Helix, it can achieve: 对于搭载 Helix 的机器人来讲，它能够实现：

  • Loading individually stacked plates in order; 单独堆叠的盘子按照顺序进行装载；
  • Picking up a single glass with one hand, adjusting its orientation, and then placing it with the other hand; 用一只手抓起单个玻璃杯并且调整方向，再用另一只手进行放置；
  • Able to adjust strategies to cope with cluttered environments; 能够调整策略以应对杂乱的环境；
  • Gracefully recover from erroneous operations (e.g., misgrabbing or collisions). 优雅地从错误操作中恢复（例如抓错或者碰撞了）。

Figure states that while tasks like organizing tableware, sorting logistics packages, and folding clothes are different, Helix can handle them all with the same general architecture, marking another step forward for humanoid robots: Figure 表示，整理餐具跟整理物流包裹、叠衣服等任务虽然不同，但通过 Helix 却能用同一种通用架构来处理以上任务，而这也标志着人形机器人迈出了又一步：

A single system can progressively learn new functions through additional data, enabling broader applications without special design. 一个单一系统可以通过额外数据逐步学习新功能，无需特殊设计即可实现更广泛的应用。

💡 AI 教父辛顿：重审 AI 被遗忘的关键路径

Recently, the full text of Nobel laureate Geoffrey Hinton's speech "Boltzmann Machines" delivered at the 2024 Nobel Prize ceremony was officially published in the authoritative journal "Reviews of Modern Physics". In the speech, he systematically reviewed a key but almost forgotten technical path in the history of artificial intelligence development. 近日，诺贝尔奖得主杰弗里·辛顿（Geoffrey Hinton）在 2024 年诺贝尔颁奖典礼上发表的演讲《玻尔兹曼机》全文，正式刊登于权威期刊《现代物理学评论》。演讲中， 他系统性地回顾了人工智能发展史上的一条关键但几乎被遗忘的技术路线。

Hinton pointed out that in the 1980s, alongside today's ubiquitous "backpropagation" algorithm, there was the "Boltzmann machine" based on physical principles. He emphasized that this model's learning mechanism does not rely on complex error signal feedback, but rather through an elegant "wake-sleep" cycle. "You are essentially teaching the network: to believe what it sees when awake, and not to believe what it dreams when asleep," thereby allowing the model's internal "imagination" to align with external reality. 辛顿指出，在 1980 年代，与今天无处不在的「反向传播」算法并存的，是基于物理学思想的「玻尔兹曼机」。他强调，这一模型的学习机制并非依赖复杂的误差信号回传，而是通过一个优雅的「清醒-睡眠」循环。「你本质上是在教导网络：要相信清醒时所见，而不信睡梦中所梦」，通过这种方式让模型的内部「想象」与外部现实达成一致。

When discussing this historical turning point, Hinton shared how "Boltzmann machines" evolved and ultimately became a key catalyst for igniting the deep learning revolution. 在谈及这段历史的转折时，辛顿分享了「玻尔兹曼机」如何演化并最终成为引爆深度学习革命的关键催化剂。

He believes that it was the technological breakthroughs along this path that enabled deep neural networks to achieve decisive success in areas such as speech recognition around 2012. He vividly compared this process to, "Boltzmann machines are like a 'historical enzyme' that catalyzed the birth of deep learning, and once the reaction is complete, the enzyme itself is no longer needed." 他认为，正是这一路线的技术突破，才使得深度神经网络在 2012 年前后于语音识别等领域取得决定性成功。他将此过程生动地比喻为，「玻尔兹曼机就像一种‘历史的酶’，它催化了深度学习的诞生，而一旦反应完成，酶本身也就不再被需要了。」

Looking to the future, Hinton expressed a deep belief in this "outdated" technology. He believes that backpropagation is difficult to explain biologically, and the learning process represented by the "sleep" phase of Boltzmann machines may be closer to the actual working mechanism of the brain. He firmly believes: 在展望未来时，辛顿表达了对这一「过时」技术的深刻信念。他认为，反向传播在生物学上难以解释，而玻尔兹曼机的「睡眠」阶段所代表的学习过程，可能更接近大脑真实的运作机制。他坚信：

"When we one day figure out how the brain learns, we will surely discover that the learning mechanism during sleep is a crucial part." 「当我们有一天搞明白大脑如何学习时，一定会发现，睡眠中的学习机制是关键一环。」
