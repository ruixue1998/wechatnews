融了 1220 亿，OpenAI CFO 却担心付不起算力账单

According to The Wall Street Journal, OpenAI failed to achieve several key internal goals this year, with company executives divided over its aggressive computing power expansion strategy, and its IPO plans for the year also facing uncertainty. 据《华尔街日报》报道，OpenAI 今年未能完成多项关键内部目标，公司高层对其激进的算力扩张策略产生分歧，年内 IPO 计划也面临不确定性。

OpenAI failed to reach its goal of 1 billion weekly active ChatGPT users by the end of last year, and its annual revenue also fell short — the strong rise of Google Gemini snatched a large market share. Since the beginning of this year, OpenAI has continued to be eroded by Anthropic in the programming and enterprise markets, with several monthly revenue targets successively missed. OpenAI 去年底未能实现 ChatGPT 周活跃用户突破 10 亿的目标，年度营收同样落空 —— Google Gemini 的强势崛起抢走了大量市场份额。今年以来，OpenAI 又在编程和企业市场持续被 Anthropic 蚕食，多个月度营收目标相继未达。

This led company CFO Sarah Friar to publicly express concern: If revenue cannot keep up, OpenAI may be unable to pay for its signed computing power contracts . 这让公司 CFO Sarah Friar 公开表达担忧： 若营收跟不上，OpenAI 可能无力支付已签下的算力合同 。

Last year, CEO Sam Altman locked the company's future spending commitments at approximately $600 billion through a series of deals. Even though OpenAI just completed Silicon Valley's largest funding round ever — $122 billion — at its current burn rate, it is expected to run out within three years. CEO 山姆 · 奥特曼去年通过一系列交易，将公司未来支出承诺锁定在约 6000 亿美元。即便 OpenAI 刚刚完成硅谷史上最大融资轮 —— 1220 亿美元，按当前烧钱速度，预计三年内也将耗尽。

Regarding the IPO, Friar believes the company's internal control system is not yet ready for listing, while Altman is inclined to accelerate the process. 百度职级体系大调整，5 月 1 日起启用数字级别制度

According to reports from the STAR Market Daily and Yicai, Baidu yesterday issued an all-staff letter announcing a major adjustment to its job ranking system. 据科创板日报、第一财经报道，百度昨日发布全员信，宣布对职级体系进行重大调整。

This reform will fully integrate the professional and management tracks below the middle management level, unifying the original job ranking letter labels (T, P, E, Band, M) into a digital job ranking system from Level 5 to Level 12, which will officially take effect on May 1st this year. 此次改革将中管层以下的专业通道与管理通道全面打通，原有职级字母标签（T、P、E、Band、M）统一整合为 5 级至 12 级的数字职级体系，并于今年 5 月 1 日正式生效。

Baidu stated in its all-staff letter that the AI era requires composite talents who possess both professional and leadership skills and can stand alone. 百度在全员信中表示，AI 时代需要的是同时具备专业力和领导力、能独当一面的复合型人才。

The company decided to break down existing institutional barriers, merging professional and management tracks into a single career development path, allowing employees' growth to no longer be limited by tracks and labels, and to reserve more composite leading talents capable of fighting tough battles for the company. 公司决定打破现有机制性壁垒，将专业通道与管理通道合并为单一职业发展通道，让员工的成长不再受通道和标签的限制，为公司储备更多能打硬仗的复合型领军人才。

Regarding talent selection standards, Baidu proposed two core requirements: a youthful mindset and good use of first-principles thinking. Baidu stated that it should actively embrace outstanding young people emerging in the new era, proactively provide them with opportunities to take on major responsibilities, and accelerate their growth through practical experience. 在人才选用标准上，百度提出两个核心要求：有年轻心态、善用第一性原理思考。百度表示，要积极拥抱新时代涌现的优秀年轻人，主动为他们提供挑大梁的机会，在实战中加速成长。

曝索尼 PS4/5 数字版游戏新增「30 天联网验证」机制

According to GameSpot, Sony has reportedly introduced a "30-day mandatory online verification" mechanism for PS5 and PS4 digital games, affecting all digital games purchased from the PlayStation Store after March this year. 据 GameSpot 报道，索尼被曝在 PS5 及 PS4 数字版游戏中引入「30 天强制联网验证」机制，所有在今年 3 月之后于 PlayStation Store 新购的数字版游戏均受影响。

If the console fails to connect to Sony's servers for 30 consecutive days, the system will temporarily revoke the game's digital license, preventing players from continuing to play. 若主机连续 30 天未与索尼服务器建立连接，系统将暂时撤回该游戏的数字授权，玩家无法继续游玩。

Gaming accessibility test account "Does It Play?" first disclosed this change on X. Tests showed that a "remaining time" countdown has been added to the affected PS4 game interface, visually displaying the remaining days until the next mandatory online connection; 游戏无障碍测试账号「Does It Play?」在 X 上率先披露这一改变。测试显示，受影响的 PS4 游戏界面中新增了「剩余时间」倒计时，直观呈现距下次必须联网的剩余天数；

Although this countdown is not displayed on PS5, the relevant timer still runs in the background, and an error code will be thrown directly after timeout. It is worth noting that this restriction cannot be bypassed by enabling the "Primary Console" feature. PS5 上虽未显示该倒计时，但相关计时仍在后台运行，超时后将直接抛出错误代码。值得注意的是，该限制无法通过开启「常用主机」功能绕过。

Mod developer Lance McDonald further confirmed these findings, pointing out that Sony has triggered new DRM (Digital Rights Management) restrictions in newly purchased digital games on the PlayStation Store. Mod 开发者 Lance McDonald 进一步证实了上述发现，指出索尼在 PlayStation Store 的新购数字版游戏中触发了新的 DRM（数字版权管理）限制。

According to IT Home, Sony PS customer service confirmed that this mechanism is an intentional official setting. Since the system update took effect in March this year, all digital games newly purchased by users on the PlayStation Store are subject to this policy — the console must connect to the internet at least once every 30 days to complete genuine license verification. 另据 IT 之家报道，索尼 PS 客服确认，该机制系官方有意设定，自今年 3 月系统更新生效后，用户在 PlayStation Store 新购的所有数字版游戏均受该政策约束 —— 主机须至少每隔 30 天连接一次互联网，以完成正版授权验证。

微软 x 浙江大学团队发布 World-R1 训练框架：用强化学习治好视频生成的「几何幻觉」

Yesterday, a joint team from Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) and Zhejiang University released World-R1, a text-to-video model training framework. It uses reinforcement learning to teach text-to-video models 3D geometric consistency, without modifying the model architecture or relying on 3D datasets. 昨天，微软研究院（MSRA）与浙江大学联合团队发布文生视频模型训练框架 World-R1，通过强化学习让文生视频模型学会 3D 几何一致性，无需修改模型架构，不依赖 3D 数据集。

Current video generation models often suffer from "geometric hallucination" issues, such as object deformation or disappearance, during significant camera movements. According to the paper published by the team, World-R1's solution is to convert 3D consistency constraints into reinforcement learning reward signals: 当前视频生成模型在镜头大幅移动时，常出现物体变形、消失等「几何幻觉」问题。根据团队发布的论文，World-R1 的解法是将 3D 一致性约束转化为强化学习的奖励信号：

  • After generating the video, use Depth Anything 3 to reconstruct the 3DGS scene and render from new viewpoints; 生成视频后，用 Depth Anything 3 重建 3DGS 场景并从新视角渲染；
  • Combine reconstruction error, camera trajectory deviation, and new viewpoint credibility (Qwen3-VL score) into a reward signal; 将重建误差、镜头轨迹偏差、新视角可信度（Qwen3-VL 评分）合并为奖励信号；
  • Feed back to the video model through the Flow-GRPO algorithm, allowing it to gradually internalize 3D rules; 通过 Flow-GRPO 算法反馈给视频模型，令其逐步内化 3D 规律；
  • A round of "dynamic fine-tuning" is inserted every 100 training steps to prevent geometric constraints from suppressing dynamic effects such as character movement. 每训练 100 步插入一轮「动态微调」，防止几何约束压制人物运动等动态效果。

The base model is the open-source Wanxiang Wan 2.1 (1.3B and 14B), with training data consisting of only about 3000 pure text prompts generated by Gemini. 基座模型为开源的万象 Wan 2.1（1.3B 和 14B），训练数据仅约 3000 条由 Gemini 生成的纯文本 prompt。

In terms of quantization results, 3D consistency (PSNR) improved by 10.23dB for the Small version and 7.91dB for the Large version, with VBench general video quality also improving. In a blind test of 25 people, geometric consistency had a 92% win rate, and overall preference had an 86% win rate. 量化结果上，3D 一致性（PSNR）Small 版提升 10.23dB、Large 版提升 7.91dB，VBench 通用视频质量同步提升。25 人盲测中，几何一致性胜率 92%，整体偏好胜率 86%。
