Gurman：苹果想用眼镜复制 Apple Watch 的成功

Last night, Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman revealed in the latest PowerOn newsletter that Apple is planning to enter the eyewear market "in the same way it entered the smartwatch market," targeting the broader traditional eyewear market rather than just competing with smart eyewear manufacturers like Meta. 昨晚，彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 在最新的 PowerOn 新闻通讯中透露，苹果正计划以「进入智能手表市场的方式」切入眼镜领域，将目标对准更广泛的传统眼镜市场，而非仅与 Meta 等智能眼镜厂商竞争。

Apple's smart glasses, internally codenamed N50, were originally planned for release by the end of this year and launch early next year, but have now been postponed until the end of next year . Apple is expected to target the $200 to $500 price range, competing with traditional eyewear brands such as Essilor, Safilo Group, and Warby Parker. 苹果智能眼镜内部代号 N50，原计划今年底发布、明年初上市， 目前已推迟至明年底 。苹果预计瞄准 200 至 500 美元价格区间，与依视路、Safilo 集团及 Warby Parker 等传统眼镜品牌竞争。

Bloomberg, citing World Health Organization data, stated that the global eyewear market has an annual size of about $200 billion, far exceeding the watch market corresponding to the Apple Watch. The report also mentioned that Apple CEO Tim Cook is one of the project's most steadfast supporters, with people close to him calling smart glasses a "priority" during his tenure. 彭博社援引世界卫生组织数据称，全球眼镜市场年规模约 2000 亿美元，远超苹果手表所对应的腕表市场。报道还提到，苹果 CEO Tim Cook 是这一项目最坚定的支持者之一，接近他的人士称智能眼镜是其任期内的「优先事项」。

John Ternus, who will take over as CEO in September, is also deeply involved in the project. The Vision Products Group (VPG) team, responsible for R&D, has been operating under his leadership for the past two years. The first generation of Apple Glasses will feature an oval camera design and offer a variety of colors and frame styles. 即将于 9 月接任 CEO 的 John Ternus 同样深度参与该项目，负责研发的 Vision Products Group（VPG）团队过去两年一直在他的领导下运作。首代苹果眼镜将采用椭圆形摄像头设计，提供多种颜色与镜框风格。

Regarding other product developments, the report states that the hardware for the new Apple TV set-top box and HomePod mini was completed months ago and is currently being used by Apple employees at headquarters. Both products are awaiting the readiness of new Siri and Apple Intelligence features this autumn for a simultaneous launch. 其他产品进展方面，报道称新款 Apple TV 机顶盒与 HomePod mini 的硬件已完成数月，目前正在苹果总部员工中使用，两款产品均等待今秋新版 Siri 与 Apple Intelligence 功能就绪后同步推出。

In addition, Apple has initiated early development work for next year's iOS 28 and macOS 28, internally codenamed "Bell" and "Poppy" respectively, collectively known as "Boppy." 此外，苹果已启动明年 iOS 28 与 macOS 28 的早期开发工作，内部代号分别为「Bell」与「Poppy」，合称「Boppy」。

Gurman reiterated that iOS 27 primarily focuses on minor fixes and improvements, while iOS 28 will be "more important than iOS 27." 古尔曼重申，iOS 27 主要聚焦于小修小补，而 iOS 28 将会「比 iOS 27 更加重要」。

曝三星与 OpenAI 定制 AI 芯片项目停滞

According to Korean media Greened, Samsung and OpenAI's custom AI chip development project has stalled due to strategic differences. The project originally planned to develop an inference-oriented NPU for OpenAI based on the ARM architecture, with early development already showing progress. 据韩媒 Greened 报道，三星与 OpenAI 的定制 AI 芯片研发项目因战略分歧陷入停滞。该项目原计划基于 ARM 架构，为 OpenAI 研发一款面向推理的 NPU，前期研发已有进展。

Samsung and OpenAI signed multiple cooperation agreements last year, with Samsung SDS collaborating with OpenAI to build AI data centers, and Samsung potentially supplying memory chips to OpenAI. Following the stagnation of the custom chip project, Samsung's recent investment in Anthropic is interpreted by outsiders as another AI client strategy. Korean media also reported that Samsung might turn to manufacturing AI chips for Anthropic. 三星和 OpenAI 去年签署多项合作协议，三星数据系统公司将与 OpenAI 合作建设 AI 数据中心，三星也可能向 OpenAI 供应存储芯片。定制芯片项目停滞后，三星近期投资 Anthropic 的动作被外界解读为另一条 AI 客户路线，韩媒还称三星可能转向为 Anthropic 代工 AI 芯片。

Apple Music 出现全球性服务中断

Apple's official "System Status" page showed that Apple Music experienced a service outage at 23:40 Beijing time on May 29, with some users encountering intermittent connection, playback, or service access issues during use. 苹果官网「系统状态」页面显示，Apple Music 于北京时间 5 月 29 日 23:40 出现服务中断，部分用户在使用过程中遭遇间歇性连接、播放或服务访问异常。

Su Weijie's research interests include statistical machine learning, high-dimensional inference, large-scale multiple testing, optimization, and privacy-preserving data analysis. The Wharton Statistics and Data Science Department page shows that he was previously an associate professor of statistics and data science, and also an adjunct associate professor of computer and information science. 苏炜杰的研究方向包括统计机器学习、高维推断、大规模多重检验、优化和隐私保护数据分析。沃顿统计与数据科学系页面显示，他此前为统计与数据科学副教授，并兼任计算机与信息科学副教授。

OpenAI President Greg Brockman replied "Welcome to the team" under the relevant post. Su Weijie graduated from Peking University's School of Mathematical Sciences with a bachelor's degree, then went to Stanford University for his Ph.D. in statistics, with research interests covering statistics, machine learning optimization, and theoretical safety of large language models. OpenAI 总裁 Greg Brockman 在相关帖文下回复「欢迎加入团队」。苏炜杰本科毕业于北京大学数学科学学院，后赴斯坦福大学攻读统计学博士，研究方向涵盖统计学、机器学习优化及大语言模型理论安全。

This year, he received the COPSS Presidents' Award, one of the highest honors in the field of international statistics. 今年，他获得考普斯会长奖（COPSS Presidents’ Award），这是国际统计学领域的最高荣誉之一。

算力用电年均新增或超 1000 亿度

According to data from the National Energy Administration, by 2025, China has built 42 intelligent computing clusters with a capacity of 10,000 cards each. The total electricity consumption of national computing centers reached 170 billion kilowatt-hours, accounting for 1.6% of the total electricity consumption of society. The average growth rate of electricity consumption for computing in the 8 major hub nodes of the national integrated computing network has been approximately 39.5% in the past three years, higher than the growth rate of total societal electricity consumption. 国家能源局数据显示，2025 年我国已建成 42 个万卡级智算集群，全国算力中心总用电量达 1700 亿千瓦时，占全社会用电量 1.6%。全国一体化算力网络 8 大枢纽节点算力用电近 3 年平均增速约 39.5%，高于全社会用电量增速。

The National Energy Administration estimates that during the "15th Five-Year Plan" period, the national electricity consumption for computing will increase by over 100 billion kilowatt-hours annually, reaching approximately 800 billion kilowatt-hours by 2030, accounting for about 6% of total societal electricity consumption. 国家能源局预计，「十五五」时期全国算力用电量年均新增 1000 亿千瓦时以上，到 2030 年达到 8000 亿千瓦时左右，占全社会用电量约 6%。

The urban samples for data center electricity consumption are already evident. From May 1st to 27th, Shenzhen's data center industry's overall electricity consumption was 273 million kilowatt-hours, a year-on-year increase of 20.12%; electricity consumption by data centers in Guangming District increased by 85.31% year-on-year. In the first four months of this year, Guangdong's total societal electricity consumption was 283.797 billion kilowatt-hours, a year-on-year increase of 8.77%, of which 307 data centers consumed 3.805 billion kilowatt-hours, a year-on-year increase of 18.48%. 数据中心用电的城市样本已经很明显。5 月 1 日至 27 日，深圳数据中心行业整体用电量 2.73 亿千瓦时，同比增长 20.12%；光明区数据中心用电量同比增长 85.31%。广东今年前 4 个月全社会用电量 2837.97 亿千瓦时，同比增长 8.77%，其中 307 家数据中心用电量 38.05 亿千瓦时，同比增长 18.48%。

According to CCTV News, in the early hours of yesterday, China successfully launched a satellite internet technology experimental satellite into its predetermined orbit using a Long March 2D carrier rocket at the Xichang Satellite Launch Center. 据央视新闻，昨日凌晨，我国在西昌卫星发射中心使用长征二号丁运载火箭，成功将卫星互联网技术试验卫星送入预定轨道。

💡 纽约联储主席：AI 正在改变工作，但经济学家岗位仍安全

According to Bloomberg, at the annual economics conference in Reykjavik, Iceland, New York Fed President John Williams gave a clear response to concerns about AI's impact on employment, becoming the most optimistic Fed official at the meeting. 据彭博社报道，在冰岛雷克雅未克举行的经济学年会上，纽约联储主席约翰·威廉姆斯对 AI 冲击就业的担忧给出了明确回应，成为本次会议中立场最为乐观的联储官员。

Williams drew laughter from the audience with the remark, "The demand for macroeconomists remains strong," and then clarified his core judgment: the productivity gains brought by AI will not lead to long-term structural unemployment. 威廉姆斯以一句「宏观经济学家的需求依然旺盛」引发听众笑声，随即阐明他的核心判断：AI 带来的生产率提升不会导致长期结构性失业。

History tells us that productivity can continuously increase, and living standards can steadily rise, without causing structural unemployment. I do not believe we will face long-term structural unemployment. 历史告诉我们，生产率可以不断提高、生活水平可以持续上升，而不会造成结构性失业。我不相信我们会面临长期的结构性失业。

He believes that AI can accelerate data processing and preliminary analysis, but economic judgment still requires identifying causal relationships, understanding institutional contexts, and placing model conclusions within a policy framework. 他认为，AI 可以加速数据处理和初步分析，但经济学判断仍需要识别因果关系、理解制度背景，并把模型结论放进政策语境。

He also pointed out that the main adjustment path for the labor market is retraining and skill upgrading, rather than large-scale layoffs. He specifically mentioned that current university graduates have generally integrated AI tools into their daily work, and this generation of the workforce is already ahead of their predecessors in adaptability. 他同时指出，劳动力市场的主要调整路径是再培训与技能升级，而非大规模裁员。他特别提到，当前高校应届毕业生已普遍将 AI 工具融入日常工作，这一代劳动力在适应能力上已领先于前辈。

People hired straight out of college today are already using AI tools daily. 今天走出校园被录用的人，已经在每天使用 AI 工具了。

Beike Finance reported that Guizhou, Hubei, Shaanxi, and other regions have recently successively promoted adjustments to the time-of-use electricity pricing mechanism, with fixed peak and off-peak periods beginning to loosen. Han Fang, Deputy Director of the Planning and Development Department of the China Electricity Council, stated that this marks a continued progression of the electricity pricing mechanism from "plan-led" to "market-formed." 贝壳财经报道称，贵州、湖北、陕西等地近期相继推进分时电价机制调整，固定峰谷时段开始松动。中电联规划发展部副主任韩放表示，这标志着电价形成机制从「计划主导」向「市场形成」继续推进。

Beike Finance mentioned that after the rapid growth of new energy installations like wind and solar power, the traditional practice of dividing peak and off-peak periods based on fixed load curves has become difficult to adapt to the new power system. 贵州 5 月 16 日发布的征求意见稿在非冬季新增中午「谷电」时段，并将峰谷价差扩大到 120%。贝壳财经提到，风电、光伏等新能源装机快速增长后，传统以固定负荷曲线划分峰谷时段的做法，已经难以适配新型电力系统。
