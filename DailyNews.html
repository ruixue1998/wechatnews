苹果家庭硬件负责人离职，加入智能戒指公司 Oura

According to Bloomberg, Brian Lynch, Apple's Senior Director of Home Device Hardware Engineering, has officially left to join smart ring company Oura Health as Senior Vice President of Hardware Engineering. 据彭博社报道，苹果负责家庭设备硬件工程的高级总监 Brian Lynch 已正式离职，加入智能戒指公司 Oura Health，出任硬件工程高级副总裁一职。

Lynch has led hardware efforts for Apple's home devices division since 2022, and previously held a key engineering management position for Apple's self-driving car project (terminated in 2024). Earlier, he was involved in the development of multiple iPod products. Lynch 自 2022 年起主导苹果家庭设备部门的硬件工作，此前还曾担任苹果自动驾驶汽车项目（2024 年终止）的核心工程管理职位。更早期，他曾参与多款 iPod 产品的研发。

It is reported that Apple's smart home business is in a critical product development phase: a smart display with AI and facial recognition, a desktop robot device with a 9-inch screen, and a home security and automation sensor are all under development, and Lynch is the primary person in charge of the hardware side of these products. 据悉，苹果的智能家居业务正处于关键产品推进阶段：一款搭载 AI 与人脸识别功能的智能显示屏、一台配备 9 英寸屏幕的桌面机器人设备，以及一款家庭安防与自动化传感器均在研发之中，Lynch 正是这些产品硬件端的主要负责人。

It is worth noting that the report also mentioned that Apple is planning multiple AI wearable device product lines, including smart glasses, next-generation AirPods, and an AI pendant that can be hung on a shirt or necklace . All three devices will be equipped with computer vision cameras to provide environmental data to Siri. 值得注意的是，报道还提到苹果正在布局多条 AI 可穿戴设备产品线，包括智能眼镜、新一代 AirPods，以及一款可挂在衬衫或项链的 AI 挂坠 ，三款设备均将搭载计算机视觉摄像头，以向 Siri 提供环境数据。

Among them, the smart display has been repeatedly delayed due to the large-scale restructuring of Siri's work not being completed on time, and is currently expected to be released as early as September this year; the desktop robot and sensor are planned for next year. 其中，智能显示屏因 Siri 大规模重构工作迟迟未能完成而多次推迟，目前最早预计于今年 9 月发布；桌面机器人和传感器则计划于明年推出。

This is the second major personnel change for Apple's home hardware team in recent years. DJ Novotney, Vice President of Program Management, left at the beginning of 2024. The team is currently led by Matt Costello, who also oversees audio engineering and the Beats product line, reporting to John Ternus, Senior Vice President of Hardware Engineering. 这也是苹果家庭硬件团队近年来第二次出现重大人事变动。项目管理副总裁 DJ Novotney 已于 2024 年初离职。目前该团队由 Matt Costello 统领，他同时负责音频工程及 Beats 产品线，向硬件工程高级副总裁 John Ternus 汇报。

Oura has continuously recruited talent from Apple in recent years. After completing fundraising last year, the company was valued at $11 billion. It had previously poached Chief Medical Officer Ricky Bloomfield from Apple's health team, and its Design Director, Miklu Silvanto, also has a background working in Apple's design department. Oura 近年来持续从苹果引进人才。这家公司去年完成融资后估值达 110 亿美元，此前已从苹果健康团队挖来首席医疗官 Ricky Bloomfield，其设计总监 Miklu Silvanto 同样有苹果设计部门的从业背景。

In recent months, Alan Dye, head of user interface, and Lisa Jackson, head of environment and government affairs, have successively left. Former AI head John Giannandrea and general counsel will also step down later this year. 过去数月，用户界面负责人 Alan Dye、环境与政府事务负责人 Lisa Jackson 相继离职，前 AI 负责人 John Giannandrea 及总法律顾问也将于今年晚些时候离任。

等四年终于用上，国行 Apple Watch 房颤历史正式上线

Yesterday, Apple officially announced the launch of the mobile pulse rate atrial fibrillation indication recording software feature for Apple Watch in mainland China. 昨天，苹果官宣中国大陆的 Apple Watch 正式推出移动脉率房颤迹象记录软件功能。

🔗 Related reading: Interview with an Apple medical expert: Why should atrial fibrillation patients wear an Apple Watch? 🔗 相关阅读： 专访苹果医学家：房颤患者，为什么应该戴一块 Apple Watch？

Users diagnosed with atrial fibrillation (AFib) can enable the AFib History feature. Users can view important information such as the estimated frequency of heart rate showing AFib signs, receive weekly notifications to understand the estimated frequency of AFib episodes, and view detailed history through the Health app, including lifestyle factors that may affect AFib, such as sleep, alcohol consumption, and exercise. 被诊断患有心房颤动（房颤）的用户，可以打开房颤历史功能。用户可以查看心率显示房颤迹象的估算频率等重要信息，每周获得通知，了解预估房颤发作频率，通过健康 app 查看详细历史记录，包括可能影响房颤的生活方式因素，如睡眠、酒精摄入、运动等。

Users can also download a PDF document containing detailed AFib history and lifestyle factors, which can be easily shared with healthcare professionals for more in-depth and effective communication. 用户还可以下载包含详细房颤历史记录和生活方式因素的 PDF 文档，轻松共享给医护人员，以进行更深入有效的交流。

This feature is available for Apple Watch Series 6 and later, Apple Watch Ultra and later, and Apple Watch SE 2. It was officially announced at WWDC on June 7, 2022, along with watchOS 9, and has been four years since its launch. 这个功能适用于 Apple Watch Series 6 及后续表款、Apple Watch Ultra 及后续表款和 Apple Watch SE 2，在 2022 年 6 月 7 日的 WWDC 上，随 watchOS 9 系统正式宣布推出，至今已四年。

OpenAI 发布「最强小模型」GPT-5.4 mini 与 nano

Today, OpenAI officially released two new models, GPT-5.4 mini and GPT-5.4 nano, which the company calls 'the most capable small models to date.' These two models bring the core capabilities of GPT-5.4 into a lighter architecture, designed for high-throughput, latency-sensitive workloads. 今天，OpenAI 正式发布了 GPT-5.4 mini 与 GPT-5.4 nano 两款新模型，官方称其为「迄今为止能力最强的小型模型」。这两款模型将 GPT-5.4 的核心能力引入更轻量的架构，专为高吞吐量、对延迟敏感的工作负载而设计。

  • GPT-5.4 mini shows significant improvements over GPT-5 mini in code writing, reasoning, multimodal understanding, and tool usage, with a more than 2x increase in operating speed. It can quickly parse complex user interface screenshots and complete related operational tasks; in multiple benchmark tests, its performance is already close to that of the larger flagship model GPT-5.4; GPT-5.4 mini 在代码编写、推理、多模态理解及工具调用方面均较 GPT-5 mini 有显著提升，运行速度提升超过 2 倍。能够快速解析复杂用户界面截图，完成相关操作任务；在多项基准测试中，其表现已接近体量更大的旗舰模型 GPT-5.4；
  • GPT-5.4 nano is the lightest and fastest version in the GPT-5.4 series, positioned for sub-agent scenarios such as classification, data extraction, content sorting, and handling simple auxiliary tasks. On SWE-bench Pro, nano significantly outperforms the previous generation GPT-5 mini. GPT-5.4 nano 则是 GPT-5.4 系列中最轻量、速度最快的版本，定位于分类、数据提取、内容排序以及处理简单辅助任务的子智能体场景。在 SWE-bench Pro 上，nano 显著优于上一代 GPT-5 mini。

OpenAI positions the core application scenario for these two models within a 'sub-agent' architecture: large models like GPT-5.4 are responsible for task planning and final determination, while specific sub-tasks are distributed in parallel to GPT-5.4 mini for execution, such as code repository searches, large file reviews, and auxiliary document processing. This model has already been applied in Codex. OpenAI 将这两款模型的核心应用场景定位于「子智能体」架构：由 GPT-5.4 等大模型负责任务规划与最终判定，将具体子任务并行分配给 GPT-5.4 mini 执行，例如代码库搜索、大文件审阅及辅助文档处理。这一模式在 Codex 中已得到实际应用。

Regarding pricing, GPT-5.4 mini's API is priced at $0.75 per 1 million input tokens and $4.50 per 1 million output tokens, with a context window of 400K. In contrast, GPT-5.4 is priced at $2.50 per 1 million input tokens and $15.00 per 1 million output tokens, a significant cost difference. 定价方面，GPT-5.4 mini 的 API 定价为每 100 万输入 token 0.75 美元、每 100 万输出 token 4.50 美元，上下文窗口为 400K。相比之下，GPT-5.4 的定价为每 100 万输入 token 2.50 美元、输出 15.00 美元，成本差距悬殊。

GPT-5.4 nano is only available to API users, with even lower pricing: $0.20 per 1 million input tokens and $1.25 per 1 million output tokens. GPT-5.4 nano 仅面向 API 用户开放，定价更低，每 100 万输入 token 0.20 美元，每 100 万输出 token 1.25 美元。

英伟达 DLSS 5「AI 垃圾」引发玩家强烈反弹，黄仁勋：你们完全错了

NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang, during the Q&A session at the GTC 2026 conference, directly responded to players' criticism of DLSS 5 as 'AI garbage,' stating bluntly that 'their claims are completely wrong.' 英伟达 CEO 黄仁勋在 GTC 2026 大会的问答环节中，正面回应了玩家对 DLSS 5「AI 垃圾」的批评，直言「他们的说法完全错误」。

Regarding concerns about changing game art styles, Jensen Huang told a Tom's Hardware reporter that DLSS 5 integrates the controllability of game content such as geometry and textures with generative AI. He stated, 'It's not post-processing at the frame level, but generative control at the geometry level.' Developers can 'fine-tune the AI to suit their own style,' and artistic control will not be altered. 对于改变游戏艺术风格的质疑，黄仁勋在回应 Tom’s Hardware 记者时表示，DLSS 5 将几何体、纹理等游戏内容的可控性与生成式 AI 融合在一起，「不是在帧层面的后期处理，而是在几何层面的生成式控制」，开发者可以「对 AI 进行微调以适应自己的风格」，艺术上的掌控权不会因此改变。

He referred to this mechanism as 'content-control generative AI,' emphasizing that the input data comes from structured information provided by developers, such as 3D skeletons, motion vectors, and scene depth, rather than unsubstantiated 'speculative' generation. 他将这套机制称为「内容控制生成式 AI」（content-control），强调输入数据来自开发者提供的 3D 骨架、运动向量和场景深度等结构化信息，而非无依据的「猜测式」生成。

Unlike previous versions primarily used to improve resolution and frame rates, DLSS 5, leveraging neural rendering technology, can directly alter game visuals, adding detail to character hair, faces, game environments, and lighting. Jensen Huang called this the 'GPT moment' for graphics. 与以往主要用于提升分辨率和帧率不同，DLSS 5 借助神经渲染技术，能够直接改变游戏画面表现，为角色头发、面部、游戏环境和光照添加细节，黄仁勋将其称为图形领域的「GPT 时刻」。

However, the unveiling of this technology sparked an unexpectedly strong negative reaction. In a demo comparison for 'Resident Evil: Requiem,' enabling DLSS 5 resulted in character Grace's lips appearing fuller and her makeup heavier, presenting a distinctly different texture from the original. Similar 'AI beautification' effects were observed in characters and environments in 'Starfield'. 然而，这项技术的亮相却引发了意外强烈的负面反响。在《生化危机：安魂曲》的演示对比中，启用 DLSS 5 后角色格蕾丝的嘴唇更丰满、妆容更浓重，整体呈现出明显异于原版的质感；《星空》中的角色和环境也出现了类似的「AI 美化」效果。

Many players on social media and Reddit criticized this as 'AI garbage,' believing that the technology would homogenize game visuals and erase artists' original creative intentions. 大量玩家在社交媒体和 Reddit 上批评这是「AI 垃圾」，认为该技术会让游戏画面趋于同质化，抹杀艺术家的原创意图。
