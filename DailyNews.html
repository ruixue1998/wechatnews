努比亚总裁回应豆包预定餐厅乌龙：豆包手机真的可以

Yesterday, Ni Fei, President of ZTE Terminal Business Unit and President of Nubia Technology Co., Ltd., released a video responding to the recent "Doubao AI restaurant reservation blunder" incident. 昨天，中兴通讯终端事业部总裁、努比亚技术有限公司总裁倪飞发布视频，回应近日「豆包 AI 预约餐厅乌龙」事件。

The incident originated from a netizen's previous sharing of an experience using Doubao AI to book a restaurant: the AI gave a virtual response in the conversation, which the user believed to be true and went to the restaurant, only to be rejected. 事件起因于此前有网民分享使用豆包 AI 预订餐厅的经历：AI 在对话中给出了虚拟回复，用户信以为真前往餐厅，最终遭到拒绝。

Ni Fei stated in the video that although the Doubao APP may not currently support actual reservation functions, the Nubia M153, equipped with the Doubao mobile assistant, can perform such operations. He demonstrated live in the video: directly issuing voice commands to the phone, requesting to filter nearby restaurants, book for 6 PM the next evening, for a 5-person dinner. 倪飞在视频中表示，虽然豆包 APP 目前可能尚不支持实际预约功能，但搭载豆包手机助手的努比亚 M153 可以执行此类操作。他在视频中现场演示：直接向手机发出语音指令，要求筛选附近餐厅、预约次日傍晚 6 点、5 人聚餐。

Doubao then selected a restaurant based on its previous dining preferences, confirmed the time and number of people, and completed the reservation. Ni Fei concluded the video by saying, "I believe it won't be long before everyone feels that using Doubao to book a restaurant is just a common thing in daily life." 豆包随后根据其此前的饮食偏好选定餐厅、确认时间与人数，并完成预约。倪飞在视频结尾称，「相信过不了多久，大家就会觉得，用豆包预约餐厅只是生活中习以为常的一件事了。」

The new film stars Brad Pitt, is directed by David Fincher, and written by Quentin. 据博主「守望好莱坞」消息，昆汀 · 塔伦蒂诺作品《好莱坞往事》续集《克里夫 · 布斯继续冒险》定档今年 11 月 25 日上线 Netflix。新片由布拉德 · 皮特主演、大卫·芬奇执导、昆汀编剧。

This film revolves around Cliff Booth from "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood," serving as an extended project for the original character. Brad Pitt previously won an Oscar for Best Supporting Actor for this role. 这部片围绕《好莱坞往事》中的 Cliff Booth 展开，属于原片人物的延展项目。布拉德 · 皮特此前凭借该角色获得奥斯卡最佳男配角。

苹果娱乐业务 VP Eddy Cue 获戛纳国际创意节年度娱乐人物奖

The Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity recently announced that Eddy Cue, Apple's Senior Vice President of Services and Health, will receive the 2026 "Entertainment Person of the Year" award and will accept it at the Cannes Lions in June. 戛纳国际创意节日前宣布，苹果服务与健康业务高级副总裁 Eddy Cue 将获得 2026 年「年度娱乐人物」奖项，并将在 6 月的戛纳国际创意节领奖。

The organizers stated that Cue has long driven Apple's business in entertainment, media, and content creativity. Apple has also previously won awards at the Cannes Lions, including "Creative Marketer of the Year" in 2025. 主办方称，Cue 长期推动苹果在娱乐、媒体和内容创意领域的业务。苹果此前也曾在戛纳国际创意节获奖，包括 2025 年「Creative Marketer of the Year」。

Cue is currently responsible for services such as Apple TV and Apple Music. Cue 目前负责 Apple TV、Apple Music 等服务业务。

火山引擎上线 AIGC 短剧创作平台火山剧创 1.0

Volcano Engine announced yesterday that its one-stop AIGC short drama creation platform, Volcano Drama Creator 1.0, has officially launched. The platform targets the creative workflow of the short drama industry, adapting to models like Seedance and Seedream, and Volcano Engine claims it can shorten the production cycle by over 80%. 火山引擎昨日宣布，一站式 AIGC 短剧创作平台火山剧创 1.0 正式上线。平台面向短剧行业创作流程，适配 Seedance、Seedream 等模型，火山引擎称其可让制作周期缩短 80% 以上。

Volcano Drama Creator breaks down short drama production into nodes such as script understanding, storyboard analysis, material generation, and strategic review, and opens up core node editing permissions to users. This update adds prompt word editing, an IP asset library, and team collaboration features: the former is used to adjust visual details and styles, the IP asset library covers real and virtual human images, and the team function supports multi-role collaboration and point allocation. 火山剧创把短剧制作拆成剧本理解、分镜解析、素材生成、策略审片等节点，并向用户开放核心节点编辑权限。平台这次新增提示词编辑、IP 资产库和团队协作功能：前者用于调整画面细节和风格，IP 资产库覆盖真人与虚拟人像，团队功能则支持多角色协作和积分分配。
