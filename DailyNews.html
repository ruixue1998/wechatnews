曝苹果「天价扫货」DRAM，阻止竞争对手获取

According to a revelation published by source "Jukan", Apple, at an extremely high premium, has virtually "swept up all available mobile DRAM in the market", even willing to bear the cost of damaged operating profit margins. 据消息源「Jukan」发布的爆料，苹果以极高的溢价，在市场上几乎「扫光了市场上所有可取得的移动 DRAM」，甚至愿意为此承受营业利润率受损的代价。

The source emphasized that Apple's move is not merely to meet its own regular stocking needs, but rather aims to artificially inflate DRAM market prices to prevent other smartphone manufacturers from acquiring sufficient memory chips. 该消息人士强调，苹果此举并非单纯为了满足自身的常规备货需求，而是旨在通过人为抬高 DRAM 市场价格，阻止其他智能手机厂商获取足够的内存芯片。

Analyst Ming-Chi Kuo previously released a research report pointing out that Apple is leveraging its supply chain influence to strongly lock in production capacity during the storage chip price increase cycle, and plans to absorb cost pressures in exchange for a larger market share. 而分析师郭明錤此前曾发布研报指出，苹果正凭借其供应链话语权在存储芯片涨价周期中强力锁定产能，并计划通过吸收成本压力以换取更大的市场份额。

Although rising memory costs will inevitably squeeze iPhone's gross profit margin, Ming-Chi Kuo pointed out that Apple's core strategy is to use market turbulence to lock in chips, absorb costs, and seize market share, subsequently replenishing profits through the growth of its services business. 尽管内存成本攀升将不可避免地挤压 iPhone 的毛利率，但郭明錤指出，苹果的核心策略是利用市场动荡期锁定芯片、吸收成本并抢占份额，后续再通过服务业务的增长进行利润回补。

In addition, for marketing purposes, Apple currently plans to maintain the starting price of the iPhone 18 series, to be released in the second half of 2026, as much as possible. 此外，为利于市场营销，苹果目前计划在 2026 年下半年发布的 iPhone 18 系列上尽量维持起售价不变。

充电宝迎史上最严国标，明年强制实施

According to CCTV News, the mandatory national standard GB 47372-2026 "Safety Technical Specification for Mobile Power Supplies," organized and formulated by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, was officially released yesterday (April 3) and will be formally implemented starting April 1 next year. 据央视新闻报道，由工业和信息化部组织制定的强制性国家标准 GB 47372-2026《移动电源安全技术规范》已于昨天（4 月 3 日）正式发布，将于明年 4 月 1 日起正式实施。

The new national standard covers two types of products: "power banks" and "outdoor power stations." 新国标覆盖「充电宝」和「户外电源」两类产品。

In terms of safety, the standard introduces a needle penetration test, explicitly enhancing the safety protection capabilities of mobile power supplies in extreme scenarios such as high temperatures, overcharging, and extrusion; it also adds cyclic aging detection to reduce the risk of internal short circuits after long-term use, and requires products to indicate a recommended safe service life, reminding consumers to replace aged products promptly. 安全性方面，标准引入针刺试验，明确提升移动电源在高温、过度充电、挤压等极端场景下的安全防护能力；同时新增循环老化检测，以降低长期使用后的内部短路风险，并要求产品标明建议安全使用年限，提醒消费者及时更换老化产品。

Furthermore, the new national standard promotes unique product coding management, allowing consumers to query core information such as battery brand via the code; in terms of manufacturing processes, it clearly proposes raw material and production process control requirements, enhancing safety levels from the source. 此外，新国标推行产品唯一性编码管理，消费者可通过编码查询电池品牌等核心信息；在生产制造流程方面，明确提出原材料及生产过程管控要求，从源头提升安全水平。

Experts state that after the implementation of the new national standard, power banks previously purchased and certified under the old 3C standard can still be carried on board normally, provided they comply with current civil aviation regulations. 专家表示，新国标实施后，此前已购买并通过旧 3C 认证的充电宝，只要符合民航现行相关规定，仍可正常携带乘机。

The main drafting units for this new national standard boast a large lineup, encompassing over 30 enterprises and academic institutions, including the China Electronics Standardization Institute, Contemporary Amperex Technology (CATL), Huawei Terminal, Sunwoda, Anker Innovations, Xiaomi, OPPO Guangdong Mobile Communications, BYD Lithium Battery, Lenovo, and Bull Group. 本次新国标的主要起草单位阵容庞大，涵盖中国电子技术标准化研究院、宁德新能源科技、华为终端、欣旺达、安克创新、小米、OPPO 广东移动通信、比亚迪锂电池、联想、公牛集团等逾 30 家企业与学术机构。

Vibe Coding 应用 Anything 被苹果下架后重新上架

The official account for Vibe Coding App "Anything" just posted a confirmation that Apple has relisted the previously removed Vibe Coding tool, Anything, on the App Store. To celebrate its relisting, Anything announced a $5,000 weekend hackathon event, along with 20,000 Credits. Vibe Coding App「Anything」官方账号刚刚发文确认，苹果已将此前下架的 Vibe Coding 工具 Anything 重新上架 App Store。为庆祝此次重新上架，Anything 宣布发起一场 5000 美元的周末黑客马拉松活动，并附赠 2 万 Credits。

Previously, Apple completely removed Anything from the App Store, citing a violation of App Store Review Guideline 2.5.2. 此前，苹果以违反 App Store 审核指南第 2.5.2 条款为由，将 Anything 彻底下架。

This clause stipulates that apps must be self-contained within their bundles and must not download, install, or execute code that introduces or changes the app's features or functionality. Anything, which allows users to describe requirements in natural language on an iPhone, have AI generate code in real-time, and run it on the device, directly conflicts with this clause. 该条款规定，App 应自包含在自己的套装中，不得下载、安装或执行会引入或更改 App 特性或功能的代码。Anything 支持用户在 iPhone 上通过自然语言描述需求、由 AI 实时生成代码并在设备上运行，与该条款直接冲突。

Regarding this removal, Anything's official account previously posted a rebuttal on the X platform: "Breaking: Apple is panicking about Vibe Coding and has removed Anything from the App Store, so we're moving app development to iMessage. Good luck to Apple trying to remove this one." 对于此次下架，Anything 官方曾在 X 平台发文反击：「重磅：苹果对 Vibe Coding 感到恐慌，把 Anything 从 App Store 下架了，所以我们把应用开发转移到了 iMessage。祝苹果顺利移除这个。」

It's worth noting that Apple itself is not opposed to AI-assisted programming – Xcode 26.3, released in February this year, has integrated Claude Agent and OpenAI Codex. 值得注意的是，苹果本身并不反对 AI 辅助编程——今年 2 月推出的 Xcode 26.3 已将 Claude Agent 与 OpenAI Codex 集成其中。

What Apple truly resists is users generating and executing new code through third-party apps outside of store review; this controversy is essentially a battle for platform control. 苹果真正抵制的，是用户通过第三方 App 在商店审核之外生成并执行新代码，这场风波本质上是一场平台控制权之争。
