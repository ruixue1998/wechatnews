已交付 286 台全部召回，张雪机车 820RR 因机油泄漏隐患停售整改

Yesterday, Zhangxue Motorcycle officially released the "820RR Recall Notice," initiating a comprehensive recall and rectification for its 820RR model. It is reported that the cause of this recall was a crankshaft case rupture accident that occurred in Yinchuan on April 16. 昨天，张雪机车官方发布《820RR 召回通告》，对旗下 820RR 车型展开全面召回整改。据悉，此次召回的起因是 4 月 16 日发生在银川的一起曲轴箱破裂事故。

The damaged vehicle was returned to the factory on April 21. After disassembly and inspection, the factory confirmed that the root cause of the malfunction was an incorrect assembly of the O-ring on the pressure relief valve—after the vehicle had been in use for some time, the pressure relief valve loosened, leading to oil pressure leakage, which in turn caused insufficient lubrication and ultimately resulted in connecting rod bearing failure. 受损车辆于 4 月 21 日返回工厂，经拆解检查，工厂确认故障根源为泄压阀的 O 型密封圈装配错误——车辆使用一段时间后，泄压阀出现松动，导致机油压力泄漏，进而引发润滑不足，最终造成连杆拉瓦。

Currently, 286 units of the 820RR have been delivered. Zhangxue Motorcycle requires all owners to immediately cease using the model and contact authorized dealerships to arrange for towing, with the dealerships responsible for carrying out the rectification. 目前，820RR 已累计交付 286 台。张雪机车要求所有车主即刻停止使用该车型，并联系授权门店安排拖运，由门店负责执行整改。

Based on the actual condition of the vehicle, Zhangxue Motorcycle offers two rectification plans: 根据车辆实际状态，张雪机车提供两套整改方案：

  • Owners whose pressure relief valves have already loosened or developed play can choose to return or exchange their vehicle; 泄压阀已出现松动或旷量的车主，可选择退车或换车；
  • Owners whose pressure relief valves have not yet loosened and have no play, and only need to replace the O-ring, will receive 2 free maintenance services as compensation. 泄压阀尚未松动、无旷量，仅需更换 O 型密封圈的车主，则将获得 2 次免费保养作为补偿。

Stanford University's Fei-Fei Li team recently published a paper revealing a systemic flaw in current mainstream multimodal AI: even without receiving any images, cutting-edge models like GPT-5, Gemini 3 Pro, and Claude Opus 4.5 still 'confidently' describe image details and provide diagnostic conclusions. 斯坦福大学李飞飞团队近日发表论文，揭示了当前主流多模态 AI 存在一种系统性缺陷——即便没有收到任何图片，GPT-5、Gemini 3 Pro、Claude Opus 4.5 等前沿模型依然会「自信地」描述图像细节并给出诊断结论。

Researchers named this phenomenon 'Mirage Reasoning'. 研究者将这一现象命名为「海市蜃楼式推理」（Mirage Reasoning）。

The team constructed a test set called Phantom-0, removing all images from 200 questions that required image viewing to answer, without informing the models. The results showed that all tested models 'described' an image that did not exist in over 60% of cases . 团队构建了一个名为 Phantom-0 的测试集，将 200 道需要看图才能作答的问题的图片全部拿掉，同时不告知模型。结果显示， 所有被测模型在超过 60% 的情况下会「描述」一张根本不存在的图片 。

Even more strikingly, the team trained a pure text small model with only 3 billion parameters using Qwen-2.5, which had never seen any images. On the chest X-ray Q&A benchmark, it not only outperformed all multimodal large models but also surpassed the average level of human radiologists by more than 10 percentage points. 更具冲击力的是，团队用 Qwen-2.5 训练了一个仅有 30 亿参数、从未看过任何图片的纯文本小模型，在胸部 X 光问答基准上不仅击败了所有多模态大模型，还将人类放射科医生的平均水平甩开了 10 个百分点以上。

This flaw is particularly dangerous in medical scenarios: when an image upload fails, the model does not report an error but directly outputs a professionally worded diagnostic report, and the content systematically biases towards severe conditions requiring urgent treatment, such as myocardial infarction and melanoma. 这一缺陷在医疗场景中尤为危险：图片上传失败时，模型不会报错，而是直接输出措辞专业的诊断报告，且内容系统性地偏向心肌梗死、黑色素瘤等需要紧急处置的重症。

To address this vulnerability, the team proposed the B-Clean cleaning framework, which identified 74%~77% of questions in three authoritative benchmarks as 'answerable without looking at the image' and removed them.
