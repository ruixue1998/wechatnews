曝苹果智能眼镜砍到仅剩 2 款，Vision Pro 系列出局

Ming-Chi Kuo, an analyst at Tianfeng International Securities, posted on X today that Apple's previously planned roadmap for XR headsets and smart glasses has been significantly adjusted. Currently, only two smart glasses products have visibility, and the Apple Vision series has effectively been removed. 天风国际证券分析师郭明錤今日在 X 发帖称，苹果此前规划的 XR 头戴设备与智能眼镜产品路线图已大幅调整，目前仅剩两款智能眼镜产品具有可见度，Apple Vision 系列实际上已被移除。

Kuo Ming-Chi stated that this major roadmap change was finalized by Apple's next CEO, John Ternus. The adjustment involves cutting the Vision Pro series and redirecting resources towards smart glasses products with greater consumer potential. 郭明錤表示，此次路线大改由苹果下一任 CEO John Ternus 拍板定案，调整方向为砍掉 Vision Pro 系列，将资源转向消费潜力更大的智能眼镜类产品。

According to the latest supply chain investigation, Apple's AR/XR smart glasses with display functionality (using a waveguide solution) will be postponed until 2029; AI glasses without display functionality (similar to Ray-Ban Meta) are still expected to maintain their 2027 release plan. 根据最新供应链调查，苹果具有显示功能的 AR / XR 智能眼镜（采用光波导方案）将推迟至 2029 年推出；无显示功能的 AI 眼镜（类似 Ray-Ban Meta 形态）预计仍维持 2027 年发布计划。

ChatGPT 成为全球最快月活超过 10 亿用户的应用

Reuters, citing data from market research firm Sensor Tower, reported that just about three years after its launch, ChatGPT's global monthly active users officially surpassed 1 billion in May, making it the fastest application in history to reach this milestone, surpassing the growth rates of products like Google Maps, Instagram, and YouTube. 路透社援引市场调研机构 Sensor Tower 数据报道称，上线仅约三年时间，ChatGPT 的全球月活已于 5 月正式突破 10 亿，成为有史以来最快达成这一里程碑的应用程序，超越了 Google 地图、Instagram 和 YouTube 等产品的增长速度。

Meanwhile, competitor Anthropic's Claude also maintained strong growth momentum. However, in terms of absolute user scale, there is still a significant gap between the two. 不过，在绝对用户规模上，两者仍存在明显差距。

乘联分会：5 月新能源乘用车零售 97.4 万辆

The China Passenger Car Association (CPCA) released data showing that from May 1st to 31st, national passenger car new energy vehicle (NEV) retail sales reached 974,000 units, a year-on-year decrease of 5%, and a month-on-month increase of 15%; cumulative retail sales since the beginning of this year reached 3.732 million units, a year-on-year decrease of 14%. 乘联分会发布数据显示，5 月 1 日至 31 日，全国乘用车新能源市场零售 97.4 万辆，同比下降 5%，较上月同期增长 15%；今年以来累计零售 373.2 万辆，同比下降 14%。

Wholesale performance was relatively stronger. During the same period, national passenger car manufacturers' new energy vehicle (NEV) wholesale volume reached 1.365 million units, a year-on-year increase of 12%, and a month-on-month increase of 11%; cumulative wholesale volume since the beginning of this year reached 5.318 million units, a year-on-year increase of 2%. 批发端表现相对更强。同一周期内，全国乘用车厂商新能源批发 136.5 万辆，同比增长 12%，较上月同期增长 11%；今年以来累计批发 531.8 万辆，同比增长 2%。

In terms of penetration rate, from May 1st to 31st, the new energy vehicle (NEV) retail penetration rate in the national passenger car market was 63%, and the NEV wholesale penetration rate for manufacturers was 61.1%. During the same period, production of pure fuel light vehicles was 535,000 units, a year-on-year decrease of 45%; total production of hybrid and plug-in hybrid vehicles was 379,000 units, a year-on-year decrease of 17%, and a month-on-month increase of 11%. 渗透率方面，5 月 1 日至 31 日，全国乘用车市场新能源零售渗透率为 63%，厂商新能源批发渗透率为 61.1%。同期纯燃料轻型车生产 53.5 万辆，同比下降 45%；混合动力与插混总体生产 37.9 万辆，同比下降 17%，较上月同期增长 11%。

日本车企统一放宽轻微零部件瑕疵判定

According to Nikkei, Japanese automakers and parts manufacturers, including Toyota and Honda, are formulating a unified policy for determining parts defects. The new policy aims to alleviate parts waste and quality inspection pressure amidst tight raw material supplies. 据日本经济新闻报道，包括丰田、本田在内的日本汽车制造商和零部件生产商正在制定统一零部件缺陷判定政策。新政策旨在缓解原材料供应紧张下的零部件浪费和质检压力。

This unified policy will be jointly formulated by the Japan Automobile Manufacturers Association and the Japan Auto Parts Industries Association. After implementation, for minor imperfections that do not affect the overall vehicle performance, such as black spots, small scratches, harmless burrs, and frayed edges, parts manufacturers will no longer unilaterally deem them as defective and discard them. As long as they meet national standards and have no significant functional or appearance issues, vehicle manufacturers can accept them. 这项统一政策将由日本汽车制造商协会和日本汽车零部件工业协会共同制定。实施后，对不影响整车性能的轻微瑕疵，例如黑点、细小划痕、无害毛刺和毛边，零部件厂商将不再自行判定为不良品并废弃，只要符合国家标准且功能和外观无重大问题，整车厂可以接受。

Taking automotive electronic wiring harness plastic connectors as an example, approximately 60% of defect judgments come from black spots. After the standardization, Japan is expected to reduce 10,000 discarded items per month. Toyota has established a dedicated department to work with suppliers to review parts according to unified standards and regularly holds joint review meetings with other Japanese automakers and suppliers. 以车载电子线束塑胶接插件为例，约 60% 的不良判定来自黑点。统一标准后，日本国内预计每月可减少 1 万个废弃品。丰田已设立专项部门，联合配套厂商按统一标准评审零部件，并定期与其他日系车企及供应商召开联合评审会议。

The official Weibo account for the animated film "Big Fish & Begonia" announced yesterday that the film's tenth-anniversary re-release is scheduled for June 19, and will premiere its IMAX 2D version. The film will also have an exclusive IMAX screening at the 28th Shanghai International Film Festival. 动画电影《大鱼海棠》官方微博昨日宣布，影片十周年重映定档 6 月 19 日，并将首次上映 IMAX 2D 版本。该片还将在第 28 届上海国际电影节进行 IMAX 独家展映。

"Big Fish & Begonia" was jointly produced by B&T Cultural, Beijing Enlight Pictures, and Horgos Coloroom Pictures, and co-directed by Liang Xuan and Zhang Chun. The film took 12 years to produce, was released in mainland China on July 8, 2016, re-released in 2020, with a cumulative box office of 575 million yuan, and currently has a Douban rating of 7.0. 《大鱼海棠》由彼岸天文化、北京光线影业、霍尔果斯彩条屋影业联合出品，梁旋、张春联合执导。影片耗时 12 年制作，2016 年 7 月 8 日在中国内地上映，2020 年曾重映，累计票房 5.75 亿元，目前豆瓣评分为 7.0。

The first part tells the story of Chun, a girl who controls the growth of begonia flowers, who resurrects the soul of a human boy named "Kun" out of gratitude, allowing him to grow into a giant fish and return to the sea, thereby triggering a series of disasters that defy the laws of the world. A teaser trailer for the second part of the film was also previously released. 第一部讲述掌管海棠花生长的少女椿，为报恩复活人类男孩「鲲」的灵魂，使其成长为巨鱼并回归大海，由此引发一连串违背世界规律的灾难。影片第二部先导预告此前也已发布。
