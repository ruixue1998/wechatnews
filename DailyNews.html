喜马拉雅「升龙」烟花秀引发「炸山」争议

Renowned outdoor brand Arc'teryx collaborated with artist Cai Guo-Qiang to set off the art fireworks "Ascending Dragon" in Relong Township, Gyantse County, Tibet (at an altitude of approximately 5500 meters), sparking widespread public concern about ecological protection. 知名户外品牌始祖鸟携手艺术家蔡国强，在西藏江孜县热龙乡（海拔约 5500 米）燃放艺术烟花《升龙》，引发舆论对生态保护的广泛关注。

On-site videos showed the fireworks being set off in three acts, forming a soaring dragon-shaped spectacle. Cai Guo-Qiang explained that it symbolizes "auspiciousness" and "endless vitality." The audience consisted mostly of outdoor media, photographers, and KOLs. The display lasted less than 5 minutes, but the setup and construction process involved the entire ridge. 现场视频显示，烟花分三幕燃放，形成腾飞的龙形景观，蔡国强在解说中称其象征「吉祥」与「生生不息」。观众多为户外媒体、摄影师及 KOL，燃放时间不足 5 分钟，但布置与施工过程涉及整条山脊。

The organizers stated that the colored powder used is biodegradable, the firing level is V (lowest risk), and a "prevention-monitoring-restoration" plan was developed, including relocating livestock, guiding small animals away, cleaning up residues after firing, and vegetation restoration. 主办方表示，所用彩色粉为生物可降解材料，燃放等级为 V 级（最低风险），并制定了「预防—监测—恢复」方案，包括转移牲畜、引导小型动物离开、燃放后清理残留物与植被修复。

However, several ecologists pointed out that the low-temperature, low-rainfall environment of the plateau would slow down the degradation rate, and dust and pigments could persist for many years; explosions might also disrupt plant growth cycles and interfere with wild animals' energy reserves during the "fattening up for autumn" phase. 然而，多位生态学者指出，高原低温少雨环境会延缓降解速度，粉尘与颜料可能多年存在；爆破亦可能破坏植物生长周期，干扰野生动物在「贴秋膘」阶段的能量储备。

According to the local ecological environment department, the activity has been filed and approved, the site is not in an ecological protection area, there are no residents nearby, and the use of environmentally friendly materials does not require an environmental assessment. 据当地生态环境部门介绍，活动已备案并获批，选址不在生态保护区，周边无人居住，且使用环保材料无需环境评估。

However, experts question whether wild animals can fully relocate, and foreign substances might exacerbate the ecological burden . 但专家质疑，野生动物难以完全迁离，外来物质或加重生态负担 。

In response, the "Cloud Peak Everest" WeChat official account issued a situation report, stating that "the matter will be handled according to laws and regulations based on the verification results." 对此，「云端珠峰」微信公众号发布情况通报，表示「将根据核查结果依法依规处理」。

On September 20, after the video of "Cai Guo-Qiang: Ascending Dragon" fireworks show was released online, it attracted netizens' attention. The Shigatse Municipal Party Committee and Government attached great importance to it, and a task force has been immediately dispatched to the site for verification, and subsequent actions will be handled according to laws and regulations based on the verification results . 9月20日，《蔡国强：升龙》烟花秀视频在网络发布后，引发网民关注。日喀则市委、市政府高度重视， 已成立调查组第一时间赶赴现场核查，后续将根据核查结果依法依规处理 。

It is reported that Arc'teryx brand released an apology letter on Weibo yesterday morning, and Cai Guo-Qiang's studio also issued a statement on its WeChat official account, stating that "the assessment of the boundaries of artistic expression needs to be more professional, and reverence for nature needs to be more humble" and "will actively cooperate with third-party institutions and relevant departments to conduct multi-faceted assessments of the impact of this incident on the natural ecosystem." 据悉，始祖鸟品牌方于昨日早间在微博发布致歉信，蔡国强工作室也在其微信公众号发布情况说明，表示「对艺术表达边界的评估需要更加专业，对自然的敬畏需要更加谦卑」「将主动配合第三方机构及相关部门，就此事对自然生态造成的影响进行多方面的评估」。

Meta CTO 自曝「灾难级演示」内幕

Recently, Meta CTO Andrew Bosworth responded on Instagram to the reasons for multiple failures of the smart glasses live demo at the Meta Connect conference. 日前，Meta 首席技术官 Andrew Bosworth 在 Instagram 上回应了 Meta Connect 大会上智能眼镜现场演示多次失灵的原因。

He emphasized that the problem was not due to Wi-Fi, but rather a resource management error . 他强调， 问题并非源于 Wi-Fi，而是一次资源管理上的失误 。

Last week, Meta released three new smart glasses, including the upgraded Ray-Ban Meta, the Meta Ray-Ban Display with a new wrist controller, and the motion-tracking Oakley Meta Vanguard. 上周，Meta 发布了三款新智能眼镜，包括升级版 Ray-Ban Meta、新增腕带控制器的 Meta Ray-Ban Display，以及运动定位的 Oakley Meta Vanguard。

However, during multiple live demonstrations, the devices experienced stuttering and even interruptions. 然而，在多场现场演示中，设备出现卡顿甚至中断的情况。

Among them, when culinary creator Jack Mancuso asked the Ray-Ban Meta glasses for the first step of a sauce recipe during a demonstration, the AI did not respond, then directly skipped the step, forcing him to stop the demo and hand the microphone back to Zuckerberg. At the time, it was widely believed to be a network issue. 其中，厨艺创作者 Jack Mancuso 在演示中向 Ray-Ban Meta 眼镜询问酱汁食谱的第一步操作时，AI 并未作出回应，随后直接跳过步骤，迫使他只好中止演示并将话筒交还给扎克伯格。当时现场普遍认为是网络问题所致。

Bosworth explained that the real reason was that when the chef said 'Hey, Meta, start Live AI,' the Live AI function of all Ray-Ban Meta glasses on site was triggered simultaneously, instantly sending a large number of requests to the development server, leading to a 'self-DDoS'. Bosworth 解释称，真正的原因是当厨师说出「Hey, Meta，start Live AI」时，现场所有 Ray-Ban Meta 眼镜的 Live AI 功能被同时触发，瞬间向开发服务器发起大量请求，导致「自我 DDoS」。

Furthermore, the failed WhatsApp video call stemmed from a 'race condition' vulnerability that had never appeared before — the glasses' display went into sleep mode the moment a call came in, and failed to show the answer prompt after waking up. 此外，失败的 WhatsApp 视频通话则源于一个此前从未出现过的「竞争条件」漏洞 —— 眼镜显示屏在来电瞬间进入休眠，唤醒后未能显示接听提示。

Bosworth stated that these issues have been fixed, emphasizing that 'this was just a demo accident, not a product defect,' and expressed full confidence in the product's overall performance. Bosworth 表示，这些问题已被修复，并强调「这只是一次演示事故，而非产品缺陷」，对产品的整体性能充满信心。

任天堂美国将外包客服业务

According to IGN, Nintendo of America recently decided to outsource its customer support operations to South American countries such as Brazil, Argentina, and Nicaragua, a move that resulted in approximately 200 long-term contract customer service personnel in the U.S. losing their jobs. 据 IGN 报道，任天堂美国公司近日决定将客户支持业务外包至巴西、阿根廷、尼加拉瓜等南美国家，此举导致约 200 名长期在美工作的合同制客服人员失业。

To circumvent U.S. labor law requirements regarding permanent employment, they typically signed 11-month contracts, then resigned for 2 months before re-signing. 为了规避美国劳动法中关于转正的要求，他们通常签订 11 个月合约，离职 2 个月后再续签。

Contract workers generally believe that this outsourcing was primarily due to cost considerations — Brazil's monthly minimum wage is approximately $245 USD (about 1,744 RMB), with an hourly wage of about $1.53 USD (less than 11 RMB), while Washington State's minimum hourly wage is $16.66 USD (about 118.6 RMB). 合同工普遍认为，此次外包更多是出于成本考量 —— 巴西月最低工资折合约 245 美元（约合 1744 元人民币），时薪约 1.53 美元（不到 11 元人民币），而华盛顿州最低时薪为 16.66 美元（约合 118.6 元人民币）。
