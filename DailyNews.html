DeepSeek 论文发了又删，「给 AI 装上手指」的视觉推理方案提前曝光

Last night, DeepSeek published a new multimodal inference paper titled "Thinking with Visual Primitives," but the related tweet and GitHub page were deleted hours later. APPSO had previously read the entire paper. 昨晚，DeepSeek 发布多模态推理新论文《Thinking with Visual Primitives》，但数小时后相关推文与 GitHub 页面均被删除，APPSO 在此前读完了整篇论文。

🔗 Related Reading: What did DeepSeek's new paper, deleted overnight, actually say? 🔗 相关阅读： DeepSeek连夜删掉的新论文，到底说了什么

The paper points out that current multimodal large models have a "Reference Gap" in visual reasoning – the model can see the image clearly but cannot precisely point to specific objects within it during the reasoning process. 论文指出，当前多模态大模型在视觉推理上存在「引用鸿沟」（Reference Gap）—— 模型看清了图像，却无法在推理过程中精确指向其中的具体对象。

DeepSeek's solution is to have the model directly output image coordinates (bounding boxes or coordinate points) in its chain of thought, embedding the act of "pointing" into the reasoning process itself, rather than just outputting it as a final answer. Researchers liken this to the human cognitive method of "thinking while pointing." DeepSeek 的解法是让模型在思考链中直接输出图像坐标（边界框或坐标点），将「指」这一动作嵌入推理过程本身，而非仅作为最终答案输出，研究者将其类比为人类「边指边想」的认知方式。

In terms of efficiency, an image of the same size requires approximately 1100 tokens for Gemini-3-Flash, about 870 for Claude-Sonnet-4.6, and about 740 for GPT-5.4. DeepSeek uses only 90 information units, with all freed-up computational power dedicated to coordinate annotation during inference. 在效率上，同等尺寸图像 Gemini-3-Flash 需消耗约 1100 个 token，Claude-Sonnet-4.6 约 870 个，GPT-5.4 约 740 个，DeepSeek 仅用 90 个信息单元，腾出的算力全部用于推理中的坐标标注。

In the "maze navigation" benchmark test, DeepSeek leads with a 66.9% accuracy rate. For reference, GPT-5.4 achieved 50.6%, Gemini-3-Flash 49.4%, and Claude-Sonnet-4.6 48.9% (the random guess accuracy for this task is 50%, meaning the latter three are close to random levels); 在「迷宫导航」基准测试中，DeepSeek 以 66.9% 的正确率领跑，作为参考，GPT-5.4 50.6%，Gemini-3-Flash 49.4%，Claude-Sonnet-4.6 48.9%（该任务随机猜测正确率为 50%，后三者接近随机水平）；

The paper also acknowledges existing limitations: coordinate accuracy is still insufficient in fine-grained scenarios (directly reflected in "counting fingers" failures); visual primitive mode requires specific trigger words for activation, and the model cannot yet autonomously determine when to use it; topological reasoning has limited generalization ability outside the training distribution. 论文也坦承了现有局限：精细场景下坐标精度仍不足（数手指翻车即为直接体现）；视觉原语模式需特定触发词激活，模型尚不能自主判断何时使用；拓扑推理在训练分布外的泛化能力有限。

三星 Q1 芯片利润暴涨 49 倍，单部门吃掉全集团 94% 营业利润

According to Reuters and CNBC, Samsung Electronics achieved revenue of 133.9 trillion Korean Won (approximately $90 billion) in Q1 this year, a year-over-year increase of approximately 69%; operating profit reached 57.2 trillion Korean Won, an increase of approximately 8.5 times year-over-year, setting a new historical high and exceeding analysts' expectations of 55.28 trillion Korean Won. 据路透社、CNBC 报道，三星电子今年 Q1 实现营收 133.9 万亿韩元（约 900 亿美元），同比增长约 69%；营业利润 57.2 万亿韩元，同比增长约 8.5 倍，创历史新高，超出分析师预期的 55.28 万亿韩元。

The DS (Device Solutions, including memory chips and foundry) division's Q1 revenue was 81.7 trillion Korean Won, a significant year-over-year increase of 225%; operating profit was 53.7 trillion Korean Won, compared to only about 1.1 trillion Korean Won in the same period last year, an increase of approximately 49 times, accounting for 94% of the group's total operating profit , almost squeezing out the profit margins of other divisions. DS（Device Solutions，含存储芯片及晶圆代工）部门 Q1 营收 81.7 万亿韩元，同比大增 225%；营业利润 53.7 万亿韩元，去年同期仅约 1.1 万亿韩元，增幅约 49 倍， 占集团整体营业利润的 94% ，几乎榨干其他部门的利润空间。

In contrast, during the same period last year, the MX (Mobile eXperience, including mobile phones and networks) division alone supported 64% of the group's profit with 4.3 trillion Korean Won, while the DS division accounted for only 16%. In Q1 this year, the MX division's operating profit plummeted to 2.8 trillion Korean Won, a 35% year-over-year decrease; the display division's operating profit also fell by 20% to 400 billion Korean Won. 反观去年同期，MX（手机及网络）部门以 4.3 万亿韩元独撑集团利润的 64%，DS 部门占比仅 16%。今年 Q1，MX 部门营业利润骤降至 2.8 万亿韩元，同比下滑 35%；显示屏部门营业利润亦下跌 20% 至 4000 亿韩元。

The boom in AI data center construction is the core driving force this quarter. Samsung stated frankly on its earnings call that "demand fulfillment rate has fallen to a historical low," and customers are locking in next year's orders in advance due to concerns about supply shortages, with the supply-demand gap expected to widen further next year. AI 数据中心建设热潮是本季核心驱动力。三星在财报电话会上直言「需求 满足率 已跌至历史最低」，客户因担忧供货短缺而提前锁定明年订单，供需缺口预计在明年进一步拉大。

Samsung also disclosed that it had achieved mass production of HBM4 chips in February this year and shipped them to Nvidia's Vera Rubin platform. This year's HBM revenue target is to more than triple year-over-year, as it accelerates its efforts to catch up with SK Hynix's leading position in the HBM market. 三星同时披露，今年 2 月已率先实现 HBM4 芯片量产并向英伟达 Vera Rubin 平台出货，今年 HBM 营收目标为同比增长逾三倍，正加速追赶 SK 海力士在 HBM 市场的领先地位。

D 加密反制破解出新招，正版玩家被迫每 14 天联网「打卡」

Recently, to counter cracking, Denuvo (D encryption) and 2K Games have jointly implemented stricter licensing restrictions on several of their games, sparking a strong backlash within the player community. 近期，为应对破解，Denuvo（D 加密）与 2K Games 联合对旗下多款游戏实施了更严格的授权限制，在玩家社群中引发强烈反弹。

Affected games include "NBA 2K25," "NBA 2K26," and "Marvel's Midnight Suns." These games now use offline authorization tokens with a fixed validity period, which will automatically expire after approximately 14 days. 受影响的游戏包括《NBA 2K25》《NBA 2K26》及《漫威暗夜之子》。这些游戏现已启用固定有效期的离线授权令牌，令牌将在约 14 天后自动失效。

Regardless of whether players change hardware or reinstall the system, once the token expires, the game will be unable to launch directly, and players must connect to the internet to re-acquire authorization to continue playing. 无论玩家是否更换硬件或重装系统，一旦令牌到期，游戏将直接无法启动，必须联网重新获取授权才能继续游玩。

Earlier this year, cracking groups MKDev and DenuvOwO developed a Hypervisor-based bypass solution (HVB), which intercepts and simulates Denuvo's verification requests by installing kernel-level drivers, leading to almost all Denuvo-protected single-player games being cracked or bypassed. 今年早些时候，破解组织 MKDev 和 DenuvOwO 开发出基于 Hypervisor 的绕过方案（HVB），通过安装内核级驱动拦截并模拟 Denuvo 的验证请求，使得几乎所有受 Denuvo 保护的单机游戏均遭到破解或绕过。
