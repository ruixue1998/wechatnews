MacBook Neo 推高销量，苹果笔记本一季度线上市占升至 13%

Yesterday, research firm Lotu Technology released data on China's online omni-channel laptop sales for the first quarter of 2026, showing that online omni-channel retail sales of laptops in China were 2.3 million units, a year-on-year decrease of 19.2%; sales revenue was 14.8 billion yuan, a year-on-year decrease of 14.7%. 昨天，研究机构洛图科技发布 2026 年第一季度中国笔记本电脑线上全渠道销量数据显示，中国笔记本电脑线上全渠道零售销量为 230 万台，同比下降 19.2%；销额为 148 亿元，同比下降 14.7%。

Apple was the biggest highlight this quarter, with its sales share on mainstream online e-commerce platforms climbing to 13%, an increase of about 6 percentage points from the same period last year. The research report believes that the immediate hot sales of the MacBook Neo were a key driver for this market share jump. 苹果是本季度最大亮点，其在线上主流电商平台的销量份额攀升至 13%，较去年同期提升约 6 个百分点。研报认为，MacBook Neo 上市即热销，是份额跃升的关键推手。

曝苹果探索将 AI Agent 引入 App Store

According to The Information, Apple is exploring ways to introduce AI agents into the App Store, aiming to embrace industry trends while ensuring platform rules are maintained. 据《The Information》报道，苹果正在探索将 AI 智能体引入 App Store 的方案，以在把握行业趋势的同时，确保平台规则得到延续。

The report states that Apple's internal team is designing a new management mechanism, aiming to restrict AI agents' overstepping behavior, such as preventing them from unauthorized deletion of user emails, under the premise of ensuring privacy and security standards. 报道称，苹果内部团队正在设计一套新的管理机制，目标是在保障隐私与安全标准的前提下，约束智能体的越界行为，例如防止其擅自删除用户邮件等极端情形。

The commercial value of the App Store is a significant driving force behind Apple's push in this direction. According to app analytics firm Appfigures, Apple's global App Store commission revenue is estimated to reach approximately $50 billion this year, a year-on-year increase of about 20%. App Store 的商业价值是苹果推进这一方向的重要动因。据应用分析机构 Appfigures 估计，苹果今年 App Store 全球佣金收入约达 500 亿美元，同比增长约 20%。

Previously, Apple had repeatedly rejected 'vibe coding' tools from the App Store, citing policy violations. 此前，苹果已多次以违反政策为由，将「氛围编程」（vibe coding）工具拒于 App Store 之外。

李想：L4 无人驾驶预计 2028 至 2029 年落地，需近万 TOPS 算力

In the 27th issue of 'Luo Yonghao's Crossroads' released yesterday, Li Xiang, founder of Li Auto, shared several views on autonomous driving, the AI industry, and the future car market. He stated that L4 autonomous driving is not expected to truly be implemented until 2028 or 2029. 在昨天发布的《罗永浩的十字路口》第 27 期中，理想汽车创始人李想分享了多项关于自动驾驶、AI 行业及未来用车市场的看法。他表示，L4 级无人驾驶预计要到 2028 年至 2029 年才能真正落地。

L4 autonomous driving requires 3D ViT, pre-trained models of the physical world, control systems faster than humans, and basically nearly 10,000 TOPS of computing power, which is likely to be available around 2028 or 2029. L4 无人驾驶需要 3D ViT、物理世界的预训练模型、比人还快的控制系统，基本上得有近万 TOPS 的算力，大概要到 2028 年、2029 年。

Regarding whether the popularization of autonomous driving would reduce car purchasing demand, Li Xiang gave a negative answer. He drew an analogy between renting and buying a house: 针对无人驾驶普及后是否会减少购车需求的问题，李想给出了否定答案。他以租房与买房作类比：

Roads are limited, and there's no way to increase supply. When you need a car the most, the supply still won't be able to meet demand, because it's not determined by the supply of cars, but by the supply of roads. 道路是有限的，没有办法增加更多供给。在你最需要车的时候，供给仍然供不上，因为它不是由车的供给决定的，是由道路的供给决定的。

Li Xiang believes that people who are usually unwilling to hire a full-time driver and value travel privacy will be more inclined to purchase their own vehicles at that time. 李想认为，那些平时不愿雇专职司机、又在意出行隐私的人，届时将更倾向于自购车辆。

💡 李彦宏提出「日活智能体数 (DAA)」概念：未来全球 DAA 可能超过 100 亿

Yesterday, at the Create2026 Baidu AI Developer Conference, Baidu founder Robin Li proposed a new 'metric' for the AI era — DAA (Daily Active Agents), and predicted that global DAA might exceed 10 billion in the future. 昨天，在 Create2026 百度 AI 开发者大会上，百度创始人李彦宏提出 AI 时代的新「度量衡」—— DAA（Daily Active Agents，日活智能体数），并预测未来全球 DAA 可能超过 100 亿。

Robin Li believes that tokens measure input and cost, but do not represent output and revenue, and do not necessarily represent the ultimate outcome; while DAA roughly corresponds to DAU (Daily Active Users) commonly used in the mobile internet era. 李彦宏认为，Token 衡量的是投入与成本，并不代表产出与收益，不一定代表终局；而 DAA 大致对应移动互联网时代通用的 DAU（日活用户数）。

To measure the prosperity of a platform and ecosystem, one should look at the DAA metric, focusing on how many Agents are working for humans and delivering results. This is closer to value and essence than meaningless token consumption. 衡量一个平台和生态的繁荣，更应该看的是 DAA 这个指标，关注有多少 Agent 在给人类干活，并交付结果。这比无谓的 Token 消耗，更接近价值，也更接近本质。

Robin Li judged that the emergence of agents signifies that the development of AI is moving from the model stage to the application stage. 'For the first time, the protagonist of AI is not the model, but the application.' He views Chatbots and Agents as two types of entry points: 李彦宏判断，智能体出圈代表着 AI 的发展正从模型阶段走向应用阶段，「第一次，AI 的主角不是模型，而是应用」。他将 Chatbot 与 Agent 视为两类入口：

Chatbots represented by ChatGPT primarily solve 'information acquisition' problems, while Agents solve 'task completion' problems. The more complex the task and the higher its value, the higher the value ceiling for Agents compared to Chatbots. 以 ChatGPT 为代表的 Chatbot 主要解决「信息获取」问题，而 Agent 解决的是「任务完成」问题，任务越复杂、价值越高，Agent 的价值天花板高于 Chatbot。
