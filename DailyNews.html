小米 Vision GT 概念超跑亮相巴塞罗那

Last night, Xiaomi held the global launch event for the Xiaomi 17 Series in Barcelona, Spain. The Xiaomi Vision GT two-door supercar concept car officially debuted, becoming the most visually impactful "One more thing" of the entire conference. 昨晚，小米在西班牙巴塞罗那举行 Xiaomi 17 Series 全球发布会，小米 Vision GT 双门超跑概念车正式亮相，成为整场发布会最具视觉冲击力的「One more thing」。

This Vision GT features an ultra-low, wide-body design, with body lines emphasizing aerodynamic efficiency. It is equipped with a central cockpit, scissor doors, a massive carbon fiber rear wing, and circular star-ring taillights, making its overall shape closer to a track-level prototype car. 这款 Vision GT 采用极致低趴宽体设计，车身线条强调空气动力学效率，配备中置驾驶舱、剪刀门、巨型碳纤维尾翼与环形星环尾灯，整体造型更接近赛道级原型车。

Xiaomi stated that the car combines an extremely low drag coefficient with powerful downforce, aiming to maximize performance without relying on additional aerodynamic kits. Li Tianyuan, Xiaomi's chief designer, stated that "less is more" is the core philosophy, with almost every exterior detail serving an aerodynamic function. 小米方面表示，该车兼具极低风阻系数与强大下压力，目标是在不依赖额外空气动力学套件的前提下实现性能最大化。小米首席设计师李田原称「少即是多」是核心理念，几乎每一处外观都承担空气动力学功能。

The cockpit continues Xiaomi's smart cockpit design language, featuring a panoramic sky screen display and supporting human-car-home connectivity. The center console retains physical buttons, including a circular pointer-style air conditioning knob and an aircraft thrust lever-style gear selector. 座舱部分延续小米智能座舱设计语言，搭载全景显示天际屏，支持人车家联动。中控岛保留实体按键，包括圆形指针式空调旋钮与飞机推杆式档把。

It is reported that the car will be available in "Gran Turismo 7" in the future, allowing PS5 players to experience this concept electric supercar in the game. 据悉，该车未来将登陆《GT 赛车 7》，PS5 玩家可在游戏中体验这款概念级电动超跑。

The Xiaomi 17 Ultra overseas Leica version LEITZPHONE was also released. This model adjusted the Leica Logo arrangement and frame design in terms of appearance details, and the battery capacity was adjusted from 6800mAh in the domestic version to 6000mAh. It also supports imaging control designs such as the Master Zoom Ring, but the price has significantly increased to 1999 Euros (approximately 16,000 RMB) . 小米 17 Ultra 海外徕卡版 LEITZPHONE 同步发布。该机型在外观细节上调整了徕卡 Logo 排列方式与边框设计，电池容量从国内版的 6800mAh 调整为 6000mAh，同样支持大师变焦环等影像操控设计， 但售价大幅提升至 1999 欧元（约合 1.6 万元人民币） 。

The global version of the Xiaomi 17 series, starting at 999 Euros, as well as the Xiaomi Tag tracker and Xiaomi Electric Scooter 6 series, were also released at this conference. 本次发布会上还发布了小米 17 系列全球版，售价 999 欧元起，以及小米 Tag 追踪器和小米电动滑板车 6 系列。

不止手机，汽车或因存储芯片成本飙升涨价

According to a report by China Business News, the automotive industry is facing a new wave of storage chip price increases this year. Prices for automotive-grade DRAM and NAND Flash continue to climb, driving up overall vehicle costs, and some models have already shown signs of rising retail prices. 据第一财经报道，今年汽车行业正面临新一轮存储芯片涨价潮，车规级 DRAM 与 NAND Flash 价格持续攀升，带动整车成本上扬，部分车型已出现终端售价上涨迹象。

Storage chips used in automobiles mainly include DRAM and NAND Flash, with DRAM being the most scarce category currently. Relevant personnel from Beijing Ingenic Semiconductor stated that the supply of automotive DRAM chips will remain tight in 2026, with prices continuing to rise and no room for price reduction for now. 汽车使用的存储芯片主要包括 DRAM 与 NAND Flash，其中 DRAM 是当前最紧缺的品类。北京君正集成电路相关人士表示，2026 年汽车 DRAM 芯片供应依旧紧张，价格持续上涨，且暂无降价空间。

According to TrendForce data, prices for general-purpose DRAM are expected to rise by 55% to 60%, NAND Flash by 33% to 38%, and spot prices for high-end automotive-grade DDR5 even reaching a 300% increase. 根据 TrendForce 数据，通用型 DRAM 价格预计上涨 55%～60%，NAND Flash 上涨 33%～38%，高端车规级 DDR5 现货涨幅甚至达到 300%。

NIO CEO Li Bin, Xiaomi Group Chairman Lei Jun, and other senior executives from car manufacturers have publicly expressed concerns about rising memory prices. Lei Jun stated that memory prices are increasing quarterly, with a 40% to 50% increase last quarter, and will continue to rise this year. 蔚来 CEO 李斌、小米集团董事长雷军等多位车企高层已公开表达对内存涨价的担忧。雷军表示，内存价格按季度上涨，上季度涨幅达 40%～50%，今年仍将继续上涨。

A HSBC research report points out that rising storage chip prices could lead to an increase of 1000 to 3000 yuan in overall vehicle costs, with a more significant impact on electric vehicles. 汇丰研报指出，存储芯片涨价可能带来 1000～3000 元的整车成本提升，对电动车影响更为明显。

宇树科技王兴兴：希望未来实现「机器人生产机器人」

According to China Securities Journal, Wang Xingxing, founder and chairman of Unitree Robotics, recently stated at the Humanoid Robot and Embodied AI Standardization (HEIS) annual conference that the company has been continuously promoting the deployment of humanoid robots in factories over the past few years, and hopes to achieve "robots producing robots" in its own factories in the future. 据中证报报道，宇树科技创始人兼董事长王兴兴近日在人形机器人与具身智能标准化（HEIS）年会上表示，公司过去几年持续推动人形机器人在工厂落地，并希望未来能在自家工厂实现「机器人生产机器人」。

Wang Xingxing introduced that currently, when humanoid robots perform single tasks, if sufficiently trained, the success rate can basically reach 100%; however, there is still room for improvement in success rates when long-term task planning is involved. 王兴兴介绍，目前人形机器人在执行单一任务时，如果训练充分，成功率基本可以达到 100%；但在涉及长任务规划时，成功率仍有提升空间。

He emphasized that robot mobility is a "prerequisite for work." When the stability and richness of movement are high enough, the robot's replicability and stability will also increase. 他强调，机器人运动能力是「干活的先决必要条件」，当运动的稳定性与丰富度足够高，机器人的可复制性与稳定性也会随之增强。

Regarding industry standards, Wang Xingxing pointed out that robots need a complete standard system for key indicators such as efficiency, stability, and safety. 在行业标准方面，王兴兴指出，机器人在效率、稳定性、安全性等关键指标上都需要建立完整的标准体系。

He stated that Unitree Robotics hopes to actively participate in promoting the standardization of industry standards to foster healthy industry development. He believes that the robotics industry is still in its early stages, and healthy competition along with unified standards will help push the industry towards maturity. 他表示，宇树科技希望积极参与推动行业标准规范化，以促进行业健康发展。他认为，目前机器人行业仍处于早期阶段，良性竞争与统一标准将有助于推动产业走向成熟。

蔚来李斌获中国人工智能最高奖

Recently, the Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence announced the evaluation results for the 2025 "Wu Wenjun Artificial Intelligence Science and Technology Award." 近日，中国人工智能学会公布 2025 年度「吴文俊人工智能科学技术奖」评审结果。

The research achievement "Key Technologies and Industrial Applications of Spatiotemporal Cognition from Visual Understanding to World Models," primarily completed by Professor Ren Shaoqing of the University of Science and Technology of China, NIO CEO Li Bin, and others, received the first prize for scientific and technological progress. 由中国科学技术大学教授任少卿、蔚来 CEO 李斌等作为主要完成人的科研成果「从视觉理解到世界模型的时空认知关键技术及产业应用」，获得科技进步奖一等奖。

This award is hailed as the "highest award in Chinese AI," representing the highest honor in the field of intelligent science and technology in China. 该奖项被誉为「中国人工智能最高奖」，代表国内智能科学技术领域的最高荣誉。

The project addresses the key challenge of "spatiotemporal cognition," building a technical system from visual understanding to world models, achieving multiple original breakthroughs, including candidate box generation algorithms and region proposal network modules based on deep neural networks, end-to-end object detection paradigms, and world models with spatiotemporal cognitive abilities. 项目围绕「时空认知」关键难题，构建了从视觉理解到世界模型的技术体系，形成多项原创性突破，包括基于深度神经网络的候选框生成算法及区域提议网络模块、端到端目标检测范式，以及具备时空认知能力的世界模型。

NIO's official Weibo pointed out that the new version of the "NIO World Model," based on this award-winning achievement, was fully pushed to over 600,000 users on January 28 this year, becoming the first intelligent assisted driving system in China based on the "world model + closed-loop reinforcement learning" development paradigm. 蔚来官方微博指出，基于此次获奖成果的「蔚来世界模型」全新版本已于今年 1 月 28 日全量推送超 60 万用户，成为国内首个基于「世界模型 + 闭环强化学习」研发范式的智能辅助驾驶系统。

💡 亚马逊 CEO：AI 时代「堆人力」的岗位将大幅减少

According to Business Insider, Amazon CEO Andy Jassy recently stated that many jobs that relied on "piling up manpower" over the past two or three decades will no longer require the same scale of human involvement with the advancement of AI. 据《商业内幕》报道，亚马逊 CEO 安迪·贾西（Andy Jassy）近日表示，过去二三十年依赖「堆人力」完成的大量岗位，在 AI 的推动下将不再需要同等规模的人类参与。

He emphasized that this is not a simple disappearance of jobs, but a structural reorganization: 他强调，这并非简单的岗位消失，而是结构性重组：

AI will eliminate some repetitive, process-oriented jobs while also giving rise to new professional forms. For example, the role of a cloud solutions architect did not exist 15 years ago, but now there are tens of thousands of practitioners. AI 会淘汰部分重复性、流程化的工作，同时催生新的职业形态。例如云解决方案架构师这一岗位在 15 年前并不存在，如今已有数以万计从业者。
