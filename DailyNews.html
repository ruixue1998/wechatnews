OpenAI 发布 Sora 2：AI 视频进入「GPT-3.5 时刻」

OpenAI today officially released the new generation AI video generation model Sora 2, and simultaneously launched the Sora application for C-end users. OpenAI 今日正式发布全新一代 AI 视频生成模型 Sora 2，并同步上线面向 C 端用户的应用 Sora。

The official statement calls this the "GPT-3.5 moment" for AI video generation, marking the transition of the technology from the demonstration phase to large-scale application. 官方称这是 AI 视频生成的「GPT-3.5 时刻」，标志着技术从演示阶段迈入大规模应用阶段。

Sora 2 Core Upgrades: Sora 2 核心升级：

  • First-time realization of synchronized audio and video generation, capable of simultaneously generating visuals with matching ambient sounds, interactive sounds, and dialogue. 首次实现音视频同步生成，可同时生成画面与匹配的环境音、交互声及对话
  • Physical accuracy significantly improved, actions and fluid effects are more consistent with real-world dynamics. 物理精确性显著提升，动作与流体效果更符合真实世界动力学
  • Image resolution and detail realism enhanced, with more flexible style control capabilities. 图像分辨率与细节真实感增强，风格操控能力更灵活
  • Instruction consistency improved, with stronger controllability for complex scenes across shots. 指令一致性提高，跨镜头复杂场景的可控性更强

Sora App Highlight Features: Sora App 亮点功能：

  • “Cameo”: Users can seamlessly integrate their own image and voice into AI-generated scenes. 「客串 (Cameo)」：用户可将自身形象与声音无缝融入 AI 生成场景
  • “Remix”: Supports secondary creation or remixing of other people's videos to co-generate content. 「Remix」：支持对他人视频进行二次创作或混编，共同生成内容
  • All videos in the feed are AI-generated; users can directly modify prompts or add their own images. 信息流内所有视频均由 AI 生成，用户可直接修改提示词或加入自身形象

Currently, the Sora App has launched first in the United States and Canada, supporting iOS and web versions, and uses an invitation code mechanism. Sora 2 is initially free to use, and ChatGPT Pro users can prioritize experiencing the higher-quality Sora 2 Pro model. OpenAI also plans to release an API for developers. 目前，Sora App 已在美国、加拿大首发，支持 iOS 与网页版，采用邀请码机制。Sora 2 初期免费开放，ChatGPT Pro 用户可优先体验更高质量的 Sora 2 Pro 模型，OpenAI 还计划推出 API 供开发者使用。

罗永浩谈小米「小字」争议：各家都这样

Recently, Luo Yonghao discussed online criticisms regarding the small text in the bottom right corner of Xiaomi's "King of Backlight" poster during a livestream. 近日，罗永浩在直播中谈及网络上针对小米「逆光之王」海报中右下角小字的质疑。

He stated that the widely discussed practice is not unique to Xiaomi but rather a "bad habit" prevalent across the entire mobile phone industry, with various manufacturers adopting similar methods for a long time. 他表示，外界热议的相关做法并非小米独创，而是整个手机行业的「陋习」，各家厂商长期以来都在采用类似方式。

Luo Yonghao pointed out during the livestream: "Many people have been criticizing Xiaomi for this recently, but it's actually an industry 'bad habit' that all companies do. It's not unique to Xiaomi. The mobile phone industry has always done this; if you don't believe me, go check it yourselves." 罗永浩在直播中指出：「这两天很多人在拿这个黑小米，其实这个是行业陋习，各家都这么做。并不是小米独创的。手机行业其实也一直都这么搞，不信你们自己去查一下。」

He frankly stated that "this practice is not right," but it is unfair for public opinion to focus the issue solely on Xiaomi. 他直言「并不是说这样做对」，但舆论将问题集中到小米身上并不公平。

Recently, Wan Zhiqiang, CMO of Xingji Meizu Group, also posted on Weibo to express his views on this issue. 日前，星纪魅族集团 CMO 万志强也就此问题在微博发文表态。

He stated that companies add disclaimers to product promotions primarily to comply with regulatory requirements. 他表示，企业在产品宣传中加入备注说明，主要是为了遵循法规要求。

In fact, it's all about complying with regulations, and every company will have some disclaimers. However, Meizu internally emphasized at the time that while preserving the contextual meaning, supplementary qualifiers should be written as clearly as possible to avoid misleading people, as product philosophy includes values. 其实都是为了遵循法规，各家都会有一些备注说明。不过魅族内部当时特别强调了，保留语境语义前提下，补充限定词我们要写的尽量明显些，不要让人有被误导的感觉，产品观中包含价值观。

That afternoon, Luo Yonghao replied to netizens on Weibo, stating his hope that this incident would lead the industry to eliminate "misleading small print," but added that "non-misleading small or large print is sometimes still necessary." 当天下午，罗永浩在微博回复网友，表示希望经过本次事件，行业能够消灭「误导性的小字」，但「没有误导性的大小字，有时候还是必要的」。

Galaxy Ring 电池鼓包取不下来，受害者：再也不戴智能戒指了

Yesterday, overseas tech blogger Daniel (@ZONEofTECH) posted on X, stating that the Samsung Galaxy Ring he was wearing experienced battery swelling just before boarding, causing the ring to get stuck on his finger and become impossible to remove. 昨天，海外科技博主 Daniel（@ZONEofTECH）在 X 发帖，称其佩戴的三星 Galaxy Ring 在准备登机前出现电池鼓胀情况，导致戒指卡在手指上无法取下。

Due to the emergency, he was taken to the hospital for treatment, and the ring was finally removed with the help of ice packs and medical lubricant. 由于情况紧急，他被送往医院处理，最终通过冰敷与医疗润滑剂协助才将戒指移除。

Daniel stated: "My Galaxy Ring battery started swelling on my finger just as I was about to board the plane. The ring couldn't be removed and was very painful." Daniel 表示：「我的 Galaxy Ring 电池在手指上开始鼓胀，当时我正要登机，戒指无法取下并且非常疼痛。」

He added that the incident led to him being denied boarding, having to pay extra hotel fees, and delaying his return. Daniel captioned the photo: "You can see the battery is completely swollen. Never wearing a smart ring again." 他补充称，事故导致其被拒绝登机，不得不额外支付酒店费用，并推迟返程。Daniel 配图称：「你能看到电池全部鼓胀了。再也不戴智能戒指了。」

According to his speculation, possible reasons include "Hawaii's high temperatures, saltwater exposure, continuous flights," and "a defect in the battery itself." 据其推测，可能的原因包括「夏威夷的高温、盐水接触、连续乘机」以及「电池本身存在缺陷」。

He also pointed out that the device's battery life performance had been poor for the past few months. 他还指出，过去数月该设备的续航表现一直不佳。

Currently, Samsung has not yet made a public statement regarding this incident. 目前，三星方面尚未就此事件作出公开回应。

Daniel emphasized: "Any device with a battery that needs to be worn close to the body should be designed to be very easy to remove, to prevent battery swelling." Daniel 强调：「任何带电池且需要贴身佩戴的设备，都应该设计得非常容易取下，以防电池膨胀。」

法拉第未来总部子办公楼失火，官方回应：与量产车无关

Yesterday, Faraday Future officially posted an article on Weibo stating that in the early morning of September 28th, US Western Time, a fire broke out in a sub-office building at Faraday Future's US headquarters. 昨天，法拉第未来官方在微博发表文章称，美西时间 9 月 28 日凌晨，Faraday Future 美国总部一栋子办公楼发生火情。

The company emphasized in its statement that the incident caused no casualties, and headquarters operations are normal. 公司在说明中强调，此次事件未造成人员伤亡，总部运营一切正常。

The official disclosure includes: 官方披露的情况包括：

  • The ignition point was an FF 91 display vehicle inside the building; the office building's walls suffered minor damage, and the glass remained intact. 起火点位于楼内一台 FF 91 展车，办公楼墙面轻微受损，玻璃完好；
  • Preliminary findings rule out the possibility of battery pack fire or explosion; the most likely causes are a short circuit in the showroom's electrical system or poor contact in the 12V low-voltage wiring. 初步排除电池包起火或爆炸的可能，最可能原因是展厅电路短路或 12V 低压线路接触不良；
  • The vehicle involved, codenamed B40, is one of the earliest BETA prototypes of the FF 91, having been in use for nearly 9 years. Both its powertrain and battery pack are early versions, and the interior materials do not meet the flame retardant standards for production vehicles. 涉事车辆代号为 B40，为 FF 91 最早的 BETA 样车之一，已使用近 9 年，动力系统和电池包均为早期版本，内饰材料也不符合量产车阻燃标准。

The company emphasized that the accident vehicle is completely different from production vehicles, and therefore will not trigger a recall of FF 91 production vehicles. 公司强调，事故车辆与量产车完全不同，因此不会引发 FF 91 量产车召回。

The official statement said: "Safety is always the primary requirement. This incident serves as a wake-up call for fire prevention in office areas. The company will complete the final investigation as soon as possible and implement necessary improvement measures." 官方表示：「安全始终是第一要求，此次事故为办公区火灾防范敲响警钟。公司将尽快完成最终调查，并采取必要改进措施。」

宇树科技回应机器人安全漏洞：大部分修复已完成

Media reports indicate that "IEEE Spectrum" recently disclosed critical vulnerabilities in several models of Unitree Robotics robots when configuring Wi-Fi interfaces via BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy), which attackers could exploit to gain root access. 媒体报道指出，「IEEE Spectrum」近日披露多个型号的宇树科技机器人在通过 BLE（蓝牙低能耗）配置 Wi-Fi 接口时存在关键漏洞，攻击者可能借此获取最高权限控制。

In response, Unitree Robotics issued a statement on social media, saying that the company immediately initiated repairs, and most issues have now been resolved, with subsequent updates to be pushed out soon. 对此，宇树科技在社交平台发布声明称，公司已第一时间展开修复，目前大部分问题已得到解决，后续更新将很快推送。

Unitree Robotics emphasized that its robot products are designed for offline use by default and do not actively connect to the internet; devices only access the network when manually configured and authorized by the user. 宇树科技强调，其机器人产品在默认情况下设计为离线使用，不会主动连接互联网；只有在用户手动配置并授权时，设备才会接入网络。

After connecting, robots may send basic information such as serial numbers and health status to the server, a mechanism similar to electronic devices like smartphones. 连接后，机器人可能会向服务器发送序列号、健康状态等基础信息，这一机制与智能手机等电子设备类似。

The company also stated that it will continue to optimize its permission management mechanism to reduce potential misunderstandings and enhance user security experience. 公司同时表示，将持续优化权限管理机制，以减少潜在误解并提升用户安全体验。

全尺寸人形机器人 Oli 成为「赛博球童」：自主完成捡球与深蹲动作

Recently, the full-size humanoid robot Oli demonstrated its new "cyber caddy" capabilities. The robot can autonomously perform actions such as tennis ball recognition, tracking, picking up, and placing, while also achieving complex postures like walking, bending, and deep squatting. 近日，全尺寸人形机器人 Oli 展示了「赛博球童」的全新能力。该机器人能够全自主完成网球识别、跟踪、捡球、放置等动作，并在过程中实现行走、弯腰、深蹲等复杂姿态。

The film's plot has not yet been revealed, but the official channels released an iconic pink donut poster on social media with the tagline "Homer's gonna do it again," sparking heated discussion among fans. 影片剧情暂未公开，但官方通过社交媒体释出标志性的粉色甜甜圈海报，并配上标语「荷马要再来一次」，引发粉丝热议。

《震耳欲聋》发布新预告

The movie "Deafening" released its "Am I Wrong" trailer today. The film takes the growth experience of "gray area lawyer" Li Qi as its main storyline, deeply revealing the inherent trauma and real-world dilemmas brought by the CODA identity. 电影《震耳欲聋》今日发布「我有错吗」预告，影片以「灰度律师」李淇的成长经历为主线，深刻揭示了 CODA 身份带来的原生创伤与现实困境。

The film tells the story of Li Qi, who gradually finds himself at a crossroads of desire and morality after becoming involved in a deaf anti-fraud case. Although he strongly resisted being associated with his "deaf" identity, he revealed his true feelings after a drink: "From the day I was born, I spoke every day, but no one could hear me." This line strikes a chord, reflecting the complex situation of the CODA community. 影片讲述李淇因卷入聋人反诈案而逐渐走向欲望与道德的十字路口。虽然他极力抗拒与「聋人」身份的关联，却在一次酒后吐露心声：「从我出生那天开始，我每天都说话，没有人听得见。」这一台词直击人心，折射出 CODA 群体的复杂处境。

According to the official introduction, the film is partly based on the true experiences of CODA lawyer Zhang Qi. Director Wan Li stated that he hopes to convey the value of "being your own hero" through Li Qi's story, allowing the audience to find resonance and courage in the character's struggles and redemption. 官方介绍称，本片部分取材自 CODA 律师张琪的真实经历。导演万力表示，希望通过李淇的故事传递「做自己的英雄」的价值观，让观众在角色的挣扎与救赎中获得共鸣与勇气。

"Deafening" stars Tan Jianci, Lan Xiya, and Wang Ge, with Wang Yanhui and Chi Peng making special appearances. The film will have nationwide sneak previews from October 2nd to 3rd and officially premiere on October 4th. 《震耳欲聋》由檀健次、兰西雅、王戈领衔主演，王砚辉、迟蓬特邀出演，影片将于 10 月 2 日至 3 日进行全国点映，并于 10 月 4 日正式上映。

《浪浪人生》今日上映

The movie "Wild Life" officially landed in cinemas nationwide yesterday. 电影《浪浪人生》昨天正式登陆全国院线。

The film is produced by Han Han, directed by Ma Lin, and stars Huang Bo, Fan Chengcheng, and Yin Tao, with Chang Yuan, Li Jiaqi, Liu Xuehua, Fu Hang, and others in supporting roles. It tells the story of a family fighting "wildly" to protect their home amidst adversity. 影片由韩寒监制、马林执导，黄渤、范丞丞、殷桃领衔主演，常远、李嘉琦、刘雪华、付航等参演，讲述一家人在逆境中「疯浪」搏命守家的故事。

The film is adapted from Cai Chongda's bestselling book "The Skin," based on real-life stories and integrating elements of comedy, family, and drama. 影片改编自蔡崇达畅销书《皮囊》，以真人真事为创作基础，融合喜剧、家庭与剧情元素。

During its sneak preview period, the film's reputation continued to rise, with a Maoyan preview score of 9.5 and a recommendation rate of 98% from influential users. Many viewers commented, "It has both laughter and tears, and the meaning of home is perfectly conveyed." 该片点映期间口碑持续走高，猫眼点映评分 9.5，大 V 推荐度高达 98%。不少观众表示「笑点与泪点并存，家的意义被诠释得淋漓尽致」。

Currently, "Wild Life" is a hit nationwide, coinciding with the National Day and Mid-Autumn Festival holiday, making it a popular choice for family viewing. 目前，《浪浪人生》正在全国热映中，适逢国庆中秋双节档期，被视为合家欢观影的热门之选。
