北京大学团队研制新型模拟计算芯片，精度突破至 24 位

According to Xinhua News Agency, a research team from Peking University recently announced the successful development of a new type of analog computing chip. 据新华社报道，北京大学科研团队近日宣布成功研制出一种新型模拟计算芯片。

According to reports, the chip is based on resistive random-access memory (RRAM), and for the first time, it has increased the precision of analog computing to 24-bit fixed-point precision, significantly reducing energy consumption while greatly improving computing efficiency, providing a new path for breakthroughs in computing power in fields such as artificial intelligence. 据介绍，该芯片基于阻变存储器，首次将模拟计算的精度提升至 24 位定点精度，在显著降低能耗的同时大幅提升计算效率，为人工智能等领域的算力突破提供了全新路径。

Sun Zhong, head of the research team, introduced that analog computing differs from traditional digital computing. It no longer relies on the binary translation of '0' and '1' but directly uses continuous physical quantities like voltage and current for analog calculations. 研究团队负责人孙仲介绍，模拟计算不同于传统数字计算，不再依赖「0」和「1」的二进制转译，而是直接利用电压、电流等连续物理量进行类比计算。

This method not only inherently offers advantages of low power consumption, low latency, and high parallelism but also shows immense potential in high-precision tasks such as solving matrix equations. 这种方式不仅天然具备低功耗、低延迟和高并行的优势，还能在矩阵方程求解等高精度任务中展现出巨大潜力。

This achievement is attributed to synergistic innovations in three aspects: devices, circuits, and algorithms. Programmable resistive memory achieved 'in-memory computing,' original circuit design broke through the challenge of one-step matrix equation solving, and 'bit slicing' and 'iterative optimization' algorithms improved accuracy from 1% to 24-bit fixed-point level. 此次成果得益于器件、电路与算法三方面的协同创新：可编程阻变存储器实现了「存算一体」，原创电路设计突破了矩阵方程一步求解的难题，而「位切片」与「迭代优化」算法则将精度从 1% 提升至 24 位定点水平。
