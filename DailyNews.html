阿尔忒弥斯 2 号成功发射，人类时隔 54 年重返月球轨道

According to CBC News and BNN Bloomberg, today, NASA's Artemis II mission successfully launched from Kennedy Space Center in Florida. 据 CBC News 及 BNN Bloomberg 报道，今天，NASA 阿尔忒弥斯 2 号（Artemis II）任务从佛罗里达州肯尼迪航天中心成功发射升空。

This marks humanity's first return to lunar orbit since Apollo 17 in 1972, and the first crewed flight of the "Artemis Program" since its inception in 2017. 这是自 1972 年阿波罗 17 号以来，人类首次重返月球轨道，也是「阿尔忒弥斯计划」自 2017 年启动以来的首次载人飞行。

During the mission, the crew will conduct comprehensive tests on the spacecraft's critical life support systems, including air, water, safety equipment, and will for the first time test a new generation waste management system in a deep space environment. 任务期间，机组将对飞船的关键生命支持系统进行全面测试，包括空气、水、安全设备，以及首次在深空环境下测试新一代废物处理系统。

Mike Greenley, CEO of Canada's MDA Space, stated that this is a significant moment that 'opens a new phase for the Canadian economy.' As a technical contribution to the Artemis program, Canada has gained opportunities for lunar science experiments, technology demonstration rights, and two astronaut flight seats. 加拿大 MDA Space 公司 CEO 迈克·格林利（Mike Greenley）表示，这是「开启加拿大经济新阶段」的重要时刻。作为对阿尔忒弥斯计划的技术贡献，加拿大由此获得了月球科学实验机会、技术演示权益，以及两个宇航员飞行席位。

According to data from The Planetary Society, the SLS rocket has cost approximately $23.8 billion since its inception in 2011, and the Orion spacecraft's development cost reached $20.4 billion. NASA's total expenditure on the entire program from 2006 to its first test launch in 2022 amounted to $49.9 billion. 根据行星协会数据，SLS 火箭自 2011 年立项以来已耗资约 238 亿美元，猎户座飞船研发成本达 204 亿美元，NASA 在整个计划上从 2006 年至 2022 年首次测试发射期间的总支出高达 499 亿美元。

Greenley also stated that the company has deployed related technology on Mars probes for over 15 years and is currently designing a new type of Mars rover for the European Space Agency. 格林利还表示，该公司已在火星探测器上部署相关技术超过 15 年，目前正在为欧洲航天局设计新型火星探测车。

Greenley stated that all lunar activities are ultimately preparations for human landings on Mars—'The lunar program is not just for the Moon itself, but to solve all problems so that we can go further to Mars in the future.' 格林利表示，所有月球活动最终均是为人类登陆火星做准备——「月球计划不仅是为了月球本身，更是为了解决一切问题，以便未来更远地前往火星。」

Claude Code 高危漏洞曝光：clone 一个项目，摄像头和密码就被拿走

Security researcher Jack Cui recently publicly tested and demonstrated a high-risk security vulnerability in Claude Code, revealing severe risks in AI programming tools at the system permission level. 安全研究者 Jack Cui 近日公开实测并演示了 Claude Code 中一个高危安全漏洞，揭示 AI 编程工具在系统权限层面存在的严峻风险。

Jack Cui's demo project only contained a .claude configuration folder and an empty Python script. After typing 'claude' and pressing Enter in the terminal, the computer's camera was silently activated, and local key information was automatically written to a text file, with zero interaction and zero prompts throughout the entire process. Jack Cui 的演示项目仅包含一个 .claude 配置文件夹和一个空的 Python 脚本。在终端输入 claude 并回车后，电脑摄像头被静默调起、本地密钥信息被自动写入文本文件，整个过程零交互、零提示。

The problem stems from Claude Code's built-in 'hooks' mechanism, which allows developers to define automated scripts in the .claude/settings.json configuration file within the project directory. These scripts are then silently executed when the user runs the 'claude' command, without any confirmation prompts. 问题源自 Claude Code 内置的「hooks」机制，允许开发者在项目目录下的 .claude/settings.json 配置文件中定义自动化脚本，并在用户运行 claude 命令时静默执行，全程不弹出任何确认提示。

In addition to poisoning hooks configurations, attackers can also configure malicious MCP servers via .mcp.json files to automatically connect to external tools, bypassing user approval; or define malicious hooks in the frontmatter area of skill plugins, triggering them when sub-agents execute tasks. 除 hooks 配置投毒外，攻击者还可通过 .mcp.json 文件配置恶意 MCP 服务器，绕过用户审批自动连接外部工具；或通过 skill 插件的 frontmatter 区域定义恶意 hooks，在子代理执行任务时触发。

Currently, Anthropic's official source has pushed a patched version. Developers using Claude Code should immediately run `npm install @anthropic-ai/claude-code@latest` to update to the latest version, and should also be cautious about cloning unfamiliar open-source projects containing a `.claude` directory recently. 目前，Anthropic 官方源已推送修复版本。使用 Claude Code 的开发者应立即执行 npm install @anthropic-ai/claude-code@latest 更新至最新版，近期还应谨慎 clone 包含 .claude 目录的陌生开源项目。

广州早茶新规正式公布：茶位费必须配茶、传统点心须 24 小时内现制

According to Yangcheng Evening News, Guangzhou City officially announced the "Guangzhou Morning Tea Inheritance Protection Regulations," which will come into effect on May 1st this year. 据羊城晚报报道，广州市正式公布《广州早茶传承保护规定》，该规定将于今年 5 月 1 日起施行。

The "Regulations" for the first time clarified the legal definition of Guangzhou morning tea, defining it as a food and beverage consumption pattern that originated, developed, and is popular in the Guangzhou region, conforms to the characteristics of intangible cultural heritage, takes tea drinking paired with Cantonese dim sum as its core form, integrates Lingnan food culture, social customs, and traditional techniques, and possesses unique regional identification. 该《规定》首次明确了广州早茶的法律定义，将其界定为产生、发展并流行于广州地区、符合非物质文化遗产特征，以饮茶搭配广式点心为核心形式，融合岭南饮食文化、社交习俗与传统技艺的具有独特地域标识性的饮食消费形态。

Regarding the identification of production methods, the regulations define "handmade, freshly made and sold, not fresh, not eaten" as the core boundary distinguishing traditional from non-traditional production, requiring that morning tea food prepared on-site in a traditional manner must not exceed 24 hours from preparation to serving . 在制作方式的认定上，规定以「手工现做现售、不鲜不食」作为区分传统与非传统制作的核心界限，要求 以传统方式现场制作的早茶食品，从制成到提供食用的时间不得超过 24 小时 。

Operators must also clearly indicate to consumers on menus or other prominent locations whether food is "traditionally prepared on-site" or "non-traditionally prepared." The two preparation methods for the same category of items may have different prices. Furthermore, the regulations require operators to display the dim sum processing procedures to consumers through transparent, open, or video-monitored kitchens. 经营者还须在菜单或其他显著位置向消费者明示食品属于「传统方式现场制作」还是「非传统方式制作」，同一品类的两种制作方式可执行不同价格。此外，规定要求经营者通过透明式、开放式或视频监控式厨房等形式向消费者展示点心加工过程。

Regarding the long-standing consumer concern about tea charges, the regulations stipulate that operators charging tea fees must provide options such as black tea, green tea, oolong tea, Pu-erh tea, and chrysanthemum tea for consumers to choose from, and provide corresponding tea services; 针对消费者长期关注的茶位费问题，规定明确：收取茶位费的经营者必须提供红茶、绿茶、乌龙茶、普洱茶、菊花茶等供消费者选择，并做好相应茶饮服务；

All charged items must be clearly and prominently priced. The pricing content displayed on in-store menus, price tags, self-ordering applications, and other channels must remain consistent. For illegal acts of failing to clearly mark prices as required, the regulations set corresponding legal liabilities. 所有收费项目须以显著、清晰方式明码标价，店内菜单、价签、自助点餐应用程序等渠道显示的标价内容须保持一致。对于未按规定明码标价的违法行为，规定设置了相应法律责任。

央视起底「进口」保健品乱象：澳洲工厂地址是汽修站，品牌故事是编的

According to CCTV News, "Aussie Health," which ranks first in sales of lutein products on Douyin and Tmall, is actually a domestic product. The Australian production address marked on its product packaging was found by reporters to be a car repair shop after on-site verification. 据央视新闻报道，在抖音、天猫叶黄素品类销量均位居第一的「澳洲优思益」，实为国产产品。其产品包装上标注的澳大利亚生产地址，经记者实地核查，实际上是一家汽车维修站。

Mr. Lu, the head of Hangzhou Suoxiang Marketing Planning Co., Ltd., which is responsible for the product's marketing planning, frankly told reporters: "This is a company in Guangzhou, and the product is also from Guangzhou; we just packaged it as Australian." 负责该产品营销策划的杭州索象营销策划有限公司负责人卢总对记者直言：「这是广州的一家公司，产品也是广州的，只是我们把它包装成了澳洲的。」

Furthermore, the "Aussie Health" product packaging lacks the "blue hat" label required for health food products. Customer service on both Tmall and Douyin platforms refused to provide consumers with product origin and producer information, citing "merchant information protection." Currently, both "Aussie Health" Douyin and Tmall overseas flagship stores have suspended operations. 此外，「澳洲优思益」产品包装上并无保健食品须具备的「蓝帽子」标识，天猫、抖音两大平台客服亦均以「商家信息保护」为由，拒绝向消费者提供产品产地和生产者信息。目前，「优思益」抖音、天猫海外旗舰店两家店铺均已暂停营业。

In response to the aforementioned reports, the official "Aussie Health" WeChat account issued a statement yesterday. 针对上述报道，「优思益」官方公众号于昨日发布声明回应。

The statement said that the Melbourne, Australia address mentioned in the report is the registered address of the brand owner, YARRA VIBE PTY LTD, which is the brand's own property, was previously used as an office space, and was marked as the brand's postal address (POSTAL ADDRESS) on the official website of the Australian Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA). 声明称，报道中提及的澳大利亚墨尔本地址，系品牌持有人 YARRA VIBE PTY LTD 的注册地址，属品牌方自有物业，曾作为办公场所使用，并经澳大利亚治疗用品管理局（TGA）官网标注为品牌通讯地址（POSTAL ADDRESS）。

The statement explained that due to business adjustments, the address has ceased to be used, and the brand owner has subleased the property to a car repair shop. The registration and update procedures for the new office address are currently being processed. 声明解释，因业务调整，该地址已停止使用，品牌方将该物业转租给一家汽修厂，新办公地址的注册更新手续正在办理中。

Regarding the product's origin, the statement admitted that its first-generation iron drink product was previously authorized for production by a factory of a domestic listed nutrition company, and stated that "its domestic production-related information has been fully and publicly explained to consumers through various channels such as product detail pages and customer service inquiries." 在产品产地问题上，声明承认旗下第一代铁饮产品曾授权国内营养品上市企业工厂生产，并表示「其国内生产相关信息已通过产品详情页、客服咨询等多种渠道，向消费者进行了充分、公开的说明」。

The statement also indicated that, starting from 2025, the production of this product has been transferred to a New Zealand factory. 声明同时表示，自 2025 年起，该产品的生产已转移至新西兰工厂。

The statement also said that the company has delisted all relevant products and suspended all external sales channels, will fully cooperate with the investigation by relevant departments, and promised to disclose the progress of the investigation and self-inspection results at an appropriate time. 声明还表示，公司已下架全部相关商品并暂停所有对外销售渠道，将全力配合有关部门调查，并承诺在合适时机公开调查进展及自查结果。

曝迪士尼高层有意收购 Epic Games，正在等待时机

According to IGN and the Los Angeles Times, Disney executives are internally discussing the possibility of a full acquisition of Epic Games, but are currently waiting for the right moment. 据 IGN 和《洛杉矶时报》报道，迪士尼高层内部正在讨论全面收购 Epic Games 的可能性，但目前仍在等待合适时机。
