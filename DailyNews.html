OpenAI 前 CTO 创业的首个成果公布

Yesterday, Thinking Machines Lab (TML), founded by several former OpenAI executives, finally released its first major research paper, "Defeating Nondeterminism in LLM Inference," half a year after its establishment. 昨天，由多位 OpenAI 前高管创立的 Thinking Machines Lab（TML），终于在成立半年后，发布了其首篇重磅研究论文《Defeating Nondeterminism in LLM Inference》（克服 LLM 推理中的不确定性）。

According to TML, it will frequently publish blog posts, open-source code, and various research findings in the future, both to "benefit the public" and to "optimize our own research culture." Its first paper serves as the inaugural work for the new blog series "Connectionism." 据 TML 介绍，未来将频繁发布博客文章、开源代码以及各种研究成果，既是「造福公众」，也是为了「优化我们自己的研究文化」。而其首篇论文则作为全新博客系列《Connectionism》的开山之作。

Previously, it was common to see that AI models like ChatGPT often gave different answers each time, a phenomenon that had long been taken for granted—everyone believed that current AI models are probabilistic models. TML, however, is attempting to solve this long-standing problem with its latest achievement. 此前，大家经常能看到 ChatGPT 等 AI 每次给出的答案都不太一样，而这一现象早就被视作习以为常——大家都认为现在的 AI 模型就是概率模型。而 TML 则试图通过本次成果来解决这个老大难。

Regarding the paper's content, TML believes that the culprit behind the randomness in AI models is actually the GPU kernels—the small programs running on Nvidia chips—which have issues with how they are pieced together during the inference process. 在论文内容方面，TML 认为 AI 模型产生随机性的罪魁祸首，其实是 GPU 内核 —— 就是那些在英伟达芯片里跑的小程序 —— 在推理过程中的拼接方式有问题。

The core author of the article is Horace He, an AI researcher at Thinking Machines Lab. His solution is ingenious: if the execution flow at this layer can be precisely controlled, it might be possible to make AI model outputs more stable and reliable. 文章的核心作者是 Thinking Machines Lab 的 AI 研究员 Horace He，他的解决思路很巧妙，如果能精确控制这一层的执行流程，就有可能让 AI 模型的输出变得更加稳定可靠。

Furthermore, beyond providing more reliable AI responses, enabling AI models to generate repeatable answers has an even greater value—it can significantly improve reinforcement learning (RL) training effectiveness. Horace He believes that more consistent responses can make the entire RL training process much "smoother." 此外，除了提供更靠谱的 AI 响应，让 AI 模型生成可重复的答案还有个更大的价值——能显著改善强化学习（RL）训练效果。Horace He 认为，更一致的响应能让整个 RL 训练过程「丝滑」很多。

It is understood that Mira Murati, former CTO of OpenAI, is also the founder and CEO of Thinking Machines Lab. In July, she stated that Thinking Machines Lab had completed $2 billion in funding, and its first product would debut in the coming months, greatly benefiting researchers and startups developing custom models. 据了解，Mira Murati 是 OpenAI 的前 CTO，同时目前也是 Thinking Machines Lab 的创始人兼 CEO。她在 7 月份就曾表示 Thinking Machines Lab 已经完成 20 亿美元的融资，且首款产品将在未来几个月内亮相，并且会对研究人员和正在开发定制模型的初创公司大有裨益。

According to the latest news, TML's valuation has soared to $12 billion (approximately 85 billion RMB), despite having "0 products and 0 users." 而据最新消息显示，TML 在「0 产品 0 用户」的情况下，估值已经狂飙 120 亿美元（折合人民币 850 亿元）。
