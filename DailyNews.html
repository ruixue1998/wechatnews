内存成本暴涨 300%，中国手机市场进入「大涨价元年」，千元机加速消失

This year, China's smartphone market is experiencing an unprecedented wave of price increases across the board. The core reasons stem from the sharp rise in upstream memory and storage chip costs, coupled with production capacity squeeze caused by the surge in AI server demand. The industry widely believes that 2026 will mark the "first year of major price increases" for the mobile phone industry. 今年中国智能手机市场正迎来史无前例的全线涨价潮，核心原因来自上游内存与存储芯片成本的急剧攀升，叠加 AI 服务器需求暴涨导致的产能挤压，行业普遍认为 2026 年将成为手机行业的「大涨价元年」。

Multiple supply chain and channel sources confirm that the procurement cost of smartphone storage chips has increased by over 80% compared to the same period last year, with no signs of the upward trend slowing down. 多家供应链与渠道人士确认，智能手机存储芯片采购成本较去年同期已上涨超过 80%，且涨势仍未见放缓迹象。

Affected by this, mainstream brands such as OPPO, OnePlus, vivo, Xiaomi, iQOO, and Honor have planned to launch a new round of price adjustments in early March, which will be the largest and most significant collective price adjustment in nearly five years. 受此影响，OPPO、一加、vivo、小米、iQOO、荣耀等主流品牌已拟定于 3 月初启动新一轮价格调整，这将成为近五年来规模最大、涨幅最显著的一轮集体调价。

Citing multiple sources, Sina Tech reported that starting in March, mobile phone price increases will enter an accelerated phase, with new product price increases exceeding 1,000 yuan at a minimum, and mid-to-high-end flagship models potentially seeing increases of 2,000-3,000 yuan. 新浪科技援引多方消息称，自 3 月起，手机涨价将进入加速阶段，新品涨幅最低将超过 1000 元，中高端旗舰机型涨幅可能达到 2000-3000 元。

At the same time, some brands will also raise the prices of older models. The Chinese mobile phone market in 2026 may see multiple price adjustments within a single year for the first time, and the budget phone market may face the dilemma of "losing money if you release, and losing money if you sell." 同时，部分品牌还将上调老款机型售价，2026 年中国手机市场或将首次出现一年内多次调价的情况，千元机市场也或将迎来「发了就是亏，卖了就是赔」的窘境。

It is worth noting that, according to Korean media DealSite, during negotiations for Samsung Semiconductor to supply LPDDR5X memory for the iPhone 17 series to Apple, the original plan was to increase prices by only about 60%, but Apple quickly nodded and accepted the 100% initial quote . 值得注意的是，据韩媒 DealSite 报道，三星半导体在向苹果供应 iPhone 17 系列所需的 LPDDR5X 内存谈判中，原先计划只涨价约 60%， 但苹果却迅速点头接受了 100% 的起始报价 。

This result not only established Samsung's absolute pricing power in the memory market but also triggered a chain reaction, forcing Samsung's own mobile business division to bear the same price increase pressure. Ultimately, it led to the introduction of competitor Micron's chips to ensure supply, adopting a "Samsung and Micron each account for 50%" strategy in the initial batches of the new S26 series. 这一结果不仅确立了三星在内存市场的绝对定价权，更引发连锁反应，导致三星自家的移动业务部门被迫承受同等涨价压力，最终引入竞争对手美光的芯片以保障供应，在新机 S26 系列的初期批次中采用了「三星与美光各占 50%」的策略。

Yesterday, Hu Xinxin (@馨心_Mia), product manager for Xiaomi REDMI, also expressed her views on the matter, stating that the current storage super cycle is truly a "ghost story" for mobile phone manufacturers . 昨天，小米REDMI产品经理胡馨心（@馨心_Mia）也就此事发表了看法，她表示， 当前的存储超级周期对手机厂商而言，真是「鬼故事」系列 。

Multiple institutions predict that the memory shortage will continue until 2027, and as TSMC's more advanced processes enter mass production in 2028, costs will be difficult to return to past levels. This means that mobile phone prices will remain high for the next three years, and the industry will enter a long-term structural price increase cycle. 多家机构预测，内存短缺将持续至 2027 年，且随着台积电更先进制程在 2028 年量产，成本难以回落至过去水平。这意味着未来三年手机价格仍将维持高位，行业将进入长期结构性涨价周期。

Nano Banana 2 上线：高画质与高速生成首次兼得

Today, Google officially launched its new generation image generation model, Nano Banana 2 (Gemini 3.1 Flash Image), which focuses on further improving image quality, understanding, and subject consistency based on high-speed generation. It is positioned as a lightweight alternative to Nano Banana Pro, open for use by a wider range of users. 今天，Google 正式推出新一代图像生成模型 Nano Banana 2（Gemini 3.1 Flash Image），主打在高速生成的基础上进一步提升画质、理解力与主体一致性，定位为 Nano Banana Pro 的轻量替代方案，面向更广泛用户开放使用。

Nano Banana 2 continues the Gemini series' real-world knowledge base and combines it with real-time information from web searches, enabling the model to perform more naturally in terms of spatial understanding, proportional relationships, light and shadow processing, and Chinese text rendering. Nano Banana 2 延续 Gemini 系列的真实世界知识库，并结合网页搜索实时信息，使模型在空间理解、比例关系、光影处理与中文文本渲染方面表现更自然。

  • Can maintain up to 5 character faces unchanged and 14 object appearances consistent in a single generation, suitable for complex scenarios such as comic serialization and storyboard production; 可在单次生成中保持最多 5 个角色面部不变、14 个物体外观一致，适用于漫画连载与分镜制作等复杂场景；
  • Covers output from 512px to 4K, with sharper image quality and more natural light and shadow compared to the previous generation, directly usable for marketing materials or visual design; 覆盖从 512px 到 4K 的输出，画质相比前代更锐利、光影更自然，可直接用于营销素材或视觉设计；
  • Adopts the same efficient architecture as Gemini Flash, emphasizing "near real-time" high-speed generation experience, suitable for rapid iteration and multiple rounds of modifications; 采用与 Gemini Flash 相同的高效架构，强调「接近实时」的高速生成体验，适合快速迭代与多轮修改；
  • In terms of cost, regular users can generate 100 images for free daily in the Gemini app and Google Search, while Pro users' quota increases to 1000 images; API prices are halved compared to the previous generation Pro model, with the cost of generating a 4K image being approximately $0.15; 成本方面，普通用户每日可在 Gemini 应用与 Google 搜索中免费生成 100 张图片，Pro 用户额度提升至 1000 张；API 价格较上一代 Pro 模型腰斩，生成一张 4K 图像成本约为 0.15 美元；
  • Regarding content credibility, Google has upgraded SynthID digital watermarking and C2PA content credentials to enhance AI content traceability. 在内容可信度上，Google 升级 SynthID 数字水印与 C2PA 内容凭证，增强 AI 内容溯源能力。

Google 官方「豆包手机」曝光：可让 Gemini 直接操控 App

According to 9to5Google, Google will introduce a new API in the Android system to enable AI Agents to control apps, similar to the "Doubao Phone". 据 9to5Google 报道，Google 将在 Android 系统中推出一个新的 API 接口，以实现类似「豆包手机」让 AI Agent 操控 App 的功能。

According to Android's official documentation, AppFunctions is an Android 16 platform capability, accompanied by the Jetpack library, allowing developers to expose specific in-app functionalities to AI Agents in a structured manner for invocation. 根据 Android 官方文档，AppFunctions 是 Android 16 的平台能力，并配套 Jetpack 库，允许开发者将应用内的特定功能以结构化方式暴露给 AI Agent 调用。

Developers can generate necessary code via annotations, metadata, and the KSP compiler, enabling AI Agent applications like Gemini to execute tasks directly in the device background without needing to jump to the application interface. 开发者可通过注解、元数据与 KSP 编译器生成必要代码，使 Gemini 等 AI Agent 应用在设备后台直接执行任务，无需跳转应用界面。

At yesterday's Samsung Galaxy S26 series launch event, Google demonstrated the practical implementation of AppFunctions: users simply tell Gemini "Show me cat photos in Samsung Gallery," and the system automatically invokes the gallery function and returns the results directly, without needing to manually open the app and search. 在昨天三星 Galaxy S26 系列的发布会上，Google 展示了 AppFunctions 的实际落地：用户只需向 Gemini 说出「给我看三星图库里猫的照片」，系统即可自动调用图库的函数并直接返回结果，无需手动打开应用翻找。

This feature will first launch on the Galaxy S26 and Pixel 10 series in the US and South Korea, initially supporting only some food delivery, fresh produce, and ride-hailing apps, and will also be rolled out to more Samsung devices with OneUI 8.5. 该功能将率先在美国与韩国的 Galaxy S26 与 Pixel 10 系列上线，初期仅支持部分外卖、生鲜与网约车应用，还将随 OneUI 8.5 推送至更多三星设备。

Ni Fei, President of Nubia, stated on Weibo yesterday that while the combination of Samsung and Google demonstrated phone automation capabilities, it still belongs to "local capabilities" and has not reached the "full-scenario system-level" self-driving AI experience of the "Doubao Phone" Nubia M153. 努比亚总裁倪飞昨天在微博表示，三星与 Google 的组合虽展示了手机自动化能力，但仍属于「局部能力」，并未达到「豆包手机」努比亚 M153「全场景系统级」的自动驾驶式 AI 体验。

He emphasized that Nubia had already released a technical preview of the Doubao Phone last year, promoting the deep system-level application of AI agents on mobile phones, and looks forward to more manufacturers joining to collectively improve scenario coverage and implementation depth. 他强调，努比亚在去年已率先发布豆包手机技术预览版，推动手机端 AI 智能体进入系统级深度应用，并期待更多厂商加入，共同完善场景覆盖与落地深度。

IBM 回应 AI 冲击：现代化远不止改代码

Recently, Rob Thomas, IBM Senior Vice President, published an article responding to external discussions surrounding "Can AI quickly replace COBOL and mainframes?" 近日，IBM 高级副总裁 Rob Thomas 发布文章，回应外界围绕「AI 能否快速替代 COBOL 与大型机」的讨论。

Previously, Anthropic announced that Claude Code can automatically sort COBOL dependencies, generate documentation, and identify risks, sparking market concerns about the impact on IBM's mainframe business. IBM's stock price recorded its largest single-day drop in nearly 26 years this Monday local time, with its market value evaporating by approximately $31 billion. 此前，Anthropic 宣布 Claude Code 能自动梳理 COBOL 依赖、生成文档并识别风险，引发市场对 IBM 主机业务受冲击的担忧，IBM 股价在当地时间本周一录得近 26 年最大单日跌幅，市值蒸发约 310 亿美元。

The article points out that the core value of the IBM Z mainframe platform lies not in COBOL, but in its vertically integrated architecture from chip to operating system, which provides extremely high transaction processing capabilities, security, and availability. 文章指出，IBM Z 大型机平台的核心价值不在 COBOL，而在于其从芯片到操作系统的垂直整合架构，能够提供极高的事务处理能力、安全性与可用性。

IBM states that simply converting COBOL to other languages cannot replicate these capabilities, nor can it solve true modernization challenges, including data architecture refactoring, runtime replacement, transactional consistency, disaster recovery capabilities, encryption systems, and integration with surrounding systems. IBM 表示，单纯把 COBOL 转成其他语言，并不能复制这些能力，也无法解决真正的现代化难题，包括数据架构重构、运行时替代、事务一致性、灾备能力、加密体系以及与周边系统的集成等。

The article also points out that the advantages of IBM Z are even more prominent in the AI era. AI tools are helping enterprises automatically analyze legacy system structures, accelerate DevOps processes, reduce manual operations, and alleviate the skill gap caused by the retirement of COBOL talent. 文章还指出，IBM Z 的优势在 AI 时代反而更突出。AI 工具正在帮助企业自动分析遗留系统结构、加速 DevOps 流程、减少人工操作，并缓解 COBOL 人才退休带来的技能缺口。

IBM believes that these trends mean enterprises should continue to increase their investment in IBM Z. IBM 认为，这些趋势意味着企业应继续加大对 IBM Z 的投入。

IBM emphasizes that AI can indeed bring value in the code domain, but it should not be misunderstood as being able to "one-click replace" mainframe platforms. True modernization work is far more complex than language conversion. IBM 强调，AI 在代码领域确实能带来价值，但不应被误解为可以「一键替代」大型机平台。真正的现代化工作远比语言转换复杂得多。

停产 CCD 相机再翻红，涨价达数倍

According to CCTV Finance, CCD digital cameras, which have been out of production for many years, have recently become popular again, becoming a 'hit' product sought after by young consumers. Several merchants in Huaqiangbei stated that models originally priced at only a few hundred yuan are now generally selling for over 2,000 yuan, with some models even seeing price increases of several times. 据央视财经报道，停产多年的 CCD 数码相机近期再次翻红，成为年轻消费群体追捧的「爆款」产品。多位华强北商户表示，原本售价仅数百元的机型，如今普遍涨至 2000 元以上，部分型号甚至出现数倍涨幅。

Huaqiangbei merchants revealed that in earlier years, CCD cameras were even sold by the jin (half-kilogram), at about 100 yuan per jin. The current price surge is mainly due to being out of production for many years, limited stock, and driven by aesthetic trends. 华强北商户透露，早些年 CCD 相机甚至曾按斤出售，约 100 元一斤，如今价格飙升主要源于停产多年、存量有限，加之审美潮流推动。

However, merchants also warned that most CCD cameras currently circulating in the market are old second-hand units, generally posing risks such as battery aging, sensor wear, and shutter life nearing its limit, with product condition significantly impacting failure rates. 不过，商户也提醒，目前市场流通的 CCD 相机大多为老旧二手机，普遍存在电池老化、传感器损耗、快门寿命接近极限等风险，品相对故障率影响显著。
