曝苹果折叠屏 iPhone 已在试产，今年下半年有望发布

According to China Securities Journal · China Securities Golden Bull, Apple's first foldable iPhone has entered the trial production stage. 据中国证券报·中证金牛座报道，苹果首款折叠屏 iPhone 已进入试产阶段。

Reports indicate that Foxconn has begun trial production of Apple's foldable screen iPhone. According to previously disclosed information from supply chain companies, Apple has provided suppliers with a shipment target guideline to launch its first foldable phone in the second half of this year, positioned as a large foldable iPhone. 报道指出，富士康已开始试产苹果折叠屏 iPhone 手机。根据此前供应链公司透露的消息，苹果给供应商提供的出货目标指引为今年下半年推出首款折叠屏手机，定位为一款大折叠屏 iPhone。

Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman also stated that iOS 27 will be adapted for the foldable iPhone Fold. 彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 也曾表示，iOS 27 将对折叠屏 iPhone Fold 做适配。

It is reported that iOS 27 will have a new layout on the iPhone Fold: when the device is unfolded, the overall interface will resemble iPadOS more, and there might also be an app navigation bar similar to iPadOS on the left side. 据悉，iOS 27 在 iPhone Fold 上将拥有全新的布局：设备展开时，整体界面会更像 iPadOS，并且左侧或许还会拥有类似 iPadOS 的应用导航栏。

Given the high-end positioning of foldable screens, early demand for Apple's foldable iPhone is expected to come primarily from existing iPhone users. 代号「土豆」，曝 GPT-6 本月发布

GPT-6, internally codenamed "Spud" (potato), underwent two years of secret development and completed pre-training on March 24 at the Stargate data center in Texas. 内部代号「Spud」（土豆）的 GPT-6 历经两年秘密研发，于 3 月 24 日在德克萨斯州的 Stargate 数据中心完成预训练。

OpenAI President Greg Brockman personally confirmed the model's existence in a podcast interview, stating it "is not an incremental improvement, but a significant change in how we think about model development." Sam Altman told all employees on the same day that it is "a very powerful model that can truly accelerate economic development." OpenAI 总裁 Greg Brockman 在播客访谈中亲口确认了该模型的存在，称其「不是增量改进，而是我们思考模型开发方式的重大改变」。Sam Altman 同日向全体员工表示，这是「一个非常强大的模型，可以真正加速经济发展」。

According to leaked documents, GPT-6 shows over 40% improvement in coding, reasoning, and AI agent tasks compared to GPT-5.4, and its context window has expanded from 1 million tokens to 2 million. 根据泄露文档，GPT-6 在编码、推理和 AI 智能体任务上比 GPT-5.4 提升 40% 以上，上下文窗口从 100 万 token 扩展至 200 万。

Brockman also introduced the concept of "Big Model Smell" (the aura of large models ) , emphasizing the model's ability to proactively align with user intent without repeated prompting. Furthermore, the model supports native unified processing of text, images, audio, and video, and possesses stronger long-range AI agent autonomous operation capabilities. Brockman 还提出了「Big Model Smell」（大模型的气息 ） 概念，强调模型能够主动对齐用户意图，无需反复提示。此外，该模型支持文本、图像、音频、视频的原生统一处理，并具备更强的长程 AI 智能体自主运行能力。

Regarding pricing, leaks indicate an input price of approximately $2.5 per million tokens and an output price of $12 per million tokens, which is largely consistent with GPT-5.4. Several leaker bloggers claim the new model will be released on April 14, but official confirmation is still pending. 定价方面，爆料显示输入价格约为 2.5 美元/百万 token，输出为 12 美元/百万 token，与 GPT-5.4 基本持平。多位爆料博主称新模型将在 4 月 14 日发布，但尚未获得官方确认。

Less than 24 hours after GPT-6 completed pre-training, OpenAI announced the shutdown of its video generation product Sora and simultaneously canceled its $1 billion character licensing agreement with Disney. GPT-6 完成预训练后不到 24 小时，OpenAI 宣布关停视频生成产品 Sora，并同步取消与迪士尼价值 10 亿美元的角色授权协议。

Altman personally called Disney CEO Bob Iger to apologize. In a recent interview, he explained the reason for the shutdown: "The core issue is computing power; it's always a matter of computing power." Altman 亲自致电迪士尼 CEO Bob Iger 道歉。他在近期访谈中解释关停原因：「核心在于算力，永远都是算力的问题。」

罗福莉谈 Anthropic 封杀「龙虾」：情有可原，OpenClaw 上下文管理「糟透了」

Yesterday, Luo Fuli (@_LuoFuli), head of Xiaomi's MiMo team, posted on X, commenting on Anthropic's recent decision to cut off third-party tool frameworks (harnesses) from accessing Claude's subscription services. 昨天，小米 MiMo 团队负责人罗福莉（@_LuoFuli）在 X 发文，对 Anthropic 近日切断第三方工具框架（harness）对 Claude 订阅服务的访问权限一事进行评价。

It is reported that this move directly affected AI agent development tools such as "Lobster" OpenClaw, which rely on Claude's subscription interface. 据悉，此举直接影响了「龙虾」OpenClaw 等依赖 Claude 订阅接口的 AI 智能体开发工具。

Luo Fuli pointed out that Claude Code's subscription mechanism itself is a "carefully designed system for balanced allocation of computing power," but after third-party frameworks were integrated, this system bore far more pressure than anticipated. 罗福莉指出，Claude Code 的订阅机制本身是一套「经过精心设计的算力均衡分配系统」，但在第三方框架接入后，这套系统承受了远超预期的压力。

Taking OpenClaw as an example, she elaborated on its serious flaws in context management: 她以 OpenClaw 为例，详细说明了其上下文管理存在的严重缺陷：

When processing a single user request, OpenClaw triggers multiple rounds of low-value tool calls, each carrying a long context window of over 100,000 tokens. Even with cache hits, there is significant waste, and in extreme cases, it can increase the cache miss rate for other requests. The actual number of requests is several times that of Claude Code's native framework, and when converted to API pricing, the true cost could be dozens of times the subscription price. 在处理单个用户请求时，OpenClaw 会触发多轮低价值的工具调用，每次调用均携带超过 10 万 Token 的长上下文窗口，即便命中缓存也存在大量浪费，极端情况下还会拉高其他请求的缓存未命中率。实际请求次数是 Claude Code 原生框架的数倍，换算成 API 定价，真实成本可能是订阅价格的数十倍。

She described this disparity as "not a chasm, but a deep pit." 她将这一差距形容为「不是鸿沟，而是深坑」。

Regarding this ban, Luo Fuli believes that the short-term pain is actually a positive pressure. After third-party frameworks are forced to switch to API payments, the pressure of costs increasing dozens of times will compel developers to improve context management, enhance prompt cache hit rates, and reduce invalid token consumption, stating that "pain will ultimately transform into engineering discipline." 对于此次封禁措施，罗福莉认为短期阵痛反而是正向压力。第三方框架被迫转向 API 付费后，成本骤增数十倍的压力将倒逼开发者改善上下文管理、提升 prompt 缓存命中率、削减无效 Token 消耗，「痛苦最终会转化为工程纪律」。

She also warned other large model manufacturers, urging them not to blindly follow the price war before clarifying the pricing model for programming subscription plans. 她同时向其他大模型厂商发出警示，呼吁在尚未厘清编程订阅计划定价模型之前，不要盲目跟进价格战。

Selling Tokens at a low price while opening the door to third-party frameworks may seem user-friendly, but it is actually a trap—Anthropic has just climbed out of this pit. 低价出售 Token 的同时对第三方框架大开门户，看似对用户友好，实则是个陷阱——Anthropic 刚刚从这个坑里爬出来。

She also pointed out that if users continuously use low-quality AI agent frameworks, unstable inference services, and downgraded models to control costs, they will ultimately still be unable to complete actual tasks. This creates a vicious cycle for user experience and retention. 她还指出，若用户长期使用低质量的 AI 智能体框架、不稳定的推理服务和为控制成本而降级的模型，最终仍无法完成实际任务，这对用户体验和留存都是恶性循环。

AI 界再来新词：被淘汰恐惧 FOBO

According to a report released by Fortune magazine, combining the latest research from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), the automation impact of artificial intelligence on the job market is developing gradually and is causing an increasingly intense "Fear of Becoming Obsolete (FOBO)" among the workplace population. 据《财富》杂志结合麻省理工学院（MIT）的最新研究发布报告，指出人工智能对就业市场的自动化冲击呈渐进式发展，且正引发职场群体日益加剧的「被淘汰恐惧（FOBO）」。

It is understood that FOBO stands for Fear of Becoming Obsolete. Unlike traditional "unemployment anxiety," this emotion points more to "becoming irrelevant." 据悉，FOBO 全称为 Fear of Becoming Obsolete（对被淘汰的恐惧）。与传统「失业焦虑」不同，这种情绪更多指向「自己会变得无关紧要」。

In its latest report, "Crashing Waves vs. Rising Tides," the MIT research team conducted 17,000 manual evaluations of more than 40 cutting-edge large models, including GPT-5, Gemini 2.5 Pro, and DeepSeek R1. MIT 研究团队在最新报告《Crashing Waves vs. Rising Tides》中，对包括 GPT-5、Gemini 2.5 Pro 及 DeepSeek R1 在内的 40 余款前沿大模型进行了 1.7 万次人工评估。

Test results show that AI can currently complete 50% to 75% of text-based work tasks with minimum acceptable standards. The study points out that the AI task failure rate is decreasing at a rate of halving every 2 to 3 years; according to this trend, by 2029, AI is expected to complete most routine text work with an 80% to 95% success rate. 测试结果表明，AI 目前已能以最低合格标准完成 50% 至 75% 的文本类工作任务。研究指出，AI 任务失败率正以每 2 至 3 年减半的速度下降；按此趋势测算，至 2029 年，AI 预计能以 80% 至 95% 的成功率完成大部分常规文本工作。

The sense of crisis among the workplace population has been reflected in statistical data. KPMG data shows that currently 40% of employees view AI-induced job loss as a core concern, a proportion that has nearly doubled year-on-year. 职场群体的危机感已反映在统计数据中。毕马威（KPMG）数据显示，目前有 40% 的员工将 AI 引发的失业视为核心担忧，该比例同比激增近一倍。

Joe Depa, EY Global Chief Innovation Officer, confirmed this workplace polarization, stating that junior employees within enterprises have extremely high adoption rates of AI tools, while some senior software engineers, due to their resistance to using AI, have seen their actual productivity fall behind AI-assisted peers by 10 to 20 times. 安永（EY）全球首席创新官 Joe Depa 证实了这一职场分化现象，他表示企业内部初级员工对 AI 工具的采用率极高，而部分资深软件工程师因抗拒使用 AI，其实际生产力已被借助 AI 的同行拉开 10 倍至 20 倍的差距。

OpenAI 推出安全研究奖学金

OpenAI today announced the launch of the "OpenAI Safety Fellowship" program, open to external researchers, engineers, and practitioners, aiming to support independent research on the safety and alignment of advanced AI systems and cultivate the next generation of AI safety talent. OpenAI 今天宣布推出「OpenAI Safety Fellowship」（OpenAI 安全研究奖学金）项目，面向外部研究人员、工程师及从业者开放申请，旨在支持针对先进 AI 系统安全性与对齐问题的独立研究，并培养下一代 AI 安全领域人才。

This program is a pilot initiative, running from September 14 this year to February 5 next year. Priority areas include safety evaluation and ethics, robustness and scalable mitigation measures, privacy-preserving safety methods, AI agent oversight, and high-risk misuse scenarios. 该项目为试点计划，将于今年 9 月 14 日至明年 2 月 5 日期间运行，优先方向涵盖安全评估与伦理、健壮性与可扩展缓解措施、隐私保护安全方法、AI 智能体监督与高危误用场景。

Selected Fellows will work closely with internal OpenAI mentors and form a learning community with fellow researchers. The program provides office space in Berkeley, shared with other Constellation Fellows, and also supports remote participation. 入选的 Fellow 将与 OpenAI 内部导师紧密合作，并与同期研究者组成学习社群。项目在伯克利提供办公场所，与 Constellation 的其他 Fellow 共享空间，同时也支持远程参与。

During the program, Fellows must complete a substantial research output, which can be in the form of a paper, benchmark, or dataset. The program provides a monthly stipend, computing power support, and continuous mentor guidance, as well as resources such as API credits, but does not grant access to OpenAI's internal systems. 项目期间，Fellow 须完成一项实质性研究成果，形式包括论文、基准测试或数据集。项目提供月度津贴、算力支持及持续导师指导，同时提供 API credits 等资源，但不授予 OpenAI 内部系统访问权限。

Applications require submission of recommendation letters. The application channel is now open, with a deadline of May 3 this year, and admission results will be announced by July 25. 申请需提交推荐信，目前申请通道已开放，截止日期为今年 5 月 3 日，录取结果将于 7 月 25 日前通知。
