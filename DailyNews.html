OpenAI 被指「偷换模型」，神秘新模型曝光

Recently, a user named "Lex ( @xw33bttv )" posted on the X platform, claiming that OpenAI has quietly launched a new model called "GPT-5-Chat-Safety," which does not appear in FAQs, API documentation, or terms of service. 日前，一名叫「Lex（ @xw33bttv ）」的用户在 X 平台上发文，声称 OpenAI 已经悄然上线一款名为「GPT-5-Chat-Safety」的新模型，该模型并未出现在 FAQ、API 文档或服务条款中。

According to the description, when users input emotionally charged content into GPT-4o, the original response is ignored and replaced with a version generated by "GPT-5-Chat-Safety." 据描述，当用户在 GPT-4o 中输入带有情感背景的内容时，原始回复会被忽略并替换为由「GPT-5-Chat-Safety」生成的版本。

Whether it's expressions involving emotions like "I had a terrible day" or "I love you," this mechanism will be triggered. 无论是「我今天过得很糟」、还是「我也爱你」等涉及情绪的表达，都会触发这一机制。

The leaker pointed out that this model might have originally been designed for crisis intervention scenarios, but this replacement mechanism is extremely opaque and even widely applied in ordinary conversations, leading users to be switched to the new model without their knowledge. 爆料者指出，这一模型最初可能是为危机干预场景设计，但这一替换机制极不透明，甚至被广泛应用于普通对话中，导致用户在不知情的情况下被切换至新模型。

Some netizens even commented that merely sending a simple "Hello" resulted in the model being replaced. 甚至，有网友在评论指出，只是发送了一个简单的「你好」就被替换模型。

More concerning is that the system does not actively notify users that they have been switched, which outsiders believe may involve consumer rights issues. 更令人担忧的是，系统不会主动提示用户已被切换，外界认为这可能涉及消费者权益问题。

From actual experience, "GPT-5-Chat-Safety"'s responses are more concise and tend to use a formalized narrative style rather than natural one-on-one conversation. 从实际体验来看，「GPT-5-Chat-Safety」的回答更为简短，并倾向于使用格式化的叙述方式，而非自然的 1 对 1 对话。

Lex stated bluntly that this model is "much worse" than GPT-5 and pointed out that this behavior violates consumer law in some countries and regions. Lex 直言，这款模型比 GPT-5「要差得多」，并指出这一行为在一些国家和地区违反了消费者法。

面世 21 年，「越狱神器」91 助手停止服务

Yesterday, the classic Apple iPhone / iPad device management tool "91 Assistant" officially ceased all services. 昨天，经典苹果 iPhone / iPad 设备管理工具「91 助手」正式全面停止所有服务。

Currently, its client can no longer be launched, and functions such as mobile phone connection, file transfer, application management, and VIP exclusive features have all been terminated. At the same time, the official website, help center, and customer service channels have also been closed. 目前，其客户端已无法启动，手机连接、文件传输、应用管理以及 VIP 专属功能等均已终止。同时，官方网站、帮助中心及客服渠道也已同步关闭。

The official announcement states that after the service termination, all user data in "91 Assistant" will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered in any way. 官方公告显示，服务终止后，用户在「91 助手」中的所有数据将永久丢失，无法再以任何方式找回。

It is understood that "91 Assistant" can be traced back to 2007, developed by the NetDragon company team, and was originally named "91 Mobile Assistant." 据了解，「91 助手」最早可追溯至 2007 年，由网龙公司团队开发，最初命名为「91 手机助手」。

In the early stages when the iOS ecosystem was relatively closed and iTunes was inconvenient to use, this tool quickly became popular with features like "jailbreaking," app installation, and file management, earning it the title "jailbreak artifact" among Apple fans. 在 iOS 生态相对封闭、iTunes 使用不便的早期阶段，该工具凭借「越狱」、应用安装和文件管理等功能迅速走红，被果粉称为「越狱神器」。

In 2009, "91 Assistant" expanded to the Android platform, adding features like flashing and mobile phone management, further broadening its user base. 2009 年，「91 助手」拓展至 Android 平台，新增刷机和手机管理功能，进一步扩大用户群体。

At its peak in 2013, its user base on both iOS and Android platforms exceeded 100 million, with total downloads surpassing 10 billion. 2013 年达到巅峰时，其在 iOS 与 Android 平台的用户数超过 1 亿，总下载量突破 100 亿。

In the same year, Baidu acquired 91 Wireless for US$1.9 billion (approximately RMB 13.564 billion), making it one of the largest acquisition deals in China's internet industry at the time. 同年，百度以 19 亿美元（约合人民币 135.64 亿元）收购 91 无线，这一交易成为当时中国互联网行业规模最大的收购案之一。

However, with the evolution of the mobile ecosystem, "91 Assistant" gradually faded from public view. 然而，随着移动生态的演进，「91 助手」逐渐淡出公众视野。

In February 2020, Baidu announced the discontinuation of "91 Assistant" and Android Market, though some functions remained operational. Now, with the complete shutdown, this once popular tool has officially come to an end. 2020 年 2 月，百度宣布下线「91 助手」及安卓市场，但部分功能仍在运行。如今，随着全面停服，这款曾经风靡一时的工具彻底画上句号。
