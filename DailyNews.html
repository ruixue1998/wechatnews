The Verge：液态玻璃的界面统一是「难以忍受的」

David Pierce, editor-in-chief of The Verge, recently wrote an article providing an in-depth experience and evaluation of Apple's new "Liquid Glass" design system introduced across platforms such as iOS 26, macOS Tahoe 26, watchOS 26, and Vision Pro. The Verge 总编辑 David Pierce 近日撰文，对苹果在 iOS 26、macOS Tahoe 26、watchOS 26 及 Vision Pro 等平台引入的全新「Liquid Glass」设计系统进行了深入体验与评价。

According to the introduction, "Liquid Glass" brings a unified three-dimensional interface visual effect to almost all Apple devices, with buttons and menus floating above the content, capable of changing color and refracting light with the environment, simulating a real glass texture. On immersive devices like Vision Pro, this design is considered highly compatible; on Mac, due to fewer system interface elements, the impact is relatively limited. 据介绍，「Liquid Glass」为几乎所有苹果设备带来统一的立体界面视觉效果，按钮与菜单悬浮于内容之上，并可随环境变色、折射光线，模拟真实玻璃质感。在 Vision Pro 等沉浸式设备上，这一设计被认为契合度较高；在 Mac 上，由于系统界面元素较少，影响也相对有限。

However, Pierce pointed out that the design performs poorly on high-frequency interaction devices like iPhone and Apple Watch, where frequent color changes and size variations of interface elements cause visual interference and difficulty in information recognition. He mentioned that when browsing colorful web pages in Safari, the search bar is almost illegible; in watchOS 26, the time display easily blends with the watch face background, affecting readability. 然而，Pierce 指出，该设计在 iPhone 和 Apple Watch 等高频交互设备上表现不佳，界面元素频繁变色、尺寸变化，反而造成视觉干扰与信息辨识困难。他提到，在 Safari 浏览彩色网页时，搜索栏几乎难以看清；在 watchOS 26 中，时间显示容易与表盘背景融合，影响可读性。

The article believes that "Liquid Glass" reflects Apple's current trend of promoting multi-platform interface unification, but this "one-size-fits-all" design philosophy ignores the usage scenarios and interaction needs of different devices. Pierce concluded that this system might be suitable for a future world where AR/VR is widespread, but in the current reality of multiple screens coexisting, the sense of unity it brings is more like "unbearable convergence." 文章认为，「Liquid Glass」体现了苹果当前推动多平台界面统一的趋势，但这种「一刀切」的设计理念忽视了不同设备的使用场景与交互需求。Pierce 总结称，这套系统或许适用于未来 AR / VR 普及的世界，但在当下多屏并存的现实中，其带来的统一感更像是「难以忍受的趋同」。

OpenAI 最新报告：揭秘 ChatGPT 最火用途

On September 15, OpenAI released a detailed survey of ChatGPT usage, covering multi-dimensional data including gender, age, region, and purpose. OpenAI 于 9 月 15 日公布了 ChatGPT 详细使用情况调查，涵盖性别、年龄、地域及用途等多维度数据。

The report shows that as of June 2025, ChatGPT's weekly active users have exceeded 700 million, equivalent to 10% of the global adult population, and the user base continues to grow across all genders and countries. 报告显示，截至 2025 年 6 月，ChatGPT 每周活跃用户已突破 7 亿，相当于全球成年人口的 10%，且用户规模在各性别与国家中持续增长。

Notably, the proportion of female users surpassed that of males for the first time in June this year, marking a significant narrowing of the gender gap. In terms of age distribution, the younger demographic aged 18 to 25 contributed approximately 46% of message volume, becoming the most active user group. 值得关注的是，女性用户比例在今年 6 月首次超过男性，标志着性别差距显著缩小。年龄分布方面，18 至 25 岁的年轻群体贡献了约 46% 的消息量，成为最活跃用户群。

Regional data indicates that in low-to-middle-income countries with a per capita GDP of $10,000 to $40,000, the growth rate of ChatGPT's penetration is four times that of high-income countries, demonstrating its rapid infiltration into emerging markets. 地域数据表明，在人均 GDP 1 万至 4 万美元的低中收入国家，ChatGPT 普及率增长速度是高收入国家的 4 倍，显示其在新兴市场的快速渗透。

Regarding usage topics, "practical guidance," "information retrieval," and "writing" ranked in the top three, accounting for a combined 78%. Among these, writing was the primary use for work-related tasks, at 40%; while 73% of conversations were unrelated to work, a significant increase from 53% a year ago. 在使用话题上，「实用指导」、「信息查询」与「写作」位列前三，合计占比达 78%。其中，写作是工作相关任务的首要用途，占比 40%；而 73% 的对话与工作无关，相较一年前的 53% 有明显提升。

Furthermore, the research team categorized user intentions into three types: "questioning," "executing," and "expressing." The results showed that 49% of interactions involved asking for information or advice, 40% involved requesting specific outcomes, and 11% were for communication without a specific purpose. In work scenarios, the "executing" category rose to 56%, but "questioning" interactions still had the highest satisfaction. 此外，研究团队还将用户意图分为「提问」、「执行」与「表达」三类，结果显示 49% 的互动为提问获取信息或建议，40% 为要求具体成果，11% 为无特定目的的交流。在工作场景中，执行类占比升至 56%，但满意度最高的仍是提问类互动。

According to Red Star News, the 4-year rights protection case of the "car roof protest" owner has seen new developments. The Beijing Daxing District People's Court ruled in the first instance, ordering Tesla Automobile Sales Service (Beijing) Co., Ltd. to provide Ms. Zhang, the car owner, with complete driving data for 30 minutes before the accident, recognizing that operators should guarantee consumers' right to know. 据红星新闻报道，「车顶维权」车主历时 4 年的维权案件迎来新进展。北京市大兴区人民法院一审判决，责令特斯拉汽车销售服务（北京）有限公司向车主张女士提供事故发生前 30 分钟的完整行车数据，认定经营者应保障消费者的知情权。

The case's origin dates back to February 2021, when Ms. Zhang's father was involved in a rear-end collision while driving a Tesla. Traffic police determined he was fully responsible, but the family insisted the accident was due to "brake failure." In April of the same year, Ms. Zhang protested on the roof of a Tesla at the Shanghai Auto Show, drawing widespread attention, and was subsequently detained by police for 5 days. 案件起因可追溯至 2021 年 2 月，张女士父亲驾驶特斯拉发生追尾事故，交警认定其负全责，但家属坚持认为事故源于「刹车失灵」。同年 4 月，张女士在上海车展站上特斯拉车顶维权，引发广泛关注，并因此被警方行政拘留 5 日。
