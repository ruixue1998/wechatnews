曝折叠屏 iPhone 发售将推迟

Tianfeng International Securities analyst Ming-Chi Kuo stated yesterday on X that Apple's first foldable iPhone (rumored to be named "iPhone Ultra") is likely to repeat the situation of the 2017 iPhone X in terms of its release schedule: it will be unveiled alongside the iPhone 18 Pro series, but pre-orders and official sales will be delayed until the fourth quarter. 天风国际证券分析师郭明錤昨日在 X 表示，苹果首款可折叠 iPhone（传闻命名为「iPhone Ultra」）在发布节奏上很可能重现 2017 年 iPhone X 的情形：与 iPhone 18 Pro 系列同台发布，但预购及正式开售时间将推迟至第四季度。

Kuo Ming-Chi pointed out in his latest industry survey that, constrained by manufacturing difficulties, the assembled shipments of foldable iPhones in the second half of this year are estimated to be approximately 7 million to 8 million units, with third-quarter shipments being only 500,000 to 1 million units, accounting for about 10% of the annual total. 郭明錤在其最新行业调查中指出，受制造难度制约，今年下半年可折叠 iPhone 的组装出货量预计约为 700 万至 800 万台，其中第三季度出货量仅为 50 万至 100 万台，约占全年总量的 10%。

At the demand level, after synthesizing feedback from operators, sales channels, and scalpers, Ming-Chi Kuo believes that even with a starting price of approximately $2299 to $2499, market demand for the foldable iPhone will remain strong at least until the end of this year. 在需求层面，郭明錤综合运营商、销售渠道及黄牛方面的反馈后认为，即便起售价约为 2299 至 2499 美元，可折叠 iPhone 的市场需求至少在今年年底前仍将保持强劲。

He predicts that the model may quickly sell out after pre-orders open, with shipping times potentially extending to 4 to 6 weeks or even longer, and could see a 50% to 100% premium in the secondary market. 他预计，该机型开放预购后可能迅速售罄，发货周期或很快延伸至 4 至 6 周甚至更长，并可能在二手市场出现 50% 至 100% 的溢价。

豆包、千问将下线智能体功能

Recently, Doubao and Qianwen both announced the discontinuation of their AI agent features. A reminder within the Qianwen App indicates that Qianwen's AI agent features and services will be officially taken offline on July 15, 2026; after discontinuation, users will no longer be able to access relevant AI agent configurations and historical chat records, and the official recommendation is to copy, screenshot, or export conversations in advance. 近日，豆包、千问均宣布下线智能体功能。千问 App 内提醒显示，千问智能体功能与服务将于 2026 年 7 月 15 日正式下线；下线后，用户无法继续访问相关智能体配置及历史对话记录，官方建议提前复制、截图或导出对话。

Doubao also issued a 'Doubao AI Agent Feature Discontinuation Notice' on the same day, stating that due to product feature adjustments, the AI agent feature will also be taken offline on July 15, 2026. 豆包同日发布《豆包智能体功能下线通知》，称由于产品功能调整，智能体功能也将于 2026 年 7 月 15 日下线。

It is worth noting that the discontinuation date of the AI agent feature coincides with the official implementation date of the 'Interim Measures for the Management of AI Personification Interactive Services' jointly issued by five departments, including the Cyberspace Administration of China. 值得注意的是，智能体功能的下线日期与国家互联网信息办公室等五部门联合发布的《人工智能拟人化互动服务管理暂行办法》正式施行日期一致。

The measures, announced in April this year, explicitly bring continuous emotional interactive services that use AI technology to simulate natural human personality traits, thought patterns, and communication styles for the domestic public under regulatory scope, covering emotional care, companionship, and support services in forms such as text, images, audio, and video. 该办法于今年 4 月公布，明确将利用人工智能技术向境内公众提供模拟自然人人格特征、思维模式和沟通风格的持续性情感互动服务纳入监管范围，涵盖文字、图片、音频、视频等形式的情感照护、陪伴与支持服务。

手机电脑又要涨价，三星 DRAM 拟开启今年第三轮大幅提价

According to ZDNet Korea, Samsung Electronics is planning to significantly raise the average selling price (ASP) of its DRAM products in the third quarter of this year, targeting an increase of up to 20%. 据 ZDNet Korea 报道，三星电子正计划在今年第三季度大幅上调 DRAM 产品的平均售价（ASP），目标涨幅最高达 20%。

This price adjustment marks Samsung's third round of price increases this year. Data shows that Samsung's DRAM ASP surged by approximately 90% quarter-over-quarter in the first quarter of this year, and continued to rise by 50% to 60% in the second quarter. 此次调价是三星今年以来推动的第三轮价格上涨。数据显示，三星 DRAM 平均售价在今年第一季度环比飙升约 90%，第二季度继续上涨 50% 至 60%。

Although the target increase of 20% in the third quarter is slower than the previous two quarters, it still appears very aggressive given the industry's high base. Among them, low-power DRAM (LPDDR) for servers and mobile devices is expected to see an increase of over 20% due to supply bottlenecks. 尽管第三季度 20% 的目标涨幅较前两季度有所放缓，但在行业高基数背景下依然显得十分激进。其中，针对服务器和移动端的低功耗 DRAM（LPDDR）由于供应瓶颈， 预计涨幅将超过 20% 。

Supply chain cost pressure is consequently being passed on to end-users. Due to the increase in storage chip prices, costs in areas such as smartphones, personal computers, and automotive electronics continue to rise. 产业链成本压力正随之向终端传导。受存储芯片涨价影响，智能手机、个人电脑及汽车电子等领域的成本持续上升。

The report points out that although Apple and Samsung's mobile divisions have secured some inventory for their third-quarter flagship products, models expected to be released in Q4 2026 and Q1 2027 will face greater cost challenges. 报道指出，尽管苹果和三星的移动部门已为第三季度旗舰产品锁定了部分货源，但预计于 2026 年第四季度及 2027 年第一季度发布的机型将面临更大的成本挑战。

华为何庭波发布「韬定律」V2 版论文

ChinaXiv, the preprint platform of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, shows that He Tingbo, head of Huawei Semiconductor, released V2 of "Time Miniaturization Theory for Multi-level Electronic Systems" on July 3. Compared to V1 released on May 25, the new version adds engineering implementation details, measured quantitative data, and product evolution roadmap. 中国科学院科技论文预发布平台 ChinaXiv 显示，华为半导体负责人何庭波于 7 月 3 日发布《面向多层级电子系统的时间缩微理论》V2 版本。相比 5 月 25 日发布的 V1，新版补充工程落地细节、实测量化数据和产品演进路线。

V2 organizes post-Moore era scaling theory around the time constant τ, adding schematic diagrams such as the τ hierarchical spatiotemporal model, LogicFolding architecture, bonded interface cross-section, Unified Bus interconnect architecture, and Hi-ONE optical engine. V2 版围绕时间常数 τ 组织后摩尔时代缩放理论，新增 τ 分层时空模型、LogicFolding 架构、键合界面截面、Unified Bus 互连架构和 Hi-ONE 光引擎等示意图。

The paper also explains the gear ratio concept of LogicFolding: when the hybrid bonding pitch approaches the top metal routing size, 3D design can shift from macro-block level discrete optimization to cell-level continuous optimization. 论文还解释 LogicFolding 的齿比概念：当混合键合间距接近顶层金属布线尺寸时，3D 设计可从宏块级离散优化转向单元级连续优化。

The new version also adds voltage, frequency, normalized power consumption, area, and power density parameters for Kirin 2026 and the benchmark Kirin 9030 Pro, and refines mobile TSV downshifting, multi-active layer stacking, and the iteration rhythm of the Ascend series accelerators. 新版还加入 Kirin 2026 与基准 Kirin9030 Pro 的电压、频率、归一化功耗、面积和功率密度参数，并细化移动端 TSV 下移、多有源层堆叠，以及 Ascend 系列加速器迭代节奏。

《华尔街日报》：科技巨头 AI 数据中心的耗水量比想象中大得多

According to The Wall Street Journal, tech giants like Microsoft, Google, and Amazon are expected to invest $1 trillion in 2025 and this year to build AI infrastructure, but their underlying water consumption far exceeds official disclosures. 据《华尔街日报》报道，微软、Google、亚马逊等科技巨头在 2025 年及今年预计投入 1 万亿美元建设 AI 基础设施，但其背后的水资源消耗远超官方披露。

Research shows that due to the exclusion of indirect water use from power generation, the actual water consumption of US data centers has historically been about 12 times their reported values . 研究显示，由于未计算发电环节的间接用水， 美国数据中心的实际耗水量历来约为其报告值的 12 倍 。

Currently, only Meta accounts for indirect water consumption in its reports, with its indirect water use reaching 19 billion gallons in 2024, more than 20 times its direct water use. 目前，仅 Meta 一家在报告中核算间接耗水，其 2024 年间接用水达 190 亿加仑，是直接用水的 20 倍以上。

Google's direct water consumption last year was 10.9 billion gallons, a 34% year-on-year increase, but its indirect water consumption is estimated to be three times that amount. Although giants procure renewable energy for offsets, critics argue this does not solve the problem of resource depletion in specific water-scarce areas. Google 去年直接耗水 109 亿加仑，同比增长 34%，但其间接用水量预计为该数值的三倍。虽然巨头们采购可再生能源进行抵消，但批评者认为这无法解决特定缺水区域的资源枯竭问题。

Attracted by cheap land and electricity, about two-thirds of new data centers in the US are located in water-scarce areas like Phoenix. By 2031, the total water consumption of Phoenix data centers could soar from 3% to over 20%. 由于廉价土地和电力的吸引，美国约三分之二的新建数据中心位于凤凰城等缺水地区。到 2031 年，凤凰城数据中心的总用水量占比或将从 3% 飙升至 20% 以上。

Although Nvidia and Microsoft are promoting "closed-loop cooling" technology that requires no additional water replenishment, a large number of existing high-water-consumption evaporative cooling systems will still face severe water resource pressure due to expensive retrofitting. 尽管英伟达和微软正在推行无需额外补水的「闭环冷却」技术，但现有大量高耗水的蒸发式冷却系统由于改造昂贵，仍将面临严峻的水资源压力。

💡 宇树科技陈立：具身智能的「ChatGPT 时刻」需达成两个 80%

According to Cailian Press, Chen Li, co-founder of Unitree Robotics, recently stated at the 12th Innovation Annual Conference of the 2026 Yabuli China Entrepreneurs Forum that embodied AI robots have not yet achieved widespread application, primarily because embodied AI large models are not yet mature. 据财联社报道，宇树科技联合创始人陈立日前在 2026 亚布力中国企业家论坛第十二届创新年会上表示，具身智能机器人目前尚未实现大面积应用，核心原因在于具身智能大模型尚未成熟。

In his speech, Chen Li defined the "ChatGPT moment" for embodied AI, whose core marker is "two 80%s": robots successfully completing approximately 80% of tasks in 80% of unfamiliar scenarios through voice or text commands. 陈立在演讲中定义了具身智能的「ChatGPT 时刻」，其核心标志为「两个 80%」：机器人在 80% 的陌生场景中，通过语音或文字指令能够顺利完成约 80% 的任务。

He predicted that in vertical B2B scenarios, the goal of fully autonomously solving 80% of tasks is expected to be achieved; while in B2C scenarios, which require higher generalization capabilities, it may take 8 to 10 years or even longer to mature. 他预计，在垂直的 To B 场景中，完全自主解决 80% 任务的目标有望达成；而在对泛化能力要求更高的 To C 场景，预计可能需要 8 至 10 年甚至更久才能成熟。

Regarding the industry's development over the next 2 to 5 years, Chen Li proposed three key elements: 针对未来 2 至 5 年的行业发展，陈立提出了三大关键要素：

  • A unified end-to-end large robot model is needed; 需要统一的端到端机器人大模型；
  • Hardware with lower cost and longer lifespan is needed; 需要更低成本、更高寿命的硬件；
  • Low-cost, large-scale computing power. 低成本的大规模算力。

He pointed out that due to limitations in endurance, heat dissipation, and cost for edge computing power, and latency issues with cloud computing, the future form of computing power may be distributed. 他指出，由于端侧算力存在续航、散热和成本限制，而云端存在延时问题，未来的算力形式可能是分布式的。
