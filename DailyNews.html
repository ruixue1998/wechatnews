郭明錤：小米 17 系列出货量下调 20%，标准版需求远低于预期

Renowned analyst Ming-Chi Kuo posted on X yesterday, stating that the total shipments of Xiaomi's new Xiaomi 17 series have been significantly lowered by about 20%, from the original target of approximately 10 million units to less than 8 million units, which may be lower than the previous 15 series. 知名分析师郭明錤昨天在 X 上发表帖文称，小米新款 Xiaomi 17 系列总出货量显著下调约 20%，由原先目标约 1,000 万部降至不足 800 万部，恐低于前代 15 系列水平。

  • Reasons for the reduction: Demand for the 17 standard version was much lower than expected, originally estimated to account for 50% – 55%, but actually only about 15% – 20%. Although Pro and Pro Max orders increased, they were not enough to offset the cut in standard version orders; 下调原因：17 标准版需求远低于预期，原估占比 50% – 55%，实际仅约 15% – 20%。尽管 Pro 与 Pro Max 订单增加，但不足以抵消标准版砍单；
  • Competitive pressure: Ming-Chi Kuo believes that the iPhone 17 standard version's better-than-expected performance in the Chinese market is one of the main reasons for the sluggish demand for the Xiaomi 17 standard version; 竞争压力：郭明錤认为，iPhone 17 标准版在中国市场表现优于预期，是小米 17 标准版需求不振的主要因素之一；
  • Market challenges: Ming-Chi Kuo pointed out that Xiaomi will face two major challenges in the Chinese high-end market in 2026: 市场挑战：郭明錤指出，2026 年小米在中国高端市场将面临两大挑战：
  • Continuous pressure from Apple's iPhone 17e (first half) and high-end series (second half); 苹果 iPhone 17e（上半年）与高端系列（下半年）的持续压力；
  • Huawei's high-end models face limited demand due to compatibility issues with "Pure HarmonyOS," but this is expected to improve in 2026. 华为高端机型因「纯血鸿蒙」兼容性问题需求受限，但预计 2026 年将改善。

Furthermore, he stated that if sales during the National Day holiday do not improve, Xiaomi might stimulate demand through price reductions. Looking ahead to 2026, the competitive strategy and edge AI deployment of the Xiaomi 18 series will be key to whether it can return to growth. 此外，他还表示，若十一长假销售未见改善，小米可能通过降价刺激需求。展望 2026 年，Xiaomi 18 系列的竞争策略与端侧 AI 布局将成为能否重返增长的关键。

In stark contrast, Xiaomi Group President Lu Weibing posted on Weibo yesterday afternoon, stating that "the Xiaomi 17 series is confident that its sales will be higher than the previous Xiaomi 15 series," and attributed the lower-than-expected sales of the Xiaomi 17 standard version to the temporary addition of a 1TB version. 与此形成鲜明对比的是，小米集团总裁卢伟冰昨天下午在微博发文表示，「小米17系列有信心销量会比上代小米15系列更多」，并称小米 17 标准版销量不及预期是临时增加 1TB 版本所致。

Lei Jun reposted this Weibo post with the caption "Congratulations to the phone team ❤️❤️❤️". 雷军转发了此条微博，并配文「祝贺手机团队 ❤️❤️❤️」。

「Mac 不会感染病毒」神话正在加速破灭

Recently, MacPaw's cybersecurity division Moonlock released its "Mac Security Survey 2025" report, which shows that the long-standing myth of "Macs being immune to malware" is rapidly being debunked. 日前，MacPaw 网络安全部门 Moonlock 发布《Mac Security Survey 2025》调查报告，显示长期流传的「Mac 免疫恶意软件」的神话正在加速破灭。

According to the report, the survey was conducted in June 2025, covering 1,000 adult American users. All respondents were recruited through the Cint survey platform and completed the questionnaire online, with an average completion time of about 10 minutes. The statistical margin of error for the results was approximately ±3 percentage points. 据介绍，此次调查于 2025 年 6 月进行，覆盖 1,000 名美国成年用户，所有受访者均通过 Cint 调研平台招募，并以在线形式完成问卷，平均答题时长约 10 分钟，结果的统计误差约为 ±3 个百分点。

The report indicates that only 15% of macOS users still believe that Macs are not susceptible to malware infection, a significant drop from 28% in 2023. 报告指出，仅有 15% 的 macOS 用户仍然认为 Mac 不会受到恶意软件感染，而在 2023 年这一比例高达 28%。

This change reflects a gradual shift in public perception of Mac security; the past impression that "Macs are safer than Windows" is slowly collapsing in the face of frequent malware and cyberattacks. 这一变化反映了大众用户对 Mac 安全性的认知正在逐步转变，过去「Mac 比 Windows 更安全」的印象在频繁出现的恶意软件与网络攻击面前正逐渐崩塌。

The survey also showed that 66% of respondents had experienced at least one cyber threat in the past year, and 31% explicitly stated they had been affected by malware. 调查还显示，66% 的受访者在过去一年中至少遭遇过一次网络威胁，31% 明确表示曾被恶意软件波及。

Meanwhile, 72% of users are concerned that generative AI will accelerate the growth of cyber threats, while only 34% believe that AI will provide stronger security. 与此同时，72% 的用户担心生成式 AI 将加速网络威胁的增长，而仅有 34% 的人认为 AI 会带来更强的安全保障。

Moonlock emphasizes that although Apple provides built-in security tools in macOS such as the "Passwords" App and FileVault, relying solely on system protection is not enough to withstand increasingly sophisticated attacks. Moonlock 强调，尽管苹果在 macOS 中提供了「密码」App、文件保险箱等内置安全工具，但仅依赖系统防护并不足以抵御日益复杂的攻击。

The report also shows that 48% of users still reuse passwords across multiple accounts, 59% save passwords in their browsers, and 25% frequently skip system updates, behaviors that expose them to greater risk. 报告还显示，48% 的用户仍在多个账户间重复使用密码，59% 将密码保存在浏览器中，25% 经常跳过系统更新，这些行为使他们暴露在更大的风险之下。

微信 iOS 更新：聊天支持发送实况照片，误删对话 5 秒内可撤销

Recently, WeChat for iOS officially pushed version 8.0.61. The official WeChat account "WeChat Pai" published an article introducing two major new features brought by this update: 近日，微信 iOS 端正式推送 8.0.61 版本，微信官方在公众号「微信派」发表文章，介绍了本次更新带来的两大新特性：

Chat now supports sending Live Photos: Users can tap "Photos" in the chat box to select and send Live Photos, which include dynamic visuals and sound, consistent with "Live Photo" effects in albums. 聊天支持发送实况照片： 用户在聊天框点击「照片」即可选择实况照片发送，实况照片包含动态画面与声音，效果与相册中的「Live Photo」一致。

It is understood that this feature previously only supported scenarios like Moments and Status, but after the update, it has been extended to one-on-one and group chats. 据悉，该功能此前仅支持朋友圈、状态等场景，更新后扩展至一对一及群聊对话。

Accidentally deleted chats can be undone: When a user swipes left to delete a single friend or group chat, an "Undo" prompt will appear at the bottom of the interface. Clicking it within 5 seconds will restore the deleted conversation, preventing loss of chat history due to accidental operations. 误删对话可撤销： 当用户左滑删除单个好友或群聊对话后，界面底部会出现「撤销」提示，5 秒内点击即可恢复已删除的对话，避免因误操作导致的聊天记录丢失。

It should be noted that undo is only available for the most recent deletion operation and cannot be recovered after the timeout. 需要注意的是，撤销仅限于最近一次删除操作，超时后无法恢复。

Currently, this update has been pushed to iOS users, and can be experienced by upgrading to version 8.0.61 or higher. 目前，该更新已面向 iOS 用户推送，升级至 8.0.61 及以上版本即可体验。

F-Droid 警告 Google 新规或使第三方应用商店难以为继

Yesterday, open-source app store F-Droid stated in its latest announcement that Google's upcoming "developer registration regulations" could put independent Android app stores into an existential crisis. 昨天，开源应用商店 F-Droid 在最新声明中表示，谷歌即将实施的「开发者注册规定」可能会让独立安卓应用商店陷入生存危机。

According to the new regulations, all Android developers must submit personal information such as government ID, and directly report app identifiers and signing keys to Google. 根据新规，所有 Android 开发者必须提交政府身份证件等个人信息，并将应用标识符和签名密钥直接上报谷歌。

This means that even if an app is not distributed through Google Play, it must still follow Google's unified verification process. 这意味着，即便应用不是通过 Google Play 分发，也必须遵循谷歌的统一验证流程。

F-Droid pointed out that this requirement is equivalent to cutting off the operational foundation of third-party stores, as they can neither complete registration on behalf of developers nor possess developers' keys. F-Droid 指出，这一要求等于切断了第三方商店的运作基础。因为它们既无法代替开发者完成注册，也不能掌握开发者的密钥。

Once the policy is fully implemented, F-Droid will no longer be able to provide app updates or installation services to users. The team stated bluntly: "This regulation will end the operation of F-Droid and other open-source distribution channels." 一旦政策全面落地，F-Droid 将无法继续为用户提供应用更新或安装服务。该团队直言：「这项规定将终结 F-Droid 及其他开源分发渠道的运营。」

Google, on its part, emphasized that the new regulations are aimed at improving security and preventing the spread of malware. 谷歌方面则强调，新规是为了提升安全性、防止恶意软件传播。

However, F-Droid countered that Google Play itself has had multiple cases of malicious apps, and the Android system already has security mechanisms like Play Protect. In comparison, the open-source model offers higher transparency and trustworthiness. 但 F-Droid 反驳称，Google Play 本身也多次出现恶意应用案例，而安卓系统已有 Play Protect 等安全机制。相比之下，开源模式的透明度和可信度更高。

More worryingly, users may only be able to rely on official Google channels to obtain apps in the future, significantly weakening the openness of the Android ecosystem. F-Droid calls on regulatory bodies to intervene and investigate, to prevent Google from further strengthening its monopoly under the guise of "security." 更令人担忧的是，用户未来可能只能依赖谷歌官方渠道获取应用，安卓生态的开放性将被大幅削弱。F-Droid 呼吁监管机构介入调查，防止谷歌借「安全」之名进一步强化垄断。

Google plans to implement this policy in phases starting from September 2026, at which point the prospects for third-party app stores will face severe challenges. 谷歌计划自 2026 年 9 月起分阶段推行该政策，届时第三方应用商店的前景将面临严峻考验。

特斯拉前员工被机器人打成重伤，已起诉公司索赔 5100 万美元

According to Manufacturing Dive, a former Tesla factory technician recently filed a lawsuit in the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of California, accusing Tesla and robot manufacturer Fanuc of negligence in safety management, leading to him being "suddenly and without warning" struck by an industrial robot arm while working at the Fremont factory in July 2023, causing him to lose consciousness on the spot. 据 Manufacturing Dive 报道，日前，一名前特斯拉工厂技术员在美国加州北区法院提起诉讼，指控特斯拉及机器人制造商 Fanuc 疏于安全管理，导致其在 2023 年 7 月于弗里蒙特工厂作业时被工业机器人手臂「突然且毫无预警」击中，当场失去意识。

The complaint states that the worker, named Peter Hinterdobler, was assisting engineers in disassembling a Fanuc industrial robot at the time. 诉状显示，该工人名为 Peter Hinterdobler，当时正协助工程师拆解一台 Fanuc 工业机器人。

The robot arm released a force equivalent to "approximately 8,000 pounds of counterweight," knocking him to the ground. The plaintiff claims to have suffered severe physical, mental, and economic damages as a result, with medical expenses totaling approximately $1 million, and is currently seeking total compensation of $51 million. 机器人手臂释放的力量相当于「约 8000 磅的平衡重」，将其击倒在地。原告称因此遭受严重身体、精神及经济损失，医疗费用已累计约 100 万美元，目前索赔总额达 5100 万美元。

The document also pointed out that the robot involved was temporarily placed in an "unspecified area," and workers interacted with it in a "reasonably foreseeable manner." 文件还指出，涉事机器人被临时安置在「非指定区域」，工人以「合理可预见的方式」与其交互。

After the accident, Tesla reportedly implemented new lifting and safety protocols for this robot model. The plaintiff also stated that the company possesses accident videos but has not provided them despite multiple requests. 事故发生后，特斯拉据称已针对该型号机器人实施新的吊装与安全规程。原告同时表示，公司掌握事故视频，但多次请求仍未获提供。

Tesla has filed a motion to dismiss, citing the "exclusive remedy provision" in California's Workers' Compensation Act as a defense. 特斯拉方面已提出驳回动议，援引加州《工人补偿法》中的「唯一救济条款」作为抗辩。

It is understood that this factory is Tesla's largest production base in California, responsible for the manufacturing of Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y. 据了解，该厂区是特斯拉在加州最大的生产基地，负责 Model S、Model 3、Model X 与 Model Y 的制造。
