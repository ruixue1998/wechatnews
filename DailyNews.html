Apple 50 周年，苹果 CEO Tim Cook 给所有用户写了封信

On April 1st this year, Apple will officially celebrate its 50th birthday. On the occasion of this extraordinary milestone, Apple CEO Tim Cook wrote an open letter to global users, thanking those who dare to pursue different ideas and truly change the world. 今年 4 月 1 日，Apple 将正式迎来 50 周岁生日。在这个意义非凡的里程碑之际，苹果 CEO Tim Cook 给全球用户写了一封公开信，感谢那些敢于追求不同想法、并真正改变世界的人。

In the letter, Apple laid several "core Easter eggs," which are not only a microcosm of half a century of technological evolution but also a return to its original intention: 信中，Apple 埋下了几个「核心彩蛋」，它们不仅是半个世纪科技进化的缩影，更是对初心的回溯：

  • Reshaping a classic slogan: The letter's title "50 Years of Thinking Different" directly reuses Apple's classic advertising slogan "Think Different" (Think Differently) from 1997, which changed business history. Changing "Think" to "Thinking" emphasizes the continuous practice over the past half-century; 重塑经典口号： 信件标题「50 Years of Thinking Different」直接化用了 Apple 1997 年改变商业史的经典广告语「Think Different」（不同凡想）。将 Think 改为 Thinking，强调了这半个世纪以来的持续践行；
  • Return of the retro rainbow Logo: The colorful stripes at the top of the official visual are a variant of Apple's early rainbow Logo used from 1976 to 1998. Re-enabling it for the 50th anniversary is a full embrace of nostalgia; 复古彩虹 Logo 回归： 官方视觉顶部的彩色条纹，正是 Apple 1976 年至 1998 年期间使用的早期彩虹 Logo 变体。50 周年重新启用，情怀拉满；
  • Back to that "garage": The opening line of the letter, "Fifty years ago, in an unassuming garage," directly echoes the legendary origin of Apple founded by Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak. The subsequent list (Apple Computer ➡️ Mac ➡️ iPod ➡️ iPhone ➡️ iPad/Watch/AirPods) is a complete product evolution timeline; 重回那间「车库」： 信件开篇的一句「五十年前，在一个不起眼的车库」，直接呼应了乔布斯与沃兹尼亚克创立 Apple 的传奇起源。随后列举的（Apple 电脑 ➡️ Mac ➡️ iPod ➡️ iPhone ➡️ iPad/Watch/AirPods），则是一条完整的产品进化时间线；
  • Homage to "the crazy ones": The end of the letter fully quotes the most classic narration from the 1997 "Think Different" advertisement: "Here’s to the crazy ones… The ones who see things differently." This is not only a declaration of Apple's brand spirit but also a reaffirmation of the philosophy Jobs most advocated during his lifetime. 致敬「疯狂的人」： 信件末尾完整引用了 1997 年「Think Different」广告最经典的旁白：「Here’s to the crazy ones… The ones who see things differently.」 这不仅是 Apple 品牌精神的宣言，更是对乔布斯生前最推崇理念的再次强化。

Tim Cook wrote in the letter: "What truly inspires us has never been what technology itself can do, but what you can achieve with technology." The most exciting chapters of every innovation are, in fact, personally written by each user. Tim Cook 在信中写道：「真正激励我们的，从来不是科技本身能做什么，而是你能用科技实现什么。」每一项创新的最精彩篇章，其实正由每一位用户亲自撰写。

蔚来李斌：成立时就预期盈利需 10 年，首次单季转正「只是回归常识」

According to Tencent News, after Nio announced its first quarterly profit in 11 years since its establishment, founder and CEO William Li stated at a media briefing in Shanghai that Nio had expected it would take at least 10 years to achieve profitability from its inception, which is a basic rule of the automotive industry. 据腾讯新闻报道，在蔚来公布成立 11 年来的首次单季盈利后，创始人兼 CEO 李斌在上海举行的媒体沟通会上表示，蔚来从成立之初就预期盈利至少需要 10 年时间，这是汽车行业的基本规律。

He emphasized that profitability is not a "surprise moment" but the normal state of business operations. 他强调，盈利并非「惊喜时刻」，而是企业经营应有的常态。

Li Bin said that the "sense of shame" from external perceptions of losses is not important; the key is to spend money wisely, avoid fatal mistakes, and keep the company "alive." 李斌称，外界对亏损的「耻感」并不重要，关键在于把钱花在刀刃上、避免致命错误，让公司「活下去」。

He pointed out that Nio has shifted from "burning money for scale" to "precise calculation for profit," and the internal CBU mechanism's cost reduction and efficiency improvement have become part of the company's culture. He cited an example where an R&D project that typically costs 30 million yuan in the industry was completed by Nio for 2 million yuan with better results. 他指出，蔚来从过去的「烧钱换规模」转向「精算求盈利」，内部 CBU 机制带来的降本增效已成为公司文化的一部分。他举例称，行业普遍需要 3000 万元的研发项目，蔚来最终以 200 万元完成且效果更好。

The financial report shows that in the fourth quarter of last year, Nio's overall average selling price per vehicle increased quarter-on-quarter to 253,000 yuan. Driven by the strong sales of the ES8, single-quarter vehicle sales revenue grew by 60% to 31.6 billion yuan, and the gross profit margin rose to 18.1%. 财报显示，去年第四季度蔚来整体单车售价环比提升至 25.3 万元，受 ES8 热销带动，单季卖车收入增长六成至 316 亿元，毛利率升至 18.1%。

Under the combined effect of reduced expenses and increased gross profit, the company recorded a Non-GAAP net profit of 720 million yuan, marking its first positive turn in history. 在费用下降与毛利提升的共同作用下，公司 Non-GAAP 净利润录得 7.2 亿元，为历史首次转正。

于东来公布胖东来 40 亿元分配细则：不要再猜想了

Pang Donglai founder Yu Donglai recently responded consecutively on social media to external discussions about the company's "4 billion yuan asset distribution plan," emphasizing that the relevant information is true and urging outsiders "not to speculate further." 胖东来创始人于东来近日连续在社交平台回应外界对公司「40 亿元资产分配方案」的讨论，强调相关信息均属实，并呼吁外界「不要再猜想了」。

Yu Donglai stated that over the past 26 years, Pang Donglai has consistently adhered to an operational model based on systems and shareholding. Early shares were mostly arranged through gratuitous gifts or nominee holdings, with distribution following principles of fairness and standards set according to job value. 于东来表示，过去 26 年间，胖东来始终坚持依靠制度和股份制的运营模式，早期股份多以无偿赠予或代持方式安排，分配遵循公平原则，按岗位价值制定标准。

He emphasized that the core of the distribution system lies in stimulating employee potential, making employees realize that their income comes from their own abilities, not from external charity. 他强调，分配制度的核心在于激发员工潜能，让员工意识到收入来源于自身能力，而非外界施舍。

The asset distribution plan announced on March 8 shows that Pang Donglai will distribute approximately 4 billion yuan in assets, with 50% allocated to the management team and 50% to employees, to serve as future company capital. Annual profits will continue to be distributed as "50% team bonuses, 50% shareholder returns." 3 月 8 日公布的资产分配方案显示，胖东来将约 40 亿元资产按管理团队与员工各 50% 的比例分配，并作为未来公司股本，每年利润继续按「50% 团队奖金、50% 股东收益」的方式执行。

According to the disclosed details, the group has a total of 10,194 employees and total assets of approximately 3.793 billion yuan. Among these, 12 store managers each received 20 million yuan, totaling 240 million yuan; two highest-level personnel in the technical team each received 10 million yuan; and ordinary employees received approximately 200,000 yuan per person. 根据披露的明细，集团共有员工 10194 人，总资产约 37.93 亿元，其中 12 名店长每人分配 2000 万元，总计 2.4 亿元；技术团队两名最高级别人员人均可获 1000 万元；普通员工人均约 20 万元。

Yu Donglai further pointed out in his response that a company's distribution system not only concerns employee happiness but also the long-term happiness of entrepreneurs and investors. Only when all three parties benefit can the company develop healthily. He emphasized that increasing the income of the management team and employees is the most scientific and effective way for ordinary enterprises. 于东来在回应中进一步指出，企业分配制度不仅关乎员工幸福，也关系到企业家与投资者的长期幸福，只有三方共同受益，企业才能健康发展。他强调，提高管理团队与员工收入，是普通企业最科学有效的方式。

Pang Donglai also explicitly stated that it will maintain its positioning as a "school-like enterprise" and will never go public , and will cease scale expansion after completing sample city planning. The highest management must retire from positions of power before the age of 60 to maintain organizational youthfulness and vitality. In February this year, Yu Donglai announced his official retirement after the Spring Festival. 胖东来还明确 将保持「学校式企业」定位，永不上市 ，并在完成样本城市规划后停止规模扩张；最高管理层需在 60 岁前退出权力岗位，以保持组织年轻化与活力。今年 2 月，于东来已宣布春节后正式退休。

受 AI 冲击，Adobe CEO 将卸任

According to Bloomberg, Adobe has confirmed that CEO Shantanu Narayen, who has led the company for nearly 18 years, will step down after the company selects a successor, but will continue to serve as chairman. 据彭博社报道，Adobe 已确认执掌公司近 18 年的 CEO Shantanu Narayen 将在公司选定继任者后卸任，但会继续担任董事长职务。

Adobe stated that the successor selection process has begun, with independent board director Frank Calderoni overseeing the process. In his statement, he said the company would seek a suitable leader to guide Adobe into its next phase of growth and thanked Narayen for continuing as CEO during the transition to ensure a smooth handover. Adobe 表示，继任者遴选工作已启动，董事会独立董事 Frank Calderoni 将负责监督流程。他在声明中称，公司将寻找能够带领 Adobe 进入下一阶段增长的合适领导者，并感谢 Narayen 在过渡期继续担任 CEO 以确保平稳交接。

Since the beginning of this year, affected by the impact of generative AI and investor concerns about the software industry's prospects, Adobe's stock price has cumulatively fallen by approximately 23%, reaching a three-year low. 今年以来，受生成式 AI 冲击及投资者对软件行业前景的担忧影响，Adobe 股价累计下跌约 23%，创近三年低位。

In terms of financial performance, Adobe's latest quarterly data shows revenue grew by approximately 12% year-over-year to $6.4 billion, exceeding market expectations; annualized revenue from AI-first products more than tripled year-over-year. The company expects next quarter's revenue to be in the range of $6.43 billion to $6.48 billion, slightly above market consensus. 在财务表现方面，Adobe 公布的最新季度数据显示，营收同比增长约 12% 至 64 亿美元，超出市场预期；AI-first 产品的年化收入同比增长超过三倍。公司预计下一季度营收将达到 64.3 亿至 64.8 亿美元区间，略高于市场一致预期。

However, Adobe Stock's business experienced a greater-than-expected decline this quarter. Management stated that the rapid popularization of generative AI is accelerating changes in user behavior, and the company will continue to offer more choices between traditional assets and AI-generated content. 不过，Adobe Stock 业务在本季度出现超预期下滑，管理层称生成式 AI 的快速普及正在加速用户行为变化，公司将继续在传统素材与 AI 生成内容之间提供更多选择。

OPPO 手机也吃上龙虾，陈希晒小布 Claw 实机演示

Yesterday, Chen Xi, Design Director of ColorOS, demonstrated multiple capabilities of Xiaobu Claw on Weibo, from local content retrieval to cross-device collaboration, presenting an intelligent interactive experience closer to an "open world" style. 昨天，ColorOS 设计总监陈希在微博展示了小布 Claw 的多项能力，从本地内容调取到跨端协同，呈现出更接近「开放世界」式的智能交互体验。

In the demonstration, Chen Xi sent a command to Xiaobu, asking it to read the audio file of the broadband installation communication with the operator and generate a core summary. 在演示中，陈希向小布发送指令，请其读取与运营商沟通宽带报装的录音文件，并生成核心摘要。

Xiaobu then retrieved the 17-minute call content with China Telecom on February 17, extracted key information such as the installation address, converged broadband package price, suspension and retention fees, and reasons for incomplete processing (e.g., requiring original ID verification), and identified follow-up items. 小布随后调取了 2 月 17 日与中国电信的 17 分钟通话内容，提取出报装地址、融合宽带套餐价格、停机保号费用、未完成办理的原因（如需身份证原件核验）等关键信息，并识别出后续需跟进的事项。

Chen Xi also demonstrated Xiaobu Claw's cross-device coordination capabilities by directly sending commands from a tablet to Xiaobu on a phone, allowing it to view browsing history on Find N6's Xiaohongshu and generate a summary. After the task is completed, the results are synchronized back to the tablet, along with actionable follow-up suggestions. 陈希还演示了小布 Claw 的跨端协调能力，通过平板直接向手机端的小布发送指令，让其查看 Find N6 上的小红书浏览记录并生成摘要。任务完成后，结果同步回平板，并附带可执行的后续操作建议。

Chen Xi stated that in addition to conventional system control, Xiaobu Claw also offers more "interesting ways to play," an experience that is "as free and imaginative as playing an open-world game for the first time." 陈希表示，小布 Claw 除了常规系统控制外，还具备更多「更有意思的玩法」，这种体验「像第一次玩开放世界游戏一样，自由、充满想象力」。

However, he also emphasized that as a mature product for the general public, Xiaobu Claw still has many fundamental capabilities and security issues to address. The team hopes to "safely raise shrimp," providing the best user experience after perfecting the underlying capabilities. 不过他也强调，作为面向大众的成熟产品，小布 Claw 仍有大量基础能力与安全问题需要解决，团队希望「安全养虾」，在完善底层能力后再向用户提供最佳体验。
