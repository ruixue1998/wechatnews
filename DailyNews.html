消息称苹果正在收购视觉 AI 创企

According to a recent CNBC report, Apple is in late-stage negotiations to acquire a computer vision startup called "Prompt AI." 据 CNBC 日前报道，苹果正在收购一家名为「Prompt AI」的计算机视觉初创公司，目前处于后期谈判状态。

The report states that Apple plans to acquire Prompt AI's engineering talent and the company's technology. It is understood that Prompt's senior management announced the transaction at an all-hands meeting this week, also stating that employees who do not join Apple will receive lower salaries and encouraging them to apply for vacant positions within the company. 报道称， 苹果计划收购 Prompt AI 的工程人才资源和该公司的技术。 据悉，Prompt 高层于本周的全员会议中公布了上述交易，同时表示最后没有加入苹果的员工将会获得较低薪资，并鼓励他们申请公司空缺的职位。

Additionally, Prompt's senior management also stated that potential buyers, including Elon Musk's xAI and his brain-computer interface company Neuralink, had expressed interest in contacting Prompt. 另外，Prompt 高层还表示，马斯克旗下的 xAI 和其脑机公司 Neuralink 在内的潜在买家，也提出了向 Prompt 接触的意愿。

Prompt was founded in 2023 and secured $5 million in seed funding that year, led by AIX and Abstract Ventures. Co-founder and CEO Tete Xiao holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of California, Berkeley, and is also the founder of the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) Lab. 据悉，Prompt 成立于 2023 年，并在当年获得了由 AIX 和 Abstract Ventures 领投的 500 万美元种子轮融资。联合创始人及 CEO Tete Xiao 为加州大学伯克利分校计算机科学博士，同时也是伯克利人工智能研究（BAIR）实验室的创始人。

According to the introduction, Prompt has a flagship application called "Seemour," which can connect to home security cameras and monitor people, dogs, cats, or other living beings and objects in the home that need attention, sending alerts about unusual activities and based on scene descriptions. 据介绍，Prompt 旗下拥有一个名为「Seemour」的旗舰应用，能够连接家庭式安全摄像头，并通过摄像头对家中需要关照的人、小狗小猫或其他生命、物体进行看管，并能够发送关于异常活动的警报和基于场景描述。

Regarding the aforementioned acquisition, media outlet "The Information" also offered its perspective. It believes that this deal continues Apple's tradition of absorbing small technology companies, primarily for talent rather than acquiring large corporations. 针对上述收购，媒体《The Information》也给出了看法。 其认为这笔交易延续了苹果吸收小型技术公司的传统，主要是为了人才，而不是收购大型公司。

"The Information" previously reported that Apple is generally cautious about large acquisitions because integrating large teams into Apple's unique corporate culture can be quite complex. 《The Information》此前层报道，由于将大型团队融入苹果独特的公司文化会较为复杂，因此苹果通常对大型收购持谨慎态度。

中国联通开启 eSIM 业务预约，人数破 3.9 万

According to a report by C114 Communication Network on October 10, China Unicom recently officially opened its eSIM service reservation channel, with online reservations exceeding 39,000 people. 据 C114 通信网 10 月 10 日报道， 中国联通近期正式开启 eSIM 业务预约通道，线上预约人数已超 3.9 万人。

According to China Unicom's official introduction, both the APP and self-operated business halls support eSIM services. For details, please consult your local China Unicom or the 10010 customer service hotline. 据中国联通官方介绍，APP 和自有营业厅均支持办理 eSIM 业务，具体需详询当地联通或 10010 客服热线。

Previously, China Mobile officially launched its eSIM reservation interface on September 21st. Just 4 hours after its launch, the system showed that the number of people in the reservation queue exceeded 150,000. 此前， 中国移动已于 9 月 21 日正式上线 eSIM 预约界面，上线仅 4 小时，系统即显示预约排队人数超过 15 万。

In contrast, China Mobile provides more detailed explanations of its business rules, including that the total number of eSIM + physical SIM numbers processed with the same ID card must not exceed 5, a single eSIM phone can activate a maximum of 2 domestic operator numbers, and the number of monthly card replacements for a single number is limited to 5 times. 相较之下，中国移动在业务规则说明方面更为详尽，包括同一身份证办理的 eSIM+实体 SIM 号码总数不得超过 5 个、单台 eSIM 手机最多激活 2 个国内运营商号码、单个号码每月补换卡次数限制为 5 次等。

It is worth noting that China Mobile also stated that during the commercial trial period for eSIM services, applications are only supported through offline self-operated business halls. Users need to go to a business hall for appointment registration, and entrusted agency, online applications, and applications from overseas users are not currently supported. 值得注意的是，中国移动还表示，eSIM 业务商用试验期间仅支持通过线下自由营业厅办理，用户需到营业厅进行预约登记，暂不支持委托代办、线上办理及境外用户申请。

In September this year, Apple officially released the new ultra-thin model iPhone Air, which features a new eSIM design without a physical SIM card slot, and therefore could not be launched for sale immediately. 今年 9 月，苹果正式发布全新超薄机型 iPhone Air，其采用了全新的 eSIM 设计，没有实体 SIM 卡槽，因此未能第一时间启动发售。

At that time, iFanr also immediately confirmed with Apple officials, who stated that they are working closely with regulatory authorities and will launch the iPhone Air for sale as soon as approval is granted. 当时爱范儿也在第一时间与苹果官方确认，苹果官方表示，正与监管机构紧密合作，并将在获批后第一时间启动 iPhone Air 发售。

OpenAI 年度账单出炉，共计 70 亿美元

On October 10th, the institution EPOCH AI released its latest data report, revealing OpenAI's astronomical annual bill "burned" in 2024. 10 月 10 日，机构 EPOCH AI 发布最新数据报告，显示了 OpenAI 在 2024 年「烧掉」的天价年度账单。

The released data shows that OpenAI spent a total of approximately US$7 billion (approximately RMB 49.947 billion) in 2024 on leasing cloud computing power from Microsoft. Specifically: 公布的数据显示， OpenAI 在 2024 年共计花费约 70 亿美元（约合人民币 499.47 亿元） ，用于向微软租赁云计算算力。具体来看：

  • Approximately US$5 billion was used for R&D computing resources, of which US$4.5 billion was for behind-the-scenes research and various experimental runs; only US$400 million was for the final training of GPT-4.5; and US$80 million was used to train other models; 约 50 亿美元用于研发算力资源，其中 45 亿美元用于幕后研究和各种实验性运行；而仅有 4 亿美元用于 GPT-4.5 的最终训练；以及 8000 万美元用来训练其他模型；
  • Approximately US$2 billion was used for customer-facing inference computing power. 约 20 亿美元用于面向客户的推理算力。

Analysts stated that based on their calculations and cost estimates for models released by OpenAI from Q2 2024 to Q1 2025, most of OpenAI's R&D computing in 2024 was likely allocated to research, experimental training runs, or training runs for unreleased models, rather than the final primary training of released models such as GPT-4.5, GPT-4o, and o3. 机构人员分析表示，其根据对 OpenAI 从 2024 年第二季度到 2025 年第一季度发布的模型的计算和成本估计，OpenAI 在 2024 年的研发计算中， 大部分可能分配给了研究、实验训练运行或未发布模型的训练运行，而不是发布模型如 GPT-4.5、GPT-4o 和 o3 的最终主要训练。

It is worth mentioning that, according to a recent report by the Financial Times, OpenAI signed a US$1 trillion computing power cooperation agreement this year, to be used for running its AI models. 值得一提的是，据金融时报日前报道，OpenAI 今年签署了达 1 万亿美元的算力合作协议，并用于运行其人工智能模型。

Currently, NVIDIA, AMD, Oracle, and CoreWeave have already established computing power cooperation with OpenAI. These collaborations will provide OpenAI with over 20 gigawatts of computing power over the next decade, equivalent to the electricity produced by 20 nuclear reactors. 目前，已与 OpenAI 达成算力合作的已有英伟达、AMD、甲骨文、CoreWeave。而这些合作将会为 OpenAI 在未来十年内，提供超过 20 吉瓦的计算算力，电力将抵 20 个核反应堆所产。

The report states that, according to OpenAI executives' estimates, deploying 1 gigawatt of AI computing power would cost approximately US$500 billion at current prices, bringing the total cost mentioned above to about US$1 trillion. 报道称，据 OpenAI 高管估计，在当下价格下，部署 1 吉瓦的 AI 计算能力大约需要 5000 亿美元，而上述的总成本约为 1 万亿美元。

Meanwhile, a recent Reuters report indicates that OpenAI's current valuation has reached US$500 billion. According to previous reports by "The Information," OpenAI generated approximately US$4.3 billion in revenue in the first half of 2025, an increase of about 16% compared to the entire previous year. 而路透社最新报道显示，OpenAI 目前估值已经达到 5000 亿美元。据《The Information》此前报道，OpenAI 在 2025 年上半年创造了约 43 亿美元的收入，比去年全年增长了约 16%。

He emphasized that the core of AI-generated malicious rumors is the infringer's abuse of technology and trampling on the rights of others, essentially aiming to make victims retreat through psychological manipulation. Whether they are professional women, public figures, or ordinary individuals, everyone has the basic right not to be insulted by false information and does not need to bear moral pressure for the illegal and criminal acts of others. 他强调， AI 造黄谣的核心是侵权者滥用技术、践踏他人权益，其本质是想通过精神控制让受害者退缩。 无论是职场女性、公众人物还是普通个体，都拥有不被虚假信息侮辱的基本权利，无需为他人的违法犯罪行为承担道德压力。

(referred to as 'Wahaha Group') on September 12. The ownership of the 'Wahaha' trademark does not belong to the Zong Fuli family but is held by Wahaha Group.
