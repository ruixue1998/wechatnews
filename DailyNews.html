曝华为 Mate90 系列将搭载基于「韬定律」的新麒麟芯片

Kechuang Daily quoted sources as saying that Huawei plans to equip the Mate90 series, to be released this autumn, with a new Kirin chip based on the 'Tao's Law'. 科创日报援引知情人士消息称，华为计划在今年秋季发布的 Mate90 系列上搭载基于「韬定律」的新麒麟芯片。

Huawei proposed 'Tao's Law' in May as a development principle for the semiconductor industry. Its core is to systematically reduce the time constant τ, combined with technologies like logic folding, to compress signal propagation delay within chips and increase transistor density. 华为在 5 月提出「韬定律」作为半导体产业发展原则，核心是通过系统性降低时间常数 τ，结合逻辑折叠等技术，压缩芯片内部信号传播时延，提升晶体管密度。

Kechuang Daily stated that the Kirin chip, set to debut in the autumn of 2026, will be the first to adopt logic folding technology, leading to performance improvements. 科创日报称，2026 年秋季面世的麒麟芯片将率先采用逻辑折叠技术，性能会有提升。

According to Bloomberg, Apple and Broadcom have reached a new cooperation agreement to jointly develop custom chips and extend their partnership until 2031. 据彭博社报道，苹果与博通已达成一项新的合作协议，双方将共同开发定制芯片，并将合作伙伴关系延长至 2031 年。

Previous reports indicated that Apple is working on dedicated chips for glasses and AI servers. 博通昨日提交的 8-K 文件显示，此次合作将重点围绕 ASIC（专用集成电路）领域展开。这类定制芯片预计将应用于未来多代苹果产品中。此前有报道称，苹果正致力于研发用于眼镜和 AI 服务器的专用芯片。

Currently, Apple is developing its first dedicated AI server chip, codenamed 'Baltra'. This chip is considered an enhanced version of the M5 Ultra and is expected to debut in the Mac Studio as early as this year. 目前，苹果正开发代号为「Baltra」的首款专用 AI 服务器芯片。该芯片被视为 M5 Ultra 的增强版本，预计最快将于今年在 Mac Studio 中亮相。

三星第二季度利润预计增长 18 倍

According to Reuters, Samsung Electronics is expected to see its operating profit surge more than 18-fold year-on-year in the second quarter of this year, driven by soaring demand for memory chips fueled by the AI boom. 据路透社报道，受人工智能热潮带动的内存芯片需求激增影响，三星电子今年第二季度的营业利润预计将实现逾 18 倍的同比增长。

According to LSEG SmartEstimate data from a survey of 27 analysts, Samsung's operating profit for the second quarter ending June 30 is expected to reach 8.8 trillion Korean won (approximately $6.4 billion). 根据 LSEG SmartEstimate 对 27 位分析师的调查数据，三星在截至 6 月 30 日的第二季度中，营业利润有望达到 8.8 万亿韩元（约合 64 亿美元）。

The significant rebound in performance is mainly attributed to the strong showing of its core semiconductor division. Analysts expect the division's operating profit for the second quarter to reach 4.6 trillion Korean won, turning a profit. 业绩的大幅反弹主要得益于其核心半导体部门的强劲表现。分析师预计，该部门第二季度的营业利润将达到 4.6 万亿韩元，实现扭亏为盈。

In contrast, the mobile business performed relatively flatly. Affected by rising raw material costs and increased marketing expenses, Samsung's mobile business is expected to have an operating profit of approximately 2.2 trillion Korean won in the second quarter, a slight decrease from the same period last year. 相比之下，移动业务的表现则相对平淡。受原材料成本上涨及营销费用增加的影响，三星移动业务第二季度的营业利润预计约为 2.2 万亿韩元，较去年同期略有下滑。

Samsung Electronics is expected to release its preliminary second-quarter earnings brief today and plans to disclose its full financial report later this month. 三星电子预计将于今日发布第二季度初步业绩简报，并计划于本月晚些时候披露完整的财务报告。

天问二号抵达目标小行星 2016HO3，开始科学探测

The Economic Information Daily learned from the China National Space Administration that the Tianwen-2 probe, after approximately 400 days and a journey of about 1 billion kilometers, successfully rendezvoused with asteroid 2016HO3 recently, reaching a distance of about 20 kilometers from the asteroid, and began scientific exploration. 经济参考报从国家航天局获悉，天问二号探测器历经约 400 天、行程约 10 亿千米，于近日与小行星 2016HO3 成功交会，到达距离小行星约 20 千米处，并开始科学探测。

During its approach, the probe has obtained asteroid image data. The mission team also used optical navigation data to improve the asteroid's ephemeris, reducing the previously hundreds-of-kilometer-level positional error obtained through ground-based observations to the kilometer level. 探测器在抵近过程中已获得小行星影像数据，任务团队还利用光学导航数据改进小行星星历，把此前依靠地基观测得到的上百千米级位置误差缩小到千米量级。

Tianwen-2 was launched from Xichang on May 29, 2025. It first captured the asteroid on June 6, 2026, performed capture control at a distance of 30,000 kilometers from the asteroid on June 7, and reached a distance of 2,000 kilometers from the asteroid on June 19. 天问二号于 2025 年 5 月 29 日在西昌发射，2026 年 6 月 6 日首次捕获小行星，6 月 7 日在距小行星 3 万千米处实施捕获控制，6 月 19 日到达距小行星 2000 千米处。

Subsequently, Tianwen-2 will continue to conduct more detailed scientific exploration, obtaining information on the asteroid's morphology, material composition, and internal structure, in preparation for sample collection. 后续，天问二号将继续开展更精细科学探测，获取小行星形貌、物质成分和内部结构等信息，为采样做准备。

💡 哈佛研究：「AI 原生」初创公司规模缩小 25%，初级岗位正在消失

According to Business Insider, a new study by Harvard Business School and INSEAD shows that AI-native startups are reshaping workplace structures, with smaller, flatter teams and a significant reduction in demand for junior employees. 据《商业内幕》报道，哈佛商学院与 INSEAD 的一项最新研究显示，AI 原生初创公司正在重塑职场结构，其团队规模更小、更扁平，且对初级员工的需求显著减少。

The study analyzed Y Combinator startups and venture-backed U.S. startups between 2020 and 2024. It defined "AI-native startups" as companies that deeply embed AI in both their internal processes (such as assisted programming, sales, and design) and core product functionalities. 该研究分析了 2020 年至 2024 年间的 Y Combinator 初创公司以及获得风投支持的美国初创企业。研究将「AI 原生初创公司」定义为在内部流程（如辅助编程、销售和设计）及产品核心功能中均深度嵌入 AI 的企业。

Data shows that AI-native startups are on average 25% smaller than their non-AI counterparts but have a 13% higher proportion of engineers. Simultaneously, the proportion of junior employees and management in these companies decreased by about 15% each. The study points out that AI is creating a higher demand for expert-level talent, with the proportion of senior employees in AI-native companies being 20% higher. 数据显示，AI 原生初创公司的平均规模比非 AI 同类公司小 25%，但工程师比例高出 13%。与此同时，这类公司的初级员工和管理层比例分别下降了约 15%。研究指出，AI 正在创造更高的专家级人才需求，AI 原生企业中资深员工的比例高出 20%。

These companies tend to recruit male-dominated technical talent from top universities, concentrated in Silicon Valley. Researchers express concern that AI may further concentrate job opportunities among highly educated and skilled groups. If AI tools only accelerate the learning process of existing users, it could further widen the performance gap between employees and entrepreneurs from different backgrounds. 这些企业倾向于招聘来自顶尖学府、集中于硅谷且以男性为主的技术人才。研究人员对此表示担忧，认为 AI 可能将就业机会进一步集中在已具备高学历和高技术的群体中。如果 AI 工具仅能加速现有使用者的学习进程，可能会导致不同背景员工及创业者之间的绩效差距进一步扩大。

美团开源万亿参数模型 LongCat-2.0，SWE-bench 跑分超越 GPT-5.5
