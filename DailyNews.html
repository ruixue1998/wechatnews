奥特曼回应成人内容争议：我们不是世界道德警察

According to CNBC, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman recently stated in response to criticism about relaxing ChatGPT's content restrictions, "We are not the world's chosen moral police." 据 CNBC 报道，日前，OpenAI CEO Sam Altman 在回应外界对其放宽 ChatGPT 内容限制的批评时表示，「我们并非世界选出的道德警察（moral police）」。

Previously, Altman announced that adult themes, including erotic content, would be allowed online, sparking widespread controversy. 此前，奥特曼宣布将允许包括情色内容在内的成人主题上线，引发广泛争议。

He posted on social media platform X, stating that as the company develops new technical tools and alleviates "serious mental health issues," it is now able to "safely relax" most content restrictions. 他在社交媒体 X 平台发文称，随着公司开发出新的技术工具，并缓解了「严重的心理健康问题」，现在已能够「安全地放宽」大部分内容限制。

Altman emphasized that OpenAI will adhere to the principle of "treating adults as adults," but will still not allow any content that could cause harm to others. He cited that this is similar to how society sets boundaries for R-rated movies, for example. 奥特曼强调，OpenAI 将坚持「把成年人当作成年人对待」的原则，但仍不会允许任何可能对他人造成伤害的内容存在。他举例称，这与社会对 R 级电影等设定界限的方式类似。

It is worth noting that this statement contrasts with Altman's remarks in a podcast program in August this year. 值得注意的是，这一表态与奥特曼今年 8 月在播客节目中的说法形成对比。

At the time, he stated that he was "proud" that OpenAI refused to launch short-term growth-stimulating features such as "sex robot avatars." 当时他曾表示，为 OpenAI 拒绝推出「性爱机器人头像」等短期刺激增长的功能感到「自豪」。

OpenAI recently also launched parental control features and is developing an age prediction system to automatically enable youth mode for users under 18. OpenAI 近期也推出家长控制功能，并正在开发年龄预测系统，以便自动为 18 岁以下用户启用青少年模式。

Nothing：苹果 MagSafe 专利限制阻碍安卓手机普及磁吸无线充电

According to Android Authority, Nothing recently stated in a video that the Android camp's slow adoption of magnetic wireless charging is primarily due to restrictions from Apple's related patents. 据 Android Authority 报道，Nothing 近日在一段视频中直言，Android 阵营迟迟未能普及磁吸式无线充电，主要原因在于苹果相关专利的限制。

According to Nothing's content team, while Qi2 has become an open wireless charging standard, the magnet arrangement solution fully compatible with Apple MagSafe is patent-protected. Android manufacturers, if they want to bypass this restriction, can only develop their own magnetic system. 根据 Nothing 内容团队的解释，虽然 Qi2 已经成为开放的无线充电标准，但与苹果 MagSafe 完全兼容的磁铁排布方案受到专利保护，Android 厂商若要绕过这一限制，只能自行研发一套磁吸系统。

This could not only lead to inaccurate coil alignment and reduced charging efficiency but also cause additional heat issues. 这不仅可能导致线圈无法精准对齐、充电效率下降，还会带来额外的发热问题。

Nothing pointed out that developing an independent magnetic wireless charging system could cost over $10 million. For most manufacturers, such an investment is difficult to recoup, so they prefer to add magnets to protective cases rather than integrating them into the phone body. Nothing 指出，开发一套独立的磁吸无线充电系统，成本可能高达 1000 万美元以上。对于多数厂商而言，这样的投入难以收回，因此宁可选择在保护壳中加入磁铁，而非在手机本体中集成。
