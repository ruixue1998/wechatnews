苹果或「小幅重新设计」macOS 27 的液态玻璃界面

According to Bloomberg, Apple plans a visual redesign of the "Liquid Glass" interface in macOS 27, set to debut at the upcoming WWDC developer conference in June. 据彭博社报道，苹果计划在即将于六月 WWDC 开发者大会亮相的 macOS 27 中，针对「Liquid Glass」界面进行视觉重构。

The report suggests that because most Mac devices currently still use LCD screens, the rendering effects of transparency, shadows, and glass textures in the "Liquid Glass" design language are inferior to those on OLED panels, leading to reduced readability in dense text areas of the Control Center, Finder, and some sidebars. 报道认为，由于目前大部分 Mac 设备仍搭载 LCD 屏幕，「Liquid Glass」设计语言的透明度、阴影及玻璃质感的渲染效果不及 OLED 面板，导致控制中心、访达及部分侧边栏的密集文本区域出现可读性下降的问题。

To address this display flaw, Apple is internally preparing a "minor redesign" for macOS 27, focusing on optimizing the rendering mechanisms for shadows and transparency. The report also notes that iOS 27 and iPadOS 27 will include a series of interface tweaks, with the overall direction being cleanup and refinement rather than a major overhaul. 为解决这一显示瑕疵，苹果内部正在为 macOS 27 筹备一次「小幅重新设计」，重点优化阴影及透明度的渲染机制。报道同时指出，iOS 27 和 iPadOS 27 也将包含一系列界面微调，整体方向是清理和打磨，而非大幅重构。

On the functional front, the report states that Apple is testing a "Tab Organization" feature for the Safari browser, which can automatically group tabs by browsing topic. 在功能层面，报道称苹果正在测试 Safari 浏览器的「整理标签页」功能，可自动将标签页按浏览主题分组。

According to the South China Morning Post, ByteDance has raised its 2026 AI capital expenditure plan to over 200 billion yuan, an increase of at least 25% compared to the 160 billion yuan plan initially discussed at the end of last year. 据《南华早报》报道，字节跳动已将 2026 年 AI 资本开支计划上调至逾 2000 亿元人民币，较去年底初步讨论的 1600 亿元方案增幅至少 25%。

Sources say the main drivers for this increase in spending include the company's continued deep commitment to its AI business and the ongoing rise in memory chip costs. Notably, the report states that ByteDance also increased the proportion of domestically produced AI chips purchased in this budget adjustment. 知情人士称，此次增支的主要驱动因素包括公司对 AI 业务的持续深度押注，以及内存芯片成本的持续上涨。值得注意的是，报道称字节跳动在本次预算调整中还提高了国产 AI 芯片的采购比例。

魏建军：汽车不是快消品，中国车企出海要保证长期可信赖

According to Sina Technology, Wei Jianjun, Chairman of Great Wall Motor, held a dialogue with several media outlets yesterday during the 2026 Great Wall Motor Smart Factory Half Marathon event, discussing the topic of Chinese car companies expanding overseas. 据新浪科技报道，长城汽车董事长魏建军昨日在 2026 长城汽车智慧工厂半程马拉松活动期间，与多家媒体就中国车企出海话题进行了对话。

美国企业开始回避「AI 原生」毕业生：会用 ChatGPT，但不会独立思考

According to Futurism, several American companies have found that "AI-native" university graduates entering the workforce in recent years generally lack depth of thought. Some employers have begun actively avoiding STEM graduates with AI proficiency, instead favoring job seekers with humanities backgrounds. 据 Futurism 报道，多家美国企业发现，近年来进入职场的「AI 原生」大学毕业生普遍存在思维深度不足的问题，部分雇主已开始主动回避具备 AI 使用能力的 STEM 毕业生，转而青睐人文学科背景的求职者。

A New York financial industry professional told Gillian Tett, a reporter for the Financial Times, that their institution currently values critical thinking skills more than AI operational skills and has adjusted its recruitment strategy accordingly. 一位纽约金融业从业者向《金融时报》记者 Gillian Tett 表示，其所在机构目前更看重批判性思维能力，而非 AI 操作技能，并已调整招聘策略。

The report points out that it has become quite common for American university students to "outsource" a large amount of their coursework to AI tools like ChatGPT. This trend not only affects students' written expression abilities but has also begun to impact the quality of participation in face-to-face classroom discussions. 报道指出，美国高校学生将课业大量「外包」给 ChatGPT 等 AI 工具的情况已相当普遍，这一趋势不仅影响了学生的书面表达能力，也开始波及课堂面对面讨论的参与质量。

Troy Jollimore, a professor of ethics at California State University, Chico, warned in an interview with The New Yorker that "a large number of students will leave university with degrees and enter the workforce, while they are effectively illiterate." 加州州立大学奇科分校伦理学教授 Troy Jollimore 在接受《纽约客》采访时警告，「大量学生将带着学位走出大学、进入职场，而他们实际上处于文盲状态」。

If your brand hasn't come alive in the real world but has in the AI world, what are you? A fraud. If your brand is thriving and very popular in the real world, then it will certainly thrive and become popular in the AI world too. 如果你的品牌在现实世界里没有活起来，但在 AI 世界里活起来了，你是什么？是骗子。如果你的品牌在现实世界活得很好，并且非常火的话，那它在 AI 世界也一定能活，并且一定会火起来。

微软开源 Phi-Ground 模型家族：让 AI 准确点击屏幕，五项评测 10B 以下全部第一

Microsoft recently open-sourced the Phi-Ground model family. This series of models focuses on a specific problem: giving AI a screenshot and an instruction, and having it accurately output the coordinates to click. 微软于近日开源了 Phi-Ground 模型家族。该系列模型专注于一个具体问题：给 AI 一张屏幕截图和一条指令，让它准确输出应该点击的坐标位置。

智源联合多家机构发布大模型安全平台 FlagSafe

The Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence (BAAI), in collaboration with Peking University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and other institutions, officially launched the FlagSafe large model security platform yesterday. The platform provides integrated security capabilities for large models, from risk discovery to defense and governance, focusing on three areas: red team exercises, blue team defense, and white-box introspection. 北京智源人工智能研究院昨日联合北京大学、上海交通大学、中国科学院等多家机构，正式发布 FlagSafe 大模型安全平台。平台围绕红队演练、蓝队防御、白盒透视三个方向，为大模型提供从风险发现到防御治理的一体化安全能力。

  • Red team exercises are responsible for actively finding vulnerabilities, supporting multi-modal evaluations for text, images, audio, and video, and conducting special detection for embodied intelligence scenarios like robots and hidden behaviors such as models being "outwardly compliant, inwardly deceptive"; 红队演练负责主动找漏洞，支持文本、图像、音视频等多模态评测，并针对机器人等具身智能场景和模型「表面顺从、内部欺骗」等隐蔽行为进行专项检测；
  • Blue team defense is responsible for patching vulnerabilities, covering model training safety alignment, AI agent runtime protection, harmful content identification and remediation, and dynamic monitoring of cyberspace risk content. White-box introspection delves deep into the model, tracing the origin of risks and pinpointing specific training data to provide a basis for remediation. 蓝队防御负责堵漏洞，覆盖模型训练安全对齐、AI 智能体运行防护、有害内容识别修复，以及网络空间风险内容的动态监测。白盒透视则深入模型内部，追踪风险从何而来，并定位到具体训练数据，为修复提供依据。

The platform sets five insurmountable red lines based on the "Beijing AI Safety International Consensus," including prohibiting autonomous replication and improvement, prohibiting assistance in designing weapons of mass destruction, and prohibiting launching destructive cyberattacks. 平台基于《北京 AI 安全国际共识》设定了五条不可逾越的红线，包括禁止自主复制改进、禁止协助设计大规模杀伤性武器、禁止发动破坏性网络攻击等。

动画电影《钟馗》定档 5 月 30 日

The animated film "Zhong Kui" officially announced its release date on May 30, targeting the family-friendly market for the June 1 Children's Day holiday. 动画电影《钟馗》官宣定档 5 月 30 日上映，主打六一儿童节档期合家欢市场。

The film features the Chinese folk mythological figure Zhong Kui as the protagonist, telling a fantastical story of human girl Chujiu accidentally entering the underworld and joining Zhong Kui to capture demons and subdue monsters. 影片以中国民间神话人物钟馗为主角，讲述人间少女初九误闯地府、与钟馗一同捉妖降魔的奇幻故事。
