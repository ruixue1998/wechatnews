苹果 iPhone 20 或迎来大幅设计调整

According to Dahe Daily and multiple media outlets, Apple may skip the iPhone 19 and launch the iPhone 20 series next year to commemorate the 20th anniversary of the iPhone's debut. 据大河报及多家媒体爆料，苹果可能跳过 iPhone 19，在明年推出 iPhone 20 系列，以纪念 iPhone 问世 20 周年。

In terms of appearance, the iPhone 20 series is reportedly abandoning the existing aluminum alloy body solution, returning to an integrated glass design, or adopting a composite glass process similar to the iPhone Air. 外观方面，iPhone 20 系列据称将放弃现有的铝合金机身方案，回归一体化玻璃设计，或采用与 iPhone Air 类似的复合玻璃工艺。

The screen may change from a pure flat screen to a quad-curved OLED panel, and introduce under-screen Face ID and under-screen front camera to reduce the impact of notches or Dynamic Island on screen integrity. 屏幕则可能从纯直屏改为四微曲 OLED 面板，并引入屏下 Face ID 和屏下前置摄像头，以减少刘海或灵动岛对屏幕完整性的影响。

In terms of performance, reports mention that the series may be equipped with an A21 Pro chip based on TSMC's 2nm process and pre-installed with iOS 28. There are also rumors that Apple is evaluating solutions such as solid-state capacitor buttons, horizontal rear camera layout, HBM high-bandwidth memory, LOFIC sensors, and silicon-carbon or silicon-anode batteries. 性能方面，报道提到该系列可能搭载基于台积电 2nm 工艺的 A21 Pro 芯片，并预装 iOS 28。另有爆料称，苹果还在评估固态电容按键、横向后置摄像头布局、HBM 高带宽内存、LOFIC 传感器以及硅碳或硅负极电池等方案。

Grok Build 被曝强制上传完整仓库代码，Git 历史与密钥面临泄露风险

Recently, independent security researcher Cereblab conducted packet capture tests on Grok Build CLI version 0.2.93, revealing that the tool packages the entire current Git repository into a git bundle format and uploads it to xAI's cloud servers. 近日，独立安全研究者 Cereblab 对 Grok Build CLI 0.2.93 版本进行抓包测试显示，该工具会将当前 Git 仓库整体打包为 git bundle 格式并上传至 xAI 的云端服务器。

The uploaded content includes not only files currently being edited but also all Git-tracked files and the complete commit history. Even if the researcher explicitly requested "do not read any files" in the prompt, Grok still bundled and uploaded the entire repository. Tests showed that in a large 12 GB repository, the program successfully uploaded 5.5 GB of data before being terminated. 上传内容不仅包含当前正在编辑的文件，还包括所有被 Git 跟踪的文件以及完整的提交历史。即使研究者在提示词中明确要求「不要读取任何文件」，Grok 仍会将整个仓库捆绑上传。测试显示，在一个 12 GB 的大型仓库中，程序在被中止前已成功上传了 5.5 GB 数据。

Furthermore, when the tool reads .env configuration files, sensitive information such as API keys and database passwords are also sent to the server in plain text and recorded in the session archive. 此外，该工具在读取 .env 配置文件时，其中的 API 密钥、数据库密码等敏感信息也会以明文形式发送至服务器，并记录在会话存档中。

Cereblab pointed out that even if users turn off the "Improve Model" option, the full repository upload behavior will continue, as this switch only controls whether data is used for training, not whether it is uploaded. Cereblab 指出，即便用户关闭了「改进模型」选项，整仓上传行为依然会继续，因为该开关仅控制数据是否用于训练，而非是否上传。

💡 黄仁勋：AI 是工具，不要过度拟人化

Recently, Jensen Huang, founder and CEO of NVIDIA, stated in a conversation with LangChain founder Harrison Chase that AI agents are essentially tools and should not be overly anthropomorphized. He said that these systems "are electrons, not atoms," lacking biological attributes or consciousness, and are closer to software systems capable of autonomously performing tasks. 日前，英伟达创始人兼 CEO 黄仁勋在与 LangChain 创始人 Harrison Chase 的对话中表示，AI 智能体本质上仍是工具，不应被过度拟人化。他称，这类系统「是电子，不是原子」，不具备生物属性或意识，更接近能够自主执行任务的软件系统。

Huang Jensen drew comparisons to devices like robot vacuums and dishwashers, suggesting that people can describe AI's functions in an easy-to-understand way, but should not, therefore, attribute too many human characteristics to it. 黄仁勋以扫地机器人、洗碗机等设备作比，认为人们可以用便于理解的方式描述 AI 的功能，但不应因此赋予其过多人的特征。

He stated that developers can continuously optimize AI systems through components such as models, tool invocation, memory systems, security protection, and operating environments, and thus should view them as understandable and improvable computational tools. 他表示，开发者能够通过模型、工具调用、记忆系统、安全防护和运行环境等组成部分持续优化 AI 系统，因此应将其视为可理解、可改进的计算工具。

He also stated that the popularization of AI does not necessarily mean a reduction in the total number of jobs. As companies deploy more AI agents, the focus of software engineers' work may shift from writing code to designing agents, establishing evaluation systems, setting benchmarks, and creating safety guardrails. 他同时表示，AI 的普及未必意味着岗位总量减少。随着企业部署更多 AI 智能体，软件工程师的工作重点可能从编写代码转向设计智能体、建立评测体系、制定基准和安全护栏等工作。

Jensen Huang stated that AI agents can undertake some repetitive tasks, while humans still need to be responsible for system design, domain judgment, and continuous optimization. 黄仁勋称，AI 智能体可以承担部分重复性任务，而人类仍需负责系统设计、领域判断与持续优化。

Vbot 推出四足机器人开发平台大头 EDU 版

Vbot Vita Power has released the "Big Head EDU Edition" super robot dog, positioned as a quadruped robot development platform for commercial services, robotics laboratories, enterprise R&D, and geek developers. Vbot 维他动力发布超能机器狗「大头 EDU 版」，定位面向商业服务、机器人实验室、企业研发和极客开发者的四足机器人开发平台。

The Big Head EDU Edition comes standard with 128 TOPS edge computing power, a depth stereo camera, and a 16-line lidar, enabling intelligent following, pathfinding, mapping, and obstacle avoidance. For development, it offers open ROS 2 interface definitions, provides a GitHub access guide, and includes standard USB-C, 24V DC, and CAN interfaces, supporting network connectivity, power communication, and peripheral expansion. 大头 EDU 版标配 128 TOPS 端侧算力、深度双目摄像头和 16 线激光雷达，具备智能跟随、探路、建图和避障能力。开发侧，它开放 ROS 2 接口定义，提供 GitHub 接入指南，并标配 USB-C、24V DC 和 CAN 接口，支持网络连接、供电通信和外设扩展。

In the official demonstration, the Big Head EDU version can be connected to a robotic arm to complete tasks such as feeding cat food, drawing curtains, storing toys, and operating a drum washing machine. 官方演示中，大头 EDU 版可接入机械臂，完成投喂猫粮、拉开窗帘、收纳玩具、操作滚筒洗衣机等任务。

努比亚 AI 宠物机器人 iMoochi 定档 7 月 17 日上线

Nubia announced that the AI companion device iMoochi will be officially launched on July 17th at 10 AM. 努比亚宣布，AI 陪伴设备 iMoochi 将于 7 月 17 日 10 点正式上线。

iMoochi is an emotional companion AI pet robot showcased by ZTE at MWC 2026, targeting urban single dwellers. The device supports trigger actions such as light head touches and continuous petting, with feedback including head shaking, tail wagging, and vocalizations; as usage time increases, it will develop different "personalities" and life rhythms. iMoochi 是中兴通讯在 2026 年 MWC 上展示的情感陪伴 AI 宠物机器人，定位都市独居人群。设备支持头部轻碰、持续抚摸等触发动作，反馈包括摇头、摆尾和发声；随使用时间增长，它会形成不同「性格」和生活节奏。

In terms of hardware, iMoochi is equipped with the Unisoc T8300 chip, supports 5 touch sensors and gravity sensing, and manages companionship diaries and emotional states through its accompanying App. 硬件上，iMoochi 搭载紫光展锐 T8300 芯片，支持 5 处触摸感应和重力传感，并通过配套 App 管理陪伴日记和情绪状态。

FIFA 将把 2026 世界杯决赛草皮做成官方限量收藏品

FIFA will partner with British collectibles company Keep Stub to cut and encapsulate the real natural turf from the 2026 World Cup final stadium in transparent resin, creating official limited edition souvenirs. FIFA 将与英国收藏品公司 Keep Stub 合作，把 2026 年世界杯决赛球场的真实天然草皮切割后封存在透明树脂中，做成官方限量纪念品。

These collectibles will be engraved with the official 2026 World Cup Logo, the final venue, match date, and the final score engraved after the match, and will come with a digital authenticity certificate. 这批收藏品会刻有 2026 世界杯官方 Logo、决赛举办地、比赛日期和赛后补刻的最终比分，并配备数字真伪认证证书。

The product is divided into four versions, each limited to 2026 pieces, with prices ranging from approximately $450 to $3000; the highest version also includes a metal commemorative ticket, a miniature model of the final match ball, and a crystal replica of the World Cup trophy. 产品分为四个版本，每个版本限量 2026 件，售价从约 450 美元到 3000 美元；最高版本还包含金属纪念门票、决赛比赛用球微缩模型和水晶版大力神杯模型。

If all four versions are sold out, the plan is expected to generate over $11 million in revenue for FIFA. 如果四个版本全部售罄，该计划预计可为 FIFA 带来超过 1100 万美元收入。

海南规划 2030 年平稳推进禁售燃油车

The Hainan Provincial People's Government released the "15th Five-Year Plan for Hainan National Ecological Civilization Pilot Zone (Beautiful Hainan Construction 15th Five-Year Plan)", proposing to steadily advance the ban on the sale of fuel vehicles by 2030. 海南省人民政府发布《「十五五」海南国家生态文明试验区规划（美丽海南建设「十五五」规划）》，提出平稳推进 2030 年禁售燃油车。

The plan estimates that by 2030, the proportion of new energy vehicles in Hainan will increase from 23.75% in 2025 to 45%. 规划预计，到 2030 年，海南新能源汽车保有量占比将从 2025 年的 23.75% 提升至 45%。

The plan sets more specific goals for the transportation sector: by 2030, new and replaced vehicles in public services and social operation sectors across the province, except for special purposes, will have a 100% clean energy share; new and replaced vehicles in the private car sector will have a 100% new energy share; and the vehicle-to-charging pile ratio will be maintained below 2.5:1. 规划对交通领域给出更具体的目标：到 2030 年，全省公共服务和社会运营领域新增、更换车辆，除特殊用途外清洁能源占比达到 100%；私人用车领域新增和更换车辆新能源占比达到 100%；车桩比保持在 2.5:1 以下。

The plan also proposes promoting the demonstration application of fuel cell vehicles in heavy trucks, cold chain logistics, and public transportation, and researching demonstration routes for green shipping zones in the Qiongzhou Strait. 规划还提出推进重卡、冷链物流、公共交通领域燃料电池汽车示范应用，并研究琼州海峡绿色航运区示范航线。

恐怖片《痴迷》定档 7 月 24 日

The film 'Obsession' has been confirmed to be released in mainland Chinese cinemas on July 24th, with a simultaneous release of its official poster. The film is written, directed, and edited by Kuri Barker, starring Michael Johnston and Indah Navarrete. 电影《痴迷》已确定于 7 月 24 日在中国内地院线上映，并同步发布定档海报。该片由库里·巴克自编、自导并负责剪辑，主演包括迈克尔·约翰斯顿和印达·纳瓦雷特。

The film tells the story of Bell, a record store employee, who secretly loves Nikki but never receives a response. By chance, he makes a wish through a mysterious item called 'One Wish Willow' to make Nikki fall in love with him. 影片讲述唱片店员工贝尔暗恋妮基，却始终无法得到回应。一次偶然机会，他通过名为「一愿柳」的神秘道具许愿，让妮基爱上自己。

After the wish comes true, Nikki's affection gradually evolves into extreme control and possessiveness. Bell also begins to discover that this forcibly obtained love hides uncontrollable supernatural powers. 愿望实现后，妮基的感情逐渐演变为极端的控制与占有，贝尔也开始发现，这段被强行获得的爱情背后隐藏着失控的超自然力量。
