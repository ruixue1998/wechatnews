苹果 iOS 27 AI Siri 界面曝光

Yesterday, Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman released a set of UI mockups for iOS 27, showcasing the new Siri interface, a new chatbot-style application, and other major iOS 27 changes Apple plans to release at WWDC26. 昨天，彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 发布了一组 iOS 27 的 UI 示意图，展示了新版 Siri 界面、新的聊天机器人式应用，以及苹果计划在 WWDC26上发布的其他 iOS 27 重大变化。

It is reported that the new version of Siri will have two launch entry points: 据悉，新版 Siri 将会有两个启动入口：

  • Traditional method: That is, shouting "Siri" or long-pressing the iPhone power button will trigger the new Siri animation in the Dynamic Island. 传统方式：也就是喊出「Siri」或长按 iPhone 电源键，将触发灵动岛中的新版 Siri 动画。
  • New method "Search or Ask": iOS 27 will allow users to swipe down from the top center of the iPhone from any location in the system to launch the new "Search or Ask" interface. 新方式「Search or Ask」：iOS 27 将允许用户在系统任意位置，从 iPhone 顶部中央向下滑动，来启动新的「搜索或提问」（Search or Ask）界面。

It is reported that "Search or Ask" supports voice and text input, has screen content awareness and personal data analysis capabilities, and can perform multi-level tasks across applications based on context. 据悉，「Search or Ask」支持语音与文本输入，具备屏幕内容感知及个人数据分析能力，可基于上下文跨应用执行多级任务。

In addition, Apple will launch a standalone Siri App similar to ChatGPT, which will retain historical conversation records and support multi-modal attachment uploads for AI analysis. 此外，苹果将推出类似于 ChatGPT 的独立 Siri App，该应用将保留历史对话记录，并支持多模态附件上传以供 AI 分析。

In terms of imaging and native applications, Siri will be directly integrated into the camera app for the first time, using third-party AI agents for object recognition and reverse image search. 在影像与原生应用方面，Siri 将首次直接整合进相机应用中，借由第三方 AI 代理进行物体识别与逆向图像搜索。

It is worth mentioning that iOS 27's camera interface will introduce a customizable control panel to meet professional shooting needs; the Photos app will also add computational photography features such as AI Reframe and AI Extend. 值得一提的是，iOS 27 的相机界面将引入可自定义控件面板，以满足专业拍摄需求；而相册应用则新增 AI 构图（Reframe）与 AI 扩图（Extend）等计算摄影功能。

Beyond its AI initiatives, iOS 27 will also focus on fixing bugs related to last year's "Liquid Glass" design. 在 AI 布局之外，iOS 27 还将重点修复去年「液态玻璃」（Liquid Glass）设计引发的相关 Bug。

Claude Opus 4.8 发布：快速模式成本降至三分之一

Early this morning, Anthropic officially released its flagship model Claude Opus 4.8, upgrading its capabilities in programming, AI agent tasks, reasoning, and knowledge work while maintaining the same pricing. 今天凌晨，Anthropic 正式发布旗舰模型 Claude Opus 4.8，在保持定价不变的前提下，对编程、AI 智能体任务、推理和知识工作能力进行了升级。

Standard mode pricing remains at $5 per million input tokens and $25 per million output tokens; fast mode pricing is $10 per million input tokens and $50 per million output tokens, with a running speed increase of approximately 2.5 times compared to the previous generation, and model costs reduced to one-third of the previous generation. 标准模式定价维持每百万输入 token 5 美元、每百万输出 token 25 美元；快速模式定价为每百万输入 token 10 美元、每百万输出 token 50 美元，运行速度较前代提升约 2.5 倍，模型成本降至前代的三分之一。

The core improvements in this upgrade lie in judgment and honesty. Official evaluations show that Opus 4.8 is about one-quarter as likely as its predecessor, Opus 4.7, to allow its own code defects to pass unexplained, and is more willing to proactively flag uncertainties, reducing conclusions lacking basis. 此次升级的核心改进在于判断力与诚实性。官方评估显示，Opus 4.8 放任自身代码缺陷未经说明通过的概率约为前代 Opus 4.7 的四分之一，更愿意主动标注不确定性，减少缺乏依据的结论。

In terms of benchmark tests, Opus 4.8 scored 69.2% on SWE-Bench Pro, higher than Opus 4.7's 64.3%, and surpassed GPT-5.5 and Gemini 3.1 Pro in multiple tests such as Agentic Coding, Agentic Computer Use, Knowledge Work, and Finance Agent. However, on the Terminal-Bench 2.1 programming benchmark, GPT-5.5's 78.2% is still higher than Opus 4.8's 74.6%. 基准测试方面，Opus 4.8 在 SWE-Bench Pro 上得分 69.2%，高于 Opus 4.7 的 64.3%，并在 Agentic Coding、Agentic Computer Use、Knowledge Work、Finance Agent 等多项测试中超过 GPT-5.5 与 Gemini 3.1 Pro。但在终端编程基准 Terminal-Bench 2.1 上，GPT-5.5 的 78.2% 仍高于 Opus 4.8 的 74.6%。

Anthropic also stated that it plans to roll out the more powerful Claude Mythos series models to all customers in the coming weeks. Anthropic 还表示，计划在未来数周内向所有客户推出能力更强的 Claude Mythos 系列模型。

OpenAI 基金会设立 2.5 亿美元 AI 基金

The OpenAI Foundation, the non-profit entity controlling OpenAI, announced yesterday that it would invest an initial $250 million to help workers, communities, and economies adapt to changes in job and income structures brought about by AI. 控制 OpenAI 的非营利主体 OpenAI Foundation 昨日宣布，将投入首期 2.5 亿美元，用于帮助劳动者、社区和经济体应对 AI 带来的岗位与收入结构变化。

OpenAI Foundation 去年已承诺投入 5000 万美元支持公共利益项目，这次 2.5 亿美元基金把重点从泛公益扩展到 AI 劳动力冲击和收益分配。

广东回应高考 AI 批卷传言

According to Guangdong Education Media, the Guangdong Provincial Education Examinations Authority responded, stating that "Guangdong's summer college entrance examination uses AI for grading" is a false rumor. The report mentioned that Guangdong has indeed used intelligent technology in college entrance examination venue management in recent years, but its purpose is for examination safety aspects such as assisting patrols, security checks, radio signal shielding, and confidential room inspections. 据广东教育传媒报道，广东省教育考试院回应，称「广东夏季高考用 AI 批卷」为不实传言。报道提到，广东近年确实在高考考场管理中使用智能技术，但用途是辅助巡考、安检、无线电信号屏蔽和保密室巡检等考试安全环节。

The Guangdong Provincial Education Examinations Authority pointed out that subjective questions are still manually graded by teachers, with independent double grading. If the score difference exceeds the threshold, a third review is initiated. The system only performs random checks, real-time monitoring, and error correction assistance. In 2025, Guangdong's grading center will be located at South China Normal University, with over 6,200 teachers selected from across the province to participate in grading. 广东省教育考试院指出，主观题仍由教师人工评阅，实行双人独立评阅，评分差超过阈值时进入三评，系统只承担随机抽检、实时监控和纠错辅助。2025 年广东评卷点设在华南师范大学，全省选聘超 6200 名教师参与评卷。
