iPad mini 明年有望「吃」上 OLED 屏

According to Bloomberg, Apple is planning major adjustments to three products – the MacBook Air, iPad mini, and iPad Air – by equipping them with OLED screens. 据彭博社报道， 苹果正在计划对 MacBook Air、iPad mini、iPad Air 三款产品进行重大调整 —— 配备 OLED 屏幕。

Insiders revealed that the iPad mini has the chance to be the first of the three to receive an OLED screen upgrade, with the OLED iPad mini launching as early as next year. However, the report also pointed out that the new screen will cause the price of the OLED iPad mini to increase by $100 (approximately RMB 709). 知情人士透露，iPad mini 有机会成为三者里面最早一个获得 OLED 屏幕升级，OLED iPad mini 最早会在明年推出。但报道也指出，新的屏幕将会让 OLED 版 iPad mini 售价上涨 100 美元（约合人民币 709 元）。

At the same time, the new iPad mini is also expected to support water resistance, retain speaker openings, and adopt a waterproof rubber ring and waterproof adhesive solution. 同时，新款 iPad mini 还有望支持防水，并且保留扬声器开孔，采用防水胶圈、防水胶方案。

The next-generation iPad Air and MacBook Air will still use LCD screens, and the iPad Air will be the last model to complete the aforementioned plan. Additionally, there are no plans for the entry-level iPad to join the OLED lineup. 而 iPad Air 和 MacBook Air 在下一代则依然采用 LCD 屏幕，并且 iPad Air 将成为最晚完成上述计划的一个机型。另外，入门版 iPad 未有计划加入 OLED 阵营。

According to reporter Mark Gurman, Apple is currently preparing a new MacBook Pro with touchscreen functionality, planned for release in late 2026 or early 2027. This MacBook Pro will feature an OLED screen. 据记者 Mark Gurman 消息，苹果目前还在准备一款配备触屏功能的全新 MacBook Pro，并计划 2026 年底或 2027 年初推出。而该款 MacBook Pro 将搭载 OLED 屏幕。

Photoshop 将支持直连 ChatGPT

Image courtesy of Computer Base 图自 Computer Base

Recently, Adobe released new AI assistants for its Photoshop and Express tools, which can help users quickly create images. 日前，Adobe 发布了旗下 Photoshop、Express 两款工具的新款 AI 助手，其能帮助用户进行图像快速创作。

According to reports, the AI assistant used by Adobe Express can directly create new images and designs for users, who can then perform secondary creation. 据介绍，Adobe Express 所采用的 AI 助手可以直接为用户创建新图像和设计，随后用户再进行二次创作。

Photoshop's AI assistant, on the other hand, uses a sidebar approach to help the AI understand different layers. The official statement indicates that Photoshop's AI assistant can complete repetitive tasks such as removing backgrounds or changing colors. 而 Photoshop 的 AI 助手则是采用侧边栏的方式，方便 AI 理解不同图层。官方表示，Photoshop 的 AI 助手能够完成如移除背景或更改颜色等重复性任务。

Adobe stated that it is currently experimenting with a new type of assistant called "Project Moonlight." This assistant can collaborate with different assistants in other Adobe tools and connect to creators' social channels to better understand their style. Adobe 表示，其目前还在试验一种新型助手，名为「Project Moonlight」。该助手可以与其他 Adobe 工具中的不同助手协同工作，并连接到创作者的社交渠道以更好地理解其风格。

Additionally, at the recently held MAX 2025 conference, Adobe announced a new round of cooperation with OpenAI, integrating its Photoshop and Adobe Express into ChatGPT, allowing users to edit images simply by chatting with ChatGPT. 另外，在近期举办的 2025 年度 MAX 大会上， Adobe 宣布与 OpenAI 达成新一轮合作，将旗下 Photoshop 和 Adobe Express 整合进 ChatGPT，让用户只需要跟 ChatGPT 聊天就能修图。

Alexandru Costin, Adobe's Vice President, stated that this collaboration merely integrates the strengths of all parties, and they will not abandon their proprietary Firefly model. The advantage of this model is that its training data is entirely sourced from licensed content, ensuring no copyright compliance issues are violated, and the generated content can be used commercially. Adobe 副总裁 Alexandru Costin 表示，这次合作只是整合各方优势，他们不会放弃自有模型 Firefly，而这款模型的优势就是其训练数据完全源自己授权内容，确保不会触犯版权合规性问题，并且生成的内容可以商业使用。

内存条价格大涨

According to Jiemian News, on October 29, the storage chip sector fluctuated upwards, with Shishang Technology hitting its daily limit for 6 consecutive trading days, Tongyou Technology rising over 7%, and Defu Technology, Yunhanxincheng, Xi'an Yicai, Biwin Storage, and others all climbing. 据界面新闻报道，10 月 29 日，存储芯片板块震荡走高，时空科技涨停走出 6 连板，同有科技涨超 7%，德福科技、云汉芯城、西安奕材、佰维存储等纷纷上扬。

Recently, storage product prices have shown an astonishing upward trend. Looking at spot market data, between October 13 and 18, in terms of DRAM, the average prices of DDR5 16G and DDR4 16G increased by 20.59% and 11.11% respectively compared to the previous week, and by as much as 58.73% and 43% compared to the previous month. 近期，存储产品价格呈现出惊人的上涨态势。从现货市场数据来看，在 10 月 13 日至 18 日期间，DRAM 方面，DDR5 16G、DDR4 16G 均价相较上周分别上涨 20.59%、11.11%，相较上月涨幅更是高达 58.73%、43%。

DDR stands for Double Data Rate, which is a core technical standard for memory modules (i.e., Dynamic Random Access Memory, DRAM) and one of the key indicators for measuring memory performance. DDR 全称为 Double Data Rate（双倍数据速率），是内存条（即动态随机存取存储器，DRAM）的核心技术标准，也是衡量内存性能的关键指标之一。

According to Haibao News, in Hangzhou, Zhejiang, memory stick prices have doubled in the past month. Some merchants stated that memory sticks that originally cost over 200 yuan have now risen to over 400 yuan, and those that cost over 1,000 yuan have risen to nearly 2,000 yuan. 据海报新闻报道，在浙江杭州，内存条近一个月价格翻了一番。有商户表示， 原来 200 多元的内存条现在涨到 400 多元，原来 1000 多元的则涨到近 2000 元。

Many netizens on social media have shown off the memory sticks they hoarded, feeling lucky to have assembled their computers in advance, saying, 'I never expected memory sticks to become the 'electronic Maotai' now, rising faster than gold.' 社交媒体上不少网友纷纷晒出自己所囤的内存条，庆幸提前组装了电脑，「真没想到内存条如今成了『电子茅台』，涨得比黄金还快。」

TrendForce data shows that in the third quarter of 2025, DRAM prices surged by 171.8% compared to the same period last year. If calculated with today's international spot gold price of $3956, it has only risen by less than 110% compared to last year's low of $1890. TrendForce 集邦咨询数据显示，2025 年第三季度，DRAM 价格较去年同期大幅上涨 171.8%。若以今日国际现货黄金价格 3956 美元计，较去年低位时的 1890 美元也只涨了不到 110%。

The rise in memory stick prices mainly stems from the increase in the price of their core functional unit, storage chips. A storage chip is a semiconductor device used to store digital information, capable of 'writing,' 'saving,' and 'reading' data in electronic systems such as computers, mobile phones, and IoT devices, effectively serving as the 'memory unit' of an electronic system. 内存条价格的上涨主要源于其核心功能单元存储芯片涨价。存储芯片是一种用于存储数字信息的半导体器件，能够将计算机、手机、物联网设备等电子系统中的数据进行「写入」「保存」和「读取」，相当于电子系统的「记忆单元」。

And the mobile phones and computers closest to us have also been affected by the environment, and have begun to show an upward trend in prices. 而离我们最近的手机、电脑也已受环境影响，开始出现了涨价趋势。

It is reported that Soraon previously directed his father Ryuichi Sakamoto's posthumous documentary film "Ryuichi Sakamoto: Opus." "Yesterday's Youth" is his debut feature film as writer and director. It was nominated for the Horizons section at the 81st Venice International Film Festival and won the Roberto Rossellini Award - Jury Honor at the 8th Pingyao International Film Festival, among others. 据悉，空音央曾执导过父亲坂本龙一的遗作纪录电影《坂本龙一：杰作》，《昨日青春》是他自编自导的首部剧情长片，曾入围第 81 届威尼斯国际电影节地平线单元，获第 8 届平遥国际电影展罗伯托·罗西里尼荣誉·评审荣誉等。

The film is set in a near-future Tokyo, plagued by frequent earthquakes, and revolves around five high school students about to graduate, including Yuta and Sachi. One day, the two conspired to prank the principal, leading to the installation of a surveillance system at school. Subsequently, amidst Japan's increasingly dire situation, Sachi grew more disillusioned with the world, while Yuta seemed completely oblivious. Due to differing goals and philosophies, the two gradually drifted apart, and their deep friendship forged since childhood faced rupture... 电影设定在近未来地震频发的东京，围绕悠太、幸等五名即将毕业的高中生展开故事。某天，两人合伙捉弄了校长，导致学校安装了监控系统。随后在日本日益严峻的局势中，幸对世界愈发感到失望，而悠太却似乎完全没有意识到这一点。因目标和理念不同，两人逐渐疏离，自幼结下的深厚友谊面临破裂……

The film stars Hayato Kurihara and Yukito Hidaka. 该片由栗原飒人、日高由起刀主演。

中国电影诞生 120 周年金银纪念币下月发行

According to the People's Bank of China, it is scheduled to issue a set of gold and silver commemorative coins for the 120th anniversary of the birth of Chinese cinema on November 11, 2025. This set of gold and silver commemorative coins consists of 3 pieces, including 2 gold commemorative coins and 1 silver commemorative coin, all of which are legal tender of the People's Republic of China. 据中国人民银行消息，其定于 2025 年 11 月 11 日发行中国电影诞生 120 周年金银纪念币一套。该套金银纪念币共 3 枚，其中金质纪念币 2 枚，银质纪念币 1 枚，均为中华人民共和国法定货币。

  • The reverse design of the 150-gram circular gold commemorative coin is composed of elements such as film, mountains, digital micromirrors, streaming player symbols, and the number "120", and is inscribed with the Chinese characters "中国电影诞生一百二十周年", the English words "The 120th Anniversary of the Birth of Chinese Cinema", and its denomination. The face value is 2000 yuan, with a fineness of 99.9%, and a maximum mintage of 300 pieces. 150 克圆形金质纪念币背面图案由胶片、山峰、数字微镜及流媒体播放器符号、数字「120」等元素组合设计而成，并刊「中国电影诞生一百二十周年」中文字样、「The 120th Anniversary of the Birth of Chinese Cinema」英文字样及面额。面额 2000 元，成色 99.9%，最大发行量 300 枚。
  • The reverse design of the 3-gram circular gold commemorative coin is composed of elements such as a film reel decorative shape and the number "120", and is inscribed with the Chinese characters "中国电影诞生一百二十周年", the English words "The 120th Anniversary of the Birth of Chinese Cinema", and its denomination. The face value is 50 yuan, with a fineness of 99.9%, and a maximum mintage of 20,000 pieces. 3 克圆形金质纪念币背面图案由胶片放映盘装饰造型、数字「120」等元素组合设计而成，并刊「中国电影诞生一百二十周年」中文字样、「The 120th Anniversary of the Birth of Chinese Cinema」英文字样及面额。面额 50 元，成色 99.9%，最大发行量 20000 枚。
  • The reverse design of the 30-gram circular silver commemorative coin is composed of elements such as a projector decorative shape, light beams, and the number "120", and is inscribed with the Chinese characters "中国电影诞生一百二十周年", the English words "The 120th Anniversary of the Birth of Chinese Cinema", and its denomination. The face value is 10 yuan, with a fineness of 99.9%, and a maximum mintage of 30,000 pieces. 30 克圆形银质纪念币背面图案由放映机装饰造型、光束、数字「120」等元素组合设计而成，并刊「中国电影诞生一百二十周年」中文字样、「The 120th Anniversary of the Birth of Chinese Cinema」英文字样及面额。面额 10 元，成色 99.9%，最大发行量 30000 枚。

电影《旁观者》重新定档 11 月

Recently, the film "The Bystander" has been rescheduled for November 1st, after its official withdrawal announcement on October 23rd. 日前，电影《旁观者》重新定档 11 月 1 日，此前曾于 10 月 23 日官宣撤档。

The film tells the story of Chen Jiahui, who returns to his hometown after 16 years away because his father Chen Weimin is critically ill. After reuniting with his elder brother Chen Jiaxing and elder sister Chen Jiayuan, he uncovers long-hidden family secrets by exploring his father's life trajectory, ultimately confronting the unresolved issues between father and son. 影片影片讲述离家 16 年的陈家辉因父亲陈为民病危重返故乡，在与哥哥陈家兴、姐姐陈家媛重聚后，通过探寻父亲生活轨迹揭开家庭隐藏多年的秘密，最终直面父子间未解心结的故事。

The film is directed by Songtaiga, produced by Du Qingchun, starring Chen Kun, Wang Yanhui, and Liu Mintao, with Rongzhong Erjia, An Zehao, Chen Yisha, and Shan Yuhao as main cast, and Wang Jinsong making a special appearance. 该片由松太加执导、杜庆春监制，陈坤、王砚辉、刘敏涛领衔主演，容中尔甲、安泽豪、陈依莎、单禹豪主演，王劲松特别出演。
