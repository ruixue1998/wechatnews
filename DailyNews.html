苹果官宣新 CEO，John Ternus 九月接任

Early this morning, Apple officially announced that Tim Cook will step down as CEO, with John Ternus taking over. Simultaneously, two internal letters were leaked, one from current CEO Tim Cook and the other from John Ternus. 今天凌晨，苹果官宣库克将卸任 CEO，由约翰·特努斯 (John Ternus) 接手。同期，两封内部信流出，一封来自现任 CEO Tim Cook，另一封来自 John Ternus。

In the letter, Cook stated that he would step down as CEO and transition to Chairman within this year. He had multiple in-depth conversations with Ternus and found him to possess vision and outstanding character. 库克在信中表示，自己将在今年内卸任 CEO 并转任董事长。他与特努斯深谈多次，发现他具有远见和卓越人格。

John is the right leader to help us move into the future and forge bold new directions. 约翰是帮助我们迈向未来、开拓大胆新方向的合适领导者。

Cook confirmed that he would continue as CEO this summer and complete the handover with Ternus in September; Ternus also confirmed in his letter that he would take over as CEO in September. 库克确认将在今年夏天继续担任 CEO，并在 9 月与特努斯完成交接；特努斯在信中同样确认了自己将会在今年 9 月接任 CEO。

This September, I will become Apple's new CEO. It has been a tremendous honor to lead the hardware engineering team, participate in such outstanding work, and personally witness each of you striving to do your best for our users. I look forward to working closely with you in this new role, and of course, I will continue to be hands-on and deeply involved. 今年九月，我将成为苹果的新任 CEO。能够领导硬件工程团队，参与如此卓越的工作，亲眼看到你们每一个人全力以赴为用户做到最好，是我莫大的荣幸。我期待在新角色中与你们紧密合作，当然，我依然会亲力亲为地深入其中。

Effective today, Ternus will step down as head of hardware engineering, with Tom Marieb taking over and reporting to Johny Srouji. Additionally, Apple announced that Srouji will assume the newly created position of Chief Hardware Officer. 从今天起，特努斯将卸任硬件工程负责人，由 Tom Marieb 接手，汇报给 Johny Srouji。并且，苹果还宣布了 Srouji 将担任新设立的首席硬件官职位 (Chief Hardware Officer)。

重押 AI，字节跳动去年净利润同比缩水逾七成，高管：没那么大幅度

According to Jiemian News and 21st Century Business Herald, sources reveal that ByteDance's net profit in 2025 will decline by over 70% year-on-year, with a significant contraction in net profit margin. 据界面新闻、21 世纪经济报道消息，知情人士透露，字节跳动 2025 年净利润同比下滑幅度超过 70%，净利润率出现显著收缩。

The core reason is that the company significantly increased its resource investment in artificial intelligence during the third and fourth quarters of last year, including the procurement of high-end AI chips, the development of foundational models, and other key areas. These massive expenditures directly impacted the full year's profitability. 核心原因在于：公司在去年三、四季度大幅加码人工智能领域的资源投入，包括高端 AI 芯片采购、底层模型研发等多个核心环节，巨额开支直接拖累了全年盈利表现。

Despite the significant decline in net profit, ByteDance's revenue side maintained strong growth. According to sources, ByteDance's overall revenue continued to grow last year, with domestic revenue increasing by approximately 20% year-on-year, and overseas revenue growing by nearly 50%. The proportion of overseas business revenue increased from 25% in 2024 to over 30%, reaching a new historical high. 在净利润大幅下滑的背景下，字节跳动的营收端依然保持强劲增长。据知情人士透露，去年字节跳动整体营收延续增长态势，国内营收同比增幅约 20%，海外营收同比增长近 50%，海外业务营收占比从 2024 年的 25% 提升至三成以上，创下历史新高。

Regarding the pace of future investments, ByteDance revealed in communications with shareholders that technological resource investment will further expand this year, coupled with compliance-related supporting investments in overseas markets, meaning short-term net profit margins will continue to face pressure. 对于未来的投入节奏，字节跳动在与股东的沟通中透露，今年技术资源投入将进一步扩大，叠加海外市场合规相关的配套投入，短期净利润率仍将继续承压。

In response, ByteDance executive Li Liang clarified in a post that the aforementioned net profit decline of over 70% was calculated using international accounting standards. Besides increased investment in emerging businesses, it also included factors like changes in preferred stock and option costs, which 'do not reflect the substance of operations'. 对此，字节跳动高管李亮发文澄清，上述超 70% 的净利润降幅采用国际会计准则口径，除新兴业务投入增大外，还包含优先股和期权成本变动等「不反映运营实质」的因素。

He stated that the actual operating profit margin in the second half of the year only saw a slight decline, 'far less significant than reported'. After excluding the aforementioned non-operating factors, overall revenue and profit still achieved growth. 他表示，实际下半年经营利润率仅小幅下滑，「远没有报道中这么大幅度」，剔除上述非经营性因素后，总体营收和利润仍实现增长。

追觅俞浩：手机要和华为、小米三分天下，但我现在还没法告诉你怎么做

We originally didn't plan to say anything, but we couldn't hide it anymore. From point A to point B, constantly testing and receiving feedback to correct the route. 下一代徕卡相机将搭载国产定制 CMOS 传感器

Chinese CMOS image sensor supplier Gpixel yesterday officially announced a strategic partnership with Leica. The two parties will jointly customize and develop a high-performance CMOS image sensor chip for the next generation of Leica cameras. 国产 CMOS 图像传感器供应商长光辰芯（Gpixel）昨日与徕卡正式宣布战略合作，双方将联合为下一代徕卡相机定制开发一款高性能 CMOS 图像传感器芯片。

It is understood that this collaboration will integrate Leica's years of accumulated expertise in high-end imaging with Gpixel's leading CMOS image sensor design capabilities to create customized chips that precisely meet Leica cameras' stringent standards for color reproduction, noise optimization, dynamic range, and detail rendering. 据悉，此次合作将整合徕卡在高端成像领域积累多年的专业经验，与长光辰芯领先的 CMOS 图像传感器设计能力，打造定制化芯片，精准匹配徕卡相机在色彩还原、噪声优化、动态范围及细节呈现上的严苛标准。

The two parties will also collaborate closely on chip verification, image tuning, and mass production preparation, covering the entire chain from R&D to mass production. Engineering and technical teams from Wetzlar, Germany, Antwerp, Belgium, and Changchun, China, will participate. 双方还将在芯片验证、图像调校及量产准备等方面密切协作，覆盖从研发到量产的完整链条。来自德国韦茨拉尔、比利时安特卫普和中国长春三地的工程技术团队将参与其中。

Yesterday, after Wanda Films officially changed its name to "Ruyi Film Entertainment Co., Ltd.," the company's Chairwoman and President, Chen Zhixi, issued an all-staff letter clarifying the logic behind the renaming: 昨天，在万达电影正式更名为「儒意电影娱乐股份有限公司」之后，公司董事长兼总裁陈祉希发布全员信，阐明更名逻辑：

"Ruyi" represents the company's inherent content creation gene, while "Film Entertainment" highlights the strategic determination to focus on offline scenarios and deeply cultivate diverse entertainment experiences. 「儒意」代表公司血脉里的内容创作基因，「电影娱乐」则彰显以线下场景为核心、深耕多元娱乐体验的战略决心。

It is worth noting that this renaming only applies to the listed company entity; its physical cinemas will continue to use the "Wanda Cinemas" brand, and new brands will be planned in the future based on market changes and user needs. 值得注意的是，此次更名仅针对上市公司主体，旗下实体影城将继续沿用「万达影城」品牌，未来会根据市场变化和用户需求规划新品牌。

At the strategic level, Chen Zhixi reviewed the transformation path since Ruyi's acquisition two years ago in her all-staff letter: the company has evolved from a pure exhibition terminal to an industry closed-loop of "content development—cinema exhibition—IP operation," and has introduced diverse content such as the Spring Festival Gala, F1, and League of Legends World Championship finals into cinemas. 在战略层面，陈祉希在全员信中回顾了儒意入主两年来的转型路径：公司已从单纯的放映终端，向「内容开发—院线放映—IP 运营」的行业闭环演进，并将春晚、F1、英雄联盟总决赛等多元内容引入影院。

Chen Zhixi stated that the company will leverage its more than 700 directly operated cinemas nationwide and its Ruyi-affiliated IP reserves to create "super scenarios" and "super IPs." 陈祉希称，公司将依托全国 700 多家直营影城及儒意系 IP 储备，打造「超级场景」与「超级 IP」。
