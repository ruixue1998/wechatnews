苹果国行 AI 凌晨意外上线后被撤下

Early this morning, Apple Intelligence suddenly appeared on the settings page of Chinese iPhone users, and Apple quickly took it offline. Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman explicitly stated that the feature has not yet been officially approved, and its appearance this time was an accidental event, unrelated to the iOS 26.5 beta. 今天凌晨，Apple Intelligence 突然出现在国行 iPhone 用户的设置页面中，随后苹果迅速将其下线。 彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 明确表示，该功能目前尚未正式获批，此次上线系意外事件，且与 iOS 26.5 测试版无关。

APPSO discovered that the original "Siri" entry on the settings page of Chinese iPhones has been renamed "Apple Intelligence and Siri." After clicking, users can download the on-device model and unlock a series of AI features. With only one day left until Apple's 50th anniversary (April 1st), Apple Intelligence landed on Chinese users' phones in this almost silent manner. APPSO 发现，国行 iPhone 设置页面中原有的「Siri」入口已更名为「Apple 智能与 Siri」，点击后可下载端侧模型并解锁一系列 AI 功能。距离苹果成立 50 周年纪念日（4 月 1 日）仅剩一天，Apple Intelligence 以这种几乎静默的方式落在了国行用户的手机上。

Gurman provided three reasons to judge this as an accident: Gurman 给出了三条判断这是一次意外的依据：

Apple would not launch AI features in China's most important market without any official announcement; Apple would not choose to release it in the early hours of China time; furthermore, the feature uses Google's visual recognition engine, and Google services are not normally accessible in China. 苹果不会在没有任何官方宣传的情况下在中国最重要的市场上线 AI 功能；苹果不会选择在中国时间凌晨发布；此外，该功能调用了 Google 的视觉识别引擎，而 Google 服务在国内无法正常访问。

APPSO completed the first-time activation and actual testing before the feature was taken offline. APPSO 在功能下线前完成了第一时间的激活与实测。此次上线的功能包括全新 Siri 界面、写作工具、图乐园、AI 消除、视觉智能及翻译等。

Regarding model invocation, the situation is more complex. Apple Intelligence debuted at WWDC24 in June 2024 and was first launched in the U.S. in October of the same year with iOS 18.1. Last March, the iOS 18.4 update enabled support for Simplified Chinese. Apple Intelligence 于 2024 年 6 月的 WWDC24 首次亮相，同年 10 月随 iOS 18.1 在美国率先上线。去年 3 月，iOS 18.4 更新使其支持简体中文。

苹果开源编程语言 Swift 正式支持 Android

Recently, Apple's open-source programming language Swift officially launched official support for the Android platform, with the first Swift SDK for Android now officially available, nearly a year after the plan was announced. 近日，苹果旗下开源编程语言 Swift，正式推出了对 Android 平台的官方支持，首个 Swift SDK for Android 已正式上线，距离该计划官宣已过去近一年时间。

With this SDK, developers can start developing native Android applications using Swift, and update existing Swift projects to support Android builds... This is an important milestone, opening up new opportunities for Swift's cross-platform development. 借助该 SDK，开发者可以开始用 Swift 开发原生 Android 程序，将现有 Swift 项目更新为支持 Android 构建……这是一个重要里程碑，为 Swift 的跨平台开发开辟了新的机遇。

From a technical implementation perspective, building Swift packages on Android requires cross-compilation, meaning compiling code on a desktop host platform like macOS or Linux into a program that can run on an Android device or emulator. 从技术实现层面来看，在 Android 上构建 Swift 包需要进行交叉编译，即在 macOS 或 Linux 等桌主机平台上，将代码编译为可在 Android 设备或模拟器上运行的程序。

The complete toolchain consists of three parts: the Swift Toolchain (core compiler), Swift SDK for Android (an extended support package specifically for Android cross-compilation), and Android NDK (providing platform-specific headers, system libraries, and linker tools). Currently, the SDK supports both x86_64 and aarch64 Android architectures. 完整的工具链由三部分组成：Swift 工具链（核心编译器）、Swift SDK for Android（专为 Android 交叉编译提供的扩展支持包），以及 Android NDK（提供平台专属头文件、系统库和链接器工具）。目前该 SDK 支持 x86_64 和 aarch64 两种 Android 架构。

Theoretically, a single Swift codebase could eventually run on both iOS and Android, significantly reducing the barrier and cost of cross-platform porting. Of course, Kotlin remains the absolute primary language for Android development, and Swift's addition primarily expands developers' choices rather than replacing the existing ecosystem. 理论上，一套 Swift 代码未来可在 iOS 和 Android 双端通行，跨平台移植的门槛和成本将大幅降低。当然，Kotlin 目前仍是 Android 开发的绝对主力语言，Swift 的加入更多是拓展了开发者的选择空间，而非替代现有生态。

ClawBench 最新榜单：智谱 GLM-5-Turbo 登顶，字节、小米四款模型跻身全球前十

Agent evaluation firm ClawBench yesterday released its latest large model ranking, covering 30 complex agent tasks across five core business scenarios: office collaboration, information retrieval, content creation, data processing, and software engineering. Agent 评测机构 ClawBench 昨日发布了最新大模型榜单，覆盖 30 项复杂 Agent 任务，涵盖办公协作、信息检索、内容创作、数据处理与软件工程五大核心业务场景。

  • Zhipu GLM-5-Turbo topped the list with a CLAW SCORE of 93.9, becoming the model with the strongest overall performance in this evaluation; 智谱 GLM-5-Turbo 以 93.9 分的 CLAW SCORE 登顶榜首，成为本次评测中综合表现最强的模型；
  • ByteDance Doubao-Seed-2.0-lite ranked second with 93.1 points, and its usage cost is only $0.33, the lowest on the entire list; 字节跳动 Doubao-Seed-2.0-lite 以 93.1 分位居第二，其使用成本仅为 $0.33，为全榜最低；

ClawBench adopts an isolated sandbox execution mechanism, where each model must complete tasks in a realistically simulated enterprise development environment, deliberately embedding engineering challenges such as "inconsistent naming," "missing directories," and "date traps." ClawBench 采用隔离沙箱执行机制，每款模型须在真实模拟的企业开发环境中完成任务，并刻意内嵌「命名不一致」「目录缺失」「日期陷阱」等工程挑战。

Regarding scoring, ClawBench introduces a "triple scoring mechanism," using automated script assertions, cutting-edge LLMs acting as "expert judges," and a hybrid scoring method combining both in a weighted manner, aiming to more accurately reflect the model's actual deployment capabilities in complex workflows. 评分方面，ClawBench 引入「三重评分机制」，依据任务类型分别采用自动化脚本断言、前沿 LLM 担任「专家评委」，以及两者加权结合的混合评分方式，以期更真实地反映模型在复杂工作流中的实际部署能力。
