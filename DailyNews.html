独家｜iPhone 17 支持 AVS 充电协议

At the September 10th launch event, an inconspicuous protocol abbreviation appeared on the "40W Dynamic Power Adapter (up to 60W)" that quietly debuted with the iPhone 17 series: AVS (Adjustable Voltage Supply). 在 9 月 10 日的发布会上，随着 iPhone 17 系列一同悄然登场的「40W 动态电源适配器(最高 60W)」上，出现了一个不起眼的协议缩写：AVS (Adjustable Voltage Supply，可调电压电源)。

We found that the AVS supported by the iPhone 17 series and the new charger is more precisely SPR AVS (Standard Power Range Adjustable Voltage Supply). It is understood that SPR AVS is a new protocol that has just been implemented in the USB PD 3.2 standard proposed at the end of 2024. 据我们发现，本次 iPhone 17 系列及新充电器支持的 AVS，更确切的说是 SPR AVS (Standard Power Range Adjustable Voltage Supply，标准电源范围可调电压电源)。据悉，SPR AVS 是一个新协议，在 24 年底提出的 USB PD 3.2 标准中才刚刚落地。

For a long time, Apple devices, including the iPhone 16, have relied on a "fixed voltage charging + secondary step-down inside the device" fast charging scheme. The PPS fast charging chosen by subsequent Android manufacturers allows for more precise voltage and current regulation between the charger and the phone, thereby reducing the step-down work inside the phone. 长久以来，包括 iPhone 16 在内的苹果设备，其快充方案都依赖于「固定电压充电 + 机内二次降压」的模式。而后续 Android 阵营的厂商们所选择的 PPS 快充，允许充电器和手机之间进行更精细的电压、电流调节，从而减少手机内部的降压工作。

However, Apple's choice of the new SPR AVS protocol actually stems from dissatisfaction with the mainstream fast charging protocol PPS, including two major "fatal flaws": 但苹果选择了 SPR AVS 这个新协议，背后其实是对主流快充协议 PPS 的不满，包括两大「硬伤」：

  • The so-called 20mV voltage step is practically impossible to achieve: line loss, the charger's own voltage ripple, and other factors can cause deviations exceeding this 20mV specification. 所谓 20mV 的电压步进，实际几乎无法实现：线路损耗、充电器本身的电压纹波等原因都会造成超过 20mV 这一规定。
  • The "maximum power" concept of PPS leads to simultaneous activation of maximum current and maximum voltage limits, with the actual result being that the nominal maximum power is simply not achieved. PPS 的「最大功率」概念导致最大电流和最大电压的限制同时生效，最后实际结果就是标称的最大功率根本达不到。

It is understood that AVS first originated in the PD 3.1 EPR (Extended Power Range) specification designed for high-power devices such as laptops, capable of providing an ultra-high power of up to 48V 5A = 240W. Apple's own MacBook Pro 16-inch was the first mainstream commercial product to feature AVS. 据了解，AVS 其最早诞生于为笔记本电脑等高功率设备设计的 PD 3.1 EPR (Extended Power Range 扩展功率范围) 规范中，可以最大提供 48V 5A = 240W 的超大功率。苹果自家的 MacBook Pro 16 英寸便是 AVS 的第一个主流商业化产品。

The SPR AVS supported by iPhone 17 is the USB-IF association officially bringing this advanced voltage regulation technology down to the Standard Power Range (below 100W) in the latest PD 3.2 specification. 而 iPhone 17 所支持的 SPR AVS，则是 USB-IF 协会在最新的 PD 3.2 规范中，正式将这一先进的调压技术下放到 100W 以下的标准功率范围（Standard Power Range）内。

The technology is too complex, so for users, what AVS can achieve after implementation is: 技术太复杂，那么对用户来说，AVS 实装后能做到：

  • Cooler fast charging: Because the voltage transformation work is handled by the charger during charging, body heat generation is reduced, the charging power curve is smoother, and high power can be maintained for longer. 快充更凉爽：因充电时变压工作交给充电器，机身发热减少，充电功率曲线更平稳，高功率也能维持更久。
  • Batteries are more durable: Long-term high temperatures and voltage shocks can cause batteries to age faster, while SPR AVS is more friendly to low-voltage, high-current precise power supply, reducing unnecessary losses. 电池更耐用：长期高温和电压冲击会让电池老化更快，而 SPR AVS 对低压大电流更加友好精准供电，减少不必要的损耗。
  • No licensing fees: It is an open protocol, requiring no licensing fees, meaning no protocol costs are passed on to consumers, further reducing product prices. 无需授权费：其是一个开放的协议，无需授权费，意味着没有协议成本转嫁给消费者，产品价格进一步降低。

At the same time, precisely because this SPR AVS is a standard extension, it also allows SDC (Software-Defined Charger) products, represented by the Sugar Factory's small power bank, to immediately support the new protocol by upgrading their existing devices. 同时， 正因为本次 SPR AVS 是标准的扩展，也让制糖工厂小电拼为代表的 SDC（Software-Defined Charger 软件定义的充电器）产品，可以第一时间通过协议升级的方法，让现有设备「摇身一变」支持新协议。

It is worth mentioning that the Sugar Factory AI Power Bank will open internal testing for its AVS function on September 15th. 值得一提的是， 制糖工厂 AI 小电拼将在 9 月 15 日开放 AVS 功能内测。

工信部等部门联合整治汽车行业网络乱象

According to MIIT Micro-Report, to implement the deployment requirements of the State Council's executive meeting on effectively regulating the competitive order of the new energy vehicle industry, six departments including the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, the Central Social Work Department, the Cyberspace Administration of China, the National Development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of Public Security, and the State Administration for Market Regulation recently jointly issued the "Notice on Carrying out Special Rectification Actions for Network Chaos in the Automotive Industry," deciding to carry out a 3-month special rectification action for network chaos in the automotive industry nationwide. 据工信微报消息，为落实国务院常务会议关于切实规范新能源汽车产业竞争秩序部署要求，工业和信息化部、中央社会工作部、中央网信办、国家发展改革委、公安部、市场监管总局等六部门近日联合印发《关于开展汽车行业网络乱象专项整治行动的通知》，决定在全国范围内开展为期 3 个月的汽车行业网络乱象专项整治行动。

This special rectification action will focus on rectifying network chaos such as illegal profiteering, exaggerated and false propaganda, and malicious defamation and attacks, improving the quality and efficiency of handling network chaos involving automotive enterprises, urging enterprises to standardize marketing and promotional behaviors, creating a good public opinion environment, and safeguarding the high-quality development of the automotive industry. Key issues to be rectified mainly include: 本次专项整治行动将集中整治非法牟利、夸大和虚假宣传、恶意诋毁攻击等网络乱象，提升涉汽车企业网络乱象处置质效，督促企业规范营销宣传行为，营造良好舆论环境，护航汽车产业高质量发展。重点整治问题主要包括：

Illegal Profiteering: 非法牟利：

  • By creating fake images and videos, fabricating stories, hyping and spreading negative topics related to car companies, maliciously interpreting fluctuations in car sales, and attacking car companies' operational development strategies, etc., to gain online traffic and commercial benefits. 通过制作虚假图片、视频，捏造故事，炒作和散布涉车企负面话题，恶意解读汽车企业销量波动，攻击汽车企业经营发展战略等，赚取网络流量，获取商业利益。
  • Utilizing their discourse power and influence, under the guise of "news supervision," "public opinion supervision," "popular science," etc., to obtain commercial benefits through false or non-standard evaluations that combine business and testing, or testing for business, and even extorting "protection fees" from car companies. By publishing false or untrue information or negative information about enterprises, or setting the tone in comment sections, at important junctures such as new car product launches and car company financing, to coerce enterprises into business cooperation. 利用自身话语权和影响力，以「新闻监督」「舆论监督」「科普」等名义，通过以商养测、以测养商、开展商测结合的虚假或不规范测评等方式，获取商业利益，甚至要挟汽车企业提供「保护费」。通过在汽车产品上市发布和汽车企业融资等重要节点，发布涉企虚假不实信息或负面信息，或在评论区带节奏等，胁迫企业开展商务合作。
  • Utilizing technologies such as motherboard machines and generative AI to create new types of "internet trolls," producing false content, generating fake popularity and trends, evading platform monitoring, and seeking illegal profits. 利用主板机、生成式人工智能等技术，制造新型「网络水军」，产出虚假内容，制造虚假热度和趋势，规避平台监测，谋取非法利益。

Exaggerated and False Propaganda: 夸大和虚假宣传：

  • Making false or misleading claims about the performance, functions, quality, sales status, etc., of automobiles and power batteries, deceiving and misleading consumers. 对汽车、动力电池的性能、功能、质量、销售状况等作虚假或者引人误解的宣传，欺骗误导消费者。
  • Car companies manipulating agencies or review accounts to conduct false or non-standard evaluations. Citing and fabricating false data, selectively disclosing sales data, and creating various lists such as sales rankings under false pretenses. 汽车企业操纵机构或评测类账号等搞虚假或不规范测评。引用、捏造虚假数据，选择性披露销售数据，巧立名目发布销量排行等各种榜单。
  • Using industry events such as exhibitions and forums, as well as online marketing and promotional activities, to create and hype topics, causing negative impacts on the industry and society. 借助展会、论坛等行业活动及网络营销宣传活动，制造、炒作话题，造成行业及社会不良影响。

Malicious Slander and Attack: 恶意诋毁攻击：

  • For the purpose of curbing and suppressing competitors, slandering and attacking automotive companies or products, tarnishing corporate or product reputation, and making malicious complaints against companies. 以遏制、打压竞争对手为目的，诋毁攻击汽车企业或者汽车产品，抹黑企业声誉或者商品声誉，对企业进行恶意投诉。
  • Organizing and manipulating online trolls, "black public relations," "black mouths," and "fan circle" followers to jointly publish false and negative information involving automotive companies and entrepreneurs, inciting netizens' emotions, engaging in "flame wars," and maliciously slandering competitors. 组织、操纵网络水军、「黑公关」「黑嘴」及「饭圈」粉丝，联动发布涉汽车企业及企业家的虚假、负面信息，煽动网民情绪，打「口水战」，恶意抹黑竞争对手。
  • Automotive company executives using their influence to "drag down" and provoke online disputes. 汽车企业高管利用自身影响力在网上「拉踩」引战。

The "Notice" emphasizes guiding automotive companies to report clues about online chaos such as online trolls, "black public relations," "black mouths," and "fan circle" followers in the automotive industry to website platforms, the Central Cyberspace Administration of China's Illegal and Undesirable Information Reporting Center, and provincial cyberspace administrations in the companies' localities. 《通知》强调，引导汽车企业向网站平台、中央网信办违法和不良信息举报中心、企业属地省级网信办举报反映汽车行业网络水军、「黑公关」「黑嘴」及「饭圈」粉丝等网络乱象问题线索。

The "Notice" also requires that departments such as industry and information technology, social work, cyberspace administration, development and reform, public security, and market supervision in various regions strengthen organizational leadership and coordinated linkage; network platform enterprises should conduct in-depth self-inspection and self-correction, and strengthen the identification and control of online trolls and "black mouths" that use generative AI technology. 《通知》还要求，各地工业和信息化、社会工作、网信、发展改革、公安、市场监管等部门要加强组织领导和协调联动；网络平台企业要深入开展自查自纠，加强对采用生成式人工智能技术等的网络水军、「黑嘴」的甄别管控。

英伟达上线新 GPU，专为百万 token 推理而生

Recently, Nvidia announced the launch of a new generation GPU named Rubin CPX, designed specifically for large-scale context processing. Officially, Rubin CPX can run software programming and video generation with millions of tokens at unprecedented efficiency. 日前，英伟达宣布推出一款名为 Rubin CPX 的新一代 GPU，其专为大规模上下文处理而设计。官方表示，Rubin CPX 能够以前所未有的效率跑百万级 tokens 的软件编程和视频生成。
