曝苹果独立 Siri 应用支持对话自动删除、底层模型大量替换为 Gemini

Last night, Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman revealed more details about Apple's upcoming standalone Siri app in his latest Power On newsletter. 昨晚，彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 在最新的 Power On 新闻通讯中透露了更多关于苹果即将发布的独立 Siri 应用的细节。

Privacy design is one of the core selling points of this new app. Gurman revealed that the app will feature a built-in automatic conversation deletion function, allowing users to choose conversation retention periods in settings: 30 days, 1 year, or permanent storage. Apple is expected to continue emphasizing privacy protection at this year's WWDC and highlight that its AI experience will be ad-free. 隐私设计是此次新应用的核心卖点之一。Gurman 透露，该应用将内置对话自动删除功能，用户可在设置中选择对话保留时长：30 天、1 年或永久保存。苹果预计在今年的 WWDC 继续主打隐私保护，并强调其 AI 体验不含广告。

Regarding the model, Apple has largely replaced the underlying AI model of the new Siri with Google's Gemini, and will run some services on Google's cloud infrastructure. Previously, there were reports that Apple and its original partner OpenAI had a rift in their relationship, and might even go to court, because OpenAI believed the results of their cooperation were not as expected. 模型方面，苹果已将新 Siri 的 AI 模型底层大量替换为 Google 的 Gemini，并将部分服务运行在 Google 的云基础设施之上。而此前有消息称，苹果和原本的合作对象 OpenAI 关系出现裂痕，甚至有可能对簿公堂，原因是 OpenAI 认为双方合作的结果不及预期。

It is worth noting that although the new AI Siri has been delayed for about two years, Gurman said that Apple's internal test version still labels it as "Beta" and may continue to do so until its official release. 值得注意的是，尽管新的 AI Siri 已经跳票约两年，Gurman 称苹果内部测试版本仍将其标注为「Beta」，并可能沿用至其正式发布。

arXiv 出台新规禁止 AI 垃圾，违者封禁一年加强制同行评审双重处罚

Recently, the preprint academic paper platform arXiv announced that if an author's submitted paper contains irrefutable evidence of AI-generated content, their account will be banned for one year, and thereafter all submissions will be required to be accepted by a peer-reviewed journal before they can be uploaded. 近日，预印本学术论文平台 arXiv 宣布，若作者提交的论文中包含无可辩驳的 AI 生成内容证据，将被封禁账号一年，并在此后要求所有提交须先经过同行评审期刊接受方可上传。

Thomas Dietterich, Chair of the arXiv Computer Science section, posted on X that if a paper shows obvious signs such as "hallucinated citations" or large language model meta-comments (e.g., "Here is a 200-word summary, do you need to modify it?"), it can be concluded that the author has not verified the AI-generated content. arXiv 计算机科学板块主席 Thomas Dietterich 在 X 发文表示，若论文中出现「幻觉引用」、大语言模型元注释（如「以下是 200 字摘要，是否需要修改？」）等明显迹象，即可认定作者未对 AI 生成内容进行核查。

arXiv is currently managed by Cornell Tech, and will operate independently as a non-profit legal entity starting in July this year. Greg Morrisett, Dean of Cornell Tech, stated that after independence, arXiv will be able to receive financial support from a wider range of donors to cope with the operational pressure brought by "AI spam". arXiv 目前由康奈尔科技学院管理，今年 7 月将独立运营，成为一家非营利法人机构。康奈尔科技学院院长 Greg Morrisett 表示，独立后 arXiv 将能从更广泛的捐助方获得资金支持，以应对「AI 垃圾内容」带来的运营压力。

特斯拉 Robotaxi 碰撞事故报告解封

Recently, Tesla officially declassified and released the complete, unredacted reports of 17 "Robotaxi" autonomous driving collision incidents submitted to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA). 近期，特斯拉正式解密并公开了向美国国家公路交通安全管理局（NHTSA）提交的 17 起「Robotaxi」自动驾驶碰撞事故的完整未脱敏报告。

According to Electrek, these 17 incidents occurred between July 2025 and March 2026, all involving 2026 Model Y vehicles undergoing "Robotaxi" testing in Austin, Texas, and safety drivers were present during all tests. 据 Electrek 报道，这 17 起事故发生于 2025 年 7 月至 2026 年 3 月，均涉及在得克萨斯州奥斯汀进行「Robotaxi」测试的 2026 款 Model Y，且测试时均有安全员在场。

Casualty data shows that the incidents were generally minor: 13 incidents resulted only in property damage, 2 had no injuries, 1 involved minor injuries not requiring hospitalization, and another 1 involved minor injuries requiring hospitalization (this was when the vehicle was rear-ended by an SUV while creeping at 2 mph at an intersection). 伤亡数据显示事故整体较轻微：13 起仅造成财产损失，2 起无人员受伤，1 起轻伤未入院，另有 1 起轻伤需入院治疗（系车辆在路口以 2 英里/小时蠕行时被后方 SUV 追尾）。

Official reports indicate that most incidents were not the fault of Tesla's Autopilot Driving System (ADS), but rather occurred when vehicles were completely stationary at traffic lights, stop signs, or in traffic jams, and were rear-ended or sideswiped by human drivers. 官方报告表明，大部分事故并非特斯拉自动驾驶系统（ADS）的过失，多为车辆在红绿灯、停止标志或交通拥堵中完全静止时，被后方人类驾驶员追尾或侧擦。

However, the data also reveals flaws in the system and backend intervention: 但数据同时也暴露出系统与后台干预的缺陷：

Two incidents occurred during remote operator takeover, causing the vehicles to collide with a metal fence and a construction barrier at 8 mph and 9 mph respectively; several other incidents showed that ADS had blind spots in spatial perception, with vehicles hitting metal chains, trailer hitches, wooden utility poles, and curbs while turning or reversing. 两起事故发生在远程操作员接管期间，导致车辆分别以 8 英里和 9 英里的时速撞上金属围栏与施工路障；另有数起事故显示 ADS 存在空间感知死角，车辆在转弯或倒车时撞上了金属链条、拖车挂钩、木制电线杆及路缘石。

ChatGPT 能帮你管钱了，支持连接超 1.2 万家金融机构

Recently, OpenAI launched a personal financial management feature for ChatGPT Pro users in the US, supporting connection to bank accounts and credit cards from over 12,000 financial institutions via Plaid. 近日，OpenAI 面向美国 ChatGPT Pro 用户推出了个人财务管理功能，支持通过 Plaid 连接超过 1.2 万家金融机构的银行账户和信用卡。

After connecting accounts, ChatGPT automatically reads the user's real balance, spending records, and debt data, generating a financial dashboard covering investments, daily expenses, subscriptions, and recent bills. 字节开源 Cola DLM：不再从左到右生成，扩散模型也能写文本

ByteDance's Seed team recently open-sourced the diffusion text generation model Cola DLM. Unlike mainstream large models that write word by word, token by token from left to right, this model first 'conceptualizes the main idea' in a continuous semantic space before converting it back into specific text. 字节跳动 Seed 团队近日开源扩散文本生成模型 Cola DLM。与主流大模型逐字逐 token 往右写不同，该模型先在连续语义空间里「想好大意」，再还原成具体文字。

Technically, Cola DLM consists of Text VAE and block-causal DiT. Text VAE compresses text into a continuous latent space, DiT learns semantic distribution within it via Flow Matching, and finally, a decoder reconstructs the text. Diffusion denoising occurs at the semantic layer, not the token layer. 技术上，Cola DLM 由 Text VAE 和 block-causal DiT 组成。Text VAE 把文本压缩进连续潜在空间，DiT 通过 Flow Matching 在其中学习语义分布，最后由解码器还原文本。扩散去噪发生在语义层，而非 token 层。

The open-source version has a total of approximately 2.3B parameters. In 8 evaluations including LAMBADA, MMLU, and HellaSwag, the paper states that its average score under a unified generative protocol surpasses that of AR and LLaDA baselines of similar scale. 本次开源版本总参数约 2.3B，在 LAMBADA、MMLU、HellaSwag 等 8 项评测中，论文称其在统一生成式协议下的平均得分超过同规模 AR 及 LLaDA 基线。
