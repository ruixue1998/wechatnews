17999 元起，华为 Mate XTs 正式发布

昨日，华为发布了世界上第二台量产的「三折叠」手机 Mate XTs非凡大师。芯片提升到麒麟 9020，价格比第一代 Mate XT 还降了 2000 元。

值得一提的是，华为常务董事、终端 BG 董事长余承东再次在华为发布会上公开谈到了芯片： 华为 Mate XTs 官宣搭载麒麟 9020 处理器。 有业内人士指出，这向外界释放了一个很明确的信号：国产芯片供应链，已经实现全链路自主可控。

外观方面，全新一代 Mate XTs非凡大师，除了新增「皓白」和「槿紫」两个配色，手机外观和整个三折叠的方案对比上一代都没有太大变动，更新主要集中在一些参数、铰链机制、后盖工艺上。

在 Mate XTs 上，这次加入了 Mate 70 的红枫原色摄像头，以及之前首发于 Pura 80 系列的超清全景、个性色卡、AI 辅助构图等影像体验。

影像硬件基本没有改变：前代的 1300 万像素广角摄像头升级 4000 万像素 RYYB 传感器，主摄和长焦保持不变。

软件上，华为将不少「PC 级」的生产力应用，如 WPS、万兴绘图、Wind 金融终端等，都下放给 Mate XTs，还支持像电脑一样多窗口灵活排布。

另外，华为 Mate XTs 配备了一支全新手写笔 M-Pen 3，售价 599 元。其支持手机内全局批注，不仅能写能画，还能快速唤起语音识别转文字记录。甚至还可以化身「遥控器」：可以隔空操作 Mate XTs 进行翻页，当做「激光笔」。

苹果自研 AI 搜索曝光

据彭博社昨日报道， 苹果内部正在研发一套代号为 World Knowledge Answers（世界知识问答）的 AI 搜索引擎，准备整合进 Siri 里。

据报道，该计划不只是 Siri，Safari 浏览器和 Spotlight 功能以后也都要用上这套技术。值得一提的是，苹果高管们给这个项目起了个挺唬人的名字——「答案引擎」，预计明年春天就要和 Siri 的重大升级一起发布。

具体来看，苹果所推的这一 AI 搜索引擎要让 Siri 不仅具备全网搜索能力，同时跟 ChatGPT、Google 的 AI 概览一个路数，直接整合文字、图片、视频和本地信息，还配个 AI 总结系统。

据消息人士称， 支撑新版 Siri 的底层技术，部分可能来自苹果的老合作伙伴 Google。 两家公司本周达成了正式协议，苹果将评估并测试 Google 开发的 AI 模型来增强 Siri。

报道中提到，苹果内部给 Siri 的技术改造起了两个代号：Linwood 和 LLM Siri。负责软件工程的 Craig Federighi 最近在内部会议上表示，这次升级会超出预期：「这次端到端的 Siri 改造让我们拿到了想要的成果，不仅能兑现之前的承诺，还能带来更大幅度的提升。」

另外， 苹果还准备完全重构 Siri，预计基于三个核心组件： 规划器、搜索系统和总结器。与以往完全自研不同，这次苹果同样开始考虑引入第三方模型，项目代号叫 Glenwood。

Figure 的机器人又学新技能：整理餐具

Recently, after showcasing the robot's ability to fold clothes in August, robotics company Figure once again unveiled a new skill for its humanoid robot VLA (Vision-Language-Action) model "Helix": neatly placing bowls and glasses into a dishwasher. 近期，机器人公司 Figure 继 8 月展示了「机器人能叠衣服」后，再一次公布了其人形机器人 VLA（视觉-语言-行动）模型「Helix」的一项新技能：将碗碟、玻璃杯整齐放进洗碗机中。

As with the "folding clothes" period, Figure also stated that "putting dishes in the dishwasher" is not an easy task for robots: 与「叠衣服」时期一样，Figure 同样表示「将碗碟放入洗碗机」对于机器人来说并非易事：

Cutlery usually needs to be separated from a messy stack and repositioned, or it may need to be passed between two simultaneously operating robotic arms; and the slipperiness and fragility of the cutlery require fingertip-level precision; putting it into the dishwasher is not a simple matter: the machine rack only provides a centimeter-level tolerance range. 餐具通常要从杂乱的堆叠中分离，并且进行重新定位，亦或者需要在两个同时运作的机械臂之间传递餐具；并且餐具的湿滑、易碎需要指尖级别的精准操作；放进洗碗机也不是简单事：机器架子只提供了厘米级别的容错范围。

In addition, the robot may pick up something new each time. 另外，机器人每次拿取的东西都有可能是新的。

For the robot equipped with Helix, it can achieve: 对于搭载 Helix 的机器人来讲，它能够实现：

  • Loading plates stacked individually in order; 单独堆叠的盘子按照顺序进行装载；
  • Picking up a single glass with one hand and adjusting its orientation, then placing it with the other hand; 用一只手抓起单个玻璃杯并且调整方向，再用另一只手进行放置；
  • Adapting strategies to cope with cluttered environments; 能够调整策略以应对杂乱的环境；
  • Gracefully recovering from errors (such as grabbing incorrectly or colliding). 优雅地从错误操作中恢复（例如抓错或者碰撞了）。

Figure indicates that although tasks such as organizing tableware, organizing logistics packages, and folding clothes are different, Helix can use the same general architecture to handle these tasks, which also marks another step forward for humanoid robots: Figure 表示，整理餐具跟整理物流包裹、叠衣服等任务虽然不同，但通过 Helix 却能用同一种通用架构来处理以上任务，而这也标志着人形机器人迈出了又一步：

A single system can gradually learn new functions through additional data, achieving wider applications without special design. 一个单一系统可以通过额外数据逐步学习新功能，无需特殊设计即可实现更广泛的应用。

💡 AI 教父辛顿：重审 AI 被遗忘的关键路径

Recently, the full text of Nobel laureate Geoffrey Hinton's speech "Boltzmann Machine" at the 2024 Nobel Prize ceremony was officially published in the authoritative journal "Reviews of Modern Physics." In the speech, he systematically reviewed a key but almost forgotten technical route in the history of artificial intelligence development. 近日，诺贝尔奖得主杰弗里·辛顿（Geoffrey Hinton）在 2024 年诺贝尔颁奖典礼上发表的演讲《玻尔兹曼机》全文，正式刊登于权威期刊《现代物理学评论》。演讲中， 他系统性地回顾了人工智能发展史上的一条关键但几乎被遗忘的技术路线。

Hinton pointed out that in the 1980s, coexisting with today's ubiquitous "backpropagation" algorithm was the "Boltzmann machine" based on physical ideas. He emphasized that the learning mechanism of this model does not rely on the complex back-propagation of error signals, but on an elegant "wake-sleep" cycle. "You are essentially teaching the network: to believe what you see when you are awake, and not what you dream in your sleep," in this way, the model's internal "imagination" is consistent with external reality. 辛顿指出，在 1980 年代，与今天无处不在的「反向传播」算法并存的，是基于物理学思想的「玻尔兹曼机」。他强调，这一模型的学习机制并非依赖复杂的误差信号回传，而是通过一个优雅的「清醒-睡眠」循环。「你本质上是在教导网络：要相信清醒时所见，而不信睡梦中所梦」，通过这种方式让模型的内部「想象」与外部现实达成一致。

When discussing the turning point of this history, Hinton shared how the "Boltzmann machine" evolved and ultimately became a key catalyst for igniting the deep learning revolution. 在谈及这段历史的转折时，辛顿分享了「玻尔兹曼机」如何演化并最终成为引爆深度学习革命的关键催化剂。

He believes that it was the technological breakthroughs in this approach that enabled deep neural networks to achieve decisive success in speech recognition and other fields around 2012. He vividly compared this process to, "The Boltzmann machine is like a kind of 'historical enzyme,' it catalyzed the birth of deep learning, and once the reaction is complete, the enzyme itself is no longer needed." 他认为，正是这一路线的技术突破，才使得深度神经网络在 2012 年前后于语音识别等领域取得决定性成功。他将此过程生动地比喻为，「玻尔兹曼机就像一种‘历史的酶’，它催化了深度学习的诞生，而一旦反应完成，酶本身也就不再被需要了。」

Looking to the future, Hinton expressed his deep belief in this "outdated" technology. He believes that backpropagation is difficult to explain biologically, and the learning process represented by the "sleep" stage of the Boltzmann machine may be closer to the true working mechanism of the brain. He firmly believes: 在展望未来时，辛顿表达了对这一「过时」技术的深刻信念。他认为，反向传播在生物学上难以解释，而玻尔兹曼机的「睡眠」阶段所代表的学习过程，可能更接近大脑真实的运作机制。他坚信：

"When we one day understand how the brain learns, we will surely find that the learning mechanism during sleep is a key link." 「当我们有一天搞明白大脑如何学习时，一定会发现，睡眠中的学习机制是关键一环。」

《超人》续作定档 2027 年 7 月

Yesterday, DCU head Jmaes Gunn announced that the sequel to the Superman movie will be released in North America on July 9, 2027, titled "Man of Tomorrow". 昨日，DCU 掌门人 Jmaes Gunn 宣布，《超人》续作电影将于 2027 年 7 月 9 日北美上映，片名为《Man of Tomorrow》（超人：明日之子）。

According to IT House, judging from the picture shared by Jmaes Gunn, "Man of Tomorrow" will continue to tell the story between Lex Luthor and Superman. 据 IT 之家消息，从 Jmaes Gunn 分享的图片来看，《超人：明日之子》将继续讲述莱克斯・卢瑟和超人之间的故事。

It is worth mentioning that the title "Man of Tomorrow" is one of Superman's classic titles, which has appeared in many classic Superman comics. The sequel will continue to star David Corenswet as Clark Kent, Rachel Brosnahan as Lois Lane, and Nicholas Hoult as Lex Luthor. 值得一提的是「明日之子」称呼是超人经典称呼之一，曾出现在多部经典《超人》漫画中。续集将继续由大卫・科伦斯维特饰演克拉克・肯特、瑞秋・布罗斯纳安饰演露易丝・莱恩、尼古拉斯・霍尔特饰演莱克斯・卢瑟。

Warner Bros. previously stated that "Superman" has grossed over US$331 million in North America (currently over US$352 million, US$639 million globally), making it the highest-grossing Superman film in history and the highest-grossing superhero film so far this year. 华纳兄弟此前表示，《超人》在北美已斩获超过 3.31 亿美元（目前已超 3.52 亿美元，全球 6.39 亿美元），成为史上票房最高的超人电影，并且是今年目前为止票房最高的超级英雄电影。

《偷天盗日》定档 10 月

Recently, the action crime film "The Score" is set to be released in mainland China on October 18. 日前，动作犯罪电影《偷天盗日》中国大陆定档 10 月 18 日上映。

The story is adapted from the novel of the same name by Charlie Huston, who also served as the screenwriter. It tells the story of Hank Thompson, an exhausted former baseball player, who inadvertently gets involved in the criminal underworld of 1990s New York City, embarking on a violent battle for survival. 故事改编自查理·休斯顿所著同名小说，同时他也担任编剧，讲述身心俱惫的前棒球运动员汉克·汤普森无意中卷入 1990 年代纽约市的犯罪黑社会世界，展开一场狂暴的生存之战。

The film is directed by Darren Aronofsky and stars Austin Butler, Matt Smith, Zoë Kravitz, Regina King, and Liev Schreiber. It is worth mentioning that this is also Aronofsky's first work to be shown on the mainland Chinese big screen. 该片由达伦·阿伦诺夫斯基导演，奥斯汀·巴特勒、马特·史密斯、佐伊·克拉维茨、雷吉娜·金、列维·施瑞博尔等主演。值得一提的是，该片也是阿伦诺夫斯基作品首登内地大银幕。

《呼啸山庄》新版电影首曝预告

According to Sina Movie news, the new version of the movie "Wuthering Heights" recently released its first trailer. 据新浪电影消息，《呼啸山庄》新版电影近日首次曝光预告片。

It is reported that the film once again depicts the tense and destructive relationship between the Earnshaw and Linton families in Yorkshire, England, as well as the passionate and tragic love between the orphan Heathcliff and Catherine, daughter of the Earnshaw family. 据悉，影片故事再次演绎英格兰约克郡，恩萧家族和林顿家族之间紧张、破坏性的关系，以及孤儿希斯克利夫和恩萧家的女儿凯瑟琳那充满激情和悲剧的爱情。

The film is directed by Emerald Fennell and stars Margot Robbie and Jacob Elordi. It will be released in North America on February 13 next year. 电影由埃默拉尔德·芬内尔执导，玛格特·罗比、雅各布·艾洛蒂主演，将于明年 2 月 13 日北美上映。
