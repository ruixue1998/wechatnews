苹果正式涨价，MacBook Pro 起售价涨至 15999 元

According to Bloomberg, Apple yesterday raised the prices of several products, including Mac, iPad, HomePod, Apple TV, and Vision Pro product lines. 据彭博社报道，苹果昨日上调多款产品价格，涉及 Mac、iPad、HomePod、Apple TV 及 Vision Pro 等产品线。

Apple stated that the rapid expansion of AI data centers has led to a surge in memory and storage demand, and the prices of related components have risen sharply in a short period, making it impossible for the company to fully absorb the cost pressure. 苹果表示，AI 数据中心快速扩张带来内存和存储需求激增，相关组件价格在短时间内大幅上涨，公司已无法继续完全消化成本压力。

The starting prices of several Apple products in the Chinese market have also been adjusted: 苹果多款产品在中国市场的起售价也已调整：

  • MacBook Neo: The starting price has risen to 5499 yuan, up from 4599 yuan for the original 8GB+256GB version; MacBook Neo：起售价涨至 5499 元，原 8GB+256GB 版本为 4599 元；
  • MacBook Air: The starting price has risen to 9999 yuan, up from the original starting price of 8499 yuan; MacBook Air：起售价涨至 9999 元，原起售价为 8499 元；
  • MacBook Pro: The starting price has risen to 15999 yuan, up from the original starting price of 13499 yuan; MacBook Pro：起售价涨至 15999 元，原起售价为 13499 元；
  • iMac: The starting price has risen to 12499 yuan, up from the original starting price of 10999 yuan; iMac：起售价涨至 12499 元，原起售价为 10999 元；
  • iPad Pro: The starting price has risen to 10799 yuan, up from the original starting price of 8999 yuan; iPad Pro：起售价涨至 10799 元，原起售价为 8999 元；
  • iPad Air: The starting price has risen to 5999 yuan, up from the original starting price of 4799 yuan; iPad Air：起售价涨至 5999 元，原起售价为 4799 元；

曝苹果折叠屏 iPhone 七月底量产，铰链问题已「大部分解决」

According to The Elec, a South Korean electronics industry media outlet, Apple's first foldable iPhone is scheduled to begin mass production around late July and is still on track for a September release as originally planned. 据韩国电子产业媒体 The Elec 报道，苹果首款折叠屏 iPhone 计划于 7 月底前后开始量产，并仍按原计划推进 9 月发布。

The report states that Apple has finalized the main specifications for this foldable iPhone, including the display, body, and structural components, and is currently in the mass production preparation phase. The first batch of products will be produced by Foxconn, and Apple completed the first round of trial production in April. 报道称，苹果已确定这款折叠屏 iPhone 的显示屏、机身、结构件等主要规格，目前进入量产准备阶段。首批产品将由富士康负责生产，苹果已在 4 月完成第一轮试生产。

The hinge components are supplied by Taiwan's Shin Nihon Kohden and the US's Amphenol, and both companies will provide hinge modules manufactured using 3D printing. 铰链部分由中国台湾新日兴和美国安费诺供应，两家公司将提供 3D 打印方式制造的铰链模组。

It is worth mentioning that the report states Apple encountered hinge issues during development, including slight noise after millions of durability tests and increased defect rates due to larger tolerances in some assembly stages. However, these issues have now been "mostly resolved." 值得一提的是，报道称苹果在开发过程中曾遇到铰链问题，包括经历数百万次耐久测试后出现轻微噪音，以及部分组装环节公差偏大导致不良率上升。不过，相关问题目前已「大部分解决」。

苹果 Siri AI 新增限制，明确拒绝读取网页链接内容

According to 9to5Mac, Apple has added a new rule to Siri AI's system prompt in iOS 27 Developer Beta 2, requiring it to explicitly inform users that it cannot access web pages and must not provide subsequent suggestions or workarounds. The specific prompt is as follows: 据 9to5Mac 报道，苹果在 iOS 27 开发者测试版 Beta 2 中，为 Siri AI 的系统提示词 (prompt) 加入了一条新规则，要求其明确告知用户无法访问网页，并且不得提供后续建议或绕过方式。具体 prompt 如下：

You cannot access the content behind a URL: When a user provides a URL and asks you to summarize, read, or extract information from it, please inform them that you cannot access web pages. Do not provide subsequent suggestions or workarounds. 你无法访问 URL 背后的内容：当用户提供 URL，并要求你总结、阅读或提取其中的信息时，请告知他们你无法访问网页。不要提供后续建议或变通方法。

The report pointed out that Siri AI itself could not directly access the web content behind a URL before, but this new prompt rule further clarifies the handling method. 报道指出，Siri AI 此前本身就不能直接访问 URL 背后的网页内容，但这次新增的提示词规则进一步明确了处理方式。

Apple has not yet explained the specific reason for adding this rule. The report mentioned that this adjustment may be related to the controversy over web content being directly scraped and summarized by AI chatbots: if users can get content summaries without visiting the original web page, it may reduce website traffic and affect the sustainability of the open web ecosystem. 苹果尚未说明加入这一规则的具体原因。报道提到，这一调整可能与网页内容被 AI 聊天机器人直接抓取和总结的争议有关：如果用户无需进入原网页即可获取内容摘要，可能会减少网站访问量，并影响开放网页生态的可持续性。

IBM 发布 0.7 纳米芯片技术，晶体管数量接近 1000 亿个

Yesterday, IBM unveiled its first chip technology below the 1-nanometer node, adopting a 0.7-nanometer transistor architecture based on a new three-dimensional "nanostack" architecture. IBM claims it can integrate nearly 100 billion transistors on a fingernail-sized chip, with transistor density nearly double that of IBM's 2-nanometer chip released in 2021. 昨天，IBM 发布其首个低于 1 纳米节点的芯片技术，采用 0.7 纳米晶体管架构，基于新的三维「nanostack」纳米堆叠架构，IBM 称其可在指甲盖大小的芯片上集成近 1000 亿个晶体管，晶体管密度接近 2021 年发布的 IBM 2 纳米芯片的两倍。

IBM stated that this node chip is expected to deliver up to a 50% performance improvement or a 70% energy efficiency improvement compared to IBM's 2-nanometer node chip, targeting computing scenarios such as generative AI, cloud infrastructure, and next-generation electronic devices. IBM 表示，这一节点芯片相比 IBM 2 纳米节点芯片，预计最高可带来 50% 的性能提升，或 70% 的能效提升，面向生成式 AI、云基础设施和下一代电子设备等计算场景。

In a recent Silicon Valley Girl interview, Fei-Fei Li, a member of the U.S. National Academy of Engineering and founder of World Labs, stated that in the face of technology with rapidly improving cognitive abilities, what individuals most need to cultivate is "agency," which is the ability to actively understand, use, and master tools. 在日前的 Silicon Valley Girl 访谈中，美国国家工程院院士、World Labs 创始人李飞飞围绕 AI 对未来职场的影响表示，面对一种认知能力快速提升的技术，个人最需要培养的是「agency」，即主动理解、使用并驾驭工具的能力。
