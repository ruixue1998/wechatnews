时隔四年，红色 iPhone 或回归

According to Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman, Apple may introduce a dark red color as a "flagship model" option for the iPhone 18 generation. 据彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 报道，苹果可能会在 iPhone 18 这一代上推出深红色作为「旗舰机型」的配色。

According to reports, Apple is testing a new dark red color for the iPhone 18 Pro series. This marks the first time a red hue has appeared on an iPhone Pro model, and it's also the return of red after a four-year hiatus since the iPhone 14 in 2022. 据报道，苹果正为 iPhone 18 Pro 系列测试全新深红色配色。这是红色系首次登陆 iPhone Pro 机型，也是自 2022 年 iPhone 14 后，红色时隔四年的回归。

(In March 2017, the iPhone 7 and iPhone 7 Plus (PRODUCT)RED Special Edition were released, but Apple has not launched one since the iPhone 14.) （2017 年 3 月，iPhone 7 和 iPhone 7 Plus 红色特别版发布，而在 iPhone 14 之后苹果再无推出过）

The return of red is thanks to the unibody aluminum alloy manufacturing process adopted by the iPhone 17 Pro. This new process significantly enhances the color rendering and consistency of metal surfaces, giving the Pro series stronger feasibility in color expansion. 此次红色回归得益于 iPhone 17 Pro 启用的铝合金一体成型机身工艺。这种新工艺显著提升了金属表面的着色表现力与一致性，让 Pro 系列在色彩拓展方面具备更强可行性。

According to leaks, the iPhone 18 Pro may adopt a "dual flagship color" strategy: deep red as a new color, while retaining the previous generation's popular "Starry Orange." Previously rumored colors such as purple and brown are merely test variants of deep red under different process parameters. 据爆料，iPhone 18 Pro 可能采用「双旗舰配色」策略：深红色作为新配色，同时保留上一代大热的「星宇橙」。此前传闻的紫色、棕色等配色，则只是深红色在不同工艺参数下的测试变体。

It is worth noting that Apple has adopted a completely different color strategy for the foldable iPhone Fold: offering only two basic shades, dark gray/black and white/light silver, returning to the classic color logic of the original iPhone. 值得注意的是，苹果对折叠屏 iPhone Fold 采取了截然不同的配色策略：仅提供深灰/黑色和白色/浅银色两款基础色调，回归初代 iPhone 的经典配色逻辑。

Regarding sales information, blogger "Chanashuma" recently posted that the iPhone Fold foldable screen will begin formal mass production around July, similar to the iPhone 18 Pro, and will be released simultaneously. 销售信息方面，博主「刹那数码」近期发文表示，iPhone Fold 折叠屏会跟 iPhone 18 Pro 一样在 7 月左右开启正式的大规模量产，并同步发售。

This news is also consistent with analyst Ming-Chi Kuo's previous predictions. Kuo previously pointed out that these two products would be launched in the second half of 2026, while the standard iPhone 18 and iPhone 18e would be delayed until the first half of 2027. 而这一消息也与分析师郭明錤此前预测一致，郭明錤曾指出这两款产品会在 2026 年下半年推出，而标准版 iPhone 18 与 iPhone 18e 则推迟到 2027 年上半年。

Gurman also recently reported that Apple is accelerating the development of three new wearable devices: smart glasses, wearable pendants, and AirPods with cameras. Gurman 还在近期报道提到，苹果正在加速研发三款新型可穿戴设备：智能眼镜、可穿戴挂件以及带摄像头的 AirPods。

The smart glasses are positioned at the high end, benchmarked against Meta's AI glasses series, with an internal codename N50, making them the flagship product in Apple's AI hardware lineup; similar to Meta's Ray-Ban glasses, they do not have a display but interact via speakers, microphones, and cameras. 智能眼镜定位最高端，对标 Meta AI 眼镜系列，内部代号为 N50，是苹果 AI 硬件阵容中的旗舰产品；与 Meta 的 Ray-Ban 眼镜类似，它不具备显示屏，而是通过扬声器、麦克风和摄像头进行交互。

Apple will differentiate itself through high-end materials in manufacturing processes and camera technology, not relying on third-party frames but rather entirely self-developed designs. It is reported that Apple has already prototyped various colors and material styles internally. 苹果将在制造工艺和摄像头技术上通过高端材料做出差异化，并不依赖第三方镜框，而是完全自研设计。据悉，苹果内部已经打样了多种颜色、材质的款式。

Functionally, these glasses will be equipped with dual cameras: one for capturing high-resolution photos and videos, and the other focused on computer vision for understanding environmental context (similar to Vision Pro technology). 在功能层面，这款眼镜将配备双摄像头：一颗用于拍摄高分辨率照片和视频，另一颗专注于计算机视觉，用于理解环境背景（类似 Vision Pro 的技术）。

As for the new AI pendants and AirPods, they represent a lighter intervention method. This is a low-barrier solution Apple has prepared specifically for users who prefer not to wear glasses: 至于新的 AI 挂件与 AirPods，则是一种更轻量的介入方式。这是苹果专门针对不愿佩戴眼镜的用户准备的低门槛方案：

  • One is a pendant similar to the Humane AI Pin, which can be clipped to clothing or worn as a necklace, acting as an "external sense" for the phone. 一款是类似 Humane AI Pin 的挂件，可夹在衣服或作为项链佩戴，充当手机的「外置感官」。
  • There is also a version of AirPods with a camera, primarily relying on a low-resolution camera to assist AI operations, offering a discreet form factor and high popularity. 还有一款带摄像头的 AirPods，主要依靠低分辨率摄像头辅助 AI 运行，形态隐蔽且普及度高。

Currently, the development progress of these three products varies: AirPods with cameras are progressing the fastest, expected to be released as early as this year; the AI pendant device is currently in its early stages, and if not canceled, will be launched as early as next year; while the mass production plan for smart glasses is set to start in December, with an official release expected in 2027. 目前，这三款产品的研发进度不一：带摄像头的 AirPods 进展最快，最早有望于今年面世；AI 挂件设备目前处于早期阶段，若未被取消，最早将于明年推出；而智能眼镜的量产计划定于 12 月启动，预计 2027 年正式发布。

It is worth mentioning that the computing power of these three AI hardware products still relies on the iPhone, and they are not independent computing platforms. 值得一提的是，这三款 AI 硬件产品的算力仍需依赖 iPhone，并非独立的计算平台。

特斯拉无线充电技术获批

The U.S. Federal Communications Commission (FCC) has officially approved Tesla's deployment of Ultra-Wideband (UWB) technology in its CyberCab models for high-precision positioning in wireless charging systems. 美国联邦通信委员会（FCC）正式批准了特斯拉在其 CyberCab 车型上部署超宽带（UWB）技术，用于无线充电系统的高精度定位。

According to documents disclosed by the FCC, this system is defined as a pulsed UWB radio device that establishes a point-to-point communication link through UWB transceivers mounted on both the vehicle and the ground charging pad. 根据 FCC 披露的文件，这套系统被定义为一种脉冲式 UWB 无线电装置，通过车载与地面充电板各自搭载的 UWB 收发器建立点对点通信链路。

When the vehicle approaches the charging area, the system can track its spatial position in real-time, guiding the CyberCab to precisely park at the alignment point with the highest charging efficiency. 当车辆驶近充电区域时，系统可实时追踪其空间位置，引导 CyberCab 精准停靠至充电效率最高的对准点。

It is worth noting that the UWB signal designed by Tesla is only briefly activated the moment the vehicle approaches the charging pad, and primarily propagates within the near-field space between the vehicle body and the ground. 值得注意的是，特斯拉设计的 UWB 信号仅在车辆靠近充电板的瞬间短暂激活，且主要在车身与地面之间的近场空间内传播。

This low-power, short-duration, directional communication strategy not only ensures positioning accuracy but also effectively avoids potential interference with surrounding electronic devices. 这种低功耗、短时、定向的通信策略，既保障了定位精度，又有效规避了对周边电子设备的潜在干扰。

Tesla specifically emphasized that the signal is completely turned off when not in operation, and the vehicle body structure itself also acts as a shield, further reducing the risk of electromagnetic leakage. 特斯拉特别强调，信号在非工作状态下完全关闭，且车身结构本身也起到屏蔽作用，进一步降低电磁泄露风险。
