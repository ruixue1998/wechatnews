M5 Max 早期跑分曝光：或登顶 Mac 性能榜

According to 9to5Mac, the first Geekbench benchmark data has been leaked ahead of the official launch of the new MacBook Pro with the M5 Max chip. 据 9to5Mac 报道，在搭载 M5 Max 芯片的新款 MacBook Pro 正式开售前夕，首批 Geekbench 跑分数据已提前流出。

This test was based on a device with the model number "Mac17,7", corresponding to the 16-inch MacBook Pro set to launch on March 11. 本次测试基于一台型号为「Mac17,7」的设备，对应即将于 3 月 11 日上市的 16 英寸 MacBook Pro。

The M5 Max, equipped with an 18-core CPU, recorded a single-core score of 4268 and a multi-core score of 29233 in Geekbench. 搭载 18 核 CPU 的 M5 Max 在 Geekbench 中录得单核得分 4268 分、多核得分 29233 分。

Compared to the 16-core M4 Max (average single-core 3915, average multi-core 25702) in the 2024 16-inch MacBook Pro, single-core performance increased by about 9%, and multi-core performance by about 13.7%. 与 2024 款 16 英寸 MacBook Pro 所搭载的 16 核 M4 Max（单核均值 3915 分、多核均值 25702 分）相比，单核性能提升约 9%，多核性能提升约 13.7%。

In a cross-comparison, the M5 Max's scores also surpassed all versions of the M4 Max equipped in this year's Mac Studio—the 14-core version scored 4015 in single-core and 23560 in multi-core, while the 16-core version scored 4028 in single-core and 26166 in multi-core. 横向对比来看，M5 Max 的成绩同样超越了今年 Mac Studio 所配备的 M4 Max 全部版本——14 核版本单核 4015 分、多核 23560 分，16 核版本单核 4028 分、多核 26166 分。

If the benchmark data for the final production version matches this, the M5 Max will simultaneously top both the single-core and multi-core charts of the Geekbench Mac rankings. 若最终量产版本的跑分数据与此吻合，M5 Max 将同时登顶 Geekbench Mac 榜单的单核与多核两项榜首。

Regarding the GPU, the M5 Max achieved a Metal score of 232718 in this test, ranking second on the Geekbench Metal chart, only behind the M3 Ultra (32-core CPU + 80-core GPU) equipped in this year's Mac Studio. GPU 方面，本次测试中 M5 Max 的 Metal 得分为 232718 分，在 Geekbench Metal 榜单中位列第二，仅次于今年 Mac Studio 所搭载的 M3 Ultra（32 核 CPU + 80 核 GPU）。

烧钱建数据中心，甲骨文计划裁员数千人应对现金流压力

According to Bloomberg, Oracle is planning large-scale layoffs affecting thousands of employees, as the company's massive investments in AI data center expansion are causing significant cash flow pressure. 据彭博社报道，甲骨文正计划大规模裁员，受影响员工人数达数千人，原因是公司在 AI 数据中心扩张上的巨额投入正引发严重的现金流压力。

The layoffs will affect multiple departments within the company and could begin as early as this month. According to sources, some of the eliminated positions belong to job categories that the company believes will gradually shrink due to AI development. The scale of these layoffs is expected to exceed Oracle's usual rolling layoffs. 裁员将波及公司多个部门，最快可能于本月内开始实施。据知情人士透露，部分被裁撤的岗位属于公司认为将因 AI 发展而逐渐萎缩的职能类别。本次裁员规模预计将超出 Oracle 以往常规的滚动式裁员范畴。

Meanwhile, Oracle announced internally this week that it would review a large number of vacant positions in its cloud computing division, effectively slowing down or even freezing the department's hiring process. As of the end of May 2025, Oracle's global workforce totaled approximately 162,000 people. 与此同时，甲骨文本周已在内部宣布，将对云计算部门的大量空缺职位展开审查，实际上相当于放缓乃至冻结了该部门的招聘进程。截至 2025 年 5 月底，甲骨文全球员工总数约为 16.2 万人。

Behind this major layoff is the immense financial pressure Oracle is currently facing. Last December, Oracle disclosed that its capital expenditure forecast for fiscal year 2026 would increase by another $15 billion from the $35 billion estimated in its Q1 earnings report. 这场大裁员的背后，是甲骨文正在承受的巨大财务压力。去年 12 月，Oracle 披露其 2026 财年资本支出预期将比第一季度财报时预估的 350 亿美元再增加 150 亿美元。

In February this year, the company further stated its plan to raise $45 billion to $50 billion for the full year through debt and equity financing to support the continuous expansion of its cloud infrastructure, a move that intensified investors' concerns about its debt burden. 今年 2 月，公司进一步表示计划通过债务和股权融资方式，全年筹资 450 亿至 500 亿美元，以支撑云基础设施的持续扩张，此举加剧了投资者对其债务负担的担忧。

Wall Street analysts expect that Oracle's data center spending in its cloud computing division will continue to depress the company's cash flow for several years, with a positive turnaround not expected until around 2030. 华尔街分析师预计，Oracle 云计算部门的数据中心支出将在未来数年内持续压低公司现金流，直至 2030 年前后才有望转正。

The high upfront costs brought by AI are driving a wave of layoffs in the tech industry. Microsoft cut about 15,000 employees last year as spending on data centers and AI software development continued to climb; last week, Block co-founder Jack Dorsey announced a reduction of nearly half of its staff, citing AI for efficiency gains. AI 带来的高额前期成本正在推动科技行业的裁员潮。微软去年在数据中心与 AI 软件开发支出持续攀升之际裁减了约 1.5 万名员工；上周，Block 公司联合创始人 Jack Dorsey 以 AI 提升效率为由，宣布裁减近一半员工。

MacBook Neo 桃粉色预售即告急，高管解释命名由来

According to 9to5Mac, after pre-sales for Apple's entry-level laptop, the MacBook Neo, began, "Peach Pink" became the most popular choice and was the first to experience supply delays. Shipping dates were pushed from the official release date of March 11 to between March 13 and 20, while the remaining seven configurations are still available for delivery on the release day. 据 9to5Mac 报道，苹果入门级笔记本 MacBook Neo 预售开启后，「桃粉色」成为最受欢迎的选择，并率先出现供货延迟，发货日期从官方发布日 3 月 11 日推迟至 3 月 13 日至 20 日之间，其余七种配置仍可在发布当天收货。

Regarding the origin of the "Neo" naming, Colleen Novielli, Apple's Mac Product Marketing Director, stated in an interview with TechRadar that the name "Neo" was chosen to convey a youthful and vibrant product character. 关于「Neo」这一命名的由来，苹果 Mac 产品市场总监 Colleen Novielli 在接受 TechRadar 采访时称，「Neo」这一名称是为了传递一种年轻、充满活力的产品气质。

We wanted the name to sound fun, approachable, and fresh, truly fitting the spirit of this product. 我们希望这个名字听起来有趣、亲切、充满新鲜感，真正契合这款产品的精神。

She also emphasized that the MacBook Neo is a "product completely redesigned from the ground up," rather than a continuation of the previous 12-inch MacBook. 她同时强调，MacBook Neo 是一款「从零开始全新设计的产品」，而非此前 12 英寸 MacBook 的延续。

Another focus of external attention is why Apple did not equip this product with the newer A19 Pro. 外界关注的另一焦点，是苹果为何没有为这款产品搭载更新的 A19 Pro。

Some media outlets pointed out that Apple CEO Tim Cook's statement during the Q1 fiscal year 2026 earnings call might have provided the answer. 有媒体指出，苹果 CEO Tim Cook 在 2026 财年 Q1 财报电话会议上的表态或许已给出答案。

He noted at the time that shipments of the iPhone 17 Pro series were affected by tight capacity at advanced process nodes, and explicitly stated, "The supply of advanced nodes our SoC production relies on is constrained, and current supply chain flexibility is below normal levels." 他当时指出，受制于先进制程节点的产能紧张，iPhone 17 Pro 系列的出货量受到影响，并明确表示「我们的 SoC 生产所依赖的先进节点供应受限，当前供应链灵活性低于正常水平」。

The A19 Pro uses a packaging process similar to the A18 Pro, but its memory reaches 12GB LPDDR5X specifications. If it could be integrated, it would significantly enhance the competitiveness of this entry-level Mac. However, facing high packaging costs and tight production capacity, Apple ultimately chose to compromise. A19 Pro 采用与 A18 Pro 相近的封装工艺，但内存来到了 12GB LPDDR5X 的规格，若能搭载，将显著提升这款入门 Mac 的竞争力，但面对高昂的封装成本与吃紧的产能，苹果最终选择了妥协。

Analyst Ming-Chi Kuo pointed out that the successor to the MacBook Neo is planned for release next year, at which time Apple will shift to TSMC's more advanced process nodes, which is expected to create conditions for equipping entry-level models with more powerful chips. 分析师郭明錤指出，MacBook Neo 的继任机型已计划于明年发布，届时苹果将转向台积电更先进的制程节点，有望为入门级机型搭载更强的芯片创造条件。

🦞 龙虾首次走进手机，小米开启 miclaw 封闭测试

Yesterday, Xiaomi announced the launch of its mobile agent product "Xiaomi miclaw" and initiated a small-scale closed beta test. This marks the first attempt in China to bring the OpenClaw application to mobile phones, built upon Xiaomi's MiMo large model, with initial support for Xiaomi 17 series models. 昨天，小米宣布推出移动端 Agent 产品「Xiaomi miclaw」，并开启小范围封闭测试。这是国内首个将 OpenClaw 应用带到手机端的尝试，基于小米 MiMo 大模型构建，首批支持小米 17 系列机型。

Xiaomi miclaw focuses on verifying the execution capabilities of large models within Xiaomi's "Human-Car-Home All-Scenario Ecosystem" system, exploring the path for models to transition from "dialogue capabilities" to "system-level execution capabilities." Xiaomi miclaw 聚焦验证大模型在小米「人车家全生态」系统中的执行能力，探索模型从「对话能力」向「系统级执行能力」的落地路径。

Unlike traditional AI assistants running within application sandboxes, miclaw operates as a system application, encapsulating 50+ system-level tools and ecosystem services. After user authorization, it can invoke first-party applications and ecosystem capabilities, autonomously selecting system-level tools to complete commands. 与运行在应用沙箱内的传统 AI 助手不同，miclaw 以系统应用身份运行，封装了 50+ 系统级工具与生态服务，可在用户授权后调用一方应用及生态能力，自主选择系统级工具完成指令。

  • Regarding system underlying capabilities, its core engine adopts an inference-execution loop mechanism, where the model autonomously determines which tool to call and what parameters to pass at each step. Tool execution has independent timeout protection, and the entire asynchronous architecture does not block system threads; 系统底层能力方面，其核心引擎采用推理-执行循环机制，模型在每一步自主判断调用哪个工具、传入什么参数，工具执行具备独立超时保护，全程异步架构不阻塞系统线程；
  • Context management employs a three-level intelligent memory management system. Through round compression and token compression, actual tests show it can save 50% to 90% of token overhead; 上下文管理采用三级智能记忆管理，通过轮次压缩与 Token 压缩，实测可节省 50%~90% 的 token 开销；
  • After user authorization, the system can read personal data such as SMS messages and calendars, achieving a complete "perceive → associate → judge → act" chain. For example, after recognizing a ticket purchase SMS, it can automatically link 6 tools to complete travel preparations, or by analyzing consumption SMS messages from the past 3 months, identify duplicate subscriptions and provide unsubscription suggestions; 在用户授权后，系统可读取短信、日历等个人数据，实现「感知→关联→判断→行动」的完整链路，例如识别购票短信后自动串联 6 个工具完成出行准备，或通过分析近 3 个月消费短信发现重复订阅并给出退订建议；
  • In terms of ecological interconnection, Xiaomi miclaw has implemented a complete Mijia protocol client, theoretically capable of controlling all IoT devices connected to Mijia by users , and also supports MCP (Model Context Protocol) open standards and third-party application access SDKs; 生态互联方面，Xiaomi miclaw 实现了完整的米家协议客户端， 理论上可控制用户接入米家的所有 IoT 设备 ，同时支持 MCP（Model Context Protocol）开放标准及第三方应用接入 SDK；
  • In terms of self-evolution, the system possesses four meta-capabilities: file-level memory, sub-agent creation, MCP service configuration, and sandbox script execution (supporting Python/JavaScript), allowing it to continuously accumulate user preferences and experience during use; 自进化方面，系统具备文件级记忆、子智能体创建、MCP 服务配置与沙箱脚本执行（支持 Python/JavaScript）四种元能力，可在使用过程中持续沉淀用户偏好与经验；
  • In terms of data security, conversation history, user configurations, and skill files are all stored locally on the device. The cloud only transmits current conversation messages during inference, and the data is encrypted; for sensitive operations such as sending SMS messages or creating schedules, a confirmation box will pop up before each execution. 数据安全方面，对话历史、用户配置、技能文件均存储于设备本地，云端仅在推理时传输当前对话消息，且数据经过加密处理；涉及发短信、建日程等敏感操作，每次执行前均会弹出确认框。

This closed beta test is not open for public recruitment and uses an invitation system. Xiaomi stated that because the product is still in the early exploration stage and is continuously being optimized for stability, power consumption, and execution success rate in complex scenarios, it is not recommended to upgrade on daily primary devices. Users who obtain closed beta access will receive a specific version of the system push and can use it after upgrading. 本次封测不公开招募，采用邀请制。小米表示，由于产品仍处于前沿探索阶段，在稳定性、功耗及复杂场景执行成功率方面持续优化中，不推荐在日常主力设备上升级。获得封测权限的用户将收到特定版本的系统推送，升级后方可使用。

魏建军就魏牌 V9X 海报抄袭公开致歉

According to BBN News, Wei Jianjun, Chairman of Great Wall Motor, publicly apologized yesterday for the plagiarism incident involving the Wey V9X advertising poster. 据鞭牛士报道，长城汽车董事长魏建军昨天就魏牌 V9X 广告海报抄袭事件公开致歉。

The incident originated from a promotional poster released by Wey after officially announcing Wei Jianjun as the V9X spokesperson. 事件起因于魏牌官宣魏建军担任 V9X 代言人后发布的一张宣传海报。

Some netizens discovered that the poster was highly similar to a creative proposal they had previously made for the Range Rover Sport, and publicly posted on social media, stating, "The Range Rover Sport creative was something I did around this time last year, and the WEY material is what I saw today," while also calling on Wey to demand creative fees. 有网友发现该海报与其此前为路虎揽运所制作的创意方案高度雷同，并在社交平台公开发文指出「揽运的创意是我去年这会儿做的，WEY 的物料是我今天刷到的」，同时向魏牌喊话索要创意费用。

He also stated that he and Great Wall Motor are willing to bear all legal and economic responsibilities arising from this incident. Wei Jianjun also emphasized that Great Wall Motor has always adhered to the spirit of originality and welcomes continuous supervision from netizens and users. 魏建军还强调，长城汽车一直坚持原创精神，并欢迎广大网友和用户持续监督。

曝奔驰启动「凤凰」平台，拟用吉利 GEEA 架构开发全球紧凑型电动车

According to 36Kr, since late January this year, Mercedes-Benz management has frequently appeared at Geely's Hangzhou Bay R&D Center, and subsequently, Geely management also went to Shanghai Jinqiao to further finalize cooperation directions with Mercedes-Benz. However, ZEEA 3.0 is currently only for the Zeekr brand and has higher costs, so it was ultimately not selected. The more powerful and cost-effective GEEA 4.0 won out. The report also pointed out that Mercedes-Benz is also in preliminary talks with BYD to explore cooperation in the battery field. 但 ZEEA 3.0 目前仅供极氪品牌使用且成本较高，最终未能入选，性能更强、成本更低的 GEEA 4.0 胜出。报道还指出，奔驰还在与比亚迪初步接洽，探讨电池领域合作。

💡 雷军：5 年内限定场景可实现无人驾驶，开放道路「大撒把」路还很长

According to The Paper, Lei Jun, Chairman and CEO of Xiaomi Group, recently stated in an interview that within the next 5 years, true autonomous driving can be achieved in some restricted areas; however, it will take more time for ordinary private cars to achieve full-scenario "hands-off" driving on open roads. 据澎湃新闻报道，近日，小米集团董事长兼 CEO 雷军在接受采访时表示，未来 5 年内，在一些限定场所可以实现真正意义的无人驾驶；但若要普通私家车在开放道路全场景「大撒把」，则还需要更多时间。

He particularly emphasized that current intelligent driving is essentially still assisted driving and cannot replace human driving. When using assisted driving functions, drivers must keep both hands on the steering wheel, look straight ahead, and remain focused. 他特别强调，当下的智能驾驶本质上仍是辅助驾驶，无法取代人驾。 使用辅助驾驶功能时，驾驶员必须双手持方向盘、目视前方、保持专注。

In Lei Jun's view, the implementation of intelligent driving is not just a technical issue, but a systemic engineering challenge. Various technologies such as intelligent driving chips, algorithms, cockpits, and chassis require collaborative efforts across the entire industry chain; at the same time, road infrastructure, laws and regulations, and driving test training and assessment systems also need to be upgraded simultaneously. 在雷军看来，智能驾驶的落地不仅是技术问题，更是系统性工程。智能驾驶芯片、算法、座舱、底盘等各项技术需要整个产业链协同攻关；与此同时，道路基础设施、法律法规、驾考培训与考核体系也都需要同步升级。

According to previous reports, Lei Jun suggested during this year's Two Sessions that efforts should be accelerated to establish automotive intelligent technology standards and optimize motor vehicle driving assessment items, increasing the weight of content related to intelligent connected vehicles in driving tests; 据此前报道，雷军在今年两会期间建议，应该加快建设汽车智能化技术标准和优化机动车驾驶考核项目，提升智能网联汽车相关内容在驾考中的权重；

买书不读指南｜《海的呼吸：碳循环与气候演变的海洋史》

The Ocean's Breath: A Marine History of the Carbon Cycle and Climate Evolution is a popular science book by Dutch paleoceanographer Eelco J. Rohling. The book centers on 4.4 billion years of ocean evolutionary history, covering major geological and climatic events such as Snowball Earth, the Permian-Triassic extinction event, the breakup and drift of the Pangea supercontinent, and contemporary global warming. 《海的呼吸：碳循环与气候演变的海洋史》是荷兰古海洋学家埃尔科·J. 罗林（Eelco J. Rohling）的科普著作，全书以 44 亿年的海洋演化史为主轴，涵盖雪球地球、二叠纪末生物大灭绝、盘古超大陆分裂漂移直至当代全球变暖等重大地质气候事件。

Rohling's core argument is that the ocean is the most important regulator of Earth's climate system and the world's largest carbon reservoir—its carbon content is 15 times the total amount in the atmosphere and all living and dead organisms on Earth; since the Industrial Revolution, the ocean has absorbed over one-third of human-emitted carbon dioxide. 罗林的核心论点在于：海洋是地球气候系统最重要的调节器，也是全球最大的碳储存库——其碳含量是大气与地球上所有生物及死亡有机体总量的 15 倍；自工业革命以来，海洋已吸收人类排放超三分之一的二氧化碳。

Rohling uses this to issue a warning: 'How much longer can the ocean help humanity in this way?' The answer remains unknown for now. 罗林以此发出警示：「海洋还能以这种方式帮助人类多长时间？」答案目前仍未可知。

The table of contents has eight chapters: Introduction, Origins, Factors Influencing Climate Change, Snowball Earth and the Cambrian Explosion, Ocean Acidification, The Age of Reptiles, Winter Is Coming, and The Future Ocean and Climate. The structure is clear, balancing profound geological history with current climate crisis issues. 目录共八章，依次为引言、起源、气候变化的影响因素、雪球地球和生命大爆发、海洋酸化、爬行动物时代、冬天来了，以及未来的海洋和气候，脉络清晰，兼顾深远的地质历史与当下的气候危机议题。
