曝苹果将推 Siri 独立 App 对标 ChatGPT

The report points out that the reconstructed Siri will function as a standalone app, allowing users to directly open and converse with Siri, with a UI similar to iMessage. The main interface will display historical conversations in a list or rounded rectangular grid, allowing users to pin frequently used conversations and search historical records. 报道指出，重构后的 Siri 将作为单独的 App，用户可直接打开与 Siri 对话，UI 与 iMessage 类似。主界面以列表或圆角矩形网格呈现历史对话，可置顶常用会话、搜索历史记录。

The conversation interface adopts a bubble-style layout, supporting free switching between text and voice modes, and also supports uploading documents and images for analysis. When initiating a new conversation, Siri will also recommend prompt words based on usage habits. 对话界面则采用气泡式布局，支持文字与语音模式自由切换，也支持上传文档和图片进行分析。发起新对话时，Siri 还会根据使用习惯推荐提示词。

Meanwhile, Apple is also testing two new system-level entry points: a "Talk to Siri" option will be added to the long-press menu, allowing users to directly send selected content to Siri to start a conversation; a "Siri Write Assist" button will be added to the top of the keyboard. 与此同时，苹果还在测试两个新的系统级入口：长按呼出菜单中将新增「与 Siri 对话」选项，用户可将选中内容直接发送至 Siri 展开对话；键盘顶部则将新增「Siri 帮写」按钮。

Kimi CEO：AI 研发正进入「AI 主导研究」时代，行业标准「已具备被挑战的条件」

Yesterday, Yang Zhilin, CEO of Moonshot AI, delivered a speech at the 2026 Zhongguancun Forum plenary session, titled "Open Source AI: Accelerating the Exploration of Intelligence Limits," systematically elaborating on Kimi's latest progress in large model scalability efficiency and underlying architectural innovation. 昨天，月之暗面 CEO 杨植麟在 2026 中关村论坛全体会议上发表演讲，主题为《开源 AI：加速探索智能上限》，系统阐述了 Kimi 在大模型规模化效率与底层架构创新方面的最新进展。

Yang Zhilin summarized the essence of large models as "converting more energy into intelligence," emphasizing that scalability has been the core foundation of AI development in recent years. 杨植麟将大模型的本质概括为「把更多的能源转化成智能」，强调规模化是过去数年 AI 发展的核心基础。

He pointed out that Kimi improves scalability efficiency across three dimensions: 他指出，Kimi 围绕三个维度提升规模化效率：

  • TOKEN efficiency: Extracting more intelligence from the same data through superior network architecture and optimizers; TOKEN 效率：通过更优的网络架构与优化器，从相同数据中提取更多智能；
  • Long context capability: Kimi's self-developed Kimi Linear architecture can achieve lower loss functions with longer contexts, supporting the completion of more complex tasks; 长上下文能力：Kimi 自研的 Kimi Linear 架构可在更长上下文下获得更低的损失函数，支持更复杂任务的完成；
  • Agent Swarm: By scheduling hundreds of Agents to work collaboratively in parallel, task execution time no longer increases exponentially with complexity, thereby making previously "completely impossible tasks possible". Agent 集群（Agent Swarm）：通过并行调度数百个 Agent 协同工作，使任务执行时间不再随复杂度指数增长，从而让此前「完全不可能实现的任务变得有可能」。

Yang Zhilin also cited a slide from Jensen Huang's keynote speech at NVIDIA GTC 2026, pointing out that the Kimi series of open-source models is becoming a benchmark for global chip manufacturers and research institutions. "If you want to release a new chip, you must evaluate your performance improvement using Kimi or other open-source models." 杨植麟还援引英伟达 GTC 2026 黄仁勋主题演讲中的幻灯片指出，Kimi 系列开源模型正在成为全球芯片厂商和研究机构的评测基准，「如果要发布一个新的芯片，你必须要通过 Kimi 或者其他的开源模型来评测你的性能的提升」。

Regarding the judgment of R&D paradigms, Yang Zhilin outlined a clear evolutionary path: 在研发范式的判断上，杨植麟描绘了一条清晰的演进路径：

Three years ago, it was mainly based on natural internet data with a small amount of manual annotation. Last year, it shifted to large-scale reinforcement learning, and starting this year, it will enter the "AI-led research" phase – AI will autonomously synthesize tasks and training environments, design reward functions, and even explore new network architectures. 三年前以互联网天然数据加少量人工标注为主，去年转向大规模强化学习，而今年起将进入「AI 主导研究」阶段——AI 将自主合成任务与训练环境、设计奖励函数，乃至探索新的网络架构。

He believes that technologies considered industry standards over the past decade (such as the Adam optimizer and standard Attention architecture) are now ripe for challenge. 他认为，过去十年被视为行业标准的技术（如 Adam 优化器、标准 Attention 架构）如今均已具备被挑战的条件。

Many things previously considered standard can now be challenged. 很多以前认为是标准的东西，现在都是可以被挑战的。

💡 经典科幻小说《雪崩》作者：我对头戴设备的判断是错的，二十年后人们还是会看手机

Yesterday, Neal Stephenson, author of the classic science fiction novel "Snow Crash," published an article commenting on Meta's decision to shut down its metaverse project. 昨天，经典科幻小说《雪崩》（Snow Crash）作者 Neal Stephenson 发文，就 Meta 关闭元宇宙项目一事发表评论。

In the article, Stephenson reflected on his core beliefs during his tenure at Magic Leap, a mixed reality (MR) headset company. Stephenson 在文中回顾了自己在混合现实（MR）头戴设备公司 Magic Leap 任职期间的核心信念。

He once convinced colleagues and outsiders: "Do you really think that in twenty years, everyone will still be staring at small rectangular screens in their hands all day?" At the time, he thought the answer was obvious: no. 他曾说服同事和外界：「你真的认为二十年后，每个人还会整天盯着手里的小矩形屏幕吗？」彼时他认为答案显而易见：不会。

However, in this latest article, he directly overturned that judgment. 不过，在这篇最新的文章中，他直接推翻了这一判断。

Readers, I've changed my mind. Twenty years from now, everyone will still be staring at handheld rectangular screens – at least, that's the case if the only alternative is wearing something on your face. 读者，我改变了想法。二十年后，每个人依然会盯着手持的矩形屏幕——至少在唯一的替代方案是在脸上戴东西的前提下，是这样。

Stephenson further pointed out that trying to make head-mounted devices look more and more like ordinary glasses does not solve the acceptance problem; instead, it creates a new crisis of trust. Stephenson 进一步指出，试图将头戴设备做得越来越像普通眼镜，并不能解决接受度问题，反而带来了新的信任危机。

The advent of Google Glass almost immediately spawned the term "glasshole," and Meta's smart glasses products, which resemble ordinary glasses, also encountered similar social backlash. Google Glass 问世后几乎立刻催生了「glasshole」（眼镜傻 X）一词，Meta 外形接近普通眼镜的智能眼镜产品同样遭遇了类似的社会反弹。

He believes the root cause lies in the lack of transparency: when someone is staring at their phone, bystanders can at least tell they are looking at their phone; but when facing a wearer of a head-mounted device, no one knows if the other person is looking at them, or if the camera is recording.
