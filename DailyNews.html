It is worth noting that the day before the trial, Musk posted over 20 times on X, calling Altman "Scam Altman" (fraudster). "How can we proceed with the trial when you are constantly making things worse outside of court?" Ultimately, both parties agreed to "restrain their comments" on social media. 「我们怎么能在你不断在法庭外让事情变得更糟的情况下推进庭审？」最终，双方同意在社交媒体上「克制发言」。

Musk then appeared as the first witness, positioning himself on the stand as "the savior of humanity" and attributing the future of AI to two outcomes: "either a Star Trek-style utopia or a Terminator-style dystopia." He directly called Altman a "thief": 马斯克随后作为首位证人出庭，在证人席上将自己定位为「人类的拯救者」，并将 AI 的未来归结为两种结局：「要么是《星际迷航》式的乌托邦，要么是《终结者》式的反乌托邦。」他直接称奥特曼是「小偷」：

DeepSeek 内测「识图模式」，多模态新模型或将发布

DeepSeek yesterday launched its "Image Recognition Mode" test, alongside existing "Fast Mode" and "Expert Mode," possessing full multimodal image understanding capabilities, not just simple OCR text recognition. DeepSeek 昨日开启「识图模式」测试，与现有的「快速模式」、「专家模式」并列，具备完整的多模态图像理解能力，并非简单的 OCR 文字识别。

🔗 Related reading: Just now, DeepSeek's big update! Finally "opened its eyes" | Includes extensive real-world tests 🔗 相关阅读： 刚刚，DeepSeek大更新！终于「开眼」了| 附大量实测

From the actual test results, DeepSeek's Image Recognition Mode has a high overall accuracy, and can provide an answer in as little as half a second without enabling thinking mode. For common scenarios such as movie stills, abstract images, and product images, its recognition and understanding performance is good. 从实测表现来看，DeepSeek 识图模式的整体准确率较高，在不开启思考模式的情况下，最快半秒即可给出回答。对于电影剧照、抽象图片、商品图等常见场景，识别与理解表现良好。

What's more noteworthy is its thinking process: in addition to describing the visual content, it actively inquires about the publisher's identity, image metaphors, and subtext, self-corrects multiple times during the reasoning process, and even spontaneously lists questions to verify premises one by one before drawing conclusions, demonstrating a reasoning logic close to human reading habits. 更值得关注的是其思考过程：在描述画面内容之外，还会主动追问发布者身份、图像隐喻与潜台词，并在推理过程中多次自我纠正，甚至在给出结论前，自发列出问题逐一验证前提假设，呈现出接近人类阅读习惯的推理逻辑。

However, the image recognition mode still has clear limitations. In the classic "counting fingers" test, DeepSeek made an error in its first attempt, claiming it was "dizzy from counting," but was able to provide the correct answer after user guidance or prompts. 不过，识图模式目前仍存在明显局限。经典的「数手指」测试中，DeepSeek 首次作答出错，自称「数晕了」，但在用户引导或提示后能够给出正确答案。

Furthermore, the image recognition process currently does not support online search and relies solely on the model's own knowledge base for answers. For newer things, such as Apple's mascot "Finder-chan" launched this year, it cannot be recognized. 此外，识图过程暂不支持联网搜索，仅依赖模型自身知识库作答，对于较新的事物，如苹果今年推出的吉祥物「Finder 酱」，无法识别。

And just yesterday, Xiaokang Chen, a researcher from the DeepSeek multimodal team, posted on X, "Now, we see you. 👀", accompanied by an image comparing DeepSeek's whale mascot from "blindfolded" to "eyes open," which is widely interpreted as a preview of an upcoming new multimodal model. 而就在昨天，DeepSeek 多模态团队研究员 Xiaokang Chen 在 X 上发文「Now, we see you. 👀」，并配图 DeepSeek 鲸鱼吉祥物从「蒙眼」到「睁眼」的对比，外界普遍将其解读为多模态新模型即将上线的预告。

曝苹果 iOS 27 将大幅升级照片 App

According to Bloomberg, Apple is making significant upgrades to the built-in photo editing features for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Leveraging the Apple Intelligence platform, it plans to introduce a new suite of AI image editing tools in iOS 27, iPadOS 27, and macOS 27, scheduled for release this June. 据彭博社报道，苹果正为 iPhone、iPad 和 Mac 的内置照片编辑功能进行重大升级，依托 Apple Intelligence 平台，计划在今年六月发布的 iOS 27、iPadOS 27 和 macOS 27 中推出一套全新的 AI 图像编辑工具套件。

Reporter Mark Gurman stated that the new features will add an "Apple Intelligence Tools" section to the Photos app's editing interface, including four tools: Extend, Enhance, Reframe, and Clean Up. 记者 Mark Gurman 表示，新功能将在照片 App 的编辑界面中新增「Apple Intelligence Tools」专区，包含 Extend（扩图）、Enhance（增强）、Reframe（重新构图）和 Clean Up（消除）四项工具。

  • "Extend" allows users to generate additional image content beyond the original frame or automatically fill in the surrounding scene. Users can drag the edges of the image with their fingers to control the direction and extent of the expansion; 「扩图」允许用户在原始画面边框之外生成额外的图像内容或自动填充周围的场景，用户可通过手指拖拽画面边缘，控制扩展的方向和幅度；
  • "Enhance" uses AI to automatically optimize color, light, and overall image quality; 「增强」则利用 AI 自动优化色彩、光线与整体画质；
  • "Recompose" is mainly for spatial photos, allowing users to adjust the perspective after shooting; 「重新构图」主要面向空间照片，支持用户在拍摄完成后调整视角；
  • "AI Erase" already exists in the current version of iOS 26, supporting the removal of specified objects from images, and will be retained in iOS 27. 「AI 消除」在现有版本的 iOS 26 上已经存在，支持从图像中移除指定对象，将在 iOS 27 中继续保留。

追觅全面回应造车：「华为模式」轻资产、自研芯片、押注全球高净值人群

According to Jiemian News, Ma Junye, President of Dreame's Starry Sky Project, revealed in a media interview yesterday in San Francisco, USA, that the Dreame automobile team actually began preparations as early as 2021-2022, concurrently with Xiaomi Auto, followed by approximately three years of technical quiet period. 据界面新闻报道，追觅星空计划总裁马俊野昨日在美国旧金山接受媒体专访时透露，追觅汽车团队实际早在 2021 至 2022 年间便已启动筹备，与小米汽车同期，此后经历约三年技术静默期。

Currently, the team size exceeds a thousand people, with R&D personnel accounting for about 70%, and is expected to expand to nearly 2,000 people in the second half of this year. CEO Yu Hao is deeply involved in vehicle ID styling and product definition, maintaining high-frequency communication with the team almost daily. 目前团队规模超千人，研发人员占比约 70%，预计今年下半年扩充至近 2000 人。CEO 俞浩深度参与整车 ID 造型与产品定义，几乎每天与团队保持高频沟通。

In terms of business model, Dreame adopts a "Huawei-like model," collaborating with mature domestic and international OEMs for joint R&D and contract manufacturing, without building its own vehicle factories. 商业模式上，追觅采用类「华为模式」，与国内外成熟主机厂联合研发并代工生产，不自建整车工厂。

At the core technology level, it insists on independent R&D, covering full-wire intelligent chassis, in-vehicle motors, solid-state power batteries, and intelligent cockpits, and has for the first time confirmed independent planning and development of cockpit and intelligent driving computing chips. 核心技术层面则坚持自研，涵盖全线控智能底盘、车载电机、固态动力电池、智能座舱，并首次确认独立规划研发座舱与智驾计算芯片。

Addressing external doubts about the capital chain, Ma Junye stated that they have "reserved sufficient funds for car manufacturing" and introduced some social capital and industrial funds to share the risks. 针对外界对资金链的质疑，马俊野表示已「储备了足够的资金去造车」，并引入部分社会化资本与产业基金分摊风险。

Regarding product pricing, Dreame explicitly stated that "cars under 200,000 yuan will definitely not be made" . The starting price for mass-produced models will be over one million yuan , with some versions exceeding two million yuan. A pure electric coupe will be launched next year, followed by an SUV in the same series. In terms of market logic, Dreame targets approximately 400 million high-net-worth individuals globally, aiming to fill the gap for high-end Chinese new energy vehicles in overseas markets. 产品定价方面， 追觅明确「20 万元以内的车肯定不会做」 ，量产车型起步价在 百万元以上 ，部分版本突破 200 万元，明年上市纯电轿跑，随后推出同系列 SUV。在市场逻辑上，追觅将目标锁定全球约 4 亿高净值人群，意在填补高端中国新能源汽车在海外市场的空白。

As for the analogy of being "the next LeEco," he explicitly denied it , emphasizing that Dreame is engaged in genuine underlying technology R&D, committed to delivering tangible products to the global market. 而对于「下一个乐视」的类比，他明确否认 ，强调追觅进行的是真实的底层技术研发，致力于交付面向全球的实体产品。

AI 分词器存在「语言歧视」：用印地语问 Claude，token 消耗是英文的 3 倍以上

Yesterday, AI researcher Aran Komatsuzaki published a comparative review of mainstream large model tokenizers, revealing that tokenizers exhibit "language discrimination": 昨天，AI 研究员 Aran Komatsuzaki 公布了一项针对主流大模型分词工具（tokenizer）的横评结果，揭示了 Tokenizer 存在「语言歧视」：

Non-English users consume significantly more tokens than English users when using the same model, which is equivalent to being quietly charged a "non-English tax". 非英语用户在使用同一模型时，实际消耗的 token 数远多于英语用户，相当于被悄悄征收了一笔「非英文税」。

He translated Rich Sutton's well-known paper "The Bitter Lesson" into 9 languages and fed them into the tokenizers of 6 models, measuring the consumption multiplier for each language on different models, using the token count of the English original on OpenAI's tokenizer as a 1x baseline. 他将 Rich Sutton 的知名论文《The Bitter Lesson》翻译成 9 种语言，并分别喂入 6 家模型的 tokenizer，以英文原文在 OpenAI 分词工具上的 token 数为 1 倍基准，测量各语言在不同模型上的消耗倍数。

The results show that when the same content is queried in Chinese, Claude's token consumption reaches 1.71 times the baseline, while OpenAI's is only 1.15 times. The situation is even more pronounced for Hindi on Claude, with token consumption exceeding 3.24 times the baseline, and Arabic also reaching 2.86 times. 结果显示，同样的内容用中文提问，Claude 的 token 消耗达到基准的 1.71 倍，而 OpenAI 仅为 1.15 倍。印地语在 Claude 上的情况更为突出，token 消耗超过基准的 3.24 倍，阿拉伯语也达到 2.86 倍。

In the comparative review of 6 models, Anthropic had the highest "non-English tax", followed by Kimi; Gemini and Qwen had the lowest non-English tax. Komatsuzaki stated frankly: "Honestly, I didn't expect Claude to be this bad, and the difference is so stark. I believe enterprise customers will be very concerned about these kinds of issues." 6 家模型横评中，Anthropic 的「非英文税」最高，Kimi 次之；Gemini 和 Qwen 的非英文税最低。Komatsuzaki 直言：「坦白说，我没想到 Claude 会差到这个程度，而且差距如此悬殊。我相信企业客户会非常在意这类问题。」

Komatsuzaki pointed out that tokenization efficiency depends on the proportion of each language in the model's training data: with a large amount of English data, English vocabulary is efficiently compressed; with less non-English data, it can only be cut into smaller pieces. Komatsuzaki 指出，分词效率取决于模型训练数据中各语言的占比：英文数据量大，英文词汇被高效压缩；非英文数据较少，只能被切得更碎。

For users, increased token consumption means a direct rise in API call costs, longer waiting times before model responses, and faster exhaustion of the context window. His conclusion is: the larger the market, the more token-efficient it is. 对用户而言，token 消耗增加意味着 API 调用成本直接上升，模型响应前的等待时间更长，上下文窗口也会更快耗尽。他的结论是：谁的市场大，谁的 token 就更省。

  • Action Space : A unified hand-centric action space is proposed, mapping all body actions to the end-effector's wrist pose changes and hand contact forms, completely decoupling operational semantics from specific mechanical structures, allowing operations like grasping, rotating, and inserting to share dynamic principles across different bodies. 动作空间 ：提出统一的 hand-centric action space，将所有本体动作映射至末端执行器的手腕位姿变化与手部接触形态，彻底解耦操作语义与具体机械结构，使夹取、旋转、插入等操作能够跨本体共享动力学规律。
