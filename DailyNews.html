苹果或允许第三方 AI 接入 Siri

Today, Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman wrote that Apple plans to open up Siri access to third-party competitors in the upcoming iOS 27 system to restructure its on-device AI ecosystem. 今日，彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 发文称，苹果计划在即将发布的 iOS 27 系统中向第三方竞争对手开放 Siri 接入权限，以重构其端侧 AI 生态。

Sources revealed that Apple is developing a new system called "Extensions." This feature will allow third-party AI chatbots installed via the App Store (such as Google Gemini and Anthropic's Claude) to be deeply integrated with Siri. 知情人士透露，苹果正在开发一套名为「Extensions」的新系统。该功能将允许通过 App Store 安装的第三方 AI 聊天机器人（如 Google Gemini 和 Anthropic 的 Claude）与 Siri 深度集成。

It is reported that users can freely enable or disable various AI services in the settings panel of iOS 27, iPadOS 27, and macOS 27, and directly route requests to specified external large models when issuing commands. 据悉，用户可在 iOS 27、iPadOS 27 及 macOS 27 的设置面板中自由开启或关闭各项 AI 服务，并在下达指令时直接将请求路由至指定的外部大模型。

Currently, Apple Intelligence is powered by OpenAI's ChatGPT, and this adjustment means that ChatGPT is no longer Apple's only option. 目前，苹果的 Apple Intelligence 由 OpenAI 的 ChatGPT 提供服务，而本次调整则意味着 ChatGPT 不再是苹果的唯一选择。

林俊旸离职后首发长文：反思千问得失

In the early morning of March 4, Lin Junyang, the technical lead for Alibaba's Tongyi Qianwen (Qwen), suddenly posted on X, bidding farewell to the open-source model project he had nurtured. 3 月 4 日凌晨，阿里通义千问（Qwen）技术负责人林俊旸在 X 突然发文，向自己一手带大的开源模型项目告别。

Nearly a month after leaving Alibaba, Lin Junyang published a lengthy article on social media, pointing out that the first wave of inference models, represented by OpenAI o1 and DeepSeek-R1, validated the value of reinforcement learning in the post-training phase. However, the core of the industry's next stage will shift towards "agent-like thinking," where models continuously refine strategies by interacting with real environments and acting. 在离开阿里近一个月后，林俊旸于社交平台发表长文指出，第一波以 OpenAI o1 和 DeepSeek-R1 为代表的推理模型验证了强化学习在后训练阶段的价值，但行业下一阶段的核心将转向「智能体式思考」，即模型通过与真实环境交互并在行动中持续修正策略。

Additionally, Lin Junyang rarely reviewed the limitations of Qwen3's early attempts to integrate "thinking" and "instruction" dual modes. 此外，林俊旸罕见复盘了 Qwen3 早期尝试融合「思考」与「指令」双模式的局限性。

微信官方解读「正在输入」背后原理

Yesterday, WeChat's official public account "WeChat Pai" published an article explaining the trigger mechanism and underlying technical principles of the "对方正在输入…" (The other party is typing...) feature in WeChat chats. The article pointed out that the appearance of "The other party is typing" requires two conditions to be met simultaneously: 昨天，微信官方公众号「微信派」发文，解答了微信聊天中「对方正在输入…」功能的触发机制及其背后技术原理。文章指出，「对方正在输入」的出现需同时满足两个条件：

  • Timeliness, meaning the recipient must open the chat window within 10 seconds after the message is sent; 时效性，即收信方必须在消息发出后 10 秒内打开对话框；
  • Action, meaning the other party must actually type content into the input box after opening the chat window (even if it's just a space). 动作感，即对方在打开对话框后还需在输入框内实际输入了内容（哪怕只是一个空格）。

On a technical level, WeChat officially defines this as a "signaling handshake" mechanism. WeChat chat uses a model similar to a "walkie-talkie control tower": when a user initiates a chat, the system requests a temporary private channel; if the other party opens the chat window within 10 seconds, the server captures this action and immediately notifies the sender. 在技术层面，微信官方将其定义为「信令握手」机制。微信聊天采用类似「对讲机指挥塔」的模式，当用户发起聊天，系统会申请临时私密频道；若对方在 10 秒内打开对话框，服务器便会捕捉这一动作并即时通知发送方。

In this article, WeChat also responded to the question of "why there is no 'read receipt' feature": "Read" implies "seen, but not replied to," which can easily lead to suspicion, anxiety, and even create pressure to reply. 在这篇文章中，微信还回应了「为何没有已读功能」的疑问：「已读」意味着「看见了，但没回你」，容易引发猜忌焦虑，甚至形成催促回复的压力。

WeChat emphasized that not having a read receipt feature returns the choice of "whether to respond or not" to the user; while "The other party is typing" is a "limited transparency," conveying an online signal while also preserving space for thought and silence for both parties. 微信强调，不设已读功能，是将「回应与否」的选择权交还给用户；而「对方正在输入」则是一种「有限度的透明」，在传递在线信号的同时，也为双方保留了思考与沉默的空间。

Google 发布新型 AI 内存压缩技术，存储股集体下跌

Yesterday, Google Research released the AI memory compression algorithm "TurboQuant." 昨天，Google 研究院发布 AI 内存压缩算法「TurboQuant」。

According to the introduction, this algorithm can compress the KV Cache (Key-Value Cache) during large language model inference by at least 6 times, while achieving up to 8 times faster inference on NVIDIA H100 GPUs. The related paper will be officially published at ICLR 2026 next month. 据介绍，该算法可将大语言模型推理过程中的 KV Cache（键值缓存）压缩至少 6 倍，同时在英伟达 H100 GPU 上实现最高 8 倍的推理提速，相关论文下月将在 ICLR 2026 上正式发布。

Key metrics are as follows: 核心指标如下：

  • KV Cache memory footprint compressed by at least 6 times, with quantization precision as low as 3 bits, no retraining or fine-tuning required; KV Cache 内存占用压缩至少 6 倍，量化精度低至 3 bit，无需重新训练或微调；
  • On NVIDIA H100 GPUs, 4-bit TurboQuant inference speed is up to 8 times faster than the 32-bit unquantized version; 在英伟达 H100 GPU 上，4 bit TurboQuant 推理速度较 32 bit 未量化版本提升最高 8 倍；
  • Achieves zero precision loss in long-context benchmark tests such as "Needle in a Haystack." 在「大海捞针」等长上下文基准测试中实现零精度损失。

Following the announcement, the US stock storage chip sector experienced significant intraday sell-offs, with leading companies like Micron Technology and SanDisk closing significantly lower. The storage chip related index saw a single-day decline of over 2%. Cloudflare CEO Matthew Prince called this release Google's "DeepSeek moment." 消息发出后，美股存储芯片板块盘中遭遇明显抛售，美光科技、闪迪等头部企业股价显著收跌，存储芯片相关指数单日跌幅超 2%。Cloudflare CEO Matthew Prince 将此次发布称为 Google 的「DeepSeek 时刻」。

TurboQuant is currently still in the laboratory stage and only addresses memory issues during the inference phase, while GPU memory consumption during the training phase remains unaffected. TurboQuant 目前仍处于实验室阶段，且仅解决推理阶段的内存问题，训练阶段的显存消耗不受影响。
