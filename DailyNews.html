「豆包手机二代」努比亚 Navi X Ultra 现身 WAIC

At the WAIC event, Nubia Navi X Ultra debuted as the "Doubao Phone 2.0," featuring the Doubao Phone Assistant. This phone adopts a front and rear flat design with a straight-edge middle frame. The rear camera module has been changed from the irregular triangle of the previous generation Nubia M153 to a horizontal integrated design. WAIC 现场，努比亚 Navi X Ultra 作为搭载豆包手机助手的「豆包手机 2.0」亮相。这款手机采用前后直板设计和直边中框，后置镜组从上一代努比亚 M153 的不规则三角形改为横向贯穿式设计。

Navi X Ultra integrates the Doubao Phone Assistant deeper into the system. The assistant will gain deeper system invocation permissions and combine with Nubia's hardware and interactive design, allowing users to directly state their goals, which the AI agent will understand and complete by invoking phone functions. Navi X Ultra 将豆包手机助手更深地接入系统。助手将获得更深的系统调用权限，并与努比亚的硬件及交互设计结合，用户可以直接提出目标，由 AI 智能体理解任务并调用手机功能完成操作。

According to "LatePost," the new generation of Doubao phones no longer operates applications by reading the screen and simulating clicks, but instead accesses MCP services provided by the applications themselves. 据《晚点 LatePost》报道，新一代豆包手机不再采用读取屏幕，模拟点击的方式操作应用，转向接入应用自行提供的 MCP 服务。

The stock of the new generation of Doubao phones has increased from the previous 30,000 units to hundreds of thousands of units. "Phones cannot forever remain in the technical experimental stage; ultimately, they have to be sold," said a person close to Doubao Phone. 新一代豆包手机的备货量从此前的 3 万台提升到了数十万台，「手机不可能永远只停留在技术实验阶段，最终还是得卖出去。」一位接近豆包手机人士说。

复旦团队实现室温单电子非易失存储

The team of Zhou Peng and Liu Chunshen from Fudan University published research on "quantum flash memory" in "Science." Utilizing two-dimensional semiconductors and coplanar drain-channel-source structures, they observed single-electron non-volatile storage at room temperature (27℃): each injected electron corresponds to one bit, with a memory window reaching 0.5 volts. 复旦大学周鹏、刘春森团队在《Science》发表「量子闪存」研究，利用二维半导体与共面的漏极—沟道—源极结构，在 27℃ 室温下观测到单电子非易失存储：每注入一个电子即可对应一个比特，存储窗口达到 0.5 伏特。

The team stated that the device has advanced the 55 mV, approximately 5-second retention of 1997 silicon-based single-electron memory to stable storage at room temperature, and has completed prototype verification compatible with CMOS silicon processes. The next step will be to promote large-scale chip integration, with plans to launch products with AI clients within 1 to 3 years. 团队称，该器件把 1997 年硅基单电子存储的 55 mV、约 5 秒保持推进到室温稳定保存，并已完成与 CMOS 硅工艺兼容的原型验证。下一步将推进大规模芯片集成，计划在 1 至 3 年内对接 AI 客户实现产品落地。

油电平权再进一步，新能源车可靠性试验里程拟统一提高到 3 万公里

According to Yicai, the National Automotive Standardization Technical Committee recently solicited opinions on amendments to three recommended national standards, proposing to uniformly increase the total reliability test mileage for pure electric, hybrid, and fuel cell vehicles to no less than 30,000 kilometers, consistent with the requirements for traditional fuel vehicles. 据第一财经报道，全国汽车标准化技术委员会近日就三项推荐性国家标准修改单征求意见，拟把纯电动、混合动力和燃料电池汽车的可靠性行驶试验总里程统一提高到不低于 3 万公里，与传统燃油车的要求一致。

The current standard for pure electric vehicles was formulated in 2005, with reliability test mileage executed at 50% of the corresponding fuel vehicle standard; referring to this year's access requirements for road motor vehicle manufacturers, it is usually 15,000 kilometers. After this revision, the test mileage for pure electric vehicles will double, with the cumulative DC charging mileage accounting for no less than 90%, i.e., at least 27,000 kilometers. 现行纯电动车标准制定于 2005 年，可靠性试验里程按对应燃油车标准的 50% 执行；参照今年道路机动车辆生产企业准入要求，通常为 1.5 万公里。本次修改后，纯电动车试验里程将翻倍，其中直流充电累计里程占比不得低于 90%，即至少 2.7 万公里。

In addition to completing the 30,000 km total test, plug-in hybrid electric vehicles also need to complete a separate pure electric reliability test of at least 10,000 km; fuel cell vehicles must complete the full 30,000 km in hybrid drive mode. The three amendments also update the safety requirements for hybrid vehicles to GB 18384 "Safety Requirements for Electric Vehicles," which came into effect on July 1. 插电式混合动力汽车除完成 3 万公里总试验外，还需单独完成至少 1 万公里纯电可靠性试验；燃料电池汽车则要在混合动力驱动模式下完成全部 3 万公里。三项修改还把混动车安全要求更新为 7 月 1 日实施的 GB 18384《电动汽车安全要求》。

月之暗面联合创始人周昕宇：Kimi 暂不开发视频生成模型

Zhou Xinyu, co-founder and head of the algorithm team at Moonshot AI, posted on X that Kimi currently has no plans to develop video generation models. The team is currently focusing its resources on improving the upper limit of the model's intelligence, rather than following the competitive pace of video generation products. 月之暗面联合创始人、算法团队负责人周昕宇在 X 发文表示，Kimi 目前没有开发视频生成模型的计划。团队现阶段把资源集中在提升模型的智能上限，而不是跟随视频生成产品的竞争节奏。

Zhou Xinyu believes that while video generation has clear uses, from a theoretical and practical perspective, it does not significantly help improve model intelligence. He separates capability enhancement from functional expansion: generating videos can add an output form, but it is not the main path, in the team's current judgment, to continue advancing reasoning and task capabilities. 周昕宇认为，视频生成虽然有明确用途，但从理论和实际效果看，对提升模型智能的帮助都不大。他把能力提升与功能扩展分开：生成视频可以增加一种输出形态，却不是团队当前判断中推动推理和任务能力继续上升的主要路径。

Kimi K3's public capabilities also continue this choice, focusing on software engineering, knowledge work, deep reasoning, and image understanding. The multimodal approach currently leans more towards enabling the model to understand images and use them for reasoning, rather than directly generating video content. Kimi K3 的公开能力也延续这一选择，重点放在软件工程、知识工作、深度推理和图像理解。多模态路线目前更偏向让模型理解图像并用于推理，而非直接生成视频内容。

💡 阶跃星辰董事长印奇：智能体正从数字屏幕走向物理世界

On July 17, the 2026 World Artificial Intelligence Conference (WAIC 2026) opened. Yin Qi, Chairman of Jiyue Xingchen and Chairman of Qianli Technology, delivered a speech at the main forum of the conference, stating that with the improvement of model capabilities and the accelerated integration of intelligent agents with terminal products, AI is moving from digital screens to the physical world, and intelligent agents will also transform from chat tools into productivity units capable of perceiving, deciding, and executing tasks. 7 月 17 日，2026 世界人工智能大会（WAIC 2026）开幕。阶跃星辰董事长、千里科技董事长印奇在大会主论坛发表演讲称，随着模型能力提升和智能体与终端产品加速结合，AI 正从数字屏幕走向物理世界，智能体也将从聊天工具转变为能够感知、决策和执行任务的生产力单元。

Yin Qi stated that by 2026, models have gradually moved from being able to perform tasks for only a few seconds to working independently for dozens of hours, with programming becoming an important indicator for measuring changes in AI capabilities. He believes that in the future, professions such as engineers, designers, and researchers may all have exclusive intelligent agents, thereby enhancing individuals' ability to complete complex tasks. 印奇表示，2026 年模型已从只能执行数秒任务，逐步迈向独立工作数十小时，编程则成为衡量 AI 能力变化的重要指标。他认为，未来工程师、设计师和研究员等职业都可能拥有专属智能体，从而提升个人完成复杂工作的能力。

In his view, the development of intelligent agents will bring about three aspects of change: new systems, new carriers, and new networks. Agentic OS will connect models, data, tools, interfaces, and devices; computers, mobile phones, cars, and robots may become carriers for the same intelligent agent in different scenarios, enabling cross-terminal collaboration; and A2A networks will connect humans with intelligent agents and support identity authentication, capability collaboration, and transactions between intelligent agents. 在他看来，智能体的发展将带来新系统、新载体和新网络三方面变化。Agentic OS 将连接模型、数据、工具、接口和设备；电脑、手机、汽车和机器人则可能成为同一智能体在不同场景下的载体，实现跨终端协作；A2A 网络将连接人与智能体，并支持智能体之间的身份认证、能力协作和交易。

Yin Qi also pointed out that after intelligent agents enter the real world, issues such as trusted identity, access control, responsibility attribution, and behavior traceability need to be addressed. The future focus is not on machines replacing humans, but on humans and intelligence jointly expanding capabilities. 印奇同时指出，智能体进入现实世界后，还需要解决身份可信、权限控制、责任归属和行为追溯等问题。未来的重点不是机器替代人，而是人与智能共同完成能力扩展。
