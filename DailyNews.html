GPT-5.6 正式亮相：三级命名体系、分层定价，但多数用户暂时无缘使用

Today, OpenAI officially released the GPT-5.6 series models, including three models: the flagship model Sol, the balanced Terra for daily workflows, and the Luna, which focuses on speed and low cost. OpenAI 今天正式发布 GPT-5.6 系列模型，包含三款型号：旗舰模型 Sol、面向日常工作流的平衡型 Terra，以及主打速度与低成本的 Luna。

The release adopts a 'limited preview' format. According to U.S. government requirements, initial access is only open to a small number of government-approved trusted partners, and individual users currently have no application channels. Bloomberg reported that the first batch of partners numbers around 20, and Amazon Bedrock platform may be one of the entry points. 发布采取「有限预览」形式，根据美国政府要求，首批访问权限仅开放给一小部分经政府批准的可信合作伙伴，个人用户暂无申请渠道。彭博社报道称，首批合作伙伴约为 20 家，亚马逊 Bedrock 平台可能是入口之一。

In terms of capability, OpenAI positions Sol as its strongest model to date, focusing on strengthening agent capabilities in three areas: programming, bioinformatics, and cybersecurity. 在能力层面，OpenAI 将 Sol 定位为迄今最强模型，重点强化了编程、生物信息学与网络安全三个方向的智能体能力。

Sol scored 88.8% on Terminal-Bench 2.1, reaching 91.9% in Ultra mode, which is higher than GPT-5.5's 88.0% and also ahead of Claude Mythos 5's 84.3%. Sol 在 Terminal-Bench 2.1 上得分 88.8%，Ultra 模式下达到 91.9%，高于 GPT-5.5 的 88.0%，也领先于 Claude Mythos 5 的 84.3%。

OpenAI states that Sol's performance in the ExploitBench cybersecurity evaluation is close to Claude Mythos Preview, but it used only about one-third of the output tokens. OpenAI 称，Sol 在 ExploitBench 网络安全评测中的表现接近 Claude Mythos Preview，但仅使用了约三分之一的输出 token。

In terms of pricing, Sol is billed at per million tokens, with input at $5 and output at $30; Terra is $2.5 for input and $15 for output; Luna is $1 for input and $6 for output. 定价方面，Sol 按每百万 token 计费，输入 5 美元、输出 30 美元；Terra 为输入 2.5 美元、输出 15 美元；Luna 为输入 1 美元、输出 6 美元。

曝苹果跳过 M6 Pro/Max，M7 系列直接主打 AI 性能

According to Bloomberg, Apple's Mac chips might skip the high-end versions of the M6 series and move directly to the AI-centric M7 generation. 据彭博社报道，苹果 Mac 芯片可能跳过 M6 系列的高端版本，直接推进以 AI 为核心的 M7 世代。

The base M6 is expected to launch as early as this year, with approximately 200 GB/s memory bandwidth, an updated neural engine, and up to 12 graphics cores. However, Apple will not release M6 Pro or M6 Max, marking a first in Apple chip history. 基础版 M6 最快将于今年推出，内存带宽约 200 GB/s，并配备更新的神经网络引擎和最多 12 个图形核心。但苹果将不会推出 M6 Pro 或 M6 Max，这是苹果芯片历史上的首次。

The M7 Pro and M7 Max are expected to be released as early as the end of next year, with the M7 Ultra anticipated in 2028. The base M7 will have approximately 240 GB/s memory bandwidth, and the entire M7 series is designed with a core goal of significantly enhancing local AI processing capabilities. M7 Pro 和 M7 Max 最快于明年底发布，M7 Ultra 则预计在 2028 年推出，基础版 M7 的内存带宽将达约 240 GB/s，整个 M7 系列以大幅提升本地 AI 处理能力为核心设计目标。

Additionally, Apple still plans to release the M5 Ultra this year, featuring approximately 36 CPU cores, 80 GPU cores, and support for up to 768 GB of memory. However, due to supply shortages, the release date for new Mac Studio models equipped with this chip remains uncertain. 此外，苹果仍计划今年发布 M5 Ultra，约 36 个 CPU 核心、80 个 GPU 核心，支持最高 768 GB 内存，但受供应短缺影响，搭载该芯片的新款 Mac Studio 发布时间存在变数。

删除好友后朋友圈互动还在吗？微信公布显示逻辑

WeChat Pai yesterday published an article explaining the visibility rules for Moments groups and after deleting friends. The scope of old Moments visible to new friends depends on whether the new friend was added to the corresponding group, and whether that Moment was set to "visible to this group" or "not visible to this group" at the time. 微信派昨日发布文章，集中解释朋友圈分组和好友删除后的可见规则。新好友看到旧朋友圈的范围，取决于新好友是否被加入对应分组，以及该条朋友圈当时设置的是「该分组可见」还是「该分组不可见」。

If a user sets a group as invisible, and a newly added friend is not in that group, they will still be able to see that Moment; if a group is set as visible, and a newly added friend is not in that group, they will not be able to see that Moment. 如果用户设置某个分组不可见，新加好友不在该分组里，就仍能看到这条朋友圈；如果设置某个分组可见，新加好友不在该分组里，就看不到这条朋友圈。

WeChat Pai summarized this rule as: new friends added to which group will follow the Moments rules for group visibility or invisibility. 微信派把这条规则概括为：新加好友在哪个分组，就按分组可见或不可见的朋友圈规则来。

The article also explained the traces of interaction after deleting a friend. If it's a unilateral deletion, interactions such as comments and likes in past Moments will still be retained from both perspectives; if both parties delete each other, relevant interactions will disappear from the Moments details visible to both. 文章还解释了删除好友后的互动痕迹。如果只是单方删除，对过去朋友圈里的评论、点赞等互动，双方视角下仍会保留；如果双方互删，相关互动会从双方可见的朋友圈详情里消失。

特斯拉就 FSD 致死行人诉讼达成和解

According to Bloomberg, Tesla has reached a settlement with the family of a pedestrian in the U.S. regarding a fatal accident lawsuit, with the terms of the settlement not disclosed by either party. 据彭博社报道，特斯拉已与美国一名行人家属就致死事故诉讼达成和解，双方没有公开和解条款。

The accident occurred on November 28, 2023, on a highway between Flagstaff and Phoenix, Arizona, USA; 71-year-old Johna Story was struck and killed by a Model Y in FSD mode while assisting with traffic caused by a previous accident. 事故发生在 2023 年 11 月 28 日，地点位于美国亚利桑那州 Flagstaff 与 Phoenix 之间的高速公路；71 岁的 Johna Story 下车协助疏导此前事故造成的交通时，被一辆处于 FSD 模式的 Model Y 撞击身亡。

Electrek stated that this is the first known pedestrian fatality related to Tesla FSD. The U.S. National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) initiated a preliminary assessment in October 2024, which was subsequently upgraded to an engineering analysis in March 2026, covering approximately 3.2 million vehicles. Electrek 称，这是首个已知与 Tesla FSD 相关的行人死亡事故。美国国家公路交通安全管理局（NHTSA）在 2024 年 10 月开启初步评估，随后在 2026 年 3 月升级为工程分析，覆盖约 320 万辆车。
