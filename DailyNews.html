《时代》杂志公布年度最佳发明公布：DeepSeek R1、AirPods Pro 3 上榜

Yesterday, Time magazine officially released its 2025 "Best Inventions" list, featuring 300 innovative achievements from around the world, covering various fields such as artificial intelligence, consumer electronics, healthcare, and green energy. 昨天，《时代》杂志正式发布 2025 年度「最佳发明」榜单，共收录 300 项来自全球的创新成果，涵盖人工智能、消费电子、医疗健康、绿色能源等多个领域。

In the artificial intelligence sector, the DeepSeek R1 large model developed by a Chinese team was successfully selected. With its core advantages of low cost and high efficiency, the model is considered a strong challenge to the existing landscape of large language models, demonstrating China's new breakthroughs in global AI competition. 在人工智能板块，中国团队研发的 DeepSeek R1 大模型成功入选。该模型以低成本、高效率为核心优势，被认为是对现有大型语言模型格局的有力挑战，展现了中国在全球 AI 竞争中的新突破。

In the consumer electronics category, Apple's newly launched AirPods Pro 3 was also selected for its multiple groundbreaking features. 在消费电子类别中，苹果最新推出的 AirPods Pro 3 也凭借多项突破性功能入选。

Building on upgraded noise cancellation, AirPods Pro 3 for the first time incorporates an optical heart rate sensor, capable of real-time monitoring of user health and, when necessary, functioning as a hearing aid. AirPods Pro 3 在降噪效果升级的基础上，首次加入光学心率传感器，可实时监测用户健康状况、在必要时还可充当助听器。

This time, Time magazine highlighted the "real-time translation" feature launched with AirPods Pro 3. It is reported that this feature supports instant interpretation between multiple languages, including English, French, German, Spanish, and Portuguese, and will be expanded to include Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Italian by the end of the year. 本次《时代》杂志重点提及了随 AirPods Pro 3 一同推出的「实时翻译」功能。据悉，该功能支持英语、法语、德语、西班牙语和葡萄牙语等多种语言之间的即时传译，年底还将扩展至中文、日语、韩语和意大利语。

It is worth noting that OpenAI had "zero entries" on this list, with no work making it into the annual best inventions. 值得注意的是，本次榜单 OpenAI「零入选」，没有一项工作进入年度最佳发明。

Time's editors commented that these selected products "not only transformed user experience but also pushed the boundaries of industry expansion." 《时代》编辑评价称，这些入选产品 「不仅改变了用户体验，也推动了行业边界的拓展」 。

In addition to DeepSeek R1 and AirPods Pro 3, several Huawei products were also selected, including Pura 80 Ultra and Watch GT 6 Pro; Claude Sonnet 4 and Cursor are also on the list, with other products including cutting-edge tech like Figure 03, Unitree R1, and Meta Ray-Ban Display. 除 DeepSeek R1 与 AirPods Pro 3 外，华为也有多款产品入选，包括 Pura 80 Ultra、Watch GT 6 Pro；Claude Sonnet 4、Cursor 也同样在列，其他产品还包括 Figure 03、宇树 Unitree R1、Meta Ray-Ban Display 等前沿科技产品。

According to Business Insider, at the recent DevDay conference, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman announced the latest data: ChatGPT's weekly active users have reached 800 million. 据 Business Insider 报道，在近期举行的 DevDay 大会上，OpenAI CEO Sam Altman 公布了最新数据： ChatGPT 的周活跃用户已达到 8 亿人次。

This figure has not only doubled in the past four months but also surpassed the combined total of its five major competitors, including Meta AI, Gemini, xAI, Claude, and Perplexity. 这一数字不仅在过去四个月内实现翻倍增长，更是超过了包括 Meta AI、Gemini、xAI、Claude 和 Perplexity 在内的五大竞争对手的总和。

According to statistics, if calculated by weekly active users, ChatGPT's monthly active user base is approximately 3.2 billion, while the combined total of the other five major competitors is only 1.575 billion. Even if the latter were doubled, it would still be slightly less than ChatGPT's overall scale. 根据统计，若以周活跃用户数推算，ChatGPT 的月活跃用户规模约为 32 亿，而其余五家主要对手合计仅为 15.75 亿。即便将后者翻倍，仍略低于 ChatGPT 的整体规模。

Altman stated at the conference: "Thanks to all users, AI has transformed from a tool people tried and explored into a core platform for daily building and use." Altman 在会上表示：「感谢所有用户，AI 已经从人们尝试和探索的工具，变成了日常构建和使用的核心平台。」

The timeline shows that ChatGPT first surpassed 100 million weekly active users in November 2023, reached 400 million by February 2025, surged to 700 million in August this year, and has now set a new record. 时间线显示，ChatGPT 在 2023 年 11 月首次突破 1 亿周活跃用户，至 2025 年 2 月已达到 4 亿，而在今年 8 月则冲至 7 亿，如今再度刷新纪录。

索尼携手 AMD 公布三大图形技术，或率先应用于次世代主机

According to EUROGAMER, Sony and AMD recently jointly announced three key graphics technologies, which are expected to be applied in the next-generation PlayStation 6 and future AMD graphics card products. 据 EUROGAMER 报道，索尼与 AMD 日前联合公布三项关键图形技术，预计将应用于下一代 PlayStation 6 以及未来的 AMD 显卡产品。

These three technologies are "Neural Arrays," "Radiance Cores," and "Universal Compression," aiming to enhance ray tracing efficiency, optimize image upscaling and denoising, and significantly improve memory bandwidth utilization: 这三项技术分别为「Neural Arrays」「Radiance Cores」和「Universal Compression」，旨在提升光线追踪效率、优化图像放大与降噪处理，并显著改善显存带宽利用率：

  • Neural Arrays: By connecting multiple compute units in a "smart and efficient" manner to form a structure similar to a "single AI engine," it supports larger-scale machine learning models and accelerates image upscaling and denoising algorithms. Neural Arrays：通过将多个计算单元以「智能高效」的方式连接，形成类似「单一 AI 引擎」的结构，从而支持更大规模的机器学习模型，加速图像放大与降噪算法。
  • Radiance Cores: Dedicated cores are added inside the GPU (theoretically similar to Nvidia's RT Cores) for unified processing of ray and path tracing tasks. Radiance 核心：在 GPU 内部加入专用核心（理论上类似于英伟达的 RT 核心），用于统一处理光线与路径追踪任务。
  • Universal Compression: Compresses all data transmitted to video memory, not just texture data, thereby increasing effective bandwidth, reducing power consumption, and further enhancing the performance of the first two technologies. Universal Compression：对所有传输至显存的数据进行压缩，而不仅限于纹理数据，从而提升有效带宽、降低功耗，并进一步增强前两项技术的表现。

Mark Cerny, Sony PS5 system architect, and Jack Huynh, AMD Senior Vice President, stated that these technologies are currently still in the simulation phase, but will be extended to "all gaming platforms" in the future, including not only home consoles but also potentially PC graphics cards and handheld devices. 索尼 PS5 系统架构师 Mark Cerny 与 AMD 高级副总裁 Jack Huynh 表示，这些技术目前仍处于模拟阶段，但未来将推广至「所有游戏平台」，不仅包括家用主机，也可能覆盖 PC 显卡与掌机设备。
