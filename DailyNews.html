小米机器人进汽车工厂「实习」

According to official Xiaomi news, yesterday, the Xiaomi humanoid robot completed a 3-hour autonomous operation test at the Beijing automobile factory, achieving a success rate of 90.2% and meeting the production line's fastest cycle time requirement of 76 seconds. 据小米官方消息，昨天，小米人形机器人在北京汽车工厂完成了为期 3 小时的自主作业测试，成功率达到 90.2%，并满足最快 76 秒的产线生产节拍要求。

During the test, the robot was required to perform precise gripping and installation at the self-tapping nut loading station. The Xiaomi team utilized an end-to-end data-driven control method, combining the Vision-Language-Action large model Xiaomi-Robotics-0 with a reinforcement learning framework, enabling the robot to maintain stability and efficiency under complex working conditions. Key technical highlights include: 在测试中，机器人需在自攻螺母上件工站完成精准抓取与安装。小米团队通过端到端数据驱动的控制方法，结合 Vision-Language-Action 大模型 Xiaomi-Robotics-0 与强化学习框架，使机器人能够在复杂工况下保持稳定与高效。技术亮点包括：

  • Multimodal Perception : Integrates visual, tactile, and joint proprioceptive information to reduce the probability of state misjudgment; 多模态感知 ：融合视觉、触觉与关节本体信息，降低状态误判概率；
  • Whole-Body Motion Control : Employs a hybrid architecture of optimized control and reinforcement learning, achieving millisecond-level solutions and zero-shot transfer, ensuring balance under extreme disturbances; 全身运动控制 ：采用优化控制与强化学习混合架构，实现毫秒级求解与零样本迁移，确保在极端干扰下保持平衡；
  • Tactile Information Fusion : Introduces tactile feedback in dense contact scenarios to enhance stability and robustness. 触觉信息融合 ：在密集接触场景中引入触觉反馈，提升稳定性与鲁棒性。

During the pilot process, the robot successfully completed self-tapping nut installation, but typical failure cases still existed due to insufficient alignment precision, leading to jamming or loose fitting. Xiaomi stated that to achieve broader industrial deployment, core bottlenecks such as production cycle time and qualification rate still need to be addressed. 在试点过程中，机器人成功完成了自攻螺母安装，但仍存在因对位精度不足导致卡滞或贴合不紧密的典型失效案例。小米表示，要实现更大范围的产业化部署，仍需解决生产节拍与合格率的核心瓶颈。

Currently, Xiaomi has conducted verification at workstations such as material box handling and front emblem installation. In the future, it will continue to advance the application of robots in automobile manufacturing and make relevant technical resources and experimental videos available for industry reference. 目前，小米已在料箱搬运、前徽标安装等工站开展验证，未来将继续推进机器人在汽车制造中的应用，并开放相关技术资源与实验视频供业界参考。

According to media reports, Xiaomi Group President Lu Weibing stated at MWC 2026 that he believes there is a high probability that robots will be able to enter Xiaomi's production lines on a large scale within the next five years. 另据媒体报道，小米集团总裁卢伟冰在 MWC 2026 现场表示，相信在未来的五年之内，机器人能够大规模地进入小米产线工作，具有很大的可能性。

小鹏发布第二代 VLA：用时比导航预估还短

Xpeng CEO He Xiaopeng stated at the press conference that the second-generation VLA achieves end-to-end direct decision-making by eliminating the traditional "vision → language translation → action" intermediate steps, resulting in an 80% reduction in system response latency and a 12-fold increase in inference efficiency. 小鹏 CEO 何小鹏在发布会上表示，第二代 VLA 通过去除传统「视觉→语言转译→动作」的中间环节，实现端到端直接决策，系统响应延迟下降 80%，推理效率提升 12 倍。

Test videos show that the system can identify unexpected situations such as pedestrians in dark clothing, small animals, and potholes at night through pure vision, and slow down or avoid them in advance; on unlit muddy roads, the vehicle can autonomously identify the road surface and navigate around obstacles, with zero human intervention throughout. 实测视频显示，系统在夜间可通过纯视觉识别黑衣行人、小动物、坑洼等突发情况，并提前减速或避让；在无灯泥路中，车辆可自主识别路面并绕行，全程零接管。

面向下一代智能驾驶芯片，理想汽车发布「软硬协同设计定律」

According to Li Auto's official statement, Li Auto and the National Institute of Decision Intelligence Technology recently jointly announced the "Software-Hardware Collaborative Design Law" for edge-side large models, proposing a software-hardware integrated design method for large models in vehicle and edge computing scenarios, laying a systematic theoretical foundation for the next generation of self-developed intelligent driving chips. 据理想汽车官方，理想汽车与国创决策智能技术研究所近日联合公布端侧大模型「软硬协同设计定律」，提出面向车载与边缘计算场景的大模型软硬一体化设计方法，为下一代自研智能驾驶芯片奠定系统性理论基础。

Li Auto pointed out that software-hardware co-design has become a focal point of industry competition. The key is no longer who uses more or more expensive chips, but rather who can achieve more efficient utilization of computing power with limited resources. 理想汽车指出，软硬协同已成为行业竞争焦点，关键不再是谁使用更多、更贵的芯片，而是谁能在有限资源下实现更高效的算力利用。

The "Software-Hardware Collaborative Design Law" for the first time establishes a quantifiable and predictable mathematical framework for software-hardware co-design. By jointly modeling model accuracy and inference latency, it achieves system-level optimized matching of algorithm architecture and hardware resources, breaking through the previous fragmented modes of "chip first, then algorithm" or "algorithm first, then find hardware." 「软硬协同设计定律」首次建立可量化、可预测的软硬协同数学框架，通过对模型精度与推理延迟的联合建模，实现算法架构与硬件资源的系统级优化匹配，突破过去「先芯片后算法」或「先算法再找硬件」的割裂模式。

Based on this research, Li Auto is advancing the architectural definition of its next-generation self-developed intelligent driving chip. Starting from algorithmic requirements, it will natively support sparse computing, dynamic resource scheduling, and mixed-precision inference, creating an "algorithm-native chip" for vehicle VLA systems to achieve higher energy efficiency and stronger intelligent performance. 基于该研究，理想汽车正在推进下一代自研智能驾驶芯片的架构定义，将以算法需求为起点，原生支持稀疏计算、动态资源调度与混合精度推理，打造面向车载 VLA 系统的「算法原生芯片」，以实现更高能效比与更强智能表现。
