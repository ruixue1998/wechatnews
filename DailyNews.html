苹果智能眼镜玩法曝光：既能连 Mac，又能连 iPhone

According to the latest report from Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman, Apple has suspended the development of a lightweight version of VisionPro (VisionAir) and is fully shifting its R&D focus to smart glasses. 据彭博记者 Mark Gurman 最新报道，苹果已暂停开发轻量版 VisionPro（VisionAir），将研发重心全面转向智能眼镜。

Gurman stated that this will be a key shift for Apple in its next-generation computing platform. Due to the lukewarm reception of VisionPro, Apple believes that closed headsets are difficult to become mainstream devices. Gurman 表示，这将是苹果在下一代计算平台上的关键转向。由于 visionPro 遇冷，苹果认为封闭式头显难以成为大众化设备。

Although VisionPro boasts leading technology, its high price of $3499, heavy weight, and sparse application ecosystem make it difficult to achieve iPhone-level market scale. In contrast, lightweight, everyday wearable smart glasses align more with Apple's "mainstream product" philosophy. Here is the information we currently have: 尽管 VisionPro 技术领先，但售价高达 3499 美元、重量偏重、应用生态稀薄，使其难以形成 iPhone 级的市场规模。相比之下，轻量、日常可佩戴的智能眼镜更符合苹果「主流化产品」哲学，这是目前我们已掌握的信息：

  • Smart glasses are expected to run a lightweight version of visionOS and can work in conjunction with an iPhone or Mac. 智能眼镜预计将运行 visionOS 的轻量版本，可与 iPhone 或 Mac 协同工作。
  • When the glasses are connected to an iPhone, they will run a lightweight version of the system, while when connected to a Mac, they can run the full version of visionOS. 当眼镜接入 iPhone 时将会运行轻量版的系统，而接入 Mac 时则可以运行完整版的 visionOS。
  • Based on current information, it is speculated that Apple is still advancing a multi-tiered Vision product line internally: low-end for screenless smart glasses, mid-range glasses equipped with displays, and high-end continuing to retain VisionPro. 根据目前的消息推测，苹果内部仍在推进多层次 Vision 产品线：低端为无屏智能眼镜，中端眼镜配备显示屏，高端则继续保留 VisionPro。
  • In fact, Apple has already reserved many technology patents for smart glasses, including patents for technology that links content between glasses and iPhones. 实际上，苹果已经为智能眼镜储备了不少技术专利，其中就包括眼镜和 iPhone 进行内容联动的技术专利。

Gurman also mentioned Apple's smart wearable product line in the article. Gurman 还在文中提到了苹果的智能穿戴产品线。

He pointed out that although Apple has slowed down the upgrade pace of the Apple Watch in recent years, it has achieved a double harvest of "profit" and "technological accumulation" on this product line. 其指出，苹果近年来虽然放缓了 Apple Watch 的升级步伐，但是其已经在该产品线上「利润」「技术积累」双丰收。

It's worth noting that Gurman predicts that although the camera-equipped Apple Watch plan has been postponed, Apple will transform the Apple Watch into a more powerful AI device in the future. 值得一提的是，Gurman 预测，尽管带摄像头版 Apple Watch 计划推迟， 但苹果会在未来把 Apple Watch 打造成一个更加强大的 AI 设备。

As for AirPods, Gurman stated that Apple is already working on a new version of its next-generation in-ear headphones (speculated to be new AirPods Pro). He revealed that Apple's chip team is developing the H3 chip, aiming to reduce latency and improve sound quality; meanwhile, AirPods 5 is also in development. 而 AirPods 方面， Gurman 表示苹果已经在着手研发下一代入耳式耳机的新版本（推测为新 AirPods Pro）。 其透露，苹果的芯片团队正在开发 H3 芯片，旨在降低延迟和提升音质；同时，AirPods 5 也已经在研发的路上。

Gurman said that Apple might not bring the heart rate sensor to AirPods 5, but will add more health features to AirPods, such as temperature detection, for a period of time. Additionally, in the future, AirPods, like the Apple Watch, are being considered by Apple to have cameras and transform into AI devices. 💡 AI 教父：AI 在未来 20 年内将远比人类聪明

Recently, Geoffrey Hinton, known as the "Godfather of AI," explained the working principles of neural networks and large language models in an accessible way on the podcast "The Weekly Show," and issued a severe warning about their potential social, economic, and existential risks. 日前，被誉为「AI 教父」的 Geoffrey Hinton 在播客《The Weekly Show》中，深入浅出地解释了神经网络和大型语言模型的工作原理，并对其潜在的社会、经济及生存风险发出了严峻警告。

In the program, Hinton compared AI's neural networks to the human brain, with its basic units being interconnected neurons. 节目中， Hinton 将 AI 的神经网络比作人脑，其基本单元是互相连接的神经元。

Hinton explained that AI does not follow rigid rules written by humans, but rather learns how to identify "birds" by analyzing vast amounts of data (for example, after seeing countless bird images). The core breakthrough is the "backpropagation" learning algorithm—allowing AI to self-correct and continuously optimize, ultimately becoming extremely "smart." Hinton 解释表示，AI 并非遵循人类编写的死板规则，而是通过分析海量数据（例如，看过无数鸟的图片后）来自行学习如何识别「鸟」。其核心突破则是「反向传播」这一学习算法——让 AI 能自我纠错并不断优化，最终变得极其「聪明」。

When asked when he began to worry about his creation, Hinton admitted that it wasn't until early 2023 that he truly realized the speed of AI's evolution far exceeded imagination. 而当被问及何时开始对自己的创造感到忧虑时，Hinton 坦言，直到 2023 年初，他才真正意识到 AI 的进化速度远超想象。

He believes that digital intelligence is a "better form of computation" than humans because thousands of AI copies can simultaneously learn different data, then instantly share knowledge, achieving exponential growth. Regarding future risks, Hinton continued with his predictions: 他认为数字智能是一种比人类「更好的计算形式」，因为成千上万个 AI 副本可以同时学习不同数据，然后瞬间共享知识，实现指数级成长。针对未来的风险，Hinton 则继续了自己的预测：

  • Short-term: misused by bad actors , for example, using AI to create disinformation to interfere with elections, or design dangerous weapons; 短期： 被坏人滥用 ，例如利用 AI 制造虚假信息干预选举，或设计危险武器；
  • Mid-term: disruption of the job market. AI will soon replace a large amount of repetitive mental labor , potentially causing severe social unrest and mass unemployment in a very short period; 中期：颠覆就业市场。 AI 将很快取代大量的重复性脑力劳动 ，可能在极短时间内造成严重的社会动荡和大规模失业；
