英伟达豪掷 50 亿美元押注英特尔

Reuters reported yesterday that Nvidia announced it would spend $5 billion to acquire approximately 4% of Intel's shares and reach a deep cooperation agreement covering PC and data center chips. This move not only makes Nvidia a significant shareholder in Intel but also marks a strategic alliance between the two American chip giants in the AI era. 路透社昨日报道，英伟达宣布将斥资 50 亿美元 收购英特尔约 4% 的股份，并与其达成一项涵盖 PC 与数据中心芯片的深度合作协议。此举不仅使英伟达成为英特尔的重要股东，也标志着两家美国芯片巨头在 AI 时代的战略联手。

Nvidia announced in an article on its official WeChat public account early this morning that it would purchase Intel common stock at a price of $23.28 per share, which is slightly lower than the previous trading day's closing price of $24.90, but higher than the price at which the U.S. government previously acquired shares at $20.47 per share. 英伟达今日凌晨在其官方微信公众号发文表示，将以每股 23.28 美元的价格购买英特尔普通股，该价格略低于前一交易日收盘价 24.90 美元，但高于美国政府此前以每股 20.47 美元收购的价格。

Nvidia stated in the article, "Both parties achieve seamless architectural interconnection through NVIDIA NVLink technology — integrating NVIDIA's strengths in AI and accelerated computing with Intel's advanced CPU technology and x86 ecosystem, to provide cutting-edge solutions for customers." 英伟达在文中称，「双方通过 NVIDIA NVLink 技术实现架构无缝互连 —— 融合 NVIDIA 在 AI 与加速计算领域的优势，以及英特尔先进的 CPU 技术与 x86 生态，为客户提供前沿解决方案。」

This cooperation does not involve Intel's foundry business, but both parties will jointly develop AI chips with high-speed interconnect technology. This technology can achieve faster data transfer between GPUs and CPUs, which is expected to pose a direct challenge to AMD and Broadcom in the AI server market. Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang stated: "This historic collaboration closely integrates Nvidia's AI and accelerated computing platform with Intel's CPU and x86 ecosystem, laying the foundation for next-generation computing." 此次合作不涉及英特尔的代工业务，但双方将联合开发具备高速互联技术的 AI 芯片。该技术可实现 GPU 与 CPU 间更快的数据传输，有望在 AI 服务器市场中对 AMD 与博通构成直接挑战。英伟达 CEO 黄仁勋表示：「这项历史性的合作将英伟达的 AI 与加速计算平台与英特尔的 CPU 及 x86 生态系统紧密结合，为下一代计算奠定基础。」

Affected by this news, Nvidia's stock price rose by over 3%, while Intel surged 22.8%, marking its largest single-day gain since October 1987. AMD and TSMC, on the other hand, fell by nearly 4% and 2% respectively. Industry insiders believe that Nvidia's endorsement will significantly boost Intel's market confidence and may also change the global chip manufacturing landscape. 受此消息影响，英伟达股价上涨逾 3%， 英特尔大涨 22.8%，创 1987 年 10 月以来最大单日涨幅。 而 AMD 与台积电则分别下跌近 4% 与 2%。业内人士认为，英伟达的背书将显著提升英特尔的市场信心，也可能改变全球芯片制造格局。

Intel CFO David Zinsner previously stated that the company currently has a healthy cash position and will gradually expand its factories based on market demand in the future. New CEO Chen Liwu has pledged to streamline operations and focus on efficient capacity building. 英特尔 CFO David Zinsner 此前曾表示，公司目前现金状况良好，未来将根据市场需求逐步扩建工厂。新任 CEO 陈立武则承诺将精简运营，并专注于高效产能建设。

Both parties have not yet announced the launch date for their first joint products, but have confirmed that they will release "multiple generations of products" and provide chips to each other to build new-generation platforms. It is widely believed in the industry that this cooperation will be a key turning point in the AI chip market. 双方尚未公布首批联合产品的上市时间，但已确认将推出「多代产品」，并互相提供芯片以打造新一代平台。业内普遍认为，这一合作将成为 AI 芯片市场的关键转折点。

Additionally, according to 36Kr Auto, Nvidia has internally launched a Robotaxi project, planning to use end-to-end neural networks combined with reinforcement learning technology to build a sample fleet with L4-level autonomous driving capabilities. The project is led by Nvidia Senior Director Ruchi Bhargava and is expected to be launched in cities across the United States in the future, with an initial investment of up to $3 billion . 此外，据 36 氪汽车报道，英伟达已在内部启动 Robotaxi 项目，计划采用端到端神经网络结合强化学习技术，打造具备 L4 级 自动驾驶能力的样本车队。该项目由英伟达高级总监 Ruchi Bhargava 负责，预计未来将在美国落地开城， 初期投入高达 30 亿美元 。

携程被约谈

Yesterday, People's Daily reported that, according to a notice from the "Zhengzhou Market Supervision" WeChat official account, the Zhengzhou Market Supervision Bureau legally conducted an administrative interview with the operating entity of Ctrip.com. 昨天，人民日报报道，据「郑州市场监管」微信公众号通报，郑州市市场监管局依法对携程旅行网运营主体进行了行政约谈。

Upon investigation, the platform was found to have engaged in unreasonable restrictions on the transactions and prices of operators within the platform by utilizing service agreements, transaction rules, and technical means, violating Article 35 of the "E-commerce Law of the People's Republic of China" and Article 24 of the "Interim Provisions on Anti-Unfair Competition in the Network." 经调查，该平台存在利用服务协议、交易规则及技术手段，对平台内经营者的交易及价格进行不合理限制的行为，违反了《中华人民共和国电子商务法》第三十五条及《网络反不正当竞争暂行规定》第二十四条的相关规定。

Previously, the regulatory authorities had issued a "Notice of Order for Correction" to the enterprise on September 4. This interview required Ctrip to complete rectification tasks such as revising contract terms and optimizing pricing tools within a specified period, and to establish and improve a long-term compliance mechanism to prevent similar problems from recurring. 此前，监管部门已于 9 月 4 日向企业下达《责令改正通知书》。本次约谈要求携程在规定期限内完成合同条款修订、调价工具优化等整改任务，并建立健全长效合规机制，防止类似问题再次发生。

The meeting emphasized that internet platform enterprises should earnestly enhance their awareness of the rule of law, actively accept supervision, adhere to the principle of integrity, eliminate unreasonable restrictive behaviors such as "forced activation and inability to exit," and truly respect merchants' independent operating rights. At the same time, it strictly prohibits manipulating prices or restricting transactions through technical means in disguise, and actively fosters an open, fair, and transparent market environment. 会议强调，互联网平台企业应切实增强法治意识，主动接受监管，坚守诚信原则，杜绝「强制开通、无法退出」等不合理限制行为，真正尊重商户自主经营权。同时，严禁通过技术手段变相操控价格或限制交易，积极营造开放、公平、透明的市场环境。

The Zhengzhou Market Supervision Bureau stated that it will continue to track the progress of rectification, conduct acceptance inspections in accordance with the law, urge enterprises to fully implement their primary responsibilities, and jointly maintain a fair and orderly online market environment. 郑州市市场监管局表示，将持续跟踪整改进展，依法开展验收检查，督促企业全面落实主体责任，共同维护公平有序的网络市场秩序。

特斯拉正在重新设计车门把手

According to Bloomberg, Tesla is redesigning its car door handles, intending to integrate electronic and manual release mechanisms into a single button to improve intuitiveness and operability in "emergency situations." 据彭博社报道，特斯拉正重新设计车门把手，拟 将电子与手动释放机制整合为单一按钮 ，以提升在「紧急情况下」的直观性与可操作性。

According to Tesla's chief designer Franz von Holzhausen, the electronic and manual release devices are currently located in different positions, making it difficult for some passengers, especially those in the back seat, to find the manual release in time after a power outage or accident. 据特斯拉首席设计师 Franz von Holzhausen 介绍，目前电子与手动释放装置分处不同位置，部分乘客在车辆断电或事故后难以及时找到手动释放，尤其是后排乘客。

It is reported that the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) had previously launched an investigation into door handle failures in some models, involving approximately 174,300 2021 Model Y vehicles. 据悉，美国国家公路交通安全管理局（NHTSA）此前已就部分车型门把手失灵展开调查，涉及约 17.43 万辆 2021 款 Model Y。

The investigation stemmed from multiple incidents of external handle failure and passengers being trapped inside vehicles, including cases of children being trapped. Since 2018, NHTSA has received over 140 related complaints. 调查缘于多起外部把手失效、乘客被困车内的事件，其中包括儿童被困案例。自 2018 年以来，NHTSA 已收到超过 140 起相关投诉。

Tesla, on its part, stated that the new design solution will balance aesthetics and safety, and that it is studying relevant details in the Chinese market to address regulatory trends in some regions regarding fully concealed door handles. 特斯拉方面则表示，新的设计方案将兼顾美观与安全，并在中国市场研究相关细节，以应对部分地区对全隐藏式门把手的监管趋势。

微信员工回应「去重瘦身脚本」：无效且有风险

Recently, a so-called "WeChat quick deduplication and slimming Python code" has been circulating online, claiming that running it in the PC version of WeChat can delete duplicate files and free up storage space. In response, WeChat employee "Kecun Xiao Jiang" responded on Weibo, explicitly stating that the method is "ineffective and risky." 近日，网络上流传一段所谓的「微信迅速去重瘦身 Python 代码」，声称在电脑版微信中运行后即可删除重复文件，从而释放存储空间。对此，微信员工「客村小蒋」在微博平台上进行了回应，明确指出该方法「无效且有风险」。

This code is useless for solving storage occupation issues and may instead lead to some very troublesome consequences. It is strongly advised not to try it lightly. 这个代码对解决存储占用没啥用，反而可能带来一些很麻烦的后果，强烈建议不要轻易去尝试。

According to reports, some users believe that WeChat repeatedly stores the same file when forwarding it, leading to a large number of files with suffixes like "(1)" and "(2)" appearing in folders. However, WeChat explained that the actual situation is that the client uses hard link technology, where the same file is only stored once, and suffix files are only used to avoid naming conflicts, not to indicate duplicate storage. 据介绍，部分用户认为微信在转发同一文件时会重复存储，导致文件夹中出现大量带有「(1)」「(2)」等后缀的文件。然而，微信方面解释称，实际情况是客户端采用了硬链接技术，同一份文件只会存储一次，后缀文件仅用于避免重名冲突，并不代表重复占用空间。

Regarding the circulating Python script, WeChat employees pointed out that its logic is overly simplistic and crude: it directly searches for and deletes files whose names contain suffixes like "(1)" and "(2)". This approach not only fails to optimize storage but may also accidentally delete user files that legitimately have similar suffixes, leading to data loss. 对于流传的 Python 脚本，微信员工指出其逻辑过于简单粗暴：直接搜索并删除文件名中包含「(1)」「(2)」等后缀的文件。这种做法不仅无法带来存储优化，还可能误删用户本身命名带有类似后缀的文件，造成数据丢失。

Files forwarded in WeChat are not duplicated in storage, so this brings no actual benefit; if some filenames legitimately have such suffixes, they would be directly deleted. 微信里转发的文件本身就没有重复存，它带不来任何实际收益；如果有些文件名本身就带有这样的后缀，那就直接被删了。
