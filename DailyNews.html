阿里认领屠榜神秘模型「欢乐马」，ATH 郑波团队打造

Yesterday, Alibaba ATH confirmed that the mysterious chart-topping video model HappyHorse was developed by its innovation business unit. 昨天，阿里巴巴 ATH 确认神秘屠榜视频模型 HappyHorse 为旗下创新事业部研发。

Previously, the model burst onto the scene on the AI evaluation platform Artificial Analysis's video arena rankings, dominating multiple tracks such as text-to-video and image-to-video with overwhelming performance, directly surpassing the recently popular ByteDance Seedance 2.0, sparking widespread speculation. 此前，该模型在 AI 评测平台 Artificial Analysis 视频竞技场榜单上横空出世、以压倒性表现登顶文生视频、图生视频等多个赛道，直接超越近期热度颇高的字节 Seedance 2.0，引发广泛猜测。

APPSO exclusively learned that the team responsible for the HappyHorse video generation model is Zheng Bo's team from Alibaba ATH. APPSO 独家获悉，负责 HappyHorse 视频生成模型的是来自阿里 ATH 的郑波团队。

Zheng Bo is currently Vice President of Alibaba. After joining Alibaba in September 2017, he successively served as head of Taobao search and recommendation algorithms, CTO of Alimama, and head of algorithm technology for Taotian Group. His research areas cover large models, multimodal AI, decision intelligence, deep learning, and search recommendation advertising algorithms. 郑波现任阿里巴巴副总裁，2017 年 9 月加入阿里巴巴后历任淘宝搜推算法负责人、阿里妈妈 CTO、淘天集团算法技术负责人，研究方向涵盖大模型、多模态、决策智能、深度学习及搜索推荐广告算法等领域。

Alibaba stated that HappyHorse is currently in internal testing, and its API interface is scheduled to open on April 30. The model supports four video generation modes: text-to-video and image-to-video, with the option to add native audio for each mode. 阿里方面表示，HappyHorse 目前仍处于内测阶段，API 接口计划于 4 月 30 日开放。该模型支持四种视频生成模式：文本转视频与图像转视频，每种模式均可选择是否添加原生音频。

The ATH Innovation Business Unit also revealed that it has launched an exploration plan for new interaction methods in the AI era, of which HappyHorse is a component, and more products will be launched sequentially. ATH 创新事业部同时透露，已启动一项面向 AI 时代全新交互方式的探索计划，HappyHorse 是该方向的组成部分，后续还将陆续推出更多产品。

Alibaba also noted that all "official websites" circulating online previously are not official channels. 阿里方面同时提示，此前网络上流传的「官网」均非官方渠道。

苹果 Mac 一季度出货量增长 9%，增速领跑全球 PC 市场

During the same period, the global PC market's overall growth rate was only 2.5%. 市场研究机构 IDC 发布的最新数据显示，苹果 2026 年第一季度 Mac 出货量同比增长 9%，增速跑赢全球 PC 大盘。同期，全球 PC 市场整体增长率仅为 2.5%。
