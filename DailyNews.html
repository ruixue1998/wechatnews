华为公布芯片设计新成果，华为 Mate 90 或首发麒麟 2026

According to People's Daily, the 2026 International Circuits and Systems Symposium was held in Shanghai on the 25th, where He Tingbo, Director of Huawei and President of the Semiconductor Business Unit, officially unveiled the "Tao (τ) Law" in his keynote speech titled "Exploring and Practicing New Paths in Semiconductors." 据《人民日报》消息，2026 国际电路与系统研讨会 25 日在上海举行，华为公司董事、半导体业务部总裁何庭波在题为《半导体新路径探索与实践》的主旨演讲中，正式发表「韬（τ）定律」。

The report points out that this is the first time China has proposed a new principle guiding industrial development in the global semiconductor field. Based on this law, Huawei has successfully designed and mass-produced 381 chips in the past six years. 报道指出，这是中国在全球半导体领域首次提出指导产业发展的新原则。基于该定律，华为过去六年已成功设计并量产了 381 款芯片。

It is worth mentioning that He Tingbo mentioned in his speech that the Kirin 2026 chip, set to be released this autumn, will be the first to fully adopt "logic folding" technology, and the Huawei Mate 90 is expected to be the first device to feature it. 值得一提的是，演讲中何庭波提到，今年秋天面世的麒麟 2026 芯片将首次完整采用「逻辑折叠」技术，华为 Mate 90 有望首发搭载。

It is understood that the "Tao Law" proposes "time miniaturization" to replace "geometric miniaturization," aiming to systematically reduce the time constant (τ). Through innovative technologies such as logic folding, it continuously compresses signal propagation delay, constantly increases transistor density, and achieves the continuous evolution of semiconductors and electronic systems. 据悉，「韬定律」提出以「时间缩微」替代「几何缩微」，以系统性降低时间常数（韬τ）为目标，通过逻辑折叠等创新技术，持续压缩信号传播时延，不断提升晶体管密度，实现半导体与电子系统的持续演进。

The "Tao Law" establishes a multi-level collaborative optimization system spanning devices, circuits, chips, and systems. It is expected that by 2031, the transistor density of high-end chips based on this law will reach the equivalent level of 1.4-nanometer process technology. 「韬定律」构建了贯穿器件、电路、芯片到系统层面的多层级协同优化体系。预计到 2031 年，基于该定律的高端芯片晶体管密度将达到 1.4 纳米制程的同等水平。

Regarding the future development of the semiconductor industry, He Tingbo stated: "The future certainly belongs to open cooperation. Under the path of the 'Tao Law,' we look forward to close collaboration with global scientists, engineers, and industry partners to jointly promote the continuous development of the semiconductor and electronics industries." 针对半导体行业未来的发展，何庭波表示：「未来一定属于开放合作。在『韬定律』的路径下，我们期待与全球科学家、工程师和产业伙伴紧密合作，共同推动半导体与电子产业持续发展。」

Gurman：Apple Watch 需要重启健康叙事

Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman wrote in his PowerOn newsletter that the Apple Watch is facing more direct competition from health wearables like Whoop, Oura, and Fitbit, and Apple needs to re-evaluate the product rhythm for its Watch and health businesses. 彭博社记者马克・古尔曼 (Mark Gurman) 在 PowerOn 通讯中写道，Apple Watch 正面临 Whoop、Oura、Fitbit 等健康穿戴设备的更直接竞争，苹果需要重新梳理 Watch 和健康业务的产品节奏。

The report mentioned that the Apple Watch has expanded from a notification and activity tracking tool to health scenarios such as heart rate, sleep, hearing assistance, and fall detection. However, hardware updates in recent generations have been minor, and new health sensors and service capabilities have not formed a clear growth narrative. 报道提到，Apple Watch 已经从通知和运动记录工具扩展到心率、睡眠、助听、跌倒检测等健康场景，但近几代硬件更新幅度较小，新的健康传感器和服务化能力没有形成清晰的增长叙事。

Meanwhile, Oura strengthens sleep and recovery monitoring with its ring form factor, Whoop retains core athletic users with subscriptions and high-frequency body data services, and Google continues to integrate Fitbit capabilities. 与此同时，Oura 以戒指形态强化睡眠和恢复监测，Whoop 以订阅和高频身体数据服务留住核心运动用户，Google 也继续整合 Fitbit 能力。

Gurman believes that Apple does not lack health entry points, but rather needs to update its hardware, software, and health services more closely. His communication also mentioned that iOS 27, AirPods settings, and health features might be further strengthened around WWDC, and the Watch business will be repositioned within a larger portfolio of personal health devices. 古尔曼认为，苹果并不缺健康入口，而是需要让硬件、软件和健康服务更紧密地更新。其通讯还提到 iOS 27、AirPods 设置与健康功能可能在 WWDC 前后继续补强，Watch 业务会被放在更大的个人健康设备组合里重新定位。

立讯精密：光连接和铜连接会长期共存

According to People's Finance, Luxshare Precision recently responded to investor questions during its annual shareholders' meeting, stating that the company's advocacy of "advancing both optical and copper" is not contradictory to its top clients' layout of optical chip and fiber manufacturers. The company stated that in the products it currently serves, the future demand for optical connections will be far greater than for copper connections, but copper connections themselves still have high technical barriers. 据人民财讯报道，立讯精密近日在年度股东会交流环节回应投资者提问，称公司主张的「光铜并进」与头部客户布局光芯片、光纤厂商并不矛盾。公司表示，在当前服务的产品中，未来光连接的需求规模会远大于铜连接，但铜连接本身仍有较高技术门槛。

The two routes cater to different distance and speed scenarios: copper connections are suitable for short-distance scenarios, while long-distance and higher-speed scenarios have a greater demand for optical connections; when copper connections approach their signal transmission limit, optical connections are needed to solve the problem. 两条路线面向不同距离和速率场景：短距离场景适用铜连接，长距离及更高速率场景对光连接需求更大；当铜连接接近信号传输极限时，需要通过光连接解决问题。

This response corresponds to the rising demand for AI server and data center interconnects. Leading clients like Nvidia are advancing optical chips, optical fibers, and high-speed interconnect ecosystems. Supply chain manufacturers need to cover copper connections, optical connections, and related components simultaneously to adapt to different cabinet, node, and cluster distances. 这番回应对应的是 AI 服务器和数据中心互连需求上升。英伟达等头部客户正在推进光芯片、光纤和高速互连生态，供应链厂商需要同时覆盖铜连接、光连接和相关组件，才能适应不同机柜、节点和集群距离。

银河证券：钠离子电池规模化节点临近

Galaxy Securities' research report states that CATL announced at its "Polar Region Pact" Super Technology Day that new sodium-ion batteries are expected to achieve mass production in the fourth quarter of this year. Consequently, the sodium-ion battery industry chain has entered a clearer mass production verification phase. The report judges that power, energy storage, and two-wheelers are key areas for the volume release of sodium-ion batteries. 银河证券研报称，宁德时代在「极域之约」超级科技日宣布钠新电池预计于今年第四季度规模化量产，钠离子电池产业链因此进入更明确的量产验证阶段。研报判断，动力、储能和两轮车是钠离子电池重点放量领域。

  • In power scenarios, the wide temperature range and economic efficiency of sodium batteries can improve the problem of electric vehicles operating in low temperatures; 动力场景中，钠电池的宽温域和经济性可以改善电动车低温使用问题；
  • In energy storage scenarios, high safety and high discharge power are more suitable for some fixed energy storage needs; 储能场景中，高安全性和高放电功率更适合部分固定式储能需求；
  • The two-wheeler sector corresponds to the market for low-cost alternatives to lead-acid batteries. 两轮车领域则对应低成本替代铅酸电池的市场。

Galaxy Securities predicts that the shipment volume of sodium-ion batteries will reach 25GWh, 92GWh, and 221GWh in 2026, 2027, and 2028 respectively, breaking through 600GWh by 2030. 银河证券预计，钠离子电池出货量将在 2026、2027、2028 年分别达到 25GWh、92GWh、221GWh，到 2030 年突破 600GWh。

Sodium batteries do not directly replace high-end lithium batteries, but rather first establish scale in scenarios with clearer requirements for cost, low temperature, and safety. Subsequent volume increases will depend on the verification pace of cathode and anode materials, electrolytes, separators, and vehicle/energy storage customers. CATL's mass production timeline will be an important reference for the industry to follow. 钠电池并不直接取代高端锂电池，而是先在成本、低温、安全性要求更明确的场景建立规模。后续放量还要看正负极材料、电解液、隔膜和整车 / 储能客户验证节奏，宁德时代的量产节点会成为产业链跟进的重要参照。

ModelBest, Tsinghua University, and the OpenBMB open-source community yesterday announced the open-sourcing of BitCPM-CANN, a new achievement in their low-bit large model training direction, and also a series of ternary 1.58-bit large models that have completed end-to-end training and been open-sourced based on Huawei Ascend's domestic computing power platform. 面壁智能、清华大学和 OpenBMB 开源社区昨日宣布开源 BitCPM-CANN，这是其低比特大模型训练方向的新成果，也是基于华为昇腾国产算力平台完成端到端训练并开源的三值 1.58-bit 大模型系列。
