Claude Code「被开源」，隐藏功能「赛博宠物」和永久记忆 AI 提前曝光

Yesterday afternoon, developer Chaofan Shou posted on X, directly releasing the complete source code package for Anthropic's command-line tool, Claude Code, which sparked widespread attention in the developer community. The post garnered over 12 million views within hours. 昨天下午，开发者 Chaofan Shou 在 X 发文，直接放出了 Anthropic 旗下命令行工具 Claude Code 的完整源码压缩包，引发开发者社区广泛关注。该帖子数小时内浏览量突破 1200 万次。

The incident began when Anthropic forgot to delete .map files (Source Map) when publishing packages to the npm public registry. These files are used to map compressed and obfuscated production code back to the original source code for local debugging, but deleting them during official release is a basic operation. 事件起因是 Anthropic 在向 npm 公共镜像库发包时，忘记删除 .map 文件（Source Map）。这类文件的作用是将压缩、混淆后的生产代码映射回原始源码，方便本地调试，但在正式发布时将其删除是基本操作。

It was this oversight that led to nearly 2,000 files and over 500,000 lines of TypeScript code being publicly exposed online. 正是这一失误，导致近 2000 份文件、超过 50 万行 TypeScript 代码在网上公开裸露。

Among the leaked source code, developers uncovered several unreleased features. The most discussed project is codenamed BUDDY — a "cyber pet" system planned to be built into the terminal. 在已泄露的源码中，开发者们扒出了多个尚未发布的功能。讨论度最高的是代号 BUDDY 的项目——一个计划内置于终端的 「赛博宠物」 系统。

Pets are generated based on the user ID's hash value, making each one unique. There are 18 preset biological forms, including ducks, dragons, capybaras, ghosts, etc., and a rarity system ranging from "Common" to "1% Legendary," as well as five dynamic stats: DEBUGGING, PATIENCE, CHAOS, WISDOM, and SNARK. 宠物根据用户 ID 的哈希值生成，每人独一无二，预设了 18 种生物形态，包含鸭子、龙、水豚、幽灵等，并设有从「普通」到「1% 传奇」的稀有度体系，以及五项动态数值：DEBUGGING（调试力）、PATIENCE（耐心）、CHAOS（混沌）、WISDOM（智慧）和 SNARK（阴阳怪气值）。

The source code shows that the feature was originally planned as an Easter egg warm-up from April 1st to 7th, with internal beta testing opening to Anthropic employees in May. 源码显示，该功能原计划于 4 月 1 日至 7 日作为彩蛋预热，5 月份向 Anthropic 内部员工开放内测。

Another highly anticipated feature is the "always-on" AI assistant codenamed KAIROS. This assistant can maintain continuous memory across sessions, storing user work habits and project context in a private directory, and has the authority to proactively initiate tasks. 另一个备受关注的功能是代号 KAIROS 的「永远在线」AI 助手。 该助手能够跨会话保持持续记忆，将用户的工作习惯和项目背景存储在私密目录中，并有权限主动发起任务。

Notably, the source code also includes a mechanism called "Nightly Dreaming" — automatically starting background processes at night to review the day's interactions, remove redundant information, and consolidate core content into long-term memory to prevent context logs from infinitely expanding. 值得注意的是，源码中还包含一个名为「Nightly Dreaming（夜间做梦）」的机制——在夜间自动启动后台进程，回溯当天交互内容，剔除冗余信息，将核心内容沉淀为长期记忆，以防止上下文日志无限膨胀。

However, whether this feature will ultimately be released is not yet confirmed. Apple recently updated its keyboard algorithm to improve the "missing word" issue. 此外，苹果还在探索升级系统键盘，通过类似 Grammarly 的方式扩展自动更正功能，提供替代词建议。不过该功能是否最终发布尚未确定。苹果近日已对键盘算法进行了更新，以改善「缺字」问题。

Apple is expected to officially release the new version of Siri and related Apple Intelligence features at the Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) on June 8th local time. 苹果预计将于当地时间 6 月 8 日的全球开发者大会（WWDC）上正式发布新版 Siri 及 Apple Intelligence 的相关功能。

千问测试「引证」功能，AI 回答支持信源核查

Qianwen recently launched a new feature called "Citation," which allows for secondary fact-checking of answers that involve news, current affairs, policy dynamics, and other content requiring external sources. This feature is currently still in its testing phase. 千问近日上线了一项名为「引证」的新功能，可对涉及新闻时事、政策动态等需引用外部信源的回答内容进行二次事实核查。目前该功能仍处于测试阶段。

In actual use, the "Citation" button only appears at the end of an answer when the user's question involves content related to news, current affairs, policy dynamics, or other information requiring reference to online sources. 实际体验中，「引证」按钮仅在用户提问涉及新闻时事、政策动态等需参考网络信息的内容时，才会出现在回答末尾。

After clicking, the system will automatically enter verification mode, intelligently marking key information in the answer: content supported by reliable, cross-verifiable authoritative sources will be highlighted in green; content with ambiguous or contradictory sources, or not yet confirmed by mainstream media, will be marked in red with a prompt "requires further verification." 点击后，系统将自动进入核查模式，对回答中的关键信息进行智能标注：有可靠、可交叉验证的权威信源支持的内容以绿色高亮显示；信息来源模糊、存在矛盾，或尚未被主流媒体证实的内容则标记为红色，并提示「需进一步核查」。

《AI 纪录片》在美上映：Altman、Amodei、Hassabis 齐聚

According to Business Insider, the AI documentary "The AI Doc: Or How I Became an Apocaloptimist," co-directed by Oscar-winning director Daniel Roher and Charlie Tyrell, premiered in US theaters yesterday (March 27). 据《商业内幕》报道，由奥斯卡获奖导演 Daniel Roher 与 Charlie Tyrell 联合执导的 AI 纪录片《The AI Doc: Or How I Became an Apocaloptimist》（AI 纪录片：或我如何学会与末日共存）已于昨日（3 月 27 日）登陆美国院线。

For its lineup of interviewees, the film invited key figures in the AI field such as OpenAI CEO Sam Altman, Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei, and Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis, as well as several AI ethics and risk experts. Elon Musk was originally invited but, according to the director, he was "too busy" and ultimately could not appear. 在受访者阵容上，影片邀请到了 OpenAI CEO Sam Altman、Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei、Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis 等 AI 领域核心人物，还有多位 AI 伦理与风险专家。马斯克原本也在受邀之列，但据导演透露，他「太忙了」，最终未能出镜。

The film delves into the current state, potential, and risks of AI technology from Roher's personal perspective as an expectant father. The term "apocaloptimist" runs throughout the film, reflecting the creators' complex emotions towards the AI era—both full of apprehension and anticipation. 影片以 Roher 即将为人父的个人视角切入，试图厘清 AI 技术的现状、潜力与风险。「apocaloptimist」（末日乐观主义者）一词贯穿全片，折射出创作者对 AI 时代的复杂情绪——既充满忧虑，又抱有期待。

The film also directly addresses the business logic behind the AI race. Altman admits in the film that, under similar conditions, the party willing to take shortcuts on safety often gains an advantage, but he also states that OpenAI is leveraging its leading position to invest more time in safety testing. 影片也直面了 AI 竞赛背后的商业逻辑。Altman 在片中坦承，在同等条件下，愿意在安全上走捷径的一方往往能抢占先机，但他同时表示 OpenAI 正利用自身的领先优势投入更多时间进行安全测试。

Despite the film's pervasive underlying concern about AI losing control, Altman still expresses personal optimism for the future: "I'm not worried about children growing up in a world with AI." The film concludes with Roher's private footage of welcoming his newborn, bringing the grand technological theme back to an ordinary father's hopes for the future. 尽管影片弥漫着对 AI 失控的隐忧，Altman 仍对未来表达了个人层面的乐观：「我不担心孩子们在一个有 AI 的世界里成长。」影片最终以 Roher 迎接新生儿的私人影像作结，将宏大的技术命题落回到一个普通父亲对未来的期许之上。

The film currently holds an 89% "Fresh" rating on Rotten Tomatoes (based on 35 critics) and a Metacritic score of 60, indicating "mixed or average reviews." 影片目前在烂番茄上获得 89% 的新鲜度（基于 35 位影评人），Metacritic 综合评分为 60 分，评价呈「褒贬不一」态势。
