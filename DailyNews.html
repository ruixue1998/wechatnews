触控版 MacBook Pro 曝光：配备灵动岛与 OLED 屏、今年亮相

According to Bloomberg, the Dynamic Island on the touch-enabled MacBook Pro will feature a smaller cutout design than existing iPhones, and is expected to officially debut by the end of this year; this year's iPhone 18 Pro series will also simultaneously reduce the size of the Dynamic Island. 据彭博社报道，触控版 MacBook Pro 的灵动岛将采用比现有 iPhone 更小的开孔设计，预计将在今年年底正式亮相；而今年的 iPhone 18 Pro 系列也将同步缩小灵动岛尺寸。

Reporter Mark Gurman stated that the new models will be equipped with Dynamic Island and OLED screens, and the overall appearance will continue the design language of the existing 14-inch and 16-inch MacBook Pro. 记者 Mark Gurman 称，新机型将配备灵动岛以及 OLED 屏幕，整体外观仍延续现有 14 英寸与 16 英寸 MacBook Pro 的设计语言。

Touch capabilities will drive macOS to introduce a brand new dynamic interface: the system will switch between a larger touch mode and a traditional mouse-click mode based on the user's interaction method. The system will also support fast scrolling and pinch-to-zoom similar to iPhone and iPad. 触控能力将推动 macOS 引入全新的动态界面：系统会根据用户的操作方式在界面更大的触控模式与传统鼠标点按模式之间切换。系统还将支持与 iPhone、iPad 类似的快速滚动与双指缩放。

The report suggests that macOS Tahoe's Liquid Glass design update last year (including adjustments to the whitespace of icons and notifications, and changes to the control center slider style) was precisely in preparation for the touch experience. 报道认为，macOS Tahoe 去年的 Liquid Glass 设计更新（包括图标与通知的留白调整、控制中心滑块样式变化）正是为触控体验做准备。

苹果推送 iOS 26.4 Beta 2，液态玻璃再调整

Yesterday, Apple pushed iOS 26.4 Beta 2 to developers, once again fine-tuning the "Liquid Glass" effect, with other updates primarily consisting of minor adjustments. 昨天，苹果面向开发者推送了 iOS 26.4 Beta 2，再次对「液态玻璃」（Liquid Glass）的效果进行了微调，其他更新内容以小幅调整为主。

The transparency of the Liquid Glass in the Home Screen editing menu has been further increased, making the glass texture more pronounced. However, in some backgrounds, this can lead to reduced contrast between text and icons, impairing readability; the background of the Control Center pop-up in dark mode has also become darker simultaneously. 主屏幕编辑菜单的液态玻璃透明度进一步提升，玻璃质感更明显，但在部分背景下会导致文字与图标的对比度下降，导致可读性变差；深色模式下的控制中心弹窗背景也同步变得更暗。

Additionally, a "Reduce Highlighting Effects" option has been added to Accessibility, likely intended to reduce the visual highlight effects on the edges of buttons and sliders. However, the actual changes from this option are not currently significant. 此外，辅助功能中新增了「Reduce Highlighting Effects（降低高光效果）」选项，或用于减少按钮与滑块边缘的高光视觉效果。不过，该选项目前的实际变化并不明显。

Other update details, compiled from reports by MacRumors and AppleInsider, are as follows: 其他更新细节参考 MacRumors 和 AppleInsider 报道整理如下：

  • End-to-end encryption for RCS messages has been extended between iPhone and Android devices, previously only available between iPhones, but requires carrier support; RCS 信息的端到端加密扩展到 iPhone 与安卓设备之间，此前仅 iPhone 之间可用，但需运营商支持；
  • The title bars of the App Store, Apple Music, and Podcast personal pages are uniformly designed with colored icons + left-aligned layout; App Store、Apple Music、播客个人页面标题栏统一为彩色图标 + 左对齐布局；
  • The Games App search bar has been moved to the top, consistent with the App Store design; Games App 搜索栏上移至顶部，与 App Store 设计保持一致；
  • Beta Channel Rule Adjustment: If a user has not installed any beta version for four months, the system will automatically switch back to the public update channel; Beta 通道规则调整：若用户四个月未安装任何测试版，系统将自动切回公开版更新通道；
  • iBoot Renamed to mBoot: Apple has changed the name of its bootloader, which has been in use for nearly two decades, to "mBoot," with the version number jumping to the 18000 range. The reason has not been announced. iBoot 更名为 mBoot：苹果将沿用近二十年的引导程序名称改为「mBoot」，版本号跳升至 18000 段位，原因未公布。

微软公布「玻璃盘」，容量 4.8TB 可保存上万年

Recently, the Microsoft Research team unveiled 'Silica,' a glass-based storage technology for ultra-long-term data archiving, and demonstrated a complete write, read, and decode system in a paper published in Nature. 近日，微软研究院团队公布了一项面向超长期数据归档的玻璃基存储技术「Silica」，并在发表于《自然》的论文中展示了完整的写入、读取与解码系统。

It is reported that this technology uses femtosecond lasers to engrave voxel structures inside glass, achieving high-density, low-energy, and data storage capabilities that can be preserved for tens of thousands of years. 据悉，这项技术通过飞秒激光在玻璃内部刻写体素（voxel）结构，实现高密度、低能耗且可保存上万年的数据存储能力。

Microsoft stated that a 120mm square, 2mm thick piece of borosilicate glass can store approximately 4.8TB of data, equivalent to the content of about 2 million printed books. The team estimated in accelerated aging experiments that data can be preserved for more than 10,000 years at 290℃, and even longer at room temperature. 微软表示，一块 120mm 见方、2mm 厚的硅酸硼玻璃可存储约 4.8TB 数据，相当于约 200 万本印刷书籍的内容。团队在加速老化实验中推算，在 290℃ 条件下数据可保存 1 万年 以上，而在室温下寿命将更长。

The paper shows that Silica uses two types of voxel writing methods: one is birefringent voxels based on refractive index anisotropy, and the other is phase voxels based on refractive index changes. 论文显示，Silica 采用两类体素写入方式：一种是基于折射率各向异性的双折射体素，另一种是基于折射率变化的相位体素。

The team also demonstrated multi-beam parallel writing technology, increasing writing throughput to 65.9 Mbit/s, and expects it to be scalable to hundreds of beams in the future. 团队还展示了多束并行写入技术，使写入吞吐量提升至 65.9Mbit/s，并预计未来可扩展至数百束。

Researchers point out that glass media is non-rewritable and has high read/write costs, making it unsuitable for everyday data storage. However, it is ideal for preserving information that needs to span decades or even centuries, such as scientific data, cultural heritage, and natural disaster records. 研究人员指出，玻璃介质不可重写、读写成本较高，因此不适合日常数据存储，但非常适合保存科学数据、文化遗产、自然灾害记录等需跨越数十年甚至数百年的信息。
