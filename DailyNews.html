苹果或推出官方租赁服务：iPhone 租期 24 个月，Mac 租期 36 个月

According to Bloomberg, Apple plans to launch its "Apple Upgrade" device leasing service on July 28, covering most iPhone, Mac, iPad, and Apple Watch models. The service will first launch in the United States, supporting both Apple's offline retail stores and official website channels, with Apple partnering with Klarna for financial backing. 据彭博社报道，苹果计划于 7 月 28 日推出「Apple Upgrade」设备租赁服务，覆盖大多数 iPhone、Mac、iPad 和 Apple Watch 机型。该服务将首先在美国上线，同时支持苹果线下零售店和官网渠道，苹果与 Klarna 合作，由后者提供资金支持。

The "Apple Upgrade" operates similarly to a subscription service, allowing users to pay off the device early, upgrade early during the lease term, or keep or return the device after the lease ends. 「Apple Upgrade」的运作方式类似订阅服务，用户可在租赁期内提前付清设备款、提前换新，也可以在租期结束后保留设备或归还设备。

The lease term for iPhones and Apple Watches is 24 months, and for Macs and iPads, it's 36 months. Users will need to pass a soft credit check. Some early repayment, upgrade, or device retention operations may incur additional fees. iPhone 和 Apple Watch 的租期为 24 个月，Mac 和 iPad 为 36 个月，用户需要通过软信用审查。部分提前还款、换新或保留设备的操作可能产生额外费用。

Apple plans to market the service as a way to lower monthly payments and will stop new user registrations for the existing iPhone Upgrade Program and standard installment plans. 苹果计划将该服务宣传为降低月供的购买方式，并停止现有 iPhone Upgrade Program 和标准分期付款计划的新用户注册。

Unlike the current iPhone Upgrade Program, Apple Upgrade does not include AppleCare. Devices such as Apple Watch SE, entry-level iPads, iPhone 16, and MacBook Neo are also not part of the plan, and enterprise and educational users are not applicable. 与当前的 iPhone Upgrade Program 不同，Apple Upgrade 不包含 AppleCare，Apple Watch SE、入门款 iPad、iPhone 16 和 MacBook Neo 等设备也不参与该计划，企业和教育用户同样不在适用范围内。

Google 发布 Gemini 3.6 Flash，输出 token 使用量减少 17%

Google today released Gemini 3.6 Flash, 3.5 Flash-Lite, and 3.5 Flash Cyber for code security. Google 今天发布 Gemini 3.6 Flash、3.5 Flash-Lite 和面向代码安全的 3.5 Flash Cyber。

Gemini 3.6 Flash reduces output tokens by 17% compared to 3.5 Flash and lowers inference steps and tool calls in multi-step workflows. Gemini 3.6 Flash 相比 3.5 Flash 减少 17% 输出 Token，并降低多步工作流中的推理步骤和工具调用次数。

API pricing is $1.5 per million input tokens and $7.5 per million output tokens; DeepSWE score increased from 37% to 49%, OSWorld-Verified from 78.4% to 83%, and knowledge cutoff updated to March 2026. API 定价为每百万输入 Token 1.5 美元、输出 Token 7.5 美元；DeepSWE 得分由 37% 提高到 49%，OSWorld-Verified 由 78.4% 提高到 83%，知识截止时间更新至 2026 年 3 月。

Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite is designed for high-throughput, low-latency search and document processing, with pricing at $0.3 per million input tokens and $2.5 per million output tokens, respectively. Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite 面向高吞吐、低延迟搜索和文档处理，每百万输入、输出 Token 分别为 0.3 美元和 2.5 美元。

3.5 Flash Cyber is used to find, verify, and fix code vulnerabilities, with the first batch only available to government and trusted partners. Google also stated that Gemini 4 has started its largest pre-training run to date. 3.5 Flash Cyber 用于查找、验证和修复代码漏洞，首批只向政府与可信合作伙伴开放。Google 同时表示，Gemini 4 已启动目前规模最大的预训练运行。

OpenAI 称模型曾突破沙盒、入侵 Hugging Face 基础设施

According to official OpenAI disclosure, during an internal network capability assessment, two of its models, including GPT-5.6 Sol, broke through testing environment restrictions and accessed test reference answers in Hugging Face's production infrastructure. 据 OpenAI 官方披露，在内部网络能力评估中，旗下包括 GPT-5.6 Sol 在内的两款模型突破了测试环境限制，并访问了 Hugging Face 生产基础设施中的测试参考答案。

OpenAI referred to it as an "unprecedented network incident" and is currently collaborating with Hugging Face on investigation and remediation efforts. OpenAI 将其称为一起「前所未有的网络事件」，目前正与 Hugging Face 共同展开调查和修复工作。

This incident occurred during an internal assessment called ExploitGym. OpenAI stated that the model first exploited a previously unknown vulnerability in an internally hosted package registry agent to gain internet access, then performed privilege escalation and lateral movement within the research testing environment. 这次事件发生在名为 ExploitGym 的内部评估中。OpenAI 表示，模型首先利用内部托管的软件包注册代理中的一个此前未知漏洞，获得了互联网访问能力，随后在研究测试环境中进行权限提升和横向移动。

After gaining external network access, the model inferred that Hugging Face might store ExploitGym-related models, datasets, and answers. It then used stolen credentials and other vulnerabilities to find a remote code execution path on Hugging Face servers, ultimately accessing test information in the production database. 获得外网访问后，模型推断 Hugging Face 可能存储了 ExploitGym 相关的模型、数据集和答案，并通过被盗凭证及其他漏洞，在 Hugging Face 服务器上找到远程代码执行路径，最终接触到生产数据库中的测试信息。

OpenAI stated that it has implemented stricter configuration controls on relevant infrastructure, responsibly disclosed zero-day vulnerabilities in agents to software vendors, and will strengthen the isolation, monitoring, access control, and security protection of future training and evaluation environments. OpenAI 表示，已对相关基础设施实施更严格的配置控制，并负责地向软件供应商披露了代理中的零日漏洞，同时将加强未来训练和评估环境的隔离、监控、访问控制与安全防护。

Infinity 融资 1500 万美元，用 AI 为不同芯片编写推理软件

AI infrastructure company Infinity completed a $15 million seed round of financing, valuing the company at $100 million post-investment. Touring Capital, Principal Venture Partners, chip company executives, and researchers from OpenAI and Anthropic participated in this round. The funds will be used to expand the engineering team, scale the automated AI research platform, and advance collaborations with chip companies such as d-Matrix. AI 基础设施公司 Infinity 完成 1500 万美元种子轮融资，投后估值为 1 亿美元。Touring Capital、Principal Venture Partners、芯片公司高管以及来自 OpenAI、Anthropic 的研究人员参与本轮，资金将用于扩充工程团队、扩大自动化 AI 研究平台，并推进与 d-Matrix 等芯片公司的合作。

Infinity's Ignition intelligent agent automatically generates, tests, and optimizes underlying inference code for new chips. The company states that Ignition increased the inference throughput of Qwen3-8B to approximately 20,000 Tokens per second in one day, a 14-fold increase compared to the initial approximately 1,400 Tokens per second, and 34% higher than vLLM; this test result has not yet been independently verified. Infinity 的 Ignition 智能体会为新芯片自动生成、测试和优化底层推理代码。公司称，Ignition 在一天内把 Qwen3-8B 的推理吞吐提升至约每秒 2 万 Token，相比初始约每秒 1400 Token 提升 14 倍，并比 vLLM 高出 34%；这一测试结果尚未经过独立验证。

The company is currently collaborating with d-Matrix on chip design, stating that related businesses have generated millions of dollars in annual recurring revenue. Infinity charges based on the actual improvement in inference throughput, typically receiving about 20% of the new computing power procurement costs saved by customers. 公司目前与 d-Matrix 开展芯片设计合作，称相关业务已产生数百万美元年度经常性收入。Infinity 按推理吞吐的实际提升收费，通常从客户因此节省的新增算力采购成本中获得约 20% 分成。

Google 研发 Frozen v2 芯片，把部分 Gemini 架构写入硬件

According to The Information, Google is developing a server chip internally codenamed Frozen v2, hoping to reduce computation and data transfer during inference by solidifying part of the Gemini model's architecture into hardware. Google engineers estimate that its token processing capability per unit of power consumption can reach 6 to 10 times that of the latest generation of TPUs. 据 The Information 报道，谷歌正在研发内部代号为 Frozen v2 的服务器芯片，希望通过把 Gemini 模型的部分架构固化进硬件，减少推理时的计算量与数据传输。谷歌工程师预计，它的单位功耗 Token 处理能力可达到最新一代 TPU 的 6 至 10 倍。

Frozen v2 is planned for deployment in 2028 and will not replace general-purpose TPUs but will serve as a specialized branch for specific model architectures. The trade-off is reduced flexibility: the chip will only be compatible with new versions if subsequent Gemini models continue to use the same underlying architecture. Frozen v2 计划于 2028 年部署，不会取代通用 TPU，而是作为面向特定模型架构的专用分支。代价是灵活性下降：只有后续 Gemini 继续沿用相同底层架构，芯片才能兼容新版本。

Google responded that the company continuously researches co-design of software and hardware, but not all experimental projects will enter mass production. Currently, Frozen v2 is still an experimental project, and there are no plans for large-scale production like TPUs. Google 回应称，公司持续研究软硬件协同设计，但并非所有试验项目都会进入量产。目前 Frozen v2 仍是试验性项目，暂无像 TPU 一样大规模生产的计划。
