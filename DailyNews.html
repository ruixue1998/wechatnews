DeepSeek V4 开源，华为昇腾首发，性能比肩顶级闭源

Yesterday, DeepSeek officially released and open-sourced the V4 series model preview version , launching two products, DeepSeek-V4-Pro and DeepSeek-V4-Flash, both featuring a million-token context, with API services going live simultaneously. 昨天， DeepSeek 正式发布并开源了 V4 系列模型预览版 ，推出 DeepSeek-V4-Pro 与 DeepSeek-V4-Flash 两款产品，双双标配百万 token 上下文，API 服务同步上线。

V4-Pro has 1.6T parameters (49B active parameters), and V4-Flash has 284B parameters (13B active parameters). Both models are primarily focused on upgrading AI Agent capabilities and have been specifically adapted for mainstream products such as Claude Code, OpenCode, and CodeBuddy. V4-Pro-Max's performance is particularly outstanding: V4-Pro 参数量达 1.6T（49B 激活参数），V4-Flash 参数量为 284B（13B 激活参数）。两款模型均以 AI Agent 能力为核心升级方向，已针对 Claude Code、OpenCode、CodeBuddy 等主流产品完成专项适配。V4-Pro-Max 的性能表现尤为突出：

  • LiveCodeBench Pass@1 reached 93.5, and Codeforces Rating reached 3206, both the highest among the models tested, currently ranking 23rd in the Codeforces human player leaderboard; LiveCodeBench Pass@1 达 93.5，Codeforces Rating 达 3206，均为参测模型最高，目前在 Codeforces 人类选手排行榜位列第 23 名；
  • SWE Verified Resolved reached 80.6, almost on par with Claude Opus 4.6 Max's 80.8; SWE Verified Resolved 达 80.6，与 Claude Opus 4.6 Max 的 80.8 基本持平；
  • IMOAnswerBench Pass@1 was 89.8, second only to GPT-5.4's 91.4; IMOAnswerBench Pass@1 为 89.8，仅次于 GPT-5.4 的 91.4；
  • BrowseComp Pass@1 reached 83.4, and MCPAtlas Public Pass@1 reached 73.6, placing them among the top models tested. BrowseComp Pass@1 达 83.4，MCPAtlas Public Pass@1 达 73.6，处于参测模型前列。

DeepSeek officially stated that V4-Pro-Max has "firmly secured the throne as the best open-source model," achieving top-tier performance on programming benchmarks and significantly narrowing the gap with leading closed-source models in reasoning and Agentic tasks; V4-Flash-Max, when given sufficient thinking budget, can achieve reasoning performance comparable to the Pro version. DeepSeek 官方表示，V4-Pro-Max 已「稳坐最佳开源模型宝座」，在编程基准上达到顶级水平，并在推理与 Agentic 任务上显著缩小与领先闭源模型的差距；V4-Flash-Max 则在给予充足思考预算时，可实现与 Pro 版本相当的推理表现。

It's worth noting that Nvidia is no longer the only option. DeepSeek has exclusively opened early access to V4 to domestic chip manufacturers, with Huawei Ascend becoming the first platform – this marks the first time a top-tier open-source large model has fully run on domestic computing power, and it's a significant step for domestic models in "de-Nvidia-fication." 值得注意的是，英伟达不再是唯一选项。DeepSeek 将 V4 的早期访问权限独家开放给国产芯片厂商，华为昇腾成为首发平台 —— 这是顶级开源大模型首次完整跑通国产算力，也是国产模型在「去英伟达化」上迈出的重要一步。

Just last week, Jensen Huang said in a podcast interview: "If DeepSeek had launched on the Huawei platform first, that would have been very scary for us." 而就在上周，黄仁勋在播客访谈里说了一句话：「如果当初 DeepSeek 先在华为平台上发布，那对我们来说非常可怕。」

On the day of the release, major cloud service providers quickly followed suit: 发布当日，各大云服务厂商迅速跟进：

  • PPIO became one of the first AI cloud platforms in the industry to launch DeepSeek-V4-Pro and DeepSeek-V4-Flash, allowing developers to directly call them after registration without self-deployment; PPIO 成为业内首批上线 DeepSeek-V4-Pro 和 DeepSeek-V4-Flash 的 AI 云平台，开发者注册后即可直接调用，无需自行部署；
  • Huawei Cloud MaaS platform simultaneously launched its adaptation, providing a one-click API Tokens service for DeepSeek-V4-Flash; 华为云 MaaS 平台同步首发适配，已提供一键调用 DeepSeek-V4-Flash API 的 Tokens 服务；
  • China Unicom's Unicom Cloud and Unicom Yuanjing platforms also completed integration on the release day, and directly embedded the V4 model in their CodingPlan subscription package; 中国联通的联通云与联通元景平台也在发布当天完成集成，并在 CodingPlan 订阅套餐中直接内置了 V4 模型；
  • Tianshu Zhixin and Cambricon both completed Day 0 level adaptation for both versions, and Cambricon's adaptation code has been open-sourced to the GitHub community. 天数智芯、寒武纪均完成了对两个版本的 Day 0 级适配，寒武纪的适配代码已开源至 GitHub 社区。

Google 拟向 Anthropic 投资最高 400 亿美元

According to Bloomberg, Google plans to invest up to $40 billion in Anthropic. According to Anthropic, Google will immediately inject $10 billion in cash at a valuation of $35 billion, with an additional $30 billion to follow if Anthropic meets specific performance targets. 据彭博社报道，Google 计划向 Anthropic 投资最高 400 亿美元。根据 Anthropic 方面的说法，Google 将以 350 亿美元的估值立即注入 100 亿美元现金，若 Anthropic 达成特定业绩目标，另外 300 亿美元将随后跟进。

In terms of computing power, Google Cloud will provide Anthropic with 5 gigawatts of computing resources over the next five years, with more gigawatts of capacity potentially following. This is a further expansion of the tripartite agreement between Anthropic, Google, and Broadcom earlier this month. 在算力层面，Google Cloud 将在未来五年内为 Anthropic 提供 5 吉瓦的算力资源，后续或有更多吉瓦的容量跟进。这是本月早些时候 Anthropic、Google 与博通三方协议的进一步扩展。

Intel 发布一季度财报：营收增长 7%，AI 需求拉动 CPU 与封装业务

Yesterday, Intel released its financial report for the first quarter of fiscal year 2026. 昨日，Intel 发布 2026 财年第一季度财报。

First-quarter revenue was $13.6 billion, a 7% year-over-year increase, marking six consecutive quarters of exceeding revenue expectations. Earnings per share were -$0.73 under GAAP and $0.29 under non-GAAP, with single-quarter operating cash flow reaching $1.1 billion. 第一季度营收 136 亿美元，同比增长 7%，连续六个季度实现高于预期的营收表现。按通用会计准则每股收益为 -0.73 美元，非通用会计准则每股收益为 0.29 美元，单季度经营现金流达 11 亿美元。

  • On the business front, Intel launched the third generation Core Ultra series processors based on the Intel 18A process, introducing this process to the mainstream market for the first time, and simultaneously released several new products including Xeon 600, Core Ultra 200S Plus, and 200HX Plus. 业务层面，Intel 推出基于 Intel 18A 制程的第三代酷睿 Ultra 系列处理器，首次将该制程引入主流市场，同步发布至强 600、酷睿 Ultra 200S Plus 及 200HX Plus 等多款新品。
  • Intel and Google have entered into a multi-year partnership to continuously deploy Xeon processors in Google workload-optimized instances and jointly develop custom ASIC Infrastructure Processing Units (IPUs); Xeon 6 processors are also confirmed to be integrated into Nvidia DGX Rubin NVL8 systems as the main CPU. 英特尔与 Google 达成为期多年合作，将在 Google 工作负载优化实例中持续部署至强处理器，并共同开发定制 ASIC 基础设施处理器（IPU）；至强 6 处理器同时确认进入英伟达 DGX Rubin NVL8 系统，担任主控 CPU。
  • Regarding its foundry business, Intel has expanded its packaging and testing capacity at its Penang, Malaysia factory and repurchased a 49% minority stake in the Fab 34 wafer factory joint venture in Ireland. 代工业务方面，英特尔扩大了马来西亚槟城工厂封装测试产能，并回购爱尔兰 Fab 34 晶圆厂合资企业中 49% 的少数股权权益。

Looking ahead to the second quarter of this year, Intel expects revenue to be between $13.8 billion and $14.8 billion, with non-GAAP earnings per share projected at $0.20. 展望今年第二季度，英特尔预计营收为 138 亿至 148 亿美元，非通用会计准则每股收益预计为 0.20 美元。

Apple Music 副总裁：AI 音乐投稿泛滥，但几乎没人听

According to AppleInsider, Apple Music is facing the challenge of a massive influx of AI-generated music, but listeners are not buying into it. 据 AppleInsider 报道，Apple Music 正面临 AI 生成音乐大量涌入的挑战，但听众对此并不买账。

Apple Music Vice President Oliver Schusser revealed in a recent interview that more than one-third of all music submitted to the platform is "100% AI-generated" content, yet AI music accounts for less than 0.5% of actual plays on Apple Music. Apple Music 副总裁 Oliver Schusser 在日前的采访中透露，目前提交至该平台的全部音乐中，超过三分之一属于「100% AI 生成」的内容，然而 AI 音乐在 Apple Music 上的实际播放占比却不到 0.5%。

The listening rate for AI music on Apple Music is indeed very low, rounding to less than 0.5%. AI 音乐在 Apple Music 上的收听率真的非常低，四舍五入不到 0.5%。

Schusser revealed that Apple has developed an internal detection system that can accurately identify the source AI model of submitted content, and also requires record labels and distributors to proactively disclose AI usage. In terms of anti-fraud, Apple's anti-fraud penalty mechanism has cumulatively reduced platform fraudulent behavior by approximately 60%. Schusser 透露，苹果已开发了一套内部检测系统，可精准识别提交内容的 AI 模型来源，同时要求唱片公司和分发商主动披露 AI 使用情况。在反欺诈方面，苹果的反欺诈处罚机制已使平台欺诈行为累计减少约 60%。

阿联酋要让 AI Agent 接管一半政府工作

Yesterday, Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, announced on X that the country has officially launched a new government operating model — within the next two years, 50% of UAE government departments, services, and operations will be fully driven by Agentic AI. 昨天，阿联酋副总统兼总理、迪拜酋长穆罕默德 · 本 · 拉希德 X 上宣布，该国正式启动一项全新政府运作模式 —— 未来两年内，阿联酋 50% 的政府部门、服务和运营将全面由 Agentic AI 驱动。

Mohammed emphasized that "AI is no longer just a tool." These AI agents will be positioned as the government's "executive partners," with core objectives focused on three dimensions: enhancing the quality of public services, accelerating decision-making processes, and comprehensively improving administrative efficiency. 穆罕默德强调「AI 不再是工具。」这种 AI 智能体将被定位为政府的「执行伙伴」，核心目标指向三个维度：提升公共服务质量、加速决策流程、全面提高行政效率。

To this end, the UAE government has set a strict timeline and quantifiable evaluation standards. Within the two-year window, government performance will be measured by three indicators: the speed of AI adoption, the quality of implementation, and the proficiency in using AI to reshape government workflows. 为此，阿联酋政府制定了严格的时间表和可量化评估标准。两年窗口期内，政府绩效将按三项指标进行衡量 —— AI 采用速度、实施质量以及在重塑政府工作流程中对 AI 的运用熟练度。

Mohammed stated that every federal employee will receive AI-related training, a move the UAE says is aimed at building one of the "world's strongest AI-driven government capabilities." 穆罕默德表示，每位联邦雇员都将接受 AI 相关培训，阿联酋方面称此举意在构建「全球最强的 AI 驱动型政府能力」之一。

💡 小米罗福莉：AGI 两年内实现，中美顶尖模型代差仅两三个月

Recently, Luo Fuli, head of Xiaomi's large model team, gave a series of specific judgments on the current competitive landscape of large models, the paradigm shift of AI agents, and the progress of AGI during an interview with "Language is the World." 近日，小米大模型团队负责人罗福莉在接受「语言即世界」访谈时，就当前大模型竞争格局、AI 智能体范式转变及 AGI 进程给出了一系列具体判断。

Luo Fuli predicts that AGI will be achieved within two years . She estimates that the current progress is about 20% complete, with hopes to advance to 60% to 70% this year. 罗福莉预计， AGI 将在两年内实现 。她估计当前进度已完成约 20%，今年有望推进至 60% 到 70%。

Luo Fuli stated that several domestic companies, including Kimi and MiMo, currently possess foundational models with over 1T parameters, and the gap between China and the United States in the pre-training phase "is basically non-existent." 罗福莉表示，目前国内已有包括 Kimi、MiMo 在内的多家公司具备 1T 参数以上的基座模型，中美两国在预训练阶段的差距「基本上已经没有」。

She believes that domestic teams actually have certain advantages in pre-training architecture. As long as they react quickly enough to the Agent paradigm, the generation gap between top domestic models and international cutting-edge models like Claude Opus 4.6 is actually only two or three months . 她认为，国内团队在预训练结构上反而存在一定优势，只要对 Agent 范式的反应速度足够快， 国内顶尖模型与 Claude Opus 4.6 等国际前沿模型之间的代差，实际上只有两三个月 。

"The next two or three months will be very exciting," she said, adding that this window period will be a critical test of each team's overall research level, technical agility, and ability to embrace new paradigms. 「接下来两三个月会非常精彩」，她说，这一窗口期将是对各家团队整体研究水平、技术敏捷度以及拥抱新范式能力的关键考验。

Luo Fuli also pointed out that the competition in large models has fully shifted from the pre-training-dominated Chat era to the post-training-dominated Agent era. 罗福莉同时指出，大模型竞争已从预训练主导的 Chat 时代，全面转向后训练主导的 Agent 时代。

A 1T parameter scale is currently the "entry ticket" to achieve near top-tier Agent capabilities, and the logic of computing power allocation has also reversed — the ratio of pre-training to post-training computing power investment by top teams has narrowed from the previous 5:1 to 1:1 this year. 1T 参数规模是当前实现接近顶尖 Agent 水平的「入场券」，而算力分配逻辑也随之逆转 —— 顶尖团队的预训练与后训练算力投入比例已从过去的 5:1，收窄至今年的 1:1。
