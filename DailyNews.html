曝 WWDC26「藏」了三项功能，折叠屏与触控屏 Mac 代码已就位

Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman revealed yesterday that Apple has several features that have completed development but were not announced at the recent WWDC26, and code evidence for a foldable iPhone and a touchscreen MacBook has already appeared in the first developer beta of iOS 27. 彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 在昨日发文披露，苹果有多项已完成开发的功能并未在日前的 WWDC26 上对外公布，并且 iOS 27 首个开发者测试版中也已出现折叠屏 iPhone 与触控屏 MacBook 的代码证据。

  • New Apple Watch face "Modular"; 新款 Apple Watch 表盘「Modular」（模块化）；
  • Camera App with customizable control interface; 可自定义操控界面的相机 App；
  • An open framework "Extensions" that supports third-party AI models connecting to Siri — Apple has discussed integration details with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google, and related settings panels and App Store sections are already built into the beta version, shielded by a backend switch. 支持第三方 AI 模型接入 Siri 的开放框架「扩展」——苹果已与 OpenAI、Anthropic 和 Google 就接入细节展开讨论，相关设置面板与 App Store 分区已内置于测试版，通过后端开关屏蔽。

Gurman believes the reasons for not disclosing the extensions include: EU regulatory pressure, avoiding overshadowing the launch of the new Siri, potential legal friction with OpenAI, and already complex external communication. He expects the feature to go live at some point in the future. Gurman 认为扩展未公开的原因包括：欧盟监管压力、避免盖过新版 Siri 的发布声量、与 OpenAI 之间的潜在法律摩擦，以及对外沟通已足够复杂。他预计该功能将在未来某个时间点上线。

He also stated on X that, in the long run, Apple might develop its own "OpenClaw competitor" to create an agent system capable of directly operating iPhones, iPads, and Macs. 他还在 X 上表示，从长远来看，苹果可能会开发自己的「OpenClaw 竞争对手」，打造一套能直接操作 iPhone、iPad 和 Mac 的智能体系统。

Additionally, the beta code also revealed signs of two new products: 另外，测试版代码还透露了两款新品的迹象：

  • Foldable iPhone: iPhone Mirroring supports widescreen iPad-style layout, the system can detect the device's folding angle, and there are references to multi-screen devices; 折叠屏 iPhone：iPhone Mirroring 支持宽屏 iPad 式布局、系统可检测设备折叠角度、存在多屏设备引用；
  • Touchscreen MacBook: New pull-to-refresh gesture, Sidecar supports full touch input, and the Mac version of Siri interface adopts a capsule shape similar to Dynamic Island. 触控屏 MacBook：新增下拉刷新手势、Sidecar 支持完整触控输入、Mac 版 Siri 界面采用类似灵动岛的胶囊造型。

Gurman previously reported that the foldable iPhone (internal codename V68) is expected to be released in September this year, and the high-end touchscreen MacBook (codenames K114/K116) is expected to be launched from late this year to early next year. Gurman 此前报道称，折叠屏 iPhone（内部代号 V68）预计今年 9 月发布，触控高端 MacBook（代号 K114/K116）预计于今年底至明年初推出。

一汽大众为速腾空调喷粉致歉并提供 15 年担保

According to Sina Technology, FAW-Volkswagen has issued "A Letter to Customers" regarding the issue of white powder being sprayed from the air conditioning vents, as reported by some Sagitar owners, stating that they will provide free air conditioning system inspections. 据新浪科技报道，一汽大众已就部分速腾车主反馈的空调出风口喷出白色粉末问题发布《致客户的一封信》，称将提供免费空调系统检测。

FAW-Volkswagen stated that analysis shows the phenomenon is related to the accumulation of original hydrophilic coating liquid in some air conditioner evaporators, with the excess liquid forming white particles after drying. Third-party testing indicates that the main component of the white particles is polyvinyl alcohol. The affected vehicles are primarily some Sagitar models produced from September 2025 to January 2026. 一汽大众表示，经分析，该现象与部分空调蒸发器亲水涂层原液积存有关，多余原液干燥后形成白色颗粒。第三方机构检测显示，白色颗粒主要成分为聚乙烯醇。涉及车辆主要为 2025 年 9 月至 2026 年 1 月生产的部分速腾。

The company stated that if a problem is found during inspection, they will replace the air conditioning evaporator with a brand new original part free of charge and provide a 15-year quality guarantee for the entire air conditioning assembly. The relevant free inspection services and rights protection are valid long-term, and car owners can contact an authorized FAW-Volkswagen dealer to schedule an inspection. 公司称，如检测发现问题，将免费更换全新原厂空调蒸发器，并提供空调器总成 15 年质量担保。相关免费检修服务和权益保障长期有效，车主可联系一汽大众授权经销商预约检测。

KPMG 一份报告被指充斥 AI 幻觉内容，45 条引用中仅 5 条真实

According to Engadget, a report titled "Total Experience: Redefining Excellence in the Age of Agentic AI" published last October by KPMG, one of the global "Big Four" professional services and accounting firms, was found to be riddled with AI hallucination content, including false citations, fabricated cases, and incorrect attributions. 据 Engadget 报道，全球「四大」专业服务和会计事务所之一 KPMG 于去年 10 月发布的一份题为《Total Experience: Redefining Excellence in the Age of Agentic AI》的报告被发现充斥着大量 AI 幻觉内容，包括虚假引用、捏造案例及错误归因。

An investigation by AIGC content detection platform GPTZero revealed that out of 45 citations in the report, only 5 accurately pointed to real sources; 28 had their titles rewritten or had fictitious content added; and another 12 were too vague to determine their authenticity. AIGC 内容检测平台 GPTZero 的调查显示，报告中 45 条引用里，仅有 5 条准确指向真实来源；28 条对标题进行了改写或添加了虚构内容；另有 12 条表述过于模糊，无法判断其真实性。

At the level of specific facts, the investigation found that about half of the claims in the report contained false or misattributed information, which GPTZero believes "is likely due to AI research tools over-catering to the instruction to 'find real-world examples of AI agents'." 在具体事实层面，调查发现报告中约半数主张存在虚假或归因错误的问题，GPTZero 认为这「很可能是 AI 研究工具过度迎合『寻找 AI 智能体现实案例』的指令所导致的」。

Among them, the report claimed that Emirates launched a mobile chatbot named Sara, which could converse with passengers and modify flights; however, Sara is actually a mobile assistant launched in 2023 and does not have such booking modification capabilities. KPMG stated that it values content accuracy and completeness, has withdrawn the report, and is reviewing its publication process. 其中，报告称阿联酋航空推出名为 Sara 的移动聊天机器人，可与乘客对话并修改航班；但 Sara 实际是 2023 年上线的移动助手，不具备这类订票变更能力；KPMG 方面称重视内容准确性和完整性，已撤下该报告并审查发布经过。
