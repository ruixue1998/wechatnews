DeepSeek-R1 论文登上 Nature 封面

Yesterday, the latest issue of 《Nature》 featured research from the DeepSeek team on its cover — "DeepSeek-R1: Incentivizing Reasoning Capability in LLMs via Reinforcement Learning." The corresponding author of this paper is Liang Wenfeng . 昨天，最新一期《Nature》封面刊登了 DeepSeek 团队的研究成果 ——「DeepSeek-R1: Incentivizing Reasoning Capability in LLMs via Reinforcement Learning」。该论文的通讯作者为 梁文锋 。

In its recommended introduction, 《Nature》 points out that if large models can plan the steps needed to solve a problem, they often perform tasks better. This "reasoning" approach is similar to how humans tackle complex problems but is extremely challenging for artificial intelligence. DeepSeek-R1's research demonstrates how models can gradually learn to reason through reinforcement learning with minimal human annotation. 在推荐介绍中，《Nature》指出，如果大模型能够规划解决问题所需的步骤，它们往往能更好地完成任务。这种「推理」方式与人类处理复杂问题的思路类似，但对人工智能而言极具挑战。DeepSeek-R1 的研究展示了如何在极少人工标注的情况下，通过强化学习让模型逐步学会推理。

DeepSeek-R1's training method is as follows: the model receives a reward when it correctly solves a mathematical problem and a penalty when it answers incorrectly. As training progresses, the model not only gradually learns to reason but also develops self-verification and reflection capabilities, thereby demonstrating stronger performance in programming and graduate-level scientific problems. DeepSeek-R1 的训练方式是：模型在正确解答数学问题时获得奖励，答错则受到惩罚。随着训练深入，模型不仅能逐步推理，还具备了自我验证与反思能力，从而在编程和研究生水平的科学问题上展现出更强表现。

It is worth noting that R1 is considered the first large language model to pass peer review by an authoritative academic journal. Hugging Face engineer Lewis Tunstall commented: "This is a welcome precedent. Without such norms for openly sharing the R&D process, it would be difficult for us to assess the potential risks of these systems." 值得注意的是，R1 被认为是首个通过权威学术期刊同行评审的大语言模型。Hugging Face 工程师 Lewis Tunstall 评论称：「这是一个备受欢迎的先例。如果缺乏这种公开分享研发过程的规范，我们将很难评估这些系统的潜在风险。」

Huan Sun, a researcher at Ohio State University, also stated: "Undergoing rigorous peer review undoubtedly effectively verifies the model's reliability and practical value. Other companies should also follow suit." 俄亥俄州立大学研究员 Huan Sun 也表示：「经历严格的同行评审，无疑能有效验证模型的可靠性与实用价值。其他公司也应效仿此举。」

Industry insiders believe that this is not only a scientific breakthrough but also an important step for the AI industry towards "scientific discipline." The peer review mechanism will force companies to shift from "self-promotion" to "evidence and reproducibility," helping to curb industry chaos and build public trust. 业内人士认为，这不仅是一次科研突破，更是 AI 行业迈向「科学纪律」的重要一步。同行评审机制将迫使企业从「自我宣传」转向「证据与复现」，有助于遏制行业乱象并建立公众信任。

工信微报：一定程度帮驾驶者完成「开车」才算驾驶自动化

Yesterday, the official government new media account of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, "MIIT Micro-Report," published an article explaining related questions such as "What is 'driving assistance'?" and "What constitutes 'autonomous driving'?" 昨日，工业和信息化部官方政务新媒体账号「工信微报」发布发文，解释了「什么是『驾驶辅助』？」「怎样才算『自动驾驶』？」等相关问题。

The article points out that "driving automation" means the vehicle can assist the driver to a certain extent in completing the task of "driving," ranging from reminding the driver of前方 risks, to assisting with following and lane changes, and even completely driving the vehicle under certain conditions, all falling within the scope of "driving automation." 文章中指出，「驾驶自动化」就是车辆能够在一定程度上帮助驾驶员完成「开车」这项任务，从提醒驾驶员注意前方风险，到辅助跟车、辅助换道，甚至在某些条件下完全驾驶车辆，都属于「驾驶自动化」的范畴。

The MIIT Micro-Report states that, according to national standards, the core distinctions in driving automation levels are threefold: the extent to which the driving automation system can perform dynamic driving tasks, the allocation of roles between the system and the driver, and the presence or absence of design operational domain limitations. Simply put, the core question is whether "driving" is the responsibility of a human or the system. 工信微报表示，根据国家标准，驾驶自动化等级的核心区别有三个方面：驾驶自动化系统能够执行动态驾驶任务的程度、系统与驾驶人之间的角色分配、有无设计运行范围限制。简单来说，核心就是「开车」这件事到底是人负责，还是系统负责。

Currently, according to national standards, driving automation is divided into six levels, from L0 to L5: 目前，根据国家标准，驾驶自动化分成 L0 至 L5 六个等级：

  • L0: The system can detect the external driving environment or the status of occupants inside the vehicle, providing safety-related prompts and warnings, requiring the driver's attention or appropriate action; or, in some emergency situations, the system performs brief lateral or longitudinal motion control. (e.g., lane departure warning, automatic emergency braking, etc.) L0：系统可探测车外驾驶环境或车内驾乘人员状态，提供与安全性相关的提示、预警，需要驾驶人注意或做出相应的处置；或在部分紧急情况下系统进行短暂横向或纵向运动控制。（如车道偏离预警、自动紧急制动等）
  • L1: Under specific design operating conditions, the system can provide continuous motion assistance control for the vehicle in a single direction, either laterally or longitudinally, reducing some of the driver's burden; the driver cannot disengage from vehicle control and must remain constantly aware of the driving environment to respond to any unexpected situations. (e.g., lane centering control, adaptive cruise control, etc.) L1：在特定设计运行条件下，系统可在横向或纵向为车辆提供单一方向的持续的运动辅助控制，能减轻驾驶人部分驾驶负担；驾驶人不能脱离对于车辆的控制，仍需时刻关注驾驶环境，应对任何突发情况。（如车道居中控制、自适应巡航控制等）
  • L2: Under specific design operating conditions, the system can simultaneously provide continuous lateral and longitudinal motion assistance control, reducing some of the driver's burden; the driver still needs to constantly monitor the driving environment, maintain control of the vehicle, and respond to any unexpected situations. (e.g., lever-actuated lane change assist, navigation-assisted driving assist, etc.) L2：在特定设计运行条件下，系统能同时提供持续的横向和纵向运动辅助控制，能减轻驾驶人部分驾驶负担；驾驶人仍需要时刻关注驾驶环境，保持对车辆的控制，应对任何突发情况。（如拨杆换道辅助、领航组合驾驶辅助等）
  • L3: Under specific design operating conditions, the system can achieve autonomous driving, meaning it performs all driving tasks, thereby allowing the user to disengage from driving tasks under those conditions; the user must still maintain the ability to take over and promptly respond to special situations that the system cannot handle. L3：在特定设计运行条件下，系统可实现自动驾驶，即执行全部驾驶任务，从而让用户在该条件下从驾驶任务中脱离出来；用户仍要保持接管能力，及时应对系统无法处理的特殊情况。
  • L4: Under specific design operating conditions, the system can achieve autonomous driving without user intervention and can automatically reach a minimum risk state when necessary. It has two modes: without human driving controls (the user is merely a passenger, with no possibility of driving the vehicle) and with human driving controls (although user intervention is not required, the user can manually drive the vehicle if needed). L4：在特定设计运行条件下，系统可实现自动驾驶，无需用户接管，并能在必要时自动达到最小风险状态。其具有两种模式：无人工驾驶装置（用户只是乘客身份，不存在驾驶车辆的可能性）和配备人工驾驶装置（虽然不需要用户接管，用户可以在需要时人工驾驶车辆）。
  • L5: Under any drivable conditions, the system can achieve autonomous driving without user intervention and can reach a minimum risk state when necessary; the system is not restricted by design operating domains; its performance in general driving processes is like that of a professional driver; and in terms of continuous driving and emergency handling, it surpasses the limits achievable by humans. L5：在任何可行驶条件下，系统均可实现自动驾驶，无需用户接管，并能在必要时达到最小风险状态；系统不受设计运行范围限制；在一般驾驶过程中的表现犹如一个专业驾驶人；在持续驾驶和应急情况处理方面则要超越人类能够达到的极限。

影片《731》预售总票房突破亿

据灯塔专业版，截至 9 月 16 日 18 时 44 分，影片《731》预售总票房突破 1 亿，同时也成为今年春节档后首部预售票房突破 1 亿（不含点映）的影片。

该片主要讲述了在抗战胜利前夕，侵华日军为了扭转战局在我国东北哈尔滨平房区开展细菌战研究，屠戮百姓进行人体实验的故事。

《731》由山东省委宣传部、吉林省委宣传部、黑龙江省委宣传部、哈尔滨市委宣传部、青岛市委宣传部联合制作，长春电影集团等联合出品，赵林山执导，赵林山、刘恒编剧，姜武、王志文、李乃文领衔主演。
