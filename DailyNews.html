曝苹果新 CEO 正在重组设计团队

According to Bloomberg, John Ternus, who is set to take over as Apple CEO in September this year, is reorganizing the industrial design team, which has been plagued by talent drain, in an effort to restore product design to its core decision-making position within the company. 据彭博社报道，即将于今年 9 月接任苹果 CEO 的约翰·特努斯（John Ternus）正着手重组陷入人才流失的工业设计团队，以期恢复产品设计在公司内部的核心决策地位。

According to reports, CEO-designate Ternus has personally intervened and taken over the supervision of Apple's design team. 据报道，候任 CEO 特努斯已亲自介入并接管苹果设计团队的督导工作。

At recent internal meetings, he explicitly stated that design is at the core of Apple's business and that the company will continue to focus on ensuring the delivery of products with industry-leading design standards to users. 其在近期的内部会议上明确表态，设计是苹果业务的核心，未来将持续聚焦并确保向用户交付具备行业顶级设计标准的产品。

Ternus plans to overturn the existing management structure after officially taking office, seek a new design head who can reshape the organizational structure, and restore the position's direct seat in Apple's core executive team. 特努斯计划在正式上任后推翻现有的管理架构，寻找一位能够重塑组织结构的新任设计主管，并恢复该岗位在苹果核心高管团队中的直属席位。

Currently, Apple's Industrial Design (ID) department is led by veteran designer Molly Anderson, who previously lacked experience in managing large teams; meanwhile, the software interface design department, after former head Alan Dye led part of the team to Meta, is now overseen by Steve Lemay. 目前，苹果工业设计（ID）部门由资深设计师莫莉·安德森（Molly Anderson）领导，但其此前缺乏大型团队管理经验；同时，软件界面设计部门在原负责人艾伦·戴伊（Alan Dye）带领部分团队跳槽加盟 Meta 后，现由史蒂夫·勒梅（Steve Lemay）接管。

Due to management turmoil and insufficient core talent reserves, the pace of aesthetic iteration for several of Apple's main product lines, including iPhone, Apple Watch, AirPods, and Mac, has significantly slowed in recent years. 受制于管理层动荡与核心人才储备不足，苹果旗下 iPhone、Apple Watch、AirPods 及 Mac 等多条主力产品线的外观迭代速度近年来已显著放缓。

OpenAI 上调收入预期，但预计 2030 年前多消耗 1110 亿美元现金

According to The Information, OpenAI recently raised its revenue forecast for the next five years, expecting revenue from businesses such as ChatGPT subscriptions, AI models, advertising, and hardware to be about 27% higher than previously projected. 据 The Information 报道，OpenAI 近期上调未来五年收入预期，预计来自 ChatGPT 订阅、AI 模型、广告和硬件等业务的收入将较此前预测高出约 27%。

However, the company also expects cash consumption to increase by $111 billion by 2030 compared to previous forecasts, with $665 billion to be invested in AI operation and training costs. 但公司同时预计，到 2030 年前现金消耗将较此前预测增加 1110 亿美元，并将在 AI 运行和训练成本上投入 6650 亿美元。

The report mentioned that OpenAI's revenue last year was $13.1 billion, is expected to increase to $30 billion this year, and approximately $62 billion next year. 报道提到，OpenAI 去年收入为 131 亿美元，今年预计增至 300 亿美元，明年约为 620 亿美元。

In terms of costs, OpenAI's cash consumption last year was $8 billion, is expected to rise to $25 billion this year, and further increase to $57 billion next year. Its AI model inference costs grew approximately fourfold last year, and its adjusted gross margin fell from 40% in the previous year to 33%, lower than the previously expected 46%. OpenAI still expects to achieve positive cash flow by 2030. 成本方面，OpenAI 去年现金消耗为 80 亿美元，今年预计升至 250 亿美元，明年进一步增至 570 亿美元。其 AI 模型推理成本去年增长约 4 倍，调整后毛利率从前一年的 40% 降至 33%，低于此前 46% 的预期。OpenAI 仍预计将在 2030 年实现现金流转正。

In terms of user growth, ChatGPT recently reached 910 million weekly active users, falling short of OpenAI's previous goal of reaching 1 billion by the end of last year. The company expects ChatGPT's weekly active users to reach 2.75 billion by 2030. 用户增长方面，ChatGPT 近期周活跃用户达到 9.1 亿，低于 OpenAI 此前希望去年底达到 10 亿的目标。公司预计，到 2030 年 ChatGPT 周活跃用户将达到 27.5 亿。

刘强东：未来不再需要快递员，京东拟培训 70 万蓝领员工转岗

According to Sina Finance, at yesterday's 2026 APEC China Business Leaders Forum, JD.com founder Richard Liu revealed that JD.com recently proposed an internal 'Nirvana Plan,' hoping to send its existing 700,000 blue-collar employees, such as delivery personnel, to schools for technical training to cope with the impact of AI and technological development. 据新浪财经报道，在昨日的 2026 年 APEC 工商领导人中国论坛上，京东集团创始人刘强东透露，京东近期在内部提出「涅槃计划」，希望将已有的 70 万快递员等蓝领员工送到学校接受技术培训，以应对 AI 和技术发展带来的冲击。

Richard Liu stated that future delivery work will be completed by robots, 'in the future, robots will deliver everything, there will be no need for delivery personnel at all,' but JD.com does not want its 700,000 employees to lose their jobs because of this. 刘强东表示，未来配送工作将由机器人完成，「将来都是机器人送货了，根本不需要快递员」，但京东并不希望 70 万名员工因此失去工作。

To this end, JD.com has signed contracts with 120 schools nationwide, planning to send relevant employees for training, who can then engage in work such as robot repair and maintenance in the future. 为此，京东已在全国签约 120 所学校，计划将相关员工送去接受培训，未来可从事机器人维修、保养等工作。

He mentioned that machines can also malfunction and still require human maintenance. Through training, JD.com hopes to encourage some blue-collar employees to transition to technical positions, 'allowing them to work in offices, no longer having to brave the wind and rain.' 他提到，机器也可能发生故障，仍需要人员进行维护。通过培训，京东希望推动部分蓝领员工转向技术岗位，「让他们住在办公室里面，不用再风里来雨里去」。

Richard Liu also stated that when applying new technologies, enterprises should make human lives better and work more interesting, rather than letting technology deprive humans of their rights. 刘强东还表示，企业在应用新技术时，应让人类生活变得更好、工作变得更有趣，而不是让技术剥夺人类的权利。

挪威拟禁止小学生使用生成式 AI

According to Reuters, the Norwegian government will almost completely ban primary school students from using generative AI tools and restrict their use in secondary schools starting from the new academic year in late August. 据路透社报道，挪威政府将从 8 月下旬新学年开始，近乎禁止小学阶段学生使用生成式 AI 工具，并限制中学阶段的相关使用。

Norwegian Prime Minister Jonas Gahr Stoere stated that the school's most important task is to teach children to read, write, and do math, and uncritical use of AI would cause students to skip critical learning steps. The Norwegian government had previously banned the use of smartphones in schools in 2024 and granted teachers more authority over classroom discipline. 挪威首相 Jonas Gahr Stoere 表示，学校最重要的任务是让孩子学会阅读、写作和数学，无批判地使用 AI 会让学生跳过关键学习步骤。挪威政府此前已在 2024 年禁止学校使用智能手机，并赋予教师更多课堂纪律管理权。

The new rules apply to grades 1 to 7, meaning students aged approximately 6 to 13 are generally prohibited from using generative AI; older students may still use it in more controlled teaching scenarios. 新规适用于 1 至 7 年级，约 6 至 13 岁学生原则上不得使用生成式 AI；高年级学生仍可在更受控的教学场景中使用。

Compared to Kimi K2.7 Code, GLM-5.2's Pass@1 is 13 percentage points higher, but its average cost is also higher than the latter's $2.82. 相比 Kimi K2.7 Code，GLM-5.2 的 Pass@1 高出 13 个百分点，但平均成本也高于后者的 2.82 美元。

央视曝光部分廉价充电宝 CCC 认证失效后仍在售

According to CCTV News, after reporters randomly investigated 7 merchants, they found that 4 of them were still selling power banks without valid CCC certification. China has included power banks in the scope of CCC certified products since 2024, and power banks without CCC certification cannot be shipped or sold. 据央视新闻报道，记者随机调查 7 家商户后发现，其中 4 家仍在销售没有有效 CCC 认证的充电宝。我国自 2024 年起已将充电宝纳入 CCC 认证产品范围，没有 CCC 认证的充电宝不能出厂和销售。

The report mentioned that the CCC certification marked on the detail page of power banks sold by a certain platform store has been canceled; after the reporter received the goods, it was found that the CCC certification certificates corresponding to the manufacturers marked on the products had also been canceled. 报道提到，某平台店铺销售的充电宝详情页标注的 CCC 认证已被注销；记者收货后发现，产品上标注的制造商对应的 CCC 认证证书也均已注销。

Subsequently, reporters sent several power banks without valid CCC certification certificates to the Safety Center Laboratory of the China Electronics Technology Standardization Institute for testing. 随后，记者把多款没有有效 CCC 认证证书的充电宝送至中国电子技术标准化研究院安全中心实验室检测。

Laboratory disassembly revealed that some power bank internal cells did not have manufacturer information printed as required; in the shell flame retardancy performance test, some products could not slow down combustion and might even become combustion aids. Experts stated that CCC certification certificates are not permanently valid, and continued sales after a certificate is suspended, revoked, or canceled are the current focus of industry rectification. 实验室拆解发现，部分充电宝内部电芯没有按要求印刷生产厂家信息；外壳阻燃性能测试中，部分产品无法减缓燃烧，甚至可能成为助燃物。专家称，CCC 认证证书并非永久有效，证书暂停、撤销或注销后继续销售，是当前行业整治重点。
