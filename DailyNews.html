曝 DeepSeek V4 四月亮相，但「很难是碾压级的强」

According to LatePost, DeepSeek's next-generation flagship model V4 is expected to be released in April this year. In January this year, a small-parameter version of V4 was leaked to some open-source framework communities for adaptation. The large-parameter version was originally scheduled to be launched around the Spring Festival but was ultimately postponed. 据晚点 LatePost 报道，DeepSeek 的下一代旗舰模型 V4 预计将于今年 4 月发布。今年 1 月，V4 的小参数版本已流出至部分开源框架社区进行适配，大参数版此前原定于春节前后上线，但最终推迟。

It is worth noting that the report points out that V4 will most likely still be the strongest model in the open-source domain, but "it's hard to be overwhelmingly strong" . 值得注意的是，报道指出， V4 大概率仍将是开源领域最强模型，但「很难是碾压级的强」 。

As AI evaluation standards become increasingly diverse, Benchmark scores can no longer fully measure model capabilities, especially in Agentic application scenarios, where the importance of product reach and long-tail data is increasingly prominent—which is precisely an area where DeepSeek had limited investment previously. 随着 AI 评测标准愈发多元，Benchmark 分数已难以全面衡量模型能力，尤其在 Agentic 应用场景下，产品触手和长尾数据的重要性日益凸显——而这恰恰是 DeepSeek 此前投入有限的领域。

The report also mentioned DeepSeek's 'anti-involution' culture: most employees leave the company between 6 and 7 PM on weekdays, don't clock in in the morning, and have no clear performance reviews or deadlines. 报道还提到 DeepSeek 的「反内卷」文化：多数员工平日在下午 6 ~ 7 点离开公司，早上也不打卡，且没有明确的绩效考核和截止时间。

Founder Liang Wenfeng's reason is straightforward: 'It's hard for a person to work effectively for more than 6 to 8 hours a day. Poor judgment due to overtime fatigue would instead waste valuable computing resources, which is not worth it.' 创始人梁文锋的理由直接：「一个人每天能高质量工作的时间很难超过 6 ~ 8 小时，加班疲劳下的昏庸判断反而会浪费宝贵的算力资源，得不偿失。」

XREAL 递交港股 IPO 申请

Google 开源 Gemma 4 系列模型

Early this morning, Google DeepMind released the new generation of open-source model series Gemma 4, launching four models at once, covering all scenarios from edge devices to workstations. 今天凌晨，Google DeepMind 发布了新一代开源模型系列 Gemma 4，一次性推出四款覆盖端侧到工作站全场景的模型。

– E2B : 5.1 billion total parameters, 2.3 billion effective parameters, 128K context, officially stated that memory usage on some devices can be reduced to below 1.5GB; – E2B ：51 亿总参数、23 亿有效参数，128K 上下文，官方称部分设备内存占用可压至 1.5GB 以下；

– E4B : 8 billion total parameters, 4.5 billion effective parameters, 128K context, MMLU Pro reaches 69.4%, close to the previous generation's 27B level; – E4B ：80 亿总参数、45 亿有效参数，128K 上下文，MMLU Pro 达 69.4%，接近上一代 27B 水平；

– 26B A4B MoE : 25.2 billion total parameters, only 3.8 billion active parameters, inference speed close to 4B models, ranked sixth on the Arena AI open-source leaderboard; – 26B A4B MoE ：252 亿总参数、仅 38 亿激活参数，推理速度接近 4B 模型，Arena AI 开源榜排名第六；

– 31B Dense : 31 billion parameters fully activated, 256K context, ranked third on the Arena AI open-source leaderboard. – 31B Dense ：310 亿参数全激活，256K 上下文，Arena AI 开源榜排名第三。

In terms of performance, there's a significant improvement compared to the previous generation Gemma 3 27B. The 31B scored 89.2% in the AIME 2026 math test (up from 20.8% for the previous generation), jumped from 29.1% to 80.0% in the LiveCodeBench v6 code test, and improved from 13.5% to 66.4% in long document processing MRCR v2 128K. 性能方面，与上一代 Gemma 3 27B 相比提升显著。31B 在 AIME 2026 数学测试中得分 89.2%（上一代为 20.8%），LiveCodeBench v6 代码测试从 29.1% 跃升至 80.0%，长文档处理 MRCR v2 128K 从 13.5% 提升至 66.4%。

All models support image and video input, over 140 languages, and have a built-in switchable thinking mode. E2B and E4B are additionally equipped with an audio encoder of approximately 300 million parameters, supporting up to 30 seconds of speech recognition and translation, and can run completely offline on mobile phones, Raspberry Pi, and NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano. 所有模型均支持图像和视频输入及 140 多种语言，并内置可开关的思考模式。E2B 和 E4B 额外搭载约 3 亿参数的音频编码器，支持最长 30 秒的语音识别与翻译，可在手机、树莓派及 NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano 上完全离线运行。

Notably, the Gemma series' open-source license has fully switched to Apache 2.0, allowing developers to freely modify, distribute, and commercialize it without user volume restrictions. Hugging Face co-founder Clément Delangue described this as a "major milestone." 值得注意的是，Gemma 系列开源协议此次全面切换至 Apache 2.0，开发者可自由修改、分发及商用，无用户量门槛限制。Hugging Face 联合创始人 Clément Delangue 对此评价为「重大里程碑」。

广电联合会演员委员会发声明：AI 换脸、声纹克隆须授权，「二创」不免责

According to a report by "People's Daily," the Actors Committee of the China Association of Radio and Television Social Organizations issued a solemn statement yesterday, collectively addressing infringing acts such as AI face-swapping and voice cloning, demanding a comprehensive regulation of the application boundaries of AI technology in the performing arts field: 据《人民日报》报道，中国广播电视社会组织联合会演员委员会于昨日发布严正声明，针对 AI 换脸、声纹克隆等侵权行为集中表态，要求全面规范 AI 技术在演艺领域的应用边界：

  • Rights Protection : Performing artists legally enjoy personality rights such as portrait rights, voice rights, and artistic image rights. Any entity is strictly prohibited from unauthorized collection, use, synthesis, or dissemination of related images, voiceprints, and artistic images without formal written authorization from the individual; 权益保护 ：演艺人员依法享有肖像权、声音权、艺术形象权等人格权益，任何主体未经本人书面正式授权，严禁擅自采集、使用、合成或传播相关影像、声纹与艺术形象；
  • "Secondary Creation" Not Exempt from Liability : Infringing content such as AI face-matching, voice imitation performances, face-swapping short dramas, and virtual human replication, even if labeled with phrases like "non-commercial," "public sharing," or "personal secondary creation," still bears full responsibility for infringement; 「二创」不免责 ：AI 撞脸、仿声演绎、换脸短剧、虚拟人复刻等侵权内容，即便标注「非商用」「公益分享」「个人二创」等字样，仍需承担全部侵权责任；
  • Platform Responsibility : Online platforms such as short video, live streaming, and film/television distribution must establish and improve AI performing arts content authorization verification mechanisms, immediately investigate and remove existing infringing works, and retain infringement data to assist with evidence collection and traceability; 平台责任 ：短视频、直播、影视分发等网络平台须建立健全 AI 演艺内容授权核验机制，立即排查并下架存量侵权作品，留存侵权数据配合取证溯源；
  • Technical Platform Compliance : AI technology research and development and service platforms must strengthen their pre-screening obligations, verifying the authorization qualifications for actor portraits, audio, and other materials uploaded by users; 技术平台合规 ：AI 技术研发及服务平台须强化前置审核义务，对用户上传的演员肖像、声频等素材进行授权资质核验；
  • Batch Rights Protection : The Actors Committee of the China Association of Radio and Television will launch a network-wide regular infringement monitoring, notarization and evidence preservation, and batch rights protection campaign, pursuing civil, administrative, and even criminal liability against malicious infringing individuals, organizations, and platforms that fail to fulfill their review obligations; 批量维权 ：广电联合会演员委员会将启动全网常态化侵权监测、公证固证及批量维权行动，对恶意侵权个人、机构及未尽审核义务的平台，依法追究民事、行政乃至刑事责任；
  • Compliance Advocacy : Support AI technology in empowering innovation in film, television, and arts under the premise of compliance, advocating for the industry to establish unified and transparent authorization standards and revenue distribution mechanisms for portraits, voiceprints, and artistic images. 合规倡导 ：支持 AI 技术在合规前提下赋能影视文艺创新，倡导行业建立统一透明的肖像、声纹及艺术形象授权标准与收益分配机制。

On April 2nd, at the Hygon Information 2026 Spring Technology Communication Conference, Hygon officially unveiled several new achievements based on the concept of "inherent security" and released the annual version of the Hygon DCU software stack, providing computing power support for the training of multiple trillion-parameter large models in China. 4 月 2 日，在海光信息2026年春季技术沟通会上，海光正式公开基于「内生安全」理念的多项新成果，并首发海光 DCU 软件栈年度版本，为国内多个万亿参数大模型训练提供算力支撑。

华境 S 超前达标行业首个 L2 强标，全系标配华为乾崑智驾 ADS 4 Pro

Huajing S announced yesterday that its Huawei Qiankun Smart Driving ADS 4 Pro, standard across all models, has preemptively met all performance requirements of the draft mandatory national standard "Safety Requirements for Intelligent Connected Vehicle Combined Driving Assistance Systems." 华境 S 昨日宣布，其全系标配的华为乾崑智驾 ADS 4 Pro 已超前满足《智能网联汽车 组合驾驶辅助系统安全要求》强制性国家标准征求意见稿的全部性能要求。

美团发布音频生成模型 LongCat-AudioDiT

Meituan Longcat yesterday released the audio generation model LongCat-AudioDiT, and simultaneously open-sourced two versions, 1B and 3.5B. 美团龙猫昨日发布音频生成模型 LongCat-AudioDiT，并同步开源 1B 和 3.5B 两个版本。

According to reports, LongCat-AudioDiT performs diffusion modeling directly in the waveform latent space. The entire process only requires one Waveform Variational Autoencoder (Wav-VAE) and one Diffusion Transformer (DiT), fundamentally eliminating error accumulation caused by multi-stage cascading. 据介绍，LongCat-AudioDiT 直接在波形潜空间进行扩散建模，整个流程仅需一个波形变分自编码器（Wav-VAE）和一个扩散 Transformer（DiT），从根源上消除了多阶段级联带来的误差累积。

  • Training-Inference Alignment : In each inference step, the latent variables in the prompt region are forcibly reset to ground truth, solving the long-standing timbre drift problem. 训练-推理对齐 ：在每步推理中将提示区域的隐变量强制重置为真值，解决长期存在的音色漂移问题。
  • Adaptive Projection Guidance (APG) : Replaces traditional Classifier-Free Guidance (CFG), decomposing the guidance signal into orthogonal and parallel components, retaining beneficial parts and suppressing deteriorating parts, thereby improving timbre similarity while avoiding spectral 'oversaturation'. 自适应投影引导（APG） ：取代传统的无分类器引导（CFG），将引导信号分解为正交与平行分量，保留有益部分、抑制劣化部分，在提升音色相似度的同时避免频谱「过饱和」。

In the Seed benchmark test, LongCat-AudioDiT-3.5B achieved a speaker similarity (SIM) of 0.818 on the Chinese test set (Seed-ZH) and 0.797 on the Chinese difficult sentence set (Seed-Hard), surpassing models such as Seed-TTS, CosyVoice3.5, and MiniMax-Speech, achieving the current state-of-the-art (SOTA) results. 在 Seed 基准测试中，LongCat-AudioDiT-3.5B 的说话人相似度（SIM）在中文测试集（Seed-ZH）达到 0.818，中文难句集（Seed-Hard）达到 0.797，超越 Seed-TTS、CosyVoice3.5、MiniMax-Speech 等模型，取得当前最优（SOTA）成绩。
