简配版 Model Y/3 正式发布

Yesterday, Tesla officially released its new Model Y/3 vehicle — "Standard" (Standard Edition) — in the North American market. First, let's look at the prices: 昨天，特斯拉正式在北美市场发布了旗下 Model Y/3 全新车型 ——「Standard」（标准版），先看售价：

  • Model Y Standard is priced at $39,990 USD (approximately 284,000 RMB) Model Y Standard 价格为 39990 美元（约合人民币 28.4 万元）
  • Model 3 Standard is priced at $36,990 USD (approximately 263,000 RMB) Model 3 Standard 价格为 36990 美元（约合人民币 26.3 万元）

With the introduction of the Standard version, Tesla has also adjusted its vehicle naming system. Above the entry-level Standard version is the Premium (Enhanced) version, which was previously the Long Range version, and above that is the Performance version. 随着标准版的出现，特斯拉对车辆的命名体系也做了调整，在入门标准版之上是 Premium （臻享） 版本，即原来的 Long Range（长续航版），再往上则是 Performance（性能版）。

In terms of exterior, the Model 3 Standard version has not undergone significant adjustments; the Model Y Standard version, however, has removed the iconic full-width light bar. Standard models offer 18/19-inch wheels, and body colors are limited to black, white, and gray. Additionally, the side mirrors are now manually folding. 外观方面，Model 3 标准版并未进行大幅度调整；Model Y 标准版则取消了标志的贯穿式灯带。标准版车型提供 18/19 寸轮毂，车身颜色则仅有黑白灰三种颜色可选。另外，后视镜变为手动折叠。

Regarding the interior, the Standard version's seat material has been changed to fabric, retaining front seat heating but removing ventilation; steering wheel adjustment is now manual; the rear touch control screen has been removed and replaced with manual air vents; all speakers are 7 (subwoofer removed); HEPA filter and Bioweapon Defense Mode have been removed. 内饰方面，标准版车型座椅材质更换为了织物材质，保留前排座椅加热，取消通风；方向盘调节更换为手动；后排触摸控制屏取消，变为手动空调出风口；扬声器均为 7 个（取消低音炮）；移除 HEPA 过滤器和生物武器防御模式。

It's worth noting that the Model Y Standard version has removed the glass roof and electric rear seat folding; while the Model 3 Standard version retains its original roof design. 值得一提的是，Model Y 标准版取消掉了玻璃天幕以及电动后排座椅折叠；而 Model 3 标准版则保留了原来的天幕设计。

In terms of performance, all Standard models feature a rear-wheel-drive motor, with 0-100 km/h acceleration times of 6.8 seconds (Model Y) and 5.8 seconds (Model 3), respectively; the Standard battery uses 69.5kWh, providing a range of 516 kilometers, and the peak charging power is limited to 225kW. 性能方面，标准版车型均为后驱电机，零百加速分别为 6.8 秒（Model Y）和 5.8 秒（Model 3）；标准版电池采用 69.5kWh，续航里程为 516 公里，充电峰值功率被控制在 225kW。

Compared to the original version, the two new cars have been reduced in price by approximately $5,000 and $5,500 respectively. Proportionally, their starting prices in the Chinese market might drop to around 230,000 yuan and 200,000 yuan respectively. 相较原版本，两款新车分别降价约 5000 美元和 5500 美元。若按比例推算，其在中国市场的起售价可能分别下探至 23 万元与 20 万元左右。

2025 年诺贝尔化学奖公布

On October 8, the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences announced that the 2025 Nobel Prize in Chemistry would be awarded to Susumu Kitagawa, Richard Robson, and Omar M. Yaghi, in recognition of their pioneering contributions to the field of Metal–Organic Frameworks (MOF). 10 月 8 日，瑞典皇家科学院宣布，将 2025 年诺贝尔化学奖授予 Susumu Kitagawa、Richard Robson 和 Omar M. Yaghi， 以表彰他们在金属有机框架（Metal–Organic Frameworks，简称 MOF）领域的开创性贡献。

The three laureates will equally share the prize money of 11 million Swedish Kronor (equivalent to 8.36 million RMB). 三位获奖者将平分 1100 万瑞典克朗（折合人民币 836 万元）奖金。

It is understood that the metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) created by the three laureates have huge internal cavities, allowing molecules to freely enter and exit. Researchers use them to extract water from desert air, remove pollutants from water, capture carbon dioxide, and store hydrogen. 据了解，三名获奖者创造的金属有机框架（MOF）内部拥有巨大的空腔，分子可以自由进出。研究人员利用它从沙漠空气中提取水分，从水中去除污染物，捕获二氧化碳并储存氢气。

Over the past few decades, countless similar structures have been developed in laboratories around the world. Some are designed to capture carbon dioxide, some to separate per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) from water, others to precisely deliver drugs within the body, or safely handle extremely toxic gases. Some MOFs can even adsorb ethylene gas released by fruits, slowing down their ripening; others can encapsulate enzymes that break down antibiotic residues in the environment. 过去几十年里，全球各地的实验室研发出无数类似的结构。有的被设计用于捕捉二氧化碳，有的用于从水中分离全氟和多氟烷基物质（PFAS），还有的能在体内精准递送药物，或安全处理极度有毒的气体。有的 MOF 甚至可以吸附水果释放的乙烯气体，让它们更慢成熟；也有的能包裹分解环境中抗生素残留的酶。

Simply put, metal-organic frameworks have extremely wide-ranging applications. Susumu Kitagawa, Richard Robson, and Omar Yaghi were awarded the 2025 Nobel Prize in Chemistry because they were the first to create MOFs and demonstrate their enormous potential. 简单来说，金属有机框架的用途极其广泛。Susumu Kitagawa、Richard Robson 和 Omar Yaghi 之所以荣获 2025 年诺贝尔化学奖，是因为他们首次创造出 MOF，并展示了其巨大的潜力。

It is thanks to the work of the three laureates that chemists have been able to design thousands of different MOFs, leading to the birth of one miracle after another in the field of chemistry. 正是由于三位获奖者的工作，化学家们才能设计出成千上万种不同的 MOF，推动了化学领域一个又一个奇迹的诞生。

Heiner Linke, Chair of the Nobel Committee for Chemistry, stated: "Metal-organic frameworks have enormous potential, bringing unprecedented possibilities for customized functional materials." 诺贝尔化学委员会主席 Heiner Linke 表示：「金属有机框架具有巨大潜力，它为定制化功能材料带来了前所未有的可能性。」

Additionally, on October 7, the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences announced that the 2025 Nobel Prize in Physics would be awarded to three quantum physicists, John Clarke, Michel H. Devoret, and John M. Martinis, in recognition of their contributions to realizing macroscopic quantum mechanical tunneling effects and energy quantization in circuits. 另外，瑞典皇家科学院在 10 月 7 日宣布，将 2025 年诺贝尔物理学奖授予 John Clarke、Michel H. Devoret 和 John M. Martinis 三名量子物理学家， 以表彰他们在电路中实现宏观量子力学隧穿效应和能量量子化方面的贡献。

The Nobel Committee for Physics stated on the same day that this year's Nobel Prize in Physics achievements have made possible the development of next-generation quantum technologies such as quantum cryptography, quantum computers, and quantum sensors. 诺贝尔物理学委员会当天表示，今年的诺贝尔物理学奖成果为开发量子密码学、量子计算机和量子传感器等下一代量子技术提供了可能。

On October 6, the Karolinska Institute in Sweden announced that the 2025 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine would be awarded to American scientists Mary E. Brunkow, Fred Ramsdell, and Japanese scientist Shimon Sakaguchi, in recognition of their pioneering discoveries in mechanisms of peripheral immune tolerance. 10 月 6 日，瑞典卡罗琳医学院宣布，将 2025 年诺贝尔生理学或医学奖授予美国科学家 Mary E. Brunkow、Fred Ramsdell 和日本科学家 Shimon Sakaguchi， 以表彰他们在外周免疫耐受机制方面的开创性发现。

中国科研团队突破全固态电池实用瓶颈

According to Science and Technology Daily, important progress has recently been made in the research of interface contact for all-solid-state metal lithium batteries. 据科技日报报道，全固态金属锂电池界面接触研究日前迎来重要进展。

The report states that a team led by Researcher Huang Xuejie from the Institute of Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences/Beijing National Laboratory for Condensed Matter Physics, in collaboration with Professor Zhang Heng's team from Huazhong University of Science and Technology and Researcher Yao Xiayin's team from Ningbo Institute of Materials Technology and Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, has developed an anion regulation technology that can form a brand new interface between the electrode and the electrolyte. 报道称，中国科学院物理研究所/北京凝聚态物理国家研究中心黄学杰研究员团队，联合华中科技大学张恒教授团队、中国科学院宁波材料技术与工程研究所姚霞银研究员团队，开发出一种阴离子调控技术，能够在电极和电解质之间形成一层全新的界面。

Specifically, this research solves a tricky problem for all-solid-state metal lithium batteries: the solid electrolyte and metal lithium electrode must maintain close contact. Traditional methods rely on bulky external equipment to continuously apply pressure, making the battery large and heavy, and difficult to put into practical application. 具体来看， 本次研究解决了全固态金属锂电池的一个棘手难题：固态电解质和金属锂电极之间必须保持紧密接触，传统做法要靠笨重的外部设备持续施压，导致电池又大又重，难以投入实际应用。

To solve this problem, the research team developed a new technology: they introduced iodide ions into the sulfide electrolyte. This interface can actively attract lithium ions, automatically filling all gaps and pores like "self-healing," thus keeping the electrode and electrolyte tightly fitted. 为了解决这一难题，研究团队开发出一种新技术：他们在硫化物电解质中引入了碘离子。这层界面能够主动吸引锂离子，像「自我修复」一样自动填充进所有的缝隙和孔洞，从而让电极和电解质始终保持紧密贴合。

More importantly, the prototype battery prepared based on this technology still maintains stable and excellent performance after hundreds of charge-discharge cycles under standard test conditions, far exceeding the level of existing similar batteries. 更重要的是， 基于该技术制备出的原型电池，在标准测试条件下循环充放电数百次后，性能依然稳定优异，远远超过现有同类电池的水平。

Researchers stated that the advantages of this new design are very obvious: it is not only simpler to manufacture and uses fewer materials, but also makes batteries more durable. They particularly emphasized that this technology can be used in the future to create batteries with an energy density exceeding 500 Wh/kg , which is expected to more than double the battery life of electronic devices. 研究人员表示，这种新设计的优势非常明显：它不仅制造更简单、用料更省，还能让电池更耐用。他们特别强调， 采用这项技术未来可以做出能量密度超过 500 瓦时/千克 的电池 ，这样一来，电子设备的续航时间有望提升两倍以上。

Relevant research results were published on October 7 in "Nature Sustainability" and "Advanced Materials" journals. 相关研究成果 10 月 7 日发表于《自然·可持续发展》杂志以及《先进材料》杂志。
