苹果内置摄像头 AirPods 或 9 月发布

According to Bloomberg, Apple's built-in camera AirPods, currently under development, have entered the late stages of development. Prototypes are now in DVT (Design Validation Test), which is the final major development phase before mass production, and are expected to be released as early as September alongside AI Siri. 据彭博社报道，苹果正在研发的内置摄像头 AirPods 已进入开发后期阶段，原型机目前处于 DVT（设计验证测试）环节，这是量产前的最后一个主要开发阶段，预计最快于 9 月随 AI Siri 一同发布。

Reporter Mark Gurman stated that both the left and right earbuds of this headset have built-in cameras, used to collect low-resolution visual information about the user's surroundings, for Siri to process and provide relevant responses. The cameras do not have photo or video recording capabilities; they primarily serve as Siri's "eyes." 记者 Mark Gurman 称，该款耳机的左右两侧耳塞均内置摄像头，用于采集用户周围环境的低分辨率视觉信息，供 Siri 处理并提供相关回应。摄像头并不具备拍照或录像功能，主要作为 Siri 的「眼睛」使用。

In terms of appearance, the new product will be similar to AirPods Pro 3, but with a longer stem to accommodate the camera module. To address privacy concerns, Apple has built an LED indicator into the earbuds, which will light up when visual data is uploaded to the cloud. 外观上，新品将与 AirPods Pro 3 相近，但耳柄更长以容纳摄像头模组。针对隐私问题，苹果在耳塞上内置了一颗 LED 指示灯，将在视觉数据上传至云端时亮起。

This headset was previously planned for release in the first half of this year, but was delayed due to the postponement of Siri's refactoring work, and is now expected to be released in September. Gurman also pointed out that Apple internally still has concerns about the quality of AI visual features, and if the related experience fails to meet expected standards, the release date may be further postponed. 这款耳机此前计划于今年上半年发布，但因 Siri 重构工作延期而推迟，目前预计于 9 月发布。Gurman 还指出，苹果内部对 AI 视觉功能的质量仍存有顾虑，若相关体验未能达到预期标准，发布时间可能进一步推迟。

OpenAI 发布三款实时语音模型

Today, OpenAI released three real-time speech models, targeting three scenarios: speech inference, real-time translation, and streaming transcription: 今天，OpenAI 发布三款实时语音模型，分别针对语音推理、实时翻译和流式转录三类场景：

  • GPT-Realtime-2: Builds voice agents that can be used in production environments. They can think more deeply, perform actions, handle interruptions, and keep conversations flowing naturally; GPT-Realtime-2：构建可用于生产环境的语音智能体。它们能够进行更深入的思考、执行操作、处理中断，并让对话持续自然地进行；
  • GPT-Realtime-Translate: Supports real-time translation for over 70 input languages and 13 output languages, breaking down language barriers and helping people communicate more naturally; GPT-Realtime-Translate：支持 70 多种输入语言和 13 种输出语言的实时翻译，打破语言障碍，帮助人们更自然地交流；
  • GPT-Realtime-Whisper: Real-time transcription of audio streams, generating subtitles and annotations. GPT-Realtime-Whisper：实时转录音频流，生成字幕和注释。

Among them, GPT-Realtime-2 is equipped with "GPT-5 level reasoning capabilities," designed specifically for voice interaction scenarios, capable of handling complex requests, multi-threaded calls to external tools, responding to user interruptions, and maintaining natural and fluent conversations during continuous dialogue. 其中，GPT-Realtime-2 搭载「GPT-5 级别的推理能力」，专为语音交互场景设计，能够在持续对话过程中处理复杂请求、多线程调用外部工具、应对用户中断，并保持对话的自然流畅。

All three models are now available to developers via the OpenAI Realtime API and can be tested in the OpenAI Playground. 三款模型均已通过 OpenAI Realtime API 向开发者开放，并可在 OpenAI Playground 中进行测试。

曝苹果考虑取消入门款 MacBook Neo，起售价或上涨 100 美元

Recently, tech columnist Tim Culpan reported in his Culpium newsletter that Apple is considering discontinuing the 256GB entry-level MacBook Neo to cope with rising chip and DRAM costs, effectively increasing the price by $100. 日前，科技专栏作者 Tim Culpan 在其 Culpium 通讯中报道称，苹果正在考虑取消 256GB 入门款的 MacBook Neo，以应对芯片与 DRAM 成本上升，变相涨价 100 美元。

According to MacRumors, Apple may also introduce new color options on top of the price increase to maintain market interest, but no specific plan has been finalized yet. Due to demand far exceeding expectations, Apple had previously asked suppliers to increase production targets to 10 million units, roughly double the initial forecast of 5 to 6 million units. 另据 MacRumors 的报道，苹果还可能在涨价的基础上推出新配色来维持市场热度，但目前仍未敲定具体方案。由于需求远超预期，苹果此前已要求供应商将产能目标提升至 1000 万台，约为最初预测 500 至 600 万台的两倍。

Not long ago, Apple quietly removed the 512GB memory option for Mac Studio and the 256GB storage option for Mac mini from its official website, with the latter's starting price rising to 5999 yuan. 就在不久前，苹果已悄悄在官网撤下 Mac Studio 的 512GB 内存选项和 Mac mini 的 256GB 存储选项，后者的起售价涨到 5999 元。

Switch 2 卖得越多亏得越多，任天堂股价连跌 5 个月创十年最长纪录

According to Bloomberg, Nintendo is facing continuous pressure from the capital market. It is worth noting that Nintendo's actual business performance is not bad. Since its launch in June last year, Switch 2's sales speed has continuously surpassed that of any home game console in history; "Pokémon Pokopia" sold 2.2 million copies in 4 days of its launch; "The Super Mario Bros. Movie" global box office has approached $900 million. 值得注意的是，任天堂的实际业务表现并不差。Switch 2 自去年 6 月上市以来，销售速度持续超越历史上任何一款家用游戏主机；《宝可梦 Pokopia》上线 4 天售出 220 万份；《超级马力欧银河大电影》全球票房已逼近 9 亿美元。

Affected by multiple factors such as rising memory prices, investors generally believe that Nintendo is currently selling hardware at a loss. Fluctuations in the Japanese yen exchange rate have further exacerbated cost pressures. The report suggests that a price increase for Switch 2 is only a matter of time. 受内存涨价等多重因素影响，投资者普遍认为任天堂目前正在亏本销售硬件。日元汇率波动进一步加剧了成本压力。报道认为，Switch 2 的涨价只是时间问题。

The report estimates that the price increase will be between $50 and $100. If the price adjustment is implemented, the official price of Switch 2 may approach 4000 RMB. Analyst Hideki Yasuda stated that Nintendo's stock price will remain under pressure until the price adjustment takes effect. 报道预计，此次涨幅在 50 至 100 美元之间，若调价落地，Switch 2 官方售价或将接近 4000 元人民币。分析师 Hideki Yasuda 表示，在价格调整落地之前，任天堂股价将持续承压。

理想设计副总裁首谈 MEGA 设计争议：内部从未被定性为失败，内饰才是销量问题所在

According to LatePost, Li Auto's Vice President of Design, Na Jia, stated in an exclusive interview that MEGA has never been characterized internally as a design failure, but rather as a successful practice of the second-generation design language. 据晚点 LatePost 报道，理想汽车设计副总裁那嘉在专访中表示，MEGA 在公司内部从未被定性为设计层面的失败，而是被视为第二代设计语言的成功实践。

Na Jia stated that MEGA's design truly approached Li Auto's initially defined "Future Icon" concept, and the second-generation design language was, overall, a successful generation. In his view, the core reason for MEGA's sales not meeting expectations was not an issue with the exterior design itself, but rather a severe mismatch between the breakthrough level of the interior and exterior. 那嘉称，MEGA 在设计上真正靠近了理想汽车最初定义的「Future Icon」理念，第二代设计语言整体而言是成功的一代。在他看来，MEGA 销量未达预期的核心原因，并非外观设计本身存在问题，而是内饰与外观的突破程度严重不匹配。

MEGA's interior is too close to the L series, which is an area where design could have done better: the exterior has already raised expectations, and if the interior doesn't provide a comparable level of differentiation, users will feel that the car hasn't fully delivered on "flagship" and "forward-looking" aspects. MEGA 的内饰太接近 L 系列，是设计端可以做得更好的地方：外观已经把预期抬高，内饰如果没有提供同等强度的差异，用户会觉得这辆车没有把「旗舰」和「前瞻」做完整。

Na Jia also mentioned that the MEGA Home version's sales more than doubled due to product improvements, and in his view, this result itself indicates that the problem was not with the design. 那嘉同时提到，MEGA Home 版本因产品力提升，销量翻了一倍多，在他看来，这一结果本身已说明问题并非出在设计上。

Public opinion has not been characterized internally as a malicious design event... It is not a design problem. 舆论在我们内部没有被定性为一个设计上的恶性事件……它不是一个设计问题。

According to Bloomberg, in the trial of the lawsuit between Musk and Altman regarding the future direction of OpenAI, the jury learned that Musk had considered inviting Altman to serve on Tesla's board of directors. 据彭博社报道，在马斯克与奥特曼关于 OpenAI 未来走向的诉讼审判中，陪审团获悉，马斯克曾考虑邀请奥特曼出任特斯拉董事会成员。

This information was disclosed during the trial by Shivon Zilis, a former OpenAI board member and close associate of Musk. She stated that "around late 2017, this matter was indeed brought up," but she could not confirm the exact timeline. 这一信息由前 OpenAI 董事会成员、马斯克的亲密伙伴 Shivon Zilis 在庭审中作证披露。她表示，「大约在 2017 年底，这件事确实被摆上过台面」，但她无法确认具体时间节点。

Trial materials show that at the time, Musk and OpenAI's co-founders were negotiating about the future of OpenAI, with the core issue being how to raise sufficient funds for computing power. Essentially, it was an enticement aimed at making Altman abandon his mission and join Tesla. After it failed, he (Musk) left. 本质上是一种利诱，目的是让 Altman 放弃使命、加入特斯拉。没有成功之后，他（马斯克）就离开了。

Its core capabilities include: 昨天，腾讯混元联合 UCLA、香港中文大学等机构发布 OpenSearch-VL，一套面向多模态深度搜索 AI 智能体的完整开源训练方案。其核心能力包括：
