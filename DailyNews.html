微软承认 Windows 11 更新引发恢复环境键鼠失灵

According to Tom's Hardware, a serious issue has re-emerged with the Windows 11 October update, where some users found USB keyboards and mice completely unusable when entering the Windows Recovery Environment (Windows RE), preventing any interactive operations. 据 Tom’s Hardware 报道，Windows 11 十月更新再现严重问题，部分用户在进入 Windows 恢复环境（Windows RE）时发现 USB 键盘和鼠标完全无法使用，导致无法进行任何交互操作。

It is understood that this issue stems from update patch KB5066835, affecting Windows 11 25H2, 24H2, and Windows Server. Microsoft has confirmed the existence of the problem and stated that it is developing a fix, with an update expected to be rolled out in the coming days. 据悉，本次问题源自更新补丁 KB5066835，影响范围包括 Windows 11 25H2、24H2 以及 Windows Server。微软已确认该问题的存在，并表示正在开发修复方案，预计将在未来几天内推送更新。

It is worth noting that only USB peripherals are affected; older PS/2 interface keyboards and mice can still be used normally. As Windows RE is the default diagnostic and repair tool when the system cannot start properly, this issue could cause significant trouble for ordinary users and IT administrators. 值得注意的是，受影响的仅为 USB 外设，老式 PS/2 接口键盘和鼠标仍可正常使用。由于 Windows RE 是系统无法正常启动时的默认诊断与修复工具，该问题可能给普通用户和 IT 管理人员带来较大困扰。

Microsoft also reminds users that USB peripherals are not affected when the system is running normally; the malfunction only occurs when entering the recovery environment. The official statement promises to release a patch to fix this issue as soon as possible and provide more information in subsequent announcements. 微软同时提醒用户，系统在正常运行状态下 USB 外设不受影响，仅在进入恢复环境时会出现失灵现象。官方承诺将尽快发布补丁修复该问题，并在后续公告中提供更多信息。

12306 升级积分「零元购」车票兑换功能

Yesterday, China Railway 12306 announced the official launch of an upgraded points-based "zero-cost" ticket redemption feature. 昨天，中国铁路 12306 宣布正式上线升级版积分「零元购」车票兑换功能。

According to the latest rules, frequent traveler members can use points to directly deduct ticket fares, enabling "0-yuan purchase" tickets. 根据最新规则，常旅客会员可使用积分直接抵扣票款，实现「0 元购」车票。

According to the railway department, ordinary members can earn 5 times the ticket face value in points after purchasing a ticket, senior members aged 60 and above can earn 15 times the points, and youth members aged 14 to 28 can earn 10 times the points. 据铁路部门介绍，普通会员购票后可获票面金额 5 倍积分，60 周岁及以上老年会员可获 15 倍积分，而 14 至 28 周岁的青年会员则可获 10 倍积分。

For example, an ordinary member spending 1000 yuan on tickets can earn 5000 points (redeemable for 50 yuan), while a youth member spending the same amount can earn 10000 points (redeemable for 100 yuan). 例如，普通会员花费 1000 元购票可获 5000 积分（可抵 50 元），青年会员同样消费可获 10000 积分（可抵 100 元）。

Points can not only be used to redeem tickets but also for a newly added seat upgrade function. Passengers can upgrade their seats on the train by scanning the QR code on the armrest or by contacting staff. 积分不仅可用于兑换车票，还新增了座位升席功能。乘客可在列车上通过扫描座椅扶手二维码或联系工作人员，使用积分办理升席。

It should be noted that new tickets after upgrading cannot be refunded or changed. If they cannot be used due to force majeure or railway responsibility, points for the unused segment will be returned to the account. 需要注意的是，升席后的新车票不可退改，若因不可抗力或铁路责任导致无法使用，未乘区间的积分将退回账户。

The railway reminds that points are valid for 12 months from the date of entry into the account and will automatically expire if unused after this period. Passengers aged 12 and above can apply to become members through the 12306 website, App, or station windows, and will be eligible for redemption once their accumulated points reach 10000 for the first time. 铁路方面提醒，积分自入账起 12 个月内有效，逾期未使用将自动作废。年满 12 周岁的乘客即可通过 12306 网站、App 或车站窗口申请成为会员，首次累计积分达 10000 分后即可具备兑换资格。

According to reports from The Verge and CNBC, Amazon Web Services (AWS) experienced a widespread outage in the early hours of October 20th, Eastern Time, causing multiple online services, including Amazon, Alexa, Snapchat, and Fortnite, to become unavailable. 据 The Verge 和 CNBC 相关报道，亚马逊网络服务（AWS）于美东时间 10 月 20 日凌晨发生大范围故障，导致包括 Amazon、Alexa、Snapchat、Fortnite 在内的多项在线服务无法正常使用。

According to the AWS status page, the US-EAST-1 region first reported anomalies at 3:11 AM, with officials stating they were "investigating increased error rates and latency issues across multiple services" and promising an update within 45 minutes. 根据 AWS 状态页面显示，美国东部一区（US-EAST-1）在凌晨 3:11 首次报告异常，官方称「正在调查多个服务错误率增加和延迟问题」，并承诺将在 45 分钟内更新进展。

The impact was not limited to internet applications; the aviation industry was also affected. Websites and applications of airlines such as Delta and United were temporarily unable to operate normally, preventing some users from completing online check-ins, viewing bookings, or assigning seats. 受影响的不仅是互联网应用，航空业也受到波及。Delta 与 United 等航空公司的网站和应用一度无法正常运行，部分用户无法完成在线值机、查看预订或座位分配。

United stated that some internal systems were affected and backup solutions had been activated to restore services; Delta reported "minor delays" but anticipated no long-term significant impact. United 表示部分内部系统受影响，已启用备用方案恢复服务；Delta 则称出现了「轻微延误」，但预计不会造成长期重大影响。

AWS stated around 5:30 AM Eastern Time that it was "seeing significant signs of recovery" and was gradually clearing backlogged requests. However, the official has not yet confirmed the specific cause of the outage nor provided a full recovery timeline. AWS 在美东时间 5:30 左右表示「看到显著恢复迹象」，并逐步清理积压请求。不过，目前官方尚未确认具体故障原因，也未给出完整恢复时间表。

💡 前 OpenAI 研究员：AI Agent 的真正落地仍需十年

According to The Decoder, former OpenAI and Tesla AI researcher Andrej Karpathy stated in a recent interview that the current hype surrounding "Agent AI" is overly exaggerated, with a significant gap between reality and expectations. 据 The Decoder 报道，前 OpenAI 与 Tesla 的人工智能研究员 Andrej Karpathy 在近期的一次采访中表示，当前围绕「Agent AI」的热潮过于夸大，现实与预期之间存在明显落差。

He bluntly stated that "these models simply cannot work like human interns or professional employees," and predicted that truly reliable Agent AI might still take about 10 years to achieve. 他直言「这些模型根本无法像人类实习生或专业员工那样工作」，并预测真正可靠的 Agent AI 可能还需要约 10 年时间才能实现。

Karpathy pointed out that current large models have serious deficiencies in core cognition, multimodal processing, long-term memory, and complex task execution. Karpathy 指出，目前的大模型在核心认知、多模态处理、长期记忆以及复杂任务执行方面均存在严重不足。

He believes that even advanced models like GPT-5 Pro can only function in narrow scenarios such as "code analysis auxiliary tools," and their true value remains at the "auto-completion" level. 他认为，即便是 GPT-5 Pro 这样的先进模型，也只能在「代码分析的辅助工具」等狭窄场景中发挥作用，其真正价值仍停留在「自动补全」层面。

At the data level, Karpathy criticized the poor quality of existing training corpora, stating that a large amount of internet content is "fragments, symbols, and noise," leading models to rely more on memorization than understanding. 在数据层面，Karpathy 批评现有训练语料质量低劣，大量互联网内容是「碎片、符号与噪音」，导致模型更多依赖记忆而非理解。
