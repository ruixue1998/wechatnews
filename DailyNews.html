GPT-5.4 来了：能操控电脑、写代码、做表格

Yesterday, OpenAI officially released its latest frontier model GPT-5.4, simultaneously introducing the GPT-5.4 Pro version for complex tasks. 昨天，OpenAI 正式发布最新前沿模型 GPT-5.4，同步推出面向复杂任务的 GPT-5.4 Pro 版本。

The new model integrates the latest advancements in reasoning, programming, and agent workflows, incorporating GPT-5.3-Codex's industry-leading programming capabilities, making it OpenAI's first general model with native computer control capabilities. 新模型整合了推理、编程与智能体工作流三方面的最新进展，并将 GPT-5.3-Codex 的行业领先编程能力纳入其中，成为 OpenAI 首款具备原生计算机控制能力的通用模型。

GPT-5.4 possesses native computer control capabilities in Codex and API: GPT-5.4 在 Codex 和 API 中具备了 原生的计算机控制能力 ：

  • Without additional plugins, the model can directly interact with web pages and software interfaces through screenshots combined with keyboard and mouse commands; 无需额外插件，模型可通过屏幕截图结合键盘和鼠标指令，直接与网页及软件界面进行交互；
  • In the OSWorld-Verified benchmark test, which measures desktop environment operational capabilities, GPT-5.4 achieved a score of 75.0%, not only significantly surpassing its predecessor GPT-5.2's 47.3% but also exceeding the human baseline of 72.4%; 在衡量桌面环境操作能力的 OSWorld-Verified 基准测试中，GPT-5.4 取得了 75.0% 的成绩，不仅大幅超越前代 GPT-5.2 的 47.3%，还超越了 72.4% 的人类基准线；
  • On the WebArena-Verified browser operation benchmark, GPT-5.4 achieved a success rate of 67.3%, and it led the Online-Mind2Web test with an even higher success rate of 92.8%. 在浏览器操作基准 WebArena-Verified 上，GPT-5.4 的成功率达到 67.3%，而在 Online-Mind2Web 测试中更以 92.8% 的成功率领跑。

In terms of professional knowledge work: 在专业知识工作方面：

  • GPT-5.4 achieved a win or draw rate of 83.0% in the GDPval benchmark test covering 44 professions, while GPT-5.2 only reached 70.9%; GPT-5.4 在覆盖 44 种职业的 GDPval 基准测试中达到 83.0% 的胜率或平局率，而 GPT-5.2 仅为 70.9%；
  • For investment banking-grade spreadsheet modeling tasks, GPT-5.4's score significantly increased from GPT-5.2's 68.4% to 87.3%; 针对投行级电子表格建模任务，GPT-5.4 的得分从 GPT-5.2 的 68.4% 大幅提升至 87.3%；
  • In presentation evaluations, 68.0% of human evaluators preferred GPT-5.4's output, citing reasons such as richer visuals and higher aesthetic appeal. 在演示文稿评估中，68.0% 的人工评测者更青睐 GPT-5.4 的输出结果，理由包括视觉更丰富、美观度更高。

Other aspects: 其他方面：

  • In terms of controllability, GPT-5.4 Thinking first outputs a "pre-plan" when handling complex queries, allowing users to intervene and adjust the direction at any point during the model's generation process, without needing to start from scratch. This feature is currently available on the ChatGPT web version and Android, with the iOS version to follow; 在可操控性方面，GPT-5.4 Thinking 在处理复杂查询时会先输出一份「预先计划」，用户可以在模型生成过程中随时介入并调整方向，无需从头开始。该功能目前已在 ChatGPT 网页版和 Android 端上线，iOS 版本即将跟进；
  • Regarding programming capabilities, GPT-5.4's performance on SWE-Bench Pro is on par with or slightly surpasses GPT-5.3-Codex, with lower latency. The new /fast mode in Codex can increase token generation speed by 1.5 times. 编程能力方面，GPT-5.4 在 SWE-Bench Pro 上的表现与 GPT-5.3-Codex 持平或略有超越，同时延迟更低。Codex 中新增的 /fast 模式可将 token 生成速度提升至 1.5 倍。

It's worth noting that the new "tool lookup" mechanism abandons brute-force loading, drastically reducing overall token consumption by 47% while maintaining accuracy, saving money and speeding things up. 值得一提的是，全新的「工具查找」机制告别了暴力加载，在保持准确率的同时，将总体 Token 消耗暴降了 47%，省钱又提速。

OpenAI states that GPT-5.4 is its most accurate model to date , with a 33% reduction in single factual error rate compared to GPT-5.2, and an 18% reduction in the error rate of complete responses. OpenAI 表示， GPT-5.4 是其迄今为止准确性最高的模型 ，单一事实错误率较 GPT-5.2 降低 33%，完整回复的错误率降低 18%。

In ChatGPT, GPT-5.4 is launched as "GPT-5.4 Thinking," available to Plus, Team, and Pro users, simultaneously replacing GPT-5.2 Thinking. 在 ChatGPT 中，GPT-5.4 以「GPT-5.4 Thinking」形态上线，面向 Plus、Team 和 Pro 用户开放，同步替代 GPT-5.2 Thinking。

Regarding pricing, the standard input price for GPT-5.4 in the API is $2.50 per million tokens, and the output price is $15 per million tokens; the input price for GPT-5.4 Pro is $30 per million tokens, and the output price is $180 per million tokens, both higher than the previous generation GPT-5.2. 定价方面，API 中 GPT-5.4 的标准输入价格为每百万 token 2.50 美元，输出价格为每百万 token 15 美元；GPT-5.4 Pro 的输入价格为每百万 token 30 美元，输出价格为每百万 token 180 美元，均高于前代 GPT-5.2。

雷军再谈内存涨价：手机业务压力很大，将想办法降低消费者负担

According to Jiemian News, Lei Jun stated yesterday that the surge in AI demand has led to a continuous rise in storage chip prices. 据界面新闻报道，雷军昨天表示，AI 需求暴涨引发存储芯片价格持续攀升。

Our mobile phone business faces significant pressure, and we will explore various methods to minimize the difficulty for consumers to accept. 我们的手机业务面临很大压力，会想各种办法，尽量降低消费者的接受难度。

This is the third time Lei Jun has spoken publicly about chip price increases recently. 这已是雷军近期在公开场合第三次就芯片涨价问题发声。

  • Last October, Lei Jun made his first public statement in response to the price increase of the Redmi K90 series, frankly stating that "memory prices have risen too much recently" and pointing out that product price adjustments stemmed from rising upstream storage chip costs, which phone manufacturers found difficult to fully absorb. 去年 10 月，雷军回应 Redmi K90 系列涨价时首次公开表态，直言「最近内存涨价实在太多」，并指出产品调价源于上游存储芯片成本上涨，手机厂商难以完全消化。
  • In January this year, Lei Jun again mentioned in a live broadcast that storage chip prices are rising quarterly, with a 40%~50% increase in the fourth quarter of last year, and he expects continued increases in the first quarter of this year, with the annual cost for automotive memory alone potentially increasing by several thousand yuan. 今年 1 月，雷军在直播中再次提及，存储芯片价格按季度上涨，去年第四季度涨幅达 40%~50%，并预计今年第一季度将继续上涨，仅车用内存全年成本可能增加数千元。

It is worth noting that just yesterday, Beike Finance reported, citing a vivo store employee, that all vivo mobile phone products will officially increase in price starting March 15th. Previously released older models are all within the scope of price adjustment, with an overall brand increase of approximately 10%~15%. 值得注意的是，就在昨天，贝壳财经援引 vivo 门店店员报道称，vivo 旗下全系手机产品将于 3 月 15 日起正式上调售价，此前已发布的旧机型均在调价范围内，品牌整体涨幅约 10%~15%。

The store employee explained that various brands are currently setting a buffer period mainly to deplete existing low-cost storage chip inventory. Once this inventory is exhausted and high-cost chip products are launched, manufacturers will have to implement a new pricing system to ensure profit margins. 店员解释，各品牌目前设置缓冲期，主要是为了消化现有的低价存储芯片库存，待这批库存耗尽、高成本芯片产品上市后，厂商为保证利润空间将不得不执行新的售价体系。
