苹果已关闭 iOS 18 降级通道

Apple's firmware website ipsw.me shows that Apple has stopped signing iOS 18.6.2, which means that once a device is upgraded to iOS 26, it will not be able to revert to the iOS 18 series. 苹果固件网站 ipsw.me 显示，苹果已停止对 iOS 18.6.2 进行签署验证，这意味着一旦设备升级至 iOS 26，将无法回退至 iOS 18 系列版本。

The so-called 'signing' means that the system needs to pass verification checks by Apple's servers during installation; firmware that fails verification cannot be installed. 所谓「签署」，是指系统在安装过程中需通过苹果服务器的验证检查，未通过验证的固件无法安装。

After signing stops, users will not be able to downgrade to older versions. 停止签署后，用户将无法降级至旧版。

Currently, users can still use iOS 18, but once upgraded to iOS 26, they will not be able to downgrade. 目前，用户仍可继续使用 iOS 18，但一旦升级至 iOS 26，就无法降级。

In addition, Apple has also stopped signing iPadOS 18.6.2 and tvOS 18.6, meaning iPads also cannot revert to older versions. 此外，苹果还停止签署了 iPadOS 18.6.2 和 tvOS 18.6，iPad 同样无法回退至旧版本。

It is reported that Apple has introduced a new 'Liquid Glass' design language in iOS 26 and brought multiple feature updates. 据悉，苹果在 iOS 26 上引入了全新的「Liquid Glass」设计语言，并带来多项功能更新。

Furthermore, Apple also pushed the iOS 26.1 Developer Beta version yesterday. 此外，苹果还在昨天推送了 iOS 26.1 Developer Beta 版本。

20 周年 iPhone 或将搭载更亮的屏幕

According to MacRumors, industry sources reveal that Apple plans to introduce a new OLED technology called COE (Color Filter on Encapsulation), supplied by Samsung, in its 20th-anniversary iPhone to achieve higher brightness and a thinner body design. 据 MacRumors，有产业链人士透露，苹果计划在 20 周年纪念版 iPhone 上引入由三星供应的全新 OLED 技术 COE（Color Filter on Encapsulation），以实现更高亮度和更纤薄的机身设计。

Traditionally, OLED panels add a polarizer layer above the display layer to reduce reflections and enhance contrast, but this structure absorbs some self-emitted light, reducing brightness and energy efficiency. 据悉，传统 OLED 面板在显示层上方会增加一层偏光片，用于减少反射、提升对比度，但该结构会吸收部分自发光，降低亮度与能效。

COE technology directly covers the encapsulation protection layer with a color filter layer, eliminating the polarizer, thereby reducing the display stack thickness and allowing more light to pass through, increasing brightness without increasing power consumption. COE 技术则直接将彩色滤光层覆盖在封装保护层上，取消偏光片，从而减少显示堆叠厚度，让更多光线透出，在不增加功耗的情况下提升亮度。

Industry analysis indicates that after removing the polarizer, screen reflection and glare control outdoors will be more challenging. Apple is expected to adopt advanced coatings and pixel-level material optimization for visual effects. 业内分析指出，去除偏光片后，屏幕在户外的反射与眩光控制将更具挑战，苹果预计会采用先进镀膜与像素级材料优化可视效果。

There are also reports that Apple is considering a new design for the 20th-anniversary edition, featuring a four-sided curved, bezel-less appearance, and plans to launch its first foldable iPhone in 2026. 另有消息称，苹果正考虑为 20 周年纪念版打造四边曲面、无边框的全新外观设计，并在 2026 年率先推出首款可折叠 iPhone。
