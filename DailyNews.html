3799 元，大疆 Osmo Pocket 4P 发布

DJI yesterday released the Osmo Pocket 4P, positioned as a dual-main-camera pocket cinema camera. The new product features a one-inch wide-angle main camera and a 60mm telephoto lens, covering scenarios such as environmental recording, street photography, portraits, and close-ups. 大疆昨日发布 Osmo Pocket 4P，定位双主摄口袋电影机。新品采用一英寸广角主摄和 60mm 中焦镜头，覆盖环境记录、街拍、人像和特写等场景。

  • Equipped with a one-inch CMOS wide-angle main camera, 20mm equivalent focal length, f/2.0 aperture; the telephoto lens has a 60mm equivalent focal length, f/1.8 aperture, supporting 3x optical zoom and up to 12x zoom; 搭载一英寸 CMOS 广角主摄，20mm 等效焦距、f/2.0 光圈；中焦镜头为 60mm 等效焦距、f/1.8 光圈，支持 3 倍光学变焦和最高 12 倍变焦；
  • Video supports up to 17 stops of dynamic range, D-Log 2 color mode, 10-bit color depth, and up to 4K/240fps slow-motion recording; 视频支持最高 17 级动态范围、D-Log 2 色彩模式、10-bit 色深和最高 4K/240fps 慢动作录制；
  • Equipped with three-axis mechanical gimbal stabilization and ActiveTrack 8.0, supporting full-focal-length tracking, multi-person tracking, and subject tracking; 搭载三轴机械云台增稳和智能跟随 8.0，支持全焦段跟随、多人跟随和主角跟随；
  • Built-in 103GB storage, supports USB 3.1, charges to 80% in as fast as 18 minutes, with a maximum battery life of 210 minutes. 内置 103GB 存储，支持 USB 3.1，最快 18 分钟充至 80%，最长续航 210 分钟。

The standard kit is priced at 3799 yuan, and the Vlog kit is priced at 4299 yuan. The latter additionally includes accessories such as the DJI Mic Mini 2 transmitter, a viewing remote control, a mini tripod, and a storage bag. 标准套装售价 3799 元，Vlog 套装售价 4299 元，后者额外包含 DJI Mic Mini 2 发射器、取景遥控器、迷你三脚架和收纳包等配件。

曝 Claude Fable 5 有望本周恢复访问

According to Axios, Anthropic has dispatched a senior technical team to Washington for urgent consultations with U.S. White House officials, hoping to lift the export control ban on its Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 models as soon as possible. If the consultations go smoothly, Claude Fable 5 "may" regain access within this week. 据 Axios 报道，Anthropic 已派遣高级技术团队飞赴华盛顿，与美国白宫官员展开紧急磋商，希望尽快解除针对旗下 Claude Fable 5 和 Mythos 5 模型的出口管制禁令。如果磋商顺利，Claude Fable 5 「有可能」在本周内恢复访问。

Axios pointed out that U.S. government officials had previously stated that Anthropic had not seriously cooperated with communications. However, according to sources close to the company, the technical team has held multiple online meetings with White House officials since last Friday, and both sides are keen to resolve the dispute as soon as possible. Axios 指出，美国政府官员曾表示 Anthropic 未认真配合沟通。但据接近公司的消息人士称，技术团队自上周五起已与白宫官员多次进行线上会议，双方均有意尽快化解争端。

It is understood that if negotiations make progress, Anthropic may respond to government concerns by introducing stricter security restrictions, specifically by building new safeguards against jailbreaking at the model level. 据悉，谈判若能取得进展，Anthropic 可能通过引入更严格的安全限制来回应政府关切，即在模型层面构建针对越狱的新防护措施。

Meanwhile, Anthropic's account identity verification system has also been quietly upgraded during the same period. Anthropic's official blog shows that the new privacy policy will officially take effect on July 8 this year, applicable to Claude Free, Pro, and Max consumer accounts, but not covering commercial services such as Team, Enterprise, and Developer Platform. 与此同时，Anthropic 的账号身份验证体系也在同期悄然升级。Anthropic 官方博客显示，新版隐私政策将于今年 7 月 8 日正式生效，适用范围为 Claude Free、Pro 和 Max 消费者账号，不涵盖 Team、Enterprise 及 Developer Platform 等商业服务。

The new policy clarifies that in certain circumstances, the platform may require users to verify their age or identity. 巴西模型 Rio 3.5 引发「缝合」Qwen 质疑

Recently, the Rio 3.5 Open 397B model, open-sourced by an IT company under the Rio de Janeiro City Government in Brazil, sparked discussion in the AI community. The model page shows that it is open under the MIT license, with a total of approximately 397B parameters, about 17B activations per token, and supports longer contexts. 近日，一个由巴西里约热内卢市政府旗下 IT 公司开源的 Rio 3.5 Open 397B 模型在 AI 社区引发讨论。模型页面显示，它以 MIT 协议开放，总参数约 397B，单 token 激活约 17B，并支持较长上下文。

However, less than 24 hours after the model's release, the open-source AI team Nex-AGI spoke out on the X platform, pointing out that Rio 3.5 is essentially a re-packaged product assembled through Model Merge technology. 然而，模型发布不到 24 小时，开源 AI 团队 Nex-AGI 即在 X 平台发声，指出 Rio 3.5 实质上是通过模型合并（Model Merge）技术拼凑而成的套壳产品。

After an in-depth mathematical analysis of Rio 3.5's weights, Nex-AGI disclosed that the model's underlying formula is approximately 60% Nex N2 Pro and 40% Alibaba Qwen 3.5. The team provided two independent verification paths: Nex-AGI 对 Rio 3.5 权重进行深入数学分析后披露，该模型的底层配方约为 60% 的 Nex N2 Pro 与 40% 的阿里巴巴 Qwen 3.5。团队提供了两条独立验证路径：

  • First, after removing the hardcoded "You are Rio" system prompt, the deployed model had a 79% chance of identifying itself as "Nex from Nex-AGI," a zero chance of identifying itself as "Rio," and could repeat Nex-AGI's custom backstory word for word; 一是在移除硬编码的「你是 Rio」系统提示词后，已部署模型有 79% 的概率自称「来自 Nex-AGI 的 Nex」，自称「Rio」的概率为零，且能一字不差地复述 Nex-AGI 的定制背景故事；
  • Second, every weight tensor across all 60 layers of Rio 3.5's network showed a perfectly consistent 0.6/0.4 Nex and Qwen mixing ratio, with statistical deviations reaching thousands of standard deviations, which cannot be explained by conventional fine-tuning. 二是 Rio 3.5 全部 60 层网络的每个权重张量，均呈现完全一致的 0.6/0.4 Nex 与 Qwen 混合比例，统计偏差达数千个标准差，无法以常规微调解释。

The team stated that the emergence of Rio 3.5 objectively reverse-validated the capabilities of Nex's open-source foundation, but at the same time, seriously emphasized that attribution and acknowledgment are the inviolable bottom line of the open-source community. 团队表示，Rio 3.5 的出现客观上反向验证了 Nex 开源底座的能力，但同时严肃强调，署名与致谢是开源社区不可逾越的底线。

智元远征 A3 机器人实现全自主乒乓对抗

AGIBOT announced yesterday that its Expedition A3 full-size bipedal humanoid robot successfully demonstrated autonomous table tennis playing. The official statement claimed that the demonstration was entirely without remote control, scripts, or human intervention, with the robot autonomously completing visual perception, trajectory prediction, whole-body motion planning, and hitting control. 智元 AGIBOT 昨日宣布，远征 A3 全尺寸双足人形机器人完成自主打乒乓球演示。官方称，这次演示全程无遥控、无脚本、无人工干预，机器人自主完成视觉感知、轨迹预测、全身运动规划和击球控制。
