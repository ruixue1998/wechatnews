Mark Gurman：苹果 Vision Pro 团队已被拆分重组

According to Bloomberg, Apple has significantly adjusted the strategic priorities of its closed-loop headset project, with the original Vision product team being split and reorganized, and the business focus fully shifting to AR glasses and other AI wearable devices. 据彭博社报道，苹果已大幅调整封闭式头显项目的战略优先级，原 Vision 产品团队被分拆重组，业务重心全面转向 AR 眼镜与其他 AI 可穿戴设备。

Reporter Mark Gurman, citing sources, revealed that Apple had disbanded its original Vision Product Group (VPG) a year ago, splitting its software and hardware personnel and integrating them into the company's larger engineering structure. 记者 Mark Gurman 援引知情人士透露，苹果在一年前已解散原 Vision 产品团队（VPG），将其软硬件人员拆分并入公司更庞大的工程架构中。

Currently, Mike Rockwell, the former head of VPG, has transitioned to oversee the joint software division for Siri and visionOS, with most of his and his core deputies' energy now focused on the research and development of Siri's business. 目前，原 VPG 负责人 Mike Rockwell 转为统管 Siri 与 visionOS 的联合软件部门，且其本人及核心副手的大部分精力均已倾斜至 Siri 业务的研发。

In terms of product development, Apple has paused the advancement of major closed-system new headsets, and the low-cost headset codenamed N100 (Vision Air) was also canceled last year. 产品开发方面，苹果暂缓了重大封闭式新头显的推进，代号为 N100 的低成本头显（Vision Air）亦已于去年被取消。

On the software front, Gurman states that the upcoming visionOS 27 will lack significant feature-level innovations, with updates focusing solely on underlying performance optimization, bug fixes, and feature alignment with systems like iOS 27 in new AI editing tools and Siri upgrades. 软件层面，Gurman 称即将发布的 visionOS 27 将缺乏重大功能级创新，更新重点仅限于底层性能优化、Bug 修复，以及实现与 iOS 27 等系统在全新 AI 编辑工具和 Siri 升级上的功能对齐。

The report points out that during the pause in closed-system headset development, the relevant hardware teams are concentrating resources on tackling new AI wearable devices such as "AirPods with cameras" and "AI pendants." 报道指出，在暂停封闭式头显推进期间，相关硬件团队正集中资源攻坚「带摄像头的 AirPods」及「AI 吊坠」等新型 AI 可穿戴设备。

微信状态要推出「访客记录」了

WeChat recently began gray-scale testing a "Personal Status" visitor record feature on its iOS client, allowing users to view the number of people who have viewed their posted status and, under specific conditions, display the avatars of viewers. 微信近期在 iOS 客户端灰度测试「个人状态」访客记录功能，允许用户查看自己发布状态的浏览人数，并在特定条件下显示浏览者头像。

According to a leak from Xiaohongshu blogger "C" (Yang_Ccn), this feature is currently in internal testing and is open to active status users. The leak states that the number of viewers will be displayed in the bottom right corner of the status page, and this number is likely to count unique viewers rather than total views. 据小红书博主「C」（Yang_Ccn）的爆料，该功能目前处于内测阶段，面向状态活跃用户开放。爆料称，状态页右下角将显示浏览人数，该数字大概率统计的是独立浏览用户数，而非浏览次数。

The avatars that appear after clicking are limited to users who "have viewed your status and currently also have a status set" — if the other party viewed your status but has not posted a status themselves, their avatar will not appear in the list. 点击后出现的头像，则限于「浏览了你的状态，且自身当前也设置了状态」的用户 —— 若对方浏览了你的状态但自己没有发布状态，其头像不会出现在列表中。

Additionally, other bloggers have supplemented: 另有博主补充表示：

  • Previously viewed statuses of others won't show, but if you post a status later, they will appear in the list (the appearance of bubbles doesn't seem strongly tied to chronological order); 之前刷过别人的状态不显示，但后面自己发状态了也会显示在列表（气泡出现似乎没有和时间顺序强挂钩）；
  • Clicking on a bubble avatar allows you to directly view the corresponding friend's status; 点击气泡头像可以直接查看对应好友的状态；
  • Friends who haven't been gray-scaled but have viewed a status will also appear in the visitor list of gray-scaled friends. 未被灰度的朋友看过状态也会显示在被灰度朋友的访客列表内。

Tencent Customer Service's official account subsequently responded to this feature, stating that within the status's validity period, users can view the number of people who have viewed that status in the bottom right corner; if a viewer currently also has a status set, their avatar will be visible, and tapping it will allow viewing the other party's status. 腾讯客服官方账号随后就此功能作出回应，表示在状态有效期内，用户可在右下角查看该条状态的浏览人数；若浏览者当前也设置了状态，则可看到其头像，轻点即可查看对方状态。

The official statement also confirmed that this feature is currently undergoing gray-scale testing on the iOS client and has not yet been fully rolled out. Users who do not see the relevant entry are advised to wait patiently. 官方同时确认，该功能目前正在 iOS 客户端进行灰度测试，尚未全量推送，建议未见到相关入口的用户耐心等待。

曝 AMD 下代 CPU 将使用三星 2nm 打造

Leaker Jukan yesterday cited news from Daishin Securities, stating that Samsung may have won a new 2nm laptop CPU order from a "North American fabless customer." Jukan subsequently speculated that the customer "appears to be AMD." 爆料人 Jukan 昨日援引大信证券消息称，三星可能已赢得一笔来自「北美无晶圆厂客户」的 2 纳米笔记本电脑 CPU 新订单。Jukan 随后推测该客户「看来像是 AMD」。

Furthermore, according to analysis by wccftech, with TSMC's advanced process capacity fully booked until 2028 and tech companies like Nvidia and Apple actively occupying capacity, Samsung is considered the best alternative for AMD to achieve sufficient production before the planned release dates of its Venice and Verano CPUs. 另据 wccftech 的分析，由于台积电先进制程产能已排满至 2028 年，在英伟达、苹果等科技公司积极占据产能的背景下，三星被认为是 AMD 在 Venice 和 Verano CPU 计划发布时间节点前实现足够产量的最佳替代方案。

Additionally, AMD may also hope to prioritize obtaining DRAM memory produced by Samsung through this cooperation agreement. 此外，AMD 或也希望借此合作协议优先获取三星生产的 DRAM 内存。

Samsung currently still faces yield issues, with a gap between its current state and customer expectations. Qualcomm and Tesla were previously reported to be evaluating shifting 2nm orders to Samsung, but there has been no official confirmation of progress. If AMD ultimately adopts Samsung's 2nm solution, it would be a significant customer endorsement for Samsung's advanced process business this year. 三星目前仍面临良率问题，与客户期望标准之间存在差距。高通及特斯拉此前也被报道正在评估将 2 纳米订单转向三星，但尚无官方确认进展。若 AMD 最终采用三星 2 纳米方案，将是三星先进制程业务在今年获得的重要客户背书。

Data shows that in April, national passenger car retail sales were 1.384 million units, a year-on-year decrease of 21.5% and a month-on-month decrease of 16.0%; cumulative retail sales from January to April this year were 5.604 million units, a year-on-year decrease of 18.5%. 乘联分会昨日发布《2026 年 4 月份全国乘用车市场分析》报告。数据显示，4 月全国乘用车市场零售 138.4 万辆，同比下降 21.5%，环比下降 16.0%；今年 1—4 月累计零售 560.4 万辆，同比下降 18.5%。

The fuel vehicle market continues to face pressure. In April, wholesale sales of conventional fuel passenger vehicles were 890,000 units, a year-on-year decrease of 16% and a month-on-month decrease of 28%; retail sales were 530,000 units, a year-on-year decrease of 37% and a month-on-month decrease of 33%. 燃油车市场持续承压。4 月常规燃油乘用车批发销量 89 万辆，同比下降 16%，环比下降 28%；零售量 53 万辆，同比下降 37%，环比下降 33%。

开发者让 Codex 自己去赚钱，22 小时赚回 16 刀遭群嘲

Yesterday, developer Chris shared on X his experience of letting Codex earn money by itself: he directly asked it to 'earn $5', and Codex then ran autonomously for about 22 hours, ultimately earning back $16.88. 昨天，开发者 Chris 在 X 分享了自己让 Codex 自己去赚钱的经历：他直接要求其「去赚 5 美元」，Codex 随后全程自主运行约 22 小时，最终赚回 16.88 美元。

According to reports, Codex autonomously completed the entire process of finding security vulnerabilities in open-source projects, submitting PRs, communicating with project maintainers, handling GitHub verification processes, and finally receiving payment. Chris stated that this was his first personal experience with AI autonomous revenue generation, and it corroborates Sam Altman's vision of 'AI earning money for people' becoming a reality. 据介绍，Codex 自主完成了寻找开源项目安全漏洞、提交 PR、与项目维护者沟通、处理 GitHub 验证流程直至收款的全套流程。Chris 表示，这是他亲历 AI 自主创收的第一次实验，并以此佐证 Sam Altman 所描绘的「AI 替人赚钱」愿景正在成为现实。

However, this result sparked widespread ridicule in the developer community. Several developers pointed out that the computing power cost of running a top-tier AI agent continuously for 22 hours is extremely high, with some even stating that it's equivalent to spending $2,000 to get less than $17 in revenue, calling it 'paying to work'. 然而，这一结果引发开发者社区的大规模嘲讽。多名开发者指出，让顶配 AI 智能体持续运转 22 小时所消耗的算力成本极高，有人甚至表示其相当于花 2000 美元换回不到 17 美元的收益，堪称「倒贴打工」。

Chris responded that he did not directly bear the computing costs but used a $20 monthly subscription package, shifting the cost to the platform. However, he also admitted that Codex was restricted twice by system risk control due to abnormal behavior during task execution, and its account was flagged. Chris 回应称，他并未直接承担算力费用，而是使用了 20 美元的包月订阅套餐，将成本转移至平台侧。不过他也承认，Codex 在执行任务期间因异常行为被系统风控限制了两次，账号曾遭到标记。

He stated that the current inference cost for calling GPT-5.5 is still high (approximately $30 per million output tokens), and the platform is operating at a loss; however, he expects the cost to drop to about $2 next year, at which point this fully automated order-taking model will be commercially viable for both users and the platform. 他表示，目前调用 GPT-5.5 的推理成本仍然高昂（百万输出 Token 约 30 美元），平台处于亏损状态；但他预计明年成本将降至约 2 美元，届时这种全自动接单模式将对用户和平台均具备商业可行性。

PS3 模拟器 RPCS3 发新规禁止纯 Agent 代码：开发者需对代码完全负责

Yesterday, the official PS3 open-source emulator RPCS3 announced on X that it prohibits 'autonomous' AI agents from participating in code contributions and requires developers submitting PRs to fully understand and truly own all the code they submit. 昨天，PS3 开源模拟器 RPCS3 官方在 X 发文表示，禁止「自主」AI Agent 参与代码贡献，并要求提交 PR 的开发者必须完全理解、真正拥有自己提交的所有代码。

The team pointed out that such code not only wastes review time, but some changes have even been merged into the main branch, causing features to fail for all users. 团队指出，此类代码不仅浪费审查时间，部分改动甚至已被合并进主分支，导致功能对所有用户失效。

According to the full text of the rules published by RPCS3, the project allows the use of AI for research and reverse engineering, but contributors must take full responsibility for their submissions. The rules also stipulate that all project code, comments, and GitHub comments must be submitted by humans, not AI agents. 根据 RPCS3 公布的规则全文，项目允许使用 AI 辅助研究和逆向功能，但贡献者须对提交内容负完全责任。规则同时规定，项目所有代码、注释及 GitHub 评论均须由人类提交，而非 AI 智能体。

If a PR uses an AI agent or automated tool, the contributor must disclose the extent of AI involvement in the description, specifying which code was generated by AI and what kind of human review it underwent before submission; failure to disclose may result in the PR being closed directly without review. Users who repeatedly submit untested, unverified AI-generated code will be banned from the project. 若 PR 使用了 AI 智能体或自动化工具，贡献者必须在描述中披露 AI 参与范围，注明哪些代码由 AI 生成，以及提交前经过了何种人工审查；未披露者，PR 可能在未经审核的情况下直接关闭。对于屡次提交未经测试、验证的 AI 生成代码的用户，项目将予以封禁。

软银宣布量产大规模电池，目标年产 1GWh 供 AI 数据中心使用

According to Bloomberg, SoftBank Group's mobile business subsidiary announced yesterday that it plans to start mass production of battery cells at its factory in Sakai, Osaka, to meet the continuously growing power demand driven by AI services. 据彭博社报道，软银集团旗下移动业务子公司昨日宣布，计划在其位于大阪堺市的工厂启动大规模电池单体量产，以应对 AI 服务带来的持续增长的电力需求。

SoftBank will collaborate with South Korea's Cosmos Lab and DeltaX Co., planning to achieve mass production starting from the fiscal year beginning next April, with a target annual capacity of 1 gigawatt-hour (GWh) for energy storage systems. This will make it one of Japan's largest battery production facilities, with potential to expand capacity to several gigawatt-hours in the future. 软银将与韩国 Cosmos Lab 及 DeltaX Co. 展开合作，计划从明年 4 月开始的财年起实现量产，目标年产能为 1 吉瓦时（GWh）的储能系统，这将使其成为日本规模最大的电池生产设施之一，未来有望将产能扩大至数吉瓦时。

In terms of cooperation and division of labor, Cosmos Lab is responsible for developing safer battery cells using its zinc-halogen technology, while DeltaX Co. possesses expertise in high energy density design. 在合作分工上，Cosmos Lab 负责利用其锌卤素技术开发更安全的电池单体，DeltaX Co. 则在高能量密度设计方面具备专业能力。

SoftBank stated that these batteries will first be used in its own AI data centers currently under construction, and it also plans to supply them to scenarios such as power grids, factories, and industrial and residential sectors. In the medium to long term, SoftBank will also seek to expand into overseas markets. 软银表示，这批电池将首先用于其自身正在建设的 AI 数据中心，同时也计划向电网、工厂及工业和住宅等场景供应。中长期来看，软银还将寻求拓展海外市场。

黄仁勋致 2026 届毕业生：AI 不会取代你，但更会用 AI 的人可能会

Yesterday, NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang attended Carnegie Mellon University (CMU)'s 2026 commencement ceremony and received an honorary doctorate in science and technology. 昨天，英伟达 CEO 黄仁勋出席 Carnegie Mellon University（CMU）2026 年毕业典礼，并接受科学与技术荣誉博士学位。

Jensen Huang directly responded to the widespread job anxiety. He stated that AI is ending the 60-year-old traditional computing paradigm — shifting from humans writing code to machine learning, from CPUs to GPUs, and from executing instructions to understanding, reasoning, and planning. 黄仁勋对当前弥漫的就业焦虑给出了直接回应。他表示，AI 正在终结延续 60 年的传统计算范式 —— 从人类编写代码转向机器学习，从 CPU 转向 GPU，从执行指令转向理解、推理与规划。

He believes this shift levels the playing field for old experiences, putting everyone back on the same starting line . He specifically pointed out, "AI is unlikely to replace you, but a person who uses AI more effectively might." 他认为，这一转变让旧有经验的优势被削平，所有人重新站上同一条起跑线 。他特别指出，「AI 不太可能取代你，但更会使用 AI 的人可能会取代你」。

For young people who have just received their diplomas and have no baggage, this is actually a good thing. Because everyone no longer needs to stubbornly stick to old tracks already filled by predecessors, but instead, stands on the same starting line with everyone else, once again. 对于刚拿到文凭、毫无包袱的年轻人来说，这其实是一件好事。因为大家不用再去死磕那些已经被前人占满的旧赛道，而是和所有人一起，又一次站在了同一条起跑线上。

He also called on scientists, engineers, and policymakers to jointly take responsibility, establishing safety guardrails while advancing AI capabilities, and ensuring that the benefits of AI can reach a wider population. He also mentioned that the large-scale construction required to support AI infrastructure will bring new opportunities for traditional industry workers such as electricians, construction workers, and steelworkers. 他同时呼吁科学家、工程师与政策制定者共同承担责任，在推进 AI 能力的同时建立安全护栏，并确保 AI 的红利能够惠及更广泛的人群。他还提到，支撑 AI 基础设施所需的大规模建设将为电工、建筑工人、钢铁工人等传统行业从业者带来新的机遇。

Notably, at the ceremony, Intel CEO Pat Gelsinger personally draped an honorary doctorate stole over Jensen Huang, and after the ceremony, publicly revealed that the two companies are collaborating on "exciting new products," but did not disclose further details. 值得注意的是，典礼上，Intel CEO 陈立武亲手为黄仁勋披上荣誉博士披肩，并在典礼结束后公开透露，两家公司正在合作开发「令人期待的新产品」，但未披露更多细节。

💡 姚顺宇：Scaling Law 没有到头，大多数「撞墙」是因为有 bug

In the latest episode of "Zhang Xiaojun's Business Interview Series" released yesterday, Google DeepMind researcher Yao Shunyu stated that the scaling law for pre-training has not reached its limit. When asked if the pace of model progress was slowing down, his answer was "not at all." 在昨天发布的最新一期《张小珺商业访谈录》中，Google DeepMind 研究员姚顺宇表示，预训练的 Scaling Law 并没有到头。在被问及模型进步速度是否放缓时，他的回答是「完全没有」。

He explained that using the increase in Benchmark scores to judge the speed of progress is itself a misleading framework — any Benchmark has an upper limit, and the closer it gets to a perfect score, the slower the numerical growth naturally becomes. 他解释称，用 Benchmark 分数涨幅来判断进展快慢，本身就是一个误导性框架 —— 任何 Benchmark 都有上限，越接近满分，数字增长自然越慢。

The model's ability to "learn things" is continuously strengthening, and researchers are now focusing more on "clearly defining problems and constructing appropriate data," while the rest "often seems to happen naturally." 模型「学东西」的能力在持续增强，研究者如今更多的精力是在「把问题定义清楚、构建合适的数据」，其余的事情「好像很多时候是顺其自然的了」。

For those who believe that the Scaling Law has hit its ceiling, he summarized three sources: the inherent limits of the law's applicability, and the exhaustion of critical conditions (such as data). 对于那些认为 Scaling Law 已触及天花板的观点，他归纳出三种来源：规律本身的适用范围有极限、关键条件（如数据）已耗尽。

He believes that most "hitting a wall" situations are actually due to bugs in the research work itself that researchers haven't discovered. "The progress brought by fixing a bug is far greater than some very magical techniques." 他认为大多数「撞墙」，其实是研究工作本身存在 bug，但研究者自己没有发现。「修好一个 bug 带来的进展，远大于一些很神奇的技巧。」

When talking about his biggest expectation for this year, he used a slogan he often says: 而在谈及今年最大的期待，他用了一个自己常说的口号：

Train with finite context, use as infinite context. Train with finite context, use as infinite context.

The ability to train with finite context but support nearly infinite context during inference is expected to be realized this year, he predicts. Once implemented, this will unlock a truly continuous personal assistant – a model that dynamically manages memory during long-term interaction with users, no longer limited by a fixed context window. 用有限的上下文训练、却能在推理时支持近乎无限上下文的能力，他预计今年有望实现。一旦落地，这将解锁真正意义上的持续性个人助手 —— 模型在与用户长期交互中动态管理记忆，而不再受限于固定的上下文窗口。
