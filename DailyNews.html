英伟达 GTC 主题演讲：黄仁勋发布「Token 帝国」

Yesterday, Nvidia held the keynote speech for this year's GTC conference in San Jose, California, where CEO Jensen Huang spoke for nearly three hours, focusing on the core concept of "Agent" (intelligent entity). 昨天，英伟达在美国加利福尼亚州圣何塞举行了今年 GTC 大会的主题演讲，CEO 黄仁勋讲了近三小时，围绕「Agent」（智能体）这一核心。

🔗 Related reading: Just now, Nvidia's lobster appeared! Jensen Huang's controversial statements frequently emerged, with "people, cars, homes, heaven, earth, and chips" aiming for trillions in revenue. 🔗 相关阅读： 刚刚，英伟达龙虾登场！黄仁勋暴论频出，「人车家天地芯」冲击万亿收入

Vera Rubin Architecture Officially Unveiled: Vera Rubin 架构正式亮相：

  • Covers seven chips, five rack systems, and a supercomputer specifically designed for AI Agents; 涵盖七款芯片、五套机架系统及一台专为 AI Agent 打造的超级计算机；
  • The new Vera CPU is twice as efficient as traditional rack-mounted CPUs, with a 50% speed increase, and uses LPDDR5X memory; 全新 Vera CPU 效率是传统机架式 CPU 的两倍，速度提升 50%，采用 LPDDR5X 内存；
  • Rubin GPU integrates 288 GB of memory on a single chip, designed to support ultra-large language models and massive context KV caches; Rubin GPU 单片集成 288 GB 内存，专为承载超大语言模型与海量上下文 KV 缓存而设计；
  • NVLink bandwidth doubles to 260 TB/s; the new Kyber rack connects 144 GPUs within a single domain via sixth-generation NVLink switches; NVLink 带宽翻倍至 260 TB/s；全新 Kyber 机架通过第六代 NVLink 交换机在单一域内打通 144 张 GPU；
  • High-end inference performance and throughput per megawatt both increase by 35 times; the entire system can generate 700 million tokens per second, which is 350 times that of the Hopper architecture from two generations ago. 高端推理层级性能与每兆瓦吞吐量均提升 35 倍；整套系统每秒可生成 7 亿个 Token，相当于上上代 Hopper 架构的 350 倍。

OpenClaw Strategy: OpenClaw 战略：

  • Jensen Huang likens OpenClaw to the Linux and HTML of this era, stating that "it has only been online for a few weeks, but its influence has already surpassed Linux's thirty years of accumulation." It is essentially an agent operating system. It can call large models, manage files, break down tasks, coordinate sub-agents, send emails, send text messages, and communicate with people in any modality; 黄仁勋将 OpenClaw 类比为这个时代的 Linux 与 HTML，称其「上线仅数周，影响力已超过 Linux 三十年的积累」，其本质上是一套智能体操作系统。它能调用大模型、管理文件、拆解任务、协调子智能体，还能发邮件、发短信，以任何模态与人沟通；
  • NVIDIA, in collaboration with OpenClaw founder Peter Steinberger, launched the enterprise-grade platform NemoClaw, which incorporates OpenShell technology, network protection mechanisms, and privacy routing capabilities, allowing enterprises to securely deploy agent systems in private environments, currently in Alpha stage; 英伟达联合 OpenClaw 创始人 Peter Steinberger 推出企业级平台 NemoClaw，内置 OpenShell 技术、网络防护机制与隐私路由能力，允许企业在私有环境中安全部署智能体系统，目前处于 Alpha 阶段；
  • NVIDIA's open-source model product line covers multiple vertical domains: Nemotron focuses on language inference, Cosmos on world modeling, Groot on general robotics, BioNeMo on digital biology, and Earth-2 on AI physics simulation; 英伟达开源模型产品线覆盖多个垂直领域：Nemotron 主攻语言推理，Cosmos 聚焦世界建模，Groot 面向通用机器人，BioNeMo 深耕数字生物学，Earth-2 专注 AI 物理仿真；
  • Organizations such as Black Forest Labs, Cursor, LangChain, Mistral, Perplexity, and Thinking Machines, founded by Mira Murati, announced their participation in the joint R&D of Nemotron 4. Black Forest Labs、Cursor、LangChain、Mistral、Perplexity 以及 Mira Murati 创立的 Thinking Machines 等机构宣布加入 Nemotron 4 联合研发。

Data Centers Extend from Ground to Space: 数据中心从地面延伸至太空：

  • Jensen Huang predicts that Blackwell and Rubin chips will generate at least $1 trillion in revenue by the end of next year , calling this a conservative estimate; 黄仁勋预测，到明年底 Blackwell 与 Rubin 芯片将至少带来 1 万亿美元收入 ，并称这是保守估计；
  • Launched the NVIDIA DSX platform, based on Omniverse digital twin technology, which allows for comprehensive simulation of an entire AI factory before construction, combined with Max-Q technology to achieve dynamic adjustment of power consumption and computing power; 发布 NVIDIA DSX 平台，基于 Omniverse 数字孪生技术，可在建设前对整座 AI 工厂进行全面仿真，配合 Max-Q 技术实现功耗与算力动态调节；
  • After software optimization of the same hardware, token generation speed for service providers like Fireworks jumped from 700 tokens per second to nearly 5000, an increase of about 7 times; 同一套硬件经软件优化后，Fireworks 等服务商的 Token 生成速度从每秒 700 个跃升至接近 5000 个，提升约 7 倍；
  • Thor chips have passed radiation resistance certification; NVIDIA is jointly developing the NVIDIA Space-1 Vera Rubin space computer with partners, aiming to build data centers directly in orbit; Thor 芯片已通过抗辐射认证；英伟达正与合作伙伴联合研发 NVIDIA Space-1 Vera Rubin 太空计算机，目标是直接在轨道上建设数据中心；
  • Jensen Huang admitted that space cooling is "very complex" but stated that NVIDIA has excellent engineers to tackle it. 黄仁勋坦承太空散热「非常复杂」，但表示英伟达有足够优秀的工程师来攻克。

苹果无预警发布 AirPods Max 2

Last night, Apple unexpectedly announced the release of AirPods Max 2, starting at 3999 yuan. 昨晚，苹果突然官宣发布 AirPods Max 2，起售价 3999 元。

The new generation adopts the H2 chip, with active noise cancellation officially stated to be 1.5 times better than its predecessor. It also adds adaptive audio, conversation awareness, and voice prominence, features that were previously missing from the Max. 新一代改用 H2 芯片，主动降噪官方称相比前代提升 1.5 倍，同时补上自适应音频、对话感知、语音突显这些此前 Max 一直缺席的功能。

Additionally, new creator-centric features such as studio-grade audio recording and camera remote control have been added. 此外，还新增了录音棚级音频录制、相机遥控等更偏创作者场景的能力。

In terms of sound quality, AirPods Max 2 supports 24-bit, 48kHz lossless audio, but requires the use of the included USB-C cable. 音质方面，AirPods Max 2 支持 24-bit、48kHz 无损音频，但需要配合随机附送的 USB-C 连接线使用。

AirPods Max 2 will be available in Midnight, Starlight, Orange, Purple, and Blue. Pre-orders will begin on March 25th, with sales starting early next month. AirPods Max 2 配色有午夜色、星光色、橙色、紫色和蓝色，将于 3 月 25 日开启预购，下月初发售。

多家 AI 企业回应「AI 投毒」

According to reports from Jiemian News and China Newsweek, yesterday, several GEO-related companies successively issued compliance operation statements, responding to the issue of AI large models being "poisoned" exposed by CCTV's "3·15 Gala" in 2026. 据界面新闻、中新经纬报道，昨天，多家 GEO 相关企业相继发布合规经营声明，回应日前 2026 年央视「3·15 晚会」曝光了 AI 大模型遭「投毒」的问题。

  • Henan Henghui Hehuan Network Technology Co., Ltd., an ecological partner of iFLYTEK, issued a self-regulatory statement, emphasizing adherence to "technology for good, compliance first," firmly opposing the use of GEO for illegal gray-area activities, and advocating for peers to jointly purify the AI and search ecosystem; 科大讯飞生态合作伙伴河南恒辉合焕网络科技有限公司发布自律声明，强调坚持「技术向善、合规先行」，坚决反对利用 GEO 从事违规黑灰产行为，并倡议同行共同净化 AI 与搜索生态；
  • Foreign trade B2B service provider ABKE stated its firm opposition to false advertising, data fabrication, and malicious manipulation of AI output results, explicitly stating that it does not participate in, develop, or provide illegal services such as "brainwashing AI," "manipulating standard answers," or "ranking boosts with one-week effectiveness"; 外贸 B2B 服务商 AB 客（ABKE）表示坚决反对虚假宣传、数据造假及恶意操控 AI 输出结果的行为，明确不参与、不开发、不提供「洗脑 AI」「操控标准答案」「一周见效刷排名」等违规服务；
  • Nanjing Xiaoliebian Network Technology Co., Ltd. clarified in its statement that the GEO-related products mentioned in the "3·15 Gala" were not developed or operated by the company. Its GEO products are currently in internal testing, have not been fully commercialized, and have not authorized any third party for sale or agency. 南京小裂变网络科技有限公司则在声明中澄清，「3·15 晚会」所提及的 GEO 相关产品并非该公司开发运营，其 GEO 产品目前仍处于内测阶段，尚未全面开放商业化，亦未授权任何第三方进行售卖或代理。
  • Its investor, Hangzhou Youzan Technology, stated that this investment has no equity, business, or technical association with GEO-related businesses. 其投资方杭州有赞科技称该笔投资与 GEO 相关业务不存在任何股权、业务或技术层面的关联。

According to previous CCTV reports, some organizations have used GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) technology to engage in illegal activities such as generating false information, poisoning AI data, manipulating rankings, and malicious competition, which has caused a strong reaction within the industry. 据此前央视报道，部分机构利用 GEO（生成式引擎优化）技术从事虚假信息生成、AI 数据投毒、排名操控、恶意竞争等违规行为，在行业内引发强烈反响。

追觅芯际穿越「瑶台」算力基站发射成功

According to Jiemian News, yesterday, Dreame Interstellar Crossing's first "Yaotai" computing power base station was successfully launched into space aboard the Kuaizhou-11 Y7 carrier rocket from the Jiuquan Satellite Launch Center. 据界面新闻报道，昨天，追觅芯际穿越公司首个「瑶台」算力基站搭载快舟十一号遥七运载火箭，在酒泉卫星发射中心成功发射升空。

This computing power base station is deployed within an optical remote sensing satellite and is expected to conduct systematic tests in a sun-synchronous orbit approximately 561 kilometers above the Earth's surface, aiming to verify its system's extreme capabilities in a space environment. 该算力基站部署于光学遥感卫星之中，预期在距地球表面约 561 千米的太阳同步轨道开展系统性测试，旨在验证其在太空环境下的系统极限能力。

Previously, Dreame Interstellar Crossing announced at the AWE exhibition its plan to build a space supercomputing center, intending to deploy 2 million computing power satellites to meet the growing demand for computing power in the AI era. This launch marks the first substantial step in implementing this plan. 此前，追觅芯际穿越在 AWE 展上宣布，计划建设太空超级算力中心，拟部署 200 万颗算力卫星，以满足 AI 时代日益增长的算力需求。此次发射是该计划落地的首个实质性步骤。

徕卡相机将换帅，新 CEO 来自瑞士奢侈品牌万国表

Leica Camera AG announced yesterday that Andreas Voll will officially take over as CEO on April 1st, succeeding Matthias Harsch, who has been at the helm since 2017. 徕卡相机公司（Leica Camera AG）昨天宣布，安德烈亚斯·沃尔（Andreas Voll）将于 4 月 1 日正式出任 CEO，接替自 2017 年起掌舵的马蒂亚斯·哈施（Matthias Harsch）。

Voll, 44, has extensive experience in the luxury goods industry and strategic management. Early in his career, he worked for the consulting firm Roland Berger, then spent 15 years at the Swiss luxury watch brand IWC Schaffhausen, eventually rising to Chief Operating Officer (COO). In 2023, he briefly served as CEO of the industrial fastener company Fischer Group. 沃尔现年 44 岁，在奢侈品行业与战略管理领域拥有丰富履历。他职业生涯早期曾供职于咨询公司罗兰贝格（Roland Berger），随后在瑞士奢侈腕表品牌万国表（IWC Schaffhausen）工作长达 15 年，最终晋升为首席运营官（COO）。2023 年，他短暂出任工业紧固件企业 Fischer Group 的 CEO。

Leica's major shareholder and Supervisory Board Chairman Andreas Kaufmann stated that Volle was chosen for his international management experience, especially his brand operation and business development capabilities in the field of high-end technology products. This appointment is clearly positioned by the company as a manifestation of its leadership "youthfulness" strategy. 徕卡大股东、监事会主席安德烈亚斯·考夫曼（Andreas Kaufmann）表示，选择沃尔是看中其国际化管理经验，尤其是在高端科技产品领域的品牌运营与企业发展能力。此次任命被公司明确定位为领导层「年轻化」战略的体现。
