不止折叠屏，曝苹果未来数年将推出十大新品类

Yesterday, Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman revealed in the latest edition of his PowerOn column that Apple plans to launch about 10 new product categories over the next few years, spanning smart home, AI wearables, and other hardware fields. The first to arrive will be the foldable iPhone, set to be released this September. 昨天，彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 在最新一期的 PowerOn 专栏中透露，未来数年内，苹果计划推出约 10 个全新产品品类，横跨智能家居、AI 穿戴设备及其他硬件领域。而首个到来的，正是今年 9 月即将发布的折叠屏 iPhone。

John Ternus will officially take over as CEO on September 1st this year, and will host the launch of this product less than two weeks later. Gurman stated that Apple, in planning the transition, intentionally wants the new leader to personally stand on stage, making them the "spokesperson" for this new product category. John Ternus 将于今年 9 月 1 日正式接任 CEO，不到两周后便将主持发布这款产品。Gurman 称，苹果在规划交接过渡时，有意让新任领导者亲自站台，使其成为这一新品类的「代言人」。

It is reported that the development of the foldable iPhone is led by Ternus, focusing on durability, performance, shallower creases, and an internal screen with an iPad-like ratio, with an estimated price of over $2000 . 据悉，折叠屏 iPhone 的研发由 Ternus 主导，主打耐用性、性能、更浅的折痕以及接近 iPad 比例的内屏， 售价预计将达 2000 美元以上 。

The report also points out that the foldable iPhone will bring a "tailwind" to Ternus's reputation and revenue after taking over the company; at the same time, it will also use this to showcase its own product style. 报道还指出，折叠屏 iPhone 还将为 Ternus 的声量以及接手公司后的营收带来「顺风局」；同时其还将借此展示反映自身的产品风格。

  • Smart home directions include HomePods with screens, desktop robots equipped with movable robotic arms, and home security devices that rival Ring and Google Nest; 智能家居方向包括带屏幕的 HomePod、搭载可移动机械臂的桌面机器人，以及对标 Ring 和 Google Nest 的家用安防设备；
  • AI camera wearables include smart glasses competing with Meta Ray-Ban, AI AirPods with built-in low-resolution cameras, and AI pendants that can be worn as necklaces or brooches; AI 摄像穿戴方向则涵盖竞争 Meta Ray-Ban 的智能眼镜、内置低分辨率摄像头的 AI AirPods，以及可作为项链或胸针佩戴的 AI 挂件；
  • Additionally, Apple is also developing touchscreen Macs (expected to launch by the end of this year or early next year), AR glasses planned for release between 2028 and 2030, and an approximately 20-inch foldable iPad. 此外，苹果还在研发触控屏 Mac（预计于今年底或明年初推出）、计划在 2028 至 2030 年间发布的 AR 眼镜，以及一款约 20 英寸的折叠屏 iPad。

Ternus stated at a recent all-hands employee meeting that Apple is about to "change the world" and called the current product lineup "the most exciting" of his career. Ternus 在近期的员工全员大会上表示，苹果即将「改变世界」，并称目前的产品线是他职业生涯中「最令人兴奋的」。

内存涨疯了，三星手机部门面临史上首次亏损

According to South Korean media "Money Today," Samsung's MX (Mobile eXperience) division is facing the risk of its first annual loss, as the AI industry's large-scale acquisition of low-power LPDDR memory is pushing the smartphone industry into a rare cost crisis. 据韩国《今日货币》报道，三星 MX（移动体验）事业部正首次面临全年亏损的风险，原因是 AI 产业对低功耗内存 LPDDR 的大规模抢占，正将智能手机行业推入一场罕见的成本危机。

Roh Tae-moon, head of Samsung's MX division, recently conveyed to management his concerns about the "possibility of an annual loss for the MX division." If this expectation comes true, it would be the first annual loss for Samsung's MX division since its establishment. 三星 MX 事业部负责人卢泰文近期已向管理层传达了对「MX 事业部出现年度亏损可能性」的担忧。若这一预期成真，将是三星 MX 事业部自成立以来的首次全年亏损。

The root of this crisis lies in the complete disruption of the LPDDR supply and demand structure by the AI industry — a single Nvidia AI supercomputer "Vera Rubin NVL72" consumes LPDDR5X memory equivalent to the total usage of approximately 4600 Galaxy S26 Ultra phones, and Tesla's new generation AI chips also plan to adopt LPDDR. 这场危机的根源在于 LPDDR 供需结构被 AI 产业彻底打乱 —— 英伟达 AI 超算「Vera Rubin NVL72」单台消耗的 LPDDR5X 内存，相当于约 4600 部 Galaxy S26 Ultra 的总用量，特斯拉新一代 AI 芯片同样计划采用 LPDDR。

Industry insiders stated: "The AI industry has absorbed mobile memory, making it difficult for phone manufacturers to even secure supply, and the possibility of further price increases in the second half of the year is quite high." 有行业人士表示：「AI 产业已将移动端内存一并吸纳，手机厂商连货源都难以保障，下半年售价进一步上涨的可能性相当大。」

澳大利亚未成年人社交媒体禁令形同虚设，六成青少年仍在「照刷不误」

According to Fortune, Australia officially implemented a social media ban for minors under 16 last December, but the latest survey shows that the actual implementation effect of the ban is far from ideal. 据《财富》报道，澳大利亚于去年 12 月正式实施针对 16 岁以下未成年人的社交媒体禁令，但最新调查显示，该禁令的实际执行效果远不理想。

The UK suicide prevention organization Molly Rose Foundation surveyed 1050 Australian teenagers aged between 12 and 15 last month, and the results showed that over 60% of teenagers who already had social media accounts before the ban was implemented can still access at least one platform normally. 英国自杀预防机构 Molly Rose Foundation 上月对 1050 名年龄在 12 至 15 岁之间的澳大利亚青少年展开调查，结果显示超过 60% 在禁令实施前已拥有社交媒体账号的青少年，目前仍能正常访问至少一个平台。

TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram all retained over half of their underage users, and about two-thirds of respondents stated that these platforms "did not take any action" on their existing accounts. TikTok、YouTube、Instagram 均保留了超过半数的未成年用户，约三分之二的受访者表示，这些平台对其此前已有的账号「未采取任何行动」。

Teenagers are bypassing the ban in various ways. A 14-year-old girl said she plans to use her mother's facial recognition to log into Snapchat and Instagram; in a related Reddit discussion thread, a user suggested buying mesh masks sold on Temu to trick the app's facial recognition system. 青少年绕过禁令的方式五花八门。一名年仅 14 岁的女孩表示，她计划借用母亲的面部识别功能登录 Snapchat 和 Instagram；在 Reddit 相关讨论帖中，有用户建议购买 Temu 上出售的网格面具来欺骗应用程序的人脸识别系统。

In response to these chaotic situations, Australia's internet regulator has announced an investigation into five major social media platforms to assess whether they have violated the ban. 面对上述乱象，澳大利亚互联网监管机构已宣布对五大社交媒体平台展开调查，评估其是否存在违反禁令的行为。

💡 美联储研究：自 ChatGPT 上线以来，美国编程岗位三年少了 50 万

According to The Decoder, a working paper recently published by the Federal Reserve Board shows that since ChatGPT went online in November 2022, the growth rate of employment in programming-related positions in the United States has nearly halved. 据 The Decoder 报道，美联储理事会日前发布的一项工作论文显示，自 2022 年 11 月 ChatGPT 上线以来，美国编程相关岗位的就业增速已近乎腰斩。

The research team cross-referenced the Department of Labor's occupational database with household employment surveys to identify programming-intensive jobs, which account for about 3.7% of the total US workforce. Before ChatGPT's launch, the annual growth rate for such positions was close to 5%; after its launch, it sharply declined, and employment growth in the IT services and software development industries has nearly stalled. 研究团队将劳工部职业数据库与住户就业调查交叉比对，锁定约占全美劳动力 3.7% 的编程密集型岗位。ChatGPT 上线前，此类岗位年增速接近 5%；上线后急剧下滑，IT 服务与软件开发行业就业增长几近停滞。

After constructing a "counterfactual employment curve" to exclude macroeconomic interference, researchers found that programmer employment is still contracting at a rate of about 3 percentage points per year — companies are actively reducing the proportion of programming positions, with a cumulative gap of approximately 500,000 jobs over three years . 研究者构建「反事实就业曲线」排除宏观干扰后发现，程序员就业仍以每年约 3 个百分点的速度收缩 —— 企业正在主动压缩编程岗位占比， 三年累计缺口约达 50 万个岗位 。

"The employment gap will not significantly widen until mid-2024, suggesting that companies need sufficient time to observe the actual improvement in model capabilities before translating it into recruitment reduction decisions." 「就业缺口要到 2024 年中期才显著扩大，暗示企业需要足够时间观察模型能力的实际改善，才会将其转化为招聘收缩决策。」

The study also points out that programmer salaries have not seen a significant decline currently, with the impact mainly reflected in the number of positions. The authors also cautiously emphasize that a 500,000 gap does not equate to 500,000 people losing their jobs , and changes in tax policies other than AI could also be confounding variables. 研究同时指出，目前程序员薪资并未出现明显下滑，冲击主要体现在岗位数量层面。作者也审慎强调， 50 万缺口不等同于 50 万人失业 ，AI 之外的税务政策变化亦可能是干扰变量。

The core requirement of the new regulations is that non-bank payment institutions must not include financial products such as loans and asset management products in payment tool options, nor provide marketing services for them. 新规核心要求：非银行支付机构不得将贷款、资产管理产品等金融产品列入支付工具选项，也不得为其提供营销服务。
