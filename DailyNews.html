SpaceX 上市首日收涨 19%，市值升至 2.1 万亿美元，马斯克成全球首位万亿富豪

According to Bloomberg and Wired, SpaceX officially began trading on Nasdaq last night Beijing time, with an offering price of $135 per share, an IPO valuation of approximately $1.75 trillion, and a fundraising size of $75 billion, surpassing the record set by Saudi Aramco in 2019, becoming the largest IPO in U.S. stock market history. 据彭博社、《连线》报道，SpaceX 于北京时间昨日晚间在纳斯达克正式挂牌交易，发行价为每股 135 美元，IPO 估值约 1.75 万亿美元，募资规模达 750 亿美元，超越沙特阿美 2019 年创下的纪录，成为美股历史上规模最大的 IPO。

On its first day of trading, SpaceX's stock opened at $150, briefly touching $176.52 during the day, and finally closed up 19.22% at $160.95, raising the company's market capitalization to approximately $2.1 trillion. Driven by this, Elon Musk's personal net worth surpassed $1 trillion, making him the world's first trillionaire. 上市首日，SpaceX 股价开盘报 150 美元，盘中一度触及 176.52 美元，最终收涨 19.22%，报 160.95 美元，公司市值升至约 2.1 万亿美元。受此推动，Elon Musk 个人净资产突破 1 万亿美元，成为全球首位万亿级富豪。

After the IPO, Musk will continue to hold 82.4% voting control of SpaceX and face a 366-day lock-up period for his shares. Musk's wealth is primarily held in the form of equity, including stakes in SpaceX, Tesla, and other affiliated companies, with some shares subject to selling restrictions. 上市后，马斯克将继续持有 SpaceX 82.4% 的投票控制权，并面临为期 366 天的股份锁定期。马斯克的财富主要以股权形式持有，其中包括 SpaceX、特斯拉及其他旗下企业的持股，部分股份受到出售限制。

Musk stated in a pre-IPO livestream that the funds raised from the IPO will be used to support the company's entry into a 'significant growth phase,' including deploying over 100,000 communication satellites in low Earth orbit, building space AI data centers, and jointly constructing a chip factory named 'Terafab' with Tesla and Intel in Texas. 马斯克在上市前的直播中表示，IPO 募集的资金将用于支持公司进入「显著增长阶段」，包括在低轨部署超过 10 万颗通信卫星、构建太空 AI 数据中心，以及与特斯拉、英特尔在德克萨斯州合建名为「Terafab」的芯片工厂。

Regarding rumors about a merger between SpaceX and Tesla, SpaceX President and COO Gwynne Shotwell responded in an interview with CNBC that while there are indeed technological and resource synergies and convergence between the two companies in the future, and a merger "might make Elon's life a bit easier," the team's current focus remains on rocket launches, Starlink broadband expansion, and International Space Station missions. 针对外界关于 SpaceX 与特斯拉合并的传闻，SpaceX 总裁兼首席运营官格温·肖特维尔（Gwynne Shotwell）在接受 CNBC 采访时回应称，两家公司在未来确实存在技术与资源上的协同与汇聚，合并「可能会让埃隆的生活变得更轻松一些」，但目前团队的精力仍集中在火箭发射、星链宽带拓展和国际空间站任务上。

大疆就云台相机专利起诉影石，影石反诉

According to reports from "National Business Daily," DJI and DJI Osmo filed patent infringement lawsuits against Insta360 and its affiliated companies in the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Texas on June 10th and 11th, U.S. local time. 据《每日经济新闻》报道，大疆及大疆灵眸已于美国当地时间 6 月 10 日和 11 日，在美国德州东区联邦地区法院对影石创新及其关联公司提起专利侵权诉讼。

This lawsuit targets Insta360's recently launched Luna series dual-camera handheld gimbal camera in the U.S. DJI alleges that the product infringes upon 2 of its design patents and 4 invention patents, with the patents covering technologies and designs related to gimbal electromechanical control and shooting control for the Osmo Pocket series. 本次诉讼针对影石近日在美国首发的 Luna 系列双摄手持云台相机。大疆指控该产品涉嫌侵犯其 2 件外观设计专利和 4 件发明专利，涉诉专利覆盖 Osmo Pocket 系列相关的云台机电控制、拍摄控制等技术和设计。

DJI stated in its complaint that Insta360's Luna series, from its rotating screen appearance to features like gimbal mode switching and target tracking, is suspected of falling within the scope of patent protection, and requested the court to issue a permanent injunction, demand the return of infringing profits, and order payment of damages. 大疆在诉状中称，影石 Luna 系列从旋转屏外观到云台模式切换、目标追踪等功能均涉嫌落入专利保护范围，并请求法院发布永久禁止令、要求返还侵权利润和支付损害赔偿。

Insta360 subsequently filed a patent countersuit against DJI in the U.S., involving 5 invention patents related to gimbal cameras and panoramic cameras, covering technologies such as gimbal stabilization algorithms, gimbal pointing control, camera smooth anti-shake, motion data overlay, and panoramic video anti-shake. 影石随后在美国对大疆提起专利反诉，涉及云台相机、全景相机等 5 项发明专利，覆盖云台增稳算法、云台指向控制、相机平滑防抖、运动数据叠加和全景视频防抖等技术。

OpenAI 允许 Codex 用户「囤」重置机会：额度耗尽时自己决定何时刷新

OpenAI announced yesterday the launch of a "reserve reset" feature for Codex, allowing paying users to "stockpile" opportunities to reset rate limits in the future, rather than passively waiting for the system to automatically clear them at fixed intervals. OpenAI 昨日宣布为 Codex 上线「存备重置」功能，付费用户今后可以把速率限制的重置机会「囤」起来，不必再被动等待系统按固定周期自动清零。

Previously, Codex's rate limits would automatically reset periodically, and users could not intervene with the timing – if the quota ran out, they could only wait, and if a reset occurred, they might not necessarily use it. 此前，Codex 的速率限制每隔一段时间自动重置一次，用户无法干预时机——额度耗尽了只能干等，重置到了却不一定用得上。

With the introduction of the new feature, reset opportunities can be accumulated in the account like "vouchers," and manually triggered from the personal profile menu when truly needed. Reserved reset quotas are valid for 30 days. 新功能出台后，重置机会可以像「券」一样攒在账户里，等到真正需要的时候，再从个人资料菜单手动触发。存备的重置额度有效期为 30 天。

This update is available to Go, Plus, Pro, and Business paying users, with each user receiving one free reset opportunity. 此次更新面向 Go、Plus、Pro 及 Business 付费用户，每名用户各赠送一次免费重置机会。

💡 苹果高管：Siri AI 是实用工具，不是情感伴侣

Apple's Senior Vice President of Software Engineering, Craig Federighi, and head of marketing, Greg Joswiak, were interviewed by "Mostly Human" during WWDC 2026, delving into Apple's functional positioning of AI, privacy strategy, and child safety issues. 苹果软件工程高级副总裁 Craig Federighi 与营销主管 Greg Joswiak 在 WWDC 2026 期间接受了《Mostly Human》采访，深入探讨了苹果对 AI 的功能定位、隐私策略及儿童安全等议题。

In the interview held the day before yesterday, Federighi clarified Siri's tool-like nature and emphasized that its original design intent was not to be an AI companion. Regarding the current situation where some AI products on the market focus on building emotional connections to enhance engagement, Federighi stated: 在前天举行的采访中，Federighi 明确了 Siri 的工具属性，并强调其设计初衷并非 AI 伴侣。针对市面上部分 AI 产品侧重于通过建立情感连接来提升参与度的现状，Federighi 表示：

Many existing chatbots largely focus on engagement, even with a certain "flattering" overtone. They try every means to attract you, may encourage you to reveal personal information, and build some kind of connection based on that. 现有许多聊天机器人很大程度上专注于参与度，甚至带有某种「谄媚」色彩。它们想方设法吸引你，可能会鼓励你透露个人信息，并以此为基础建立某种联系。

We hold the exact opposite view on this. In our design, Siri will explicitly state: "Listen, that's not my job. I'm here to help you, I can help you complete tasks, help you understand the world." But if you try to treat Siri as a romantic partner, Siri will not cooperate. 我们对此持完全相反的观点。在我们的设计中，Siri 会明确表示：「听着，那不是我的职责。我是来帮你的，我可以帮你完成任务，帮你了解世界。」但如果你试图把 Siri 当作浪漫伴侣，Siri 是不会配合的。

Joswiak added that Apple does not pursue "AI for AI's sake," but rather is committed to seamlessly integrating AI technology into existing products. He stated that Apple wants technology to "disappear," allowing users to focus on the content itself, and does not want iPhone users to have to become "prompt experts" to use AI features. Joswiak 补充提到，苹果并不追求「为了 AI 而做 AI」，而是致力于将 AI 技术无感地融入现有产品中。他表示，苹果希望技术能够「消失」，让用户专注于内容本身，且不希望 iPhone 用户必须成为「提示词专家」才能使用 AI 功能。

Regarding privacy protection, Federighi emphasized Apple's "privacy first" principle. He pointed out that user data remains local on the iPhone and is fully controlled by the user, and Apple Inc. cannot access this information. 在隐私保护方面，Federighi 强调了苹果「隐私先行」的原则。他指出，用户数据保留在 iPhone 本地并由用户完全控制，苹果公司无法获取这些信息。

He believes that this practice of completely isolating local knowledge from company access permissions is a key advantage that distinguishes Apple from other competitors in the industry. 他认为，这种将本地知识与公司获取权限彻底隔离的做法，是苹果区别于行业内其他竞争对手的关键优势。

One Fun Thing｜这件夹克，能从空气里取水

An engineering team from the University of Texas at Austin recently unveiled a wearable water-harvesting jacket, integrating previously stationary atmospheric water harvesting devices into textiles. The related paper was published in 'Science Advances'. 得州大学奥斯汀分校工程团队近日发布一种可穿戴取水夹克，把原本固定式的空气取水设备做进纺织品。相关论文发表于《Science Advances》。

This jacket uses a hierarchical open-pore fiber called HOP-Fiber. The material captures water vapor from the air and then transports the moisture to a detachable water collection unit. After the collection unit is placed into a foldable collection device, potable water can be produced through heating. 这件夹克使用名为 HOP-Fiber 的分级开放式多孔纤维，材料会捕获空气中的水蒸气，再把水分输送到可拆卸集水单元。集水单元放进可折叠收集装置后，经加热即可产生可饮用水。

Experimental data shows that in environments with 20% to 80% relative humidity, this textile material can produce 3.76 to 7.45 liters of water per kilogram per day; the actual system recovers approximately 410 to 894 milliliters of water daily. 实验数据显示，在 20% 至 80% 相对湿度环境下，这种纺织材料每天每公斤可产生 3.76 至 7.45 升水；实际系统每天回收约 410 至 894 毫升水。

The team also conducted outdoor tests in Xichang, China, Austin, USA, and Chengdu, China. In the arid environment of Xichang, 410 milliliters of water samples were collected daily, and the lithium ion residue conformed to WHO drinking water standards. 团队还在中国西昌、美国奥斯汀和中国成都做了户外测试，其中西昌干旱环境下每天采集 410 毫升水样，锂离子残留量符合 WHO 饮用水标准。

The paper also mentions that this fabric can withstand curling, folding and twisting, and its applications are not limited to jackets but can also be incorporated into backpacks, tents, and emergency shelters. 论文还提到，这种面料能承受卷曲、折叠和扭曲，应用对象不只限于夹克，也可以进入背包、帐篷和应急避难所。
