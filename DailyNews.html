曝苹果 M8 系列 2028 年登场，制程升级至 1.4 纳米

According to Bloomberg, Apple has significantly adjusted its Mac chip roadmap for the coming years, with the M7 series chip development accelerated. The M7 Ultra chip, in particular, is designed to support up to 1.5 TB of unified memory. 据彭博社报道，苹果已对未来数年的 Mac 芯片路线图进行大幅调整，M7 系列芯片研发进度被提前，其中 M7 Ultra 芯片的设计目标是支持最高 1.5 TB 统一内存。

In addition, reports also indicate that Apple is developing a new generation of M8 series chips, with the processor codenamed 'Soko' expected to be launched in 2028, making it a key product in Apple's M8 series designed for stronger AI capabilities. 此外，报道还指出苹果正在开发新一代 M8 系列芯片，代号「Soko」的处理器预计于 2028 年推出，是苹果 M8 系列中面向更强 AI 能力打造的重点产品。

The chip is expected to use a 1.4-nanometer process, further improving energy efficiency compared to previous processes. In addition to Soko, Apple is also developing another series of new chips codenamed 'Cardinal' for high-end Macs, also belonging to the 2028 generation of products, and also transitioning to the 1.4-nanometer process. 该芯片预计将采用 1.4 纳米制程，相比此前工艺再进一步提升能效表现。除 Soko 外，苹果还在为高端 Mac 开发另一系列代号为「Cardinal」的新芯片，同样归入 2028 年这一代产品，一并转向 1.4 纳米制程。

海力士 CEO：内存供不应求或持续至 2030 年

According to Reuters, SK Hynix CEO Kwak Noh-jung recently stated that the global memory industry is facing unprecedented challenges, and is expected to experience the most severe supply shortage situation in history next year. 据路透社报道，海力士 CEO 郭鲁正（Kwak Noh-jung）近日表示，全球内存行业正面临前所未有的挑战，预计明年将迎来史上最严峻的供应短缺局势。

Although the company is actively expanding capacity, Kwak Noh-jung predicts that customer demand will continue to exceed production capacity, this supply-demand imbalance may continue beyond 2030 . 尽管公司正积极扩充产能，但郭鲁正预测，客户需求将持续超过生产能力， 这种供不应求的局面可能一直延续到 2030 年以后 。

Regarding capacity expansion, Kwak Noh-jung mentioned that the United States, Japan, and Southeast Asia are all on the candidate list for future wafer fab construction, but no final decision has been made yet. Site selection will prioritize regions with sufficient and cost-competitive supplies of electricity, water resources, land, and talent. 针对产能扩张，郭鲁正提到美国、日本和东南亚均在未来建设晶圆厂的候选名单中，但目前尚未作出最终决定。选址将优先考虑电力、水资源、土地及人才供应充足且具备成本竞争力的地区。

Currently, SK Hynix plans to invest approximately $4 billion in Indiana to build an advanced packaging plant, and another $10 billion to develop AI solutions business in the United States. 目前，海力士已计划在印第安纳州投资约 40 亿美元建设先进封装厂，并投入 100 亿美元在美国开发 AI 解决方案业务。

问界汽车由盈转亏，赛力斯上半年业绩预告亏损最高 18 亿元

Seres today issued an announcement, stating that the company expects a net loss attributable to shareholders of listed companies of 1.5 billion to 1.8 billion yuan in the first half of 2026, compared to a profit of 2.941 billion yuan in the same period last year, marking a year-on-year shift from profit to loss. The announcement explained the main reasons for the loss: 赛力斯今日发布公告，公司预计 2026 年上半年归属于上市公司股东的净利润为亏损 15 亿元至 18 亿元，上年同期为盈利 29.41 亿元，同比由盈转亏。公告解释了亏损的主要原因：

  • The company's production costs increased due to the rising prices of major raw materials such as storage chips, industrial metals, and lithium carbonate; 受存储芯片、工业金属、碳酸锂等主要原材料价格上涨影响，公司生产成本随之增加；
  • Based on the principle of prudence, and to further solidify overall asset quality and align with expected future asset returns, the company adjusted the book value of some existing assets with limited adaptability due to technological iterations and model changes. 公司基于审慎性原则，为进一步夯实整体资产质量，结合资产后续收益预期，对部分因技术迭代、车型换代而适配性有限的存量资产调整了账面价值。

Affected by the superposition of these two factors, the performance of the company's core subsidiary, AITO Automobile, fluctuated, turning from profit to loss, which in turn dragged down the company's overall performance. 受这两方面因素叠加影响，公司核心子公司问界汽车业绩出现波动，由盈利转为亏损，进而拖累公司整体业绩。

OpenAI's Thibault Sottiaux admitted that "not everything was done right" and stated that the team has been reading feedback, analyzing usage patterns, and communicating with users for the past 24 hours. 据 The Decoder 报道，OpenAI 在 ChatGPT Work 和 GPT-5.6 Sol 发布后收到大量反馈。OpenAI 的 Thibault Sottiaux 承认「没有把所有事情都做对」，并称团队过去 24 小时一直在阅读反馈、分析用量模式和与用户沟通。

OpenAI summarized four categories of issues: OpenAI 归纳出四类问题：

  • High-compute settings are too easily selected, and users are unclear how these settings consume credits; 高算力设置太容易被选中，用户不清楚这些设置如何消耗额度；
  • Desktop-side changes were too extensive at once, making familiar entry points like chat and projects harder to find; 桌面端一次性改动太大，聊天和项目等熟悉入口更难找；
  • Some multi-agent workflows experienced degradation; 部分多 Agent 工作流出现退化；
  • Bugs appeared in plugin submissions and other product stages. 插件提交和其他产品环节出现 bug。

The company has reset Codex and ChatGPT Work usage limits twice in one day and adjusted default settings and model selectors. Sottiaux also clarified that Codex will not be shut down ; next week's update will bring chat and projects back to the sidebar in a more familiar, customizable way, and make usage metrics and reset times more visible. 公司已在一天内两次重置 Codex 和 ChatGPT Work 用量限制，并调整默认设置和模型选择器。 Sottiaux 还澄清，Codex 不会被关闭 ；下周的更新会把聊天和项目以更熟悉、可自定义的方式放回侧边栏，并让用量指标和重置时间更可见。

💡 诺兰：年轻观众正在拒绝「AI 垃圾」

Nolan stated that despite the immense effort the tech industry has put into advancing AI, he was surprised by how quickly young audiences are rejecting this "fundamental technological leap." Citing his four children as an example, he pointed out that this generation, raised in an online environment, can instantly recognize and see through the essence of AI content: 诺兰表示，尽管科技行业投入了巨大精力推动人工智能发展，但年轻观众对这种「基础性技术跃迁」的屏弃速度之快令他感到惊讶。他以自己的四个孩子为例，指出这一代生长于网络环境的年轻人能瞬间识别并看透 AI 内容的本质：

Their judgment of AI garbage is immediate and severe. They can quickly see its true colors—because it was born from the online world they are very familiar with. 他们对 AI 垃圾的判断是即时且严厉的。他们能很快看清它的真面目——因为这本身就诞生于他们非常熟悉的在线世界。

Nolan disagrees with external concerns about "young audiences' fragmented attention." He believes that mysterious, obscure, and even David Lynch-esque works like "The Backrooms" can keep young people entertained, proving they are fully capable of appreciating three-hour, contemplative epic films. 针对外界对「年轻观众注意力碎片化」的担忧，诺兰持反对意见。他认为，像《后室》这样神秘、晦涩甚至带有大卫·林奇风格的作品能让年轻人乐此不疲，足以证明他们完全有能力欣赏长达三小时、充满沉思的史诗电影。

Furthermore, Nolan reiterated his stance against smartphones. He confessed that if he owned a phone, he would fall into deep addiction, and he prefers to use fragmented time, such as waiting for a car or dining, to advance creative thinking, rather than jumping into the noise of the digital world. He believes this "disconnected" state is key to protecting creative authenticity. 此外，诺兰再次重申了自己对智能手机的抵制立场。他坦言自己如果拥有手机会陷入深度成瘾，而他更倾向于利用等车、就餐等碎片时间来推进创作思考，而非跳进数字世界的噪音中。他认为这种「断联」状态是保护创作真诚性的关键。

  • Integrated and added a "Storage Management" entry; 整合并新增了「存储管理」入口；
