中汽协会就供应链账期问题发表倡议（多车企回复中汽协会账期倡议）

Yesterday morning, the China Association of Automobile Manufacturers (CAAM) released the "Initiative on Standardizing Supplier Payment for Automobile OEMs" in Beijing, aiming to promote the implementation of the "Regulations on Ensuring Payment to Small and Medium-sized Enterprises," support key automakers in fulfilling their supplier payment term commitments with high quality, and build a collaborative win-win development ecosystem for "OEMs - Parts Suppliers." 昨天上午，中国汽车工业协会在北京发布《汽车整车企业供应商账款支付规范倡议》，旨在推动落实《保障中小企业款项支付条例》，支持重点车企高质量践行供应商账期承诺，构建「整车 — 零部件」协作共赢发展生态。

The initiative puts forward specific requirements around four key links: order confirmation, delivery and acceptance, payment and settlement, and contract term : 该倡议围绕 订单确认、交付与验收、支付与结算、合同期限 四个关键环节提出具体要求：

  • Order Confirmation: Both supply and demand parties clarify order date, specifications, quantity, and delivery requirements through purchase orders and delivery notices. 订单确认：供需双方通过采购订单和交货通知单明确订货日期、规格、数量及交货要求。
  • Delivery and Acceptance: Automakers should complete acceptance within principle of no more than 3 working days after receiving goods and issue an acceptance form. 交付与验收：车企应在收货后原则上不超过 3 个工作日完成验收，并出具验收单。
  • Payment and Settlement: Encourage payment using cash or bank acceptance bills ; small and medium-sized enterprises are advocated to use cash or bank acceptance bills entirely; if the supply unit price is not fixed, an advance payment of no less than 80% of the contract unit price or 50% of the development fixed unit price should be made, with subsequent adjustments for overpayment or underpayment. 支付与结算：鼓励使用 现金或银行承兑汇票 支付，中小企业倡导全部采用现金或银行承兑汇票；供货单价未定时，先行支付不低于合同单价 80% 或开发定点单价 50% 的金额，后续多退少补。
  • Contract Term: Advocate for establishing long-term stable cooperative relationships, with each contract valid for no less than 1 year. 合同期限：倡导建立长期稳定合作关系，每次合同有效期不少于 1 年。

CAAM stated that this move is to address issues such as "price compression and extended payment cycles" caused by intensified competition in the automotive market, protect the legitimate rights and interests of suppliers, especially small and medium-sized enterprises, and calls on OEMs to incorporate this initiative into their contracts and genuinely fulfill their payment term commitments. 中汽协会表示，此举是为应对汽车市场竞争加剧导致的「压价、延长付款周期」等问题，维护供应商特别是中小企业的合法权益，并呼吁整车企业将该倡议纳入合约，切实履行账期承诺。

Following the release of the initiative, automakers such as Nio, Great Wall, Xiaomi, Leapmotor, Xpeng, Geely, BAIC, JAC, and FAW have responded, successively issuing statements expressing their active implementation of the "Initiative," committing to complete payment within 60 days, and promoting the construction of a collaborative win-win industrial ecosystem for "OEMs-Parts Suppliers." 在该倡议发表后，蔚来、长城、小米、零跑、小鹏、吉利、北汽、江汽、一汽等车企纷纷响应，陆续发布声明表示将积极落实《倡议》，承诺在 60 天内完成账款支付，推动构建「整车—零部件」协作共赢的产业生态。

According to relevant information released by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, as of yesterday afternoon, 17 automakers, including Dongfeng, SAIC, Chery, Seres, Li Auto, Changan, GAC, and BYD, which had previously expressed their positions, have made statements. The pattern of upstream and downstream collaboration in the industry to promote high-quality development is accelerating. 据工信部发布的相关信息，截止昨日下午，包含此前已表态的东风、上汽、奇瑞、赛力斯、理想、长安、广汽、比亚迪在内，已有 17 家车企作出表态，行业上下游协同推动高质量发展的格局正在加速形成。

苹果推送 iOS 26 正式版

Early this morning, Apple pushed the official versions of operating systems such as iOS 26 and iPadOS 26. 今天凌晨，苹果推送 iOS 26、iPadOS 26 等操作系统的正式版。

For this OS 26 system family, from iPhone to Mac, from iPad to Vision Pro, Apple has unified the interface language and interaction logic across its entire product line. 本次 OS 26 系统家族，从 iPhone 到 Mac，从 iPad 到 Vision Pro，苹果统一了全家桶的界面语言和交互逻辑。

In terms of design, Apple named this UI design "Solarium," which includes new design elements such as "Liquid Glass." This glass material can reflect and refract the surrounding environment, and the overall visual style leans towards visionOS, extensively using glass material to handle hierarchical differentiation tasks within the interface. 设计方面，苹果给本次的 UI 设计取名为「Solarium（阳光房）」，包含了「液态玻璃（Liquid Glass）」等全新设计元素。这种玻璃材质可以反射和折射周围环境，整体视觉风格向 visionOS 靠拢，大量使用玻璃材质来承担界面中的层级分化任务。

For iOS 26, the lock screen has been upgraded with gravity-sensing spatial wallpaper effects; interfaces for Camera, Photos, Safari browser, etc., have been extremely simplified; CarPlay's visual language has been synchronously updated. It is understood that iOS 26 also added a battery management feature called "Adaptive Power," which is enabled by default on the iPhone 17 series and iPhone Air. iOS 26 方面，锁屏升级了基于重力感应的空间壁纸效果；相机、相册、Safari 浏览器等界面极致简化；CarPlay 同步更新视觉语言。据了解，iOS 26 还新增了一项名为「自适应电源」的电池管理功能，而该功能在 iPhone 17 系列以及 iPhone Air 上默认开启。

According to Apple's official documentation, this feature can learn user habits through on-device intelligent algorithms and automatically adjust power consumption as needed. It is worth noting that this feature requires about 7 days for background learning and calibration. 据苹果官方文档介绍，该功能能够通过设备端的智能算法学习用户的使用习惯，并根据需要来进行自动调节功耗。值得一提的是，该功能需要耗费约 7 天时间进行后台学习校准。

On iPadOS 26, there is a new window management system that supports free scaling, dragging, tiling, etc., providing a desktop-level multitasking experience; a new menu bar is introduced, supporting pull-down operations and search functions, and developers can also customize their own menus. iPadOS 26 上，拥有全新窗口管理系统，支持自由缩放、拖拽、平铺、等，提供桌面级别的多任务体验；引入全新菜单栏，支持下拉操作与搜索功能，开发者还可以定制自己的菜单。

According to 9to5Mac, Apple has updated its new technical support page, emphasizing the importance of iPhone system updates and their potential impact on devices in the documentation; it also explains to users why iPhones might experience short-term impacts on battery life and performance after updating to iOS 26. 另据 9to5Mac 报道，苹果目前已更新了新的技术支持页面，并在文档中强调了 iPhone 系统更新的重要性以及对设备的潜在影响； 其还向用户解释，为何在更新 iOS 26 系统之后，iPhone 可能会在短期内遇到续航和性能方面的影响。

Regarding battery life and performance changes, Apple explained that this is because the system needs to complete data and file indexing in the background, prepare for search functions, and also download new resources and update applications. These tasks will additionally occupy hardware resources. 针对续航、性能变化问题，苹果则解释表示，这是因为系统需要在后台完成数据和文件的索引，为搜索功能做准备，同时还会下载新资源和更新应用，这些任务会额外占用硬件资源。

At the same time, Apple also pointed out that new features in iOS 26 may enhance user experience, but some features may continuously consume more processing performance and battery. 同时，苹果还指出 iOS 26 中的新功能可能能够提升用户体验，但部分功能可能会持续消耗更多处理性能以及电池损耗。

According to reports, the vehicle has an R&D cycle of 800 days, involved over 1000 test cars, traversed 330 cities in 20 countries, accumulated over 20 million kilometers in test mileage, and underwent various complex environmental tests including extreme cold, high temperatures, and plateaus, all to ensure that every kilometer of its range is verifiable. 据介绍，该车型研发周期长达 800 天，投入超 1000 台测试车，跨越 20 国 330 城，累计测试里程超过 2000 万公里，经历极寒、高温、高原等多种复杂环境考验，旨在确保每一公里续航都经得起验证。

He Xiaopeng stated that he hopes the long-range performance of the Xpeng X9 Super Range-Extended version will allow "the warmth of home to extend further down the road." 何小鹏表示，希望小鹏 X9 超级增程的长续航表现，能让「家的温暖延续到更远的路上」。

It is worth noting that in the new product announcement of the 398th batch of "Road Motor Vehicle Manufacturers and Products Announcement" released by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology on August 8, the Xpeng X9 Range-Extended MPV also appeared, with the product name "Plug-in Range-Extended Hybrid Multi-Purpose Passenger Vehicle." 值得注意的是，8 月 8 日工信部发布的第 398 批《道路机动车辆生产企业及产品公告》新产品公示中，小鹏 X9 增程版 MPV 也现身其中，产品名称为「插电式增程混合动力多用途乘用车」。

Declaration information shows that the vehicle is 5316mm long, 1988mm wide, 1785mm high, with a wheelbase of 3160mm; it is equipped with CATL's lithium iron phosphate battery, a drive motor with a rated power of 110kW and a peak power of 210kW, and an engine with a maximum net power of 110kW. 申报信息显示，该车长 5316mm、宽 1988mm、高 1785mm，轴距为 3160mm；搭载中创新航的磷酸铁锂蓄电池，驱动电机额定功率 110kW、峰值功率 210kW，发动机最大净功率同为 110kW。
