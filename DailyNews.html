苹果发布 3 款 M5 家族新品

Last night, Apple simultaneously launched three products equipped with the latest M5 processor: the MacBook Pro 14-inch, iPad Pro, and Vision Pro. The new products will go on sale on October 22. 昨晚，苹果一口气上架了三款搭载最新 M5 处理器的产品，分别为 MacBook Pro 14 寸、iPad Pro、Vision Pro，新品将于 10 月 22 日发售。

M5 MacBook Pro 14-inch: Starting price 12999 RMB M5 MacBook Pro 14 寸：起售价 12999 元

Equipped with the base M5 processor featuring a 10+10 core specification, built on TSMC's 3nm process. According to charts on Apple's official website, this year's M5 offers 6.4 times the LLM prompt processing performance and 6.8 times the 3D rendering performance of the M1. 搭载 10+10 核规格的基础版 M5 处理器，基于 3nm 的台积电工艺打造。根据苹果官网的图表，今年 M5 的 LLM 提示词处理性能为 M1 的 6.4 倍、3D 渲染性能为 M1 的 6.8 倍。

Similar to the limitations of the M4 era, this M5 MacBook Pro still offers three memory options: 16/24/32GB, and the maximum optional hard drive has been upgraded to 4TB (minimum equipped with 512GB). 和 M4 时代的限制一样，这一次的 M5 MacBook Pro 仍然有 16/24/32GB 三档内存可选，最大可选的硬盘则升级到了 4TB（最低配备 512GB）。

The appearance remains consistent with the previous generation, featuring a 1600nits miniLED screen, with an optional anti-glare nano-texture glass version available. 外观则保持与前代一致，采用 1600nits 的 miniLED 屏幕，可以选配抗眩光的纳米纹理玻璃版本。

Also equipped with the new 10+10 core M5 processor, memory starts at 12GB (1T and 2T models come with 16GB). The appearance is identical to the previous generation, offering 11-inch and 13-inch versions with a dual-layer tandem OLED display. 同样搭载全新的 10+10 核规格 M5 处理器， 内存来到了 12GB 起步（1T 和 2T 配备 16GB）。外观与前代同样无差别，提供 11 英寸和 13 英寸两个版本选择，配备双层串联 OLED 显示屏。

M5 Vision Pro: M5 Vision Pro：

With the significant GPU performance improvements of the M5 processor mentioned earlier, this year's M5 Vision Pro, despite no changes in its exterior design, "offers faster performance, clearer system-wide details, and longer battery life." 搭配前文提到的 M5 处理器 GPU 性能大进步，今年的 M5 Vision Pro 虽然外形没有什么变化，但是「可提供更快的性能、更清晰的整个系统细节以及更长的电池续航时间」。

始祖鸟承担「蔡国强烟花秀」赔偿修复责任

According to CCTV News, on October 15, the Shigatse City, Tibet investigation and verification team reported on the handling of the "Cai Guo-Qiang: Ascending Dragon" fireworks show. The report indicates: 据央视新闻报道，10 月 15 日，西藏日喀则市调查核查组通报《蔡国强：升龙》烟花秀调查处置情况。从通报内容显示：

  • Emergency monitoring results for surface water points all met the Class II water quality standard of the "Environmental Quality Standards for Surface Water" (GB3838-2002); 地表水点位应急监测结果均满足《地表水环境质量标准》(GB3838-2002) II 类水质标准；
  • The concentrations of major characteristic pollutants at atmospheric monitoring points were all below the Class I zone limits of the "Ambient Air Quality Standards" (GB3095-2012); 大气监测点位主要特征污染物浓度均低于《环境空气质量标准》(GB3095-2012) 一类区限值；
  • Leveling operations, human trampling, and vehicle compaction caused damage to the soil turf layer structure, with a damaged area of 15.29 mu; 平整作业、人员踩踏及车辆碾压对土壤草毡层结构造成破坏，破坏面积 15.29 亩；
  • Fireworks residues, plastic debris, and other materials generated by the event were not thoroughly cleaned up; 事件产生的烟花残留物、塑料碎屑等清理不彻底；
  • The instantaneous strong light and loud noise generated by the fireworks display caused short-term disturbance to wildlife. 烟花燃放产生的瞬时强光与巨响对野生动物造成了短时惊扰。

The report concluded that this event was a human disturbance activity carried out in an ecologically sensitive plateau area, with its impact on the ecological environment manifesting as localized interference. Although the short-term direct pollution and damage were limited, potential ecological risks require monitoring and tracking. 报告认为，本次事件属于在高原生态敏感区实施的人为扰动活动，对生态环境的影响表现为局部干扰，其短期直接污染及破坏程度虽有限，但潜在的生态风险需监测跟踪。

Currently, Shigatse City has commissioned an assessment of ecological environment damage compensation, and based on the investigation of ecological environment damage: 目前，日喀则市已委托开展生态环境损害赔偿鉴定评估工作，根据生态环境损害调查情况：

  • Legally pursue Beijing Cai Guo-Qiang Art Studio for ecological environment damage compensation and ecological restoration responsibilities. Relevant departments in Shigatse City will carry out long-term monitoring and post-assessment work. 依法追究北京蔡国强艺术工作室生态环境损害赔偿和生态修复责任，日喀则市相关部门将开展长期监测和后评估工作。
  • The "Arc'teryx" brand, as a sponsor of this event, shall legally bear corresponding responsibilities for ecological environment damage compensation and ecological restoration. 「始祖鸟」品牌作为此次活动的赞助商，依法承担相应的生态环境损害赔偿和生态修复责任。

苹果在华启动 10 亿元新能源基金

Recently, Apple announced that over 90% of its manufacturing in China now uses renewable energy, with more than a hundred suppliers contributing to this progress. 日前，苹果宣布在中国超过 90% 的生产制造现已采用可再生能源，逾百家供应商共同促成了这一进展。

Apple stated that this milestone was achieved through a decade of close collaboration between the company and its suppliers, as both parties steadily advance towards the goal of fully using renewable energy for all Apple product production by 2030. 苹果方面表示，这一里程碑的达成，源于公司与供应商长达十年的紧密协作，双方正稳步推进到 2030 年前实现所有苹果产品生产全面使用可再生能源的目标。

Based on this progress, Apple also announced that its suppliers have jointly launched a new investment fund aimed at supporting the construction of renewable energy infrastructure in China. 基于此进展，苹果同时宣布其供应商已联合启动一项全新的投资基金，旨在支持中国可再生能源基础设施建设。

It is reported that the "China Renewable Energy Infrastructure Fund," with a total scale of 1 billion RMB (150 million USD), is entirely led by Apple suppliers and plans to add 1 million megawatt-hours of clean electricity to China's grid by 2030. 据悉，总规模达十亿元人民币（ 1.5 亿美元）的「中国可再生能源基础设施基金」完全由苹果供应商主导，并计划到 2030 年为中国电网新增 100 万兆瓦时的清洁电力。

This new China Renewable Energy Infrastructure Fund was jointly initiated by CICC Capital and Huaneng Investment, with battery supplier ATL participating as an anchor investor. Several Apple supply chain companies, including Pengding Holdings, Suzhou Dongshan Precision Manufacturing, Hon Hai Precision Industry, and YUTO Tech, have completed their equity investments. 这项全新的中国可再生能源基础设施基金由中金资本与华能投资联合发起，电池供应商 ATL 作为锚定投资者参与，包括鹏鼎控股、苏州东山精密、鸿海精密及裕同科技等在内的多家苹果供应链企业已完成投资入股。

Apple's Chief Operating Officer Sabih Khan stated, "Our suppliers in China are making rapid and outstanding progress in transitioning to renewable energy, and we are proud to work alongside them in this important endeavor." 苹果首席运营官 Sabih Khan 表示，「我们在中国的供应商正在向可再生能源转型方面取得快速且卓越的进展，我们很自豪能在这一重要工作中与他们携手同行。」

Recently, Xiaomi launched an independent short-form drama app "Weiguan Short Dramas" on its mobile app store, featuring "ad-free massive short dramas for free viewing," with downloads reaching 20,000. 日前，小米在旗下手机应用商城上线独立短剧 App「围观短剧」，主打「无广告海量短剧免费看」，下载量达 2 万人次。
