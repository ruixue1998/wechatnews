苹果官宣 WWDC26，定档 6 月 9 日

影石创始人回应大疆起诉

According to Jiemian News, DJI Innovations recently officially sued Insta360 in the Shenzhen Intermediate People's Court. The case involves 6 patent ownership disputes, with multiple former DJI core R&D personnel allegedly involved. The patents in question mainly focus on key technical areas such as drone flight control, structural design, and image processing. 据界面新闻报道，大疆创新近日在深圳市中级人民法院正式起诉影石创新，案件涉及 6 项专利权属纠纷，多名前大疆核心研发人员被指参与，涉案专利主要集中在无人机飞行控制、结构设计、影像处理等关键技术领域。

DJI stated in the complaint that the relevant patents were inventions created by former employees within one year of their departure, and are closely related to the work tasks they undertook during their tenure at DJI. According to the relevant provisions of the "Patent Law of the People's Republic of China" regarding service inventions, the right to apply for the patents should belong to DJI. 大疆在诉状中指出，相关专利系前员工离职后一年内作出的发明创造，与其在大疆任职期间承担的工作任务密切相关，依据《中华人民共和国专利法》关于职务发明的相关规定，专利申请权应归大疆所有。

The core point of contention in the case lies in the differing disclosure of inventor identities. 案件的核心争议点，在于发明人身份的披露差异。

According to informed sources, in two patents related to drone flight control and structural design, Insta360 listed one of the inventors as "request not to disclose name" in the Chinese application text. 据知情人士透露，影石在涉及无人机飞行控制和结构设计的两件专利中，于中国申请文本里将其中一位发明人记载为「请求不公布姓名」。

However, in the corresponding international patent applications, the inventor's real name was fully listed. DJI pointed out in the complaint that this person is a former DJI core R&D personnel who deeply participated in the technical development of key DJI drone projects and mastered the core technical system during their employment. 然而在对应的国际专利申请中，该发明人的真实姓名却被完整列明。大疆在诉状中指出，此人正是前大疆核心研发人员，在职期间深度参与了大疆无人机重点项目的技术开发，掌握核心技术体系。

In response, Insta360 founder Liu Jingkang posted a detailed reply on Weibo yesterday. He stated: 对此，影石创始人刘靖康昨日在微博发文逐条回应。他表示：

  • Existing evidence shows that the patents in question are all results of independent innovation by employees after joining Insta360, and did not originate from DJI; 现有证据显示涉案专利均为员工入职影石后自主创新的成果，并非源自大疆；
  • Regarding the matter of hiding inventors in domestic applications, he explained that Insta360's usual practice is to hide inventors in domestic applications and disclose them in PCT applications. The purpose is to delay the exposure of technical personnel lists and avoid being targeted by headhunters, and such operations are not limited to former DJI employees; 关于国内申请隐藏发明人一事，他解释称影石的惯常做法是在国内申请时隐藏发明人、PCT 申请时再予公开，目的是延迟技术人员名单曝光、避免被猎头盯上，且此类操作并不限于大疆前员工；
  • Insta360's statistics found that multiple functions of DJI's panoramic cameras and thumb cameras have fallen within the protection scope of approximately 28 Insta360 patents, but Insta360 chose not to actively sue, prioritizing investment of resources into R&D. 影石统计发现大疆的全景相机和拇指相机多项功能已落入影石约 28 件专利的保护范围，但影石选择不主动起诉，优先将资源投入研发。

He also revealed that Insta360 plans to launch 7 or 8 new products this year, including gimbal cameras, lavalier microphones, and another drone. 他还透露，影石今年计划推出 7、8 款新产品，包括云台相机、领夹麦克风和另一架无人机。

苹果地图或加搜索广告

According to Bloomberg, Apple is planning to introduce search ad features into its native Maps app, with a related announcement possibly coming as early as this month. 据彭博社报道，苹果正计划在自带地图中引入搜索广告功能，相关公告最早或于本月发布。

When users search for relevant content, the highest bidder will appear at the top of the results list. 据知情人士透露，该广告系统的运作方式与 Google 地图的广告模式类似，允许零售商和品牌针对用户的搜索词竞价广告位。当用户搜索相关内容时，出价最高的广告主将出现在结果列表顶部。

According to current plans, in-map advertisements will be launched as early as this summer on devices like iPhones and on the web. Previously, Apple had added multiple ad slots within the App Store and introduced ad monetization through Major League Soccer subscription packages on the Apple TV platform. 按照目前的计划，地图内的广告最早将于今年夏天在 iPhone 等设备以及网页端上线。此前，苹果已在 App Store 内新增了多个广告位，并通过 Apple TV 平台上的美国职业足球大联盟订阅套餐引入广告变现。

OpenClaw 创始人确认 360 独家发现漏洞

According to a report by "Beijing Business Today," the 360 Security Cloud team recently received an official email from Peter Steinberger, founder of OpenClaw. In the email, Steinberger formally confirmed the OpenClaw Gateway WebSocket unauthenticated upgrade vulnerability exclusively discovered by the 360 team. 据《北京商报》报道，360 安全云团队近日收到 OpenClaw 创始人 Peter Steinberger 的官方邮件，Steinberger 在邮件中正式确认了由 360 团队独家发现的 OpenClaw Gateway WebSocket 无认证升级漏洞。

Currently, 360 has simultaneously reported this high-risk vulnerability to the National Vulnerability Database (CNVD), assisting in cutting off the source of risk across the entire network as quickly as possible. 目前，360 已将该高危漏洞同步报送至国家信息安全漏洞共享平台（CNVD），协助在全网范围内第一时间切断风险源头。

It is reported that the confirmed vulnerability is a zero-day (0Day) high-risk vulnerability. Attackers can exploit this vulnerability to silently bypass authentication via WebSocket, gain control of the agent gateway, which could then lead to severe security issues such as resource exhaustion or complete collapse of the target system. 据悉，此次确认的漏洞属于零日（0Day）高危漏洞。攻击者可利用该漏洞通过 WebSocket 静默绕过权限认证，获取智能体网关的控制权，进而可能导致目标系统出现资源耗尽或全面崩溃等严重安全问题。
