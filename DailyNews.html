马斯克诉 OpenAI 案败诉

According to CNBC and The Wall Street Journal, a federal jury in Oakland, California, has ruled in Elon Musk's lawsuit against OpenAI and its CEO Sam Altman, finding that Musk's claims were beyond the statute of limitations and dismissing all of his assertions. 据 CNBC 和《华尔街日报》报道，美国加利福尼亚州奥克兰联邦陪审团已就埃隆 · 马斯克诉 OpenAI 及其 CEO 山姆 · 奥特曼一案作出裁决，认定马斯克的起诉超出诉讼时效，驳回其全部主张。

The case stemmed from a lawsuit filed by Musk in 2024. He accused OpenAI and Altman of violating a promise to operate the company as a non-profit organization, and stated in court that OpenAI "stole from a charity." 此案源于马斯克 2024 年提起的诉讼。他指控 OpenAI 及奥特曼违背了将该公司作为非营利机构运营的承诺，并在庭审中称 OpenAI「窃取了一家慈善机构」。

The jury's ruling did not judge the merits of the "breach of charitable trust" accusation, but rather found that Musk's lawsuit exceeded the three-year statute of limitations. The court also dismissed related allegations against Microsoft. 陪审团的裁决并未就「违反慈善信托」指控的实质内容作出判断，而是认定马斯克的起诉超过了三年诉讼时效。法院同时驳回了针对微软的相关指控。

After the ruling was announced, Musk posted on the X platform, characterizing the outcome as a "technicality on the calendar" and stating that he would appeal to the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals. 裁决宣布后，马斯克在 X 平台发文，将这一结果定性为「日程上的一个技术性问题」，并表示将向第九巡回上诉法院提起上诉。

OpenAI's chief lawyer, William Savitt, stated after the hearing that this "was not a technical ruling, but a substantive one," and claimed Musk was using the lawsuit as a competitive tool. Microsoft also issued a statement welcoming the ruling. OpenAI 首席律师威廉 · 萨维特在庭后表示，这「不是一个技术性裁决，而是一个实质性裁决」，并称马斯克是在将诉讼作为竞争手段使用。微软方面亦发表声明，对裁决表示欢迎。

国家反诈中心 App 上线「AI 内容鉴定」

According to CCTV News, the National Anti-Fraud Center App recently officially launched the "AI Content Identification" feature, which supports AI-generated trace detection for images, videos, audio, and text, primarily used to identify forged content in fraud and e-commerce scenarios. 据央视新闻报道，国家反诈中心 App 近期正式上线「AI 内容鉴定」功能，支持对图像、视频、音频及文本进行 AI 生成痕迹检测，主要用于识别诈骗及电商场景中的伪造内容。

The practice of using AI to tamper with images on e-commerce platforms to defraud "refund only" is becoming increasingly common. CCTV News reporters randomly selected ten suspected AI-generated after-sales images provided by merchants, and after uploading them to the National Anti-Fraud Center App and multiple AI authenticity tools, all were determined to "contain AI-generated traces." 电商平台上利用 AI 篡改图片骗取「仅退款」的行为日趋普遍。央视新闻记者随机选取十张商家提供的疑似 AI 生成售后图，上传至国家反诈中心 App 及多个 AI 鉴真工具后，均被判定为「图像含 AI 生成痕迹」。

According to the introduction, this detection scheme combines small models and large models: the small model learns the generation patterns of AIGC content, while the large model determines whether the content has physical flaws or logical semantic issues, and the two jointly output the final result. 据介绍，该检测方案结合小模型与大模型：小模型学习 AIGC 内容的生成模式，大模型则判断内容是否存在物理瑕疵或逻辑语义问题，二者联合输出最终结果。

Means of production have extremely high quality requirements because they are tools used to make money. Only cars that can bring more benefits to themselves will be welcomed by users and buyers. 很多消费者可能没有想明白一个问题，「网约车是生产资料」。生产资料对品质的要求极高，因为它是用来挣钱的工具。只有能为自己带来更多利益的车，才能受到使用者和购买者的欢迎。

三星良率追赶提速，韩国两大内存厂同步加码 DRAM 产能

According to DIGITIMES, Samsung Electronics recently significantly improved the yield of its latest DRAM used for High Bandwidth Memory (HBM). The yield for its fifth-generation 10nm-class 1b DRAM has reached 92%, and the yield for its sixth-generation 1c DRAM, used for next-generation HBM4 products, has also exceeded 75%. 据 DIGITIMES 报道，三星电子近期大幅提升了用于高带宽内存（HBM）的最新 DRAM 良率。其第五代 10nm 级 1b DRAM 良率已达 92%，用于下一代 HBM4 产品的第六代 1c DRAM 良率也突破 75%。

The improvement in yield is expected to enhance Samsung's cost competitiveness and boost its ability to mass-supply advanced memory products to major AI customers. 良率的提升有望增强三星的成本竞争力，并提升其向主要 AI 客户大规模供应先进存储产品的能力。

In terms of capacity expansion, according to Ddaily, Samsung and Hynix are simultaneously accelerating factory expansion and equipment orders. Samsung is accelerating DRAM investment in its Pyeongtaek P4 factory, with equipment installation expected to continue until the end of this year, and the market estimates further expansion next year. 在产能扩张方面，据 Ddaily 报道，三星和海力士正同步提前推进工厂扩建和设备订单。三星正加速平泽 P4 工厂的 DRAM 投资，设备安装预计持续至今年底，市场估计明年将进一步扩大投入。

Hynix, meanwhile, is advancing expansion at its Cheongju M15X and Yongin Y1 factories, with industry experts expecting its DRAM-related capital expenditure to double next year compared to this year. 海力士则在清州 M15X 和龙仁 Y1 工厂推进扩产，业界预计其明年 DRAM 相关资本支出有望较今年翻倍。

腾讯混元发布业界首个七字体汉字 OCR 评测基准 Chronicles-OCR

Tencent Hunyuan, in collaboration with SSV Digital Culture Lab, Anyang Normal University's Key Laboratory of Oracle Bone Inscription Information Processing, the Institute of Information Engineering of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Nankai University, yesterday released Chronicles-OCR, the industry's first ancient character recognition evaluation benchmark covering the complete evolutionary trajectory of the "Seven Transformations of Chinese Characters." 腾讯混元昨日联合 SSV 数字文化实验室、安阳师范学院甲骨文信息处理重点实验室、中科院信工所、南开大学，发布 Chronicles-OCR，这是业界首个覆盖汉字「七体之变」完整演化轨迹的古文字识别评测基准。

The dataset includes 2,800 expert-annotated images (400 for each font type) and is designed for four core evaluation tasks: 数据集包含 2,800 张专家标注图像（每种字体各 400 张），设计四项核心评测任务：

  • Cross-era character detection: End-to-end localization and interpretation, using H-mean as the metric. 跨时代字符检测：端到端定位与释读，以 H-mean 为指标
  • Fine-grained ancient character recognition: Isolating pure glyph mapping through a visual referring mechanism, using Exact Match as the metric. 细粒度古字识别：通过 visual referring 机制隔离纯字形映射，以 Exact Match 为指标
  • Ancient text transcription: Covering all seven font types, using Normalized Edit Distance (NED) as the metric. 古文转写：覆盖全部七种字体，以归一化编辑距离（NED）为指标
  • Font classification: macro font style discrimination, with accuracy as the metric 字体分类：宏观字体风格判别，以准确率为指标

Evaluation results for 28 mainstream models show that current VLLMs have systemic shortcomings in ancient text recognition: GPT-5, Gemini 2.5 Pro, and other models have an H-mean close to 0 in cross-era character detection tasks, with the strongest model only reaching 16.5; even after character regions are labeled, the highest recognition accuracy is only 27.1%. 对 28 个主流模型的评测结果显示，当前 VLLM 在古文字识别上存在系统性短板：GPT-5、Gemini 2.5 Pro 等模型在跨时代字符检测任务上 H-mean 接近 0，最强模型仅 16.5；即便标注字符区域后，识别准确率最高也只有 27.1%。
