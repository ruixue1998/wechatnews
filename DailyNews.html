小米 MiMo-V2.5 系列 API 永久降价，最高降幅 99%

Early this morning, Xiaomi Technology announced a permanent price reduction for its MiMo-V2.5 series APIs, with the highest reduction reaching 99% compared to the original pricing, and no longer distinguishing between context window lengths. 今天凌晨，小米技术宣布，对旗下 MiMo-V2.5 系列 API 进行永久降价，相比原始定价最高降幅可达 99%，且不再区分上下文窗口长度。

The MiMo-V2.5-Pro input cache hit price has been reduced from previously ≤256k window's ¥2.80 / million tokens to ¥0.025, a reduction of approximately 99%; the input cache miss price has been reduced from ¥7.00 to ¥3.00, a reduction of approximately 57%; and the output price has been reduced from ¥21.00 to ¥6.00, a reduction of approximately 71%. MiMo-V2.5-Pro 输入缓存命中价格从此前 ≤256k 窗口下的 ¥2.80 / 百万 tokens 降至 ¥0.025，降幅约 99%；输入未命中缓存价格从 ¥7.00 降至 ¥3.00，降幅约 57%；输出价格从 ¥21.00 降至 ¥6.00，降幅约 71%。

At the same time, the Token Plan billing system has been optimized, with prices remaining unchanged, but available quantities increasing to 5 to 8 times their original amounts. The MiMo-V2.5 Standard Edition (¥99) package's available token quantity has been upgraded from 200M to 1300M, and the Pro package (¥329) from 700M to 4700M. 与此同时，Token Plan 计费体系同步优化，定价不变，可用量提升至原来的 5 至 8 倍。MiMo-V2.5 标准版（¥99）套餐可用 Token 数量从原来的 200M 升级至 1300M，Pro 套餐（¥329）从 700M 升至 4700M。

In addition to the price reduction, Xiaomi also announced the early conclusion of its "Hundred Trillion Token Creator Incentive Program". The program, launched on April 28, has distributed all 100 trillion tokens as of 16:08 Beijing time on May 26. 在降价之外，小米还宣布「百万亿 Token 创造者激励计划」提前收官。该计划自 4 月 28 日上线，截至今日北京时间 5 月 26 日 16:08，100 万亿 Tokens 已全部发放完毕。

Google CEO 解释 AI 编程落后原因：缺少 Cursor 这样的用户入口

Recently, Google CEO Sundar Pichai, during an exclusive interview on The New York Times' podcast "Hard Fork," publicly admitted for the first time that Google is currently lagging behind the industry forefront in the field of AI-assisted programming. 日前，Google CEO 桑达尔·皮查伊（Sundar Pichai）在《纽约时报》旗下播客节目《Hard Fork》接受专访时，首次公开承认 Google 在 AI 辅助编程领域目前落后于行业前沿。

He attributed the reason to the lack of editor entry points like Cursor or Claude Code that can be directly embedded into developers' daily workflows—this absence led to Google losing the most critical dynamic workflow data, thereby slowing down the model's iteration speed in agent programming scenarios. 他将原因归结为缺少像 Cursor 或 Claude Code 这样能直接嵌入开发者日常工作流的编辑器入口——这一缺失导致 Google 流失了最关键的动态工作流数据，进而拖慢了模型在智能体编程场景下的迭代速度。

In the interview, Pichai focused on showcasing the closed-loop test data of Google's internal AI programming tool, Antigravity, including a weekly doubling of internal token consumption and the ability to build an operating system from scratch in 12 hours during extreme performance tests. 皮查伊在专访中侧重展示了 Google 内部 AI 编程工具 Antigravity 的闭环测试数据，包括内部 Token 消耗量每周翻倍，以及在极端性能测试中用 12 小时从零构建出操作系统。

He stated that Google's underlying technology foundation remains complete, and its current passive position in the open market primarily stems from the limited breadth of application scenarios and user feedback loops, rather than a fundamental lack of underlying capabilities. 他表示，Google 底层技术底座依然完备，当前在公开市场的被动，主要源于应用场景与用户反馈闭环的广度限制，而非底层能力的根本性缺失。

特斯拉高管：正研究三电机版 Model 3 的可行性

Recently, Lars Moravy, Tesla's VP of Engineering, revealed in a special episode of the "Ride the Lightning" podcast that he is researching the feasibility of equipping the Model 3 with a triple-motor system. 近日，特斯拉工程副总裁拉尔斯·莫拉维在《Ride the Lightning》播客特别节目中透露，他正在研究为 Model 3 搭载三电机系统的可行性。

When directly asked by host Ryan McCaffrey on the show whether the Model 3 would be equipped with three motors, Moravy did not deny this direction, stating that he has "been seriously thinking about it." 莫拉维在节目中被主持人瑞安·麦卡弗里直接追问 Model 3 是否会配备三电机时，并未否认这一方向，表示「一直在认真思考这件事」。

He specifically mentioned his hope to introduce carbon-sleeved motors to the Model 3 platform—a technology that is the core hardware enabling the Model S Plaid's sustained powerful acceleration capabilities. 他特别提到希望将碳纤维套筒电机引入 Model 3 平台——这一技术正是 Model S Plaid 得以实现持续强劲加速能力的核心硬件。

Currently, the Model 3 Performance uses a front and rear dual-motor layout. If a third motor were to be added within the rear subframe, engineering space would be extremely limited. Moravy admitted that this is a "high-investment, return-needs-to-be-weighed" plan that does not align with Tesla's overall development goals at this stage. 目前，Model 3 Performance 采用前后双电机布局。若要在后副车架内加装第三台电机，工程空间将极为有限。莫拉维坦承，这是一项「高投入、需权衡回报」的规划，在现阶段与特斯拉整体发展目标并不契合。

He further explained that all of the company's current high-performance R&D resources have been concentrated on the new generation Roadster. This flagship sports car will feature Tesla's latest generation motor technology and is currently the engineering team's top priority. Moravy stated that once the Roadster officially enters production, related high-performance motor technology is expected to extend to other models in the future. 他进一步解释，公司当前全部高性能研发资源已集中投入新一代 Roadster。这款旗舰跑车将搭载特斯拉最新一代电机技术，是目前工程团队的优先级所在。莫拉维表示，一旦 Roadster 正式投产，相关高性能电机技术未来有望向其他车型延伸。

东风牵头起草乘用车底盘强制国标过审

Dongfeng Motor recently disclosed that the first national mandatory standard it led the drafting of in the passenger car chassis field has been unanimously approved. This standard focuses on brake assist systems and has been under preliminary research for over three years. 东风汽车近日披露，其在乘用车底盘领域牵头起草的首个国家强制标准已全票通过审查。该标准聚焦制动辅助系统，预研至今历时三年多。

The brake assist system identifies the driver's intention during emergency braking when pedal force is insufficient, and instantly maximizes braking force to shorten the braking distance. 制动辅助系统会在驾驶者紧急制动但踩踏力不足时识别驾驶意图，并瞬间把制动力提升至最大，以缩短制动距离。

Dongfeng stated that the installation rate of BAS in newly launched M1 and N1 category vehicles in China is increasing year by year, with over 90% of new passenger car models now equipped with BAS as standard; export models are 100% mandatorily equipped with BAS according to UN Regulation UN R139. 东风称，我国新上市 M1 和 N1 类车型中，BAS 装备率逐年上升，乘用车新车中已有 90% 以上车型标配 BAS；出口车型按联合国法规 UN R139 已 100% 强制装备 BAS。

💡 黄仁勋：AI 时代学什么不重要，讲故事、创造力才重要

According to Business Insider, Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang recently discussed educational choices in the AI era during an interview on Channel 4 in the UK. He believes that what is most worth cultivating today is storytelling, creativity, and judgment, rather than betting on a specific discipline in advance. 据《商业内幕》报道，英伟达 CEO 黄仁勋近日在英国 Channel 4 播出的访谈中谈到 AI 时代的教育选择。他认为，今天最值得培养的是讲故事、创造力和判断力，而不是提前押注某一个具体学科。

No matter what you choose as your passion, the only thing you need to do is ask yourself: How can artificial intelligence enhance my learning, my skills, my mission? 无论你选择什么作为自己的热情所在，你唯一需要做的，就是问自己：人工智能如何能够提升我的学习、我的技艺、我的使命？

Jensen Huang stated that in the past, it was easy to advise children to study physics, engineering, or computer science; but after AI can quickly supplement tool capabilities, a single professional choice is no longer the only answer. 黄仁勋表示，过去人们很容易建议孩子学习物理、工程或计算机科学；但在 AI 可快速补足工具能力后，单一专业选择不再是唯一答案。

His core judgment is that storytelling, creativity, and judgment will continue to hold value. AI can help students enter different fields like a mentor and coach, and learners still need to ask good questions, organize information, evaluate results, and clearly explain complex content. 他的核心判断是，讲故事、创造力和判断力会继续保有价值。AI 可以像导师和教练一样帮助学生进入不同领域，学习者仍要提出好问题、组织信息、评估结果，并把复杂内容讲清楚。

Jensen Huang views AI tools as a way to expand educational feedback: students, whether studying art, science, or engineering, can use AI to get faster practice and explanations. He did not specify majors for students but rather framed "how to use AI to amplify one's judgment" as a new learning framework. 黄仁勋把 AI 工具视为扩大教育反馈的方式：学生无论学习艺术、科学还是工程，都可以借助 AI 获得更快练习和解释。他没有替学生指定专业，而是把「如何用 AI 放大自己的判断」作为新的学习框架。
