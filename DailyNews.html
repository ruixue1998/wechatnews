我国拟修订国家通用语言文字法，规范网络空间用语用字

According to Xinhua News Agency, the revised draft of the National Common Language and Characters Law was submitted for its first review to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on September 8. 据新华网报道，国家通用语言文字法修订草案 9 月 8 日首次提请全国人大常委会会议审议。

This revised draft, consisting of five chapters and 32 articles, adds regulatory requirements for language use in cyberspace, explicitly stating that the national common language and characters used on internet information platforms hosted by the government or providing public services must comply with national norms and standards. 这个五章 32 条的修订草案，增加了对网络空间用语用字的规范要求，明确政府主办的或者提供社会公共服务的互联网信息平台中使用的国家通用语言文字应当符合国家的规范和标准。

The report points out that the national common language and characters are formed on the basis of interaction, exchange, and integration among various ethnic groups. They are a fundamental element of Chinese culture, a distinct symbol of the Chinese nation, and, like the national flag, national anthem, and national emblem, an important symbol of the state. The revised draft clarifies: 报道指出，国家通用语言文字是在各民族交往交流交融的基础上形成的，是中华文化的基础要素、中华民族的鲜明标识，和国旗、国歌、国徽一样，是国家的重要象征。修订草案明确：

  • Network literary and artistic programs, web dramas, web movies, and other online audio-visual programs, as well as the names, packaging, and descriptions of goods sold domestically, should use the national common language and characters as the basic language; 网络文艺节目、网络剧、网络电影等网络视听节目，在境内销售的商品的名称、包装、说明等，应当以国家通用语言文字为基本的用语用字；
  • International exhibitions, international conferences, and other events held domestically that genuinely require the use of foreign languages and characters should simultaneously use the national common language and characters; 在境内举办的国际展览、国际会议等确需使用外国语言文字的，应当同时使用国家通用语言文字；
  • The third week of September each year is designated as the National Common Language and Characters Promotion and Popularization Week; international Chinese education should teach the national common language and characters. 每年 9 月的第三周为国家通用语言文字推广普及宣传周；国际中文教育应当教授国家通用语言文字。

It is reported that since the implementation of the current National Common Language and Characters Law in 2001, the national Mandarin popularization rate has exceeded 80%, the proportion of literate people using standardized Chinese characters has exceeded 95%, and the illiteracy rate has dropped to 2.67%. 据悉，现行国家通用语言文字法 2001 年实施以来，全国普通话普及率已超过 80%，识字人口使用规范汉字的比例超过 95%，文盲率下降至 2.67%。
