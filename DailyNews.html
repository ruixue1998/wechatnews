OpenAI 发布 AGI 研发五大原则

On April 26, local time, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman issued an official statement, formally establishing five core principles guiding the company's research, development, and deployment of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). 当地时间 4 月 26 日，OpenAI CEO Sam Altman 发表官方声明，正式确立了指导公司通用人工智能（AGI）研发与部署的五大核心原则。

According to the statement, these five core principles are Democratization, Empowerment, Universal Prosperity, Resilience, and Adaptability. 据声明显示，这五大核心原则分别为民主化（Democratization）、赋能（Empowerment）、普遍繁荣（Universal Prosperity）、韧性（Resilience）与适应性（Adaptability）。

Altman pointed out that OpenAI's goal is to decentralize control of AGI to the public, preventing technological power from concentrating in a few companies. 奥特曼指出，OpenAI 的目标是将 AGI 的控制权以去中心化形式交予公众，避免技术权力向少数企业集中。

At the operational level, the company will significantly reduce AI infrastructure costs through vertical integration and by building data centers globally; in product deployment, it will continue to adopt an "iterative deployment" strategy, emphasizing the necessity of building a society-wide defense system when facing extreme risks such as pathogen generation and cybersecurity vulnerabilities. 在具体执行层面，公司将通过垂直整合与在全球范围内建设数据中心来大幅降低 AI 基础设施成本；在产品部署上继续采用「迭代部署」策略，强调在面对病原体生成、网络安全漏洞等极端风险时，必须构建全社会的防御体系。

Furthermore, Altman revealed that OpenAI will leverage its foundation's resources to collaborate with governments, international organizations, and other AGI research entities to address the issue of technical alignment. 此外，奥特曼透露，OpenAI 将动用其基金会资源联合政府、国际机构及其他 AGI 研发实体共同解决技术对齐问题。

He explicitly stated that before major social and safety issues are fully resolved, the company is willing to pause some research and development processes when necessary, and committed to dynamically adjusting its operational guidelines and risk-benefit ratios based on evolving circumstances. 他明确表态，在未充分解决重大社会性及安全问题前，公司愿在必要时期暂停部分研发进程，并承诺根据实际情况的演变动态调整其运营准则及风险权衡比。

OpenAI「解绑」微软：修订协议，产品开放全平台部署

OpenAI and Microsoft announced yesterday the revision of their cooperation agreement, with the core change being that Microsoft's license to OpenAI's intellectual property has shifted from exclusive to non-exclusive, allowing OpenAI to now offer all its products to customers via any cloud platform. OpenAI 与微软于昨日宣布修订双方合作协议，核心变化是微软对 OpenAI 知识产权的许可由独占转为非独占，OpenAI 现可通过任何云平台向客户提供全部产品。

Microsoft remains OpenAI's "primary cloud partner," and OpenAI products will prioritize launching on Azure, but Microsoft no longer holds exclusive rights. Specific terms: 微软仍是 OpenAI 的「主要云合作伙伴」，OpenAI 产品将优先上线 Azure，但微软不再享有排他权。具体条款：

  • Microsoft's license for OpenAI models and products will continue until 2032; 微软对 OpenAI 模型及产品的许可将持续至 2032 年；
  • OpenAI's revenue share with Microsoft will continue until 2030, with the percentage remaining unchanged but subject to a total cap, and no longer tied to AGI technological progress; OpenAI 向微软的收入分成持续至 2030 年，比例维持不变，但设有总额上限，且不再与 AGI 技术进展挂钩；
  • Microsoft will no longer pay a share to OpenAI; 微软则不再向 OpenAI 支付分成；
  • Microsoft, as a significant shareholder, will continue to participate in OpenAI's growth. 微软作为重要股东，将继续参与 OpenAI 的增长。

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman confirmed these changes on X. Following the announcement, Microsoft's stock price briefly fell by about 2%, while Amazon's stock price rose by about 1%. OpenAI CEO Sam Altman 在 X 上确认了上述变化。消息公布后，微软股价一度下跌约 2%，亚马逊股价则上涨约 1%。
