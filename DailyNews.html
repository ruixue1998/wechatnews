借助 AI 气象大模型发台风预测，网络博主涉嫌违反《气象法》

After Typhoon "Bavi," the 9th typhoon of the year, entered China's 48-hour alert line on July 8, several online bloggers began to use open-source AI meteorological large models to self-publish typhoon path predictions and rainfall forecasts on social platforms. Some bloggers, under the name "weather enthusiasts," also offered paid prediction services for specific locations, charging about 10 yuan per service. 今年第 9 号台风「巴威」于 7 月 8 日进入我国 48 小时警戒线后，多名网络博主开始借助开源 AI 气象大模型，在社交平台自行发布台风路径预测和降雨预测，部分博主还以「天气爱好者」为名，针对特定地点提供付费预测服务，每次收费约 10 元。

In response, legal experts explicitly stated that such behavior is suspected of being illegal. The "Meteorological Law of the People's Republic of China" stipulates that public weather forecasts and disaster weather warnings implement a unified release system, and the releasing entities can only be meteorological stations affiliated with meteorological authorities. 对此，法律专家明确表示，相关行为已涉嫌违法。《中华人民共和国气象法》规定，公众气象预报和灾害性天气警报实行统一发布制度，发布主体只能是气象主管机构所属的气象台站。

Li Lei, an associate professor at Zhongnan University of Economics and Law, pointed out that if bloggers only analyze academic materials and meteorological data without making forecasts, it does not constitute a violation; however, if individuals directly publish weather forecasts, it is suspected of violating the law. 中南财经政法大学副教授李雷指出，若博主仅分析学术资料和气象数据、未作出预报，则不涉嫌违规；但若个人直接发布气象预报，则涉嫌违反该法。

OpenClaw announced yesterday the establishment of the OpenClaw Foundation, operating independently as a U.S. 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. The project continues to maintain its MIT license, remaining open and not controlled by a single commercial entity. OpenClaw 昨日宣布成立 OpenClaw 基金会，以美国 501(c)(3) 非营利组织身份独立运营，项目继续保持 MIT 授权、开放且不受单一商业主体控制。

Project founder Peter Steinberger has joined OpenAI, but he clarified that "OpenAI hired me, not OpenClaw," and the foundation "has no owners, only sponsors." 项目创始人 Peter Steinberger 已加入 OpenAI，但他澄清「OpenAI 雇用的是我，而非 OpenClaw」，基金会「没有所有者，只有赞助商」。

This is OpenClaw's first time forming a full-time team, led by Chief Architect Vincent Koc. In terms of collaborations, NVIDIA launched NemoClaw to support local private deployment, Microsoft released its enterprise-grade autonomous agent product Microsoft Scout based on OpenClaw technology, and the University of Michigan became the largest donor and established the Institute for Agent Computing. OpenAI also pledged to support the foundation's independent hosting status. 这是 OpenClaw 首次组建全职团队，由首席架构师 Vincent Koc 领衔。合作方面，英伟达推出 NemoClaw 支持本地私有部署，微软基于 OpenClaw 技术发布企业级自主智能体产品 Microsoft Scout，密歇根大学成为最大捐款方并成立智能体计算研究所。OpenAI 也承诺支持基金会的独立托管地位。

According to the official announcement, OpenClaw currently adds 4.5 million active instances weekly. The foundation's goal is to become "the Switzerland of AI" – a neutral platform where major models and laboratories can jointly connect. 据官方公告，OpenClaw 目前每周新增 450 万活跃实例，基金会目标是成为「AI 领域的瑞士」——各大模型与实验室共同接入的中立平台。

💡 黄仁勋：英伟达工程师现在更愿意构建 AI 智能体，而不是写代码

According to Business Insider, Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang recently stated in an interview video released by the company that the company's software engineers are increasingly inclined to build AI agents rather than write code. 据《商业内幕》报道，英伟达 CEO 黄仁勋近日在一段由公司发布的采访视频中表示，公司软件工程师越来越倾向于构建 AI 智能体，而非编写代码。

Huang pointed out that the introduction of AI is reducing engineers' daily coding work, replaced by building AI agents, setting benchmarks, and establishing protective mechanisms. 黄仁勋指出，AI 的引入正在减少工程师的日常编码工作，取而代之的是构建 AI 智能体、制定基准测试与设置防护机制。

He stated that this type of work 'requires imagination, creativity, and extensive technical accumulation,' which is fundamentally different from the previous code-input-centric work model. 他表示，这类工作「需要想象力、需要创造力，以及大量的技术积累」，与此前以代码输入为主的工作模式存在本质差异。

On the issue of employment impact, Huang once again refuted the view that AI will largely replace white-collar jobs. He believes that AI is creating a large number of new positions rather than eliminating existing ones. 在就业影响问题上，黄仁勋再次驳斥了 AI 将大规模替代白领岗位的观点。他认为，AI 正在创造大量新职位，而非消除现有岗位。

任天堂 Switch 2 欧洲新版今秋上市，电池可更换、机身重 548 克

According to IGN, Nintendo will launch a new version of Switch 2 in the European market starting this autumn, with the core change being a built-in replaceable battery to meet the EU's upcoming battery regulations. 据 IGN 报道，任天堂将于今秋起在欧洲市场推出新版 Switch 2，核心变化在于内置可更换电池，以满足欧盟即将生效的电池法规要求。

The battery capacity of the new console is reduced by about 1% compared to the current version, resulting in a slight increase in body weight—including Joy-Con 2 controllers, it increases from 534 grams to 548 grams; without controllers, it increases from 401 grams to 411 grams. In terms of functionality, Nintendo states that the revised version is no different from the current version. 新版主机的电池容量较现行版本缩减约 1%，机身重量因此小幅增加——包含 Joy-Con 2 控制器从 534 克升至 548 克，不带控制器时从 401 克升至 411 克。功能方面，任天堂表示修订版与现行版本无差异。

According to the plan, product updates will be rolled out in batches: 按计划，产品更新将分批推进：

  • The first to be launched this summer is a new version of the Switch 1 Joy-Con, with unchanged battery capacity and weight; 今夏率先上市的是 Switch 1 Joy-Con 的新版本，电池容量和重量均不变；
  • Starting this autumn, the entire Switch 2 console and its accompanying Joy-Con 2 will switch to the replaceable battery version; 今秋起 Switch 2 整机及随附的 Joy-Con 2 将切换至可更换电池版本；
  • Starting this winter, separately sold Switch 2 Joy-Con and Pro controllers will also follow suit, with the Pro controller's battery capacity reduced by 16%, while its overall weight is reduced by 7 grams; 今冬起单独发售的 Switch 2 Joy-Con 及 Pro 手柄也将跟进，其中 Pro 手柄电池容量缩减 16%，整体重量反而减轻 7 克；
  • Regarding retro controllers, the N64 controller released from early next year will weigh 1 gram, and the GameCube controller will weigh 5 grams. Corresponding battery replacement kits will be sold separately in the European Nintendo Store, with specific release dates yet to be announced. 复古手柄方面，明年初起发售的 N64 手柄将重 1 克，GameCube 手柄则重 5 克。配套的电池更换套件将在欧洲 Nintendo Store 单独销售，具体开售时间尚未公布。

It is worth noting that Nintendo chose not to release a new European version for Switch 1, but instead directly withdrew the console from the European market. 值得注意的是，任天堂选择不为 Switch 1 推出欧版新版本，而是直接将该主机退出欧洲市场。

Compared to the first generation open-sourced in January this year (with a maximum stable generation of about 10 minutes), the 2.0 version mainly features the following upgrades: 相比今年 1 月开源的第一代（最长稳定生成约 10 分钟），2.0 版本主要升级如下：

  • Supports hour-long real-time continuous generation, 720p/60fps HD output, with virtually no delay in keyboard control 支持小时级实时连续生成，720p/60fps 高清输出，键盘操控几乎无延迟

  • Supports multiple users simultaneously entering and interacting in the same world. 支持多用户同时进入同一世界交互

雀巢将在泰国投资 6.88 亿美元建设智能工厂和配送中心

According to Sina Finance, Thailand has approved a 23 billion Thai Baht investment project by Nestlé (Thai) Ltd., equivalent to approximately 688 million US dollars, for the construction of smart factories and distribution centers. 据新浪财经报道，泰国已批准雀巢泰国有限公司一笔 230 亿泰铢投资项目，约合 6.88 亿美元，用于建设智能工厂与配送中心。

The project is located in Samut Prakan Province in central Thailand, with a planned start of production in the fourth quarter of 2028. It will have an annual output of 170,000 tons of instant Nescafé, blended coffee beverages, and ready-to-drink beverages, and will create 520 new jobs. The Thai Board of Investment stated that the project serves Thailand's goal of becoming a regional food and beverage manufacturing hub. 项目选址在泰国中部沙没巴干府，计划 2028 年第四季度投产，年产速溶雀巢咖啡、复合咖啡饮品和即饮饮料 17 万吨，并新增 520 个就业岗位。泰国投资委员会称，该项目服务于泰国打造区域食品饮料制造中心。
