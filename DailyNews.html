eSIM 手机加速落地：移动、联通、电信公布办理细则

Recently, China Mobile, China Unicom, and China Telecom, the three major operators, have successively announced the launch of eSIM-related business processing. 近日，中国移动、中国联通和中国电信三大运营商先后官宣启动 eSIM 相关业务的办理。

Currently, all three major operators require the user to visit a business hall in person to apply for an eSIM. Only users aged 18 and above are eligible, and each device can activate a maximum of 2 numbers per month, with restrictions on the number of card replacements. 目前， 三大运营商办理 eSIM 均需要本人前往营业厅办理，仅 18 周岁以上用户可办，且每台设备每月最多开通 2 个号码，补换卡次数亦存在限制。

Regarding document support, China Unicom offers the widest range. For mainland residents, it supports ID cards, household registration books, and passports. For Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan compatriots, as well as foreigners in China, it supports Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan travel permits, residence permits, and permanent residence ID cards for foreigners. 在证件支持方面，中国联通支持的范围最广泛。对于大陆居民支持身份证、户口簿和护照，对于港澳台同胞以及在华外国人，则支持港澳台通行证、居住证，以及外国人永久居留身份证。

However, China Mobile and China Telecom support fewer types of documents, only accepting ID cards (for mainland residents) or permanent residence ID cards for foreigners. Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan compatriots can only use residence permits to apply for cards and travel permits are not supported. 而移动和电信支持的证件类型就相对少一些，仅支持使用身份证（大陆居民）或外国人永久居留身份证，港澳台同胞则只能使用居住证办卡，不支持通行证。

As the first model to support eSIM technology in the domestic market, Apple iPhone Air will begin pre-sales at 9 AM this Friday and officially go on sale next Wednesday. The support for domestic and international eSIMs on the iPhone Air Chinese mainland version, Hong Kong/Macau version, and overseas versions is also a concern for many friends: 作为首款在国内市场支持 eSIM 技术的机型，苹果 iPhone Air 将于本周五 9 点开启预售，下周三正式发售，而 iPhone Air 国行、港澳版和海外版对于境内外 eSIM 的支持情况，也是很多朋友关心的问题：

  • The iPhone Air itself supports up to 8 eSIM numbers, but only a maximum of 2 numbers can be activated in mainland China; iPhone Air 本身支持最多 8 个 eSIM 号码，但在中国大陆最多只能开通 2 个号码；
  • Overseas version Air: Can roam domestically, but cannot activate domestic numbers; 国外版 Air：在国内可漫游，但不能开通国内号码；
  • Chinese mainland version Air: Can roam abroad, and can activate overseas numbers after going abroad. 国行版 Air：在国外可漫游，出国后可以开通国外号码。

Specifically: 具体来说：

  • Chinese mainland version + domestic card: ✅ 国行 + 国卡：✅
  • Chinese mainland version + domestic card abroad: ✅ (depending on the operator, no difference from the physical card era) 国行 + 国卡出境：✅（依据运营商，和实体卡时代无区别）
  • Chinese mainland version + foreign card abroad: ✅ (requires physical location abroad before adding) 国行 + 外卡在国外：✅（需物理定位在国外后才能添加）
  • Chinese version + foreign card in China: ❌ 国行 + 外卡在国内：❌
  • Hong Kong/Macau version + Hong Kong/Macau card: ✅ (Normal roaming in mainland China) 港澳版 + 港澳卡：✅（在大陆为正常漫游）

eSIM technology integrates traditional physical SIM card functions into the phone's chip, allowing users to complete network access, card changes, card replacement, suspension/resumption, cancellation, and number portability services without inserting a card. Compared to physical cards, eSIM offers advantages such as space-saving, waterproof and lightweight design, and remote download activation. eSIM 技术将传统实体 SIM 卡功能集成至手机芯片，用户无需插卡即可完成入网、换卡、补卡、停机/复机、销号及携号转网等业务。相比实体卡，eSIM 具备节省空间、防水轻薄、远程下载激活等优势。

星舰 V2 谢幕：8 颗星链模拟器顺利部署，SpaceX 迈向 V3 时代

SpaceX Starship V2 completed its final test flight, successfully deploying 8 Starlink simulators, laying the foundation for the launch of the next-generation Starlink V3 satellites. SpaceX 星舰 V2 完成最后一次试飞，携带 8 颗星链模拟器顺利部署，为下一代星链 V3 卫星发射奠定基础。

This mission was carried out by Super Heavy Booster 15 and Starship 38. During its return, the booster tested a new 5-engine landing ignition configuration, enhancing future redundancy and safety. 本次任务由超重型助推器 15 号与星舰飞船 38 号执行，助推器在返回过程中测试了全新的 5 台发动机着陆点火配置，提升了未来冗余与安全性。

Meanwhile, the spacecraft released 8 Starlink simulators weighing approximately 2000 kg each in orbit, with a total payload of about 16000 kg, and the deployment process was smooth and unobstructed. 与此同时，飞船在轨释放了 8 颗重量约 2000 公斤的星链模拟器，总载荷约 16000 公斤，部署过程流畅无阻。

Compared to the previous Falcon 9, the future Starship will have higher launch efficiency, adding approximately 60 Tbps of capacity to the Starlink network per launch, which is 20 times the capacity of a single existing launch. 与此前的猎鹰 9 号相比，未来星舰将具备更高的运载效率，每次发射可为星链网络增加约 60 Tbps 容量，相当于现有单次发射能力的 20 倍。

SpaceX stated that Starship will gradually replace Falcon 9 as the primary launch vehicle for Starlink V3 satellites. SpaceX 表示，星舰将逐步接替猎鹰 9 号，成为星链 V3 卫星的主力运载工具。

This mission also verified the ability to re-ignite Raptor engines in a space environment and conducted extreme tests on the heat shield tiles. 此次任务还验证了在太空环境下重新点燃猛禽发动机的能力，并对隔热瓦进行了极限测试。

Musk emphasized that Starship's iterative goal is to achieve "full, rapid reusability" to support future large-scale Starlink deployment and Mars exploration. 马斯克强调，星舰的迭代目标是实现「完全、快速可重复使用」，以支撑未来大规模星链部署和火星探索。

苹果公布全新语言模型：长文本生成速度提升最高 128 倍

According to 9to5Mac, Apple and a research team from Ohio State University have jointly released a new study proposing a language model named 'FS-DFM (Few-Step Discrete Flow-Matching).' 据 9to5Mac 报道，苹果与俄亥俄州立大学研究团队联合发布了一项最新研究，提出名为「FS-DFM（Few-Step Discrete Flow-Matching）」的语言模型。

According to the introduction, the model shows significant advantages in long text generation, capable of producing high-quality output in just 8 iterations, with speeds up to 128 times faster than traditional methods. 据介绍， 该模型在长文本生成方面展现出显著优势，能够在仅 8 轮迭代中完成高质量输出，速度最高可比传统方法快 128 倍。

The study points out that existing mainstream large language models mostly use an autoregressive method to generate word by word, while diffusion models generate multiple tokens in parallel and gradually refine them. FS-DFM further optimizes this by leveraging 'teacher model' guidance and iterative mechanisms to achieve faster and more stable convergence. 研究指出，现有主流大语言模型多采用自回归方式逐字生成，而扩散模型则通过并行生成多个词元并逐步修正。FS-DFM 在此基础上进一步优化，借助「教师模型」指导和迭代机制改进，实现更快、更稳的收敛效果。

In comparative tests, FS-DFM's parameter scale is only 0.17 to 1.7 billion, yet it outperforms the larger Dream and LLaDA diffusion models in both Perplexity and Entropy metrics, demonstrating the potential for high efficiency in smaller models. 在对比测试中，FS-DFM 的参数规模仅为 0.17 至 1.7 亿，但在困惑度（Perplexity）和熵（Entropy）指标上均优于参数量更大的 Dream 与 LLaDA 扩散模型，展现出小模型高效能的潜力。

The research team stated that they will open-source the code and model checkpoints in the future to promote reproduction and research in related fields. 研究团队表示，未来将开放代码与模型检查点，以推动相关领域的复现与研究。

Google 紧急修复中：Android 存在可窃取聊天记录与验证码的新型漏洞

According to tech media Ars Technica, an academic research team recently disclosed a new attack method called 'Pixnapping,' capable of stealing two-factor authentication (2FA) codes, chat logs, and other sensitive information from Android devices in less than 30 seconds. 据科技媒体 Ars Technica 报道，学术研究团队近日披露了一种名为「Pixnapping」的新型攻击方式，能够在不到 30 秒的时间内窃取 Android 设备上的双重验证（2FA）验证码、聊天记录和其他敏感信息。

Researchers pointed out that a Pixnapping attack requires the user to install a malicious app beforehand, but this app does not need any system permissions. It can induce the target app to display sensitive content on the screen by calling Android interfaces. 研究人员指出，Pixnapping 攻击需要用户事先安装一款恶意应用，但该应用无需任何系统权限，即可通过调用 Android 接口诱导目标应用在屏幕上显示敏感内容。

Subsequently, the malicious app exploits the time difference in GPU rendering to reconstruct screen information pixel by pixel, thereby obtaining verification codes or message content. 随后， 恶意应用会利用 GPU 渲染的时间差来逐像素重建屏幕信息，从而获取验证码或消息内容。

Researchers emphasized that Pixnapping reveals the limitations of Android's inter-app isolation mechanisms, reminding users to exercise caution when installing applications. 研究人员强调，Pixnapping 揭示了 Android 应用间隔离机制的局限性，提醒用户在安装应用时保持谨慎。
