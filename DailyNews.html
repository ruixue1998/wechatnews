小米将召回 11 万辆 SU7 标准版

The website of the State Administration for Market Regulation shows that Xiaomi Auto Technology Co., Ltd. recently filed a recall plan with the State Administration for Market Regulation, deciding to recall some SU7 Standard Edition electric vehicles starting immediately. This recall involves vehicles produced from February 6, 2024, to August 30, 2025, totaling 116,887 units. 国家市场监督管理总局网站显示，小米汽车科技有限公司近日向国家市场监督管理总局备案了召回计划，决定自即日起召回部分 SU7 标准版电动汽车，涉及生产日期为 2024 年 2 月 6 日至 2025 年 8 月 30 日的车辆，共计 116,887 辆。

It is reported that this recall covers two types of vehicles: 据悉，本次召回涵盖两类车型：

  • Recall number S2025M0149I: Involves XMA7000MBEVR2 and XMA7000MBEVR5, totaling 98,462 vehicles; 召回编号 S2025M0149I：涉及 XMA7000MBEVR2 和 XMA7000MBEVR5，共计 98,462 辆；
  • Recall number S2025M0150I: Involves BJ7000MBEVR2, totaling 18,425 vehicles. 召回编号 S2025M0150I：涉及 BJ7000MBEVR2，共计 18,425 辆。

The official statement indicates that some vehicles within the recall scope, when the L2 high-speed navigation assisted driving function is enabled, may have insufficient recognition, warning, or handling capabilities in extreme special scenarios. If the driver fails to intervene in time, there is a safety hazard of increased collision risk. 官方表示，召回范围内部分车辆在启用 L2 高速领航辅助驾驶功能时，可能在极端特殊场景下识别、预警或处置能力不足，若驾驶员未能及时干预，存在增加碰撞风险的安全隐患。

Xiaomi Auto will provide free software upgrades for affected vehicles via OTA technology to eliminate safety hazards. Owners will receive notifications via SMS, mobile app, etc., and can also call the customer service hotline "400-182-6888" or visit the Market Supervision Administration Recall Center website " www.samrdprc.org.cn" for details. 小米汽车将通过 OTA 技术为相关车辆免费升级软件，以消除安全隐患。车主将通过短信、手机 APP 等方式接收通知，亦可拨打客户服务热线「400-182-6888」或访问市场监管总局召回中心网站「 www.samrdprc.org.cn」了解详情。

iPhone Air 磁吸电池包与手机内置电池一致

Renowned teardown firm iFixit today released its teardown report of Apple's iPhone Air MagSafe Battery Pack, revealing a controversial design choice: the battery used in the accessory is almost identical to the battery in the iPhone Air itself. 知名拆解机构 iFixit 今日发布了对苹果 iPhone Air MagSafe 电池包的拆解报告，揭示了一个颇具争议的设计选择：该配件所使用的电池与 iPhone Air 本体电池几乎完全一致。

iFixit measurements show that the battery pack contains a 12.26Wh battery with a thickness of only 2.72mm, which highly matches the battery specifications used in the iPhone Air. Considering the iPhone Air's body thickness is only 5.6mm, the slim design of this battery is clearly intended to fit the ultra-thin body. iFixit 测量显示，电池包内部搭载了一块容量为 12.26Wh、厚度仅为 2.72mm 的电池，与 iPhone Air 所采用的电池参数高度吻合。考虑到 iPhone Air 机身厚度仅为 5.6mm，这块电池的纤薄设计显然是为了适配超薄机身。

However, despite the battery pack using the same cell, its charging efficiency is not ideal. Due to energy transmission loss in wireless charging, the battery pack can only charge the iPhone Air up to 65% of its capacity. iFixit commented that "the efficiency problem of wireless charging is fully exposed here". 不过，尽管电池包使用了相同电芯，其充电效率却并不理想。由于无线充电存在能量传输损耗，电池包最多只能为 iPhone Air 充至 65％ 电量。对此，iFixit 表示「无线充电的效率问题在此暴露无遗」。

Furthermore, the battery pack's casing is made of plastic and does not continue the iPhone Air's titanium frame design. iFixit speculates that the battery pack's size might be Apple's minimal compromise between functionality and portability. 此外，电池包外壳采用塑料材质，并未延续 iPhone Air 的钛金属框架设计。iFixit 推测，电池包的尺寸可能是苹果在功能性与便携性之间的最小妥协。

This discovery sparked heated discussion among users. Some netizens joked: "Apple split the battery in two, half went into the phone, and the other half was sold to you as an accessory." Others questioned: "Since the battery pack is so thick, why not just make the phone slightly thicker?" 这一发现引发了用户热议。有网友调侃道：「苹果把电池一分为二，一半装进手机，一半卖给你当配件」，也有人质疑「既然电池包这么厚，为何不直接做成稍厚一点的手机？」
