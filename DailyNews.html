BBC 最新调研显示 AI 助手新闻回答近半数存在误导风险

The European Broadcasting Union (EBU) and BBC recently jointly released the 'News Integrity and AI Assistant Toolkit,' pointing out that in research conducted between June and July 2025, 22 public media organizations tested over 3,000 news responses from AI assistants across 18 countries and 14 languages, with approximately 45% containing significant issues that could mislead users . 欧洲广播联盟（EBU）与 BBC 近日联合发布《新闻完整性与 AI 助手工具包》，指出在 2025 年 6 月至 7 月期间的研究中，22 家公共媒体机构在 18 个国家、14 种语言环境下测试了超过 3,000 条 AI 助手的新闻回答， 其中约 45% 存在重大问题，可能对用户造成误导 。

The report shows that the problems are systemic, covering mainstream assistants such as ChatGPT, Copilot, Perplexity, and Google Gemini, primarily focusing on four areas: 报告显示，问题具有系统性，涵盖 ChatGPT、Copilot、Perplexity 与 Google Gemini 等主流助手，主要集中在四个方面：

  • Accuracy: including fabricated facts, outdated information, incorrect causal relationships, etc.; 准确性：包括虚构事实、过时信息、错误因果关系等；
  • Context: omitting key information or viewpoints, leading to one-sided answers; 上下文：遗漏关键信息或观点，导致回答片面；
  • Distinction between fact and opinion: failure to clearly label opinions or incorrect attribution; 事实与观点区分：未能清晰标注意见或错误归属；
  • Source transparency: lack of reliable sources, or citation of fabricated, outdated, or irrelevant links. 来源透明度：缺乏可靠来源，或引用虚构、过时、不相关的链接。

The toolkit also proposes four standards for 'good answers': accurate information, provision of necessary context, distinction between facts and opinions, and clear, verifiable sources. 工具包还提出了「好回答」的四大标准：信息准确、提供必要上下文、区分事实与观点、来源清晰可验证。

At the same time, the report also summarized common failure modes, such as fabricated quotes, logical errors, excessive pandering to user queries (sycophancy), refusal to answer legitimate news questions, and inappropriate editorialization tendencies. 同时，报告还总结了常见失败模式，如虚构引语、逻辑错误、过度迎合用户提问（sycophancy）、拒绝回答合法新闻问题，以及不当的编辑化倾向。

BBC and EBU emphasized that as the role of AI tools in news acquisition grows, ensuring that responses meet journalistic professional standards is crucial. The toolkit aims to provide reference for technology companies, media organizations, researchers, and the public to promote transparency and credibility of AI in the news domain. BBC 与 EBU 强调，随着 AI 工具在新闻获取中的作用日益增强，确保回答符合新闻专业标准至关重要。该工具包旨在为科技公司、媒体机构、研究者及公众提供参考，以推动 AI 在新闻领域的透明度与可信度。

特斯拉「世界模拟器」首曝：1 天蒸馏 500 年驾驶经验

According to Xinzhiyuan, Tesla officially released a "World Simulator" demonstration yesterday, showcasing its end-to-end neural network's ability to self-evolve in virtual environments. 据新智元报道，特斯拉昨日正式发布「世界模拟器」演示，展示其端到端神经网络在虚拟环境中自我进化的能力。

The official statement claims that the system can absorb the equivalent of 500 years of human driving experience in just one day and generate highly realistic driving scenarios for training and testing autonomous driving and the humanoid robot Optimus. 官方称，该系统可在 1 天内吸收相当于人类 500 年的驾驶经验，并生成高度逼真的驾驶场景，用于自动驾驶与人形机器人 Optimus 的训练与测试。

Ashok Elluswamy, head of Tesla's AI team, revealed in his ICCV 2025 keynote speech that the "World Simulator" can not only recreate real road conditions but also generate extreme or rare dangerous scenarios for closed-loop evaluation and adversarial testing. 特斯拉 AI 团队负责人 Ashok Elluswamy 在 ICCV 2025 主题演讲中透露，「世界模拟器」不仅能重现真实路况，还可创造极端或罕见的危险场景，用于闭环评估和对抗性测试。

This simulator shares the same neural network architecture as the autonomous driving system. Inputs include multi-camera footage, vehicle dynamic data, and navigation information, while outputs directly control steering wheel angle and accelerator/brake pressure. 这一模拟器与自动驾驶系统共享同一神经网络架构，输入包括多摄像头画面、车辆动态数据和导航信息，输出则直接为方向盘转角与油门/刹车力度。

Compared to traditional "modular" solutions, Tesla adheres to an "end-to-end" approach, emphasizing learning implicit value judgments through large-scale data, reducing information loss, and improving generalization capabilities for long-tail problems. The amount of driving data generated by its fleet daily is equivalent to hundreds of years of human driving experience, dubbed "Niagara Falls of data." 与传统「模块化」方案相比，特斯拉坚持「端到端」路线，强调通过大规模数据学习隐式价值判断，减少信息损耗，并提升对长尾问题的泛化能力。其车队每天产生的驾驶数据量，相当于人类数百年的驾驶经验，被称为「尼亚加拉瀑布般的数据」。

Furthermore, Tesla has introduced "Generative Gaussian Splatting" technology to achieve high-fidelity 3D scene reconstruction and partially applied natural language explanation features in FSD v14.x to enhance system interpretability. 此外，特斯拉还引入「生成式高斯泼溅」技术，实现高保真 3D 场景重建，并在 FSD v14.x 中部分应用自然语言解释功能，提升系统可解释性。
