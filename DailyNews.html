DeepSeek 悄悄上线新论文，北大清华联创

Recently, DeepSeek, in collaboration with Peking University and Tsinghua University, quietly published a paper, officially releasing a new technical solution called DualPath, which primarily addresses the historical data reading bottleneck encountered by large AI models when executing complex multi-turn tasks. 近期，DeepSeek 联合北京大学与清华大学悄悄上线了一篇论文，正式发布名为 DualPath 的新技术方案，重点解决了 AI 大模型在执行复杂多轮任务时遭遇的历史数据读取瓶颈。

According to the paper, when AI systems process ultra-long contexts, the two computational modules responsible for "processing input information" and "generating text responses" experience data channel resource mismatch. 据论文介绍，现在 AI 系统在处理超长上下文时，负责「处理输入信息」和「生成文本回答」的两个计算模块，会出现数据通道资源错配的情况。

To address this issue, the new DualPath breaks the conventional single-line transmission limitation, allowing historical data to first enter the "response generation" module through an idle channel, and then instantly forward it to the "input processing" module using the cluster's internal high-speed network. 针对此问题， 新的 DualPath 打破了常规的单线传输限制，允许历史数据先通过闲置通道进入「生成回答」模块，随后利用集群内部的高速网络，瞬间转发给「处理输入」模块。

Official evaluation data shows that when processing real and complex AI tasks, this technology boosts offline processing throughput by up to 1.87 times and online service throughput by an average of 1.96 times. 官方评估数据显示，在处理真实复杂的 AI 任务时， 该技术将离线处理吞吐量最高提升 1.87 倍，在线服务吞吐量平均提升 1.96 倍。

Currently, large models are rapidly evolving towards "agents" with autonomous planning capabilities. AI frequently needs to review contexts tens of thousands of words long, causing system performance constraints to shift from "insufficient computing power" to "slow data transmission." 当前，大模型正快速向具备自主规划能力的「智能体（Agent）」方向演进，AI 需要频繁回顾动辄数万字的上下文，导致系统性能的制约因素已从「算力不足」转变为「数据传输太慢」。

The validation of DualPath technology proves that by optimizing the underlying data flow, idle resources can be significantly revitalized without increasing hardware procurement costs. 而 DualPath 技术的验证，证明了通过优化底层数据流向，可以在不增加硬件采购成本的情况下大幅盘活闲置资源。

今年全球手机市场或将陷入暴跌

According to Bloomberg, citing IDC, global smartphone shipments are expected to shrink significantly by 12.9% in 2026, severely impacted by the storage chip supply crisis. 据彭博社援引机构 IDC 消息，受存储芯片供应危机的严重影响，2026 年全球智能手机出货量预计将大幅萎缩 12.9%。

Specifically, IDC predicts that global smartphone shipments in 2026 will drop from 1.26 billion units in the previous year to approximately 1.1 billion units. 具体来看， IDC 预测 2026 年全球智能手机出货量将从上一年的 12.6 亿部降至约 11 亿部。

Analysis indicates that the global storage supply is extremely tight due to the continuous squeeze from AI tasks' demand for advanced storage chips, forcing mobile phone manufacturers to adjust business strategies, cut back on low-margin entry-level models, and push consumers towards high-end devices. 分析指出，受人工智能任务对先进存储芯片需求的持续挤压，全球存储供应极度紧张，迫使手机制造商调整业务策略，削减利润微薄的入门级机型并推动消费者转向高端设备。

Analysts at Counterpoint, another research firm, stated that the industry has never experienced such a drastic decline and have declared 2026 to be "the worst year in smartphone history." 另一家调研机构 Counterpoint 的分析师称，行业从未经历过如此剧烈的下滑，并定调 2026 年将成为「智能手机历史上最糟糕的一年」。

It is reported that due to the sharp increase in upstream memory and storage chip costs, the industry widely believes that 2026 will be the "first year of major price increases" for the mobile phone industry. 据悉，受上游内存与存储芯片成本的急剧攀升，行业普遍认为 2026 年将成为手机行业的「大涨价元年」。

Recently, multiple supply chain and channel insiders have also confirmed that the procurement cost of smartphone storage chips has increased by over 80% compared to the same period last year, with no signs of the price hike slowing down. 近期也有多家供应链与渠道人士确认，智能手机存储芯片采购成本较去年同期已上涨超过 80%，且涨势仍未见放缓迹象。

Affected by this, mainstream brands such as OPPO, OnePlus, vivo, Xiaomi, iQOO, and Honor are planning to initiate a new round of price adjustments in early March. This will be the largest and most significant collective price adjustment in the past five years. Some analyses even suggest that the budget phone market might face the dilemma of "loss upon release, loss upon sale." 受此影响，OPPO、一加、vivo、小米、iQOO、荣耀等主流品牌已拟定于 3 月初启动新一轮价格调整，这将成为近五年来规模最大、涨幅最显著的一轮集体调价。甚至有分析之处，千元机市场或将迎来「发布即亏，卖出即赔」的窘境。

Recently, Hu Xinxin, product manager for Xiaomi REDMI, also commented on the matter, stating that the current storage super cycle is truly a "ghost story" series for mobile phone manufacturers. 日前，小米 REDMI 产品经理胡馨心也就此事发表了看法，她表示，当前的存储超级周期对手机厂商而言，真是「鬼故事」系列。
