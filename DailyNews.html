Gurman：从「AIMLess」到 WWDC 大改版，苹果用两年时间重建 AI 战略

Apple will hold WWDC 2026 in the early morning of June 9th, Beijing time, bringing its largest-ever Siri and AI feature updates. 苹果将于北京时间 6 月 9 日凌晨召开 WWDC 2026，带来其史上规模最大的 Siri 与 AI 功能更新。

Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman revealed in last night's PowerOn newsletter that Apple has gone through a difficult period in the AI field. Apple's internal AI team was once jokingly called "AIMLess," reflecting external doubts about its AI direction. The failure of Apple Intelligence 1.0, coupled with the rapid progress of competitors, forced Apple's senior management to hold a secret meeting in early 2025 to initiate reforms. 彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 在昨晚发布的 PowerOn 新闻通讯透露，苹果在 AI 领域经历的一段艰难时期。苹果内部 AI 团队曾被戏称为「AIMLess」，折射出外界对其 AI 方向感的质疑。Apple Intelligence 1.0 的失利，叠加竞争对手的快速推进，迫使苹果高层于 2025 年初召开秘密会议，启动整改。

Subsequently, Vision Pro founder Mike Rockwell took over Siri, software head Craig Federighi fully led the AI strategy, former Google and Microsoft engineering executive Amar Subramanya joined to lead model research, and former AI head John Giannandrea left this year. 此后，Vision Pro 创始人 Mike Rockwell 接管 Siri，软件主管 Craig Federighi 全面主导 AI 战略，前 Google 和微软工程高管 Amar Subramanya 加入负责模型研究，前 AI 负责人 John Giannandrea 则于今年离职。

WWDC 2026 is also CEO Tim Cook's last major product unveiling before officially stepping down. Cook previously admitted that the errors in Apple Intelligence surpassed those of the Apple Maps incident, and he personally became deeply involved in AI planning after the crisis. WWDC 2026 也是 CEO Tim Cook 在正式卸任前的最后一次重大产品亮相。库克此前承认，Apple Intelligence 的失误程度超过苹果地图事件，并在危机后亲自深度介入 AI 规划。

The core highlight of this conference is the fully upgraded AI Siri. Apple plans to launch a standalone AI assistant app that can compete with ChatGPT, supporting chatbot-like interactions and introducing generative AI image editing features. 此次大会的核心看点是全面升级的 AI Siri。苹果计划推出可与 ChatGPT 竞争的独立 AI 助手应用，支持类聊天机器人交互，并引入生成式 AI 图像编辑功能。

At the system level, new versions such as iOS 27, macOS 27, watchOS 27, and visionOS 27 will be unveiled simultaneously. In terms of underlying technology, Apple has partnered with Google to replace Siri's original technical solution with the Gemini model and Google Cloud infrastructure. 系统层面，iOS 27、macOS 27、watchOS 27、visionOS 27 等新版本将同步亮相。底层技术上，苹果已与 Google 达成合作，以 Gemini 模型和 Google Cloud 基础设施替换 Siri 原有技术方案。

Google 向 SpaceX 租用每月 9.2 亿美元 AI 算力

According to TechCrunch, SpaceX disclosed a new computing power agreement with Google before its IPO. According to the document, Google will pay SpaceX $920 million per month from October 2026 to June 2029 for computing power corresponding to approximately 110,000 Nvidia GPUs, CPUs, memory, and related components. 据 TechCrunch 报道，SpaceX 在 IPO 前披露与 Google 签署新的算力协议。根据文件，Google 将从 2026 年 10 月至 2029 年 6 月，每月向 SpaceX 支付 9.2 亿美元，使用约 11 万张英伟达 GPU、CPU、内存和相关组件对应的计算能力。

这份协议与 SpaceX 5 月同 Anthropic 签下的算力租赁安排类似，但规模约为后者的一半。Anthropic 当时约定以每月 12.5 亿美元租用 Colossus 1 数据中心可用算力。

Google 对 TechCrunch 表示，这是一份短期过渡协议，用来满足 Gemini Enterprise 和智能体平台超出预期的客户需求。协议还包含退出条款：

2026 年 12 月 31 日后，双方均可提前 90 天通知终止；如果 SpaceX 到 2026 年 9 月 30 日未能交付承诺 GPU 数量，Google 可终止协议或按实际交付数量降低月费。

💡 心理学家：把认知工作外包给 AI，会「失去对大脑的控制」

According to MIT Technology Review, Gloria Mark, a psychologist at the University of California, Irvine, discussed the impact of digital technology and AI on attention in an interview at SXSW London. She pointed out that people have largely "lost control over their attention". 据 MIT Technology Review 报道，加州大学欧文分校心理学家 Gloria Mark 在 SXSW London 接受采访时讨论了数字技术和 AI 对注意力的影响。她指出，人们已经在很大程度上「失去对注意力的控制」。

Mark's long-term experiments show that in 2003, users could focus for an average of about 2 and a half minutes before switching to another task; this dropped to about 75 seconds in 2012; and further decreased to an average of 47 seconds in studies from 2014 to 2020. She also observed a direct correlation between rapid attention switching and increased stress in her experiments. Mark 的长期实验显示，2003 年用户在切换到其他任务前平均能专注约 2 分半钟；2012 年降至约 75 秒；2014 年至 2020 年的研究中进一步降到平均 47 秒。她还在实验中看到，注意力快速切换与压力上升存在直接相关。

Her greater concern regarding AI chatbots is the outsourcing of "deep processing". Mark stated that when people actively evaluate, summarize, and understand information, they are more likely to learn, comprehend, and remember it; 她对 AI 聊天机器人更担心的部分，是「深度处理」被外包。Mark 表示，当人主动评估、总结和理解信息时，会更可能学习、理解和记住它；

If writing, summarizing, and evaluating are handed over to ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, humans are essentially entrusting cognitive work to AI. She also extended the issue to synthetic companionship products, believing that submissive robot relationships could weaken the emotional capacity humans need to maintain real relationships. 如果把写作、总结、评价交给 ChatGPT、Claude 或 Gemini，人就把认知工作交给了 AI。她还把问题延伸到合成陪伴产品，认为顺从型机器人关系会削弱人维持真实关系所需的情绪能力。

She suggests that people rethink their relationship with AI technology: read entire books instead of just summaries; meet friends in person whenever possible; and reduce reliance on GPS in familiar environments. She believes that effort itself brings deeper satisfaction, and these examples also serve as retraining for attention, memory, and emotional capabilities. 她建议人们重新思考与 AI 技术的关系：读完整本书而非只看摘要；能当面见朋友时尽量当面；在熟悉环境里减少对 GPS 的依赖。她认为，努力本身会带来更深层的满足感，这些例子也是对注意力、记忆和情绪能力的重新训练。

OpenCV 5 发布，原生支持 Transformer 与视觉语言模型

The OpenCV team officially released OpenCV 5 this week. This version modernizes the architecture of the twenty-year-old computer vision library, with key updates to the DNN engine, ONNX support, hardware acceleration layer, Python integration, and 3D vision capabilities. The main changes in OpenCV 5 include: OpenCV 团队本周正式发布 OpenCV 5。这一版本对这个已有二十多年历史的计算机视觉库做了架构现代化，重点更新 DNN 引擎、ONNX 支持、硬件加速层、Python 集成和 3D 视觉能力。OpenCV 5 的主要变化包括：

  • The next-generation DNN engine adopts a graph-based architecture, supporting operator fusion, extensive ONNX support, and includes support for Transformers, visual language models, and large language models. 下一代 DNN 引擎采用基于图的架构，支持算子融合、广泛 ONNX 支持，并加入对 Transformer、视觉语言模型和大型语言模型的支持。
  • ONNX operator coverage has increased from less than 23% in the 4.x era to over 80%. ONNX 算子覆盖率从 4.x 时代不到 23% 提升到超过 80%。
  • The core library deprecates traditional C APIs, features a more streamlined build process, and adds 0D/1D tensors, FP16/BF16, and a logging mechanism. 核心库弃用传统 C API，构建方式更精简，并加入 0D/1D 张量、FP16/BF16 和日志机制。
  • The hardware acceleration layer is clearer, making it easier for hardware vendors to directly insert optimized kernels; the 3D vision section adds ChArUco calibration boards, multi-camera calibration, and visualization capabilities. 硬件加速层更清晰，方便硬件厂商直接插入优化内核；3D 视觉部分新增 ChArUco 标定板、多相机标定和可视化能力。

瑞幸回应拿铁去冰不满杯，点单页增加提示

According to Yicai, after Shanghai consumers reported that a "super-sized latte with no ice was only half a cup," Luckin Coffee's ordering page has added a small note stating "no ice may result in a less than full cup." 第一财经报道，上海消费者反映瑞幸「超大杯拿铁去冰后仅半杯」后，瑞幸咖啡点单页面已增加「去冰可能不满杯」的小字提示。

The report mentioned that the consumer involved was told by the store that it was "the company's standard recipe": no extra milk or coffee would be added after removing ice. 报道提到，涉事消费者在门店询问后得到的答复是「公司标准配方」：去冰后不会额外多加牛奶或咖啡。

Yicai then consulted several Luckin Coffee stores in Beijing's Xicheng District as a consumer. A store employee stated that for lattes, milk would be added to compensate for ice removal, and the standard is to fill to 1cm below the rim, so a situation where it's more than half empty should not occur; if a noticeably underfilled cup is encountered, a refund can be processed with the order. 第一财经随后以消费者身份咨询北京西城区多家瑞幸门店，有门店店员表示，拿铁类去冰会补牛奶，出品标准是距离杯口 1cm，不应出现半杯有余的情况；如果遇到明显不满杯，可凭订单退款。
