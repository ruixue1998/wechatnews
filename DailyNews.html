量子手机有望今年上市

According to Economic Observer Online, the quantum communication mobile phone REED ONE GK5 prototype, jointly developed by China Telecom and QuantumCTek, recently made its official debut. 据经济观察网消息，中国电信与国盾量子联合研发的量子通信手机 REED ONE GK5 样机在近期正式亮相。

The report states that this device is a modification of an existing brand mobile phone and is expected to be launched into the civilian market by the end of the year or early next year, marking the extension of quantum secure communication technology from national key infrastructure to the lives of ordinary consumers. 报道称，这款设备基于现有品牌手机改造而成，预计将在年底或明年初推向民用市场，标志着量子保密通信技术从国家级重要基础设施向普通消费者生活的延伸。

It is understood that the core security mechanism of quantum communication mobile phones is based on the principle of quantum no-cloning. Unlike traditional encryption methods, it transmits keys through single photons, and any eavesdropping behavior will cause the photon state to change, thereby triggering an alarm and interrupting communication, fundamentally eliminating the risk of information leakage. 据悉，量子通信手机的核心安全机制基于量子不可克隆原理。与传统加密方式不同，它通过单个光子传输密钥，任何窃听行为都会导致光子状态改变，从而触发警报并中断通信，从根本上杜绝信息泄露风险。

It is worth mentioning that this quantum phone has achieved 100% localization, with all components manufactured in China. The new phone will be equipped with the Unisoc T9100 chip and a dual SPU encryption unit. 值得一提的是，这款量子手机实现了 100% 国产化，所有零部件均由中国制造，新机将搭载了紫光展锐 T9100 芯片和双 SPU 加密单元。

It is understood that in the process of technological development, the Chinese research team has undergone 17 years of effort. From initially relying heavily on imported core components to now having globally leading independent research and development capabilities, this is inseparable from the tireless exploration of scientists represented by Tang Shibiao. 据了解，在技术发展过程中，中国科研团队经历了长达 17 年的努力。从最初核心组件严重依赖进口，到如今自主研发能力全球领先，这离不开以唐世彪为代表的科学家们不懈探索。

According to previous reports by CCTV News, the sixth-generation dual-channel single-photon detector developed by Tang Shibiao's team has been reduced in size to that of a thumbnail, more than 200 times smaller than the first-generation product, making it currently the smallest and most integrated single-photon detector. 据央视新闻此前报道，唐世彪团队研发的第六代双通道单光子探测器，体积已缩小至拇指盖大小，较初代产品缩小了 200 多倍，是目前体积最小、集成度最高的单光子探测器。
