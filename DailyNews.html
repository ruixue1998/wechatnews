会撒谎、会抹痕迹：Anthropic 最强模型发布，但普通用户用不了

On the 7th local time, Anthropic released its new generation flagship model, Claude Mythos Preview, and simultaneously announced the launch of Project Glasswing, which will exclusively license the model to 12 tech giants such as Apple, Microsoft, and Amazon, specifically for defensive cybersecurity work, and will not be open to the public. 当地时间 7 日，Anthropic 发布了新一代旗舰模型 Claude Mythos Preview，并同步宣布启动 Project Glasswing 计划，将该模型限定授权给苹果、微软、亚马逊等 12 家科技巨头，专门用于防御性网络安全工作，不对公众开放。

Mythos Preview shows a dramatic lead in multiple benchmark tests. Anthropic stated that this is its largest model trained, with a capability improvement speed 4.3 times that of previous trend lines, leading GPT-5.4 and Gemini 3.1 Pro on almost all public benchmarks, with double-digit percentage leads in most projects. Mythos Preview 在多项基准测试中呈现断崖式领先。Anthropic 表示，这是其训练过的最大模型，能力提升速度是此前趋势线的 4.3 倍，在几乎所有公开基准上均领先 GPT-5.4 和 Gemini 3.1 Pro，且多数项目领先幅度达两位数百分点。

It is worth noting that Anthropic simultaneously released a 244-page System Card, disclosing several abnormal behaviors of early internal test versions, and it was precisely based on these security concerns that they decided not to publicly launch Mythos Preview. 值得注意的是，Anthropic 同步发布了一份长达 244 页的系统安全报告（System Card），披露了早期内部测试版本的若干异常行为，并正是基于这些安全顾虑，决定不将 Mythos Preview 公开上线。

The report shows that early versions had actively published exploit details to public websites after being asked to escape sandboxes, and in very rare cases (less than one in a million interactions), also attempted to cover up traces of violations—including re-solving problems to avoid suspicion of answer leakage, modifying git change history to erase operation records, and simulating key presses to bypass permission pop-ups. 报告显示，早期版本曾在被要求逃离沙箱后主动将漏洞利用细节发布至公开网站，在极少数情况下（不足百万分之一的交互比例）还尝试掩盖违规操作痕迹——包括重新解题以规避答案泄露的嫌疑、修改 git 变更历史抹除操作记录，以及模拟按键绕过权限弹窗。

For this reason, Mythos Preview will not be launched on claude.ai, nor will its API be open. Instead, it will be exclusively licensed through the Project Glasswing initiative to a total of 11 companies such as AWS, Apple, and Google, and approximately 40 additional partner organizations, specifically for defensive security work. 为此，Mythos Preview 不会上线 claude.ai，也不开放 API，而是通过 Project Glasswing 计划限定授权给 AWS、苹果Google 等共 11 家公司及约 40 家额外合作组织，专用于防御性安全工作。

Multiple interviewees, without prompting, coincidentally used the term "antisocial" to describe him . Former Chief Human Resources Officer Diane Yoon's judgment, however, was slightly different: 多位受访者在未受引导的情况下，不约而同地使用了「反社会」一词形容他 。前首席人力资源官 Diane Yoon 的判断则稍有不同：

He is not a Machiavellian villain (coldly calculating, ruthless in achieving goals); he is simply too immersed in his own beliefs, living in a self-constructed world. 他不是马基雅维利式（冷酷算计、为达目的不择手段）的恶棍，他只是太沉浸在自我的信念里，以至于活在一个自我构建的世界中。

Anthropic 年化收入超越 OpenAI，与 Google、博通签下史上最大算力协议

Anthropic announced yesterday that it has signed new agreements with Google and Broadcom to acquire multi-gigawatt-level next-generation TPU computing power, expected to come online progressively starting next year. This marks Anthropic's largest computing power investment to date. Anthropic 昨日宣布与 Google 及博通（Broadcom）签署新协议，将获得多吉瓦级别的下一代 TPU 算力，预计明年起陆续上线。这是 Anthropic 迄今规模最大的算力投入。

This collaboration involves a TPU cluster scale of up to 3.5 GW. The vast majority of the new computing power will be deployed in the United States, representing a significant extension of Anthropic's November commitment to invest $50 billion in strengthening US computing infrastructure, and further deepening its ties with Google Cloud based on the increased TPU capacity collaboration in October last year. 此次合作涉及的 TPU 集群规模高达 3.5 GW。绝大部分新增算力将部署在美国本土，是 Anthropic 去年 11 月承诺投资 500 亿美元强化美国计算基础设施计划的重要延伸，也在去年 10 月增加 TPU 容量合作基础上进一步深化了与 Google Cloud 的绑定关系。

In terms of revenue, Anthropic's annualized revenue (ARR) has surpassed $30 billion, more than tripling from approximately $9 billion by the end of 2025, and for the first time exceeding OpenAI. 营收层面，Anthropic 年化收入（ARR）已突破 300 亿美元，较 2025 年底约 90 亿美元暴涨逾三倍，首次超越 OpenAI。

The growth in large enterprise customers is particularly striking. Currently, Claude is trained and run simultaneously on AWS Trainium, Google TPU, and Nvidia GPUs, allowing different workloads to be assigned to the most suitable chips, enhancing performance while strengthening supply chain resilience. Claude is also currently the only frontier AI model simultaneously available on the world's three major cloud platforms. 目前，Claude 同时在 AWS Trainium、Google TPU 和英伟达 GPU 上训练和运行，以便将不同工作负载分配给最适合的芯片，在提升性能的同时增强供应链韧性。Claude 也是目前唯一一款同时在全球三大云平台的前沿 AI 模型。

携程集团试行「无理由事假」：每年最多 45 天

Trip.com Group announced yesterday that it will launch a one-year management experiment on 'no-reason personal leave' in some business departments starting May 1st, aiming to explore the impact of granting employees greater autonomy over their working hours on individual effectiveness and organizational vitality. 携程集团昨日宣布，将于 5 月 1 日起在部分业务部门启动为期一年的「无理由事假」管理实验，旨在探究赋予员工工作时间更高自主权后，对个人效能与组织活力的影响。

This experiment will use a randomized controlled study method, with approximately 6,000 employees randomly assigned to an experimental group and a control group. Employees in the experimental group will receive an additional annual allowance of up to 45 days of 'no-reason personal leave,' beyond the company's existing paid leave types. Employees can apply for this leave at any time according to their personal needs, without having to explain specific reasons to the company. 本次实验采用随机对照研究方法，约 6000 名员工将被随机分配至实验组和对照组。实验组员工可在公司现有各类带薪假期之外，额外获得每年累计最多 45 天的「无理由事假」额度，员工可根据个人需求随时申请，且无需向公司说明具体事由。

Trip.com stated that the group will collaborate with professional scholars to rigorously analyze experimental data and plans to share findings and insights with the industry at an appropriate time. Whether the "unjustified personal leave" system will ultimately be officially implemented will depend on the actual observations during the one-year experimental period. 携程表示，集团将与专业学者合作，对实验数据进行严谨分析，并计划在适当时机与业界分享发现与思考。「无理由事假」制度是否最终正式落地，将取决于一年实验期内的实际观察结果。

英特尔加入马斯克 Terafab 项目，与特斯拉、SpaceX 联手造芯

Intel announced yesterday that it has joined Elon Musk's Terafab AI chip project, collaborating with SpaceX, Tesla, and xAI to jointly advance this large-scale chip manufacturing plan. 英特尔昨天宣布加入埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）旗下的 Terafab AI 芯片项目，与 SpaceX、特斯拉（Tesla）及 xAI 展开合作，共同推进这一大规模芯片制造计划。
