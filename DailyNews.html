苹果推送 iOS 26.5 Beta 2，地图广告加速落地

According to Apple Insider, Apple's iOS 26.5 developer beta Beta 2, released yesterday, added a pop-up notification about an upcoming advertising feature in the built-in Maps app, marking another step forward in the official rollout of Maps ads. 据 Apple Insider 报道，苹果昨日发布的 iOS 26.5 开发者测试版 Beta 2 中，自带地图 App 新增了一个关于即将推出广告功能的弹窗提示，标志着地图广告正式落地的进程又向前迈进了一步。

Maps may display local ads based on your approximate location, current search terms, or the map view at the time of search. To protect your privacy, ad information will not be associated with your Apple account. 地图可能会根据您的大致位置、当前搜索词或搜索时的地图视图展示本地广告。为保护您的隐私，广告信息不会与您的 Apple 账户关联。

Furthermore, ads cannot access the "Visited Locations" feature, and Apple will not share any related location data with advertisers. 此外，广告无法访问「已访问地点」功能，苹果也不会与广告商共享任何相关位置数据。

Currently, this pop-up does not offer an option to opt out of ads. According to public information, after ads officially launch, they will appear in two locations: at the top of search results, and within the new "Recommended Places" feature – the latter has already appeared in iOS 26.5 Beta 1, where clicking the search bar brings up business recommendations. 目前该弹窗并不提供退出广告的选项。根据公开信息，广告正式上线后将出现在两个位置：搜索结果顶部，以及新增的「推荐地点」功能中——后者在 iOS 26.5 Beta 1 中已经出现，点击搜索栏时会弹出商家推荐。

Ads will be clearly labeled and displayed based on relevance, allowing users to directly make calls, navigate, or place orders through them. 广告将被明确标注，并基于相关性进行展示，用户可直接通过广告拨打电话、导航前往或下单。

Yesterday, it was reported that Honor was in talks with ByteDance regarding cooperation on a "Doubao phone," but Honor later denied it. 昨天有报道称，荣耀正与字节跳动就「豆包手机」相关合作展开接洽，但后续荣耀方面予以否认。

According to Jiemian News, Honor responded that, after internal confirmation, the relevant rumors are not true. 据界面新闻报道，荣耀回应称，经内部确认，相关传闻并不属实。

OpenAI 伦敦「新家」定档：首个永久办公室明年见

On April 13, OpenAI announced it would establish its first permanent office space in London, expected to officially open in 2027, thereby creating its largest research hub outside the United States. 4 月 13 日，OpenAI 宣布将在伦敦设立首个永久性办公场地，预计于 2027 年正式启用，以此打造其在美国以外的最大研究枢纽。

According to Reuters, the new office will be located in the Regent Quarter of London's King's Cross area, specifically spanning two buildings: Jahn Court and Brassworks. 据路透社报道，新办公室选址于伦敦国王十字（King’s Cross）地区的 Regent Quarter，具体横跨 Jahn Court 与 Brassworks 两栋大楼。

Official data shows that the site is planned to accommodate up to 544 team members, aiming to meet the growing business demands in the UK and European markets. 官方数据显示，该场地规划最高可容纳 544 名团队成员，旨在应对英国及欧洲市场日益增长的业务需求。

Currently, OpenAI employs approximately 200 staff in the London area, with business functions covering foundational model research, engineering development, customer support, public policy, and regional sales. 目前，OpenAI 在伦敦地区已雇佣约 200 名员工，业务职能涵盖底层模型研究、工程开发、客户支持、公共政策以及区域销售。

OpenAI 内部备忘录：微软「限制了我们触达客户」

According to CNBC, OpenAI Chief Revenue Officer Denise Dresser sent an internal memo to all employees this Sunday local time, openly supporting the company's strategic partnership with Amazon and stating that the long-term relationship with Microsoft "limits our ability to reach enterprise customers." 据 CNBC 报道，OpenAI 首席营收官 Denise Dresser 于当地时间本周日向全体员工发送了一份内部备忘录，公开力挺公司与亚马逊的战略合作，并直言与微软的长期合作关系「限制了我们触达企业客户的能力」。

This memo comes less than two months after Amazon announced an investment of up to $50 billion in OpenAI and the establishment of a strategic partnership. 这份备忘录距亚马逊宣布向 OpenAI 投资最高 500 亿美元、建立战略合作伙伴关系不足两个月。

Our partnership with Microsoft is the cornerstone of our success. However, it also limits our engagement with enterprise customers – for many businesses, their home is Bedrock. 我们与微软的合作是我们成功的基石。但它也限制了我们与企业客户的对接——对许多企业来说，他们所在的地方是 Bedrock。

Amazon Web Services (AWS)'s Bedrock platform allows enterprises to access various mainstream AI models, including OpenAI. 亚马逊云服务 AWS 旗下的 Bedrock 平台，允许企业接入包括 OpenAI 在内的各主流 AI 模型。

Since 2019, Microsoft has cumulatively invested over $13 billion in OpenAI, making it its earliest and largest external supporter. 自 2019 年起，微软已累计向 OpenAI 投资逾 130 亿美元，是其最早也是最大的外部支持者。

However, the relationship between the two parties has shown cracks in recent years—Microsoft listed OpenAI as a competitor in its 2024 annual report and began publicly testing its self-developed AI models; OpenAI, meanwhile, continues to turn to other cloud service providers such as CoreWeave, Google, and Oracle to obtain computing resources. 然而双方关系近年来已出现裂痕——微软于 2024 年中将 OpenAI 列入年报竞争对手名单，并开始公开测试自研 AI 模型；OpenAI 则持续转向 CoreWeave、Google、甲骨文等其他云服务商以获取算力资源。

Dresser also launched a direct attack on Anthropic in the memo. Dresser 还在备忘录中对 Anthropic 发起了正面攻势。

She claimed that Anthropic's annualized revenue announced this year has exceeded $30 billion, but approximately $8 billion of it is 'falsely reported'. 她声称 Anthropic 今年公布的年化营收已超过 300 亿美元，但其中约 80 亿美元存在「虚报」。

They adopted accounting methods that make revenue appear larger than it actually is, including counting the total revenue share with Amazon and Google. 他们采用了让营收看起来比实际更大的会计处理方式，包括将与亚马逊和 Google 的分成收入总额计入」。

Dresser also pointed out that Anthropic's 'failure to acquire sufficient computing power' was a strategic misstep. Earlier this month, Anthropic reached an agreement with Google and Broadcom to obtain 'several gigawatts' of computing power support. Dresser 还指出 Anthropic「未能获取足够算力」是一项战略失误。Anthropic 本月早些时候已与 Google 和博通达成协议，将获得「数吉瓦」级别的算力支持。

人形机器人「上岗」景区，智元远征 A3 首批商用交付完成

Zhiyuan Robotics recently held the first batch delivery ceremony for Expedition A3 in Qidong, Jiangsu, officially shipping this full-size humanoid robot in bulk to commercial clients. 智元机器人近日在江苏启东举行远征 A3 首批交付仪式，正式将这款全尺寸人形机器人批量发往商业客户。

At the delivery ceremony, Wang Chuang, partner and senior vice president of Zhiyuan Robotics, delivered the first batch of commercial machines to two clients, Qihong Culture & Tourism and Xiuka Technology. The first batch of robots will be deployed in key scenic areas and cultural venues in Qidong, undertaking tasks such as intelligent guidance, immersive performances, and smart operations. 在交付仪式上，智元机器人合伙人、高级副总裁王闯将首批商用机交付给启泓文旅和秀卡科技两家客户。现场，远征 A3 完成了多套舞蹈动作展示。首批机器人将部署于启东重点景区及文博场馆，承担智能导览、沉浸式演艺和智慧运营等任务。

金山办公一季度净利预增超 4 倍

Last night, Kingsoft Office disclosed its Q1 2026 performance forecast, with both revenue and profit exceeding expectations, and net profit growth being particularly striking. 昨晚，金山办公披露 2026 年一季度业绩预告，营收与利润双双超预期，其中净利润增幅尤为亮眼。

It is estimated that in the first quarter of this year, operating revenue will reach 1.565 billion to 1.662 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 20.24% to 27.68%; net profit attributable to the parent company is expected to reach 2.022 billion to 2.307 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 401.89% to 472.81%, directly more than quadrupling; non-recurring net profit is expected to be 1.999 billion to 2.282 billion yuan, also more than quadrupling year-on-year. 预计今年一季度实现营业收入 15.65 亿元至 16.62 亿元，同比增长 20.24% 至 27.68%；归母净利润预计达 20.22 亿元至 23.07 亿元，同比增幅高达 401.89% 至 472.81%，直接翻超四倍；扣非净利润预计为 19.99 亿元至 22.82 亿元，同比增幅同样超过四倍。

The main business's profitability continues to maintain a steady growth trend. 剔除该投资收益影响后，公司归母净利润约为 4.87 亿元至 5.44 亿元，同比增长 20.99% 至 35.15%；扣非净利润约为 4.65 亿元至 5.19 亿元，同比增长 19.19% 至 33.14%。主业盈利能力依然保持稳健增长态势。

💡 微软高管：AI 智能体将像员工一样购买软件许可证

According to Business Insider, Microsoft Executive Vice President Rajesh Jha recently suggested at an industry conference that in the future, when enterprises widely deploy AI agents, these agents may need to purchase software licenses just like employees. 据《商业内幕》报道，微软执行副总裁 Rajesh Jha 近日在一场行业会议上提出，未来企业大规模部署 AI 智能体后，这些智能体或许需要像员工一样购买软件许可证。

Jha believes that when the number of AI agents in an organization exceeds human employees, each agent will become an independent "user" requiring its own identity, login account, and even software seat. Jha 认为，当组织中 AI 智能体的数量超过人类员工时，每个智能体都将成为一个独立的「用户」，需要拥有自己的身份标识、登录账号乃至软件席位。

Each of these AI agents, with independent identities in the system, is a potential paid seat. 这些在系统中拥有独立身份的 AI 智能体，每一个都是潜在的付费席位。

For example, in a company with 20 employees, if each employee is equipped with 5 AI agents, and the number of employees is reduced to 10, the company may still need to purchase 50 paid seats. 以一家拥有 20 名员工的公司为例，若每位员工配备 5 个 AI 智能体，同时员工人数缩减至 10 人，企业仍可能需要购买 50 个付费席位。

However, not everyone agrees with this logic. Nenad Milicevic, a partner at consulting firm AlixPartners, believes that AI agents will reduce the number of humans directly interacting with software, thereby significantly compressing license demand. 然而，并非所有人都认同这一逻辑。咨询公司 AlixPartners 合伙人 Nenad Milicevic 认为，AI 智能体将减少与软件直接交互的人类数量，从而大幅压缩许可证需求。

Work that originally required 20 employees might only need one person supervising a few agents in the future, which would significantly reduce software vendors' bargaining power. 原本需要 20 名员工的工作，未来可能只需一人监管少数几个智能体即可完成，这将使软件厂商的议价能力显著下降。

Previously, the filmmakers announced on March 14 that all versions would be extended until April 16. Now, with another extension, the screening period is further stretched to over three months. 此前，片方曾于 3 月 14 日宣布全版本延长放映至 4 月 16 日，如今再度续期，映期进一步拉长至逾三个月。

"Blade of the Phantom Master: Wind from the Desert" was released during this year's Spring Festival holiday. Directed by "the world's number one martial arts choreographer" Yuen Woo-ping, starring Wu Jing, Nicholas Tse, and Yu Shi, it is adapted from the highly-rated domestic comic "Blade of the Phantom Master". The story is set in the third year of Daye during the late Sui Dynasty, telling the jianghu tale of swordsman Dao Ma escorting Zhi Shilang to Chang'an and encountering multiple ambushes along the way. 《镖人：风起大漠》于今年春节档上映，由「天下第一武指」袁和平执导，吴京、谢霆锋、于适领衔主演，改编自高分国产漫画《镖人》，故事背景设定于隋末大业三年，讲述镖客刀马护送知世郎前往长安、途中遭遇多方围剿的江湖故事。

贾樟柯新短片《都灵之影》杀青

According to Sina Film, Jia Zhangke's directed short film "Torino Shadow" has completed filming, and stills and behind-the-scenes photos have been released. 据新浪电影，贾樟柯执导的电影短片《都灵之影》（TORINO SHADOW）已完成拍摄，并公布了剧照及工作照。

The film tells the story of a woman who, on her way from Guangdong to Turin to visit her husband, unexpectedly rediscovers herself and simultaneously discovers the story of cinema. It is reported that a classic song by the band Wutiaoren is also used in the film. 影片讲述一位女性在从广东前往都灵探望丈夫的途中，意外找回自我、同时发现电影的故事。据悉，片中还使用了五条人乐队的一首经典歌曲。
