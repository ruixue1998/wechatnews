苹果承认 AI 落后，用「AI App Store」换一条活路

Yesterday, Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman pointed out in his latest PowerOn column that Apple has admitted its own AI technology lags behind competitors like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude, with no hope of catching up in the short term. 昨天，彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 在最新的 PowerOn 专栏文章中指出，苹果已承认自家 AI 技术落后于 ChatGPT、Gemini 及 Claude 等竞争对手，且短期内无望赶上。

Facing this reality, Apple has chosen to abandon direct competition and instead profit from hardware sales and its service ecosystem. 面对这一现实，苹果选择放弃正面竞争，转向依靠硬件销售与服务生态获利。

The core of the new strategy is the "Siri Extensions" feature, set to launch with iOS 27, which will allow users to install and run third-party AI chat tools within Siri, and will feature a dedicated section in the App Store. 新战略的核心是即将随 iOS 27 推出的「Siri 扩展」（Extensions）功能，允许用户在 Siri 中安装并运行第三方 AI 聊天工具，并在 App Store 中开辟专属区域。

Through this platform, Apple can continuously generate revenue from third-party AI service providers by leveraging the App Store's 30% commission mechanism . At the same time, Apple is also integrating Gemini technology to enhance Siri and other AI features, ensuring a basic user experience. 通过这一平台，苹果可 借助 App Store 30% 的抽成机制，从第三方 AI 服务商持续获取收入 。与此同时，苹果也在引入 Gemini 技术来强化 Siri 及其他 AI 功能、保证基础体验。

Gurman also revealed on X that Apple's 50th-anniversary celebration series will conclude this week with a grand finale party for employees, held at the Apple Park headquarters in Cupertino. Gurman 还在 X 透露，苹果 50 周年系列庆典将于本周以一场面向员工的压轴派对收官，地点设在位于库比蒂诺的 Apple Park 总部。

He stated that employees were ecstatic after learning about the headlining performer and provided three clues: 'He is still active,' 'Was part of the British Invasion,' and 'Jobs would be thrilled about this.' 他表示，员工们在获悉头条表演嘉宾后情绪高涨，并给出了三条线索：「他依然活跃」、「曾是英伦入侵的一部分」、「乔布斯会为此欣喜若狂」。

Based on various inferences, the headlining guest can almost certainly be identified as Paul McCartney. 综合多方推断，头条嘉宾几乎可以确定是 Paul McCartney。

The British Invasion specifically refers to the cultural wave in the 1960s when British rock bands, represented by The Beatles, swept across America, and McCartney remains active on global performance stages to this day. 英伦入侵（British Invasion）特指 1960 年代以披头士乐队为代表的英国摇滚乐团席卷美国的文化浪潮，而 McCartney 至今仍活跃于全球演出舞台。

Steve Jobs was an avid fan of The Beatles during his lifetime, and he once publicly stated: 乔布斯生前是披头士的狂热粉丝，他曾公开表示：

My business model is The Beatles... They balanced each other, and the whole was greater than the sum of its parts. Business greatness is never achieved by one person alone. 我的商业模式就是披头士……他们相互平衡，整体大于部分之和。商业上的伟大从来不是一个人完成的。

引发存储股暴跌的 Google 论文遭学术不端指控

Google Research's recently published AI compression algorithm paper, TurboQuant, has faced accusations of academic misconduct. Within 24 hours of its release, the paper garnered over ten million views, storage chip stocks collectively closed lower that day, and Cloudflare CEO Matthew Prince called it Google's 'DeepSeek moment.' Google Research 近日发布的 AI 压缩算法论文 TurboQuant 遭到学术不端指控。该论文发布后 24 小时内浏览量破千万，存储芯片股当天集体收跌，Cloudflare CEO Matthew Prince 称其为 Google 的「DeepSeek 时刻」。

Gao Jianyang, a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zurich and the first author of the vector quantization algorithm RaBitQ, recently issued an open clarification letter on Zhihu, pointing out three serious issues with TurboQuant: 苏黎世联邦理工学院博士后、向量量化算法 RaBitQ 的第一作者高健扬近日在知乎发出公开澄清信，指出 TurboQuant 存在三处严重问题：

  • Deliberately avoiding direct association with RaBitQ in its core method (random rotation transformation); 刻意回避与 RaBitQ 在核心方法（随机旋转变换）上的直接关联；
  • Characterizing RaBitQ's theoretical achievements as 'suboptimal' without any evidence; 在无任何证据的情况下将 RaBitQ 的理论成果定性为「次优」；
  • And in experimental comparisons, running RaBitQ on a single-core CPU while running TurboQuant on an A100 GPU, with neither point disclosed in the paper. 以及在实验对比中用单核 CPU 跑 RaBitQ、却用 A100 GPU 跑 TurboQuant，且两点均未在论文中披露。

More critically, the aforementioned issues were explicitly communicated to the TurboQuant team via email before the paper's submission, and the team acknowledged awareness but chose not to make corrections. 更关键的是，上述问题在论文投稿前已通过邮件明确告知 TurboQuant 团队，对方表示知情，却选择不予修正。

Currently, TurboQuant's first author, Amir Zandieh, has only committed to correcting two issues: experimental and theoretical descriptions, refusing to directly discuss the methodological similarities between the two papers, and stating that corrections will only be made after the ICLR 2026 official conference concludes. 目前，TurboQuant 第一作者 Amir Zandieh 仅承诺修正实验与理论描述两处问题，拒绝正面讨论两篇论文的方法相似性，且表示要等 ICLR 2026 正式会议结束后才做修正。

Gao Jianyang's team has published public comments on ICLR OpenReview, submitted a formal complaint to relevant ICLR officials with a complete email evidence package, and stated that they will subsequently publish a detailed technical report on arXiv. 高健扬团队已在 ICLR OpenReview 发布公开评论，并向 ICLR 相关负责人提交正式投诉，附完整邮件证据包，还表示后续将在 arXiv 发布详细技术报告。

DNG 正式成为相机 RAW 格式国际标准

According to PetaPixel, the RAW image format DNG (Digital Negative) officially received certification from the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) last week, becoming the official international standard for RAW images, with standard number ISO 12234-4:2026. 据 PetaPixel 报道，RAW 图像格式 DNG（数字负片，Digital Negative）上周正式获得国际标准化组织（ISO）认证，成为官方 RAW 图像国际标准，标准编号为 ISO 12234-4:2026。

DNG was introduced by Adobe in 2004, and its core advantage lies in its openness. Currently, brands such as Leica, Ricoh, Sigma, and Pentax support direct shooting of DNG format files in their cameras. DNG 由 Adobe 于 2004 年推出，其核心优势在于开放性。目前，徕卡、理光、适马、宾得等品牌已在旗下相机中支持直接拍摄 DNG 格式文件。

Unlike proprietary RAW formats from various manufacturers such as Sony .ARW, Nikon .NEF, Canon .CR2, and Fujifilm .RAF, DNG has complete public technical documentation, and its compatibility and long-term archiving capabilities far exceed those of private formats. 与索尼 .ARW、尼康 .NEF、佳能 .CR2、富士 .RAF 等各厂商专有 RAW 格式不同，DNG 拥有完整公开的技术文档，兼容性与长期存档能力都远超私有格式。

The ISO 12234-4:2026 standard document clearly defines the DNG format specifications and implementation paths, providing clear guidance for various hardware and software manufacturers. ISO 12234-4:2026 标准文件明确定义了 DNG 的格式规范与实现路径，为各硬件及软件厂商提供了清晰的落地指引。

According to the introduction, the system adopts a range-extended hybrid solution: a gas turbine drives a generator to charge the battery, while the aircraft's flight thrust is entirely provided by electric ducted fans. 据介绍，该系统采用增程式混动方案：燃气涡轮机驱动发电机为电池充电，飞机的飞行推力则完全由电动涵道风扇提供。
