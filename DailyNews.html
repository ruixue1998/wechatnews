iOS 26.4 RC 修复键盘漏字 bug

Yesterday, Apple pushed iOS 26.4 RC (Release Candidate) to developers, with the official version expected to be released next week. The update log states that this version has "improved keyboard accuracy when typing quickly," fixing the issue of missing characters during keyboard input that has plagued many users since the release of iOS 26. 昨天，苹果向开发者推送了 iOS 26.4 RC（发布候选版），正式版预计下周推送。更新日志中称该版本已「改善了快速打字时的键盘准确性」，修复了自 iOS 26 发布以来困扰大量用户的键盘输入漏字问题。

Since upgrading to iOS 26, some users of iPhone 17 and other models have reported that the system inexplicably drops some characters when typing quickly. Although visual feedback on the screen shows that the user did touch the corresponding key, the character was not actually entered successfully, affecting the typing experience. 自升级 iOS 26 系统后，部分 iPhone 17 等机型用户反映，在快速打字时系统会无故漏掉部分字符。尽管屏幕上的视觉反馈显示用户确实触控了对应按键，但实际上字符并没有成功输入，影响输入体验。

Multiple users in the comment section confirmed the prevalence of the above issue. Some users stated that the problem has persisted for several months, mainly concentrated in two scenarios: the spacebar and selecting suggested words; other users even thought their typing habits had deteriorated until they confirmed it was a system-level bug, which brought them relief. 多位用户在评论区印证了上述问题的普遍性。有用户表示该问题已持续数月，主要集中在空格键和选择联想词两个场景；也有用户称曾一度以为是自身打字习惯变差，直到确认这是系统层面的 bug 才释怀。

深圳 17 岁高中生回应成 Kimi 论文第一作者

According to a report by "Yangtze Evening Post", the Kimi team under Moonshot AI recently published a technical paper proposing a brand new "Attention Residuals" mechanism, which fundamentally reconstructs the traditional residual connection architecture that has been used in the field of deep learning for nearly a decade. 据《扬子晚报》报道，月之暗面（Moonshot AI）旗下 Kimi 团队近日发布一篇技术论文，提出全新的「注意力残差」（Attention Residuals）机制，对深度学习领域沿用近十年的传统残差连接架构进行了颠覆性重构。

Guangyu Chen (陈广宇), who topped the author list, quickly garnered widespread attention—he is a high school senior from an international school in Shenzhen, Guangdong, only 17 years old this year, and is expected to graduate in June. 排在作者名单首位的 Guangyu Chen（陈广宇）迅速引发广泛关注——他是来自广东深圳一所国际学校的高三在读生，今年仅 17 岁，预计今年 6 月毕业。

Facing the ensuing widespread attention, Chen Guangyu specifically named three equally contributing authors and team colleagues responsible for model expansion and infrastructure construction in his WeChat Moments post, stating that this work was "indispensable." 面对随之而来的大量关注，陈广宇在朋友圈发文时特意点名三位同等贡献作者，以及负责模型扩展与基础设施搭建的团队同事，称这项工作「缺一不可」。

He repeatedly emphasized in interviews: "Don't create a god; I hope the outside world writes less about individuals and more about the technology and the team. This is a collective achievement." Kimi's official statement also echoed this, saying that the paper is the result of the entire team's collaborative research. 他在接受采访时多次强调：「不要造神，希望外界少写个人、多写技术和团队，这是大家共同完成的成果。」Kimi 官方也同样表态，这篇论文是整个团队协作的研究成果。

OpenAI 收购 Python 工具公司 Astral

OpenAI announced yesterday that it will acquire Astral, an open-source Python development tools company, aiming to integrate Astral's toolchain into OpenAI's Codex programming agent ecosystem. OpenAI 昨天宣布将收购开源 Python 开发工具公司 Astral，旨在将 Astral 旗下的工具链整合进 OpenAI 的 Codex 编程智能体生态。

Astral owns three widely used open-source tools in the Python developer community: uv for dependency and virtual environment management, Ruff as the fastest code checker and formatter currently available, and ty, which focuses on type safety checking. Astral 旗下拥有三款在 Python 开发者社区中广泛使用的开源工具：uv 负责依赖管理与虚拟环境管理，Ruff 是目前速度最快的代码检查与格式化工具，ty 则专注于类型安全检查。

Thibault Sottiaux, head of OpenAI Codex, stated: "Astral's tools are used by millions of Python developers. Bringing their expertise and ecosystem into OpenAI will accelerate our vision for Codex: to make it the most capable agent for the entire software development lifecycle." OpenAI Codex 负责人 Thibault Sottiaux 表示：「Astral 的工具被数百万 Python 开发者使用。将他们的专业能力和生态系统引入 OpenAI，将加速我们对 Codex 的愿景：让其成为最能胜任整个软件开发生命周期的智能体。」

OpenAI also pledged to continue supporting Astral's open-source projects after the acquisition. OpenAI 同时承诺，交易完成后将继续支持 Astral 的开源项目。

AI 冲击就业，汇丰拟裁员 2 万人

According to Bloomberg, HSBC Holdings is evaluating a three-to-five-year medium-term redundancy plan, with potential job cuts of approximately 20,000 positions, accounting for 10% of its global workforce of about 210,000 employees. 据彭博社报道，汇丰控股正在评估一项为期三至五年的中期裁员计划，潜在裁员规模约达 2 万个职位，占其全球约 21 万名员工总数的 10%。

The core driver of this redundancy plan is CEO Georges Elhedery's strategic bet on AI technology – to massively compress the size of middle and back-office operations through AI. 此次裁员计划的核心驱动力，是 CEO Georges Elhedery 对 AI 技术的战略押注——通过 AI 大规模压缩中后台规模。

According to informed sources, the most affected positions are expected to be middle and back-office roles within global service centers that do not directly face clients. Some job reductions may be achieved by not filling vacancies, selling businesses, or exiting certain markets, rather than solely through direct layoffs. Following the announcement, HSBC's share price briefly fell by 2.3% in the Hong Kong market yesterday. 据知情人士透露，受影响最大的预计是全球服务中心内不直接面向客户的中后台岗位。部分职位削减可能通过不再填补空缺、出售业务或退出部分市场来实现，而非单纯的直接裁员。消息公布后，汇丰股价昨日在香港市场一度下跌 2.3%。

It is worth noting that since Elhedery took office as CEO in 2024, he has implemented a series of drastic restructuring measures at HSBC, cumulatively cutting thousands of jobs, while also selling, merging, or closing some businesses. 值得注意的是，Elhedery 自 2024 年出任 CEO 以来，已对汇丰实施了一系列大刀阔斧的重组措施，累计裁减数千个职位，同时出售、合并或关闭部分业务。
