OpenAI 与 Broadcom 发布首款 Jalapeño 推理芯片

Yesterday, OpenAI and Broadcom jointly released the first-ever chip, "Jalapeño" (Mexican Pepper), designed for large language model inference tasks. OpenAI 昨日与 Broadcom 联合发布史上首颗芯片「Jalapeño」 (墨西哥辣椒)，面向大语言模型推理任务设计。

OpenAI stated that Jalapeño is the first generation of AI accelerators for their multi-generational computing platform. The chip architecture was designed from scratch by OpenAI based on their model roadmap, inference kernels, service systems, and product requirements, with Broadcom responsible for silicon implementation, networking, and connectivity technology, and Celestica participating in board, rack, and system integration. OpenAI 称，Jalapeño 是双方多代计算平台的第一代 AI 加速器。芯片架构由 OpenAI 基于模型路线图、推理 kernel、服务系统和产品需求从头设计，Broadcom 负责硅实现、网络和连接技术，Celestica 参与板卡、机柜和系统集成。

Key developments officially disclosed include: 官方披露的关键进展包括：

  • Engineering samples have been running machine learning tasks in the lab at production target frequencies and power consumption, including GPT-5.3-Codex-Spark. 工程样片已在实验室以生产目标频率和功耗运行机器学习任务，包括 GPT-5.3-Codex-Spark。
  • Early tests show that Jalapeño's performance per watt is higher than current state-of-the-art levels, and a full performance technical report will be released in the coming months. 早期测试显示，Jalapeño 的每瓦性能高于当前最先进水平，完整性能技术报告将在未来数月发布。
  • The chip took only 9 months from initial design to manufacturing tape-out , which OpenAI says is one of the fastest ASIC development cycles in high-performance advanced semiconductors. 芯片从初始设计到制造流片仅用时 9 个月 ，OpenAI 称这是高性能先进半导体中最快的 ASIC 开发周期之一。
  • The platform is planned to begin deployment by the end of 2026 and expand to multiple generations of products in subsequent years; Broadcom CEO Hock Tan stated that the platform will support the construction of gigawatt-scale data centers with data center partners such as Microsoft. 平台计划于 2026 年底开始部署，并在后续年份扩展到多代产品；Broadcom CEO Hock Tan 表示，该平台将支持与 Microsoft 等数据中心伙伴建设吉瓦级数据中心。

Omdia：今年全球智能手机平均售价预计升至 3800 元

Market research firm Omdia recently published a blog post, showing that the global smartphone market is undergoing a structural change this year with "falling volume and rising prices." Global smartphone shipments are expected to decline by 12.2% year-on-year to 1.093 billion units this year, a decrease of 152 million units from last year; however, the total market value is expected to grow by 6.1% year-on-year during the same period. 市场调研机构 Omdia 日前发布博文，数据显示，全球智能手机市场今年正出现「量跌价升」的结构性变化、今年全球智能手机出货量将同比下降 12.2% 至 10.93 亿部，较去年减少 1.52 亿部；但同期市场总价值预计同比增长 6.1%。

Omdia predicts that the average selling price of global smartphones will rise from $467 last year to $565 this year (approximately RMB 3842), an increase of $98, or about 21%, with both the price level and increase being historical highs for the industry. Omdia 预计，全球智能手机平均售价将从去年的 467 美元升至今年的 565 美元（约合人民币 3842 元），增加 98 美元，涨幅约 21%，售价水平及涨幅均为行业历史新高。

The main reason for the price increase comes from supply chain cost pressures. Omdia stated that the average prices of DRAM and NAND flash memory increased by over 80% quarter-on-quarter in the first quarter of this year, and continued to rise in the second quarter. 价格上涨的主要原因来自供应链成本压力。Omdia 表示，今年一季度 DRAM 和 NAND 闪存平均价格环比上涨超过 80%，二季度仍在继续上行。

Omdia also expects the global smartphone market contraction to continue until next year, but the decline in shipments will narrow to 0.9%. As manufacturing costs for smartphones under $100 are still difficult to significantly reduce, a substantial recovery in industry shipments is not expected until 2028. Omdia 还预计，全球智能手机市场收缩将延续至明年，但出货量降幅将收窄至 0.9%。由于 100 美元以下智能手机的制造成本仍难以显著下降，行业出货量的实质性恢复预计要到 2028 年才会开始。

追觅俞浩：不会主动压低价格、希望把每个员工都培养成乔布斯

Sina Tech reported that Yu Hao, founder of Dreame, stated yesterday at the 2026 Dalian Summer Davos Forum, when discussing the company's operating methods, that Dreame adheres to a high-end strategy. As long as they produce high-quality products, they dare to set high prices benchmarked against industry-leading standards. 新浪科技报道，追觅创始人俞浩昨日在 2026 大连夏季达沃斯论坛上谈到公司经营方法时表示，追觅坚持高端化路线，只要做出优质产品，就敢于对标行业顶尖水准制定高价。

We will never be a brand that actively lowers prices and disrupts the industry's pricing system. 我们绝不做行业里主动压低价格、破坏价格体系的品牌。

He emphasized that the high-end strategy has allowed Dreame to open up all online and offline channels and simultaneously push the company to expand into global markets. 他强调，高端化路线让追觅得以打通线上、线下全渠道，并推动公司同步布局全球市场。

独立音乐平台 Jamendo 起诉英伟达用音乐数据训练音频模型

Luxembourg independent music platform Jamendo yesterday sued Nvidia in a U.S. federal court in California, accusing the latter of unauthorized use of tens of thousands of tracks to train its audio AI models, seeking at least 17.8 million euros in damages . 卢森堡独立音乐平台 Jamendo 昨日向美国加利福尼亚州联邦法院起诉英伟达，指控后者未经授权使用数万首曲目训练音频 AI 模型，诉求包含 至少 1780 万欧元赔偿 。

The lawsuit alleges that Nvidia used the MTG-Jamendo dataset in the development of audio models such as Fugatto and Audio Flamingo. 诉状称，英伟达在 Fugatto、Audio Flamingo 等音频模型研发中使用了 MTG-Jamendo 数据集。

The dataset was jointly built by Jamendo and the Music Technology Group of Pompeu Fabra University in Barcelona, Spain. It contains approximately 55,600 tracks and metadata, and its external license is limited to non-commercial research and academic use. 该数据集由 Jamendo 与西班牙巴塞罗那庞培法布拉大学音乐技术研究组联合搭建，包含约 5.56 万首曲目和元数据，对外许可仅限非商用科研和学术用途。

Jamendo stated that Nvidia's public papers have listed MTG-Jamendo as one of the training datasets, but no commercial authorization has been reached between the two parties. Jamendo 表示，英伟达公开论文已经把 MTG-Jamendo 列为训练数据集之一，但双方没有达成商业授权。
