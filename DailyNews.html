SpaceX 正式启动 IPO，估值 1.25 万亿美元，或成史上最大规模上市

According to The New York Times, today, SpaceX publicly submitted its IPO application documents, disclosing the company's financial status for the first time. 据《纽约时报》报道，今天，SpaceX 公开递交 IPO 申请文件，首次披露公司财务状况。

SpaceX's revenue last year reached $18.67 billion, a year-on-year increase of 33%; however, it incurred a net loss of $4.9 billion, a significant reversal compared to a profit of $791 million in 2024. In the first quarter of this year, the company's revenue was $4.7 billion, with a net loss of approximately $4.3 billion. SpaceX 去年营收达 186.7 亿美元，同比增长 33%；但净亏损 49 亿美元，与 2024 年盈利 7.91 亿美元相比大幅逆转。今年一季度，公司营收为 47 亿美元，净亏损约 43 亿美元。

SpaceX plans to list on Nasdaq under the ticker symbol SPCX, aiming to begin trading as early as June. The company's self-valuation is $1.25 trillion, and it reportedly intends to raise $50 billion to $75 billion through this IPO, potentially surpassing Saudi Aramco's 2020 IPO record of $29.4 billion to become the largest IPO in history. SpaceX 计划在纳斯达克上市，股票代码为 SPCX，目标最早于 6 月挂牌交易。公司自我估值为 1.25 万亿美元，据报道拟通过此次 IPO 募资 500 亿至 750 亿美元，有望超过沙特阿美 2020 年 IPO 创下的 294 亿美元纪录，成为史上规模最大的 IPO。

Regarding the equity structure, SpaceX will issue two classes of common stock, Class A and Class B, with Class B shares having 10 votes per share. According to S-1 documents, Musk holds over 50% of the common stock and controls 85.1% of the voting rights through super-voting shares. Based on current valuations, his stake is worth over $635 billion. 在股权结构方面，SpaceX 将发行 A 类和 B 类两类普通股，B 类股每股拥有 10 票投票权。据 S-1 文件，马斯克持有超过 50% 的普通股，并通过超级投票股控制 85.1% 的投票权。按当前估值计算，其持股价值逾 6350 亿美元。

Notably, the IPO filing also disclosed that Anthropic has agreed to pay SpaceX $1.25 billion per month for cloud computing infrastructure fees, with the contract lasting until May 2029, equivalent to approximately $15 billion annually. The two parties announced their collaboration earlier this month. 值得注意的是，IPO 文件还披露，Anthropic 已同意向 SpaceX 支付每月 12.5 亿美元的云计算基础设施费用，合约持续至 2029 年 5 月，相当于每年约 150 亿美元，双方于本月早些时候宣布合作。

Bloomberg also reported that, according to sources, SpaceX is expected to proceed with the acquisition of AI programming startup Cursor 30 days after initiating public trading. SpaceX's first day of public trading is anticipated to be June 12. 另据彭博社报道，知情人士称 SpaceX 预计将在启动公开交易 30 天后推进对 AI 编程创业公司 Cursor 的收购。SpaceX 的公开交易首日预计安排在 6 月 12 日。

Bloomberg previously reported that if the deal fails to close, SpaceX will pay Cursor a cash breakup fee of up to $10 billion. 据彭博社此前报道，如果交易未能完成，SpaceX 将向 Cursor 支付高达 100 亿美元现金分手费。

录音曝光：扎克伯格称用员工键鼠操作数据训练 AI，因为「外包不够聪明」

American labor media More Perfect Union yesterday exposed an internal recording in which Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg admitted at an all-hands meeting on April 30 that the company has implemented an internal tracking project called the 'Model Capability Program,' which uses employees' daily operational data, recorded through mouse tracks, keyboard presses, and screenshots, for AI model training. 美国劳工媒体 More Perfect Union 昨天曝光了一条内部录音，内容是 Meta CEO 马克 · 扎克伯格于 4 月 30 日全员会议上承认，公司已推行名为「模型能力计划」的内部追踪项目，通过记录员工的鼠标轨迹、键盘按键及屏幕截图，将其日常操作数据用于 AI 模型训练。

Zuckerberg's rationale for this is that the average intelligence level of Meta employees is significantly higher than that of outsourced personnel typically used for training data collection. The programming behaviors and computer operating habits of thousands of elite engineers constitute high-quality training material that competitors cannot replicate, which can help Meta's models widen the gap with peers in coding capabilities. 对此，扎克伯格给出的理由是：Meta 员工的平均智力水平远高于通常用于训练数据采集的外包人员，数千名精英工程师的编程行为和电脑操作习惯，是竞争对手无法复制的高质量训练素材，可帮助 Meta 模型在代码能力上拉开与同行的差距。

Addressing privacy concerns, he assured that the data would not be manually reviewed or used for performance evaluations, and emphasized that the project must remain confidential externally due to intense AI competition. 针对隐私质疑，他保证数据不会经人工审查、不用于绩效考核，并以 AI 竞争激烈为由强调项目须对外保密。

This disclosure occurred around the time Meta announced layoffs of approximately 8,000 people. Some employees expressed resistance to being asked to contribute operational data to train AI systems that might eventually replace them. Zuckerberg stated that if the project proves effective, it will be rolled out company-wide. 这一披露发生在 Meta 宣布裁员约 8000 人的前后。部分员工对被要求贡献操作数据、进而训练可能取代自身的 AI 系统表示抵触。扎克伯格表示，若项目证明有效，将在全公司范围内推广。

英伟达一季度营收 816 亿美元，数据中心营收暴增 92%

Nvidia yesterday released its first-quarter fiscal year 2027 results for the period ending April 26, with quarterly revenue reaching $81.6 billion, a 20% increase quarter-over-quarter and an 85% increase year-over-year. Key highlights include: 英伟达昨日发布截至 4 月 26 日的 2027 财年第一季度业绩，季度营收达到 816 亿美元，环比增长 20%，同比增长 85%。其中：

  • Data Center revenue was $75.2 billion, up 92% year-over-year and 21% quarter-over-quarter, marking an all-time high; within this, Compute revenue was $60.4 billion, up 77% year-over-year; Networking revenue was $14.8 billion, up 199% year-over-year; 数据中心营收 752 亿美元，同比增长 92%，环比增长 21%，为历史最高纪录；其中计算营收 604 亿美元，同比增长 77%；网络营收 148 亿美元，同比增长 199%；
  • Edge Computing revenue was $6.4 billion, up 29% year-over-year and 10% quarter-over-quarter; 边缘计算营收 64 亿美元，同比增长 29%，环比增长 10%；
  • GAAP gross margin was 74.9%, and non-GAAP gross margin was 75.0%, both significantly higher than 60.5% / 60.8% in the same period last year; GAAP 毛利率为 74.9%，non-GAAP 毛利率为 75.0%，较去年同期的 60.5% / 60.8% 均有显著提升；
  • GAAP diluted earnings per share were $2.39, up 214% year-over-year; non-GAAP diluted earnings per share were $1.87, up 140% year-over-year; GAAP 稀释每股收益 2.39 美元，同比增长 214%；non-GAAP 稀释每股收益 1.87 美元，同比增长 140%；
  • Net cash flow from operating activities was $50.344 billion; free cash flow was $48.554 billion. 经营活动产生的现金流量净额为 503.44 亿美元；自由现金流为 485.54 亿美元。

Looking ahead to the next quarter, Nvidia projects second-quarter fiscal year 2027 revenue of approximately $91 billion (plus or minus 2%), with the company explicitly stating that this guidance does not include any data center computing revenue from China. GAAP and non-GAAP gross margins are expected to be 74.9% and 75.0%, respectively. 展望下一季度，英伟达预计 2027 财年第二季度营收约 910 亿美元（上下浮动 2%），公司明确表示该指引未纳入任何来自中国的数据中心计算营收。GAAP 与 non-GAAP 毛利率预计分别为 74.9% 与 75.0%。

IEA：今年全球电动车销量将达 2300 万辆

According to Electrek, the International Energy Agency (IEA) predicts in its new Global EV Outlook that global electric vehicle sales will reach 23 million units in 2026, accounting for nearly 30% of global new car sales. 据 Electrek 报道，国际能源署在新版 Global EV Outlook 中预计，2026 年全球电动汽车销量将达到 2300 万辆，约占全球新车销量近 30%。

The report shows that global EV sales in 2025 have exceeded 20 million units, a year-on-year increase of 20%, with 1 out of every 4 new cars sold being an EV. The IEA's forecast indicates that despite a slowdown in some markets, global electric mobility consumption continues to expand. 报告显示，2025 年全球电动车销量已超过 2000 万辆，同比增长 20%，新车销售中每 4 辆就有 1 辆为电动车。IEA 的预测说明，尽管部分市场增速放缓，全球电动出行消费仍保持扩张。

Electrek reported that the growth of the global EV market is jointly driven by China, Europe, and other regional markets. Electrek 报道称，全球电动车市场的增长来自中国、欧洲和其他区域市场共同推动。
