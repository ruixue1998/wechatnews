曝 DeepSeek 首次启动融资，估值不低于百亿美元

According to The Information, DeepSeek is seeking external funding for the first time, planning to raise at least $300 million at a valuation exceeding $10 billion. 据 The Information 报道，DeepSeek 正首次寻求外部融资，计划以超 100 亿美元的估值，筹集至少 3 亿美元资金。

Insiders revealed that DeepSeek is engaging with investors to bolster its capital reserves. It is understood that DeepSeek was previously exclusively funded by hedge fund High-Flyer Quant and had repeatedly rejected investment offers from top venture capitalists and tech giants. 知情人士透露，DeepSeek 正与投资者接触以充实资金储备。据了解，DeepSeek 此前由对冲基金幻方量化独家提供资金支持，并曾多次拒绝顶级风投及科技巨头的投资报价。

The report points out that founder and CEO Liang Wenfeng's change in funding strategy this time is primarily aimed at addressing the high costs of model development, by expanding computing power investment and improving compensation to cope with fierce talent and technology competition. 报道指出，创始人兼 CEO 梁文锋此次改变资金策略，主要目的是为了应对高昂的模型开发成本，通过扩大算力投资和提高薪酬待遇来应对激烈的人才与技术竞争。

According to reports, DeepSeek has not yet launched a new generation of models since the release of its R1 model in early 2025, and has recently faced a loss of core researchers. Luo Fuli, a core contributor to the V3 model, has joined Xiaomi, while another core researcher, Guo Daya, has moved to ByteDance with a high salary. 据报道，自 2025 年初发布 R1 模型以来，DeepSeek 尚未推出新一代模型，且近期面临核心研究人员流失。V3 模型核心贡献者罗福莉已加盟小米，另一核心研究员郭达雅则高薪转投字节跳动。

Furthermore, the V4 model, originally scheduled for release in February this year, has been repeatedly delayed due to engineering and technical challenges, such as the need for out-of-the-box adaptation for Huawei chips. 此外，原定于今年 2 月发布的 V4 模型，因需进行华为芯片的开箱即用适配等工程技术难题，已数度推迟发布时间。

Alongside executive personnel changes, OpenAI is undergoing intensive internal structural adjustments. 一季度中国手机市场：华为苹果增长，小米跌幅最大

Yesterday, market research firm Counterpoint Research released its latest research report: 昨天，市场研究机构 Counterpoint Research 发布最新研究报告：

In Q1 2026, China's smartphone market shipments decreased by 4% year-on-year, with Huawei reclaiming the top spot with a 20% market share, while Apple became the fastest-growing leading brand with a 20% year-on-year increase. 2026 年第一季度中国智能手机市场出货量同比下降 4%，其中华为凭借 20% 的市场份额重返第一，苹果则以 20% 的同比增幅成为增速最快的头部品牌。

Specifically: 具体来看：

  • Huawei achieved a 2% year-on-year growth in Q1, with a market share of 20%, marking a new single-quarter high since Q4 2020. The report points out that the stable supply of Mate 80 and Enjoy 90 series, along with cost buffering from the local supply chain, were key factors supporting its ascent to the top; 华为在一季度实现 2% 的同比增长，市占率达 20%，创下自 2020 年第四季度以来的单季份额新高。报告指出，Mate 80 及畅享 90 系列的稳定供应，以及本土供应链带来的成本缓冲，是支撑其登顶的关键；
  • Apple ranked second, with Q1 shipments increasing by 20% year-on-year, driven by the continued strong sales of the iPhone 17 series and subsidy promotions, making it the fastest-growing brand among the top six manufacturers; 苹果位居第二，受 iPhone 17 系列持续热销及补贴促销拉动，一季度出货量同比增长 20%，为前六大厂商中增速最快的品牌；
  • Xiaomi, on the other hand, saw its shipments decline by 35% year-on-year in the first quarter, ranking fifth, due to core models performing below expectations and a conservative pricing strategy. 小米则因核心机型表现不及预期及定价策略偏向保守，一季度出货量同比下滑 35%，位居第五。

Ivan Lam, Senior Analyst at Counterpoint, pointed out that soaring component costs weakened the pull of Lunar New Year promotions and pushed up retail prices, but the high-end market remains resilient, driven by imaging, foldable screens, and AI agent functionalities. Counterpoint 高级分析师 Ivan Lam 指出，零部件成本飙升削弱了春节促销的拉动作用并推高了零售价，但高端市场在影像、折叠屏及 AI 代理功能的推动下仍具韧性。

It is worth noting that analytical agencies expect high storage costs to persist throughout 2026, leading to a "double blow" for terminal manufacturers, facing shrinking shipments and thinning profit margins. 值得关注的是，分析机构预计，存储成本的高企状态将贯穿 2026 全年，导致终端厂商面临出货量萎缩与利润率变薄的「双重打击」。

OpenAI 豪掷 200 亿，只为投资英伟达「备胎」

According to The Information, OpenAI has agreed to pay AI chip manufacturer Cerebras over $20 billion (approximately 136.4 billion RMB) over the next three years to purchase computing power services, in exchange for a partial equity stake in the latter. 据 The Information 报道， OpenAI 已同意在未来三年内向 AI 芯片制造商 Cerebras 支付超 200 亿美元（约合人民币 1364 亿元）以购买算力服务，并以此换取后者的部分股权。

According to multiple sources, OpenAI's promised $20 billion expenditure in this agreement is double the previously rumored amount. 据多位知情人士透露，OpenAI 在本次协议中承诺的 200 亿美元支出是此前传闻金额的两倍。

In exchange, OpenAI will acquire a minority stake in Cerebras through warrants; if OpenAI's final expenditure reaches $30 billion, its equity stake could be as high as 10%. 作为交换，OpenAI 将通过认股权证获得 Cerebras 的少数股权；若 OpenAI 的最终支出规模达到 300 亿美元，其获取的股权比例最高可达 10%。

Additionally, OpenAI has agreed to provide Cerebras with approximately $1 billion in dedicated funding for the development of data centers to run OpenAI's artificial intelligence products. 此外，OpenAI 还同意向 Cerebras 提供约 10 亿美元的专项资金，用于开发运行 OpenAI 人工智能产品的数据中心。

In February this year, OpenAI released its first model designed specifically for real-time programming, GPT-5.3-Codex-Spark. This model is also the first outcome of OpenAI's collaboration with Cerebras. 今年 2 月，OpenAI 发布了首款专为实时编程设计的模型 GPT-5.3-Codex-Spark。而该模型也是 OpenAI 与 Cerebras 合作的首个成果。

It is reported that the GPT-5.3-Codex-Spark provided by OpenAI runs on Cerebras's Wafer Scale Engine 3. 据悉，OpenAI 所提供的 GPT-5.3-Codex-Spark 运行在 Cerebras 的 Wafer Scale Engine 3 上。

It is worth mentioning that the WSE-3 boasts 4 trillion transistors and 900,000 AI cores, which is 19 times more transistors and 28 times more computing power than Nvidia's B200; additionally, thanks to making the chip directly from an entire wafer, the WSE-3 has 206 times faster memory bandwidth than Nvidia's latest generation NVLink. 值得一提的是，WSE-3 拥有 4 万亿个晶体管 和 90 万个 AI 核心，比英伟达 B200 多了 19 倍晶体管与 28 倍的算力；另外得益于整块晶圆直接做芯片，WSE-3 拥有比英伟达最新一代 NVLink 快 206 倍的内存带宽。
