  • AI Large Models "Poisoned": AI 大模型遭「投毒」：

According to industry insiders, reporters searched and found a business called GEO on multiple online platforms. Service providers engaged in this business claim that users only need to pay a corresponding fee, and it can make customers' products famous in major AI large models; it can make customers' product advertisements the "standard answer" given by AI models. 按照业内人士爆料，记者在多个网络平台查询搜索到了一项名为 GEO 的业务，这些从事该项业务的服务商号称，用户只需支付相应的费用，它就能在各大主流 AI 大模型里，让客户的产品榜上有名；让客户的商品广告，成为 AI 模型给出的「标准答案」。

Mr. Li, the operator of the Liqing GEO system, told reporters that the main reason GEO business is popular is that it can help clients "feed" and "poison" AI large models to achieve their business goals. 力擎 GEO 系统的运营者李总告诉记者，GEO 业务受热捧的主要原因就是它能在 AI 大模型里帮客户喂料、投毒，实现客户的商业目的。

Mr. Li stated that to conduct GEO business, the key to manipulating AI large models is to "publish articles" on major internet accounts. "For example, for a mobile phone brand, there are only 5 positions, at most 10 positions. How do you manage so many phones? With hundreds of millions in advertising fees per year, spending a few million to 'poison' it should be fine!" 李总表示，想做 GEO 业务，操控 AI 大模型的关键节点，就是在各大互联网账号上「发稿」。「比如说手机品牌，就 5 个位置，最多 10 个位置，这么多手机怎么弄。一年可能上亿的广告费，花个几百万投点毒，总行吧！」

He told reporters that the popularity of GEO business has spawned many companies and platforms specializing in publishing services. They undertake various publishing tasks long-term, allowing AI large models to cite and crawl their content, becoming a crucial part of "hunting" AI large models and "poisoning" data. 他告诉记者，GEO 业务的火爆，催生出了不少专门从事发稿业务的公司和平台。他们长期承揽各种发稿业务，以便让 AI 大模型引用和抓取，成为围猎 AI 大模型、进行数据「投毒」的重要一环。

Meta 被曝将裁员 20%

According to Reuters, Meta is planning large-scale layoffs affecting approximately 20% of its employees to offset massive AI infrastructure costs and adapt to team efficiency improvements brought by AI tools. 据路透社报道，Meta 正计划进行波及约 20% 员工的大规模裁员，以对冲庞大的 AI 基础设施成本并适应 AI 工具带来的团队提效。

Sources revealed that Meta executives have issued directives to internal senior leadership, requesting them to begin planning layoff schemes, with the specific timing and final scale not yet fully determined. 知情人士透露，Meta 高管已向内部高级领导层下达指令，要求开始规划裁员方案，目前具体时间和最终规模尚未完全敲定。

Based on an employee base of approximately 79,000 as of the end of last year, this round of layoffs will affect over 15,000 people, making it the largest structural adjustment since the "Year of Efficiency" from late 2022 to early 2023 (which saw a total of 21,000 layoffs). 若按截至去年底约 7.9 万名的员工基数计算，此次裁员波及人数将超 1.5 万人，成为继 2022 年底至 2023 年初「效率之年」后（共计裁员 2.1 万人）最大规模的架构调整。

In response, Meta spokesperson Andy Stone stated that the relevant news constitutes "speculative reports based on theoretical discussions." 对此，Meta 发言人安迪·斯通（Andy Stone）回应称，相关消息属于「基于理论性探讨的猜测性报道」。

The report points out that the core motivation for this layoff plan lies in the shift of business focus and cost structure. 报道指出，此次裁员计划的核心动因在于业务重心与成本结构的转移。

Meta plans to invest $600 billion by 2028 to build data centers, and recently acquired AI social platform Moltbook and invested at least $2 billion to acquire AI startup Manus. Meta 计划到 2028 年斥资 6000 亿美元建设数据中心，近期还接连收购了 AI 社交平台 Moltbook 以及斥资至少 20 亿美元收购 AI 初创公司 Manus。

CEO Zuckerberg explicitly pointed out in January this year that the efficiency dividends brought by AI investment have begun to emerge. Some projects that previously required large teams can now be completed by a single top talent. CEO 扎克伯格在今年 1 月曾明确指出，AI 投资带来的效率红利已初步显现，部分以往需要大型团队运作的项目，现阶段仅需单名顶尖人才即可完成。

🦞 多家高校禁用 OpenClaw

Recently, the Network Security Threat and Vulnerability Information Sharing Platform of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (NVDB) monitored and found that OpenClaw, which is popular across the internet, poses high security risks under default or improper configurations, easily leading to security issues such as cyberattacks and information leakage. 近期，工业和信息化部网络安全威胁和漏洞信息共享平台（NVDB）监测发现，火遍全网的 OpenClaw 在默认或不当配置情况下存在较高安全风险，极易引发网络攻击、信息泄露等安全问题。

In response, several universities have issued warning notices to prevent OpenClaw-related risks, in order to effectively protect the personal information security, campus network security, and data asset security of all faculty, staff, and students: 对此，多家高校为切实保障全校师生的个人信息安全、校园网络安全及数据资产安全，已发布防范 OpenClaw 相关风险事项的预警通知：

  • Anhui Normal University: All units, faculty, and staff within the university are strictly prohibited from using this tool in work scenarios involving teaching and research data, administrative office information, student information, etc., to prevent campus work data leakage, system attacks, and other issues, thereby safeguarding the bottom line of campus data security; 安徽师范大学：校内各单位、教职工严禁在处理教学科研数据、行政办公信息、学生信息等工作场景中使用该工具，杜绝校园工作数据泄露、系统受攻击等问题，守住校园数据安全底线；
  • Peking University: When deploying OpenClaw on servers or personal computers, it is imperative to ensure that the service is not exposed to the campus network or public internet. The computing center will regularly scan for open OpenClaw-related ports within the campus network and will notify relevant units for rectification if unhardened instances are found; 北京大学：在服务器或个人电脑上部署 OpenClaw，务必确认服务未暴露至校园网或公网。计算中心将定期扫描校园网内开放的 OpenClaw 相关端口，发现未加固实例将通知相关单位整改；
  • Guangdong Pharmaceutical University: Faculty and students who have deployed and are using OpenClaw should close unnecessary port mapping and public network access, set up file and HTTP access whitelists, and clearly stipulate that models must not treat external content as executable commands. Regularly check devices for abnormal processes, unfamiliar network connections, etc., promptly change relevant account passwords, and eliminate security risks; 广东药科大学：已部署使用 OpenClaw 的师生，关闭不必要的端口映射与公网访问，设置文件与 Http 访问白名单，明确约束模型不得将外部内容视为可执行指令。时常检查设备是否存在异常进程、陌生网络连接等，及时更改相关账号密码，消除安全隐患；
  • South China Normal University: Strictly prohibited from installing in production environments and office computers, including the school's office computers, servers, smart terminals, and other production equipment; OpenClaw must absolutely not be installed. 胖东来回应鸡蛋角黄素超标

According to Elephant News, blogger "Wang Hai Evaluation" recently posted a video claiming that eggs from Pang Dong Lai supermarket tested positive for an artificial pigment called canthaxanthin (also known as capsanthin), and its content exceeded the maximum allowed addition of canthaxanthin in poultry feed as stipulated by the "Safety Use Specification for Feed Additives" issued by the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs. 据大象新闻报道，近期博主「王海测评」发视频称，胖东来超市的鸡蛋检测出一种名为角黄素（斑蝥黄）的人工色素，并且含量超出农业农村部发布的《饲料添加剂安全使用规范》蛋禽类饲料中斑蝥黄允许的最大添加量。

In response to the above situation, a Pang Dong Lai staff member stated, "All raw egg products sold in our store are legally purchased through regular channels, with complete procedures, and have undergone corresponding inspection processes upon arrival. We have not yet received any notice (of delisting)." 针对上述情况，胖东来工作人员表示，「我们卖场售卖的生蛋产品，均通过正规的途径合法采购，手续都是齐全的，也得到了相应的进货查验的过程，暂时没有接到（下架）的通知。」

If the final investigation results show that the fresh eggs have problems, they will immediately disclose the results and initiate a delisting and recall procedure, bearing corresponding legal responsibilities according to law. 胖东来方面发文回应表示，目前市场监管部门的相关调查以及第三方复检还未出结果，如果最终的调查结果显示鲜鸡蛋存在问题，会第一时间公示结果，并立即启动下架召回程序，依法承担相应法律责任。
