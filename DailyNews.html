大疆影石专利战火烧回国内，双方互相起诉多项核心专利侵权

According to Yicai, DJI has filed patent infringement lawsuits against Insta360's Luna series in people's courts across multiple locations in China. Among them, a case accepted by the Shenzhen Intermediate People's Court in Guangdong Province will be heard on August 3. 据第一财经报道，大疆已在中国多地人民法院对影石 Luna 系列提起专利侵权诉讼。其中，广东省深圳市中级人民法院受理的案件将于 8 月 3 日开庭。

In the relevant lawsuits, DJI alleges that Insta360's Luna series infringes upon several of its invention patents, involving handheld gimbals and their control methods, shooting methods and storage media based on handheld gimbals, detachable control devices, gimbal devices, and their control solutions, among others. 大疆在相关诉讼中指控影石 Luna 系列涉嫌侵犯其多项发明专利，涉及手持云台及其控制方法、基于手持云台的拍摄方法和存储介质、可拆卸控制装置、云台装置及其控制方案等。

Insta360 subsequently responded yesterday and filed 6 patent infringement counter-lawsuits against DJI in multiple Chinese cities. 影石随后在昨日对此作出回应，并在中国多个城市对大疆提起 6 起专利侵权反诉。

Insta360 accuses DJI's main products, such as panoramic cameras and gimbal equipment, of infringing upon several of its patents. The technologies involved include panoramic shooting and editing, bullet-time shooting, camera thermal management solutions, camera expansion solutions, and gimbal support technology. On the same day, Insta360 also submitted requests to the China National Intellectual Property Administration for invalidation of DJI's relevant patents. 影石指控大疆的全景相机及云台设备等主力产品侵犯其多项专利，涉及技术包括全景拍摄与剪辑、子弹时间拍摄、相机热管理方案、相机扩展方案以及云台支撑技术等。同日，影石还向中国国家知识产权局提交了针对大疆相关专利的无效宣告请求。

This domestic lawsuit emerged after the withdrawal of both parties' US cases. On June 28, US local time, both DJI and Insta360 submitted withdrawal applications to the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Texas, withdrawing their previously filed patent infringement lawsuits and related counter-lawsuits, respectively. 这起国内诉讼出现在双方美国案件撤回之后。美国当地时间 6 月 28 日，大疆与影石均向美国德克萨斯州东区联邦地区法院提交撤案申请，分别撤回此前提起的专利侵权诉讼及相关反诉。

理想汽车调整组织架构，产品定义职能回归研发部门

According to a report by "National Business Daily," Li Auto recently carried out a new round of organizational restructuring, planning to split some key functions of the product department and integrate them into the R&D department. The core of this adjustment is to "return product definition to R&D," aiming to optimize the product decision-making process and shorten the cycle from definition to implementation. 据《每日经济新闻》报道，理想汽车近期对组织架构进行了新一轮调整，计划将产品部的部分关键职能拆分并整合至研发部门。此次调整的核心在于让「产品定义回归研发」，旨在优化产品决策流程，缩短从定义到落地的周期。

Insiders revealed that this change primarily involves two core product definition teams: electric vehicle body and autonomous driving. 知情人士透露，此次变动主要涉及电动本体和自动驾驶两大核心产品定义团队。

  • Specifically, the product department's electric vehicle body definition team has been merged into the vehicle R&D department, which is led by Liu Liguo, Senior Vice President of Vehicle Electric R&D 其中，产品部的电动本体定义团队已并入由整车电动研发高级副总裁刘立国负责的整车研发部门；
  • The autonomous driving terminal product team has been merged into the base model R&D team, led by Zhan Kun, head of base models. 自动驾驶终端产品团队则并入由基座模型负责人詹锟领导的基座模型研发团队。

After the adjustment, Li Auto's decision-making departments will be reduced from three to two, namely the product line and R&D department, with the intermediate platform product definition stage being omitted. By connecting product definition and R&D, the company hopes to ensure the feasibility of technical solutions and establish a two-way communication and feedback mechanism to enhance the product definition's dynamic adaptability to market changes. 调整完成后，理想汽车的决策部门将由三个缩减为两个，即产品线和研发部门，中间的平台产品定义环节将被省略。通过打通产品定义与研发之间的衔接，公司希望确保技术方案的可行性，并建立双向沟通反馈机制，以提升产品定义对市场变化的动态适应能力。

阿里整合三大 AI 智能体产品，92 年钉钉 CEO 陈宇森统领

Alibaba confirmed yesterday that it is strategically integrating three of its enterprise-grade AI agent products. This adjustment is based on the desktop AI agent tool QoderWork, deeply integrating the core capabilities of "Wukong," an enterprise collaborative office AI platform incubated by DingTalk, and MuleRun, an AI execution engine internally incubated by Alibaba Cloud. 阿里巴巴昨日确认，正对其旗下三款企业级 AI 智能体产品进行战略整合。此次调整以桌面 AI 智能体工具 QoderWork 为基础，深度融合钉钉孵化的企业协同办公 AI 平台「悟空」以及阿里云内部创业的 AI 执行引擎 MuleRun 的核心能力。

The integrated new AI product will target enterprise productivity scenarios and will be fully overseen by Chen Yusen, a "post-90s" executive who just took over as CEO of the Wukong Business Unit and CEO of DingTalk in mid-June this year. 整合后的全新 AI 产品将面向企业生产力场景，由今年 6 月中旬刚刚接任悟空事业部 CEO 兼钉钉 CEO 的「90后」陈宇森全面负责。

36Kr reported that Alibaba's move aims to end internal "horse racing" and concentrate resources to address competitive pressure from ByteDance's "Kouzi" and Feishu Agent, Tencent WorkBuddy, and other peers. 36 氪报道称，阿里此举旨在结束内部赛马，集中资源应对来自字节跳动「扣子」及飞书 Agent、腾讯 WorkBuddy 等同行的竞争压力。

In the future, Chen Yusen may adopt a layered integration architecture for the three products: QoderWork will provide front-end capabilities for desktop interaction and local execution, MuleRun will be responsible for the mid-end engine for Agent self-evolution and process reuse, and Wukong will serve as the back-end foundation, providing DingTalk's enterprise data, collaborative relationships, and organizational structure support. 未来，陈宇森或将对三款产品采取能力分层融合的架构：由 QoderWork 提供桌面交互与本地执行的前台能力，MuleRun 负责 Agent 自进化与流程复用的中台引擎，而悟空则作为后台底座，提供钉钉的企业数据、协同关系及组织架构支持。

Currently, Alibaba officially responded that the existing product services of QoderWork, Wukong, and MuleRun will achieve seamless upgrades in the future, and all user rights will not be affected. 目前，阿里官方已对此回应称，QoderWork、悟空、MuleRun 的现有产品服务未来将实现无缝升级，所有用户权益不会受到影响。

曝三星拿下 Meta 10 万亿韩元巨额芯片订单

According to "The Chosun Ilbo," Samsung Electronics' foundry division (Samsung Foundry) has recently entered an "allocation" phase for some advanced processes due to a surge in AI chip demand. This means Samsung has begun to adjust supply priorities, ensuring existing major customer orders first, and screening orders from new customers. 据《朝鲜日报》报道，三星电子晶圆代工部门（Samsung Foundry）近期因 AI 芯片需求激增，已针对部分先进制程进入「分配（Allocation）」阶段。这意味着三星开始调整供应优先级，优先保障现有大客户订单，并对新客户的订单进行筛选。

The report disclosed that Meta has decided to entrust Samsung with the foundry production of its third-generation self-developed AI accelerator "MTIA," with the contract value estimated to exceed 10 trillion Korean Won (approximately 7.3 billion USD). The chip is planned for mass production using Samsung's cutting-edge 2-nanometer process, with an order volume of hundreds of thousands of units. 报道披露，Meta 已决定将其第三代自研 AI 加速器「MTIA」交由三星代工，合同价值预计超过 10 万亿韩元（约合 73 亿美元）。该芯片计划采用三星 2 纳米尖端工艺量产，订单量达数十万组。

Additionally, Anthropic is also evaluating the use of Samsung's 2nm process to develop custom chips, in order to reduce reliance on Nvidia GPUs. 此外，Anthropic 也在评估利用三星 2 纳米工艺开发定制芯片，以降低对英伟达 GPU 的依赖。

Currently, Samsung's 4nm process capacity for next year is almost sold out, and some 8nm processes are also operating at full capacity. Due to nearing saturation, Samsung's order-taking strategy has shifted from 'multi-variety small-batch' to a more production-efficient and profitable 'few-variety large-batch' model, focusing on core projects from large technology companies. 目前，三星 4 纳米工艺明年的产能已基本售罄，部分 8 纳米工艺也处于满负荷运转状态。由于产能趋于饱和，三星的接单策略已从「多品种小批量」转向更具生产效率和盈利能力的「少品种大批量」模式，重点对接大型科技公司的核心项目。

💡 软件工程传奇 Kent Beck：AI 时代程序员更需要学会「做人」

According to Business Insider, Kent Beck, a legendary figure in software engineering, stated yesterday on 'The Pragmatic Engineer' show that with the popularization of AI technology, software engineers must learn 'soft skills' such as communication and empathy to survive in the industry. 据《商业内幕》报道，软件工程领域的传奇人物 Kent Beck 昨日在「The Pragmatic Engineer」节目中表示，随着 AI 技术的普及，软件工程师必须学习沟通与共情等「软技能」以在行业中生存。

Kent Beck pointed out that software engineers often lack good emotional regulation and empathy, and their communication style can sometimes be too direct. He believes that in an era where AI can automatically generate a large amount of code, this traditional 'lone wolf' work model is coming to an end. Kent Beck 指出，软件工程师往往缺乏良好的情绪调节能力和共情心，表达方式有时过于直接。他认为在 AI 能够自动生成大量代码的当下，这种传统的「孤狼式」工作模式即将终结。

Currently, 'Vibe-Coding' practices are becoming popular in the software industry, allowing senior developers to build prototypes more quickly and enabling non-programmers to turn ideas into pilot projects. This trend is blurring the lines between engineering and product work. 目前，软件行业已开始流行「Vibe-Coding」实践，这使得资深开发者能更快速地构建原型，也让非编程人员能够将创意转化为试点项目。这种趋势正在模糊工程与产品工作之间的界限。

To address this change, companies like Anthropic have asked engineers to act as 'Mini Product Managers (Mini PMs)' in small projects, not only responsible for code but also for stakeholder coordination and cross-functional collaboration. Kent Beck described this transformation of professional skills as a 'cosmic prank'. 为了应对这一变化，Anthropic 等公司已要求工程师在小型项目中担任「微型产品经理（Mini PM）」，不仅负责代码，还要承担利益相关者协调和跨职能协作的职责。Kent Beck 将这种职业技能的转型形容为一种「宇宙级的恶作剧」。

When you first started learning to program, people told you that mastering everything about computers would be enough. But then you'll find that your ability to change the world is actually limited by your communication and empathy skills. 你刚开始学编程时，别人告诉你只要掌握关于计算机的一切就万事大吉了。但随后你会发现，你改变世界的能力其实受限于你的沟通和共情能力。

The rise of this 'product engineer' role indicates that the most valuable engineers in the future will be those who can combine technical prowess with product judgment and interpersonal skills. 这种「产品工程师」角色的兴起表明，未来最有价值的工程师将是那些能够将技术实力与产品判断力、人际交往能力相结合的人才。
