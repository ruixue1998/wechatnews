「苹果税」降至 25%，百度腾讯网易回应

Yesterday, Apple announced that it would lower the commission rate for iOS and iPadOS on the App Store in mainland China starting from March 15 this year. This change covers multiple dimensions, including standard commissions, auto-renewing subscriptions, and the Small Developer Program, making it one of Apple's most significant fee reforms in the Chinese market in recent years. 昨天，苹果宣布将自今年 3 月 15 日起下调中国内地（大陆） App Store 的 iOS 与 iPadOS 佣金率。此次变动覆盖标准佣金、自动续费订阅以及小型开发者计划等多个维度，成为苹果近年来在中国市场最显著的费率改革之一。

  • The standard commission rate for in-app purchases and paid apps will be reduced from 30% to 25%; App 内购买及付费 App 的标准佣金率将由 30% 下调至 25%；
  • The commission rate for eligible in-app purchases under the App Store Small Business Program and Mini Apps Partner Program, as well as the commission rate for auto-renewing subscriptions after the first year, will be reduced from 15% to 12%. App Store Small Business Program 与 Mini Apps Partner Program 项下符合条件的 App 内购买佣金率，以及第一年后的自动续费订阅佣金率，将从 15% 下调至 12%。

Apple emphasized that developers do not need to re-sign terms before March 15 to enjoy the new rates. The "Apple Developer Program License Agreement" has been revised accordingly, and the Chinese version will be launched within one month. 苹果强调，开发者无需在 3 月 15 日前重新签署条款即可享受新费率，《Apple Developer Program 许可协议》已同步修订，中文版将在一个月内上线。

According to reports from "The Beijing News" and Sina Tech, several major internet companies have responded positively to this: 另据《新京报》和新浪科技报道，多家互联网大厂均对此作出积极回应：

  • Tencent stated that this adjustment reflects a response to the calls of Chinese users and developers, helping to build a more open and win-win platform environment and stimulate innovation; 腾讯表示，这一调整体现了对中国用户与开发者呼声的回应，有助于构建更加开放、共赢的平台环境，激发创新活力；
  • NetEase stated that the commission reduction will promote a virtuous cycle in the industry and has constructive significance for the long-term prosperity of China's mobile internet ecosystem; 网易则称，佣金下调将促进行业良性循环，对中国移动互联网生态的长期繁荣具有建设性意义；
  • Baidu stated that this adjustment not only responds to the long-standing concerns of Chinese users and developers but will also release more open and positive development signals for the application ecosystem. 百度表示，这一调整不仅回应了中国用户与开发者的长期关切，也将为应用生态释放出更加开放、积极的发展信号。

市监总局：严查「大字吸睛、小字免责」广告乱象

According to CCTV News, the State Administration for Market Regulation recently issued the "Notice on Strengthening the Regulation of Warning Language in Advertisements," launching a six-month special rectification campaign against advertising irregularities, focusing on intensifying supervision over misleading promotional practices such as "large characters for attention, small characters for disclaimer." 据央视新闻报道，市场监管总局近日印发《关于强化广告中提示性用语监管工作的通知》，启动为期半年的广告乱象专项整治，重点针对「大字吸睛、小字免责」等误导性宣传行为加大监管力度。

The "Notice" clarifies six key tasks: 《通知》明确六项重点任务：

  • Rectify "misleading large and small font" advertisements; 整治「误导性大小字」广告；
  • Investigate and punish advertisements that do not clearly indicate warning language in accordance with the law; 依法查处未显著标明提示性用语的广告；
  • Investigate and punish advertisements that illegally use absolute terms in accordance with the law; 依法查处违法使用绝对化用语的广告；
  • Strengthen supervision of citation advertisements; 加强引证广告监管；
  • Strengthen supervision of advertisers' self-attestation of advertisement content; 强化广告主自证广告内容监管；
  • Strengthen supervision over major advertising media. 强化对主要广告发布媒介监管。

Regulatory authorities require all regions to effectively strengthen the cleanup and rectification of behaviors such as "arbitrarily claiming first, original, best," "carrot-hole style citations," and weakening the display of unfavorable information, focusing on combating illegal advertisements that severely deceive and mislead consumers and disrupt market competition. 监管部门要求，各地需切实加大对「随意宣称第一、首创、最佳」「萝卜坑式引证」以及弱化不利信息标示等行为的清理整治力度，重点打击严重欺骗误导消费者、扰乱市场竞争秩序的违法广告。

Officials also emphasized that they will promote the matching of high-quality advertising resources with high-quality products, alleviate advertisers' "marketing anxiety," and guide businesses to abandon "wordplay" marketing. 官方同时强调，将推动优质广告资源与高品质产品对接，缓解广告主「营销焦虑」，并引导经营者摒弃「文字游戏」式营销。

淘宝更新支持 OpenClaw，可自动搜索比价

The Taobao desktop client recently released version v2.5.0, introducing MCP protocol support locally for the first time, allowing AI agents like OpenClaw to automatically perform multiple shopping operations with user authorization, including searching for products, browsing details, comparing prices, and adding to the shopping cart. However, final payment still requires manual confirmation from the user. 淘宝桌面客户端近日发布 v2.5.0 版本，首次在本地引入 MCP 协议支持，让 OpenClaw 等 AI 智能体能够在用户授权下自动化执行多项购物操作，包括搜索商品、浏览详情、比价、加入购物车等。不过，最终付款仍需用户手动确认。

After enabling the MCP protocol, the Taobao desktop client will expose a specified port on the local machine to pair with AI tools like OpenClaw installed on the same device. AI can input keywords, select interactive elements by simulating clicks, and extract information from product search results pages, product detail pages, and store pages. 启用 MCP 协议后，淘宝桌面客户端会在本机暴露指定端口，与安装在同一设备上的 OpenClaw 等 AI 工具进行配对。AI 可通过模拟点击方式输入关键词、选择交互元素，并在商品搜索结果页、商品详情页和店铺页面抓取信息。

Taobao also released configuration documentation, allowing users to directly provide links to AI agents for automatic completion of the configuration process. Community users have also shared configuration methods for agents like OpenClaw and QoderWork, including skill file writing and port verification steps. 淘宝同步发布了配置文档，用户可将链接直接提供给 AI 智能体，由其自动完成配置流程。社区用户也分享了 OpenClaw、QoderWork 等智能体的配置方法，包括技能文件写入、端口校验等步骤。

However, due to permission limitations, AI currently cannot read product review content, meaning it cannot filter out "water army" (fake) reviews, nor can it make further judgments based on review quality. 不过，受限于权限，AI 目前无法读取商品评价内容，意味着无法过滤水军评论，也无法基于评价质量进行进一步判断。

Additionally, the current version only supports the native MCP protocol, and AI and Taobao clients need to run on the same device. However, some technical users have achieved cross-machine control by modifying port listening. 此外，当前版本仅支持本机 MCP 协议，AI 与淘宝客户端需运行在同一台设备上，但已有技术用户通过修改端口监听实现跨机器控制。

OpenAI CEO：未来 AI 将像水电一样按量计费

According to Business Insider, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman stated at the BlackRock Infrastructure Summit held in Washington this week that AI will be metered like electricity and tap water in the future, becoming a fundamental public service . 据《商业内幕》报道，OpenAI CEO 山姆·奥特曼在本周于华盛顿举行的 BlackRock Infrastructure Summit 上表示， AI 未来将以类似电力和自来水的方式按量计费，成为一种基础性公共服务 。

Altman pointed out that as global demand for AI continues to rise, computing power will become a key factor in determining who can access AI services. 奥特曼指出，随着全球对 AI 的需求持续攀升，算力将成为决定谁能获得 AI 服务的关键因素。

We see a future where intelligence will become a public service, like electricity or water, which people buy from us by the amount, for anything they want to do. 我们看到一个未来，智能会像电力或水一样成为一种公共服务，人们按量从我们这里购买，用于任何他们想做的事情。

Altman emphasized that insufficient computing power will directly drive up the cost of AI usage and may even prevent some users from accessing services. He mentioned that if OpenAI cannot build enough computing infrastructure, "either we won't be able to sell it, or the price will become very high," which could ultimately force governments to intervene in allocating limited computing resources. 奥特曼强调，算力不足将直接推高 AI 使用成本，甚至可能导致部分用户无法获得服务。他提到，如果 OpenAI 无法建设足够的算力基础设施，「要么我们卖不出去，要么价格会变得非常高」，最终可能迫使政府介入分配有限的算力资源。

The report points out that computing power, composed of infrastructure such as chips and data centers, is the core resource for training and running large models. This year, several tech companies, including Apple and Google, plan to invest hundreds of billions of dollars to expand computing power to meet the rapidly growing demand for AI. 报道指出，算力由芯片、数据中心等基础设施构成，是训练和运行大模型的核心资源。今年包括苹果、Google 在内的多家科技公司都计划投入数千亿美元扩建算力，以应对 AI 的高速增长需求。

AMD CEO Lisa Su stated at CES in January this year that over "10 yottaflops" (a hundred million floating-point operations per second) of computing power will be needed globally in the next five years, which is 10,000 times the total global AI computing power in 2022.
