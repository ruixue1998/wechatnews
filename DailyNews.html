Google 宣布 Android 17 正式转型「智能系统」

Today, Google held an Android Show launch event separately before the official I/O developer conference, officially announcing that Android will transform from an "operating system" to an "intelligent system," and Gemini Intelligence will become the core identifier of the Android platform: 今天，Google 在正式 I/O 开发者大会前单独举办了 Android Show 发布活动，正式宣布 Android 将从「操作系统」转型为「智能系统」，Gemini Intelligence 将成为 Android 平台的核心标识：

  • The default keyboard Gboard has added multi-modal auto-fill capabilities, supporting information extraction from sources such as gallery photos, chat history, and emails; 默认键盘 Gboard 新增了多模态自动填入能力，支持从图库证照、聊天记录、邮件等来源提取信息；
  • Regarding desktop widgets, Google plans to launch the "Create my widget" feature, allowing users to generate custom widgets through natural language commands; 桌面小组件方面，Google 计划推出「Create my widget」功能，允许用户通过自然语言指令生成自定义小组件；
  • The voice input function has also been upgraded, with the new Rambler feature automatically organizing spoken language into structured text; 语音输入功能也迎来升级，新功能 Rambler 可将口语化语音自动整理为结构化文字；
  • Gemini's "proxy operation" capability has expanded from single-step tasks to multi-step tasks, supporting the direct initiation of complex commands by photographing real-world content; Gemini 的「代操作」能力从单步任务扩展至多步骤任务，支持通过拍摄实物内容直接发起复合指令；
  • Android Auto simultaneously updates Gemini Intelligence proxy operations, optimized 3D road visuals, and smarter streaming media playback features, with some car models also supporting vehicle information memory. Android Auto 同步更新 Gemini Intelligence 代操作、优化的 3D 道路画面和更智能的流媒体播放功能，部分车型还将支持记忆车辆信息。

In terms of hardware, Google has released a new laptop category, Googlebook, positioned as "the first hardware tailored for Gemini Intelligence," introducing a "shake cursor" method to summon Gemini, and also supporting direct execution of Android applications. 硬件方面，Google 发布了全新笔记本品类 Googlebook，定位为「首款为 Gemini Intelligence 量身打造的硬件」，引入「摇动光标」唤出 Gemini 的操作方式，还支持直接运行 Android 应用。

At the system level, Android 17 also brings a collaboration with Meta, where apps like Facebook and Instagram will support calling native camera functions; flat-style emojis have been upgraded to 2.5D. 系统层面，Android 17 还带来了与 Meta 的合作，Facebook、Instagram 等应用将支持调用原生相机功能；平面风格 emoji 升级为 2.5D 样式。

It is worth noting that Google also announced at the press conference that QuickShare's AirDrop compatibility feature will be extended to some models from brands such as OPPO, vivo, OnePlus, and Honor, with phased rollout expected in the second half of this year. 值得注意的是，发布会上 Google 还宣布 QuickShare 兼容 AirDrop 的功能将扩展至 OPPO、vivo、一加、荣耀等品牌的部分机型，预计今年下半年陆续推送。

He further emphasized that the read receipt and visitor features had previously received clear objections from users multiple times, stating, "These two features are welded shut; they will not be developed, nor will they be provided." 他进一步强调，已读功能和访客功能此前已多次收到用户明确反对意见，「此二项功能已焊死，不会开发，不会提供」。

全国首例 AI 代写种草笔记案终审：赔偿 10 万元，AI 批量炮制「数字泔水」构成侵权

The Hangzhou Intermediate People's Court in Zhejiang Province recently disclosed the final judgment in the nation's first unfair competition case involving AI-generated "product recommendation notes." 浙江省杭州市中级人民法院近日披露全国首例涉 AI 代写「种草笔记」不正当竞争案终审判决。

The court ruled that the AI writing tool jointly operated by Company B and Company C, which targeted a well-known social platform as its application layer, mass-generated product recommendation content and induced users to publish it, constituted unfair competition. The court ordered the two companies to cease relevant services and pay 100,000 yuan in compensation. 法院认定，B 公司和 C 公司共同运营的 AI 写作工具，以某知名社交平台为定向应用层，批量生成种草文案并诱导用户发布，构成不正当竞争，判决两公司停止相关服务并赔偿 10 万元。

The accused tool offers three versions: web, app, and WeChat mini-program. Popular modules are directly named "Xiaohongshu Recommendation Copy" and similar. Membership costs 40 yuan per month, and a lifetime membership is 168 yuan. 被诉工具提供网页版、App 及微信小程序三个版本，热门模块直接以「小某书种草文案」等命名，会员收费月付 40 元、终身会员 168 元。

The "technological neutrality defense" asserted by the two companies was not supported. The court pointed out that technology itself is neutral, but its commercial application must comply with platform rules and legal boundaries. 两公司主张的「技术中立抗辩」未获支持，法院指出技术本身中立，但商业化应用须遵守平台规则与法律底线。

💡 「vibe coding」概念提出者：AI 输出终局是「交互式神经视频」，现在先试试 HTML

Andrej Karpathy, the proponent of "vibe coding" and a founding member of OpenAI, posted on X yesterday, publicly supporting Claude Code team member Thariq's advocacy for "replacing Markdown with HTML." 「vibe coding」概念提出者、OpenAI 创始成员 Andrej Karpathy 昨日在 X 上发文，公开力挺 Claude Code 团队成员 Thariq 提倡的「以 HTML 取代 Markdown」做法。

Karpathy believes that the output format of large models is gradually upgrading: plain text is difficult to read, Markdown is better, and HTML goes a step further, supporting richer layouts, charts, and interactions. He predicts that there will be multiple iterations in the future, culminating in "interactive neural videos" directly generated by AI. Karpathy 认为，大模型的输出格式正在逐步升级：纯文本难以阅读，Markdown 好一些，HTML 则更进一步，支持更丰富的排版、图表和交互。他预测未来还将经历多轮迭代，终点是由 AI 直接生成的「交互式神经视频」。

Karpathy pointed out that about one-third of the human brain is dedicated to processing visual information, and vision is the most efficient channel for receiving information. Therefore, it is most natural for humans to "speak" to AI using voice, and AI should "present" results to humans as visually as possible. Karpathy 指出，人类大脑约三分之一专门用于处理视觉信息，视觉是接收信息效率最高的通道。因此，人类向 AI 「说话」用语音最自然，AI 向人类「展示」结果则应尽量用视觉呈现。

He also admitted that existing input methods are not enough — relying solely on voice or text lacks the spatial sense of "pointing at something on the screen while speaking." Until a better solution emerges, he suggests users directly add "structure the response as HTML" at the end of their prompts and then open it in a browser to view . 他同时承认，现有的输入方式还不够用 —— 光靠语音或文字，缺少「指着屏幕某处说话」这种空间感。在更好的方案出现之前， 他建议用户直接在提示词末尾加上「将回复结构化为HTML」，然后在浏览器里打开查看 。
