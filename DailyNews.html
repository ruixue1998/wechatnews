微软发布七款自研模型，还有一台「梦中神机」

Microsoft just held its Build 2026 developer conference, where it focused on releasing its self-developed model family, local AI development devices, new AI terminal forms, and enterprise AI governance tools, demonstrating its comprehensive layout at the AI infrastructure level. 刚刚，微软举办 Build 2026 开发者大会，集中发布了自研模型家族、本地 AI 开发设备、新型 AI 终端形态及企业 AI 治理工具，宣示其在 AI 基础设施层面的全面布局。

🔗 Related reading: Just now, Windows' "dream machine" arrived, turning your PC into an Agent workstation 🔗 相关阅读： 刚刚，Windows「梦中神机」来了，把你的 PC 变成 Agent 工位

This conference launched seven MAI models at once, covering inference, code, image, speech, and transcription. 本次大会一次性推出七款 MAI 模型，覆盖推理、代码、图像、语音和转录方向。

  • The inference model MAI Thinking 1 adopts a sparse MoE architecture, with 35B active parameters, a total parameter scale of about 1T, and supports a 256K token context; 推理模型 MAI Thinking 1 采用稀疏 MoE 架构，活跃参数 35B，总参数规模约 1T，支持 256K token 上下文；
  • The code model MAI Code 1 Flash is trained end-to-end by Microsoft, scoring 51.2% on SWE Bench Pro, higher than Claude Haiku 4.5's 35.2%, and is currently being rolled out to GitHub Copilot individual users; 代码模型 MAI Code 1 Flash 由微软端到端训练，在 SWE Bench Pro 上得分 51.2%，高于 Claude Haiku 4.5 的 35.2%，目前正向 GitHub Copilot 个人用户推出；
  • The image model MAI Image 2.5 has been integrated into PowerPoint and is planned to be extended to OneDrive; 图像模型 MAI Image 2.5 已进入 PowerPoint，并计划扩展至 OneDrive；
  • The transcription model MAI Transcribe 1.5 supports 43 languages; 转录模型 MAI Transcribe 1.5 支持 43 种语言；
  • The speech model MAI Voice 2 supports 15 languages and can adapt voices through short samples. 语音模型 MAI Voice 2 支持 15 种语言，可通过短样本适配声音。

In terms of hardware, Microsoft released the Surface RTX Spark Dev Box for developers, which Nadella called a "dream machine." 硬件方面，微软发布面向开发者的 Surface RTX Spark Dev Box，纳德拉将其称为「dream machine（梦中神机）」。

The device is based on the NVIDIA RTX Spark platform, offering 1 petaflop of AI computing power, 20 CPU cores, and 128GB of unified memory, with a thermal design power of 100W, and is planned for release this fall. NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang stated via video link that PCs are evolving from personal computers to "personal AI." 该设备基于英伟达 RTX Spark 平台，提供 1 petaflop AI 算力、20 个 CPU 核心和 128GB 统一内存，热设计功耗 100W，计划于今年秋季推出。英伟达 CEO 黄仁勋通过视频连线表示，PC 正从个人电脑向「个人 AI」演进。

Microsoft also showcased two types of reference devices codenamed Project Solara: 微软同时展示了代号 Project Solara 的两类参考设备：

  • A desktop workstation based on MediaTek chips, supporting identity recognition, voice interaction, and Microsoft 365 Copilot access; 基于联发科芯片的桌面工作终端，支持身份识别、语音交互和 Microsoft 365 Copilot 接入；
  • A digital badge using Qualcomm wearable chips, designed for mobile work scenarios such as healthcare and field data collection. 采用高通可穿戴芯片的数字工牌，面向医疗、现场采集等移动工作场景。

OpenAI 扩展 Codex 生态：插件覆盖投行、数据分析等 6 大方向

During yesterday's "Intelligence at Work" live event, OpenAI officially announced that it will integrate Codex's core capabilities directly into the ChatGPT application within the next few weeks, allowing users to directly invoke Codex's execution capabilities within ChatGPT. 在昨天的「Intelligence at Work」直播活动上，OpenAI 官宣，未来几周内将 Codex 的核心能力直接整合进 ChatGPT 应用，让用户在 ChatGPT 里直接调用 Codex 的执行能力。

OpenAI also announced several functional extensions for Codex, introducing role-specific plugins, Sites shared website functionality, and annotation features, extending its use cases from software development to broader knowledge work domains. OpenAI 还宣布对 Codex 进行多项功能扩展，推出角色专属插件、Sites 共享网站功能及标注功能，将使用场景从软件开发延伸至更广泛的知识工作领域。

Official data shows that Codex's weekly active users have exceeded 5 million, with non-developer users accounting for approximately 20% of the total, growing more than 3 times faster than developer users. 官方数据显示，Codex 每周活跃用户已超过 500 万，其中非开发者用户占整体约 20%，增速超过开发者用户的 3 倍。

  • Role-specific plugins: 6 plugins launched, integrating 62 applications and 110 skills, covering data analysis, creative production, sales, product design, public market equity investment, and investment banking. More directions such as corporate finance, private equity, and legal will be launched later, with plans to open up a partner ecosystem; 角色专属插件：上线 6 款插件，集成 62 款应用与 110 项技能，覆盖数据分析、创意生产、销售、产品设计、公开市场股权投资及投资银行等方向。后续还将推出企业财务、私募股权、法律等更多方向，并计划开放合作伙伴生态；
  • Sites: Preview open to Enterprise and Business users, supporting the generation of interactive web pages from analysis results and solution plans that can be shared via URL, with continuously updatable content. OpenAI has engaged in early collaborations with platforms such as Vercel, Figma, Replit, and Webflow; Sites：面向企业版和商业版用户开放预览，支持将分析结果和方案规划生成可通过 URL 分享的交互式网页，内容可持续更新。OpenAI 已与 Vercel、Figma、Replit、Webflow 等平台展开早期合作；
  • Annotation feature: Previously limited to code and website scenarios, it has now been extended to documents, spreadsheets, and presentations. Users can select content and provide modification instructions, and Codex will make targeted adjustments to the selected area. 标注功能：此前仅限代码和网站场景，此次扩展至文档、表格和幻灯片，用户可框选内容并给出修改指令，Codex 将针对选中区域定向调整。

Role-specific plugins are currently being rolled out gradually in supported regions, and the Sites feature can be enabled by enterprise administrators in the backend settings. 角色专属插件目前正在支持地区逐步推出，Sites 功能企业管理员可在后台设置中启用。

腾讯云下调 DeepSeek-V4 系列模型价格，最高降幅 97.5%

Tencent Cloud announced that its intelligent agent development platform will reduce the prices of the DeepSeek-V4 series models starting today, with a maximum reduction of 97.5%. 腾讯云发布公告，旗下智能体开发平台将于今日起，对 DeepSeek-V4 系列模型价格进行下调，最高降幅达 97.5%。

Specifically, the inference input and inference output prices for the DeepSeek-V4-Pro model have both been reduced by 75%, dropping from 0.012 yuan and 0.024 yuan per thousand tokens to 0.003 yuan and 0.006 yuan, respectively; 具体来看，DeepSeek-V4-Pro 模型的推理输入与推理输出价格均下调 75%，分别从每千 tokens 0.012 元、0.024 元降至 0.003 元、0.006 元；

The cache hit price saw the largest reduction, dropping from 0.001 yuan per thousand tokens to 0.000025 yuan, a decrease of 97.5%. For the DeepSeek-V4-Flash model, this adjustment only involves the cache hit price, which decreased from 0.0002 yuan per thousand tokens to 0.00002 yuan, a 90% reduction. 缓存命中价格降幅最大，从每千 tokens 0.001 元降至 0.000025 元，降幅达 97.5%。DeepSeek-V4-Flash 模型此次调整仅涉及缓存命中价格，从每千 tokens 0.0002 元降至 0.00002 元，降幅为 90%。

Alphabet 拟融资 800 亿美元投入 AI 基建

According to Bloomberg, Google's parent company Alphabet plans to raise a total of $80 billion in equity financing, with the proceeds to be used for AI infrastructure, computing power construction, and related capital expenditures. 据彭博社报道，Google 母公司 Alphabet 计划进行总额 800 亿美元的股权融资，所得资金将用于 AI 基础设施、算力建设以及相关资本支出。

This financing consists of three parts: a $30 billion underwritten public offering, a $40 billion at-the-market offering of Class A or Class C shares, and a $10 billion direct private investment by Berkshire Hathaway. The company also stated that a portion of the net proceeds from the ATM program will be used to cover tax obligations related to employee equity award vesting. 这笔融资由三部分组成：300 亿美元包销公开发行、400 亿美元按市值发行 A 类或 C 类股票，以及伯克希尔・哈撒韦以私募方式直接投资 100 亿美元。公司还表示，ATM 计划净收益的一部分将用于处理员工股权奖励归属相关税务义务。

Alphabet had previously raised its capital expenditure forecast for this year to $180 billion to $190 billion and expects capital expenditures to continue to increase significantly next year. Alphabet 此前已把今年资本开支预期提高至 1800 亿至 1900 亿美元，并预计明年资本支出继续显著增加。

字节 Seed 机器人团队转向周畅汇报

According to LatePost, ByteDance's Seed system recently adjusted the reporting structure of its robotics team: the Seed Robotics team, originally led by Li Hang, has been reporting to Zhou Chang, head of multimodal AI, for over a month. Li Hang now serves as an advisor for academic cooperation. 据《晚点 LatePost》报道，字节跳动 Seed 体系近期调整机器人团队汇报线：原由李航负责的 Seed Robotics 团队已向多模态负责人周畅汇报月余，李航现以顾问身份负责学术合作方向。

Zhou Chang is one of the core R&D leaders for ByteDance's Seed AI. After joining ByteDance in 2024, he has primarily been responsible for Seed's multimodal interaction and world model directions. The report cited sources close to ByteDance, stating that robotics-related work was previously handled by multiple teams responsible for hardware, data, and models, leading to overlapping capabilities in model R&D during execution; after the adjustment, robotics R&D resources are further concentrated under Zhou Chang's management. 周畅是字节 Seed AI 研发核心负责人之一，2024 年加入字节后主要负责 Seed 的多模态交互和世界模型等方向。报道援引接近字节的人士称，机器人相关工作此前由多个团队分别承担硬件、数据、模型等任务，执行过程中在模型研发上存在能力重叠；调整后，机器人研发资源进一步集中到周畅管理范围内。

ByteDance is also recruiting a technical lead for embodied AI, responsible for the overall planning of the robotics business. The position is designated as L8, benchmarked against Alibaba's P10-P11, and reports to Zhou Chang. Candidates for the position primarily come from technical leads at leading embodied AI startups. 字节还在招聘具身智能技术负责人，负责机器人业务整体规划，职级定位为 L8，对标阿里 P10-P11，并向周畅汇报。岗位候选人主要来自头部具身智能创业公司技术负责人。

The report mentioned that Zhou Chang's responsibilities in multimodal interaction and world models are highly relevant to the technical requirements of Robotics: robots need vision, multimodal capabilities, and world models to understand real-world environments, and robotic scenarios can also provide real-world interaction data such as warehouses, factories, and homes for models. 报道提到，周畅负责的多模态交互、世界模型方向与 Robotics 的技术需求高度相关：机器人需要视觉、多模态和世界模型来理解现实环境，机器人场景也能为模型提供仓库、工厂、家庭等真实世界交互数据。

💡 山姆 · 奥特曼承认错误：拥抱 AI 最积极的公司仍在招聘

According to Business Insider, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman stated in a CNBC interview yesterday that among the sample of large enterprises he has encountered, companies with the deepest AI adoption are actually hiring on a large scale ; while those companies that announced layoffs citing AI are often the slowest to implement AI. 据《商业内幕》报道，OpenAI CEO Sam Altman 昨日在 CNBC 采访中指出，在他所接触的大型企业样本中， AI 采用程度最深的公司，反而正在大规模招聘 ；而那些以 AI 为由宣布裁员的公司，往往是 AI 落地最慢的一批。

Altman stated that AI can be a "convenient excuse" for companies to explain layoffs, but this does not align with the actual application of the technology.
