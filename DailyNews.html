预制菜新国标过审：餐厅是用需明示

According to a recent report by Yicai, the draft "National Food Safety Standard for Pre-prepared Dishes" led by the National Health Commission has passed review and will soon be open for public comment. 据第一财经日前报道，由国家卫健委主导的《预制菜食品安全国家标准》草案已通过审查，即将向社会公开征求意见。

The report states that at that time, there will be a unified definition for the "identity" of pre-prepared dishes, and whether and how catering establishments use pre-prepared dishes will also be included in the scope of information disclosure for the first time. Some experts also suggest that the aforementioned information about pre-prepared dishes should be mandatorily disclosed. 报道称， 届时预制菜「身份」将有统一说法，餐饮门店是否使用、如何使用预制菜，也将首次纳入信息披露范畴。 也有专家建议，预制菜的上述信息应强制披露。

Recently, Luo Yonghao questioned Xibei's use of pre-prepared dishes for some of its meals and the unreasonable pricing, which Xibei vehemently denied, sparking widespread attention. The pre-prepared dish industry is also under scrutiny. 近期，罗永浩质疑西贝部分餐品为预制菜，且存在价格不合理等情况，而西贝断然否认引发广泛关注的当下，预制菜行业也备受关注。

According to experts involved in the drafting, "The standard mainly specifies definitions and food safety indicator requirements." The draft makes unified provisions for the classification, raw materials, processing technology, storage, transportation, and inspection methods of pre-prepared dishes, and for the first time proposes mandatory indicators such as "no preservatives added." 据参与起草的专家透露，「该标准主要是规定定义和食品安全指标要求。」草案对预制菜的分类、原料、加工工艺、贮存运输及检验方法作出了统一规定，并首次提出「不添加防腐剂」等强制性指标。

Compared with the existing 141 group and local standards, the national standard clauses focus more on the "safety bottom line," providing direct basis for supervision and enforcement. 与现行 141 项团体、地方标准相比，国标条款更聚焦「安全底线」，为监管执法提供直接依据。

As the province with the largest output value of pre-prepared dishes nationwide, Guangdong has issued 8 provincial local standards and launched the country's first "pre-prepared dish" related standard in January this year. The definition of pre-prepared dishes in this document not only covers main ingredients and pre-processing methods but also explicitly defines them as pre-packaged dish products and standardizes a series of terms related to pre-prepared dishes. 作为全国预制菜产值第一大省，广东已出台 8 项省级地方标准，并于今年 1 月上线全国首个「预制菜」相关标准。 该文件中的预制菜定义，不仅涵盖了主要原料和预加工方式，还明确其为预包装菜肴产品，并规范了一系列预制菜相关术语。

Among them, the "General Requirements for Packaging and Labeling of Cantonese Pre-prepared Dishes" is the first local standard in the country to specify packaging and labeling for pre-prepared dishes. 其中，《粤菜预制菜包装标识通用要求》则是全国首个以预制菜为标准化对象制定的包装标识地方标准。

The standard requires that the product name must indicate "pre-prepared dish" or "pre-prepared cuisine." If a certain ingredient or component is mentioned in the product name, its input amount or content in the finished product should be indicated. At the same time, the standard also requires labeling of eating/cooking methods to ensure consumers can prepare food in the best way to achieve optimal taste and flavor. 该标准要求在产品名称上必须标示 「预制菜」「预制菜肴」，产品名称中提及的某种原料或成分，应标示该种原料或成分的投料量或其在成品中的含量。同时，标准还要求标注食用/烹饪方法，确保消费者能够按照最佳方式准备食物，获得最佳的口感和风味。

In addition, in July this year, the Hunan Provincial Market Supervision Bureau clearly stated in its reply to a CPPCC proposal that it would incorporate "explicitly indicating the use of pre-prepared dishes" into food safety operating standards and guide industry associations to formulate a "Disclosure Guide". 另外，今年 7 月，湖南省市场监管局在答复政协提案时明确，将把「明示预制菜使用情况」写入食品安全操作规范，并指导行业协会制定《公示指南》。

This means that whether it's fast food in shopping malls or high-end restaurants, as long as pre-prepared dishes are used, they must be clearly marked on menus, ordering pages, or prominent locations within the establishment. Violators will be considered to have provided "untrue information" and will face penalties. 这意味着，无论商场快餐还是高端酒楼，只要使用预制菜，都需在菜单、点餐页面或店内显著位置进行标注，违者将被视为「信息不真实」面临处罚。

A local market regulator stated that promoting the explicit indication of pre-prepared dishes in the catering sector, where businesses using them inform consumers in advance, is of great significance for protecting consumers' right to know and choose. To a certain extent, it can enhance catering businesses' ability to resist market risks, encourage pre-prepared dish manufacturers to actively research and improve products, and promote the healthy development of the pre-prepared dish industry. 一位地方市场监管人员表示，推广餐饮环节使用预制菜明示，使用预制菜的商家提前告知消费者，对保障消费者的知情权、选择权有重要意义，一定程度上能提高餐饮商家抗市场风险能力，推动预制菜生产企业积极研发改良产品，促进预制菜产业良性发展。

Regarding the "pre-prepared dish" dispute between Luo Yonghao and Xibei, food experts stated, "This incident is both bad and good for the pre-prepared dish industry. It will inevitably attract the attention of relevant government departments, promote the speedy introduction of national food safety standards for pre-prepared dishes, and simultaneously advance the popularization of quality standards such as pre-prepared dish terminology and product classification, strengthening their alignment with national food safety standards." 针对罗永浩与西贝的「预制菜」大战，食品领域的专家表示，「这次事件，对预制菜行业来讲是坏事也是好事，势必会引起政府有关部门的关注，促进预制菜食品安全国家标准尽快出台，同时推进预制菜术语、产品分类等质量标准的推广，加强与食品安全国家标准的衔接。」

Luo Yonghao also reposted the above content on September 13, stating, "Great, it's highly anticipated." He emphasized that he is not against pre-prepared dishes, "In some cases, I would even actively choose pre-prepared dishes." He also stated, "Since pre-prepared dishes are legal, restaurants certainly have the right to use them, but consumers have the right to know whether a restaurant has used pre-prepared dishes." 罗永浩也于 9 月 13 日转发上述内容并表示 「太好了，万众期待」。 其强调，自己不反对预制菜，「在某些情况下，我甚至会主动选择预制菜。」其也表示，「既然预制菜合法，餐馆当然有权使用预制菜，但消费者有权知道餐馆是否使用了预制菜。」

库克：Air 续航出色，Pro 的 VC 均热板很创新

Recently, after the launch of the new iPhone, Apple CEO Tim Cook accepted interviews with multiple media outlets and shared his various views on the new iPhone. 日前，苹果 CEO 库克在新 iPhone 发布后，接受了多家媒体采访，并与大家分享了其对新 iPhone 的各种看法。

When CNBC reporter Jim Cramer asked Cook about the battery life performance of the iPhone Air, the latter said, "The battery life is excellent, you'll love it." Cook stated that Apple designed the interior of the iPhone Air, and thanks to eSIM-only support, the new phone's battery can extend into the area previously occupied by the physical SIM card slot, calling it "an incredible innovation." 在 CNBC 记者 Jim Cramer 询问库克 iPhone Air 的电池续航表现如何时，后者称 「电池续航出色，你会非常喜欢的。」 库克表示，苹果对 iPhone Air 的内部进行了设计，得益于仅支持 eSIM，新机的电池能扩展到此前放置物理 SIM 卡的卡槽区域，「这是一项令人难以置信的创新。」

In the interview videos released by Bilibili UP hosts Linksphotograph and Yingshi Jufeng, Cook appeared very excited when discussing the new VC liquid cooling vapor chamber technology on the iPhone 17 Pro series, stating, "I think this is another invention and innovation exclusive to Apple, perhaps only Apple can achieve it." 而在 B 站 UP 主 Linksphotograph、影视飓风公布的采访视频中，库克则在谈到 iPhone 17 Pro 系列新上的 VC 液冷均热板技术时，表现非常兴奋， 「我觉得这算是另一项专属于苹果的发明和创新，或许只有苹果才能够做到」。

雷军谈「黑公关」：法务部已起诉

Recently, Lei Jun, founder of Xiaomi, reposted content from CCTV Finance's "Half-Hour Economy" on social media and published a post regarding the issue of "black PR" (malicious public relations). 日前，小米创办人雷军在社交媒体转发了央视财经《经济半小时》内容，并针对黑公关一事发文。

Lei Jun stated that "Half-Hour Economy" aired content exposing "black PR," and four car manufacturers—Xiaomi, Xpeng, Nio, and Deepal—were interviewed. 雷军表示，《经济半小时》播出了起底黑公关的内容，小米、小鹏、蔚来、深蓝四家车企接受了采访。

He emphasized that the methods used to spread rumors and defame various car manufacturers are highly similar, including AI-generated defamatory content, insulting car owners in comment sections, and smear campaigns against car products through press releases before new car launches. Some platforms even show different traffic for titles that guide positive or negative interpretations of the same content. 其强调，造谣抹黑各家车企的手段高度相似，都有 AI 生成造谣内容、评论区辱骂车主、新车发布前通稿抹黑车企产品等行为，甚至有平台对于相同内容正面负面不同引导的标题会有不同的流量呈现。

Lei Jun revealed that Xiaomi's legal department has collected evidence from hundreds of accounts this year, initiated dozens of lawsuits that are awaiting trial one by one, and multiple reported cases are also in the process of investigation. 雷军透露， 小米法务部今年已取证数百个账号，发起诉讼数十起，正在等待逐一开庭，多起报警的案件也正在办案过程中。

During the program, Li Xiaoshuang, Vice President of Xiaomi Auto, stated, "We must increase penalties for the actions of 'black astroturfers' and 'black PR.' From a regulatory perspective, we need to raise the cost and consequences for 'black astroturfers' and 'black PR.'" 而在节目中，小米汽车副总裁李肖爽表示，「要对黑水军、黑公关的行为，加大处罚力度。从监管的角度，去提高黑水军、黑公关的成本和代价。」

Lei Jun also stated that this joint special rectification action by six departments aims to combat "black PR" and "black astroturfers," requiring online platform companies to strengthen the identification and control of online astroturfers and "black mouths" who use generative AI technology, thereby creating a clean and clear industrial environment for the development of China's automotive industry. 雷军还表示，此次六部门联合专项整治行动，打击黑公关、黑水军，要求网络平台企业加强对采用生成式人工智能技术等的网络水军、黑嘴的甄别管控，为中国汽车发展构建风清气朗的产业环境。

蚂蚁开源发布新版全球大模型开源生态全景图

On September 13, at the 2025 Inclusion·Bund Summit AI Open Source Insights Forum, Ant Open Source, in conjunction with Inclusion AI, released the brand new "Global Large Model Open Source Development Ecosystem Panorama and Trends Report". 9 月 13 日，在 2025 Inclusion·外滩大会 AI 开源见解论坛上，蚂蚁开源联合 Inclusion AI 发布了全新的《全球大模型开源开发生态全景与趋势报告》。

This is the 2.0 version of the report, following its initial release in May. It not only comprehensively reveals the current status and future trends of AI open source development but also incorporates new developments in the open source community over the past hundred days, providing important references for industry development. 据悉，这是报告 5 月首次发布后的 2.0 版本， 不仅全面揭示了人工智能开源领域的发展现状和未来趋势，还纳入了百余天内开源社区的新动向，为行业发展提供重要参考。

The report originally stemmed from Ant Group's internal technical trend insights, with all data sourced from the open-source community. Projects were filtered and ranked using the OpenRank algorithm through analysis of GitHub's entire platform projects. 报告最初起源于蚂蚁集团内部的技术趋势洞察，其中的数据全部来源于开源社区，通过对 GitHub 全平台项目的分析，使用 OpenRank 算法对项目进行筛选和排名。

Specifically, the large model open-source development ecosystem panorama released this time includes 114 of the most-watched open-source projects distributed across 22 technical fields, categorized into two major technical directions: AI Agent and AI Infra. 具体来看，本次发布的大模型开源开发生态全景图共收录了分布在 22 个技术领域的 114 个最受关注的开源项目，分为 AI Agent 和 AI Infra 两大技术方向。

According to the report, among approximately 360,000 global developers participating in the panorama project, US developers accounted for 24%, Chinese developers for 18%, followed by India (8%), Germany (6%), and the UK (5%). China and the US together contribute over 40% of the core strength. More notably, regarding large model open-source strategies, Chinese manufacturers tend to favor open-weight open-source model routes, while leading US manufacturers mostly adopt closed-source models. 据报告显示，在参与全景图项目开发的约 36 万全球开发者中，统计到美国开发者占比 24%，中国开发者占比 18%，其次是印度（8%）、德国（6%）和英国（5%）。中美两国合计贡献超四成核心力量。更值得关注的是，在大模型开源策略上，中国厂商更倾向于开放权重的开源模型路线，而美国头部厂商则多采用闭源模式。

Released alongside the panorama is a detailed insights report, "Revisiting the Large Model Open Source Development Ecosystem Panorama and Trends from Community Data."
