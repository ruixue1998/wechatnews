曝 DeepSeek 开发自研 AI 推理芯片

According to Reuters, citing sources, DeepSeek is developing its own AI chips, aiming to reduce its reliance on Nvidia chips. 据路透社援引知情人士消息称，DeepSeek 正在开发自有 AI 芯片，目标是降低对英伟达芯片的依赖。

Sources said that the chips DeepSeek is developing are primarily for inference scenarios, which involve the computational process where trained models generate answers for users, rather than for training new models. If successful, DeepSeek will further enter the field of semiconductor design. 知情人士称，DeepSeek 正在研发的芯片主要面向推理场景，也就是已训练模型为用户生成回答的计算环节，而非用于训练新模型。如果进展顺利，DeepSeek 将进一步切入半导体设计领域。

Three sources stated that DeepSeek's chip project is still in its early stages, and DeepSeek has begun reaching out to external partners and engaging in discussions with chip design, wafer foundry, and memory manufacturers. 三名知情人士表示，DeepSeek 的芯片项目仍处于早期阶段，DeepSeek 已开始接触外部合作伙伴，并与芯片设计、晶圆代工和存储厂商进行讨论。

One of the sources said that the relevant work started about a year ago. Two other sources revealed that DeepSeek has intensified its recruitment efforts for chip design engineers in recent months, but the recruitment is mainly conducted privately and no positions have been posted on public recruitment platforms. 其中一名人士称，相关工作约在一年前启动。另有两名人士透露，DeepSeek 近几个月加大了芯片设计工程师招聘力度，但招聘主要以私下方式进行，并未在公开招聘平台发布岗位。

曝苹果折叠屏 iPhone 已在量产

According to the Shanghai Securities News, the progress of Apple's first foldable iPhone is accelerating, and it has now entered the mass production phase. 据《上海证券报》报道，苹果首款折叠屏 iPhone 的落地进度正在提速，目前已进入量产阶段。

Industry insiders revealed that Foxconn, the leading contract manufacturer, has already initiated large-scale recruitment to reserve manpower for the production of new devices. Relevant recruitment information shows that Ganzhou Foxconn issued a recruitment announcement yesterday, hiring employees aged 18 to 50 to engage in the production and processing of precision components for Apple phones; 产业人士透露，代工龙头富士康已率先启动大规模招工，为新机的生产储备人力。相关招聘信息显示，赣州富士康于昨日发布招募公告，招聘 18 至 50 岁员工从事苹果手机精密组件的生产与加工；

Foxconn's HR department also posted a recruitment notice for temporary workers the day before yesterday, with the recruitment scope covering the Longhua/Guanlan Apple A business group and the Longhua Sanying business group, both involved in the assembly of foldable iPhones. 富士康人资部门也于前日发文招募临时工，招聘范围覆盖了涉及折叠屏 iPhone 组装的龙华/观澜苹果 A 事业群以及龙华三赢事业群。

Regarding capacity planning, sources said Apple has asked suppliers to prepare for the production of approximately 10 million foldable iPhones this year, a figure that has been revised upwards from the 7-8 million units expected a few months ago. 在产能规划方面，知情人士称苹果已要求供应商为今年生产约 1000 万部折叠屏 iPhone 做好准备，这一数字较数月前 700 万至 800 万部的预期有所上调。

前华为「天才少年」吐槽 DeepSeek 招聘流程

Li Bojie, a former Huawei "genius youth," publicly complained about DeepSeek's interview process on social media on July 6. He stated that no interview was arranged for half a month after passing the written test, and it was only after repeated urging that he entered the interview stage; the first two rounds of interviews were both coding interviews. 前华为「天才少年」李博杰 7 月 6 日在社交平台公开吐槽 DeepSeek 面试流程。他表示，笔试通过后半个月未安排面试，经过反复催促才进入面试环节；前两轮面试均为代码面试。

Li Bojie also mentioned that during the second interview, the interviewer was late and questioned him for copying code because he was using two screens during the coding interview, demanding that he stop copying code and stating that if he couldn't prove he wasn't copying, the interview couldn't continue. Li Bojie said he felt "severely offended" and decided to terminate the DeepSeek interview process and publicly expose it. 李博杰还提到，二面过程中面试官迟到，并在代码面试时因他使用两个屏幕而质疑其抄代码，要求他停止抄代码，还表示如果不能证明没有抄代码，面试无法继续。李博杰称自己「被严重冒犯」，已决定终止 DeepSeek 面试流程并公开曝光。

今年 618 手机销量同比下滑 13%

According to the latest data from Counterpoint Research, affected by rising storage chip costs and weakened promotional efforts, China's smartphone market saw a 13% year-on-year decline in sales during this year's "618" shopping festival. 据 Counterpoint Research 最新数据，受存储芯片成本上涨及促销力度减弱影响，中国智能手机市场在今年「618」购物节期间销量同比下降 13%。

During the period from May 26 to June 21, the performance of various brands in China's smartphone market showed significant differentiation. 在 5 月 26 日至 6 月 21 日期间，中国智能手机市场各品牌表现呈显著分化。

Ivan Lam, Senior Analyst at Counterpoint, pointed out that due to cost pressures, the pricing of new and old models this year is generally higher than the same period last year, and manufacturers' price reduction margins and coverage of models have both shrunk. Counterpoint 高级分析师 Ivan Lam 指出，受制于成本压力，今年新老机型的定价普遍高于去年同期，且厂商的降价幅度与覆盖机型范围均有所缩水。

  • Huawei: Ranked first with a 21% market share, the only leading brand to achieve positive growth; Enjoy 90 Pro Max and Mate 80 are hot sellers; 华为：市场份额 21% 居首，唯一实现正增长的头部品牌；畅享 90 Pro Max 及 Mate 80 热销；
  • Apple: Although it ranked second in the market due to early subsidy policies, its total sales still declined by 9% year-on-year because the overall discount margin was less than that of the iPhone 16 series in the same period last year; 苹果：虽凭借提前启动的补贴政策位居市场第二，但因整体让利幅度不及去年同期的 iPhone 16 系列，其总销量仍同比下滑 9%；
  • Xiaomi: Suffered a double-digit decline, down 24% year-on-year, affected by overall weak demand and reduced promotions. 小米：受整体需求疲软及促销收缩影响，遭遇双位数下滑，同比下滑 24%。

Counterpoint predicts that although "618" briefly boosted a month-on-month recovery in June's phone sales, the market will subsequently enter a seasonal slowdown period, and China's smartphone shipments are expected to record a double-digit decline for the full year. Counterpoint 预测，尽管「618」短暂拉动了 6 月份手机销量的环比回暖，但市场随后将进入季节性放缓期，预计今年中国智能手机全年出货量将录得双位数下降。

华强北商家：图纸全泄露也造不出真 iPhone

According to reports from Sina Tech and Time Finance, multiple sellers in Huaqiangbei and veteran figures in the mobile phone industry have explicitly stated that it is absolutely impossible for Huaqiangbei to replicate a genuine iPhone. 据新浪科技、时代财经报道，多位华强北卖家与资深手机圈人士明确表示，华强北绝无可能复刻 iPhone 真机。

Despite recent large-scale leaks of original factory data such as complete motherboard schematics for the iPhone 18 Pro, A20 Pro chip manuals, and supplier lists, and even though Huaqiangbei possesses the world's most complete supply chain for mobile phone components, industry insiders point out that there are two insurmountable technical barriers to replicating a genuine device: 尽管近期 iPhone 18 Pro 的完整主板图纸、A20 Pro 芯片手册及供应商清单等原厂资料大规模泄露，且华强北拥有全球最完整的手机零部件供应体系，但从业者指出，复刻真机存在两道无法逾越的技术壁垒：

  • First, Apple's self-developed A-series chips, whose core architecture and 2nm production process are strictly blocked externally, with no market channels for acquisition; 一是苹果自研的 A 系列芯片，其核心架构与 2 nm 生产工艺对外严格封锁，市场无渠道获取；
  • Second, the closed-loop iOS operating system, whose underlying encryption and hardware binding mechanisms are difficult to crack. 二是闭环的 iOS 操作系统，系统底层的加密与硬件绑定机制难以破解。

Currently, Huaqiangbei can only produce highly appearance-restored counterfeit phones based on leaked data. These devices mostly run modified Android systems, with huge gaps in performance, fluidity, and ecosystem experience compared to genuine products, and cannot enjoy official Apple after-sales warranty. 目前，华强北仅能基于泄露资料产出外观高度还原的山寨机。这类设备多搭载修改版安卓系统，在性能、流畅度及生态体验上与正版差距巨大，且无法享受苹果官方售后保修。

💡 Google 前高管：22 岁 AI 原生代员工效率已超越资深员工

According to Business Insider, Clay Bavor, a former Google executive and co-founder of AI startup Sierra, recently stated in an interview that 22-year-old graduates with AI literacy are demonstrating unique advantages in the workplace. 据《商业内幕》报道，Google 前高管、AI 创业公司 Sierra 联合创始人 Clay Bavor 近日在接受采访时表示，具备 AI 素养的 22 岁毕业生在职场中正展现出独特优势。

He pointed out that while some AI-native employees lack traditional work experience, their proficiency with AI tools and intuitive application have surpassed many senior employees, making them the most efficient group within the company. 他指出，部分 AI 原生代员工虽然缺乏传统工作经验，但其对 AI 工具的熟练程度和应用直觉已超越许多资深员工，成为公司内部最高效的群体。

As a former Google executive, Clay Bavor founded Sierra in 2023. He observed that AI is narrowing the gap between junior and senior employees. In his view, students who have extensively experimented with generative AI during college have a practical advantage in integrating new technologies into business processes, and many companies are eager to introduce this capability. 作为 Google 前高管，Clay Bavor 于 2023 年创立了 Sierra。他观察到 AI 正在缩小初级员工与资深员工之间的差距。在他看来，这些在大学期间长期实验生成式 AI 的学生，在将新技术融入业务流程方面具有实际优势，许多公司都渴望引入这种能力。

To screen for such talent, Sierra has adjusted its engineering interview process. The company no longer relies on traditional coding tests but instead asks candidates to build an application using their preferred AI programming tools, with the company covering the associated token costs. Interviewers then ask candidates to review the entire build process. 为了筛选此类人才，Sierra 已经调整了工程面试流程。公司不再依赖传统的编程笔试，而是要求候选人使用自己偏好的 AI 编程工具构建一个应用程序，并由公司支付相关的 Token 费用。面试官随后会要求候选人复盘整个构建过程。

Clay Bavor expects that within the next two months, all interview stages at the company will include in-depth AI-native assessment components. Clay Bavor 预计，在未来两个月内，公司所有的面试环节都将包含深度的 AI 原生考核组件。

Claude Cowork announced yesterday that its AI work agent functionality will officially expand to mobile and web platforms, allowing users to initiate, monitor, and resume tasks on any device. Claude Cowork 昨日宣布，其 AI 工作智能体功能将正式扩展至移动端与网页端，用户今后可在任意设备上发起、监控和接续任务。Beta 测试目前已开始向 Max 计划用户分阶段推送，其他订阅层级将在未来数周内陆续跟进。

  • Task status synchronized across devices: Users can start a task on a desktop, then check its progress on a mobile phone, and retrieve the completed output on any device. 任务状态跨设备同步：用户可在桌面端启动任务，随后在手机端查看进度，并在任意设备上获取完成后的输出内容。
  • Background automatic execution capability: After the user closes the device, Claude can continue to advance tasks in the cloud, without the need for the local device to remain online. 后台自动执行能力：用户关闭设备后，Claude 可在云端持续推进任务，无需本地设备保持在线。

蚂蚁灵波发布 LingBot-Depth 2.0，训练数据扩至 1.5 亿规模

Ant Lingbo yesterday released the spatial perception model LingBot-Depth 2.0 and simultaneously open-sourced the visual foundation model LingBot-Vision. 蚂蚁灵波昨日发布空间感知模型 LingBot-Depth 2.0，并同步开源视觉基座模型 LingBot-Vision。

LingBot-Depth is designed for real-world depth completion, essentially enhancing robots' 3D perception capabilities in scenarios where traditional depth cameras often fail, such as with transparent, reflective, or dense objects. LingBot-Depth 面向真实场景深度补全，相当于为机器人补足透明、反光、密集物体等传统深度相机容易失效场景中的三维感知能力。

Compared to version 1.0, LingBot-Depth 2.0's training data has expanded from 3 million to 150 million, achieving first place in 12 out of 16 depth completion benchmarks; in indoor scenarios with large areas of missing depth, the depth error RMSE decreased from 0.132 to 0.062. 相比 1.0 版本，LingBot-Depth 2.0 的训练数据从 300 万扩展至 1.5 亿规模，在深度补全基准 16 项测评中获得 12 项第一；在室内大面积深度缺失场景中，深度误差 RMSE 从 0.132 降至 0.062。

LingBot-Vision uses 'boundary structure' as its pre-training objective and offers four open-source versions: ViT-G/L/B/S. LingBot-Vision 则以「边界结构」作为预训练目标，提供 ViT-G/L/B/S 四个开源版本。

港大开源 AI 智能体导师 DeepTutor v1.5

The HKU Data Intelligence Lab (HKUDS) recently released the open-source AI agent tutor DeepTutor v1.5, which features multiple upgrades in its document parsing engine, RAG ingestion pipeline, and Python 3.14+ compatibility. 港大数据智能实验室（HKUDS）近日发布了开源 AI 智能体导师 DeepTutor v1.5，该版本在文档解析引擎、RAG 摄取管线及 Python 3.14+ 兼容性上完成了多项升级。

DeepTutor's core design philosophy integrates six teaching stages—chatting, quizzing, research, visualization, problem-solving, and learning path planning—into a single agent operational loop. Students can switch between different tasks without interrupting the backend engine, and learning context and multi-level memory are automatically synchronized. DeepTutor 的核心设计思路是将聊天、测验、研究、可视化、解题与学习路径规划六个教学环节并入同一个智能体运行闭环，学生在不同任务间切换时无需中断后台引擎，学习上下文与多层级记忆会自动同步流转。

The system employs a hybrid personalization engine, combining Static Knowledge Graph (SKG) and Dynamic Learner Memory (DPM). Through a "tracking forest" mechanism, each interaction trajectory is consolidated into multi-resolution learner profiles, ensuring that every teaching conclusion provided by the AI can be traced back to specific textual evidence or error records. 系统采用混合个性化引擎，结合静态知识溯源（SKG）与动态学习者记忆（DPM），通过「追踪森林」机制将每次互动轨迹沉淀为多分辨率的学习者画像，使 AI 给出的每一步教学结论均可回溯至具体文本证据或错题记录。
