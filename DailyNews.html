Anthropic 与 SpaceX 达成算力协议，获超 22 万块英伟达 GPU

Early this morning, Anthropic announced a computing power cooperation agreement with SpaceX, which will use all the computing resources of the SpaceX Colossus 1 data center. According to the agreement, Anthropic will acquire over 300 megawatts of new computing power this month, involving more than 220,000 Nvidia GPUs. 今天凌晨，Anthropic 宣布与 SpaceX 达成算力合作协议，将使用 SpaceX Colossus 1 数据中心的全部算力资源。依据协议，Anthropic 将在本月内获得逾 300 兆瓦的新增算力，涉及超过 22 万块英伟达 GPU。

Anthropic stated that this batch of new computing power will be directly used to increase the service capacity for Claude Pro and Claude Max subscribers. Anthropic simultaneously announced adjustments to the usage restrictions for Claude Code and Claude API, effective immediately. Anthropic 表示，这批新增算力将直接用于提升 Claude Pro 和 Claude Max 订阅用户的服务容量。Anthropic 同步宣布对 Claude Code 及 Claude API 的使用限制进行调整，即日起生效。

  • Double the five-hour rate limit for Claude Code, applicable to Pro, Max, Team, and seat-based Enterprise plans; 将 Claude Code 的五小时速率限制提升一倍，适用于 Pro、Max、Team 及按席位计费的 Enterprise 方案；
  • Remove the Claude Code rate limit degradation mechanism for Pro and Max accounts during peak hours; 取消 Pro 和 Max 账户在高峰时段的 Claude Code 限速降级机制；
  • Significantly increase the API rate limits for Claude Opus series models. 大幅提高 Claude Opus 系列模型的 API 速率上限。

As an extension of this collaboration, Anthropic has also expressed interest in exploring cooperation with SpaceX to jointly develop multi-gigawatt orbital AI computing infrastructure, though specific plan details have not yet been disclosed. 作为此次合作的延伸，Anthropic 还表示有意与 SpaceX 探索合作，共同开发数吉瓦级的轨道 AI 算力基础设施，但目前尚未披露具体计划细节。

ChatGPT 广告平台扩容：自助投放上线，按点击付费正式引入

Yesterday, OpenAI officially announced the expansion of its ChatGPT advertising system, launching a beta version of a self-service ad management tool (Ads Manager) for US advertisers. It also introduced a cost-per-click (CPC) bidding model and conversion measurement tools, allowing businesses of more sizes to directly place ads within ChatGPT. 昨天，OpenAI 官宣扩展 ChatGPT 广告系统，推出面向美国广告主的自助广告管理工具测试版（Ads Manager），并引入按点击付费（CPC）竞价模式，同时引入转化衡量工具，允许更多规模的企业直接在 ChatGPT 中投放广告。

Regarding bidding models, ChatGPT ads previously only supported cost-per-mille (CPM) bidding. With the addition of CPC bidding, advertisers only pay when a user clicks on an ad, which helps link ad spending more directly to actual user behavior. OpenAI stated that more bidding and optimization goals will be supported in the future. 竞价模式方面，此前 ChatGPT 广告仅支持按千次展示付费（CPM）。新增 CPC 竞价后，广告主只需在用户点击广告时付费，有助于将广告支出与实际用户行为更直接地挂钩。OpenAI 表示，未来还将支持更多竞价与优化目标。

In terms of measurement tools, OpenAI has launched the Conversions API and pixel tracking features. Advertisers can use these to understand user behavior after clicking on an ad, such as purchases, registrations, or form submissions. However, the data obtained is aggregated and does not involve individual conversation content. 衡量工具方面，OpenAI 已上线 Conversions API 与像素追踪功能，广告主可据此了解用户点击广告后的后续行为，如购买、注册或提交表单等，但获取的是聚合数据，不涉及个人对话内容。

Regarding purchasing channels, advertisers can now register, set budgets and bids, upload ad creatives, manage campaign plans, and view performance data directly through Ads Manager, without needing to rely on intermediaries. 在购买渠道方面，广告主现可通过 Ads Manager 自行注册、设置预算与出价、上传广告素材、管理投放计划并查看效果数据，无需再依赖中间代理。

OpenAI emphasized in the announcement that ChatGPT's responses remain independent of ad content, and user conversations will not be shared with advertisers ; the advertising platform is currently in its early stages, and will continue to expand ad formats, targeting goals, and platform capabilities in the future. OpenAI 在公告中强调，ChatGPT 的回答与广告内容保持独立，用户对话不会被共享给广告主 ；目前广告平台仍处于早期阶段，后续将继续扩展广告形式、投放目标与平台能力。

三星宣布在中国大陆停售所有家电产品

Yesterday, Samsung (China) Investment Co., Ltd. officially issued a notice regarding adjustments to its home appliance business: "To cope with the rapidly changing market environment, after careful consideration, Samsung Electronics has decided to stop selling all home appliance products, including televisions and monitors, in the Chinese mainland market." 昨天，三星（中国）投资有限公司正式发布家电产品业务调整通知：「为应对急剧变化的市场环境，经慎重研究，三星电子决定在中国大陆市场停止销售含电视、显示器在内的所有家电产品。」

It is reported that the scope of this discontinuation covers a full range of home appliance products, including televisions, monitors, large commercial displays, air conditioners, refrigerators, washing machines, dryers, washer-dryer combos, garment care machines, audio systems, projectors, vacuum cleaners, and air purifiers. 据悉，此次停售范围涵盖电视、显示器、大型商用显示器、空调、冰箱、洗衣机、干衣机、洗衣干衣一体机、衣物护理机、音响、投影仪、吸尘器、空气净化器等全线家用电器产品。

For users who have already purchased Samsung home appliances, the company stated that it will continue to provide after-sales service strictly in accordance with the "Consumer Rights Protection Law" and national "Three Guarantees" regulations. Users encountering performance issues can go to an authorized service center for inspection and troubleshooting. 针对已购买三星家电产品的用户，公司表示仍将严格按照《消费者权益保护法》及国家三包规定继续提供售后服务，用户如遇性能故障，可通过授权服务中心进行检测排查。

This adjustment does not affect the mobile phone business; Samsung explicitly stated that "mobile phone products are being sold normally." After the adjustment, Samsung's consumer electronics products sold in the Chinese mainland market will primarily consist of mobile phones and storage products. 此次调整不影响手机业务，三星方面明确表示「手机产品正常销售」。调整完成后，三星在中国内地市场销售的消费电子产品将主要剩余手机及存储类产品。

苹果因 AI Siri 延迟赔偿 2.5 亿美元，每台 iPhone 最高可获赔 95 美元

On May 5, local time, Apple officially reached a $250 million settlement agreement regarding a class-action lawsuit for false advertising stemming from the delayed launch of the new Siri. 当地时间 5 月 5 日，苹果就因新版 Siri 延迟上线引发的虚假宣传集体诉讼正式达成 2.5 亿美元和解协议。

According to 9To5Mac, the $250 million settlement agreement covers owners of Apple Intelligence-supported devices purchased within the United States between June 10, 2024, and March 29, 2025, including the iPhone 15 Pro series and all iPhone 16 series (including 16e) models. 据 9To5Mac 报道，该项 2.5 亿美元的和解协议覆盖在 2024 年 6 月 10 日至 2025 年 3 月 29 日期间，于美国境内购买支持 Apple Intelligence 设备的机主，涵盖 iPhone 15 Pro 系列及全部 iPhone 16 系列（含 16e）机型。

Eligible single devices are expected to receive $25 in compensation, with the specific claim amount dynamically adjusted based on the actual number of applications submitted, up to a maximum payout of $95 per device. 符合条件的单台设备预计可获赔 25 美元，具体索赔金额将根据实际提交申请的人数进行动态调整，单设备最高赔付上限为 95 美元。

Apple did not admit to any wrongdoing in the settlement agreement and maintained that its business operations comply with relevant regulations. 苹果在和解协议中未承认任何不当行为，并坚称其商业运作符合相关法规。

An Apple official spokesperson responded that since the release of Apple Intelligence, dozens of features such as visual intelligence, real-time translation, and writing tools have been successively launched. This settlement aims to quickly resolve disputes surrounding "two features that were not launched as scheduled" to ensure the company focuses its core efforts on product innovation. 苹果官方发言人回应称，自 Apple Intelligence 发布以来，已相继推出视觉智能、实时翻译及写作工具等数十项功能，此次和解旨在快速解决围绕「两项未如期上线功能」的争议，以确保公司将核心精力集中于产品创新。

三星市值突破 1 万亿美元

According to The Wall Street Journal, Samsung Electronics' stock price surged 13% in early trading yesterday, with its market capitalization breaking through $1 trillion to reach approximately 1,530 trillion Korean Won (about $1.04 trillion), becoming the second listed company in Asia, after TSMC, to exceed a $1 trillion market capitalization. 据《华尔街日报》报道，三星电子股价昨日早盘大涨 13%，市值一举突破 1 万亿美元，达到约 1530 万亿韩元（约合 1.04 万亿美元），成为继台积电之后，亚洲第二家市值超过 1 万亿美元的上市公司。

Samsung's first-quarter financial report released last week showed that its revenue, net profit, and operating profit all hit record highs for the quarter, with the chip manufacturing division's operating profit accounting for over 90% of the company's total profit. Driven by Samsung's stock price, the Korea Composite Index surged 6.7% yesterday to 7402.18 points, breaking the 7000-point mark for the first time. 三星上周发布的一季度财报显示，该季度收入、净利润和营业利润均创历史新高，其中芯片制造部门营业利润占公司总利润的 90% 以上。受三星股价带动，韩国综合指数昨日大涨 6.7%，至 7402.18 点，首次突破 7000 点关口。

The report points out that the driving force behind Samsung's continuously rising market capitalization is the strong demand for memory chips for AI infrastructure construction. The new version adds four capabilities: TTS-2 的核心变化在于从单向文本转语音管线转向闭环实时对话架构：模型直接接收对话中的实际音频，从而感知用户的语气、节奏与情绪状态并作出相应调整。新版本新增四项能力：

  • Voice Direction: Describe the expression method using natural language, for example, "tired but gentle, like just coming home from work," and the model adjusts the voice style accordingly, no longer relying on fixed emotional tags; 语音指令（Voice Direction）：用自然语言描述表达方式，例如「疲惫但温柔，像刚下班回家」，模型据此调整语音风格，不再依赖固定情感标签；
