李想回应理想 L9 四年换代：汽车不是手机，安全验证无法提速

Li Xiang, CEO of Li Auto, publicly responded on Weibo yesterday to external doubts about the new Li Auto L9's four-year replacement cycle. 理想汽车 CEO 李想昨日在微博公开回应外界对全新理想 L9 换代周期长达四年的质疑。

The Li Auto L9 was first launched in June 2022, and by May this year, its replacement cycle is nearly four years, significantly longer than the iteration pace of consumer electronics. Li Xiang explained that there is a fundamental difference between automobiles and consumer electronic products like mobile phones. 理想 L9 于 2022 年 6 月首次上市，至今年 5 月换代周期接近四年，明显长于消费电子产品的迭代节奏。李想对此解释称，汽车与手机等消费电子产品存在本质区别。

Cars truly cannot achieve the same replacement speed as mobile phones; they concern the life safety of a family, and a large amount of verification work requires long periods of repeated testing and refinement. 汽车真做不到像手机那样的换代速度，它关乎一家人的生命安全，有大量验证工作需要经过长时间反复测试打磨。

Li Xiang also mentioned that although AI technology is currently developing rapidly, in the specific professional field of automotive testing and verification, the improvement in actual vehicle verification efficiency is still quite limited. 李想同时提到，尽管 AI 技术目前发展迅猛，但在汽车测试与验证这一具体专业领域，对实车验证效率的提升仍然相当有限。

This screen differs from early naked-eye 3D technology by adopting a solution that directly integrates a nanostructured holographic layer into the AMOLED stack, combined with eye-tracking and diffractive beam steering technology, to redirect light at precise angles to the user's eyes. 该屏幕与早期裸眼 3D 技术有所不同，采用在 AMOLED 堆叠中直接集成纳米结构全息层的方案，并结合眼球追踪与衍射光束转向技术，将光线以精准角度重定向至用户眼部。

With the accompanying patented algorithm, users do not need to wear special glasses; they only need to tilt the device to achieve 360-degree viewing around objects, creating a spatial depth illusion that appears to float above the screen. 通过配套的专利算法，用户无需佩戴特殊眼镜，仅需倾斜设备即可实现 360 度绕物查看，产生悬浮于屏幕上方的空间深度错觉。

Regarding display performance, the leak claims that the H1 display can maintain native 4K resolution when handling regular 2D tasks, with the holographic depth layer only activating for adapted content, thereby achieving "zero clarity loss" and avoiding the image quality degradation issues of traditional lenticular 3D screens. 在显示性能方面，爆料称 H1 显示屏在处理常规 2D 任务时可维持原生 4K 分辨率，全息深度层仅在适配内容下激活，以此实现「零清晰度损耗」，规避传统透镜式 3D 屏幕的画质折损问题。

Currently, the project is still in its first phase of research and development, and the full commercialization of holographic smartphones is expected around 2030. 目前，该项目仍处于研发第一阶段，全息智能手机的全面商用时间节点预计在 2030 年左右。

MiniMax 回应大模型不认识马嘉祺

MiniMax yesterday published a technical blog, disclosing the root cause of the M2 series large model "not recognizing Ma Jiaqi" and announcing that the fix has been completed. It is worth noting that the model can still accurately answer questions related to Ma Jiaqi; what it lost was merely the ability to "write out this character." MiniMax 昨日发布技术博客，披露 M2 系列大模型「不认识马嘉祺」的根因，并宣布已完成修复。值得注意的是，模型仍能准确回答马嘉祺的相关信息，丧失的只是「写出这个字」的能力。

The starting point of the problem was that the tokenizer merged "Jiaqi" into an independent token. The model learned it during the pre-training phase, but the post-training dialogue data contained fewer than 5 samples of "Jiaqi." This led to: 问题的起点是分词器将「嘉祺」合并为一个独立 token。模型在预训练阶段学会了它，但后训练的对话数据里包含「嘉祺」的样本不足 5 条。这导致：

  • High-frequency tokens (e.g., code symbols, tool_call markers) were repeatedly practiced during post-training, continuously updating the surrounding vector space; 高频 token（如代码符号、tool_call 标记）在后训练中反复被练习，持续更新周围的向量空间；
  • The low-frequency token "Jiaqi" was almost never practiced, and its weight vector was squeezed and shifted; 「嘉祺」这个低频 token 几乎从未被练到，权重向量被挤压偏移；
  • When the model outputted, it couldn't find "Jiaqi" and instead used phonetically similar alternatives like "Jiaqi" (佳琪) or "Qiqi" (琪琪). 模型输出时找不到「嘉祺」，转而用发音相近的「佳琪」「琪琪」替代。

MiniMax then performed a full scan of the complete vocabulary of about 200,000 tokens and found that about 4.9% of the tokens showed significant degradation. The degradation was particularly severe for Japanese (29.7%), far exceeding Chinese (3.9%), English (3.5%), Korean (3.3%), and Russian (3.7%). MiniMax 随后对约 20 万 token 的完整词表做了全量扫描，发现约 4.9% 的 token 存在显著退化。日语的退化尤为严重（29.7%），远超中文（3.9%）、英文（3.5%）、韩语（3.3%）和俄语（3.7%）。

This also explains another old problem: the model occasionally mixes Russian or Korean characters into Japanese conversations, the root cause being that Japanese token parameters drift and get confused with other languages in the vector space. 这也解释了另一个旧问题：模型在日语对话中偶尔混入俄语或韩语字符，根因同样是日语 token 参数漂移后在向量空间中与其他语言发生混淆。

The fix is to construct synthetic data covering the entire vocabulary and use simple repetition tasks to ensure every token is trained. After the fix, the proportion of Russian characters mixed into Japanese responses dropped from 47% to 1%, and the stability of output parameters across the entire vocabulary increased from a minimum of 0.329 to all above 0.97. 修复方案是构造覆盖全词表的合成数据，用简单的复读任务确保每个 token 都被练到。修复后，日语回答混入俄文字符的比例从 47% 降至 1%，全词表输出参数稳定度从最低 0.329 升至全部高于 0.97。

曝甲骨文裁员 2-3 万人，员工集体争取更高遣散费遭拒

According to TechCrunch, on March 31, Oracle laid off approximately 20,000 to 30,000 employees via email. The laid-off employees subsequently attempted collective bargaining for higher severance packages but were rejected. 据 TechCrunch 报道，3 月 31 日，甲骨文 (Oracle) 通过电子邮件裁减约 2 万至 3 万名员工。被裁员工随后尝试集体谈判争取更高遣散待遇，遭到拒绝。

The severance package offered by Oracle is: after signing a waiver agreement, employees will receive 4 weeks of salary for the first year, with an additional 1 week for each full year thereafter, up to a maximum of 26 weeks, plus 1 month of COBRA health insurance. 甲骨文提供的遣散方案为：签署免责协议后，员工可获得第一年 4 周工资，此后每满一年增加 1 周，上限 26 周，另附 1 个月 COBRA 医疗保险。

The core of the dispute lies in the handling of stock — the company did not accelerate the vesting of upcoming RSUs (Restricted Stock Units), and all unvested stock before the termination date was forfeited. According to Time, one long-term employee lost $1 million in stock as a result, just 4 months shy of vesting, with RSUs accounting for approximately 70% of their total compensation. 争议核心在于股票处理 —— 公司未对即将归属的 RSU（限制性股票单位）予以加速，解雇日前未归属股票一律没收。据《时代》报道，一名长期员工因此损失 100 万美元股票，距归属仅差 4 个月，RSU 约占其总薪酬的 70%。

In contrast, Meta's severance package starts with 16 weeks of base salary, plus an additional 2 weeks per year, and covers 18 months of COBRA insurance; Cloudflare, on the other hand, provides salary until the end of this year and accelerates stock vesting. 相比之下，Meta 遣散方案起点为 16 周基本工资，另加每年 2 周，并覆盖 18 个月 COBRA 保险；Cloudflare 则提供薪资至今年年底并加速股票归属。

At least 90 laid-off employees signed a petition demanding Oracle match industry standards, but according to an email obtained by TechCrunch, Oracle refused to negotiate, offering only "accept" or "forfeit" as options. 至少 90 名被裁员工签署请愿书，要求甲骨文对标行业水平，但据 TechCrunch 获悉的邮件，甲骨文拒绝谈判，只给出「接受」或「放弃」两个选项。

💡 Anthropic 联创：AI 对就业的冲击被高估了

According to The Stanford Daily, Daniela Amodei, co-founder and president of Anthropic, stated yesterday at the Stanford Graduate School of Business's "View From The Top" speaker series that public concerns about AI's impact on the job market are significantly exaggerated. 据《斯坦福日报》报道，Anthropic 联合创始人兼总裁 Daniela Amodei 昨日在斯坦福大学商学院「View From The Top」系列演讲活动中表示，外界对 AI 冲击就业市场的担忧存在明显夸大。

Daniela stated that, according to Anthropic's continuously tracked economic indicators, AI currently appears more as "skill complementarity" than "job replacement." She pointed out that AI is changing the nature of work rather than simply eliminating jobs themselves, with the customer service sector being one of the few industries currently showing a clear replacement effect. Daniela 表示，根据 Anthropic 持续跟踪的经济指数，AI 目前呈现出的形态更接近「技能互补」而非「岗位替代」。她指出，AI 正在改变工作的性质，而非简单消灭工作本身，客服领域是目前少数确实出现明显替代效应的行业之一。

Taking software development as an example, she predicted that the engineering profession would not disappear, but the focus of work would shift from writing code to communication and collaboration with product managers and clients, i.e., aspects that AI is not good at handling. 以软件开发为例，她预测工程师这一职业不会消失，但工作重心将从编写代码转向与产品经理、客户的沟通协作，即那些 AI 不擅长处理的环节。

Daniela also proposed the concept of "comparative advantage shift." She believes that in a future where AI can handle more routine productive tasks, human advantages will lean towards empathy, social judgment, and interpersonal communication. She illustrated this with the healthcare industry: Daniela 同时提出了「比较优势位移」的概念。她认为，在 AI 能够承担更多日常生产性工作的未来，人类的优势将向同理心、社会判断力和人际沟通倾斜。她以医疗行业为例说明：

AI might surpass doctors in diagnostic accuracy, but the warmth of "bedside care" and the clinical effects brought by patient trust are something AI cannot replicate. AI 或许能在诊断准确率上超越医生，但「床边照护」的温度与患者信任关系所带来的临床效果，是 AI 无法复制的。

买书不读指南｜《语言人》以 40 年前的判断回应 AI 时代语言焦虑

"Man of Language: On the Contribution of Linguistics to the Humanities" is a representative work by Claude Hagège, Professor of Theoretical Linguistics at the Collège de France. The original work, "L'Homme de paroles," was first published in 1985. 《语言人：论语言学对人文科学的贡献》是法兰西学院理论语言学讲座教授克洛德·海然热（Claude Hagège）的代表作，原著《L’Homme de paroles》首版于 1985 年。

The entire book is structured around the central theme of "the contribution of linguistics to the humanities," divided into three parts: upper, middle, and lower. Hagège's core judgment directly addresses the language anxiety of the AI era: exponentially growing industrial technology will not threaten the rich and subtle expressive ways of various languages, because human language expression is not essentially directly related to technological progress. 全书以「语言学对人文科学的贡献」为核心命题，分上、中、下三编展开。海然热的核心判断直接回应了 AI 时代的语言焦虑：成倍增长的工业技术不会威胁各语言丰富细腻的表达方式，因为人类语言表达与科技进步本质上并无直接关联。

He substantiates this with examples of multilingual expressions for "boredom": Dutch says "the millstone is around the neck," Hungarian says "trying to break free from the elbow," and Moroccan Arabic says "catching flies," intending to illustrate that the subtlety of language continues to flourish in its respective cultural soil, rather than tending towards homogenization. 他以多语言的「厌烦」表达为例加以佐证：荷兰语说「碾盘套住了脖子」，匈牙利语说「尽力挣脱胳膊肘」，摩洛哥阿拉伯语说「捕捉苍蝇」，意在说明语言的细腻性正在各自的文化土壤中持续蓬勃发展，而非趋向同质化。
