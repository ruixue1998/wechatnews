把员工变成 skill 后，Meta 又盯上了他们的鼠标和键盘

According to CNBC, Meta is using an internal tracking tool called Model Capability Initiative (MCI) to record employees' keyboard strokes and mouse clicks on hundreds of websites and applications like Google, LinkedIn, and Wikipedia, as a data source for training AI models. 据 CNBC 报道，Meta 正在通过一款名为 Model Capability Initiative（MCI，模型能力计划）的内部追踪工具，记录员工在 Google、LinkedIn、Wikipedia 等数百个网站和应用上的键盘敲击与鼠标点击，作为训练 AI 模型的数据来源。

An internal memo from members of Meta's Superintelligence Lab (MSL) shows that MCI's monitoring scope also covers third-party platforms such as GitHub, Slack, and Atlassian, with Meta's own Threads and Manus also on the list; earlier versions of the list included OpenAI's ChatGPT and Anthropic's Claude. Meta 超级智能实验室（MSL）成员发出的内部备忘录显示，MCI 的监控范围还涵盖 GitHub、Slack 以及 Atlassian 等第三方平台，Meta 自家的 Threads 和 Manus 也在名单之列，清单早期版本曾包含 OpenAI 的 ChatGPT 和 Anthropic 的 Claude。

A Meta spokesperson confirmed the project to the media, responding that building agents to help people complete daily tasks on computers requires real human usage examples, "such as mouse movements, button clicks, and dropdown menu operations," and emphasized that the system has protective measures in place for sensitive content, and data will not be used for other purposes. Meta 发言人向媒体证实了该项目，回应称构建帮助人们用电脑完成日常任务的智能体需要真实的人类使用范例，「比如鼠标移动、按钮点击和下拉菜单操作」，并强调系统已设置保护敏感内容的防护措施，数据不会用于其他用途。

MSL further explained in the memo that to "teach models to use computers," Meta needs "large and unbiased" datasets, and the new tool can only see what is displayed on employees' screens, "it will not read files or attachments," and suggested that "employees who still have concerns can control what is displayed on their screens by not handling private matters on their work computers." MSL 在备忘录中进一步解释，为「教会模型使用电脑」，Meta 需要「庞大且无偏见」的数据集，新工具仅能看到员工屏幕上显示的内容，「不会读取文件或附件」，并建议「仍有顾虑的员工可以通过不在工作电脑上处理私人事务来控制屏幕显示内容」。

However, multiple Meta employees described the project as "dystopian" in internal messages, fearing that MCI could expose user passwords, new product development details, and personal information such as employees' immigration status, health conditions, and family members. According to Business Insider, the tracking program does not offer employees an opt-out option. 然而，多名 Meta 员工在内部消息中将该项目形容为「反乌托邦」，担忧 MCI 会暴露用户密码、新产品研发细节，以及员工的移民身份、健康状况和家庭成员等个人信息。而据《商业内幕》报道，该追踪计划并未向员工提供退出选项。

库克谈任期内「首个重大错误」：苹果地图翻车，Apple Watch 封神

According to Bloomberg, more details have been released from Apple CEO Tim Cook's recent speech at an all-hands meeting. 据彭博社报道，苹果 CEO Tim Cook 日前在全员大会上的讲话有更多细节释出。

He reflected on the successes and failures of his nearly 15-year tenure as CEO, calling the rushed launch of Apple Maps in 2012 his "first truly big mistake," while listing the Apple Watch as his proudest achievement. 他回顾了自己近 15 年 CEO 任期内的得失，将 2012 年苹果地图的仓促上线称为「首个真正的大错误」，同时将 Apple Watch 列为自己最引以为豪的成就。

Cook stated at the conference that Apple Maps was "not fully mature" at the time, and the team mistakenly believed the product was ready because testing was primarily focused on local scenarios, and it was launched to the market without adequate preparation. 库克在大会上表示，苹果地图当时「还没有完全成熟」，团队误以为产品已准备就绪，原因在于测试内容主要集中于本地相关场景，并未做好充分准备便将其推向市场。

Apple Maps was officially released with iOS 6 in 2012, aiming to replace Google Maps, which had been a long-standing partner. However, it drew widespread criticism due to numerous functional flaws—incorrect route guidance, wrongly marked landmarks, town location deviations, and even cloud cover appearing in 3D street view maps were successively exposed, becoming a landmark "flop" event in the tech industry that year. 苹果地图于 2012 年随 iOS 6 正式发布，旨在取代此前合作多年的 Google 地图，却因大量功能缺陷引发广泛批评——错误的路线指引、标注有误的地标、城镇位置偏差乃至 3D 实景地图出现云层遮挡等问题接连曝出，成为当年科技圈的标志性翻车事件。

Cook admitted that his list of mistakes was "surprisingly long," but he also pointed out that Apple has largely avoided the frequent large-scale product recalls and cancellations seen in other consumer electronics companies over the past 15 years, and the Apple Maps blunder "ultimately became a valuable experience." 库克坦言，自己所犯的错误清单「长得惊人」，但他也指出，苹果在过去 15 年间基本上避开了其他消费电子公司频繁出现的大规模产品召回与取消事件，苹果地图的失误「最终成为了一次宝贵的经验」。

When asked about his proudest moment, Cook mentioned the Apple Watch. He said he was "deeply moved" the first time he received a letter from a user stating that the Apple Watch had saved their life. 在被问及最自豪的时刻时，库克提到了 Apple Watch。他表示，第一次收到用户来信称 Apple Watch 挽救了其生命时，自己「深受触动」。

Google 发布第八代 TPU：博通做训练，联发科做推理

On Wednesday local time, Google officially released its eighth-generation TPU at the annual Google Cloud Next conference. 当地时间周三，Google 在 Google Cloud Next 年度大会上，正式发布了第八代 TPU。

Notably, Google has split the TPU into two this time, introducing the TPU 8t optimized for training and the TPU 8i designed for inference. This marks the first time in Google's history that two types of workloads have been separated into independent chips. 值得一提的是，Google 此次将 TPU 一拆为二，推出专为训练优化的 TPU 8t 与专为推理设计的 TPU 8i，这也是谷歌历史上首次将两类工作负载拆分至独立芯片。

TPU 8t (training chip, Google x Broadcom co-designed) TPU 8t （训练芯片，谷歌 x 博通联合设计）

  • Single chip specifications: 216 GB HBM, 6.5 TB/s memory bandwidth, 128 MB on-chip SRAM, up to 12.6 petaFLOPS FP4 computing power; 单颗规格：216 GB HBM、6.5 TB/s 内存带宽、128 MB 片上 SRAM、最高 12.6 petaFLOPS FP4 算力；
  • A single Pod can interconnect up to 9600 chips, equipped with 2 PB shared HBM, with an overall computing power of 121 exaflops (FP4), an increase of approximately 3 times compared to the previous generation Ironwood, and a 2.8 times performance improvement at the same price; 单 Pod 最多互联 9600 块芯片，配备 2 PB 共享 HBM，整体算力达 121 exaflops（FP4），较上代 Ironwood 提升约 3 倍，同等价格下性能提升 2.8 倍；
  • With OCS optical circuit switching and Virgo network, a single cluster can scale to over a million chips; the Managed Lustre storage system provides 10 TB/s aggregate throughput; and the effective computing time (goodput) reaches 97%. 借助 OCS 光路电路交换与 Virgo 网络，单集群可扩展至逾百万块芯片；Managed Lustre 存储系统提供 10 TB/s 聚合吞吐；有效计算时间（goodput）达 97%。

TPU 8i (inference chip, co-designed by Google x MediaTek) TPU 8i （推理芯片，谷歌 x 联发科联合设计）

  • Single chip specifications: 288 GB HBM, 8.6 TB/s memory bandwidth, 384 MB on-chip SRAM (3 times that of the previous generation), 10.1 petaFLOPS FP4 computing power; 单颗规格：288 GB HBM、8.6 TB/s 内存带宽、384 MB 片上 SRAM（是上代 3 倍）、10.1 petaFLOPS FP4 算力；
  • A single Pod expands to 1152 chips, providing 11.6 exaflops FP8 computing power, with an 80% performance increase compared to Ironwood at the same price, and a 117% increase in performance per watt; 单 Pod 扩展至 1152 块芯片，提供 11.6 exaflops FP8 算力，同等价格下性能较 Ironwood 提升 80%，每瓦性能提升 117%；
  • Adopting Boardfly network topology, the maximum number of inter-chip communication hops is compressed from 16 to 7; the Collective Acceleration Engine (CAE) reduces collective communication latency by five times, which is particularly critical for MoE architecture models. 采用 Boardfly 网络拓扑，最大芯片间通信跳数从 16 跳压缩至 7 跳；集合加速引擎（CAE）将集合通信延迟降低五倍，对 MoE 架构模型尤为关键。

Both chips adopt TSMC's 2nm process, are equipped with Google's self-developed Arm-architecture Axion CPU, completely moving away from x86, and are supported by fourth-generation liquid cooling technology for heat dissipation. They are planned to officially launch on Google Cloud Platform later this year, with full mass production targeted for the end of next year, supporting mainstream frameworks such as JAX, PyTorch, Keras, and vLLM. 两款芯片均采用台积电 2nm 制程，搭载谷歌自研 Arm 架构 Axion CPU，彻底告别 x86，并由第四代液冷技术提供散热支持，计划于今年晚些时候在 Google Cloud Platform 正式上线，完整量产目标定于明年底，支持 JAX、PyTorch、Keras 及 vLLM 等主流框架。

After a user executes the /ultrareview command in the CLI, the system will launch a set of parallel review agents in a remote sandbox to perform a comprehensive bug scan of the current branch or Pull Request. 该功能基于 Claude Code Web 基础设施运行，用户在 CLI 中执行 /ultrareview 指令后，系统会在远程沙箱中启动一组并行的审查 Agent，对当前分支或 Pull Request 进行全面的 Bug 扫描。
