「近乎免费」的芯片快用完了，苹果要为 MacBook Neo 补货

According to Culpium, Apple is negotiating with suppliers regarding follow-up production issues for the MacBook Neo. 据 Culpium 报道，苹果正与供应商就 MacBook Neo 的后续生产问题展开谈判。

Columnist Tim Culpan recently revealed that sales of this entry-level MacBook have significantly exceeded expectations, and Apple is currently facing a tricky dilemma: whether to increase stock to meet strong demand, or let component inventory run out and cease production. 专栏作者 Tim Culpan 近日透露，这款入门 MacBook 的销量已大幅超出预期，苹果目前面临一个棘手的抉择：是追加备货以满足旺盛需求，还是任由零部件库存耗尽、就此收手。

The report points out that the A18 Pro used in the MacBook Neo is not a newly produced chip, but rather inventory left over from the iPhone 16 Pro mass production cycle. However, MacBook Neo sales have far exceeded expectations, and the inventory of these downgraded chips is being rapidly depleted, with a supply gap becoming increasingly prominent. 报道指出，MacBook Neo 所使用的 A18 Pro 并非全新生产的芯片，而是 iPhone 16 Pro 量产周期中遗留的库存。然而，MacBook Neo 的销量远超预期，这批降级芯片的库存正在快速消耗，供给缺口日益凸显。

Insiders revealed that Apple originally planned to launch the next-generation Neo with downgraded A19 Pro chips next year, and had controlled the total production of this first Neo to approximately 5 to 6 million units. Currently, Apple's contract manufacturers Wistron (Quanta) and Foxconn are both uncertain about the direction of subsequent orders. 知情人士透露，苹果原本计划明年推出搭载 A19 Pro 降级芯片的下一代 Neo，并将此次首款 Neo 的总产量控制在约 500 万至 600 万台。目前，苹果的代工厂纬创（Quanta）与富士康（Foxconn）均对后续订单走向感到不确定。

Culpium's analysis suggests that if Apple decides to order an additional 10,000 to 30,000 A18 Pro wafers from TSMC (corresponding to approximately 2.3 million to 7 million chips), it would likely need to increase the price of the MacBook Neo and potentially discontinue the 256GB entry-level model (currently priced at $599), retaining only the 512GB version ($699). Culpium 分析认为，若苹果决定追加向台积电订购 1 万至 3 万片 A18 Pro 晶圆（对应约 230 万至 700 万颗芯片），大概率需要上调 MacBook Neo 售价，并可能停售 256GB 入门款（现售价 599 美元），仅保留 512GB 版本（699 美元）。

ChatGPT 推出 100 美元订阅，硬刚 Claude

Early this morning, OpenAI officially launched a new ChatGPT Pro subscription tier, with a monthly fee of $100, designed for developers who heavily use Codex. 今天凌晨，OpenAI 正式推出全新 ChatGPT Pro 订阅档位，月费为 100 美元，专为重度使用 Codex 的开发者设计。

The new $100 plan offers 5 times the Codex usage of the $20 Plus tier, comes with all Pro features and exclusive model access, and unlocks Instant and Thinking models' unlimited use . 新的 100 美元套餐提供比 20 美元 Plus 档位高出 5 倍的 Codex 使用量，并附带所有 Pro 功能及专属模型访问权限，同时解锁 Instant 和 Thinking 模型的 无限制使用 。

The original $200 Pro plan has not been discontinued and remains available for users with higher demands. The core functions of both Pro plans are the same; the only difference is the usage limit: the $100 tier offers 5 times that of Plus, while the $200 tier offers 20 times that of Plus. 原有 200 美元 Pro 套餐并未下线，继续面向更高需求用户开放。两档 Pro 套餐核心功能相同，区别仅在于使用量上限：100 美元档为 Plus 的 5 倍，200 美元档则为 Plus 的 20 倍。

The introduction of the $100 middle tier clearly signals a direct competitive intent. Neighboring Claude Max 5x and Max 20x tiers are priced at $100 and $200 per month, respectively. OpenAI's new tier precisely matches Anthropic's Max 5x plan in price. 此次推出 100 美元中间档，正面竞争意图明显。隔壁 Claude Max 5x 和 Max 20x 两档月费分别为 100 美元和 200 美元。OpenAI 此次新档位在价格上与 Anthropic 的 Max 5x 套餐精准对标。

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman revealed that Codex currently has over 3 million weekly active users, a 5-fold increase in three months, with a month-over-month growth rate of 70%. He also announced that for every 1 million new users added to the platform, the usage limit will be reset until the user base reaches 10 million . OpenAI CEO 山姆·奥特曼披露，Codex 目前已拥有超过 300 万周活跃用户，三个月内增长了 5 倍，月环比增速达到 70%。 他还宣布，平台每新增 100 万用户，使用限额就会重置一次，直至用户规模达到 1000 万 。

NASA「阿耳忒弥斯 2 号」启程返航，带回有史以来最清晰月球照片

According to Xinhua News Agency, NASA's Artemis II crewed lunar flyby mission's Orion spacecraft departed the moon's gravitational influence at 1:23 PM EDT on April 7 (1:23 AM Beijing time on April 8), officially beginning its journey back to Earth. 据新华社报道，NASA「阿耳忒弥斯 2 号」载人绕月飞行任务的「猎户座」飞船已于美东时间 4 月 7 日 13 时 23 分（北京时间 4 月 8 日 1 时 23 分）飞离月球引力影响范围，正式踏上返回地球的旅程。

The spacecraft is expected to splash down off the coast of San Diego, California, on the evening of April 10, completing its 10-day lunar flyby. 飞船预计于 4 月 10 日晚在加利福尼亚州圣迭戈海岸附近溅落，届时将完成这趟为期 10 天的绕月之旅。

During the mission, NASA released a batch of high-resolution images taken by the spacecraft during its lunar flyby on April 6, including two stunning sets of "Earthrise" and "Solar Eclipse" visuals. 任务期间，NASA 公布了飞船于 4 月 6 日绕月飞行时拍摄的一批高分辨率影像，涵盖「地落」与「日食」两组震撼画面。

Among them, the "Earthrise" photo taken from the far side of the moon clearly shows the Earth slowly sinking below the lunar horizon; the "Solar Eclipse" image records the historic moment when the moon completely blocked the sun, forming a total solar eclipse lasting nearly 54 minutes. 其中，从月球背面拍摄的「地落」照片清晰呈现了地球缓缓沉入月球地平线以下的景象；「日食」画面则记录了月球完全遮挡太阳、形成长达近 54 分钟日全食的历史瞬间。

NASA stated that these images are the clearest photos of the lunar surface and Earth-Moon framed together in history, reminiscent of the classic 'Earthrise' photo taken by 'Apollo 8' astronauts 58 years ago. The White House subsequently reposted the relevant content on social media, commenting: 'Humanity, gazing from the other side.' NASA 表示，这批影像是有史以来最清晰的月球表面及地月同框照片，令人联想起 58 年前「阿波罗 8 号」宇航员拍摄的经典「地升」照片。白宫随后在社交媒体转发相关内容，并留言：「人类，从另一侧凝望。」

微信公众号更新「非真人创作」新规，违规最高封号

According to Sina Tech, WeChat Official Accounts recently updated its 'Non-human Automated Creation Behavior' rule (Article 3.27), explicitly stipulating that official accounts and service accounts must not use AI, scripts, APIs, or other automated methods to replace real people in completing content creation, publishing, and other processes. 据新浪科技报道，微信公众号近日更新了「非真人自动化创作行为」规则（第 3.27 条），明确规定公众号和服务号不得利用 AI、脚本、接口或其他自动化方式替代真人完成内容创作、发布等流程。

According to the new rule, violations cover three categories: content generated, rewritten, spliced, or moved using AI or similar methods, deviating from real creators' expression; batch and continuous content publishing through automated methods such as scripts or program hosting; and disseminating tutorials, methods, or services for non-human automated creation. 根据新规，违规行为涵盖三类情形：脱离真实创作者表达、利用 AI 等方式生成、改写、拼接或搬运内容；通过脚本、程序托管等自动化方式批量、连续发布内容；以及传播非真人自动化创作的教程、方法或服务。

Regarding penalties, WeChat stated that it will take measures such as traffic restrictions and deletion for relevant content based on the severity of the violation, and may impose capability restrictions or even account bans. 在处罚层面，微信表示将根据违规程度对相关内容采取流量限制、删除等措施，对账号则可采取能力限制乃至封禁。

According to The Paper, the WeChat team responded to reporters, stating that the platform encourages creators to reasonably use tools to assist creation and improve efficiency, but opposes behavior where automated programs completely replace real people in content production. 另据澎湃新闻，微信团队向新闻记者回应称，平台鼓励创作者合理使用工具辅助创作、提升创作效率，但反对完全由自动化程序替代真人完成内容生产的行为。

IDC：联想 Q1 全球 PC 市占率升至 25.2%，增速是市场均值 3.4 倍

Yesterday, market research firm IDC released preliminary tracking data for the global PC market in the first quarter of 2026. 昨天，市场调研机构 IDC 发布了 2026 年第一季度全球 PC 市场初步追踪数据。

The data shows that global traditional PC shipments reached 65.6 million units in Q1 this year, a year-on-year increase of 2.5%, significantly higher than IDC's forecast of 63.6 million units (a year-on-year decrease of 0.6%) in February this year. This was primarily driven by three factors: expectations of component price increases, demand for Windows 10 migration, and the concentrated launch of new products. 数据显示，今年 Q1 全球传统 PC 出货量达 6560 万台，同比增长 2.5%，显著高于 IDC 今年 2 月预测的 6360 万台（同比 -0.6%），主要由零部件涨价预期、Windows 10 迁移需求及新品集中上市三重因素拉动。

Among them, Lenovo firmly held the global top spot with a 25.2% market share, shipping 16.5 million units, an 8.6% year-on-year increase, which is 3.4 times the market's average growth rate. Compared to the same period last year, Lenovo's market share increased by 1.4 percentage points, making it the enterprise with the largest share increase among the top five manufacturers. 其中，联想以 25.2% 的市场份额稳居全球第一，出货量达 1650 万台，同比增长 8.6%，是市场平均增速的 3.4 倍。与去年同期相比，联想市占率提升了 1.4 个百分点，是前五大厂商中份额增幅最大的企业。

In contrast, HP's shipments declined by 4.9% year-on-year, while Dell's increased by 7.7%. The combined market share of the top three manufacturers reached 59.4%, a year-on-year increase of 0.7 percentage points, further intensifying the industry's trend towards consolidation. 相比之下，惠普出货量同比下滑 4.9%，戴尔同比增长 7.7%。前三大厂商合计市场份额达 59.4%，同比提升 0.7 个百分点，行业头部集中趋势进一步加剧。

Sources revealed that the preliminary planning for these layoffs had actually begun before D'Amaro officially took office. 此次裁员的重点将集中在公司近期完成整合的营销部门。今年 1 月，迪士尼首次将娱乐、体验和体育三大业务板块的营销职能合并，由首席营销官阿萨德·阿亚兹（Asad Ayaz）统一管理。知情人士透露，此次裁员的前期规划实际上在戴明哲正式接任前便已启动。

💡 Google CEO：内存已经成为当前 AI 基础设施中「最关键的要素之一」

Google CEO Sundar Pichai stated frankly in a recent public interview that the physical bottlenecks in computing power and supply chain will be key factors limiting the speed of AI expansion this year and next. Google CEO 桑达尔·皮查伊（Sundar Pichai）在一场最新公开访谈中直言，算力与供应链的物理瓶颈将成为今年及明年限制 AI 扩张速度的关键因素。

皮查伊表示，尽管 Google 今年的资本支出规模预计在 1750 亿至 1850 亿美元之间，但「钱并不能解决所有问题」。

他指出，即便企业愿意将资本支出扩大至 4000 亿美元，也未必能够真正投入使用，「因为内存不够，电力也不够」。

在他看来，内存已经成为当前 AI 基础设施中「最关键的要素之一」 ，而领先的内存厂商在短期内几乎不可能实现显著扩产，这使得供给约束在未来一到两年内难以缓解。

另据 PC Gamer 报道，戴尔科技集团 CEO 迈克尔·戴尔（Michael Dell）近日出席美国银行「顶级 CEO 视角」系列访谈活动时预测称，到 2028 年，全球 AI 加速器领域的总内存需求将达到 2022 年的 625 倍。

戴尔指出，目前全球仅有海力士、三星和美光三家企业具备 HBM4 量产能力，而新建一座 DRAM 晶圆厂从规划到投产需耗时约 4 年。2023 年恰逢存储行业周期低点，三大原厂因大幅亏损暂停扩产，直接导致后续产能储备严重不足。
