Counterpoint：二季度中国手机出货量下降 2%，华为份额升至 23%

Preliminary market monitoring data released yesterday by Counterpoint Research shows that China's smartphone shipments in Q2 2026 decreased by 2% year-on-year, and by 3% year-on-year in the first half. Continuously rising storage costs have prompted manufacturers to reduce production of low-end models, shift resources to higher-margin products, and initiate a second round of terminal price increases. Counterpoint Research 昨日发布的初步市场监测数据显示，2026 年第二季度中国智能手机出货量同比下降 2%，上半年同比下降 3%。持续上涨的存储成本促使厂商减少低端机型生产，把资源转向利润率更高的产品，并启动第二轮终端售价上调。

Huawei maintained its top position with a 23% market share, reaching a new high since Q4 2020, with shipments growing 24% year-on-year. Counterpoint attributed the growth to the performance of the Enjoy 90 Pro Max, Pura X Max, and the overall product portfolio. 华为以 23% 的市场份额保持第一，创 2020 年第四季度以来新高，出货量同比增长 24%。Counterpoint 将增长归因于畅享 90 Pro Max、Pura X Max 和整体产品组合表现。

Apple's shipments increased by 23% year-on-year; after widespread price increases in the Android camp, Apple's relatively stable pricing narrowed the price gap with high-end Android models, and some consumers' expectations of price increases in Q3 also released demand ahead of schedule. 苹果出货量同比增长 23%；安卓阵营普遍提价后，苹果相对稳定的定价缩小了与高端安卓机型的价差，部分消费者对第三季度涨价的预期也提前释放了需求。

OPPO, vivo, Xiaomi, and Honor primarily relied on new products such as Reno 16, S60, Redmi K90, and Magic V6 to maintain their presence in the mass and high-end markets. OPPO、vivo、小米和荣耀主要依靠 Reno 16、S60、红米 K90、Magic V6 等新品维持大众和高端市场。

Institutions expect that manufacturers can still digest the storage inventory purchased at the end of last year and Q1 this year, but new phones adopting higher-cost memory will be concentrated on the market in the second half, and price increase pressure may continue to suppress replacement demand. 机构预计，厂商在上半年仍能消化去年底和今年一季度采购的存储库存，但采用更高成本内存的新机将在下半年集中上市，涨价压力可能继续抑制换机需求。

苹果在天才吧测试 AI 服务记录系统 Live Notes

According to Bloomberg, Apple is testing Live Notes in some retail stores. With the consent of both employees and customers, the system records the Genius Bar service process, uses AI to transcribe and generate summaries, and then saves the employee-edited text to an internal system; original recordings are not retained, and managers cannot see the transcribed content. 据彭博社报道，苹果在部分零售店测试 Live Notes。员工与顾客双方同意后，系统会录制天才吧服务过程，用 AI 转写并生成摘要，再把经员工编辑的文本保存到内部系统；原始录音不会留存，管理人员也看不到转写内容。

Apple hopes employees spend less time handwriting notes during repairs and consultations. Some retail employees are concerned that these materials might later be used for coaching or performance reviews. Live Notes is not currently mandatory, and Apple has not decided whether to expand its use to more stores or change the purpose of the recordings. 苹果希望员工在维修和咨询时少花时间手写记录。部分零售员工担心，这些材料以后可能被用于辅导或绩效考核。Live Notes 目前不是强制项目，苹果尚未决定是否扩大门店范围或改变记录用途。

Employees can modify transcribed text and summaries before saving, and customers can also refuse to participate. The current pilot separates recording, transcription, editing, and internal archiving into different stages, with store managers only accessing the final service record. 员工可以在保存前修改转写文本与摘要，顾客也可以拒绝参与。当前试点把录音、转写、编辑和内部存档分成不同环节，门店管理者只接触最终服务记录。

靳玉志：华为乾崑继续做汽车行业的「电子螺丝钉」

Jin Yuzhi, Huawei Senior Vice President and CEO of Qiankun, stated in an interview with 36Kr Auto that Huawei Qiankun will not operate as an independent automotive brand, but will continue to provide platform capabilities such as intelligent driving, cockpit, and vehicle control. He referred to this positioning as the 'electronic screw' of the automotive industry: jointly defining products with car manufacturers, but not replacing them in brand operation. 华为高级副总裁、引望 CEO 靳玉志接受 36 氪汽车采访时表示，华为乾崑不会把业务做成独立汽车品牌，而是继续提供智驾、座舱和车控等平台能力。他把这种定位称为汽车行业的「电子螺丝钉」：与车企共同定义产品，但不替代车企经营品牌。

He stated that the 'Jie' series primarily covers the market above 300,000 yuan, while the 'Jing' series leans towards below 300,000 yuan, with both corresponding to different price segments and product forms. Huawei Qiankun has currently partnered with multiple car manufacturers, with over 50 related models. 他称，「界」系列主要覆盖 30 万元以上市场，「境」系列更偏向 30 万元以下，两者对应不同价格带和产品形态。华为乾崑目前已与多家车企合作，相关车型超过 50 款。

Jin Yuzhi estimated that fewer than 10% of users in China truly experience and continuously use assisted driving, so the company plans to lower the trial threshold through experience centers. He believes that L2 safety levels can approach 70% of manual driving, L3 needs to achieve about a tenfold safety gain, and L4 needs to further increase to a hundredfold level. 靳玉志估计，国内真正体验并持续使用辅助驾驶的用户仍不到 10%，因此公司准备通过体验中心降低试用门槛。他认为 L2 的安全水平可以接近人工驾驶的 70%，L3 需要达到约十倍安全增益，L4 则要继续提高到百倍级别。

Regarding investment, he stated that Huawei and Qiankun's cumulative investment in intelligent automotive solutions has exceeded 100 billion yuan, with a R&D budget of approximately 18 billion yuan in 2026, of which 7-8 billion yuan is for computing power; computing power investment will remain at approximately 7-8 billion yuan annually for the next five years. 投入方面，他称华为和引望在智能汽车解决方案上的累计投入已超过 1000 亿元，2026 年研发预算约 180 亿元，其中算力投入 70 亿至 80 亿元；未来五年算力投入仍将保持在每年约 70 亿至 80 亿元。

Regarding the relationship between L3 and L4, Jin Yuzhi believes they are different stages where responsibility gradually shifts from the driver to the system provider. Consumer-facing L4 cannot rely on fixed operating areas, so it needs to first complete regulatory, product, and user adaptation, and then expand from closed parks and low-speed scenarios to open roads. 对于 L3 与 L4 的关系，靳玉志认为两者是责任逐步从驾驶员转向系统提供方的不同阶段。面向消费者的 L4 不能依赖固定运营区域，因此需要先完成法规、产品和用户适应，再从封闭园区、低速场景向公开道路扩展。

工信部：我国智能算力规模达到 2185 EFLOPS

The Ministry of Industry and Information Technology disclosed yesterday at a State Council Information Office press conference that China's intelligent computing power scale has reached 2185 EFLOPS (FP16), with an overall utilization rate of 71.4%; more than 70 high-speed computing network channels have been built nationwide, and network performance has improved by about 10% compared to before. 工业和信息化部昨日在国新办发布会上披露，我国智能算力规模已达到 2185 EFLOPS（FP16），整体利用率为 71.4%；全国已建成 70 多条算力网络高速通道，网络性能较此前提升约 10%。

In terms of software and open-source ecosystems, China's AI open-source models have accumulated over 10 billion downloads, and the number of open-source HarmonyOS ecosystem devices exceeds 1.35 billion. The National AI Open-Source Community has gathered 11 million users and over 70,000 models, with more than 50 open-source projects incubated by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology. 软件与开源生态方面，我国 AI 开源模型累计下载量超过 100 亿次，开源鸿蒙生态设备数量超过 13.5 亿台。国家人工智能开源社区已聚集 1100 万用户和 7 万多个模型，工业和信息化部孵化的开源项目超过 50 个。

The robotics industry has developed over 400 humanoid robot products, accounting for more than half of the global total, and sales of quadruped robots are close to 70% of the global market. The Ministry of Industry and Information Technology also stated that it will continue to promote research into new-generation network technologies such as 6G, optical communication, satellite communication, and integrated communication sensing and computing. 机器人产业已形成 400 多款人形机器人产品，约占全球一半以上，四足机器人销量接近全球七成。工业和信息化部还表示，将继续推进 6G、光通信、卫星通信和通信感知计算一体化等新一代网络技术研究。

红熊 AI 完成数亿元 A+ 轮融资

Red Bear AI announced yesterday the completion of a Series A+ financing round worth hundreds of millions of RMB, with a post-investment valuation approaching 3 billion RMB. This round was jointly invested by Zhejiang Jiuwei Private Equity Fund, Jiaxing Zhangyuan Venture Capital, and existing shareholder Geruifeng, marking the company's sixth round of financing completed within 15 months. 红熊 AI 昨日宣布完成数亿元人民币 A+ 轮融资，投后估值接近 30 亿元。本轮由浙江九纬私募基金、嘉兴彰元创业投资和老股东格睿丰联合投资，是公司 15 个月内完成的第六轮融资。

The funds will be used for the integration of the OpenBear general model, MemoryBear memory system, and native Agent architecture, as well as expanding four types of businesses: intelligent customer service, intelligent marketing, ChatBI, and intelligent education. 资金将用于 OpenBear 通用模型、MemoryBear 记忆系统和原生 Agent 架构的融合，并扩大智能客服、智能营销、ChatBI 与智能教育四类业务。

MemoryBear manages working memory, short-term memory, and long-term memory in a layered manner, and uses temporal memory, dynamic semantic networks, and semantic pruning to reduce information forgetting and token consumption in long-term conversations. MemoryBear 把工作记忆、短期记忆和长期记忆分层管理，并用时间记忆、动态语义网络和语义剪枝降低长期对话中的信息遗忘与 Token 消耗。

OpenBear adopts an MoE sparse expert architecture and reserves interfaces for interaction with external memory systems. MemoryBear also combines the Ebbinghaus forgetting curve with an activation model to regularly prune outdated, redundant, and incorrect information, allowing agents to maintain timelines and knowledge relationships in long-term tasks. OpenBear 采用 MoE 稀疏专家架构，并预留与外部记忆系统交互的接口。MemoryBear 还结合艾宾浩斯遗忘曲线与激活度模型，对过时、重复和错误信息进行定期剪枝，让 Agent 在长期任务中维护时间线与知识关系。

💡 Google DeepMind CEO 哈萨比斯：AI 不会让 STEM 和计算机科学学位失去价值

According to Business Insider, Demis Hassabis, CEO of Google DeepMind, stated at a business conference in London that AI is changing career paths in the tech industry, but STEM degrees and foundational computer science knowledge will not lose their value as a result. 据《商业内幕》报道，谷歌 DeepMind CEO 德米斯·哈萨比斯在伦敦一场商业会议上表示，AI 正在改变科技行业的职业路径，但 STEM 学位和计算机科学基础不会因此失去价值。

Hassabis believes that individuals with a grasp of software engineering and computer science fundamentals can use AI tools more efficiently. He mentioned that programming languages have evolved from machine code to C, then to Python, and may further shift towards using English for programming in the future. 哈萨比斯认为，掌握软件工程和计算机科学基础的人，能够更高效地使用 AI 工具。他提到，编程语言已经历从机器码到 C 语言、再到 Python 的演变，未来可能进一步转向使用英语进行编程。

People who truly deeply understand technology may use AI tools 10 times more efficiently than those lacking a technical foundation. 真正深入理解技术的人，使用 AI 工具的效率可能达到缺乏技术基础者的 10 倍。

He also stated that the AI era requires humanities and social sciences such as ethics, philosophy, and economics to address new problems brought about by technological development. 他同时表示，AI 时代也需要伦理学、哲学、经济学等人文学科和社会科学，以应对技术发展带来的新问题。

润科具能发布轮足复合「半人马」机器人

Runke Juneng unveiled the "Centaur" wheeled-legged composite robot at the 2026 World Artificial Intelligence Conference, targeting special scenarios such as steelmaking, mining, nuclear industry, field inspection, and fire rescue. 润科具能在 2026 世界人工智能大会发布轮足复合机器人「半人马」，面向炼钢、矿山、核工业、野外巡检和消防救援等特种场景。

  • Mobility: The lower body adopts a four-wheeled leg structure, balancing flat ground speed with the ability to traverse stairs, mountain roads, and icy/snowy surfaces. 移动：下半身采用四轮足结构，兼顾平地速度与台阶、山路、冰雪路面的通过能力。
  • Operation: The upper body can be extended with dual arms, dexterous hands, and force sensors, supporting grasping, twisting, carrying, and valve operation. 作业：上半身可扩展双臂、灵巧手和力传感器，支持抓取、拧转、搬运与阀门操作。
  • Load: Average load 100 to 120kg, extreme static load 210kg. 负载：平均负载 100 至 120kg，极限静态负载 210kg。
  • Perception: Integrates lidar, stereo vision, and depth cameras to autonomously identify pipelines, instruments, obstacles, and personnel, and complete path planning. 感知：融合激光雷达、双目视觉和深度相机，自主识别管线、仪表、障碍物和人员，并完成路径规划。

The entire machine is designed according to explosion-proof standards and can be remotely taken over by personnel to perform obstacle removal, maintenance, and material handling after detecting anomalies. 整机按防爆等级设计，可在发现异常后由远程人员接管执行清障、检修和物资搬运。

三星显示量产 True Black 1400 笔记本 OLED 面板

Samsung Display announced the full supply of VESA DisplayHDR True Black 1400 compliant tandem notebook OLED panels, with the first certified product being the Lenovo YOGA Pro 16 Aura 2026; products from ASUS, Dell, and MSI will adopt them sequentially from the second half of this year to next year. 三星显示宣布全面供应符合 VESA DisplayHDR True Black 1400 的串联式笔记本 OLED 面板，首款认证产品为联想 YOGA Pro 16 Aura 2026；华硕、戴尔和微星产品将在今年下半年至明年陆续采用。

至简动力北大联合实验室发布世界动作模型 HarmoWAM

ZhiJian Power and Peking University Joint Lab proposed the robot world action model HarmoWAM, using two experts with different divisions of labor to handle long-range movement and fine manipulation. 至简动力与北京大学联合实验室提出机器人世界动作模型 HarmoWAM，用两个分工不同的专家处理长程移动和精细操作。

The reactive expert plans cross-regional movements based on explicitly predicted future frames, while the predictive expert reads spatiotemporal features in the latent space to complete high-precision actions such as grasping, pouring, and writing; a gating module dynamically switches between the two based on the current frame and task stage. 反应式专家根据显式预测的未来画面规划跨区域移动，预测式专家读取隐空间的时空特征完成抓取、倾倒、书写等高精度动作；门控模块会根据当前画面和任务阶段动态切换两者。

The model uses Wan2.2-TI2V-5B as the world model backbone, pre-trained on approximately 1.9 million robot trajectories, predicting 13 future frames each time and outputting actions at 48Hz. 模型以 Wan2.2-TI2V-5B 为世界模型骨干，在约 190 万条机器人轨迹上预训练，每次预测未来 13 帧，并以 48Hz 输出动作。

The team tested six real-world tasks: fruit placement, can stacking, pouring water, writing "Yes," flower arranging, and zipping bags. The average success rate within the distribution was 89%, higher than the control model π0.5's 74%; the average success rate after changing background, position, or objects was 82%. 团队在水果放置、易拉罐堆叠、倒水、书写「Yes」、插花和装袋拉链六项真机任务中测试：分布内平均成功率为 89%，高于对照模型 π0.5 的 74%；更换背景、位置或物体后的平均成功率为 82%。
