库克：迫不及待想看看中国 AI 下一步进展

Image courtesy of The Wall Street Journal 图自华尔街日报

According to China News Service, Apple CEO Tim Cook recently stated in an interview that the development of China's robotics industry is impressive, and he is very much looking forward to artificial intelligence (AI), eager to see the next steps in this field. 据中国新闻网报道，苹果 CEO 库克日前在接受采访时表示，中国机器人行业发展令人印象深刻，并且其对人工智能（AI）非常期待，并迫不及待想看看该领域下一步进展。

The report stated that Cook pointed out at the opening ceremony of the China Development Forum 2026 Annual Meeting that the spirit of innovation is profoundly changing China's manufacturing industry: companies that once relied on manual processes have now become one of the world's most advanced manufacturers, achieving remarkable progress in automation and intelligent manufacturing, reaching new heights in efficiency and standards. 报道称，库克在中国发展高层论坛 2026 年年会开幕式上指出，创新精神在深刻改变着中国制造业：曾经依赖人工流程的企业，如今已跃升为全球最先进的制造商之一，在自动化和智能制造领域取得令人瞩目的进步，效率和水平达到全新高度。

According to CCTV Finance, Cook also stated in an interview with China Central Television that 'AI is an amplification of human capabilities, not a replacement for humans.' 另据央视财经报道，库克在接受中台采访时还表示，「人工智能是对人能力的放大，而不是对人的取代。」

He believes that people should let go of their fears and start using it in their daily lives, 'to think about how it can help you, how it can make you more efficient, and how it can help you share some of your workload.' 其认为大家应该放下恐惧，先在日常生活中开始使用它，「去思考它能如何帮到你、如何让你更高效、如何帮你分担一部分手头的工作。」

全球首个脑机接口创新产品获得医保编码

According to news from the 'National Healthcare Security Administration' yesterday, on March 13, the world's first invasive brain-computer interface medical device was officially approved for market launch. 据「国家医保局」昨日消息，3 月 13 日，全球首款侵入式脑机接口医疗器械正式获批上市。

And on March 15, the National Healthcare Security Administration proactively engaged and provided advance services, completing the medical insurance coding for the product, successfully opening up the critical link for innovative products from market approval to clinical application. 而在 3 月 15 日国家医保局已主动对接、靠前服务，为该产品完成医保编码赋码，成功打通创新产品从获批上市到临床应用的关键环节。

The report pointed out that the above marks the brain-computer interface industry's official transition from 'planning blueprint' to 'fast track implementation'. 报道指出，上述标志着脑机接口产业从「规划蓝图」正式驶入「落地快车道」。

It is understood that the 'Implantable Brain-Computer Interface Hand Motor Function Compensation System' approved this time is primarily aimed at high-level paraplegic patients due to spinal cord injury. The report noted that currently, the cumulative number of spinal cord injury patients in China exceeds 3.7 million, and is increasing at a rate of approximately 90,000 people per year. 据悉，本次获批的「植入式脑机接口手部运动功能代偿系统」，主要面向因脊髓损伤导致的高位截瘫患者。报道指出，目前我国脊髓损伤患者已累计超过 370 万人，且以每年约 9 万人的速度递增。

It is understood that by implanting a coin-sized minimally invasive device inside the patient's skull, the system can real-time collect and decode brain electrical signals, allowing patients to control external pneumatic gloves with their 'thoughts' and independently complete daily actions such as gripping, picking up objects, and drinking water; after only one month post-surgery, patients can operate it independently at home. 据了解，通过在患者颅内植入一枚硬币大小的微创装置，系统可实时采集并解码脑电信号，让患者能够凭「意念」控制外部的气动手套，自主完成抓握、取物、喝水等日常动作；术后仅需一个月，患者即可居家自主操作。

英伟达：DLSS 5 不读取 3D 引擎数据

During a recent Q&A session at GTC 2026, someone asked Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang about external criticism that DLSS 5 is merely frame-level post-processing, just a filter. Huang then retorted, "You've got it completely wrong. This isn't frame-level post-processing; it's generative control at the geometric level." 近期 GTC 2026 的现场 Q&A 上，有人问英伟达 CEO 黄仁勋，外界批评 DLSS 5 只是帧级后处理，只是一个滤镜。随后黄仁勋回击称「你们完全搞错了。这不是帧级后处理，这是几何层面的生成控制。」

Nvidia's official statement also corroborated this, saying: "DLSS 5 inputs the game's color and motion vectors into the model, anchoring the output in the source 3D content." 同时英伟达的官方声明也配合着说：「DLSS 5 将游戏的颜色和运动矢量输入模型，将输出锚定在源 3D 内容中。」

However, recently, game technology blogger Daniel Owen contacted Nvidia GeForce expert Jacob Freeman and asked him, "Does DLSS 5 actually generate output frames using only a single 2D image plus motion vectors as input?" Nvidia's response was "Yes." 但日前游戏技术博主 Daniel Owen 联系了英伟达 GeForce 专家 Jacob Freeman，并向后者提问「DLSS 5 是否实际上只以单帧 2D 图像加运动矢量作为输入来生成输出帧？」而英伟达方面表示「是的」。

It is understood that the workflow of DLSS 5 is actually: after the game engine renders a frame, this frame, along with motion vectors, is passed to the AI model, which then generates a layer of "more realistic" lighting and material effects on top of it, outputting it to the player. 据悉，DLSS 5 的工作流程实际上是： 游戏引擎渲染出一帧画面后，这帧画面连同运动矢量一起传给 AI 模型，然后模型在上面生成一层「更真实」的光线和材质效果，输出给玩家。

Simply put, DLSS 5 never reads the geometric data of game assets, nor does it read data from the depth buffer; it does not read any physics-based rendering (PBR) data from the game engine. 简单来讲，DLSS 5 从来没有读取游戏素材的几何数据，甚至没有读取深度缓冲中的数据，其不会读取来自游戏引擎的任何基于物理的渲染 (PBR) 方面的数据。

According to the answer Owen received from an Nvidia evangelist, the DLSS 5 model "infers" material properties from the final 2D rendered frame. 根据 Owen 从英伟达布道师那里获得的答案，DLSS 5 模型从最终的 2D 渲染帧里「推断（infer）」材质属性。

马斯克推出「TERAFAB」：计划每年生产千亿芯片

Yesterday, Elon Musk's Tesla, xAI, and SpaceX jointly announced plans to invest approximately $20 billion to build a vertically integrated semiconductor factory called "TERAFAB." 昨日，马斯克旗下特斯拉、xAI 与 SpaceX 联合宣布，计划投资约 200 亿美元建设垂直整合型半导体工厂「TERAFAB」。

It is reported that the TERAFAB project aims to produce over 1 terawatt of AI computing power annually. The facility will integrate the entire semiconductor manufacturing process, including chip design, lithography, wafer fabrication, memory production, advanced packaging, and testing. 据悉， TERAFAB 项目旨在每年生产超过 1 太瓦的 AI 算力。 该设施将整合芯片设计、光刻、晶圆制造、存储器生产、先进封装及测试等半导体制造全流程。

In its initial phase, a prototype factory will be built within Tesla's Gigafactory Texas in Austin, primarily for rapid testing and iteration of edge inference chips and specialized chips optimized for space environments. 项目初期将在得克萨斯州奥斯汀的特斯拉超级工厂（Giga Texas）内建设原型工厂，主要用于快速测试并迭代端侧推理芯片及针对太空环境优化的特殊芯片。

Regarding core parameters and capacity planning, TERAFAB aims to use a 2-nanometer process technology. Initial capacity is set at 100,000 wafers per month, with a final full-production target of 1 million wafers per month. Musk stated that the factory is expected to produce 100 billion to 200 billion customized AI and memory chips annually. 在核心参数与产能规划上，TERAFAB 目标采用 2 纳米制程工艺。初期产能设定为每月 10 万片晶圆，最终满产目标为每月 100 万片晶圆。马斯克表示， 该工厂每年预计将产出 1000 亿至 2000 亿颗定制化 AI 及存储芯片。

Musk also revealed yesterday that approximately 80% of TERAFAB's capacity will be dedicated to space applications, with the remaining 20% for terrestrial applications. 马斯克昨日还发文透露， TERAFAB 其中约 80% 的产能将用于太空领域，剩余约 20% 则面向地面应用。

理想产品负责人：新车的全主动悬架不是某一供应商提供

Recently, Tang Jing, head of Ideal Auto's product line, posted a response to the supplier issue of the new L9 Livis's 800V fully active suspension. 近日，理想汽车产品线负责人汤靖发文回应了新车 L9 Livis 的 800V 全主动悬架供应商问题。

Tang Jing stated that the Ideal L9 Livis's 800V fully active suspension is a complete system, "not provided by a single supplier." 汤靖表示，理想 L9 Livis 的 800V 全主动悬架是一套完整系统，「不是某一个供应商提供的。」

According to him, the new car's 800V fully active suspension includes air springs, dual-valve CDC active shock absorbers, four independent hydraulic pumps, high-pressure pipelines, and the control and tuning of the entire system. 据其透露，新车的 800V 全主动悬架包含空气弹簧、双阀 CDC 主动减震器、四个独立液压泵、高压管路，以及对整个系统的控制和调校。

As for this fully active suspension, since no suitable Tier 1 (i.e., a car manufacturer's primary supplier) could be found, Ideal chose to act as the "general contractor" themselves. 而本次的全主动悬架因寻找不到合适的 Tier 1（即车厂一级供应商），所以理想选择自己做「总包」。

At the same time, Tang Jing also revealed his view on "the biggest role of active suspension": "controlling the first order, which is displacement, which is what we commonly refer to as roll and pitch." 同时汤靖还公布了自己对「主动悬架最大的作用」的观点：「控制一阶，也就是位移， 也就是我们平常经常说的侧倾，俯仰。」

💡 陶哲轩：AI 已将科学想法生成的成本降至接近零

According to foreign media THE DECODER, renowned mathematician Terence Tao publicly stated that AI technology has reduced the cost of generating scientific ideas to near zero, making theoretical verification and evaluation a new bottleneck in current mathematical research. 据外媒 THE DECODER 报道，知名数学家陶哲轩公开表示，AI 技术已将科学想法生成的成本降至接近零，并使得理论验证与评估成为当前数学研究的新瓶颈。

The report pointed out that Terence Tao compared the impact of AI on the mathematical community to the impact of automobiles on urban development. 报道指出，陶哲轩将 AI 对数学界的影响比作汽车对城市发展的冲击。

He believes that existing infrastructure such as academic journals and conferences was built for humans, and while the human process of finding proofs is slower, it can generate valuable by-products such as accumulating expertise and exploring new directions. 其认为，现有的学术期刊与会议等基础设施是为人类建立的，人类寻找证明的过程虽然较慢，但能产生积累专业知识、探索新方向等有价值的副产品。

In contrast, while AI-assisted proofs can efficiently derive results from hypotheses, they lack the proof paths and logical narratives required by traditional journals. 相比之下，AI 辅助证明虽能高效地从假设推导至结果，却缺失了传统期刊所需的证明路径与逻辑叙事。

Terence Tao emphasized that the cost of generating scientific hypotheses under AI-driven approaches has approached zero, similar to the trajectory of the internet significantly reducing communication costs. 陶哲轩在强调， AI 驱动下生成科学假设的成本已趋近于零，这与互联网大幅降低通信成本的轨迹相似。

He explicitly stated that this has led to a substantial shift in the bottleneck of scientific research: because thousands of theories can now be instantly generated for a single scientific problem, the academic community's current core challenge has shifted from "proposing theories" to "verifying and evaluating massive theories." 他明确表示，这导致科学研究的瓶颈发生实质性转移：由于现在面对单一科学问题即可瞬间生成数以千计的理论，学术界当前的核心挑战已从「提出理论」转向「验证与评估海量理论」。

Addressing the structural contradictions brought about by this technological evolution, Terence Tao believes that the academic community should not forcibly integrate AI into existing academic evaluation systems, but rather turn to building new machine-oriented mathematical infrastructure. 针对这一技术演进带来的结构性矛盾，陶哲轩认为学术界不应该将 AI 强行塞入现有的学术评价体系，而是转向构建面向机器的全新数学基础设施。
