曝三星显示获折叠屏 iPhone 面板独供认证

According to reports from "KeChuangBan Daily", Samsung Display, a subsidiary of Samsung Electronics, has passed Apple's foldable OLED module mass production certification and has been approved to start mass production of panels for Apple's first foldable iPhone. 据《科创板日报》消息，三星电子控股子公司三星显示已通过苹果折叠 OLED 模组量产认证，获准启动苹果首款折叠屏 iPhone 配套面板的批量生产。

The report states that both parties have signed a three-year exclusive supply agreement, under which Apple will only procure foldable OLED panels from Samsung Display during the contract period. 报道称，双方已签署为期三年的独家供货协议，合约期内苹果将仅从三星显示采购折叠屏 OLED 面板。

The report mentioned that Samsung Display has activated some of its backend production lines at its Vietnam factory, with the first batch of approximately 3 million panels scheduled for delivery within the year. Apple set a mass production yield threshold of no less than 70% for foldable panels, and Samsung Display's final measured yield exceeded 80%. 报道提到，三星显示已启用越南工厂部分后段产线，首批约 300 万块面板计划于年内完成交付。苹果对折叠屏面板设定了不低于 70% 的量产良率门槛，三星显示最终实测良率突破 80%。

In terms of technical solutions, Samsung's customized new generation M-series emissive materials, combined with CoE encapsulated color filter technology, eliminate the traditional polarizer structure to reduce the risk of cracks caused by repeated bending at the fold and compress the panel thickness. 技术方案上，三星为苹果定制的新一代 M 系列发光材料搭配 CoE 封装彩色滤光技术，省去传统偏光片结构，以降低折叠处反复弯折带来的裂纹风险，并压缩面板厚度。

The first foldable iPhone is expected to adopt a horizontal folding form factor similar to the Galaxy Z Fold, featuring an approximately 7.7-inch inner screen and a 5.3-inch outer screen. The hinge component remains a variable for the release date, with Apple aiming for over 300,000 folds without damage. 首款折叠屏 iPhone 预计采用类似 Galaxy Z Fold 的横向折叠形态，配备约 7.7 英寸内屏和 5.3 英寸外屏；铰链部件仍是发布时间变量，苹果目标为 30 万次以上折叠无损伤。

联发科据称拿下 Google TPU V9 独家订单

Ming-Chi Kuo, an analyst at Tianfeng International Securities, posted on X yesterday, stating that the latest industry survey shows MediaTek has secured an exclusive order for Google's upgraded TPU V9 chips. 天风国际证券分析师郭明錤昨日在 X 发帖称，最新产业调查显示，联发科已获得 Google 升级版 TPU V9 芯片独家订单。

Kuo Ming-Chi stated that Google will develop an upgraded v9 chip, possibly codenamed Triggerfish, based on TPU v9 / Humufish. It will be positioned as a more powerful inference-capable revision of TPU v9, with goals including mitigating the CPU wall and memory wall. 郭明錤称，Google 将在 TPU v9 / Humufish 基础上开发代号可能为 Triggerfish 的升级版 v9 芯片，定位为推理能力更强的 TPU v9 改版，目标包括缓解 CPU wall 与 memory wall。

Compared to Humufish, Triggerfish's main changes include a 2 to 3 times increase in SRAM capacity, the addition of a simulation die, and an upgrade of HBM specifications from HBM4 to HBM4E. 与 Humufish 相比，Triggerfish 的主要变化包括 SRAM 容量提升至前者的 2 至 3 倍、新增 simulation die，并将 HBM 规格由 HBM4 升级至 HBM4E。

Regarding shipment expectations, Ming-Chi Kuo stated that the estimated lifetime shipments of Humufish, at 4 to 5 million units, remain unchanged, while Google has additionally ordered 1 to 2 million Triggerfish chips. The chip is expected to begin production by the end of next year and ramp up in 2028. 在出货预期方面，郭明錤称 Humufish 生命周期 400 万至 500 万颗的出货预估维持不变，Google 额外追加 100 万至 200 万颗 Triggerfish 订单。该芯片预计明年底开始生产，并于 2028 年放量。

Meta 暂停员工追踪项目，曾收集鼠标点击和键盘输入

According to WIRED, Meta has suspended an employee tracking project. The project previously collected employee computer input information, including mouse movements, click locations, keystrokes, and screen content. The suspension was due to internal security issues that allowed more Meta employees to access some of the data collected by the project. 据 WIRED 报道，Meta 已暂停一项面向员工的追踪项目。该项目此前会收集员工电脑输入信息，包括鼠标移动、点击位置、键盘敲击以及屏幕内容。暂停原因是该项目收集的部分数据因内部安全问题，曾被更多 Meta 员工访问到。

According to WIRED, Meta has suspended the Model Compatibility Initiative (MCI) tool for employees. Launched among U.S. employees in April this year, the tool collected mouse movements, click locations, keystrokes, and screen content to train AI systems on how humans operate computer software. 据 WIRED 报道，Meta 已暂停面向员工的 Model Compatibility Initiative（MCI）工具。该工具今年 4 月在美国员工中推出，会收集鼠标移动、点击位置、键盘敲击和屏幕内容，用于训练 AI 系统学习人类操作电脑软件的方式。

The suspension was due to internal access permission issues with some data collected by MCI. Meta spokesperson Tracy Clayton stated that there is currently no indication that the relevant data was improperly accessed by employees, and the company will suspend the project during the investigation. 暂停原因是 MCI 收集的部分数据出现内部访问权限问题。Meta 发言人 Tracy Clayton 表示，目前没有迹象显示相关数据被员工不当访问，公司将在调查期间暂停该项目。

Stephane Kasriel, Meta's VP of AI Research, stated in an internal notice that the issue was discovered on June 18th and addressed within 4 hours, but the initial fix was not fully effective. The company subsequently further tightened access permissions. He stated that Meta will only re-enable MCI once it confirms that data protection controls are effective. Meta AI 研究副总裁 Stephane Kasriel 在内部通知中称，相关问题于 6 月 18 日被发现，并在 4 小时内处理，但最初修复并未完全生效，公司随后进一步收紧了访问权限。他表示，Meta 只有在确认数据保护控制有效后，才会重新启用 MCI。

OpenAI 推出 Patch the Planet，帮助开源项目修复漏洞

OpenAI announced a new initiative called "Patch the Planet," aimed at helping the open-source community discover and fix security vulnerabilities in software projects. OpenAI 宣布推出一项名为「Patch the Planet」的新计划，旨在帮助开源社区发现并修复软件项目中的安全问题。

The initiative will be advanced in collaboration between OpenAI and security company Trail of Bits, whose security engineers will work directly with open-source project maintainers to review potential code issues and assist in processing them with security tools like OpenAI's Codex Security. 该计划将由 OpenAI 与安全公司 Trail of Bits 合作推进，后者的安全工程师将直接与开源项目维护者协作，对潜在代码问题进行审查，并借助 OpenAI 的 Codex Security 等安全工具辅助处理。

According to CCTV's "Xinwen Lianbo" report, the General Office of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the General Office of the Ministry of Public Security jointly issued a notice yesterday, stating that motor vehicle certificate information will be shared in real-time, allowing domestically produced passenger cars to achieve "purchase on the same day, pay tax on the same day, and register on the same day." 据《新闻联播》报道，工业和信息化部办公厅、公安部办公厅昨日联合印发通知，机动车合格证信息将实现实时共享，国产小客车可做到「当天购车，当天缴税，当天上牌」。
