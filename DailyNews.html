要抢 Windows 用户？更便宜的 MacBook 本周见

This week, Apple is expected to release several new products, with the new entry-level MacBook and the new iPhone 17e drawing significant attention. 本周，苹果预计将会发布多款全新产品，其中全新的入门级 MacBook 以及新的 iPhone 17e 备受瞩目。

The biggest highlights of the new MacBook are its "lower starting price" + the use of the same processor as the iPhone (expected to be the A18 Pro), and it is expected to have a screen size under 13 inches and feature a colorful casing design. 新款 MacBook 最大的亮点就是「起售价格更低」+采用 iPhone 同款处理器（预计为 A18 Pro），并且有望屏幕尺寸控制在 13 英寸以下，采用多彩的外壳设计。

According to Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman, Apple internally believes that the new entry-level MacBook will largely attract users switching from Windows or Chromebook camps to macOS, and may even prompt iPhone users without a computer to buy their first Mac. 据彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 报道，苹果内部认为新的入门款 MacBook 将会大量吸引从 Windows 或 Chromebook 阵营转向 macOS 的用户，甚至可能促使没有电脑的 iPhone 用户买下他们的第一台 Mac。

Gurman also pointed out that Apple is reminding Apple Store employees to extend their working hours (similar to the iPhone launch in September), and Apple Stores will also set up a dedicated counter for new products. 并且 Gurman 还指出，苹果正在提醒 Apple Store 的员工需要延长工作时间（与 9 月发布 iPhone 的时候类似），Apple Store 还将设置一个专门用于摆放新产品的柜台。

The iPhone 17e, in addition to upgrading to the same A19 chip as the iPhone 17 and adding MagSafe magnetic charging, is also planned to switch to Apple's next-generation self-developed cellular and wireless chips. 而 iPhone 17e 除了升级至 iPhone 17 同款 A19 芯片，并补上 MagSafe 磁吸功能。同时，还计划换用苹果更新一代的自研蜂窝网络与无线芯片。

Generated by Gemini 由 Gemini 生成

In last night's "Power On" column, Gurman further analyzed the touch-screen MacBook Pro expected to be released by the end of this year. 在昨晚的《Power On》专栏上，Gurman 还进一步分析了预计今年年底上市的触控版 MacBook Pro。

Gurman stated that Apple will not launch a "MacBook-iPad fusion" product for some time. In fact, the company prefers customers to buy both an iPad and a Mac, and will increasingly bundle these two product lines in marketing in the future. Gurman 表示，苹果将在一段时间内都不会推出一款「MacBook 融 iPad」的产品，事实上这家公司更希望顾客同时购买 iPad 和 Mac，未来还会越来越多地将这两条产品线捆绑营销。

Therefore, the new MacBook Pro with a touch screen will still primarily rely on "keyboard + trackpad" operation, with the touch screen acting more as an auxiliary function. 因此新款带触控屏幕的 MacBook Pro 依然以「键盘+触控板」的操作为主，而触控屏幕更像是辅助功能。

The report also re-mentioned a foldable iPad: it would have a screen size similar to a MacBook but would retain the portability expected of an iPad, not becoming a true 2-in-1 product, and would still run iPadOS. 报道还重新提及了可折叠的 iPad：拥有 MacBook 类似的屏幕尺寸大小，但依然保持 iPad 该有的便携，不会成为一款真正的二合一产品，依然跑的 iPadOS。

Additionally, Apple plans to replace the existing Core ML with a new Core AI framework at this year's Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) and deeply integrate Google's Gemini large model into the next-generation system. 另外，苹果还计划在今年的全球开发者大会（WWDC）上以全新 Core AI 框架取代原有的 Core ML，并在新一代系统中深度集成谷歌 Gemini 大模型。

As a core project, the new Siri, internally codenamed "Campo," will deeply integrate Google's Gemini large model to enhance its capabilities in chat interaction, personal data invocation, and cross-application control. 作为核心项目，内部代号为「Campo」的全新 Siri 将深度集成谷歌的 Gemini 大模型，从而提升其在聊天交互、调用个人数据以及跨应用控制方面的能力。

Sources say that the naming transition from "Machine Learning (ML)" to "Artificial Intelligence (AI)" aims to align with the current industry context. The core function of this framework is to help developers more conveniently integrate external third-party AI models into their applications. 消息人士称，从「机器学习（ML）」向「人工智能（AI）」的命名转换旨在顺应当下行业语境，该框架的核心功能是协助开发者更便捷地将外部第三方 AI 模型接入其应用程序。

英伟达：5G 不够用，6G 须由 AI 驱动

According to Bloomberg, Nvidia is forming alliances with multiple telecom operators to drive the transformation of 6G networks towards an open, software-defined, AI-driven architecture, in order to meet the network demands of the future "physical AI" era. 据彭博社报道，英伟达正与多家电信运营商结盟，推动 6G 网络向开放式、软件定义、由 AI 驱动的架构转型，以满足未来「物理 AI」时代的网络需求。

Nvidia stated yesterday at the communications industry conference in Barcelona that current 5G networks are primarily focused on connecting people and devices, making it difficult to support the real-time intelligent communication needs of a large number of machine terminals such as future robots and autonomous vehicles. 英伟达昨天在巴塞罗那通信行业大会表示，当前的 5G 网络主要面向人与设备的连接，难以支撑未来机器人、自动驾驶车辆等大量机器终端的实时智能通信需求。

Ronnie Vasishta, head of Nvidia's telecom business, pointed out that existing networks "are not ready for tomorrow's application scenarios." In the AI era, networks need to provide intelligence to machines, not just data to humans. 英伟达电信业务负责人 Ronnie Vasishta 指出，现有网络「并未为明天的应用场景做好准备」，在 AI 时代，网络需要向机器提供智能，而不仅是向人类提供数据。

Nvidia's partners in this collaboration include operators such as Nokia, SoftBank, and T-Mobile. They plan to build a 6G network based on a general computing platform, enabling wireless equipment to be controlled by updateable software and data traffic to be scheduled in real-time by AI. 英伟达此次联合的伙伴包括诺基亚、软银、T-Mobile 等运营商，计划构建基于通用计算平台的 6G 网络，使无线电设备由可更新的软件控制，数据流量由 AI 实时调度。

Nvidia believes that future networks need to achieve "hundreds of thousands of times" efficiency improvement because available spectrum is limited, while the communication demands of AI devices will grow exponentially. 英伟达认为，未来网络需要实现「数十万倍」效率提升，因为可用频谱有限，而 AI 设备的通信需求将呈指数级增长。

华为发布新一代 AI 超算集群

On February 28th, local time, Huawei officially unveiled its new generation AI supercomputing cluster Atlas 950 SuperPoD and multiple computing solutions globally at the 2026 Mobile World Congress (MWC). 当地时间 2 月 28 日，华为在 2026 年世界移动通信大会（MWC）上面向全球正式发布新一代 AI 超算集群 Atlas 950 SuperPoD 及多款计算解决方案。

According to official Huawei news, the Atlas 950 SuperPoD adopts a 'cluster+SuperPoD' architecture, equipped with a self-developed UnifiedBus interconnect protocol, enabling high-speed interconnection of up to 8192 NPU chips. 据华为官方消息，Atlas 950 SuperPoD 采用「集群+SuperPoD」架构，搭载自研 UnifiedBus 互联协议，将多达 8192 颗 NPU 芯片进行高速互联。

According to Nikkei Asia, the Atlas 950 SuperPoD's self-developed interconnect protocol directly benchmarks against Nvidia's NVLink, and the system features unified memory addressing capabilities. In its full configuration, it includes 128 computing cabinets and 32 communication cabinets, with a deployment area of approximately 1000 square meters. 另据《日经新闻》报道，Atlas 950 SuperPoD 自研的互联协议直接对标英伟达 NVLink，并且该系统具备统一内存寻址能力，满配状态下包含 128 个计算机柜与 32 个通信机柜，部署面积约 1000 平方米。

Insiders reveal that the core chips are manufactured by SMIC using an upgraded N+3 process node, and the entire system is expected to be officially released in the fourth quarter of 2026. 知情人士透露，核心芯片由中芯国际采用升级版 N+3 工艺节点代工，整套系统预计于 2026 年第四季度正式发售。

Huawei's Rotating Chairman Xu Zhijun previously stated that against the backdrop of single-chip performance being limited by advanced manufacturing equipment, Huawei achieves a computing power overtake through large-scale chip cluster integration. 华为轮值董事长徐直军此前表示，在单芯片性能受制于先进制造设备限制的背景下，华为通过大规模芯片集群整合来实现算力反超。

Official data shows that the Atlas 950 SuperPoD is equipped with 56.8 times the number of NPUs compared to Nvidia's NVL144 system, which is planned for release in the second half of 2026, and its overall computing power output can reach 6.7 times that of the latter. 官方数据显示，Atlas 950 SuperPoD 配备的 NPU 数量是英伟达计划于 2026 年下半年推出的 NVL144 系统的 56.8 倍，其整体算力输出可达后者的 6.7 倍。

Samsung stated that in the future, all manufacturing stages—from raw material entry, production scheduling, quality inspection, to final shipment—will be deeply involved and autonomized by AI. 三星表示，未来所有制造环节——从原材料入厂、生产调度、质量检测到最终出货——都将由 AI 深度参与并实现自治化。

In terms of safety and environmental management, the company plans to deploy environmental safety robots combined with digital twins in high-risk or human-inaccessible environments to achieve real-time monitoring and hazard elimination. 在安全与环境管理方面，公司计划在高风险或人类难以进入的环境中部署结合数字孪生的环境安全机器人，以实现实时监测与隐患消除。

Samsung emphasized that the core of this transformation is its Agentic AI technology, first introduced in the Galaxy S26 series.
