MacBook Ultra 曝光，搭载 OLED 触摸屏

According to MacRumors, Apple is developing a high-end laptop, possibly named "MacBook Ultra," which will be the first Mac product to feature an OLED panel and touchscreen technology. 据 MacRumors 报道，苹果正在研发一款或命名为「MacBook Ultra」的高端笔记本电脑，该设备将首次在 Mac 产品线中引入 OLED 面板与触摸屏技术。

Insiders revealed that the MacBook Ultra will be available in two sizes: 14-inch and 16-inch. The new device will not only feature an OLED panel with higher contrast and pure black display effects but will also support multi-finger touch input for the first time. Apple has specially designed reinforced hinges to reduce screen wobble during clicks. 知情人士透露，MacBook Ultra 将提供 14 英寸与 16 英寸两种尺寸。新设备不仅配备具有更高对比度及纯黑显示效果的 OLED 面板，还将首次支持手指多点触控输入，苹果为此专门设计了强化铰链以减少点击时的屏幕晃动。

In terms of appearance design, the device is expected to adopt a thinner body structure and replace the existing notch screen with an iPhone-like "Dynamic Island" to unify Siri AI's cross-device interactive experience in macOS27 (Golden Gate). 在外观设计上，该设备有望采用更薄的机身结构，并以类似 iPhone 的「灵动岛」取代现有的刘海屏，以在 macOS27（Golden Gate）中统一 Siri AI 的跨端交互体验。

Regarding core configuration and pricing, due to Apple's chip strategy adjustment and no plans for M6 Pro or M6 Max releases in the short term, the device will continue to be equipped with the existing M5 Pro and M5 Max chips. 核心配置与定价方面，由于苹果芯片战略调整且短期内无 M6 Pro 或 M6 Max 的发布计划，该设备将继续搭载现有的 M5 Pro 与 M5 Max 芯片。

As a high-end flagship line, the MacBook Ultra's pricing is expected to be significantly higher than the current starting prices of $2499 and $2999 for the 14-inch and 16-inch MacBook Pro, respectively. It is anticipated to enter mass production and be officially released from late 2026 to early 2027. 作为高端旗舰线，MacBook Ultra 的定价预计将显著高于当前起售价分别为 2499 美元和 2999 美元的 14 英寸及 16 英寸 MacBook Pro，预计将于 2026 年底至 2027 年初投入量产并正式发布。

封号潮后，Claude Fable 5 解禁

According to WIRED, the U.S. Department of Commerce recently revoked export, re-export, and domestic transfer license requirements for Anthropic's advanced models Fable 5 and Mythos 5. 据 WIRED 报道，美国商务部近日已撤销对 Anthropic 旗下高阶模型 Fable 5 和 Mythos 5 的出口、再出口及境内转让许可要求。

Anthropic officially stated on X that access to Claude Fable 5 will resume starting today. Anthropic 官方也已在 X 上表示，从今天起开始恢复 Claude Fable 5 访问。

It is worth noting that on the eve of the lifting of restrictions, the Chinese developer community is experiencing the most severe "account ban wave" in history. According to Anthropic's transparency report released in early 2026, the company banned approximately 1.45 million accounts in the second half of 2025 alone. 值得注意的是，在解禁前夕，中国开发者群体正经历着史上最严苛的「封号潮」。据 Anthropic 在 2026 年初发布的透明度报告显示，仅 2025 年下半年，该公司就封禁了约 145 万个账号。

Furthermore, blogger LegitMichel777 recently discovered through reverse engineering that Claude Code has a built-in covert detection mechanism targeting Chinese users. This mechanism has been operational since April this year, identifying user identities by checking the system's timezone and a list of 147 specific domains (including Baidu, Alibaba, ByteDance, etc.). 此外，博主 LegitMichel777 近日通过逆向分析发现，Claude Code 内置了一套针对中国用户的隐蔽检测机制。该机制自今年 4 月起运行，通过检查系统时区及 147 个特定域名清单（含百度、阿里、字节跳动等）来识别用户身份。

To achieve imperceptible data transmission, the tool uses steganography to encode detection results in system prompts: if it's a Chinese timezone, the date format will change from "2026-06-30" to "2026/06/30"; simultaneously, by replacing Unicode apostrophe characters with minimal visual difference, it stratifies and marks ordinary proxy users and AI lab institutions. 为实现无感回传，该工具利用隐写技术将检测结果编码在系统提示词中：若为中国时区，日期格式将由「2026-06-30」变更为「2026/06/30」；同时通过替换视觉差异极小的 Unicode 撇号字符，对普通代理用户与 AI 实验室机构进行分层标记。

In response to this matter, Claude Code lead Thariq Shihipar stated yesterday that this feature was an experimental measure launched in March this year, aimed at preventing account resale and model distillation, and announced that the relevant detection code would be completely removed in the new version. 针对此事，Claude Code 负责人 Thariq Shihipar 昨日回应称，该功能为今年 3 月上线的实验性措施，旨在防范账号转售与模型蒸馏，并宣布将在新版本中完全删除相关检测代码。

曝 Apple Watch 设计将大改，以后可能用不了旧表带了

Recently, Apple was reportedly planning a major design overhaul for the Apple Watch in 2027, expected to introduce an entirely new band connection system and completely discontinue compatibility with existing older bands. 日前，苹果被曝计划于 2027 年对 Apple Watch 进行重大设计重构，预计将引入全新的表带连接系统并彻底取消对现有旧款表带的兼容。

According to blogger "Chanashuma," Apple will completely change the way bands connect to the case on future Apple Watch models. 据博主「刹那数码」透露，苹果将在未来的 Apple Watch 上彻底改变表带与表壳的连接方式。

It is understood that the primary goal of the new design is to optimize internal stacking, freeing up more physical space within the device to accommodate a larger capacity battery. 据悉，全新设计的主要诉求是优化内部堆叠，为设备腾出更多物理空间以容纳更大容量的电池。

松下计划三年投入约 5000 亿日元，转向 AI 基础设施业务

According to Bloomberg, Panasonic Holdings announced an investment plan of approximately 500 billion yen, aiming to increase sales of AI infrastructure-related businesses to approximately 1.4 trillion yen within the three fiscal years ending March 2029. 据彭博社报道，松下控股公布约 5000 亿日元投资计划，目标是在截至 2029 年 3 月的三个财年内，把 AI 基础设施相关业务销售额提高到约 1.4 万亿日元。

President Yuki Kusumi stated that the company will leverage its long-term cooperation with major AI companies while proactively guarding against market change risks. 社长楠见雄规表示，公司会利用与大型 AI 企业长期合作的基础，同时提前防范市场变化风险。

💡 扎克伯格：AI 不必然导致大规模失业，关键在「赋能个人」

According to Business Insider, Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg stated yesterday during a live interview on Complex channel's 'Idea Generation' that he does not believe artificial intelligence will necessarily lead to mass unemployment. 据《商业内幕》报道，Meta CEO 马克·扎克伯格昨日在接受 Complex 频道《Idea Generation》现场访谈时表示，他不认为人工智能必然会导致大规模失业。

Zuckerberg pointed out in the interview that some leading AI companies are currently focused on automating knowledge work, which may not yield ideal results. He advocates for a balanced state where some companies are responsible for improving corporate operational efficiency, while others are dedicated to developing 'personal superintelligence,' aiming to empower individuals and enhance worker productivity. 扎克伯格在访谈中指出，目前部分领先的 AI 公司一心推动知识工作自动化，结果可能并不理想。他提倡一种平衡状态，即部分公司负责提高企业运营效率，而另一部分公司则致力于发展「个人超级智能」，旨在赋能个人并提升劳动者的生产力。

He believes that if the rate of increase in individual productivity outpaces the rate at which companies achieve automation, future job opportunities should theoretically increase. 他认为，如果个人生产力的提升速度快于企业实现自动化的速度，未来的就业岗位理论上应该会增加。

Currently, Meta has invested billions of dollars in talent acquisition and AI strategy adjustments. Zuckerberg refers to this transformation process as a 'reboot' and revealed that Meta's superintelligence lab was established less than a year ago. Although Meta has made significant progress in generative AI, Zuckerberg stated that the company still has room for improvement. 目前，Meta 已投入数十亿美元用于人才挖掘和 AI 战略调整。扎克伯格将这一转型过程称为一次「重启」，并透露 Meta 的超级智能实验室成立尚不足一年。尽管 Meta 在生成式 AI 领域已取得显著进展，但扎克伯格表示公司仍有提升空间。

It is worth noting that Meta's financial documents show that as of April this year, the company's total number of employees was 77,986. 值得注意的是，Meta 的财务文件显示，截至今年 4 月，公司总员工数为 77986 人。

However, Meta initiated a layoff plan of approximately 10% in May, affecting about 8,000 positions, including cybersecurity and content design teams. The company previously stated that the layoffs were to improve operational efficiency and offset ongoing investments in areas like AI. 然而，Meta 在 5 月份开启了约 10% 的裁员计划，涉及约 8000 个岗位，包括网络安全和内容设计等团队。公司此前表示，裁员是为了提高运营效率，以抵消在 AI 等领域的持续投资。
