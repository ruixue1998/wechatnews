曝 DeepSeek V4.1 下月发布，拟融 500 亿

According to "The Information", DeepSeek is planning to launch its first round of external financing since its establishment, targeting an amount as high as 50 billion yuan, which is expected to become the largest single round of financing for a Chinese AI company to date . 据《The Information》报道，DeepSeek 正计划启动成立以来首轮外部融资，目标金额高达 500 亿元人民币， 有望成为中国 AI 公司迄今规模最大的单轮融资 。

Founder and CEO Liang Wenfeng is expected to subscribe for up to 20 billion yuan RMB in his personal capacity, accounting for approximately 40% of the total financing amount in this round. 创始人兼 CEO 梁文锋预计将以个人身份认购最多 200 亿元人民币，占本轮融资总额约 40%。

If the financing is successfully completed, DeepSeek's valuation (including the raised funds) will exceed 350 billion yuan RMB, a significant increase from the over 10 billion US dollars initially estimated by the outside world at the start of the financing. 若融资顺利完成，DeepSeek 的估值（含募资额）将超过 3500 亿元人民币，较融资启动初期外界预计的逾 100 亿美元大幅攀升。

The report points out that DeepSeek plans to launch an upgraded version V4.1 of its V4 model in June this year. The new version will provide more tools for enterprise users, enhance support for MCP (Model Context Protocol) to facilitate the integration of AI models with other software systems, and V4.1 will also have the ability to process image and audio content. 报道指出，DeepSeek 计划于今年 6 月推出 V4 模型的升级版 V4.1，新版本将提供更多面向企业用户的工具，并增强对 MCP（模型上下文协议）的支持，方便 AI 模型与其他软件系统对接，V4.1 还将具备处理图像和音频内容的能力。

It is worth noting that DeepSeek's website and API services experienced another outage around 5 PM yesterday. The official status page shows that the issue was resolved at 18:05 Beijing time. 值得注意的是，昨日 17 时许 DeepSeek 网页及 API 服务再度出现中断，官方状态页面显示，故障已于北京时间 18:05 恢复。

任天堂官宣 Switch 全线涨价

Nintendo announced yesterday that it would implement global price increases for all Switch series consoles and several of its services, citing "changes in market conditions" and a comprehensive consideration of its medium-to-long-term global business prospects. 任天堂昨日发布公告，宣布对 Switch 全系及旗下多款服务进行全球涨价，理由是「市场环境变化」及对中长期全球业务前景的综合考量。

  • Switch 2 (Japan domestic version): ¥49980 ➡ ¥59980 (+¥10000, approximately 433 RMB, an increase of about 20%), effective from May 25; Switch 2（日本国内版）：¥49980 ➡ ¥59980（+¥10000，约合 433 元人民币，涨幅约 20%），5 月 25 日起生效；
  • Switch 2 (US): $449.99 ➡ $499.99 (+$50), effective from September 1. Switch 2（美国）：$449.99 ➡ $499.99（+$50），9 月 1 日起生效。

In addition, the original Switch, Switch OLED, and Switch Lite have all seen varying degrees of price increases in Japan, ranging from 8,000 yen to 11,000 yen; the annual fee for the Nintendo Switch Online individual basic plan will also increase from 2,400 yen to 3,000 yen starting July 1. 此外，一代 Switch、Switch OLED 以及 Switch Lite 在日本均有不同程度涨价，涨幅从 8000 日元至 11000 日元不等；Nintendo Switch Online 个人基础版年费亦将于 7 月 1 日起由 2400 日元涨至 3000 日元。

Meanwhile, Nintendo disclosed in its financial outlook released yesterday that it expects Switch 2 sales to be approximately 16.5 million units in fiscal year 2027 (ending March 31, 2027), lower than the 19.86 million units completed in the previous fiscal year, and significantly below analyst expectations. 与此同时，任天堂在昨天发布的财务展望中披露，预计 2027 财年（截至 2027 年 3 月 31 日）Switch 2 销量约为 1650 万台，低于上一财年已完成的 1986 万台，且远低于分析师预期。

苹果与英特尔达成初步芯片代工协议

According to The Wall Street Journal, Apple and Intel have reached a preliminary agreement on chip manufacturing cooperation, with Intel set to produce some chips for Apple devices. Intensive negotiations between the two parties lasted for over a year, and the framework of the agreement was formally finalized in recent months. 据《华尔街日报》报道，苹果与英特尔已就芯片代工合作达成初步协议，英特尔将为苹果设备制造部分芯片。双方的密集谈判持续了一年有余，并在近几个月内正式敲定协议框架。

Apple has long relied on TSMC to manufacture chips designed for its iPhone, iPad, and Mac devices. However, as demand for TSMC's capacity from AI chip clients like Nvidia has sharply increased, Apple's bargaining power with TSMC has declined. 苹果长期依赖台积电代工其为 iPhone、iPad 和 Mac 设计的芯片，但随着英伟达等 AI 芯片客户对台积电产能的需求急剧攀升，苹果在台积电的议价能力有所下降。

Apple CEO Tim Cook mentioned in the past two earnings calls that insufficient supply of advanced chips has constrained iPhone sales, a bottleneck expected to continue into the current quarter, and products like Mac Mini and Mac Studio may take several months to achieve supply-demand balance. 苹果 CEO Tim Cook 在最近两次财报电话会议上均提到，先进芯片供应不足制约了 iPhone 的销售，这一瓶颈预计将延续至当前季度，Mac Mini 和 Mac Studio 等产品可能需要数月时间才能实现供需平衡。

内存涨价压制 PS5 销量，索尼：今年游戏机卖不动了

Sony Group yesterday released its annual financial results for fiscal year 2025, ended March 31 this year. 索尼集团昨日发布截至今年 3 月 31 日的 2025 财年年度业绩。

Revenue was 12.48 trillion yen, up 4% year-on-year; operating profit was 1.45 trillion yen, up 13% year-on-year, with the operating profit margin rising to 11.6%; net profit attributable to shareholders was 1.03 trillion yen, down 3% year-on-year. 营收 12.48 万亿日元，同比增长 4%；营业利润 1.45 万亿日元，同比增长 13%，营业利润率升至 11.6%；归属于股东的净利润为 1.03 万亿日元，同比下滑 3%。

  • Game & Network Services (G&NS) revenue was 4.69 trillion yen, largely flat year-on-year; 游戏与网络服务（G&NS）营收 4.69 万亿日元，同比基本持平；
  • Music revenue was 2.12 trillion yen, up 15% year-on-year, with streaming revenue continuing to expand; 音乐营收 2.12 万亿日元，同比增长 15%，流媒体收入持续扩大；
  • Imaging & Sensing Solutions (I&SS) revenue was 2.15 trillion yen, up 20% year-on-year, with operating profit reaching an all-time high; 影像与传感解决方案（I&SS）营收 2.15 万亿日元，同比增长 20%，营业利润创历史新高；
  • Film and entertainment revenue was 1.50 trillion yen, largely flat year-on-year; 影视娱乐营收 1.50 万亿日元，同比基本持平；
  • ET&S revenue was 2.26 trillion yen, down 6% year-on-year. ET&S 营收 2.26 万亿日元，同比下滑 6%。

Gaming hardware was the main pressure point this fiscal year. PS5 shipments for the full year were 16 million units, a 14% decrease year-on-year. Sony stated that soaring memory chip prices have driven up hardware production costs, and the company explicitly stated that the scale of PS5 shipments in fiscal year 2026 will depend on whether sufficient storage chips can be procured at "reasonable prices" . 游戏硬件是本财年的主要压力点。PS5 全年出货 1600 万台，同比减少 14%。索尼表示，内存芯片价格飙升推高了硬件生产成本，公司明确表示 2026 财年 PS5 的出货规模将取决于能否以「合理价格」采购到足量存储芯片 。

Affected by this, the company expects gaming business revenue to decrease by 6% year-on-year in fiscal year 2026; however, due to the non-recurrence of Bungie impairment losses (totaling 120.1 billion yen in fiscal year 2025) and growth in proprietary software sales, operating profit for the gaming segment is expected to increase by 30% year-on-year to 600 billion yen. 受此影响，公司预计 2026 财年游戏业务营收同比下降 6%；但因 Bungie 减值损失（2025 财年合计 1201 亿日元）不再重现及自有软件销售增长，游戏板块营业利润预计同比增长 30% 至 6000 亿日元。

索尼与台积电官宣合作开发下一代图像传感器

Sony Semiconductor Solutions and TSMC announced yesterday that they have signed a non-legally binding basic letter of intent to establish a joint venture in Japan to jointly develop and produce next-generation image sensors. 索尼半导体解决方案与台积电昨日宣布，双方已签署一份不具法律约束力的基本意向书，计划在日本成立合资公司，共同开发和生产下一代图像传感器。

According to the announcement, Sony will hold a majority stake and serve as the controlling shareholder of the joint venture, with development and production lines to be located at Sony's new factory in Koshi City, Kumamoto Prefecture. The cooperation will combine Sony's expertise in image sensor design with TSMC's advantages in process technology and advanced manufacturing capabilities. 根据公告，该合资公司将由索尼持股过半并担任控股股东，开发与生产线将设于索尼位于熊本县合志市的新建工厂。合作将结合索尼在图像传感器设计方面的专业积累，以及台积电在工艺技术与先进制造能力方面的优势。

Regarding investment arrangements, the joint venture's related investments will be synergistically advanced with Sony's new capital expenditures for its existing Nagasaki factory and implemented in phases based on market demand, provided that corresponding support from the Japanese government is obtained. In addition, both parties will explore cooperation opportunities in "physical AI" application fields such as automobiles and robotics. 在投资安排上，合资公司的相关投资将与索尼对长崎现有工厂的新增资本支出协同推进，并根据市场需求分阶段实施，前提是获得日本政府的相应支持。此外，双方还将探索在汽车、机器人等「物理 AI」应用领域的合作机会。

Google 面试将允许使用 AI 助手

According to Business Insider, Google is piloting a new software engineer interview process that allows candidates to use AI assistants during interviews. 据《商业内幕》报道，Google 正在试行一项新的软件工程师面试流程，允许求职者在面试中使用 AI 助手。

The report cited an internal document stating that this reform applies to junior to mid-level positions and will first be piloted in some teams in the United States. If successful, it is planned to be extended to more teams and regions in the second half of this year. Google's VP of Recruiting, Brian Ong, confirmed the aforementioned plan. 报道援引一份内部文件称，这项改革适用于初级至中级职位，将首先在美国部分团队中试点，若效果良好，计划在今年下半年向更多团队和地区推广。Google 招聘副总裁 Brian Ong 确认了上述计划。

The core change in the new process lies in the "code comprehension" section: candidates will be allowed to use Google-approved AI assistants to complete tasks involving reading, debugging, and optimizing existing codebases. 新流程的核心变化在于「代码理解」环节：候选人将被允许使用经 Google 批准的 AI 助手，完成对现有代码库的阅读、调试与优化任务。

Interviewers will focus on evaluating candidates' "AI usage capabilities," including prompt engineering, output validation, and debugging skills. A Google spokesperson confirmed that the AI assistant used during the pilot phase will be the company's self-developed model, Gemini. Internal documents also disclosed several other adjustments: 面试官将重点评估候选人的「AI 使用能力」，包括提示词工程、输出验证和调试技能。Google 发言人确认，试点阶段使用的 AI 助手将是公司自研模型 Gemini。内部文件还披露了其他几项调整：

  • The original "Googleyness and Leadership" session will add technical design discussions, requiring candidates to discuss past projects; 原有的「Googleyness 与领导力」环节将新增技术设计讨论，要求候选人就过往项目展开探讨；
  • For more junior candidates, one technical interview will be replaced with an "open engineering challenge" assessment. 针对更初级的候选人，一轮技术面试将被替换为「开放性工程挑战」形式的考核。

The new format mentioned above will be piloted this month in multiple departments, including Cloud and Platform & Devices. Google positions the overall direction as "human-led, AI-assisted," stating that the new process aims to more realistically simulate the actual work methods of software engineers in the era of generative AI. 上述新形式将于本月在 Cloud 及平台与设备等多个部门率先试点。Google 将整体方向定位为「人主导、AI 辅助」，并表示新流程旨在更真实地模拟生成式 AI 时代软件工程师的实际工作方式。

宇树 G1 在韩国曹溪寺受戒，获法名「迦悲」

According to a report by South Korea's JoongAng Ilbo, Jogyesa Temple in Seoul recently held an ordination ceremony for the humanoid robot G1, produced by China's Unitree Robotics. The robot was given the Dharma name "Jiabei" and officially became a "Buddhist disciple." This marks the first time the Jogye Order of Korean Buddhism has held such a ceremony for a humanoid robot. 据韩国《中央日报》报道，日前，韩国首尔曹溪寺为中国宇树科技生产的人形机器人 G1 举行受戒仪式，机器人获赐法名「迦悲」，正式成为「佛弟子」。这也是大韩佛教曹溪宗首次为人形机器人举办此类仪式。

The ceremony was held in the front courtyard of Jogyesa Temple's Daeungjeon Hall. The ordained robot, 130 cm tall, wore a kasaya and a shaved-head-style helmet, and took vows with palms together before masters such as Ven.
