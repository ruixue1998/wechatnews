IDC 预计 2030 年全球活跃智能体将超过 22 亿个

IDC pre-released the "DAA Research Report" at WAIC 2026, using Daily Active Agents to measure agents that actually enter business processes, complete tasks, and generate results each day. IDC 在 WAIC 2026 预发布《DAA 研究报告》，用 Daily Active Agents（日活智能体数）衡量每天实际进入业务流程、完成任务并产生结果的智能体。

The report predicts that the number of active agents globally will increase from 28.6 million in 2025 to 79.4 million in 2026, and reach 2.216 billion in 2030. 报告预计，全球活跃 Agent 数量将从 2025 年的 2860 万个增至 2026 年的 7940 万个，并在 2030 年达到 22.16 亿个。

The DAA metric was proposed by Baidu founder Robin Li at the Create 2026 conference, distinguishing itself from model parameters and token consumption by focusing on the actual usage of agents. Baidu also disclosed that the daily average number of questions for the general-purpose agent "Baidu Dazi" has increased 20-fold since its launch; Wenku Wangpan GenFlow's monthly active users have reached 100 million. DAA 指标由百度创始人李彦宏在 Create 2026 大会上提出，区别于模型参数和 Token 消耗，重点统计智能体的实际使用。百度同时披露，通用智能体「百度搭子」日均提问次数自上线以来增长 20 倍；文库网盘 GenFlow 月活用户达到 1 亿。

Baidu also released several application data points: As of July, applications generated by the no-code development tool "Miaoda" have cumulatively served over 35 million users and produced 3.5 million applications; the digital store manager "Baidu Yijian" covers four scenarios: food safety, service compliance, supply, sales, and inventory, and product quality, reducing the rate of kitchen violations in partner stores by 80%. 百度还公布了多项应用数据：截至 7 月，无代码开发工具秒哒生成的应用累计服务超过 3500 万用户，产生 350 万个应用；数字店长「百度一见」覆盖食品安全、服务合规、供销存和出品品质四类场景，合作门店的后厨违规操作率降低 80%。

Eggers stated that the impact of generative AI on teachers' work 'is catastrophic'. Eggers 表示，生成式 AI 对教师工作的影响「是灾难性的」。

If students use it to write, it's the most tragic thing; they will never learn to write. Their voices are also taken away. They will never have the ability to speak their truth or tell their own stories. This is silencing a generation or two. 如果学生使用它来写作，这是最悲剧的事情，他们永远不会学会写作。他们的声音也被夺走了。他们永远不会有能力说出自己的真相、讲述自己的故事。这正在让一代人或两代人噤声。

Reportedly, Eggers' speech stemmed from Sam Altman's previous affirmation of AI-generated fiction. This is not the first time Eggers has criticized the tech industry. In his novel 'The Circle,' he depicted scenarios where large tech companies erode personal privacy and independence through surveillance and data collection; in his new work 'Contrapposto,' he further emphasized the importance of mastering creative skills through practice, teaching, and repeated revision. 这并不是 Eggers 第一次批评科技行业。他在小说《The Circle》中描绘了大型科技公司通过监控和数据收集侵蚀个人隐私与独立性的场景；在新作《Contrapposto》中，他进一步强调了通过练习、教学和反复修改来掌握创作技艺的重要性。

Facing the rapid proliferation of generative AI, Eggers believes that technology is often adopted on a large scale before society has fully judged its value. 面对生成式 AI 的快速普及，Eggers 认为，技术往往在社会充分判断其价值之前就被大规模采用。

小米解释澎程增程车采用 76kWh 电池：日常尽量不启动增程器

Xiaomi Auto recently explained in its 261st 'Q&A with Netizens' that the Pengcheng series uses a maximum 76kWh battery to allow range-extended vehicles to maintain pure electric driving in most daily scenarios. The series has a maximum WLTC pure electric range of 380km and a maximum CLTC pure electric range of 505km. 小米汽车近日在第 261 集「答网友问」中解释，澎程系列采用最高 76kWh 电池，是为了让增程车在多数日常场景里维持纯电行驶。该系列 WLTC 纯电续航最高 380km，CLTC 纯电续航最高 505km。

Xiaomi stated that with lower speeds and more traffic lights in urban commuting, the actual range will be closer to CLTC conditions, and the 505km version can cover about a week of daily travel. The large battery also means users don't have to start the range extender early to preserve power; driving habits, ambient temperature, and air conditioning use will still affect the actual mileage. 小米称，城市通勤车速较低、红绿灯较多，实际续航会更接近 CLTC 工况，最高 505km 的版本可覆盖约一周日常出行。大电池也让用户不必为保留电量而提前启动增程器；驾驶习惯、环境温度和空调使用仍会改变实际里程。

The company summarizes this product philosophy as 'long-range pure electric': the range-extender system is primarily used to extend the pure electric experience during intercity and long-distance travel, rather than frequently intervening in short-distance commutes. 公司把这套产品思路概括为「纯电长续航」：增程系统主要用于跨城和长途时延续纯电体验，而不是在短距离通勤中频繁介入。
