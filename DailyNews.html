iOS27 快捷指令或迎来大改版，一句话就能制作

According to Bloomberg, Apple is preparing to introduce several new AI features in the upcoming iOS 27 and iPadOS 27, including system-level grammar checking, natural language shortcut creation, and AI-customized wallpapers. 据彭博社报道，苹果正筹备在即将发布的 iOS 27 和 iPadOS 27 中引入包括系统级语法检查、自然语言创建快捷指令及 AI 自定义壁纸在内的多项人工智能新功能。

Sources reveal that the core of Apple's upgrade lies in expanding system-level AI writing tools, adding a grammar checker similar to Grammarly. 知情人士透露，苹果此次升级的核心在于扩展系统级 AI 写作工具，新增类似 Grammarly 的语法检查器。

The feature interface pops up from the bottom of the screen as a translucent menu, displaying the original text and suggested revisions side-by-side. Users can choose to accept individual suggestions, apply all, or ignore them with one tap. Additionally, the system integrates "Write with Siri" and "Assisted Writing" shortcut options at the top of the keyboard. 该功能界面以半透明菜单形式从屏幕底部弹出，并列显示原文与修改建议，用户可选择逐条采纳、全部应用或一键忽略，同时系统在键盘顶部集成了「通过 Siri 撰写」和「辅助撰写」快捷选项。

Furthermore, the "Shortcuts" App will undergo a major redesign. Concurrently, the new system will integrate a generative AI wallpaper tool based on Image Playground. The aforementioned operating systems are expected to be released at the Global Developers Conference (WWDC) in June this year and pushed to the public in September. 同时，新系统将集成基于 Image Playground 的生成式 AI 壁纸工具。上述操作系统预计于今年 6 月的全球开发者大会（WWDC）上发布，并于 9 月向公众推送。

华为五一假期领跑中国手机市场、安卓厂商旧款机型均价上调 13%

According to Counterpoint Research's "China Smartphone Weekly Sales Tracker Report" released yesterday, China's smartphone sales decreased by 16% year-on-year in the two weeks around this year's May Day holiday. Rising storage prices pushing up overall device prices and weaker promotional efforts compared to previous years were the main reasons for the sales decline. 根据 Counterpoint Research 昨日发布的《中国智能手机周度销量追踪报告》，今年五一假期前后两周，中国智能手机销量同比下降 16%。存储价格上涨推高整机售价、促销力度弱于往年，是销量下滑的主要原因。

  • Huawei continues to lead the market, with its weekly sales share remaining above 25% in April. The Enjoy 90 Pro Max performed exceptionally well, and sales of the Pura 90 Pro series and the new foldable Pura X Max were strong after their late April launch; 华为继续领跑市场，4 月周度销量份额持续保持在 25% 以上。畅享 90 Pro Max 表现突出，Pura 90 Pro 系列及折叠屏新品 Pura X Max 于 4 月底上市后销量强劲；
  • Apple's sales during the May Day holiday increased by 3% year-on-year, but experienced a typical sequential slowdown. Demand for the iPhone 17 series is gradually returning to normal, and the collective price increases by domestic brands have objectively enhanced the iPhone's relative competitiveness; 苹果五一假期销量同比增长 3%，但环比出现常规性放缓。iPhone 17 系列需求逐步回归常态，国产品牌集体涨价客观上提升了 iPhone 的相对竞争力；
  • The average price of older models from other Android brands has increased by an average of 13%, which may further suppress replacement demand in the short term. 其他安卓品牌旧款机型均价已平均上调 13%，短期内或进一步压制换机需求。

Counterpoint Research expects that the 618 promotion will likely lead to a moderate market rebound in early June; China's smartphone shipments are projected to decline by 9% year-on-year for the full year, but still perform better than the global average. Counterpoint Research 预计，618 促销活动有望带动 6 月初市场温和回升；今年全年中国智能手机出货量预计同比下滑 9%，但仍优于全球平均水平。

特斯拉放弃印度建厂计划

According to Drive Safe and Fast, India's Minister of Heavy Industries, Kumaraswamy, officially confirmed yesterday that Tesla has abandoned its plans to build a factory in India. 据 Drive Safe and Fast 报道，印度重工业部部长库马拉瓦米（Kumaraswamy）昨日正式确认，特斯拉已放弃在印度建设工厂的计划。

Analysts point out that the decision to abandon the factory involves multiple obstacles. India's local supply chain and industrial ecosystem have significant gaps, making it difficult to support Tesla's manufacturing needs. Furthermore, the two parties have consistently failed to reach a consensus on the core issue of whether "tariff reductions should precede factory construction" or "factory construction should precede tariff reductions." 分析人士指出，此次放弃建厂涉及多重障碍。印度本土供应链与工业生态系统存在明显缺口，难以支撑特斯拉的制造需求。此外，双方就「关税减免应先于建厂」还是「建厂应先于关税减免」这一核心问题始终未能达成共识。

Insufficient infrastructure in India, the disparity between the local automotive market's consumption capacity and Tesla's high-end product positioning, and policy uncertainties and regulatory risks in India's business environment were also cited as major reasons. 印度基础设施的不足、当地汽车市场消费能力与特斯拉高端产品定位之间的落差，以及印度营商环境中存在的政策不确定性与监管风险，也被列为主要原因。

💡 强化学习之父 Sutton 重申「苦涩的教训」引争议

Richard Sutton, the father of reinforcement learning, posted on X yesterday, reiterating his "Bitter Lesson" from 2019 in 26 English words: 强化学习之父 Richard Sutton 昨日在 X 发文，用 26 个英文单词重申了他在 2019 年提出的「苦涩的教训」：

AI development should not be distracted by human knowledge, but should focus on general methods that can scale with computational power, such as search and learning. AI 发展不应被人类知识分散注意力，而应专注于能随算力扩展的通用方法，例如搜索与学习。

"The Bitter Lesson" was first published in 2019 and is one of the most widely circulated assertions in the field of AI. After reviewing decades of AI history, Sutton pointed out that researchers repeatedly tried to encode human expertise into systems, but general methods relying on computational power always prevailed—this was the case for computer chess, Go, and speech recognition. 「苦涩的教训」首发于 2019 年，是 AI 领域流传最广的论断之一。Sutton 梳理数十年 AI 历史后指出，研究者反复试图将人类专业知识编入系统，但依靠算力扩展的通用方法每次都会胜出——计算机象棋、围棋、语音识别都是如此。

This assertion later became an important theoretical basis for the "scaling" approach of large language models. 这一论断后来成为大语言模型「规模扩展」路线的重要理论依据。

After this 26-character condensed version was released, several scholars directly raised objections. Thomas G. Dietterich, a pioneer in machine learning, responded that studying the abstract structure of human knowledge can identify cognitive blind spots for AI, and such work should not be called a "distraction." 此次 26 字浓缩版发出后，多位学者直接提出异议。机器学习奠基人 Thomas G. Dietterich 回复称，研究人类知识的抽象结构能够为 AI 标定认知盲区，这种工作不应被称为「分心」。

In the comments section, a long article titled "The Sweet Lesson" was cited multiple times. John Fletcher, co-founder and chief scientist of The Innovation Game, argued in the article that Sutton's assertion itself is a form of "encoded knowledge" that can tell you where the destination is, but not how to get there. 评论区中，一篇题为《甜蜜的教训》的长文被多次引用。The Innovation Game 联合创始人兼首席科学家 John Fletcher 在文中提出，Sutton 的论断本身就是一种「编码知识」，它能告诉你终点在哪，但无法告诉你如何抵达。

Fletcher believes that current training data faces the bottleneck of depleted written corpora, and the "tacit knowledge" — intuition, experience, and judgment — that has never been written down in experts' brains is the true battlefield of current industry competition. Fletcher 认为，当前训练数据面临书面语料枯竭的瓶颈，专家大脑中从未被写下来的「隐性知识」——直觉、经验、判断力，才是产业当前竞争的真正战场。

As of March this year, the monthly active user scale for integrated e-commerce, local life services, food delivery services, and fresh food e-commerce was 1.095 billion, 569 million, 189 million, and 137 million, respectively. After the subsidy tide receded, the focus of platform competition has shifted from price to supply chain efficiency and user mindshare. QuestMobile 昨日发布《2026 本地生活消费洞察报告》。截至今年 3 月，综合电商、本地生活、美食服务、生鲜电商月活规模分别为 10.95 亿、5.69 亿、1.89 亿、1.37 亿。补贴退潮后，平台竞争重心已从价格转向供应链效率与用户心智。

  • Traffic on food delivery platforms generally declined, with users migrating to integrated consumption platforms; the monthly active users in the food service industry decreased by 3.6% year-on-year; 外卖平台流量普遍下滑，用户向综合消费平台迁移；美食服务行业月活同比下降 3.6%；
  • "Store-warehouse integration" becomes mainstream: Meituan promotes dual-brand front-end warehouses, JD 7FRESH focuses on asset-heavy stores, and Hema accelerates its layout in lower-tier markets; 「店仓一体」成主流：美团推双品牌前置仓，京东七鲜押注重资产门店，盒马加速下沉市场布局；
  • In-store group buying becomes a new battlefield: Dianping's growth rate dropped to 11.4%, DouShengSheng's daily active users are close to 16 million, and users in second-tier and lower cities account for 67.3%; 到店团购成新战场：大众点评增速降至 11.4%，抖省省日活已接近 1600 万，二线及以下城市用户占比达 67.3%；
  • Rider capacity extends to lower-tier cities: Meituan Crowdsourcing and Fengniao Crowdsourcing saw their proportions in third-tier and below cities increase by 6.2% and 12.9% year-on-year, respectively; 骑手运力向下沉延伸：美团众包、蜂鸟众包在三线及以下城市占比分别同比提升 6.2%、12.9%；
  • The battle for AI entry points heats up: Alibaba's Tongyi Qianwen integrates consumption and payment links, while Meituan and JD.com deploy AI assistants and unmanned delivery, respectively. AI 入口争夺升温：阿里千问打通消费与支付链路，美团、京东分别布局 AI 助手与无人配送。

第二艘国产大型邮轮今日开售

According to CCTV News, China's domestic cruise brand Adora Cruises announced yesterday that its second domestically built large cruise ship, "Adora Flora," will embark on its maiden voyage from Guangzhou Nansha International Cruise Terminal on November 22. 据央视新闻报道，国产邮轮品牌爱达邮轮昨日公布，第二艘国产大型邮轮「爱达·花城号」将于 11 月 22 日从广州南沙国际邮轮母港开启首航。

The maiden voyage season routes will officially go on sale on May 20, covering November 22, 2026, to January 2, 2027, and include 6 short, medium, and long-haul products. The routes are aimed at family travel, holiday trips, and cruise experience users. 首航季航线将于 5 月 20 日正式开售，覆盖 2026 年 11 月 22 日至 2027 年 1 月 2 日，包含 6 个短、中、长线产品。航线面向家庭旅行、假期出行和邮轮体验用户。

"Adora Flora" is the second domestically built large cruise ship, following the first. Guangzhou Nansha will serve as its maiden voyage homeport and will also accommodate more cultural tourism travel demands from South China. 「爱达 · 花城号」是继首艘国产大型邮轮之后的第二艘国产大型邮轮。广州南沙将成为其首航母港，也将承接更多华南文旅出行需求。

花生漫画家族加入索尼大家庭

Sony announced early yesterday morning that the Peanuts comic family has officially joined the Sony family. 索尼昨日凌晨宣布，花生漫画家族正式加入索尼大家庭。

In March this year, Sony Music Entertainment (Japan) and Sony Pictures Entertainment announced the official completion of their equity acquisition of Peanuts Holdings LLC, the parent company of "Peanuts," with a combined shareholding ratio reaching 80%. 今年 3 月，索尼音乐娱乐（日本）与索尼影视娱乐宣布正式完成对《花生漫画》母公司 Peanuts Holdings LLC 的股权收购，合计持股比例达80%。

Following changes in the equity structure, the family of Charles M. Schulz, creator of "Peanuts," will continue to retain the remaining 20% stake in the IP. 根据股权结构变动，《花生漫画》（PEANUTS）创作者查尔斯 · 舒尔茨（Charles M. Schulz）的家族将继续保留该 IP 剩余 20% 的股份。

Sony stated that in the future, the ownership rights and daily business operations of the "PEANUTS" brand will continue to be managed by Peanuts Worldwide LLC, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Peanuts Holdings LLC. 索尼方面称，未来「PEANUTS」品牌的权利所有权和日常业务运营，将继续交由 Peanuts Holdings LLC 旗下的全资子公司 Peanuts Worldwide LLC 负责管理。
