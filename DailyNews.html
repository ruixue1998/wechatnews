300 台机器人跑半马，冠军成绩打破人类纪录，「豆包」也来凑热闹

Yesterday, the 2026 Beijing Yizhuang Humanoid Robot Half Marathon officially concluded. This year's event attracted over 300 humanoid robots and 26 mainstream brands, with the participating lineup growing nearly 5 times compared to last year. Honor's "Lightning" robots swept the top three places: 昨天，2026 北京亦庄人形机器人半程马拉松正式落幕。本届赛事吸引了 300 余台人形机器人、26 个主流品牌参赛，参赛阵容较去年实现近 5 倍增长。荣耀「闪电」机器人包揽前三名：

  • 🥇 Champion: Monkey King Team, net time 50 minutes 26 seconds; 🥇 冠军：齐天大圣队，净用时 50 分 26 秒；
  • 🥈 Runner-up: Thunder Lightning Team, net time 50 minutes 56 seconds; 🥈 亚军：雷霆闪电队，净用时 50 分 56 秒；
  • 🥉 Third Place: Spark Spreading Team, net time 53 minutes 01 second. 🥉 季军：星火燎原队，净用时 53 分 01 秒。

All top three finished within 53 minutes, not only significantly surpassing last year's champion TianGong Ultra's 2 hours 40 minutes, but also comprehensively breaking the human half-marathon world record (56 minutes 42 seconds). 前三名全部跑进 53 分钟，不仅大幅超越去年冠军天工 Ultra 的 2 小时 40 分，也全面刷新了人类半马世界纪录（56 分 42 秒）。

The track difficulty significantly increased compared to last year. The full 21.0975 kilometers course for the first time introduced an ecological section of Nanhai Wetland Park, integrating over 10 types of terrain including flat ground, slopes, curves, and narrow sections, comprising 22 turns and multiple sharp turns approaching 90°, posing a strong challenge to robots' dynamic balance and path planning. 赛道难度相较去年显著提升。全程 21.0975 公里，首次引入南海子公园生态路段，融合平地、坡道、弯道、狭窄路段等 10 余种地形，包含 22 个弯道及多处接近 90° 的急转，对机器人的动态平衡与路径规划构成较强挑战。

Regarding race rules, participating teams are divided into autonomous navigation group (performance coefficient 1.0) and remote control group (performance coefficient 1.2), with the proportion of autonomous navigation teams having jumped to 38%. 赛事规则方面，参赛队伍分为自主导航组（成绩系数 1.0）与遥控组（成绩系数 1.2），自主导航参赛队伍占比已跃升至 38%。

There were also interesting scenes on the track. Some robots suddenly braked while running, others "staged a fender bender" by sticking to the roadside fence; some robots fell to the ground just before crossing the finish line, then shakily got up with engineer assistance to complete the crossing; another one, after crossing the finish line, continued straight into the nearby green belt. 赛场上亦不乏趣味画面。有机器人跑着跑着突然刹停，有机器人贴上路边围栏完成「碰瓷表演」；有机器人在冲线前一刻栽倒在地，在工程师协助下颤颤巍巍爬起完成撞线；还有一台冲过终点后一鼓作气冲进了旁边的绿化带。

A "Doubao" humanoid robot, self-made by a Douyin blogger, also became a focal point on the track — with its round head, iconic smiling face, and short, shuffling steps, it even performed stretching exercises before the race. Related videos quickly went viral on social media. This robot belonged to the remote control group and was operated by the blogger from inside a follow car. 一名抖音博主自制的「豆包」人形机器人同样成为赛场焦点——圆脑袋、标志性笑脸、短腿小碎步，赛前还做了拉伸动作，相关视频在社交媒体上迅速走红。该机器人属遥控操作组，由博主在跟随车内进行操控。

受内存影响，新 MacBook 发布或延期

According to the latest report from Bloomberg, Apple's planned new Mac Studio and touchscreen MacBook Pro models are facing launch delays due to industry-wide memory supply shortages. 据彭博社最新报道，受全行业内存供应短缺影响，苹果原计划推出的新款 Mac Studio 与触屏版 MacBook Pro 面临发售延期。

The report revealed that Apple had originally planned to release a new Mac Studio equipped with a new high-end chip in the middle of this year, as the successor to the previous M4 Max and M3 Ultra models. 据报道透露，苹果原定于今年年中发布搭载新型高端芯片的 Mac Studio，作为前代 M4 Max 及 M3 Ultra 机型的继任者。

However, due to ongoing tightness in the high-spec memory supply chain, the shipping period for some high-memory Mac products currently on sale has been extended by several months. Internal assessments suggest that the actual shipping time for the new Mac Studio will likely be postponed until around October this year. 然而，因高规格内存供应链持续紧张，当前部分在售的高内存 Mac 产品的发货周期已被拉长数月，内部评估认为新款 Mac Studio 的实际出货时间大概率将推迟至今年 10 月前后。

At the same time, the launch schedule for the touchscreen MacBook Pro, originally planned for late 2026 to early 2027, has also been affected. #NewMacBookDelay# 同时，原计划于 2026 年底至 2027 年初问世的触屏版 MacBook Pro 发售节奏亦受波及。#新MacBook延期#

This product series features a lightweight and thin design, with the screen introducing a punch-hole "Dynamic Island" camera module and planning to be equipped with an M6 chip. Although the accompanying software system will be ready for development this autumn, due to physical supply chain shortages, its final market launch will fall to the latest period of the planned time window. 该系列产品主打轻薄化设计，屏幕将引入打孔式「灵动岛」摄像头模块，并计划搭载 M6 芯片。尽管配套软件系统将在今年秋季开发就绪，但受制于物理供应链短缺，其最终上市时间将滑落至规划时间窗口的最晚期。

微信朋友圈迎 14 周年，官方分享内部细节

Yesterday marked the 14th anniversary of WeChat Moments' launch. WeChat's official account "WeChat Pai" published an article sharing early stories and internal details behind the birth of Moments. 昨天是微信朋友圈上线 14 周年，微信官方公众号「微信派」发文，分享了朋友圈诞生背后的一批早期故事与内部细节。

On April 19, 2012, WeChat version 4.0 passed App Store review and officially launched, and the Moments feature was introduced to users. Regarding this date, the team frankly stated there was no special meaning, "it was just the day the App Store approved our new version." 2012 年 4 月 19 日，微信 4.0 版本通过 App Store 审核并正式上线，朋友圈功能随之与用户见面。对于这一日期，团队坦言并无特殊寓意，「只不过是那天 App Store 通过了我们新版本的审核」。

Before its official launch, the internal team went through a total of 34 test versions, exhausting all 26 English letters for version numbers and having to switch to other symbols for continued labeling. 而在正式上线之前，团队内部共经历了 34 个测试版本，版本号将 26 个英文字母全部用尽，不得不改用其他符号继续标注。

WeChat officials also shared several design trade-offs made for Moments. 微信官方还分享了朋友圈在产品设计上的几处取舍。

The "Like" and "Comment" functions were deliberately hidden behind buttons rather than directly exposed, to ensure that the content itself remains central in the Timeline. Moments still has no filter function because "real life shouldn't have filters." 「赞」和「评论」功能被刻意收纳进隐蔽按钮，而非直接外露，目的是确保内容本身在 Timeline 中保持主体地位。朋友圈至今没有滤镜功能，是因为「真正的生活状态不应该有滤镜。」

The plain text posting feature was initially just an internal test easter egg. The team believed that posting text required thought and editing, a much higher barrier than posting photos or videos, so it was not initially opened as an official feature. Today, this "internal test easter egg" has become a default user operation. 纯文字发布功能早期仅是一个内部测试用的彩蛋。团队认为，发文字需要经过思考和编辑，门槛远高于发照片或视频，因此最初并未将其作为正式功能对外开放。如今，这一「内测彩蛋」已成为用户的默认操作。

蔚来官方回应 ES9 仍采用隐藏式门把手：目前完全合规

Recently, Nio issued a statement responding to external doubts about the compliance of the hidden door handles on its ES9 model, clearly stating that all Nio models currently on sale can normally use hidden door handles and are fully compliant. 日前，蔚来发文回应外界对旗下 ES9 车型隐藏式门把手的合规性质疑，明确表示目前蔚来全系在售车型均可正常使用隐藏式门把手，且完全合规。

Nio stated that the mandatory national standard "Safety Technical Requirements for Automobile Door Handles," officially released on February 2 this year, includes a clause prohibiting hidden handles, which stipulates that new models announced before December 31, 2027, and current/facelift models manufactured before December 31, 2028, may continue to use hidden handles. 蔚来表示，今年 2 月 2 日正式发布的强制性国家标准《汽车车门把手安全技术要求》，其中，禁止隐藏式把手的条款要求：2027 年 12 月 31 日前上公告的全新车型，以及 2028 年 12 月 31 日前出厂的现款/改款车型，均可继续使用隐藏式把手。

Regarding safety mechanisms, Nio officially claims that the ES9 is equipped with multiple redundant designs: 在安全机制方面，蔚来官方宣称 ES9 配备了多重冗余设计：

  • The vehicle will automatically unlock immediately after detecting a collision signal. Even if the door handle fails to pop out automatically, the door can still be opened mechanically by pressing the front or rear end of the handle, facilitating rescue by external personnel; 车辆检测到碰撞信号后会立即自动解锁，即便门把手未能自动弹出，仍可通过按压把手前端或后端以机械结构开门，方便车外人员施救；
  • Each door inside the car is equipped with an emergency mechanical pull handle at the map pocket, along with fluorescent reminder stickers to ensure visibility at night. The child lock will also automatically unlock after a collision; 车内每扇车门的地图袋处均设有应急机械拉手，配合荧光提示贴纸，确保夜间可见、儿童锁在碰撞后也会自动解锁；
  • In terms of power supply, the ES9 adopts a dual low-voltage battery redundant power supply and a dual-channel communication architecture, ensuring that the door lock system can still operate normally even in extreme cases of single-channel power failure. 供电层面，ES9 采用双低压电池冗余供电与双路通信架构，确保在单路断电的极端情况下门锁系统仍可正常运作。

内存短缺或延续至 2027 年，三大厂商产能仅能满足六成需求

According to Nikkei Asia, the global memory chip shortage is expected to last until around 2027. Samsung Electronics, SK Hynix, and Micron Technology's DRAM capacity expansion can only meet about 60% of the market demand by then, while these three companies collectively account for approximately 90% of the global DRAM market share. 据《日经亚洲》报道，全球内存芯片短缺预计将持续至 2027 年前后，三星电子、海力士与美光科技的 DRAM 产能扩张速度仅能满足届时市场需求的约 60%，而三家企业合计占据全球 DRAM 市场约 90% 的份额。

In recent years, they have concentrated resources on producing High Bandwidth Memory (HBM) required for AI data centers, leading to sustained pressure on the supply of general-purpose memory. The shortage has gradually become apparent since last autumn, with memory prices rising by approximately 90% quarter-on-quarter in the first quarter of this year. 近年来，它们将资源集中投入 AI 数据中心所需的高带宽内存（HBM）生产，导致通用型内存供给持续受压，短缺态势自去年秋季起已逐步显现，今年第一季度内存价格环比涨幅约达 90%。

In terms of capacity, most of the new production lines from the three manufacturers will not be operational until next year or even after 2028. SK Hynix's HBM wafer fab, which started in Cheongju in February this year, is the only substantial capacity increase among the three this year. 产能方面，三家厂商的新增产线大多要到明年乃至 2028 年后才能投产。海力士今年 2 月在清州启动的 HBM 晶圆厂，是三家中今年唯一实质性的产能增量。

SK Group Chairman Chey Tae-won stated in mid-March this year that the tight supply of AI memory might even extend until 2030. Counterpoint Research estimates that resolving the shortage requires the industry to expand capacity by 12% annually, but current plans from all parties can only achieve 7.5%, meaning that the supply-demand relationship is not expected to normalize until 2028 at the earliest . SK 集团会长崔泰源今年 3 月中旬表示，AI 内存供给紧张甚至可能延续至 2030 年。Counterpoint Research 估算，化解短缺需行业每年扩产 12%，而目前各方规划仅能实现 7.5%， 供需关系最快要到 2028 年才有望正常化 。

The impact of the shortage is spreading to consumer electronics. The proportion of memory costs in the manufacturing cost of low-priced smartphones is expected to rise from about 20% currently to nearly 40% by mid-year. Products such as mobile phones, laptops, and VR headsets have all seen varying degrees of price increases. IDC data shows that smartphone sales are expected to decline by 13% year-on-year this year. 短缺冲击正向消费电子蔓延。内存成本占低价智能手机制造成本的比例，预计将从当前约 20% 升至今年年中的近 40%，手机、笔记本电脑、VR 头显等产品均已出现不同程度的涨价。IDC 数据显示，今年智能手机销量预计同比下滑 13%。

Panic：AI 写的文案、画的图、作的曲，Playdate 一律不收

Recently, Panic announced that its Playdate game store, Catalog, will no longer accept games that use generative AI for art, audio, music, text, or dialogue content. The Season 3 game collection also completely prohibits works developed using generative AI in any form. 日前，Panic 宣布，旗下 Playdate 游戏商店 Catalog 将不再接受使用生成式 AI 制作美术、音频、音乐、文字或对话内容的游戏作品，第三季游戏合集亦全面禁止以任何形式使用生成式 AI 开发的作品。

According to the official AI usage statement, the ban covers large language models such as ChatGPT, DeepSeek, and Google Gemini; image generation models like Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, and Midjourney; and audio generation models such as MuseNet, Suno, and Udio; 根据官方 AI 使用声明，禁令涵盖 ChatGPT、DeepSeek、Google Gemini 等大语言模型，Stable Diffusion、DALL-E、Midjourney 等图像生成模型，以及 MuseNet、Suno、Udio 等音频生成模型；

However, works that use AI-assisted tools in the programming phase are temporarily exempt. The platform will label such works and specify the extent of AI use, allowing players to decide whether to support them. 但编程环节使用 AI 辅助工具的作品暂获豁免，平台将对此类作品进行标注并注明具体使用程度，以便玩家自主决定是否支持。

This policy adjustment stems from the controversy surrounding the simulation racing game "Wheelsprung" in June last year—the game was found to have used GitHub Copilot and ChatGPT for assisted development after being selected for the Season 2 collection. 此次政策调整源于去年 6 月模拟赛车游戏《Wheelsprung》的争议——该游戏入选第二季合集后被发现使用了 GitHub Copilot 与 ChatGPT 辅助开发。

Panic co-founder Cabel Sasser admitted in an interview with "Exp.": "We completely did not anticipate developers using large language models at the time. In retrospect, it was too naive, and we take full responsibility for it." Panic 联合创始人 Cabel Sasser 在接受《Exp.》采访时坦承：「我们当时完全没有预料到开发者会使用大语言模型，回头来看过于天真，我们对此承担全部责任。」

The film is co-developed and produced by Paramount Pictures and Activision, with Paramount responsible for distribution, and Sheridan and David Glasser serving as co-producers. 该片由彼得·博格执导，剧本由博格与《黄石》《边境杀手》编剧泰勒·谢里丹联合创作。影片由派拉蒙影业与动视联合开发制作，派拉蒙负责发行，谢里丹与 David Glasser 同任制片人。

Producer Kostich stated at the press conference that he and Berg had always hoped to find "a partner who truly cares about this IP," and they found that fit with Paramount CEO David Ellison. Berg emphasized his deep connection with the special operations community, hoping the film would present the subject matter with "authenticity" and "grand scope." 制片人 Kostich 在发布会上表示，他与博格一直希望找到「真正在乎这个 IP 的合作伙伴」，并在派拉蒙 CEO David Ellison 身上找到了这种契合。博格则强调自己与特种作战群体有深厚渊源，希望影片能以「真实感」和「宏大格局」来呈现这一题材。
