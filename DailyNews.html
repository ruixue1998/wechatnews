DeepSeek 融资从 50 亿门槛降到 15 亿，梁文锋强调团队稳定

"elsewhere" yesterday published an article revealing multiple details about DeepSeek's financing process. 「elsewhere 别处发生」昨日发文披露 DeepSeek 融资过程中的多项细节。

The article stated that DeepSeek officially launched its financing in April this year, initially requiring a single institution's quota to be no less than 5 billion yuan, with no split shares, and adopting an RMB structure; ultimately, the minimum fund contribution was reduced to 1.5 billion yuan, and participation methods became more flexible. 文章称，DeepSeek 今年 4 月正式启动融资，最初要求单家机构额度不低于 50 亿元、不能拆分份额，且采用人民币结构；最终基金最小出资额降至 15 亿元，参与方式也更灵活。

The article mentioned that DeepSeek organized an online investor conference in mid-May, where Liang Wenfeng spoke first, followed by answering investor questions. Many investors had not met Liang Wenfeng offline before, and this conference became their first direct interaction. 文章提到，DeepSeek 5 月中旬曾组织一场线上投资人会议，由梁文锋先讲，再回答投资人问题。多位投资人此前没有线下见过梁文锋，这场会议成为他们第一次直接交流。

Liang Wenfeng repeatedly emphasized at the meeting that they only do things related to improving intelligence, maintain team stability, and address talent risks beyond "money and resources." Furthermore, one of Liang Wenfeng's important demands to major companies and VCs is not to poach DeepSeek's people or instigate team members to start businesses . 梁文锋在会上反复强调，只做与提高智能有关的事情、保持团队稳定，以及「钱和资源」之外的人才风险。此外，梁文锋对大厂和 VC 的重要要求之一，是 不要挖 DeepSeek 的人或撺掇团队成员创业 。

Regarding the investor list, the article stated that only 10 institutions ultimately participated, but after penetrating the fund entities, the actual participants behind them were close to 100 institutions or individuals, including local state-owned assets, listed company-related entities, and industrial capital. 投资人名单方面，文章称最终仅有 10 家机构参与，但穿透基金主体后，背后实际参与方接近 100 家机构或个人，包括地方国资、上市公司相关主体和产业资本。

曝 Manus 早期投资者拟 20 亿美元回购公司

According to The Information, early Chinese investors in AI agent company Manus are planning to repurchase the company from Meta at the $2 billion price (approximately 13.5 billion RMB) Meta paid last year, with the repurchase deal currently in progress. 据《The Information》报道，AI 智能体公司 Manus 的早期中国投资者正计划以 Meta 去年收购时支付的 20 亿美元价格（约合 135 亿元人民币），将公司从 Meta 手中回购，目前回购交易仍在推进中。

追觅系进行组织调整，手机、汽车、芯片业务归入产业研究院

According to KrASIA, Dreame Technology is currently consolidating and adjusting its various business units. Existing businesses will be merged into four main sectors: whole-house smart cleaning, smart home appliances, smart mobility, and AI embodied intelligence. Some BUs that have not yet launched actual business or deployed personnel will be canceled. 据硬氪报道，追觅系近日正在对各业务单元进行收缩和调整，现有业务将合并为四大主营板块，包括全屋智能清洁、智能家电、智慧出行和 AI 具身智能。一些尚未开展实际业务落地及人员落地的 BU 将被取消。

After the adjustment, Dreame Technology will sell related products through brands such as Dreame, MOVA, NAVEE, SKYMOTOR, and Magic Atom. 报道称，智能清洁板块目前人员接近 1.2 万人，占公司整体人数超过 5 成，其中七成以上为研发人员。调整后，追觅系将通过追觅、MOVA、坦途（NAVEE）、首驱（SKYMOTOR）、魔法原子等品牌对外销售相关产品。

💡 亚马逊创始人：AI 可能创造劳动力短缺，而不是大规模失业

According to Business Insider, Amazon founder Jeff Bezos stated yesterday at the VivaTech technology summit in Paris that AI will not eliminate jobs but will instead cause labor shortages. 据《商业内幕》报道，亚马逊创始人杰夫·贝索斯 (Jeff Bezos) 昨日在巴黎 VivaTech 科技峰会上表示，AI 不会消灭就业岗位，反而将造成劳动力短缺。

In his view, the core value of AI lies in lowering the barrier to bringing ideas to fruition. Bezos called this process the "dream-build cycle," believing that technological progress will unlock many ideas previously shelved due to excessively high execution costs. 在他看来，AI 的核心价值在于降低将创意付诸实践的门槛。贝索斯将这一过程称为「梦想-构建循环」，认为技术进步将解锁大量此前因执行成本过高而被搁置的想法。

I can guarantee that everyone here has had an idea for starting a business or developing a new product, but that idea ultimately didn't materialize for a simple reason – it was too difficult, not worth doing... If we can accelerate this cycle, all ideas will become possible. We will no longer be limited by capability, but only by imagination. 我敢保证，在场每一个人都有过创业或开发新产品的想法，但那个想法最终没有落地，原因很简单——太难了，不值得去做……如果我们能加速这个循环，所有的想法都将成为可能。我们将不再受能力限制，而只受想象力限制。

He believes that as the execution barrier lowers, society's demand for builders, creators, and entrepreneurs who can bring ideas to fruition will rise, leading to a labor market facing shortages rather than surpluses. 他认为，当执行门槛降低，社会对能够落地创意的建设者、创造者和创业者的需求将随之上升，人力市场将面临供不应求，而非过剩。

During his speech, Bezos also discussed AI's potential impact on space exploration, stating that if space travel becomes sufficiently reliable and low-cost, humanity might be able to relocate heavy industries to space, allowing Earth to gradually return to its natural state before the Industrial Revolution. 在演讲中，贝索斯还谈及 AI 对太空探索的潜在影响，称若太空旅行变得足够可靠且低成本，人类或许可将重工业迁移至太空，使地球逐步恢复至工业革命前的自然状态。
