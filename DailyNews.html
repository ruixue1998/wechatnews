星舰 V3 首飞成功，22 颗模拟卫星成功释放，IPO 前关键验证完成

Early this morning, SpaceX successfully launched its 12th Starship test flight from its Starbase facility in Texas, USA, completing the maiden flight of the new Starship V3. The rocket's launch was aborted the day before due to a hydraulic pin malfunction. 今天凌晨，SpaceX 从美国德克萨斯州星舰基地成功发射第 12 次星舰测试飞行，完成了全新星舰 (Starship) V3 的首飞。火箭此前一天曾因液压销钉故障中止发射。

The Starship V3 launched this time consists of the upper stage Ship 39 and the Super Heavy booster Booster 19, with an overall height exceeding 40 stories, making it the largest and most powerful rocket ever flown . 此次发射的 Starship V3 由上级飞船 Ship 39 与 Super Heavy 助推器 Booster 19 组成，整体高度超过 40 层楼， 是迄今为止飞行过的最大、最强火箭 。

The V3 version features multiple upgrades, including redesigned Raptor 3 engines (greater thrust, lower weight), larger capacity fuel tanks, and propulsion system improvements for future in-orbit refueling and rendezvous and docking. V3 版本搭载了多项升级，包括重新设计的 Raptor 3 发动机（更大推力、更低重量）、更大容量燃料箱，以及用于未来在轨加注和交会对接的推进系统改进。

After liftoff, the booster's first stage failed to complete its return ignition after stage separation and subsequently crashed uncontrolled into the Gulf of Mexico. The ship's upper stage experienced an engine shutdown during ascent but successfully entered its intended orbit by extending the burn with the remaining five engines. 升空后，助推器一级在完成级间分离后未能完成返回点火，随即失控坠入墨西哥湾。飞船上级在上升过程中出现一台发动机停机，但凭借剩余五台发动机延长燃烧成功进入预定轨道。

The ship then successfully opened its payload bay, releasing all 22 simulated Starlink satellites, with a deployment speed significantly faster than previous versions. Two of these improved simulated satellites, named "Dodger Dogs," carried cameras to photograph the ship's heat shield from the outside, providing data for subsequent improvements. 飞船随后顺利开启载荷舱，将 22 颗模拟星链卫星全部释放，部署速度明显快于此前版本。其中两颗被称为「Dodger Dogs」的改进型模拟卫星携带摄像头，在飞船外对其隔热板进行拍摄，为后续改进提供数据。

Additionally, SpaceX decided to abandon the planned in-orbit restart experiment for the Raptor engine. As of press time, the ship is completing its atmospheric re-entry phase, having passed through the highest heat flux region, and is expected to splash down in the Indian Ocean. 此外，SpaceX 决定放弃原计划的 Raptor 发动机在轨重启实验。截至发稿，飞船正在完成再入大气层阶段，通过了最高热流区，预计将在印度洋溅落。

微信解释消息撤回为何限制为 2 分钟

WeChat Pai yesterday explained the design basis for WeChat's ordinary messages being "retractable within 2 minutes." The WeChat team stated that within 2 minutes, the recipient has likely not finished reading, and retracting is closer to "rephrasing"; after 2 minutes, the recipient has usually read or even replied, and retracting at this point would disrupt the continuity of the chat history. 微信派昨日解释了微信普通消息「2 分钟内可撤回」的设计依据。微信团队称，2 分钟内对方大概率还没读完，撤回更接近于「重新说」；超过 2 分钟后，对方通常已经阅读甚至回复，此时撤回会破坏聊天记录的连续性。

The WeChat team also explained two complementary designs: 微信团队还解释了两个配套设计：

  • Firstly, file-type messages allow for a longer retraction period because there is a natural "time lag" for recipients to view files, as they might be in a meeting or commuting; 其一，文件类消息允许更长撤回时限，是因为接收方查看文件天然存在「时差」，可能正在开会或通勤；
  • Secondly, the person whose message is retracted will still see a notification because communication tools need to ensure delivery accuracy; if messages disappear without a trace, "what was sent" and "what was received" would become unverifiable questions. 其二，被撤回的人仍会看到提示，是因为通信工具需要保障送达准确性，如果消息毫无痕迹地消失，「发过什么」和「收到没有」会变成无法核对的问题。
