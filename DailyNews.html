iPad Pro 未来或将配备均热板

According to a report last night by Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman, Apple is expected to equip future iPad Pro models with vapor chamber cooling. 据彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 昨晚报道，苹果有望在未来的 iPad Pro 新机配备均热板散热。

Gurman pointed out that the new vapor chamber in the iPhone 17 Pro series performs excellently, solving the iPhone's long-standing thermal issues. Gurman 指出，iPhone 17 Pro 系列新增的均热板表现出色，解决了 iPhone 被诟病已久的散热问题。

According to his revelations, Apple has currently put the development of vapor chambers for the iPad Pro on its company roadmap — planning to apply this technology in the next wave of new device updates. Gurman analyzed that Apple typically has an 18-month update cycle for the iPad Pro, so an iPad Pro with vapor chamber cooling might debut in the spring of 2027. 据其透露，苹果目前已在公司路线图上，将 iPad Pro 的均热板开发提上日程 —— 计划在下一波新机更新中运用该技术。Gurman 分析，苹果通常对 iPad Pro 的更新周期为 18 个月，因此带均热板散热的 iPad Pro 可能会在 2027 年春季面世。

Gurman also stated that vapor chambers can not only solve the thermal issues of iPhones and iPads but also serve as a "prelude" for Apple to make the MacBook Air fanless — if all goes well, the MacBook Air is also expected to adopt the new vapor chamber technology. Gurman 还表示，均热板不仅能够解决 iPhone、iPad 的散热问题，同时这也是苹果为 MacBook Air 的无风扇化进行「铺垫」—— 若一切顺利，MacBook Air 也有望用上新的均热板技术。

It's worth noting that Gurman said the iPad Air has been getting closer to the Pro models in recent years, so vapor chambers might become a new "tier-limiting" feature between the iPad Pro and iPad Air. 值得一提的是，Gurman 称，iPad Air 近年来越来越接近 Pro 机型，因此均热板可能会成为 iPad Pro 和 iPad Air 之间新的「等级限制」功能。

DeepSeek 领跑 AI 实盘交易对决，收益达 9.68%

According to "Xinzhiyuan," the open-source project "AI-Trader" led by Professor Huang Chao's team at the University of Hong Kong recently announced its latest results. The DeepSeek model ranked first in real U.S. stock trading experiments with a 9.68% return rate, significantly outperforming top international large models such as GPT, Claude, and Gemini. 据「新智元」报道，香港大学黄超教授团队主导的开源项目「AI-Trader」近日公布最新成果，DeepSeek 模型在真实美股交易实验中以 9.68% 的收益率位居第一，显著超越 GPT、Claude、Gemini 等国际顶级大模型。

In the experiment, the research team allocated $10,000 to each of the 5 AI models and allowed them to autonomously trade in the Nasdaq 100 component stock market for nearly a month. 在实验中，研究团队为 5 个 AI 模型分别配置 1 万美元资金，并允许其在纳斯达克 100 成分股市场自主交易近一个月。

The rules strictly adhered to the "three no-principles": no trading scripts provided, no manual intervention allowed, and no cheating channels open. Ultimately, DeepSeek demonstrated a robust investment strategy, precisely allocating capital to tech giants like NVDA (Nvidia), AAPL (Apple), and MSFT (Microsoft), and achieving risk diversification through multi-asset portfolios and dynamic rebalancing. 规则严格限定「三不原则」：不提供交易剧本、不允许人工干预、不开放作弊通道。最终，DeepSeek 展现出稳健的投资策略，精准布局 NVDA（英伟达）、AAPL（苹果）、MSFT（微软）等科技巨头，并通过多标的组合与动态调仓实现风险分散。

In contrast, Gemini conducted 73 high-frequency trades in a short period, resulting in a 2.73% loss due to a lack of risk control; Qwen traded only 22 times, also recording negative returns. Claude ranked second with a positive return of 2.17%. 对比来看，Gemini 在短期内进行 73 次高频交易，因缺乏风险控制导致亏损 2.73%；Qwen 仅交易 22 次，同样录得负收益。Claude 则以 2.17% 的正收益位列第二。

Currently, "AI-Trader" is fully open-source, adopting the MIT license, allowing users to obtain the code via GitHub and deploy it quickly. 目前，「AI-Trader」已完全开源，采用 MIT 协议，用户可通过 GitHub 获取代码并快速部署。

The project also supports "Time Machine Mode" backtesting, customized trading agents, and multi-market expansion. The research team emphasizes that the complexity and real-time feedback mechanisms of financial markets make them the "ultimate touchstone" for testing AI intelligence levels. 项目还支持「时光机模式」回测、定制化交易代理以及多市场扩展。研究团队强调，金融市场的复杂性和实时反馈机制，使其成为检验 AI 智能水平的「终极试金石」。

价格继续飙升，存储原厂暂停报价

According to Cailian Press, the storage chip market experienced a rare sustained rally in the fourth quarter, with some original manufacturers suspending quotes for DRAM and Flash products. Industry insiders are calling this an "unprecedented" super cycle. 据财联社报道，存储芯片市场在第四季度迎来罕见的持续涨势，部分原厂已暂停对 DRAM 和 Flash 产品的报价，业内人士称这是「见所未见」的超级周期。

Analysis indicates that this round of price increases is not a traditional cyclical fluctuation but a structural imbalance triggered by the explosion of AI demand. 分析指出，此轮涨价并非传统周期性波动，而是由 AI 需求爆发引发的结构性失衡。

Several industry chain sources revealed that original manufacturers are prioritizing capacity allocation to Server DRAM and HBM, leading to a tight supply of older process products like DDR4 and LPDDR4X. CFM Flash Market data shows that the spot price of DDR4 16Gb 3200 has risen to $13.00 this week, a surge of 30% from last week; 512Gb Flash Wafer has accumulated an increase of over 20% since October. 多位产业链人士透露，原厂正将产能优先分配至 Server DRAM 与 HBM，导致 DDR4、LPDDR4X 等旧制程产品供应紧缩。CFM 闪存市场数据显示，DDR4 16Gb 3200 本周现货报价已升至 13.00 美元，较上周暴涨 30%；512Gb Flash Wafer 自 10 月以来累计涨幅超过 20%。

The terminal market has seen a chain reaction. According to previous reports, Xiaomi Group founder Lei Jun recently stated on Weibo that "memory prices have risen too much recently," and some models of the Redmi K90 series have seen their prices increase by 100 to 400 yuan compared to the previous generation. Xiaomi President Lu Weibing also admitted that upstream cost pressure has genuinely passed on to new product pricing. 终端市场已出现连锁反应。据此前报道，小米集团创始人雷军日前在微博表示，「最近内存涨价实在太多」，而 Redmi K90 系列部分机型售价较上一代上调 100 至 400 元。小米总裁卢伟冰也坦言，上游成本压力已真实传导至新品定价。

TrendForce analysts expect DDR4 shortages to continue at least until the first half of 2026. Meanwhile, the capital market has reacted strongly to storage concept stocks, with Puran Technology and Shannon Semiconductor hitting their daily limits, and Bywin Storage and Longsys seeing stock price increases of over 10%. TrendForce 分析师预计，DDR4 紧缺将至少延续至 2026 年上半年。与此同时，资本市场对存储概念股反应强烈，普冉股份、香农芯创涨停，佰维存储、江波龙等股价涨幅超过 10%。

Windows 10 停止支持引发换机潮，Mac 出货量大增

According to MacRumors, the approaching end-of-support deadline for Windows 10 is driving the largest PC upgrade wave globally, with Apple becoming one of the main beneficiaries. 据 MacRumors 报道，Windows 10 停止支持的最后期限正在推动全球范围内最大规模的 PC 更新换代潮，苹果成为主要受益者之一。

Research firm Counterpoint points out that nearly 40% of PCs globally still run Windows 10 before the support cutoff in October 2025. This pressure is prompting businesses and consumers to upgrade their devices ahead of schedule, driving an increase in overall shipments. 研究机构 Counterpoint 指出，在 2025 年 10 月支持截止前，全球近 40% 的 PC 仍运行 Windows 10，这一压力促使企业与消费市场提前更新设备，带动整体出货量上升。

Data shows that in Q3 2025, Apple's global Mac shipments increased by 14.9% year-on-year, primarily driven by demand for new MacBook models and increased adoption in the enterprise market. 数据显示，2025 年第三季度，苹果全球 Mac 出货量同比增长 14.9%，主要受新款 MacBook 需求和企业市场采用率提升的推动。

During the same period, Lenovo maintained its position as the world's largest PC manufacturer, leading with a 17.4% year-on-year increase. Asus grew by 14.1%, HP by 10.3%, while Dell experienced a slight decline of 0.9%. 同期，联想依然保持全球最大 PC 厂商地位，并以 17.4% 的同比增幅领跑。华硕增长 14.1%，惠普增长 10.3%，而戴尔则出现 0.9% 的小幅下滑。

Counterpoint also pointed out that the top five manufacturers collectively account for nearly three-quarters of the global PC market share, while the performance of small and medium-sized brands remained relatively flat or declined. At the same time, manufacturers have begun to promote new products equipped with neural processing units and integrated AI functions. Although these features have not yet become a major sales driver, enterprise customers have incorporated AI capabilities into their purchasing considerations to avoid premature obsolescence of devices before AI workflows become widespread in the future. Counterpoint 还指出，前五大厂商合计占据近四分之三的全球 PC 市场份额，中小品牌表现则相对持平或下滑。同时，厂商已开始推广配备神经处理单元和集成 AI 功能的新产品。尽管这些特性尚未成为大规模销售驱动力，但企业客户已将 AI 能力纳入采购考量，以避免设备在未来 AI 工作流普及前过早淘汰。

M5 MacBook Pro SSD 性能大提升：读速提升 211%

According to Wccftech, Apple's latest M5 MacBook Pro significantly surpasses the previous M4 model in SSD read and write performance. 据 Wccftech 报道，苹果最新发布的 M5 MacBook Pro 在 SSD 读写性能上大幅超越前代 M4 机型。

According to test results from the YouTube channel Max Tech, the M5 model achieved a sequential read speed of 6323MB/s in Blackmagic tools, an increase of approximately 211% compared to the M4's 2031MB/s; the write speed was 6068MB/s, an 84% increase compared to the M4's 3293MB/s. 根据 YouTube 频道 Max Tech 的测试结果，M5 机型在 Blackmagic 工具的顺序读取速度中达到 6323MB/s，比 M4 的 2031MB/s 提升约 211%；写入速度则为 6068MB/s，相较 M4 的 3293MB/s 提升 84%。

Tests show that the two generations of products are almost identical in appearance and internal layout, both adopting a single fan and dual NAND flash memory chip design. However, the M5's SSD controller is suspected to have been upgraded, bringing its performance close to desktop-grade PCIe NVMe Gen 4 solid-state drives. 测试显示，两代产品在外观与内部布局上几乎一致，均采用单风扇与双 NAND 闪存芯片设计。但 M5 的 SSD 控制器疑似升级，使其性能接近桌面级 PCIe NVMe Gen 4 固态硬盘。

It is worth noting that under high load, the M5 chip temperature can reach 99 degrees Celsius, but the overall operating temperature is still lower than that of the M4 model. 值得注意的是，在高负载下 M5 芯片温度可达 99 摄氏度，但整体运行温度仍低于 M4 机型。

测试显示 Xbox Ally 搭载 Linux 更流畅，帧率最高提升 32%

According to WinDiscover, the Xbox Ally handheld console, jointly launched by Microsoft and ASUS, has been optimized for handheld devices when running Windows 11, but the latest tests show that its overall performance is even more outstanding when running the Linux distribution Bazzite. 据 WinDiscover 报道，微软与华硕联合推出的 Xbox Ally 掌机在搭载 Windows 11 时已针对手持设备进行优化，但最新测试显示，若运行 Linux 发行版 Bazzite，整体性能表现更为突出。

Tests conducted by YouTube blogger Cyber Dopamine showed that in "Kingdom Come: Deliverance II," the frame rate in the Linux environment increased by approximately 32% compared to Windows; in "Hogwarts Legacy," the difference varied depending on the power consumption mode, but overall, Linux maintained a lead. 测试由 YouTube 博主 Cyber Dopamine 进行，其结果显示，在《天国：拯救 2》中，Linux 环境下的帧率比 Windows 提升约 32%；在《霍格沃茨之遗》中，差距因功耗模式不同而有所变化，但整体仍保持领先。

On average, Bazzite brought about a 13.47% increase in frame rate. More importantly, the frame rate curve under Linux was smoother, with almost no significant fluctuations, while the Windows environment experienced frequent ups and downs. 平均来看，Bazzite 带来约 13.47% 的帧率提升。更重要的是，Linux 下的帧率曲线更为平稳，几乎无明显波动，而 Windows 环境则存在频繁起伏。

In addition to performance, Linux also demonstrated advantages in power management and wake-up speed. Tests showed that Bazzite can achieve near-instantaneous wake-up, offering an experience close to a console; whereas the Windows system requires about 40 seconds to enter sleep and an additional 15 seconds to wake up . Cyber Dopamine described it this way: an ideal handheld should be like "a book that can be opened at any time," and Linux is closer to this expectation in this regard. 除性能外，Linux 在功耗管理与唤醒速度上也展现优势。测试显示， Bazzite 几乎可实现瞬间唤醒，体验接近主机；而 Windows 系统则需约 40 秒进入睡眠，并在唤醒时额外等待 15 秒 。Cyber Dopamine 这样形容：理想的掌机应像「一本随时可翻开的书」，Linux 在这一点上更接近预期。

Furthermore, the Bazzite team demonstrated quick responsiveness during the tests, with developers able to push hotfixes immediately after user feedback, further optimizing the experience. 此外，Bazzite 团队在测试期间展现出快速响应能力，开发者能在用户反馈问题后即时推送修复补丁，进一步优化体验。

Nevertheless, some games with anti-cheat systems still require running in a Windows environment, so dual-booting is considered a superior solution to balance compatibility and performance. 尽管如此，部分带有反作弊系统的游戏仍要求运行在 Windows 环境下，因此双系统启动被视为兼顾兼容性与性能的更优方案。

保时捷季度亏损 80 亿元，销售利润暴跌 99%

According to CCTV Finance, Porsche AG, a subsidiary of the German Volkswagen Group, announced its latest financial report yesterday, showing a third-quarter loss of 966 million euros (approximately 8 billion RMB), leading to a sharp 99% year-on-year decline in sales profit for the first three quarters of this year. 据央视财经报道，德国大众集团旗下保时捷汽车公司昨日公布最新财报显示，公司第三季度亏损达 9.66 亿欧元（约合人民币 80 亿元），导致今年前三季度销售利润同比大幅下滑 99%。

Porsche stated that the decline in performance was mainly due to multiple factors. Last month, the company announced a delay in the launch of some pure electric models, extended the market life cycle of several gasoline and hybrid models, and terminated its battery self-production plan. These restructuring measures resulted in additional expenditures of approximately 2.7 billion euros (approximately 22.4 billion RMB). 保时捷方面表示，业绩下滑主要受多重因素影响。上月，公司宣布推迟部分纯电动车型的推出，延长多款燃油与混动车型的市场生命周期，并终止电池自产计划。上述重组措施带来约 27 亿欧元（约合人民币 224 亿元）的额外支出。

The John Lennon and Yoko Ono documentary "One to One: John and Yoko" will premiere on HBO Max on November 14. Directed by Kevin Macdonald and Sam Rice-Edwards, the film includes previously unreleased home footage and audio, showcasing their relationship and public reactions. The film was released in cinemas in several locations earlier this year, and the streaming version will further expand its audience reach. 影片今年早些时候已在多地影院上映，流媒体版本将进一步扩大观众覆盖范围。
