库克：苹果产品涨价「不可避免」

According to The Wall Street Journal, Apple CEO Tim Cook said in an interview that price increases for Apple products are "inevitable" due to persistent memory supply shortages and rising costs. 据《华尔街日报》报道，苹果 CEO 蒂姆·库克在接受采访时表示，受内存供应持续紧张和成本上涨影响，苹果产品涨价已「不可避免」。

Cook stated that Apple has always tried to absorb price increase pressures from the supply chain and avoid passing costs on to consumers, but the current situation is "unsustainable." 库克称，苹果一直试图吸收供应链传导而来的涨价压力，并尽量避免将成本转嫁给消费者，但当前情况已经「难以为继」。

Cook did not disclose the specific timing, extent, or products involved in the price increase. The report states that Apple's next major product launch may be in September, when the iPhone 18 series could face a price adjustment; iPad and Mac product lines might also be affected earlier. 库克未透露具体涨价时间、幅度及涉及产品。报道称，苹果下一次重要新品发布可能在 9 月，届时 iPhone 18 系列或面临价格上调；iPad 和 Mac 产品线也可能更早受到影响。

The report states that the growing demand from AI companies for memory and storage chips has driven up the prices of related components and exacerbated supply shortages. TechInsights estimates that if Apple wishes to maintain its current profit margins, the iPhone 18 Pro price might need to increase by approximately $270 (about 1,820 RMB). 报道称，AI 公司对内存和存储芯片的需求增长，推高了相关元件价格，并加剧供应紧张。TechInsights 估算，如果苹果希望维持现有利润率，iPhone 18 Pro 价格可能需要提高约 270 美元（约 1820 元人民币）。

Cook stated that Apple will use cash to increase memory supply but will not build its own memory and storage factories. 库克表示，苹果将动用现金以增加内存供应，但不会自建内存和存储工厂。

曝第二代 iPhone Air 明年春季推出，改进摄像头和续航

According to Bloomberg, Apple is preparing the second-generation iPhone Air, planned for release next spring. The new device, internally codenamed V62, has now entered Apple's advanced internal testing phase. 据彭博社报道，苹果正在准备第二代 iPhone Air，计划于明年春季推出。新机内部代号为 V62，目前已进入苹果内部高级测试阶段。

According to informed sources, the most significant change in the new device is the addition of an ultra-wide-angle lens, upgrading from a single camera to a dual-camera system. It will also improve battery life, while the exterior design will continue the current model's styling. 据知情人士透露，新机最显著的变化是新增一颗超广角镜头，从单摄升级为双摄，同时将改善续航表现，外观设计则延续现款造型。

The specific method for improving battery life is currently unclear: the Air's extremely thin and light body has limited space for a larger capacity battery, so efficiency improvements may be the primary approach. In terms of chips, the new device is expected to feature a derivative version of the A20 Pro, which is also used in Apple's high-end iPhone 18 series this fall. 续航改善的具体实现方式目前尚不明确：Air 极致轻薄的机身对更大容量电池的容纳空间有限，效能提升或将成为主要途径。芯片方面，新机预计搭载 A20 Pro 的衍生版本，该处理器同样用于苹果今年秋季的 iPhone 18 高端系列。

The report also states that this fall, Apple will only release its high-end product line, including the iPhone 18 Pro, Pro Max, and its first foldable iPhone; next spring, the standard iPhone 18 will debut alongside the new Air. 报道还称，今年秋季苹果将只发布高端产品线，包括 iPhone 18 Pro、Pro Max 及首款折叠屏 iPhone；明年春季，标准版 iPhone 18 将与新款 Air 同步亮相。

上交所发布指引：大模型企业上市需至少一个产品规模化应用

According to Cailian Press, the Shanghai Stock Exchange (SSE) officially released yesterday the "Shanghai Stock Exchange Issuance and Listing Review Rules Application Guide No. 10 — Application of the Fifth Set of Listing Standards for AI Large Model Enterprises on the STAR Market," further clarifying the applicable requirements for AI large model enterprises applying for listing on the STAR Market. 据财联社报道，上交所昨日正式发布《上海证券交易所发行上市审核规则适用指引第 10 号——人工智能大模型企业适用科创板第五套上市标准》，进一步明确人工智能大模型企业申请科创板上市的适用要求。

The "Guide" focuses on the key conditions for large model enterprises to issue and list through the fifth set of listing standards on the STAR Market, requiring issuers to have at least one large model product launched and scaled for application at the time of declaration. The SSE stated that this requirement can serve as an important verification of the large model product's viable business model and commercialization capabilities . 《指引》聚焦大模型企业通过科创板第五套上市标准发行上市的关键条件，要求发行人在申报时至少有一个大模型产品已完成上线发布并实现规模化应用。 上交所称，这一要求可作为大模型产品商业模式可行、具备商业化落地能力的重要验证 。

At the same time, the "Guidelines" clearly define the issuer's main business as "independent research and development of large AI models, model services, or model applications, etc.," applicable to both general large models and industry-specific models. 同时，《指引》将发行人主营业务明确为「人工智能大模型的自主研发、模型服务或者模型应用等」，通用大模型和行业专用模型均可适用。

The Shanghai Stock Exchange stated that it will subsequently promote the issuance and listing of AI large model enterprises that meet the standards on the STAR Market, under the guidance of the China Securities Regulatory Commission. 上交所表示，后续将在中国证监会指导下，推进符合标准的人工智能大模型企业在科创板发行上市。

特斯拉「车顶维权」名誉权案再审被驳回

According to Jiemian News, the Shanghai High People's Court recently rejected the retrial application filed by Ms. Zhang, a party involved in the 2021 Shanghai Auto Show "car roof protest" incident, and related personnel Mr. Feng, regarding a reputation dispute. 据界面新闻报道，上海市高级人民法院近日驳回 2021 年上海车展「车顶维权」事件当事人张女士及相关人员封某针对名誉权纠纷提出的再审申请。

The ruling showed that the court believed Ms. Zhang's claim of "Tesla brake failure" lacked objective factual basis, and her extreme public promotion of such content exceeded the boundaries of reasonable consumer supervision and criticism, constituting defamation; the first and second instance judgments found the facts clear and applied the law correctly, and the grounds for retrial were not established. 裁定书显示，法院认为张女士主张「特斯拉刹车失灵」缺乏客观事实依据，其在公开场合采取极端方式宣扬相关内容，已超出消费者合理监督与批评边界，构成诽谤；一审、二审判决认定事实清楚、适用法律正确，再审理由不成立。

The first instance of the case was pronounced in May 2024, with the court finding Ms. Zhang guilty of reputation infringement, ordering her to publicly apologize in designated media and compensate Tesla for economic losses and reasonable rights protection expenses totaling 172,000 yuan; at the same time, it found Mr. Feng played an organizing and planning role in the incident, ordering him to publicly apologize and pay 250,000 yuan. The Shanghai No. 2 Intermediate People's Court upheld the original judgment in July 2025. 该案一审于 2024 年 5 月宣判，法院认定张女士构成名誉侵权，判令其在指定媒体上公开赔礼道歉，并赔偿特斯拉经济损失及维权合理开支共计 17.2 万元；同时认定封某在事件中起组织策划作用，判令其公开道歉并赔偿 25 万元。上海二中院 2025 年 7 月维持一审原判。

💡 罗剑岚：具身智能不能简单照搬大语言模型的发展路径

According to 36Kr, Luo Jianlan, associate professor at Shanghai Innovation Academy and chief scientist at Zhiyuan Robotics, stated in an interview that embodied AI cannot simply copy the development path of large language models. 据 36 氪报道，上海创智学院副教授、智元机器人首席科学家罗剑岚在接受采访时表示，具身智能不能简单照搬大语言模型的发展路径。

Luo Jianlan emphasized the importance of real training data. He pointed out that many so-called "robot foundation models" in the current industry are closer to task adaptation, intermediate training, or fine-tuning on open-source bases, and have not yet entered the pre-training stage driven by large-scale, heterogeneous, real interaction data. 罗剑岚强调真实训练数据的重要性。他指出，当前行业中很多所谓「机器人基础模型」更接近在开源底座上的任务适配、中训练或微调，还没有进入大规模、异构、真实交互数据驱动的预训练阶段。

Luo Jianlan's core judgment is that a decrease in offline training loss in the robotics field does not necessarily correspond to an increase in real-world deployment success rates. The Scaling Law of large language models is based on a relatively stable statistical relationship between pre-training loss and capability; 罗剑岚给出的核心判断是，机器人领域离线训练 Loss 下降，并不必然对应真实部署成功率提升。大语言模型的 Scaling Law 建立在预训练 Loss 与能力之间相对稳定的统计关系上；

Robots facing the open physical world involve contact, disturbances, long-tail scenarios, hardware differences, and task feedback. A model fitting static data does not equate to being able to master reality. 机器人面对开放物理世界，涉及接触、扰动、长尾场景、硬件差异和任务反馈，模型拟合静态数据不等于能驾驭现实。

True robot foundation model pre-training should also, like LLMs, absorb extremely broad data, even including noisy data. Data in the robotics field comes from real-world interactions, failures, error corrections, recoveries, and long-tail scenarios. 真正的机器人基础模型预训练，也应该像 LLM 一样，吸纳极其广泛、甚至包含噪声的数据。机器人领域的数据来自真实世界中的交互、失败、纠错、恢复和长尾场景。

市场监管总局起草外卖补贴新规，整治「拼补贴、拼价格」非理性竞争

According to a report by The Paper yesterday, the State Administration for Market Regulation has drafted the "Ten Guidelines for Regulating Subsidy Practices of Food Delivery Platforms (Draft for Comment)," which is open for public comment from yesterday until July 17. The aim is to regulate the subsidy practices of food delivery platforms, guide them to operate legally and compliantly, and foster healthy competition. 据澎湃新闻昨日报道，市场监管总局起草《外卖平台补贴行为规范十条（征求意见稿）》，自昨日起至 7 月 17 日向社会公开征求意见，旨在规范外卖平台补贴行为，引导平台依法合规经营、开展良性竞争。

The "Draft for Comment" was drafted based on laws and regulations such as the "Anti-Monopoly Law," "Anti-Unfair Competition Law," "Price Law," "E-commerce Law," and "Food Safety Law." It addresses issues of "competing on subsidies, competing on prices, and controlling traffic" by food delivery platforms, and mainly includes four aspects: 《征求意见稿》依据《反垄断法》《反不正当竞争法》《价格法》《电子商务法》《食品安全法》等法律法规起草，针对外卖平台「拼补贴、拼价格、控流量」问题，主要包括四方面内容：

  • Overall principles: Platforms must not exclude or restrict market competition, or disrupt market competitive order through long-term, large-scale subsidies; 总体原则：不得以长期、大额补贴的手段和方式，排除、限制市场竞争，扰乱市场竞争秩序；
  • Specific requirements: Platforms must not force operators within the platform to participate in subsidy activities or bear subsidy costs, must not use capital advantages to engage in monopolistic or unfair competitive practices, and must not sell goods below cost; 具体要求：不得强制平台内经营者参加补贴活动或承担补贴成本，不得利用资本优势实施垄断、不正当竞争行为，不得以低于成本的价格销售商品；
  • Supervision measures: Major information about subsidy activities must be disclosed to the public before and after their implementation, subject to public supervision; 监督措施：补贴活动开展前和实施后向社会公开主要信息，接受社会监督；
  • Legal risks: Reminders of relevant legal provisions that subsidy practices should comply with, and the legal consequences of conducting illegal or non-compliant subsidy activities. 法律风险：提示补贴行为应遵守的相关法律规定，以及违法违规开展补贴活动的法律后果。

The State Administration for Market Regulation stated that it will further revise and improve the "Draft for Comment" based on feedback from various parties and expedite its promulgation. 市场监管总局表示，下一步将根据各方面意见，对《征求意见稿》进一步修改完善并抓紧制定出台。
