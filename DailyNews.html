匿名模型「快乐马」HappyHorse-1.0 屠榜 AI 视频榜单

Yesterday, an anonymous AI video generation model named HappyHorse-1.0 suddenly appeared on the Video Arena ranking list of the authoritative AI evaluation platform Artificial Analysis, and topped multiple tracks with an overwhelming posture. 昨天，一个名为 HappyHorse-1.0 的匿名 AI 视频生成模型突然出现在权威 AI 评测平台 Artificial Analysis 的 Video Arena 榜单上，并以压倒性姿态登顶多个赛道。

  • In the text-to-video (no audio) track, HappyHorse-1.0 ranked first with an Elo score of 1332, leading the second-place Dreamina Seedance 2.0 by nearly 60 points; 在文本转视频（无音频）赛道，HappyHorse-1.0 以 1332 的 Elo 积分位居第一，领先第二名 Dreamina Seedance 2.0 近 60 分；
  • In the image-to-video (no audio) track, it even scored a high of 1391, breaking the historical record for that ranking list; 在图像转视频（无音频）赛道，其更跑出 1391 的高分，刷新了该榜单历史纪录；
  • Even in the audio track, which has extremely high requirements for audio-visual collaboration, the model still ranks second globally, only behind Seedance 2.0 . 即便是对视听协同要求极高的有音频赛道，该模型也稳居全球第二， 仅次于 Seedance 2.0 。

HappyHorse-1.0 was launched in an extremely low-key manner—no product launch event, no technical blog, and no corporate endorsement. Its official website, happyhorse.app, offers two modes: text-to-video and image-to-video, supporting native 1080p output, and emphasizing multi-shot narrative consistency and smooth motion synthesis. It is free to register and use. HappyHorse-1.0 的发布方式极为低调——没有产品发布会，没有技术博客，也没有任何企业背书。其官网 happyhorse.app 提供文本转视频与图像转视频两种模式，支持原生 1080p 输出，并强调多镜头叙事一致性与流畅动作合成，免费注册即可使用。

Netizen Vigo Zhao (@VigoCreativeAI) posted on X, stating that after comparing HappyHorse-1.0's public benchmark data with known models item by item, it was found to be highly consistent with daVinci-MagiHuman, which was open-sourced on GitHub in March this year: 网友 Vigo Zhao (@VigoCreativeAI) 在 X 上发文称，将 HappyHorse-1.0 的公开基准数据与已知模型逐项比对后，发现其与今年 3 月在 GitHub 开源的 daVinci-MagiHuman 高度吻合：

Multiple data points such as visual quality, text alignment, physical consistency, and speech character error rate are almost perfectly matched, and the presentation style of the official website's architecture description, performance tables, and demo videos are also identical. 视觉质量、文本对齐、物理一致性、语音字符错误率等多项数据几乎完全匹配，官网架构描述、性能表格及演示视频的呈现风格也如出一辙。

daVinci-MagiHuman was officially open-sourced on March 23 this year, jointly developed by the Generative AI Research Lab (GAIR) of Shanghai Innovation Institute (SII) and Beijing Sand.ai (Shali Technology). daVinci-MagiHuman 于今年 3 月 23 日正式开源，由上海创新研究院（SII）生成式人工智能研究实验室（GAIR）与北京 Sand.ai（沙砾科技）联合开发。

阿里巴巴 AI 大重组：设技术委员会、建 ATH 事业群，电商向 Token 经济转型

According to Sina Technology, Alibaba Group CEO Eddie Wu yesterday issued an internal letter announcing AI-related organizational adjustments, including the establishment of a new Group Technology Committee, the upgrade of the Tongyi Large Model Business Unit, and the acceleration of AI development. 据新浪科技报道，阿里巴巴集团 CEO 吴泳铭昨天发布内部信，宣布 AI 相关组织调整，包括新设立集团技术委员会，升级通义大模型事业部，加速 AI 建设。

According to the internal letter, Alibaba has established a Technology Committee at the group level, with Eddie Wu as the head, and members including Jingren Zhou, Zeming Wu, and Feifei Li. Among them, Jingren Zhou serves as the Chief AI Architect of the Technology Committee, Feifei Li is responsible for Alibaba Cloud technology and AI cloud infrastructure construction, and Zeming Wu is responsible for the group's business technology platform and AI inference platform construction. 根据内部信，阿里巴巴在集团层面设立技术委员会，由吴泳铭担任组长，成员包括周靖人、吴泽明、李飞飞。其中，周靖人担任技术委员会首席 AI 架构师，李飞飞负责阿里云技术以及 AI 云基础设施建设，吴泽明负责集团业务技术平台以及 AI 推理平台建设。

To accelerate AI development, Alibaba will also upgrade the Tongyi Lab to the Tongyi Large Model Business Unit, led by Jingren Zhou; Feifei Li will serve as Alibaba Cloud CTO; Zeming Wu will focus on his role as Alibaba Group CTO, and the CEO position of Taobao Flash Sale will be taken over by Yanqun Lei. 围绕加速 AI 建设的整体目标，阿里还将通义实验室升级为通义大模型事业部，由周靖人负责；李飞飞出任阿里云 CTO；吴泽明专注阿里巴巴集团 CTO 工作，淘宝闪购 CEO 职务由雷雁群接任。

According to 36Kr, Eddie Wu also proposed forming a new Alibaba Token Hub (ATH) business group, requiring all related businesses to commercialize around Tokens, serving as the overarching architecture for the group's AI strategy. 另据 36 氪报道，吴泳铭还提出组建新的 Alibaba Token Hub（ATH）事业群，要求所有关联业务围绕 Token 进行商业化，以此作为集团 AI 战略的统领架构。

Taotian Group's "Smart Search and Recommendation Products" business unit, which carries AI business, was subsequently split into two departments: "Platform Users and Products" and "Smart Algorithms." The original head, Kaifu Zhang, is no longer responsible for this business; the "Future Innovation Business Unit," originally responsible for multimodal directions, has been merged into the ATH business group. 淘天集团承载 AI 业务的「智能搜推产品」事业部随之拆分为「平台用户及产品」和「智能算法」两个部门，原负责人张凯夫不再负责该业务；原负责多模态方向的「未来创新事业部」则并入 ATH 事业群。

On the e-commerce side, Taotian Group will shift its AI strategic focus from AI to C to AI to B this year, with core OKRs adjusted to merchant-side AI tool retention rate and AI-driven GMV growth. It is reported that Taotian plans to upgrade the existing Qianniu platform to "Qianniu Claw" and officially promote it around 618. 电商侧，淘天集团今年将 AI 战略重心从 AI to C 转向 AI to B，核心 OKR 调整为商家侧 AI 工具留存率以及 AI 带动的 GMV 增长。据悉，淘天计划将原有千牛平台升级为「千牛 Claw」，并于 618 前后正式推广。

The core goal of this product is to reduce merchant operating costs, improve operating efficiency, and deploy merchant business systems and data on Alibaba Cloud. Merchants using Qianniu Claw will directly consume Tokens, with costs borne by the merchant alone or shared with service providers. 该产品的核心目标是降低商家经营成本、提升经营效率，同时将商家业务系统与数据部署于阿里云之上。商家使用千牛 Claw 将直接消耗 Token，费用由商家单独或与服务商共担。

Perplexity Agent 业务爆发，单月 ARR 暴增 50% 突破 4.5 亿美元

According to the Financial Times, Perplexity AI, thanks to the strong performance of its AI agent business, rapidly increased its projected Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) from $305 million to over $450 million in March this year, a single-month increase of 50%. 据《金融时报》报道，Perplexity AI 凭借 AI 智能体业务的强劲表现，在今年 3 月将预计年度经常性收入（ARR）从此前的 3.05 亿美元迅速提升至逾 4.5 亿美元，单月涨幅达 50%。

Behind this growth is Perplexity Computer, a service launched by Perplexity at the end of February this year. This service integrates model capabilities from multiple external vendors, primarily targeting office assistant scenarios for enterprise and individual users. 这一增长背后，是 Perplexity 今年 2 月末推出的 Perplexity Computer 服务。该服务整合多家外部供应商的模型能力，主打面向企业和个人用户的办公助手场景。

Meanwhile, Perplexity has introduced a usage-based pricing model in addition to its monthly subscription, with a company spokesperson stating that its revenue retention rate is "very high." 与此同时，Perplexity 在月付订阅制之外引入了基于使用量的定价模式，一位公司发言人表示，其收入留存率「很高」。

B 站宣布推出播放页暂停广告

Bilibili (B-site) announced that it will officially launch the "pause ad" feature on its app's playback page on April 10. 哔哩哔哩（B 站）宣布，将于 4 月 10 日在 App 端正式上线播放页暂停广告功能。

This ad format will be probabilistically triggered when users manually pause a video, displayed below the player and labeled "Ad," without covering the video screen. Users can click "X" to close the ad at any time before or after it appears, without affecting the normal playback process. 该广告形式将在用户手动暂停视频时概率触发，展示于播放器下方并标注「广告」字样，不覆盖视频画面。用户可在广告出现前或出现后随时点击「X」关闭，不影响正常播放流程。

In terms of revenue, the earnings from pause ads will be calculated based on conversion effects and displayed merged with UP creators' existing basic incentives. For UP creators who do not wish to participate in this ad format, Bilibili provides an opt-out option, accessible via [Creator Incentive – Ad Settings – Pause Ad Settings]. 收益方面，暂停广告的收益将基于转化效果计算，并与 UP 主现有基础激励合并展示。对于不希望参与该广告形式的 UP 主，B 站提供了自主关闭选项，操作路径为【创作激励 – 广告设置 – 暂停广告设置】。

Meta 发布首款超级智能实验室模型 Muse Spark

Today, Meta officially released Muse Spark, the first flagship AI model from its Meta Superintelligence Labs . 今天， Meta 正式发布旗下超级智能实验室（Meta Superintelligence Labs）推出的首款旗舰 AI 模型 Muse Spark 。

  • Natively supports joint understanding of images, audio, video, and text, with built-in tool invocation, visual chain of thought, and multi-agent coordination capabilities; 原生支持图像、音频、视频与文本的联合理解，内置工具调用、可视化思维链与多智能体协调能力；
  • Scored 42.8 in HealthBench Hard open-ended health Q&A, significantly surpassing GPT-5.4's 40.1, Gemini 3.1 Pro's 20.6, and Opus 4.6's 14.8; scored 86.4 in CharXiv Reasoning chart comprehension, also leading competitors; 在 HealthBench Hard 开放式健康问答中得分 42.8，远超 GPT-5.4 的 40.1、Gemini 3.1 Pro 的 20.6 以及 Opus 4.6 的 14.8；CharXiv Reasoning 图表理解得分 86.4，同样领先竞争对手；
  • At the same performance level, Muse Spark saves 10.3 times more computing power than Llama 4 Maverick Base and 8.2 times more than DeepSeek-V3.1 Base. 在相同性能水平下，Muse Spark 比 Llama 4 Maverick Base 节省 10.3 倍算力，比 DeepSeek-V3.1 Base 节省 8.2 倍。

However, the shortcomings are equally obvious. It only scored 42.5 points on ARC AGI 2 abstract reasoning puzzles, far behind Gemini 3.1 Pro's 76.5 and GPT-5.4's 76.1; its score on Terminal-Bench 2.0 terminal coding tasks was 59.0, also trailing GPT-5.4's 75.1 and Gemini 3.1 Pro's 68.5. 但短板同样明显。ARC AGI 2 抽象推理谜题上仅得 42.5 分，远落后于 Gemini 3.1 Pro 的 76.5 和 GPT-5.4 的 76.1；Terminal-Bench 2.0 终端编码任务得分 59.0，也落后于 GPT-5.4 的 75.1 和 Gemini 3.1 Pro 的 68.5。

An incident also occurred during the release. Meta highlighted its own model's scores in the evaluation charts, attempting to create a visual impression of comprehensive leadership, which immediately drew criticism from the outside world, with some netizens directly labeling it "chart crime." Chief AI Officer Alexandr Wang subsequently issued a public apology. 发布过程中还出现了一段插曲。Meta 在评测图表中对自家模型的成绩进行高亮处理，试图制造出全面领先的视觉观感，随即引发外界批评，有网友直接将此定性为「图表犯罪」。首席 AI 官 Alexandr Wang 随后公开致歉。

Meta stated that the next-generation model (internal codename "Watermelon") is already under development, with coding capability being a key area for improvement. Meta 表示，下一代模型（内部代号「Watermelon」）已在研发中，编码能力将是重点改进方向。

💡 牛津、MIT 最新研究：用 AI 做题 10 分钟，独立解题能力就开始下滑

Recently, a joint team from Carnegie Mellon University, Oxford University, MIT, and UCLA published a new research finding, providing causal evidence through the largest randomized controlled experiment to date: using AI to assist in problem-solving not only leads to a decline in independent problem-solving ability but also makes people give up thinking faster . 近日，来自卡内基梅隆大学、牛津大学、MIT 及加州大学洛杉矶分校的联合团队发布了一篇最新研究成果，通过迄今最大规模的随机对照实验提供因果证据： 使用 AI 辅助解题，不仅让人独立解题能力下滑，还会让人更快放弃思考 。

The research team recruited 1222 participants, divided into two groups: "AI-assisted" and "fully independent," to complete fraction calculation and reading comprehension tasks. In the final independent testing phase after AI assistance was removed, the AI-assisted group's answer accuracy was significantly lower than the control group (Experiment 1: 0.57 vs 0.73). 研究团队招募 1222 名参与者，分为「有 AI 辅助」与「全程独立」两组，分别完成分数计算和阅读理解任务。在最终撤走 AI 的独立测试环节，AI 辅助组的答题正确率显著低于对照组（实验一：0.57 vs 0.73）。

More critically, the proportion of participants who directly gave up answering was also significantly higher. This means that AI's impact is not just "getting it wrong," but "not wanting to do it anymore"—motivation itself was eroded. The study further broke down how AI was used: 更关键的是，他们直接放弃作答的比例也明显更高。这意味着 AI 的影响不只是「做不对」，而是「不想做了」——动力本身被侵蚀了。研究进一步拆解了 AI 的使用方式：

61% of participants chose to directly ask AI for answers, and this group's test scores were at the bottom among all groups, showing a significant decline compared to their own pre-experiment levels; while those participants who only asked AI for hints and derived answers themselves were almost unaffected negatively. 61% 的参与者选择直接向 AI 索要答案，这部分人的测试成绩在所有组别中垫底，且相比自身实验前的水平出现了明显退步；而那些只向 AI 要提示、自己推导答案的参与者，则几乎未受负面影响。

In other words, "letting AI think for you" and "letting AI help you think" yield completely different results. The above effects can be produced with only about 10 to 15 minutes of AI interaction. The researchers provided two explanations: 换句话说，「让 AI 替你想」和「让 AI 帮你想」，结果截然不同。 上述效应仅需约 10 至 15 分钟的 AI 交互即可产生。研究者给出了两个解释：

  • First, "reference point shift": after getting used to AI providing instant answers, independent thinking feels "abnormally strenuous," triggering an impulse to give up, and this mechanism self-reinforces; 一是「参照点偏移」：习惯了 AI 秒出答案后，独立思考会感觉「反常地费力」，触发放弃冲动，且这一机制会自我强化；
  • Second, "self-perception deprivation": people can only build an accurate judgment of their own abilities by independently overcoming difficulties. AI skips this entire process, leading to people neither accumulating experience nor daring to believe they can do it. 二是「自我认知剥夺」：人只有在独立克服困难的过程中，才能建立对自身能力的准确判断，AI 把这个过程全部跳过，导致人既不积累经验，也不敢相信自己能做到。

The paper clearly states that this is not a call to "stop using AI," but it does require people's vigilance: current AI systems are optimized more for "making users satisfied in the moment" rather than "helping users grow long-term." 论文明确指出，这不是「别用 AI」的呼吁，但需要引起人们的警觉：当前 AI 系统更多是针对「让用户当下满意」优化，而非「帮用户长期成长」。

The researchers believe that superficial interventions (such as limiting usage time) are only treating the symptoms. The real solution is to redesign AI's collaboration methods—like a good teacher, sometimes intentionally not giving answers. 研究者认为，表层干预（如限制使用时间）只是治标，真正的解法是重新设计 AI 的协作方式——像好老师一样，有时候故意不给答案。
