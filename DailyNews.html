全年营收突破 4160 亿美元，苹果财报创历史新高

Apple Inc. yesterday announced its Q4 fiscal year 2025 earnings report, with results exceeding market expectations. 苹果公司昨日公布 2025 财年第四财季财报，业绩超出市场预期。

The company expects revenue to grow by 10% to 12% year-over-year this quarter, setting a new historical high. CEO Tim Cook stated that demand for the newly released iPhone 17 series is "beyond expectations," which is a major source of confidence for the company. 公司预计本季度营收将同比增长 10% 至 12%，创下历史最佳水平。CEO Tim Cook 表示，新发布的 iPhone 17 系列需求「超乎预期」，是公司信心的主要来源。

The earnings report shows that for the quarter ending September 27, Apple's revenue was $102.47 billion, higher than the market expectation of $102.24 billion; earnings per share were $1.85, also exceeding analysts' estimates of $1.77. 财报显示，截至 9 月 27 日的季度，苹果营收为 1024.7 亿美元，高于市场预期的 1022.4 亿美元；每股收益为 1.85 美元，也超过分析师预估的 1.77 美元。

By business segment, iPhone revenue was $49.03 billion, slightly below the expected $50.19 billion; Mac revenue was $8.73 billion, exceeding expectations; iPad revenue was $6.95 billion, largely flat; and Services revenue was $28.75 billion, showing significant growth. 分业务来看，iPhone 收入为 490.3 亿美元，略低于预期的 501.9 亿美元；Mac 收入为 87.3 亿美元，超出预期；iPad 收入为 69.5 亿美元，基本持平；服务业务收入为 287.5 亿美元，增长显著。

Cook noted that since the iPhone 17 series went on sale on September 19, some models have been in short supply, and store traffic has significantly increased year-over-year. He emphasized that iPhone revenue in the December quarter will achieve double-digit growth, driving overall record revenue. 库克指出，iPhone 17 系列自 9 月 19 日开售以来，部分机型出现供不应求，门店客流量同比大幅上升。他强调，12 月季度 iPhone 收入将实现两位数增长，推动整体营收创纪录。

Additionally, Apple's revenue in Greater China decreased by 4% year-over-year to $14.5 billion, but Cook expects this market to resume growth this quarter with the strong sales of the iPhone 17 series. The Services business continued its rapid 15% growth, becoming the company's highest-margin segment. The Mac business achieved 13% growth due to price reductions and increased sales of the MacBook Air. 此外，苹果在大中华区营收同比下降 4% 至 145 亿美元，但库克预计随着 iPhone 17 系列的热销，该市场将在本季度恢复增长。服务业务继续保持 15% 的高速增长，成为公司利润率最高的板块。Mac 业务则因 MacBook Air 的价格下调和销量提升，实现 13% 增长。

The report also showed that Apple's net profit was $27.46 billion, a significant increase from $14.29 billion in the same period last year, primarily benefiting from the fading of one-time tax factors. The company's full-year revenue reached $416 billion, a 6% year-over-year increase. 财报还显示，苹果净利润为 274.6 亿美元，较去年同期的 142.9 亿美元大幅增长，主要受益于一次性税务因素的消退。 公司全年营收达 4160 亿美元，同比增长 6%。

小米高管回应「巨省电」是空调名

Recently, some netizens broke the news online, claiming that after consulting Xiaomi customer service, they learned that "Jushengdian" (Super Power Saving) is merely a series name for air conditioners, not a performance indicator. This statement quickly sparked heated discussion. 日前，有网友在网上爆料称，咨询小米客服后得知「巨省电」 仅是空调系列名称，并非性能指标，这一说法迅速引发热议。

According to Jiemian News, on the Black Cat Complaint platform, some consumers reported that the "Jushengdian" air conditioner's cooling effect was mediocre and did not meet promotional expectations; however, some users on social media stated that it was "indeed power-saving, quiet, and easy to use." This difference in experience further amplified the controversy. 据界面新闻报道，在黑猫投诉平台，部分消费者反映「巨省电」 空调制冷效果一般，与宣传预期不符；但也有用户在社交平台表示「确实省电、静音好用」。这种体验差异进一步放大了争议。

According to Dushao Finance, Shan Lianshan, General Manager of Xiaomi Group's Home Appliance Department, responded to the aforementioned incident in an interview. 而据读秒财经报道， 小米集团大家电部总经理单联瑜在采访中回应了上述事件。

For "Jushengdian" (Super Power Saving), the national standard EPF is 5.0. We (Xiaomi) generally achieve 5.27, and ultra-first-class energy efficiency is 5.6, which far exceeds national standards. We are inherently in a very power-saving state. 在巨省电里面，国标 EPF 是 5.0，我们（小米）一般会做到 5.27，超一级能效是 5.6，这个是远超国家标准的，我们本身是一个非常省电的状态。

Shan Lianshan also stated that Xiaomi air conditioners incorporate AI for energy saving: "Through some AI capabilities, our (Xiaomi air conditioners) operate with very high efficiency when turned on, reducing energy consumption by about 30% compared to traditional PID control algorithms. Therefore, we are definitely living up to the name 'Jushengdian' (Super Power Saving)." 同时单联瑜还表示，小米空调做了 AI 的节能，「通过 AI 的一些能力，让我们（小米空调）开机的时候运行的效率非常高，同比传统的 PID 控制算法大概会降低 30% 的能耗，所以我们肯定是名副其实的巨省电。」

Shan Lianshan emphasized, "We hope our name represents both our product's identity and its capability. Therefore, such attacks from competitors don't hold much meaning, as everyone in the industry has various 'power-saving' features like 'True Power Saving,' 'Clean Power Saving,' 'Healthy Saving,' 'Power Saving Hero,' and so on." Shan Lianshan also stated, "We are not particularly afraid of the public opinion's impact on us." 单联瑜强调，「希望我们的名称一个是代表了我们产品的名称，第二个是代表了它的能力建设，所以友商这种攻击其实也没有太多的意义，因为行业里面真省电、净省电、健康省、省电侠……各种省电大家其实都有。」同时单联瑜称，「我们也不是特别惧怕这些舆论上对我们的一些影响。」

According to Time Finance, Shan Lianshan also stated in the interview, "The home appliance industry has been very lively recently, with many topics targeting Xiaomi. Some competitors are even distorting facts. This self-defeating behavior shows that competitors are truly anxious, but the more anxious they are, the steadier Xiaomi becomes." 另据时代财经，单联瑜还在采访中表示，「近期家电圈很热闹，很多话题都是冲着小米来的，某些友商更是颠倒是非，这种自乱阵脚的行为说明友商是真急了，不过友商越急，小米越稳。」

This adjustment involves three factories, including the Factory Zero plant in Detroit, which produces models such as the Chevrolet Silverado EV, GMC Sierra EV, and Hummer EV. 此次调整涉及三座工厂，其中包括位于底特律的 Factory Zero 工厂，该厂生产雪佛兰 Silverado EV、GMC Sierra EV 及悍马 EV 等车型。

General Motors emphasized that this move is to re-evaluate electric vehicle production capacity needs and adjust the production pace according to market and policy environments. 通用汽车强调，此举是为了重新评估电动车产能需求，并根据市场和政策环境调整生产节奏。

三星第三季度利润飙升 160%，AI 芯片需求成关键驱动力

According to CNBC, Samsung Electronics yesterday released its third-quarter earnings report, showing a significant year-on-year rebound in operating profit, reaching 12.2 trillion Korean won, a 160% increase from the previous quarter, exceeding market expectations. This performance recovery primarily benefited from the surge in demand for memory chips driven by AI servers. 据 CNBC 报道，三星电子昨日公布第三季度财报，显示公司营业利润同比大幅反弹，达到 12.2 万亿韩元，较上一季度增长 160%，超出市场预期。此次业绩回升主要受益于人工智能服务器带动的存储芯片需求激增。

Data shows that Samsung's third-quarter revenue was 86.1 trillion Korean won, a year-on-year increase of 8.85%; operating profit grew by 32.9% compared to the same period last year. Among these, the semiconductor business performed particularly well, with sales reaching 33.1 trillion Korean won, setting a new quarterly high, and operating profit increasing by 81% year-on-year to 7.0 trillion Korean won. 数据显示，三星第三季度营收为 86.1 万亿韩元，同比增长 8.85%；营业利润较去年同期增长 32.9%。其中，半导体业务表现尤为突出，销售额达到 33.1 万亿韩元，创下季度新高，营业利润同比增长 81%，至 7.0 万亿韩元。

Samsung stated that demand for memory chips remains strong, especially with significant growth in sales of High Bandwidth Memory (HBM) products. The company has passed Nvidia's advanced HBM chip certification tests and regained the top position in global memory market share in the third quarter. 三星方面表示，存储芯片需求持续强劲，尤其是高带宽存储（HBM）产品销售显著增长。公司已通过英伟达的先进 HBM 芯片认证测试，并在第三季度重新夺回全球存储市场份额第一的位置。

In addition to the chip business, Samsung's smartphone division also achieved growth. The Mobile Experience and Network business recorded an operating profit of 3.6 trillion Korean won in the third quarter, a year-on-year increase of approximately 28%, primarily driven by sales of the flagship foldable phone model, Galaxy Z Fold7. 除芯片业务外，三星智能手机部门同样实现增长。移动体验与网络业务第三季度营业利润为 3.6 万亿韩元，同比增长约 28%，主要受旗舰折叠屏机型 Galaxy Z Fold7 销售推动。

Samsung executives noted in the earnings call: "We expect data center companies to continue expanding hardware investment to cope with AI infrastructure competition. Consequently, demand for AI-related servers is still growing rapidly and significantly exceeding industry supply." 三星高管在财报电话会议上指出：「我们预计数据中心企业将持续扩大硬件投资，以应对人工智能基础设施竞争。因此，AI 相关服务器需求仍在快速增长，且显著超出行业供给。」

微信官宣三大功能更新

Yesterday, WeChat officially announced three major feature updates, targeting high-frequency pain points in work scenarios, aiming to improve user communication efficiency and reduce information interference. 昨天，微信官方宣布三大功能更新，针对工作场景中的高频痛点，旨在提升用户沟通效率，减少信息干扰。

  • Batch message recall: Supports recalling all content sent this time in one go, including multi-selected forwarded messages, batch-sent pictures, videos, and files, significantly reducing the cost of remediation after accidental sending; 批量撤回消息：支持一次性撤回本次发送的全部内容，包括多选转发的消息、批量发送的图片、视频和文件，大幅降低误发后的补救成本；
  • Group chat do not disturb can set 'exception reminders': After enabling group chat do not disturb, users can now customize the reminder scope for important messages, such as '@me', '@everyone', group announcements, or manually set message reminders for up to 4 important members; 群聊免打扰可设「例外提醒」：在开启群聊免打扰后，用户现可自定义重要消息的提醒范围，如「@我」「@所有人」、群公告，或手动设置最多 4 位重要成员的消息提醒；
  • Delete friends but retain chat history: The new version adds a 'clear chat history' option when deleting friends. Users can uncheck this option to retain conversation content locally, facilitating work traceability or important information retrieval. 删除好友可保留聊天记录：新版本新增删好友「清空聊天记录」选项，用户取消勾选后可在本地保留对话内容，便于工作留痕或重要信息回溯。

WeChat stated that the above features will be gradually rolled out to users, and it is recommended to wait patiently for updates. 微信表示，上述功能将陆续向用户推送，建议耐心等待更新。

In contrast, Alphabet's stock price rose by 6%, while Microsoft's fell by over 3%. 尽管 Meta 广告业务在 AI 投入的推动下保持增长，第三季度营收同比大增 26% 至 512.4 亿美元，超出市场预期，但公司股价在盘后交易中仍下跌约 8%。相比之下，Alphabet 股价上涨 6%，微软则下跌逾 3%。

Zuckerberg emphasized: "We are already seeing returns in our core business, which gives us more confidence to continue increasing investment and ensure we don't under-invest." 扎克伯格强调：「我们已经在核心业务中看到了回报，这让我们更有信心继续加大投资，确保不会出现投入不足的情况。」

Windows 11 更新引发任务管理器「幽灵进程」

According to Windows Latest, Windows 11 optional update KB5067036 (Build 26200.7019 or 26100.7019) has caused serious anomalies on some devices: Task Manager does not truly exit after being closed, but instead continuously generates "ghost processes" in the background. 据 Windows Latest 报道，Windows 11 可选更新 KB5067036（Build 26200.7019 或 26100.7019）在部分设备上出现严重异常：任务管理器在关闭后并未真正退出，而是持续在后台生成「幽灵进程」。
