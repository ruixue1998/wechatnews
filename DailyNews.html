L2 辅助驾驶首个强制性国标发布，驾驶员脱手脱眼将被强制禁用

The Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT) officially released the mandatory national standard "Safety Requirements for Combined Driving Assistance Systems for Intelligent Connected Vehicles" (GB 47955—2026) yesterday. This standard is scheduled to be officially implemented from January 1, 2027. 工信部昨日正式发布《智能网联汽车组合驾驶辅助系统安全要求》（GB 47955—2026）强制性国家标准，该标准拟于 2027 年 1 月 1 日起正式实施。

This is the first mandatory national standard in China for L2-level assisted driving, filling a gap in safety regulations for this field. Industry-leading enterprises such as Huawei, Xiaomi, and BYD participated in the joint drafting of this standard. 这是国内首个针对 L2 级辅助驾驶的强制性国家标准，填补了该领域安全规范的空白。华为、小米、比亚迪等行业龙头企业参与了该标准的联合起草。

The standard clarifies the safety indicator system for L2-level "combined driving assistance systems." Regarding hand-off-wheel detection, if both hands are off the steering wheel for 5 seconds, the system will issue a prompt; if hands are not returned after the prompt, the warning will escalate. 该标准明确了 L2 级「组合驾驶辅助系统」的安全指标体系。在手部脱离检测方面，若双手脱离方向盘 5 秒，系统将发出提示；若提示后未握回，预警将升级。

In gaze deviation detection, if the system detects gaze deviation, it will issue a reminder within 5 seconds; if the gaze does not return after 5 seconds, an "immediate control warning" will be triggered. 在视线脱离检测中，系统若检测到视线偏离，将在 5 秒内发出提醒，若 5 秒后视线仍未回归则触发「立即控制警告」。

For behaviors that fail to respond to warnings in a timely manner, the new national standard introduces a penalty mechanism. If the driver fails to take over within 10 seconds after a warning is triggered, the system will initiate a risk mitigation function to actively intervene and find a safe spot to stop. 对于未能及时响应预警的行为，新国标引入了惩罚机制。若驾驶员在触发警告后 10 秒内仍未接管，系统将启动风险缓解功能主动介入并寻找安全点停车。

Furthermore, if the risk mitigation function is triggered or if escalated warnings are triggered multiple times within 30 minutes, the system will be forcibly disabled for 30 minutes. 此外，若出现触发风险缓解功能或在 30 分钟内多次触发升级预警等情况，系统将被强制禁用 30 分钟。

奇瑞发布犀牛电池安全保障计划，承诺因电池热失控损毁赔付新车

Chery Automobile officially released the "Rhino Battery Safety Assurance Plan" yesterday, introducing three safety commitments for both sold and currently available models equipped with Rhino batteries. This plan aims to respond to mandatory national standards such as the "Safety Requirements for Power Batteries for Electric Vehicles" (GB 38031-2025), which officially came into effect on July 1st this year. 奇瑞汽车昨日正式发布「犀牛电池安全保障计划」，针对搭载犀牛电池的已售及在售车型推出三项安全承诺。该计划旨在响应自今年 7 月 1 日起正式实施的《电动汽车用动力蓄电池安全要求》（GB 38031-2025）等强制性国家标准。

动画电影《去你的岛》定档 8 月 19 日七夕上映

The animated film "Go to Your Island," adapted from Fan Dawang's novel of the same name, officially announced its release date yesterday, set to hit cinemas nationwide on August 19, this year's Qixi Festival. 改编自番大王同名小说的动画电影《去你的岛》昨日正式官宣定档，将于今年 8 月 19 日七夕节在全国上映。

The film tells the story of the protagonist, Wang Jiexiang, who, guided by a mysterious paper crane, travels alone to a fantastical island built from memories, embarking on a healing journey of deep love, missed opportunities, and reconciliation. 影片讲述女主角王结香在一只神秘千纸鹤的牵引下，孤身前往一座由记忆构筑的奇幻小岛，开启了一段关于深爱、错过与和解的治愈旅程。

The film is directed by Zhou Haoran, with Mei Qiongyu and the original author Fan Dawang co-serving as screenwriters. As a fantasy animation, the film presents the landscape of the memory island through a unique visual style, carrying the complex emotional bonds between the characters. 该片由周浩然执导，梅琼宇与原著作者番大王共同担任编剧。作为一部奇幻题材动画，影片通过独特的视觉风格呈现了记忆孤岛的景观，承载了角色之间复杂的情感羁绊。
