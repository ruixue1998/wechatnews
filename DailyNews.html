曝 Intel CPU 全线涨价 10%，笔记本电脑零售价或涨近四成

According to South Korean media ETNews, Intel notified its major customers this month that it plans to increase the prices of its entire PC CPU lineup by 10% starting from the end of this month, covering most of its mainstream product lines, from entry-level to high-end flagship processors. 据韩媒 ETNews 报道，英特尔已于本月通知主要客户，计划从本月底起对旗下 PC 用 CPU 全线涨价 10%，涵盖其绝大多数主流产品线，从入门级到高端旗舰处理器均在涨价范围之内。

The direct background for this price increase is that AI demand is accelerating its evolution towards the inference stage. Under the premise of limited production capacity, Intel chose to prioritize its more profitable data center business, leading to a tightening supply of consumer-grade CPUs. 此次涨价的直接背景是 AI 需求正加速向推理阶段演进。在产能有限的前提下，英特尔选择优先保障利润更高的数据中心业务，导致消费级 CPU 供应趋于紧张。

Currently, Intel holds approximately 70% of the computer CPU market share, far exceeding AMD and Qualcomm, and its pricing strategy has a crucial impact on the entire industry. 目前，英特尔在电脑 CPU 市场占据约 70% 的份额，远超 AMD 和高通，其定价策略对整个行业具有举足轻重的影响。

TrendForce previously predicted that if memory and CPU prices rise simultaneously, these two core components could account for up to 58% of the material cost of laptop computers; a memory price increase alone could push the retail price of ordinary laptops up by over 30%, and with the additional CPU price increase, the total retail increase at the consumer end could approach 40%. TrendForce 集邦咨询曾预测，若内存与 CPU 价格同步上涨，这两项核心部件在笔记本电脑物料成本中的占比最高可达 58%；仅内存涨价一项，就可能推动普通笔记本零售价上涨逾 30%，叠加 CPU 涨价后，终端零售总涨幅或将接近 40%。

曝 OpenAI 将推出「超级 App」：整合 ChatGPT、Codex 与 Atlas 浏览器

According to The Wall Street Journal, OpenAI plans to integrate its ChatGPT application, AI programming platform Codex, and AI browser Atlas into a single desktop "super app" to simplify user experience and concentrate resources. 据《华尔街日报》报道，OpenAI 计划将旗下 ChatGPT 应用、AI 编程平台 Codex 以及 AI 浏览器 Atlas 整合为一款桌面端「超级 App」，以简化用户体验、集中资源投入。

OpenAI's Head of Applications, Fidji Simo, stated in an internal memo sent to employees yesterday: "We realize that our energy is spread across too many applications and technology stacks, and we need to streamline. This fragmentation has been slowing us down and making it harder for us to achieve the quality standards we desire." OpenAI 应用业务 CEO Fidji Simo 在昨天发给员工的内部备忘录中表示：「我们意识到，我们的精力分散在太多应用和技术栈上，需要精简。这种碎片化一直在拖慢我们的节奏，也让我们更难达到想要的质量标准。」

OpenAI President Greg Brockman will temporarily lead this product integration and related organizational restructuring, while Simo will lead the sales team in promoting the new products to the market. OpenAI 总裁 Greg Brockman 将临时主导此次产品整合与相关组织架构调整，Simo 则将带领销售团队推进新产品的市场推广工作。

Claude Code 上线 Channels：手机发消息就能直接操控

Yesterday, Claude Code quietly launched a preview version of its Channels feature. 昨天，Claude Code 低调上线了 Channels 功能的预览版。

Users can push external messages directly to a locally running Claude Code session via instant messaging platforms like Telegram and Discord, enabling remote command execution, progress queries, and result reception, with an overall experience similar to 'Lobster' OpenClaw. 用户通过 Telegram、Discord 等即时通讯平台，将外部消息直接推送到本地正在运行的 Claude Code 会话中，实现远程下达指令、查询进度和接收结果，整体体验与「龙虾」OpenClaw 类似。

Currently, Channels is still in the research preview phase and requires several usage conditions: Claude Code version must be v2.1.80 or higher, login must be via a claude.ai account (API Key method is not supported); it also relies on Bun to run plugin scripts. 目前，Channels 仍处于研究预览阶段，需满足多个使用条件：Claude Code 版本需为 v2.1.80 及以上，必须使用 claude.ai 账号登录，不支持 API Key 方式；同时依赖 Bun 运行插件脚本。

Regarding security mechanisms, Channels employs a pairing and whitelist strategy. Users must first obtain a one-time pairing code via a Bot, then confirm authorization within the Claude Code session. Upon success, only allowed accounts can push messages to the session. 在安全机制上，Channels 采用配对与白名单策略。用户需先通过 Bot 获取一次性配对码，再在 Claude Code 会话中确认授权，成功后仅被允许的账号才能向会话推送消息。

In terms of functionality, Channels supports attachment transfer and status feedback. Telegram supports single files up to 50 MB, while Discord supports up to 10 files, each 25 MB. 功能层面，Channels 支持附件传输与状态反馈。Telegram 单个文件最大支持 50 MB，Discord 支持最多 10 个文件、每个 25 MB。

Perplexity Health 目前已支持接入苹果 Apple Health、覆盖逾 170 万家医疗服务提供商的电子健康记录，以及 Fitbit、Ultrahuman、Withings 等多个健康平台，背后由 b.well 和 Terra API 等合作伙伴提供技术支持。

“The Status Game: How Winners Are Chosen” is a work of organizational sociology written by Toby Stuart, Professor of Management at the Haas School of Business, University of California, Berkeley, after more than 25 years of empirical research. 《地位游戏：人生赢家是如何被选中的》是加州大学伯克利分校哈斯商学院管理学教授托比·斯图尔特（Toby Stuart）历经逾 25 年实证研究后写就的组织社会学著作。

The book revolves around the core concept of "Anointment." Stuart defines it as: when high-status individuals or institutions publicly endorse someone or something, the value and opportunities of the endorsed party leap forward and continuously accumulate over time. 全书围绕「受膏机制」（Anointment）这一核心概念展开。斯图尔特将其定义为：当高地位的个人或机构公开认可某人或某事物时，被认可方的价值与机会随之跃迁，并在时间轴上持续累积。

  • A portrait painting re-identified as an authentic Rembrandt, and a similar work by his most outstanding disciple that is almost indistinguishable, differ by several orders of magnitude in price; 一幅被重新认定为伦勃朗真迹的肖像画，与其最出色弟子几乎无从分辨的同类作品，在价格上却相差数个数量级；

Stuart calls this cognitive shortcut the "Big Shift" — when evaluating works, papers, or companies, people often shift their attention from "the quality of the thing itself" to "who it is associated with." 斯图尔特将这类认知捷径称为「大偏移」（Big Shift）——人们在评估作品、论文或公司时，往往将注意力从「事物本身的质量」转移至「它与谁相关」。
