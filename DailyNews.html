消息称苹果将跟进 LOFIC 相机技术

South Korean source "yeux1122" recently posted on a blog that Apple plans to introduce LOFIC (Low Noise, High Dynamic Range) camera technology in its next-generation flagship models. 韩国消息源「yeux1122」近日在博客发布帖文称，苹果计划在新一代旗舰机型中引入 LOFIC（Low Noise, High Dynamic Range，低噪声、高动态范围）相机技术。

This technology is considered the next-generation high dynamic range imaging solution after multi-frame HDR and DCG/DXG. It can effectively solve "ghosting" and "halo" issues in traditional HDR paths and provide higher dynamic range performance. 该技术被视为继多帧 HDR 与 DCG/DXG 之后的下一代高动态影像解决方案，能够有效解决传统 HDR 路径中的「鬼影」与「光晕」问题，并提供更高的动态范围表现。

Previously, LOFIC technology has been featured in the Honor Magic 6 series and Xiaomi 17 Pro series. It is reported that Huawei's upcoming flagship models will also adopt this technology, with relevant models equipped with Sony's latest sensors LYT-838 and LYT-910. 此前，LOFIC 技术已搭载于荣耀 Magic 6 系列和小米 17 Pro 系列，消息称，华为即将发布的旗舰机型也将采用此项技术，相关机型将搭载索尼最新传感器 LYT-838 与 LYT-910。

Meanwhile, OPPO and vivo are also actively advancing the research and development of this technology, expected to be applied in their flagship products next year. 与此同时，OPPO 与 vivo 也在积极推进该技术的研发，预计将在下一年度的旗舰产品中应用。

Apple, on the other hand, plans to introduce LOFIC technology into its self-developed CMOS sensors, with the timeline set for after 2027. Samsung has not yet announced a clear technology roadmap. 苹果方面则计划在自研 CMOS 传感器中引入 LOFIC 技术，时间节点定在 2027 年之后。而三星目前尚未公布明确的技术路线图。

OpenAI 宣布公司完成重组，内部路线图曝光

Last night, OpenAI announced that the company has completed its restructuring, and the non-profit arm has been renamed the OpenAI Foundation, holding shares in the for-profit entity currently valued at approximately $130 billion, making it one of the most well-resourced philanthropic organizations in history. 昨晚 OpenAI 宣布公司完成重组，并且非营利机构部分现更名为 OpenAI 基金会，持有目前估值约 1300 亿美元的营利性实体股份，使其成为历史上资源最雄厚的慈善机构之一。

OpenAI stated that the foundation will initially focus on two areas (health and disease treatment, and technological solutions for AI resilience), committing to invest $25 billion. OpenAI 方面表示，基金会最初将专注于两个领域（健康与疾病治疗、针对人工智能弹性的技术解决方案），承诺投入 250 亿美元。

Subsequently, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman and OpenAI Chief Scientist Jakub Pachocki held a live broadcast, revealing OpenAI's internal timeline for achieving superintelligence, AGI, and other technologies. 随后，OpenAI CEO Sam Altman 和 OpenAI 首席科学家 Jakub Pachocki 进行直播，公开了 OpenAI 内部对实现超级智能、AGI 等技术的具体时间表。

Regarding AGI, Jakub believes that the arrival of AGI will be a transitional process lasting several years, not a single point in time. OpenAI is currently developing a personalized AGI. AGI 方面，Jakub 认为 AGI 的到来会是一个持续数年的过渡过程，并非一个单一的时间点。而 OpenAI 目前正在开发一个个人化的 AGI。

英伟达发布下一代超级芯片，性能提升超 3 倍

Early this morning, NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang publicly showcased its next-generation Vera Rubin Superchip for the first time at the GTC October 2025 conference. 今天凌晨，英伟达 CEO 黄仁勋在 GTC October 2025 大会上，首次公开展示了其下一代 Vera Rubin 超级芯片（Superchip）。

According to reports, the Vera Rubin motherboard integrates one Vera CPU and two massive Rubin GPUs, equipped with up to 32 LPDDR memory slots, while the GPUs will also utilize HBM4 high-bandwidth memory. 据介绍， Vera Rubin 的主板整合了一颗 Vera CPU 与两颗巨大的 Rubin GPU，并配备了最多 32 个 LPDDR 内存插槽，同时 GPU 上还将采用 HBM4 高带宽显存。

Huang Renxun stated that the Rubin GPU has returned to the lab for testing, and these are the first samples manufactured by TSMC. 黄仁勋表示，Rubin GPU 已经回到实验室进行测试，这是由台积电代工生产的首批样品。

Specifically, each GPU features 8 HBM4 interfaces and two reticle-sized GPU core dies. Additionally, the Vera CPU is equipped with 88 custom Arm architecture cores, supporting up to 176 threads. 具体来看，每颗 GPU 拥有 8 个 HBM4 接口及两颗与光罩大小相同的 GPU 核心芯片（Reticle-sized dies）。此外，Vera CPU 搭载 88 个定制 Arm 架构核心，最高可支持 176 线程。

According to NVIDIA's plan, the Rubin GPU is expected to enter mass production in the third or fourth quarter of 2026, roughly coinciding with or even earlier than the full mass production of the existing Blackwell Ultra "GB300" Superchip platform. 按照英伟达的规划，Rubin GPU 有望在 2026 年第三或第四季度进入量产阶段，时间大致与现有的 Blackwell Ultra「GB300」Superchip 平台全面量产相当或更早。

NVIDIA's Vera Rubin NVL144 platform will utilize a combination of two new chips, where the Rubin GPU consists of two reticle-sized cores, offering 50 PFLOPS (FP4 precision) of computing power and equipped with 288 GB of HBM4 memory. The accompanying Vera CPU provides 88 custom Arm cores and 176 threads, with NVLINK-C2C interconnect bandwidth reaching 1.8 TB/s. 而英伟达的 Vera Rubin NVL144 平台将采用两颗新芯片组合，其中 Rubin GPU 由两颗 Reticle 尺寸的核心组成，具备 50 PFLOPS（FP4 精度） 的算力，并配备 288 GB HBM4 显存。配套的 Vera CPU 提供 88 个定制 Arm 核心、176 线程，NVLINK-C2C 互联带宽可达 1.8 TB/s。

In terms of performance, the Vera Rubin NVL144 platform can achieve 3.6 Exaflops (FP4 inference) and 1.2 Exaflops (FP8 training) of computing power, an increase of approximately 3.3 times compared to the GB300 NVL72; the total system memory bandwidth reaches 13 TB/s, and fast storage capacity is 75 TB, representing a 60% increase over the previous generation, and it features double NVLINK and CX9 communication capabilities, with maximum speeds of 260 TB/s and 28.8 TB/s respectively. 性能方面，Vera Rubin NVL144 平台可实现 3.6 Exaflops（FP4 推理） 与 1.2 Exaflops（FP8 训练） 的算力，相较 GB300 NVL72 提升约 3.3 倍；系统总显存带宽达 13 TB/s，快速存储容量为 75 TB，分别比上一代提升 60%，并具备双倍 NVLINK 与 CX9 通信能力，最高速率分别为 260 TB/s 与 28.8 TB/s。

Additionally, NVIDIA plans to launch the more advanced Rubin Ultra NVL576 platform in the second half of 2027. This platform can achieve 15 Exaflops (FP4 inference) and 5 Exaflops (FP8 training) of computing power, a 14-fold increase compared to the GB300 NVL72. 另外，英伟达还计划在 2027 年下半年 推出更高端的 Rubin Ultra NVL576 平台。该平台可实现 15 Exaflops（FP4 推理） 与 5 Exaflops（FP8 训练） 算力，相较 GB300 NVL72 提升 14 倍。

At the conference, Jensen Huang also offered a profound insight into AI: 会上，黄仁勋还提出了一个对于 AI 的深刻洞察：

In the past, the software industry was essentially "creating tools"; Excel, Word, and browsers are all tools. In the IT field, these tools might be "databases" or similar, with a market size of approximately one trillion US dollars. 过去的软件产业，本质上是在「造工具」，Excel、Word、浏览器皆是工具。 在 IT 领域，这些工具可能就是「数据库」之类的，其市场规模大约在一万亿美元左右。

But AI is not a tool; it is a "worker." In fact, AI is a "worker who uses tools." This is the fundamental difference. 但 AI 不是工具，是「工人」。事实上，AI 是「会用工具的工人」。 这就是根本性差异。

MacBook Air、PlayStation 5 和 Lincoln Mark VIII 等词条的页面几乎与维基百科原文一致， 呈现逐字逐句的复制粘贴 。对此，维基百科基金会发言人表示：「即便是 Grokipedia 也需要维基百科的存在。」

《象山发光事件》北京首映

The thriller and suspense film "Xiangshan Glowing Incident" held its premiere in Beijing on the 27th. 惊悚悬疑电影《象山发光事件》于 27 日在北京举行首映礼。

Director and screenwriter Zhao Yu, producer Chang Lei, lead actor Xiao Shenyang, and several other principal creators attended the event and discussed behind-the-scenes details with the audience. The film, shot in a found-footage style, will be released nationwide on October 31st and is currently available for pre-sale. 导演编剧赵域、监制常雷、领衔主演小沈阳及多位主创出席活动，并与观众交流影片幕后细节。影片采用伪纪录片手法拍摄，将于 10 月 31 日在全国上映，目前已开启预售。

The premiere was themed "Do Not Approach the Light Source," and guests included veteran filmmaker Shen Kemin, agent Xu Ziyue, and iQiyi producer Tan Nanlan. 首映礼以「勿近光源」为主题，现场嘉宾包括资深电影人沈克敏、经纪人徐子玥及爱奇艺制片人覃楠岚。

In this film, Xiao Shenyang challenges the horror genre for the first time, breaking away from his usual comedic image to play a character who delves into forbidden areas to uncover the truth. Director Zhao Yu stated that Xiao Shenyang's performance was subtle and full of contrast, to the point where he was almost unrecognizable on set. 本片中，小沈阳首次挑战惊悚题材，突破以往喜剧形象，在片中饰演深入禁地探寻真相的角色。导演赵域表示，小沈阳的表演细腻且极具反差，甚至在现场让人一度认不出其身份。

The element of "light source equals danger" in the film's setting left a deep impression on the audience, with some viewers explicitly stating they were "afraid of light sources" after watching. The director revealed that the film's ending deliberately maintains suspense, hoping that audiences will still experience a "chilling realization" after leaving the cinema. 影片设定中「光源即危险」的元素让观众印象深刻，部分观众在观影后直言「害怕光源」。导演透露，影片结局刻意保留悬念，希望观众在走出影院后仍能产生「细思极恐」的回味。
