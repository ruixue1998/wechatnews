售价约 2000 美元，曝折叠屏 iPhone 命名为「iPhone Ultra」

According to Bloomberg, Apple's upcoming foldable iPhone is highly likely to be named "iPhone Ultra" and will be priced at approximately $2000. 据彭博社报道，苹果即将推出的折叠屏 iPhone 极有可能命名为「iPhone Ultra」，售价约为 2000 美元。

Bloomberg reporter Mark Gurman stated that this device will feature new technologies such as an ultra-large internal screen and under-display sensors, with both its pricing and specifications surpassing the existing iPhone product line. 彭博社记者 Mark Gurman 表示，这款设备将搭载超大内屏与屏下传感器等新技术，定价与规格均将凌驾于现有 iPhone 产品线之上。

However, Gurman also added that "Ultra" is currently the most likely naming scheme being discussed internally at Apple, but more straightforward names like "Fold" are not ruled out. 不过，Gurman 也补充道，「Ultra」目前是苹果内部讨论中最有可能采用的命名方案，但也不排除使用「Fold」等更直白的名称。

Regarding the next-generation AirPods, Gurman says they will be equipped with a visual camera to provide visual intelligence data for Siri, and the pricing is expected to exceed the current AirPods Pro. 'AirPods Ultra' is one of the naming options being discussed. 关于下一代 AirPods，Gurman 称其将配备视觉摄像头，为 Siri 提供视觉智能数据，定价预计超过现款 AirPods Pro，「AirPods Ultra」是正在讨论中的命名选项之一。

The next-generation high-end MacBook Pro will introduce a touch OLED display. Referring to Apple's past precedent of a roughly 20% price increase after switching iPads to OLED, the new models are expected to be priced higher than the existing M5 Pro and M5 Max models. The 'MacBook Ultra' naming scheme is also under consideration. 下一代高端 MacBook Pro 则将引入触控 OLED 显示屏，参考苹果过去将 iPad 换用 OLED 后起售价上涨约 20% 的先例，新款机型定价预计将高于现有 M5 Pro 和 M5 Max 机型，「MacBook Ultra」的命名方案同样在考量之中。

At the manufacturing process level, Apple's manufacturing design team is exploring the possibility of 3D printing aluminum, aiming to improve the production efficiency of Apple Watch casings and even future iPhone bodies, which is an extension of the previous Apple Watch Ultra 3's adoption of 3D printed titanium technology. 在制造工艺层面，苹果的制造设计团队正在探索 3D 打印铝材的可能性，目标是提升 Apple Watch 外壳乃至未来 iPhone 机身的生产效率，是此前 Apple Watch Ultra 3 采用 3D 打印钛金属工艺的延伸。

Regarding the Mac product line this year, Gurman expects an upgraded Mac Studio to be launched in mid-year, the iMac will receive a new color scheme, and high-end MacBooks with touchscreens and OLED are expected to be released around the end of the year. 今年 Mac 产品线方面，Gurman 预计升级版 Mac Studio 将于年中推出，iMac 将迎来全新配色方案，搭载触摸屏与 OLED 的高端 MacBook 则预计年底前后发布。

Apple recently updated its official website's leadership page, adding personal profiles for three new executives and adjusting the titles of existing executives. 苹果近日更新了官网领导层页面，新增三位高管的个人简介，同时对现有高管的职衔进行了调整。

Newstead previously served as Chief Legal Officer at Meta for six years, making her a seasoned legal executive in the tech industry. Concurrently, Katherine Adams' title has been updated to Senior Vice President and Head of Government Affairs. Newstead 此前在 Meta 担任首席法律官六年，是一位资深科技行业法律高管。与此同时，Katherine Adams 的职衔也随之更新为高级副总裁兼政府事务负责人。

  • Molly Anderson, Vice President of Industrial Design, also appears on the updated page. 工业设计副总裁 Molly Anderson 同样出现在更新后的页面中。

Anderson joined Apple in 2014 and has effectively led the industrial design team since 2024, responsible for the appearance design of all Apple products, accessories, and packaging. She is another design lead after Jony Ive and Evans Hankey, but Apple has not disclosed the specific date of her official promotion to Vice President. Anderson 于 2014 年加入苹果，并自 2024 年起实际领导工业设计团队，负责苹果全线产品、配件及包装的外观设计工作。她是继 Jony Ive、Evans Hankey 之后的又一任设计负责人，但苹果并未披露其正式晋升副总裁的具体时间。

He replaces Alan Dye, who left Apple at the end of last year to lead design work at Meta's Reality Labs division. Lemay 早在 1999 年便已加入苹果，在公司深耕逾 25 年，现负责领导苹果全平台软件界面设计团队。他接替的是 Alan Dye——后者于去年底离开苹果，转赴 Meta 的 Reality Labs 部门主导设计工作。

卖亏了？苹果悄然下架 Mac Studio 512GB 内存选项

According to AppleInsider, Apple has quietly removed the 512GB memory option for the Mac Studio. Previously, this configuration was priced as high as $14,099 (the domestic top-tier configuration with 512GB memory and 16TB SSD was priced at ¥108,749), with memory upgrade alone costing an additional $4,000. 据 AppleInsider 报道，苹果已悄然下架 Mac Studio 的 512GB 内存选项，此前该配置售价高达 14099 美元（国内顶配含 512GB 内存及 16TB SSD 售价为 108749 元），其中仅内存升级一项便需额外支付 4000 美元。

At the same time, Apple has also increased the prices of existing memory upgrade options. For example, the cost to upgrade from 96GB to 256GB of memory for one configuration has increased by $400 compared to before. 与此同时，苹果还上调了现有内存升级选项的价格。以其中一个配置为例，从 96GB 升级至 256GB 内存的费用较此前上涨了 400 美元。

Regarding delivery times, Mac Studio models with the current highest memory configuration are now expected to ship from early to mid-May this year; basic configuration models can still ship within a few days, while basic memory versions with larger SSDs are expected to ship from late March to early April. 在交货周期方面，选购当前最高内存配置的 Mac Studio，预计发货时间已推迟至今年 5 月初至 5 月中旬；而基础配置机型仍可在数日内发货，搭载更大容量 SSD 的基础内存版本则预计于 3 月底至 4 月初发货。

The 512GB Mac Studio uses a single 64GB LPDDR5 memory package, reportedly a custom specification for Apple. Currently, few other manufacturers in the industry can provide products of comparable density, and its technical difficulty and cost are at the forefront of the industry. 512GB 版 Mac Studio 所采用的是单颗 64GB 容量的 LPDDR5 内存封装，据悉为苹果定制规格，目前业内鲜有其他厂商能够提供同等密度的产品，其技术难度与成本均处于行业前沿。

Furthermore, analysts point out that this removal might be related to Apple's upcoming M5 Ultra version of the Mac Studio – Apple might be stocking high-density memory for the new product. If the M5 Ultra version is launched as scheduled, 512GB and even higher capacity memory options might return. 此外，有分析人士指出，此次下架或与苹果即将推出的 M5 Ultra 版 Mac Studio 有关——苹果可能正在为新品备货高密度内存。若 M5 Ultra 版本如期推出，512GB 乃至更高容量的内存选项或将随之回归。

ChatGPT「成人模式」二度跳票，OpenAI：优先级更高的工作更重要

According to TechCrunch, OpenAI has once again postponed the launch of ChatGPT's 'adult mode.' This feature was originally intended to provide age-verified adult users with access to adult content, such as pornography. 据 TechCrunch 报道，OpenAI 再度推迟了 ChatGPT「成人模式」的上线计划。该功能原本旨在为经过年龄验证的成年用户提供色情内容等成人内容的访问权限。

This is the second delay for this feature. 这已是该功能的第二次延期。

Last October, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman first announced this plan, stating that in December, with the improvement of age verification mechanisms, content such as pornography would be made available to verified adult users, positioning it as part of the principle of "treating adult users like adults." 去年 10 月，OpenAI CEO 山姆·奥特曼（Sam Altman）首次宣布这一计划，表示将在 12 月随着年龄验证机制的完善，向经验证的成年用户开放色情等内容，并将其定位为「像对待成年人一样对待成年用户」原则的一部分。

However, last December, Altman reportedly issued a "red alert" memo internally, urging teams to focus on optimizing the core ChatGPT experience, leading to the first delay in its launch until the first quarter of this year. 然而，去年 12 月，奥特曼据报在内部发出「红色警报」备忘录，要求各团队集中精力优化 ChatGPT 核心体验，上线时间因此首次推迟至今年第一季度。

Now, an OpenAI spokesperson told Axios that the company is "delaying the launch of adult mode" because it needs to "focus on work that is currently a higher priority for more users," including improving model intelligence, optimizing personalized experiences, and making ChatGPT "more proactive." 如今，OpenAI 发言人向 Axios 表示，公司正在「推迟成人模式的上线」，原因是需要「专注于目前对更多用户而言优先级更高的工作」，包括提升模型智能水平、优化个性化体验，以及让 ChatGPT 变得「更主动」。

The spokesperson also stated: "We still firmly believe in the principle of treating adult users like adults, but it will take more time to get this experience right." 该发言人同时表示：「我们仍然坚信像对待成年人一样对待成年用户的原则，但要做好这个体验还需要更多时间。」

He suggested that the public embrace this more advanced era with an open mind and made a bold prediction about future work models: 他建议公众以开放心态迎接这一更先进的时代，并对未来的工作模式作出大胆预判：

People will no longer need to work eight hours a day, five days a week; perhaps three days a week, two hours a day will suffice. 大家就不需要每天工作八小时、每周工作五天，可能一周工作三天、每天工作两小时就行了。

Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite 以 0.05 美元、83.2% 的成功率紧随其后。而 Claude Opus 4.6 完成测试的成本高达 5.89 美元，是 GPT-5 Nano 的近 200 倍，但成功率反而低于 MiniMax M2.1 逾 3 个百分点。

PinchBench 的评分机制包括代码运行验证（自动化检查）、质量评估（由 Claude Opus 担任评委）以及两者结合三种方式，所有题目与答案均已开源至 GitHub。完整榜单可在 pinchbench.com 查阅。

「AI 养龙虾」走红背后，工信部点名 OpenClaw 安全隐患

According to CCTV News, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology's Cybersecurity Threat and Vulnerability Information Sharing Platform recently monitored and found that some instances of the open-source AI agent tool OpenClaw pose high security risks under default or improper configurations, easily leading to cybersecurity attacks and information leakage. 据央视新闻报道，工业和信息化部网络安全威胁和漏洞信息共享平台近日监测发现，开源 AI 智能体工具 OpenClaw 的部分实例在默认或不当配置情况下存在较高安全风险，极易引发网络攻击与信息泄露等安全问题。

The Ministry of Industry and Information Technology pointed out that OpenClaw has a "blurred trust boundary" issue during deployment and possesses characteristics such as autonomous continuous operation, independent decision-making, and the ability to call system and external resources. 工信部指出，OpenClaw 在部署时存在「信任边界模糊」的问题，且具备自主持续运行、自主决策、调用系统和外部资源等特性。

In the absence of effective permission control, auditing mechanisms, and security hardening, the tool may execute unauthorized operations due to instruction inducement, configuration flaws, or malicious takeover, leading to a series of security risks such as information leakage and system compromise. 在缺乏有效权限控制、审计机制和安全加固的情况下，该工具可能因指令诱导、配置缺陷或遭恶意接管，进而执行越权操作，造成信息泄露、系统受控等一系列安全风险。

In response to the aforementioned risks, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology advises relevant entities and users to thoroughly check public network exposure, permission configurations, and credential management when deploying and applying OpenClaw.
