6999 元起，三星 S26 系列正式发布

Early this morning, Samsung officially released its latest generation flagship Galaxy S26 series. Let's look at the prices first: 今天凌晨，三星正式发布旗下最新一代旗舰 Galaxy S26 系列，先看售价：

  • Galaxy S26 is priced at 6999 yuan, offering one version with 12GB+256GB; S26+ starts at 7999 yuan, offering 12GB+256GB/512GB; Galaxy S26 售价 6999 元，提供 12GB+256GB 一个版本；S26+ 售价 7999 元起，提供 12GB+256GB/512GB；
  • The Galaxy S26 Ultra starts at 9999 yuan, offering 12GB+256GB/512GB and 16GB+1TB, with a maximum price of 13999 yuan; Galaxy S26 Ultra 售价 9999 元起，提供 12GB+256GB/512GB 和 16GB+1TB，最高售价 13999 元；
  • The Galaxy Buds 4 series, released alongside, is priced at 1399 yuan for the standard version and 1899 yuan for the Pro version. 一起发布的 Galaxy Buds 4 系列：标准版售价 1399 元，Pro 售价 1899 元。

Regarding highlights, the Galaxy S26 Ultra features "privacy screen" technology: the new phone can achieve two levels of hardware privacy protection in system settings, and the privacy screen can also achieve "local masking." 亮点方面，Galaxy S26 Ultra 给出了「防窥屏」技术：新机可以在系统设置里实现两档强度的硬件防窥，并且隐私屏幕可以实现「局部遮蔽」。

Technically, this technology originated from the Flex Magic Pixel exhibited at MWC 2024 – by separately depositing "wide-angle pixels" and "narrow-angle pixels" on the screen substrate, it simultaneously solves the two previously coexisting problems of "privacy protection" and "large brightness attenuation range." 技术上来看，这项技术脱胎于 2024 年 MWC 上展出的 Flex Magic Pixel——通过在屏幕基板上分别蒸镀「广视角像素」和「窄视角像素」，同时解决了「防窥」和「亮度衰减范围大」两个以往共存的问题。

In other aspects, the S26 series is, without a doubt, equipped with the customized Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 for Galaxy; however, there are also rumors that the latest Exynos 2600 processor will make a comeback in the Korean standard version of the S26. 其他方面，S26 系列毫无悬念地搭载了定制版骁龙 8 Elite Gen 5 for Galaxy；但也有消息称，最新的 Exynos 2600 处理器将在韩版 S26 标准版上重出江湖。

Additionally, the Samsung S26 series in China fully supports dual physical SIM card slots and eSIM. It comes in six color options: Phantom Violet, Snow White, Cosmic Black, Light Cloud Blue, and two Samsung direct sales platform exclusive colors, Sunset Gold and Moonlight Silver. 此外，三星 S26 系列国行全系支持双实体卡槽，同时支持 eSIM，共推出六款配色，分别为幽夜紫、映雪白、旷宇黑、浅云蓝，以及两款三星直销平台专属色的绯霞金与镜月银。

英伟达新财报公布：继续增长，营收破 681 亿

Early this morning, Nvidia officially released its financial results for the fourth quarter and full fiscal year 2026: 今天凌晨，英伟达正式发布 2026 财年第四季度及全年财报：

In the fourth fiscal quarter ending January 25, 2026, Nvidia achieved total revenue of US$68.13 billion (approximately RMB 468.02 billion), a 20% quarter-on-quarter increase and a 73% year-on-year increase; GAAP net profit reached US$42.96 billion, a 94% year-on-year surge. 在截至 2026 年 1 月 25 日的第四财季中，英伟达实现总营收 681.3 亿美元（约合人民币 4680.2 亿元），环比增长 20%，同比增长 73%；GAAP 净利润达 429.6 亿美元，同比激增 94%。

For the full fiscal year 2026, total revenue exceeded US$215.9 billion (approximately RMB 1.4831 trillion), a year-on-year increase of 65%. 2026 财年全年总营收突破 2159 亿美元（约合人民币 14831 亿元），同比攀升 65%。

Specifically, data centers remained the largest revenue contributor, reaching a record US$62.3 billion (approximately RMB 427.9 billion); while the consumer segment (gaming + AI PC) reached US$3.7 billion (approximately RMB 25.4 billion), a year-on-year increase of 47%. 具体来看，数据中心依然是营收大头——达到了创纪录的 623 亿美元（约合人民币 4279 亿元）；而消费级领域（游戏+AI PC）则达到了 37 亿美元（约合人民币 254 亿元），同比增长 47%。

He stated that Grace Blackwell with NVLink is currently the "king of inference," and the next-generation Vera Rubin platform will further expand this leading edge. 其表示，搭载 NVLink 的 Grace Blackwell 是目前的「推理之王」，而下一代 Vera Rubin 平台将进一步扩大这一领先优势。

For the first quarter of fiscal year 2027, Nvidia provided revenue guidance of approximately US$78 billion (with a 2% fluctuation). 针对 2027 财年第一季度，英伟达给出约 780 亿美元（上下浮动 2%）的营收指引。

Anthropic 放弃核心 AI 安全承诺

Recently, Anthropic officially released the third version of its "Responsible Scaling Policy" (RSP V3), announcing major reforms to its large model safety framework. 近期，Anthropic 正式发布第三版《责任扩展政策》（RSP V3），宣布对其大模型安全框架进行重大改革。

According to Time magazine, Anthropic has officially abandoned its original core safety commitment of "unilaterally pausing training." 而据《时代》周刊报道，Anthropic 正式放弃了原有的「单方面暂停训练」核心安全承诺。

It is understood that the core of this policy change is that Anthropic has canceled the previous rigid rule of "never training or releasing new AI models if appropriate risk mitigation measures cannot be ensured in advance." 据悉，此次政策变动的核心在于，Anthropic 取消了此前「若无法提前确保适当的风险缓解措施到位，则绝对不训练或发布新 AI 模型」的硬性规定。

As an alternative, Anthropic's new policy pledges that its safety measures will "match or exceed" those of its competitors and introduces a new information disclosure mechanism. Official documents show that the company will regularly publish a "Frontier Safety Roadmap" to detail future safety goals and simultaneously release "Risk Reports" to quantitatively assess the actual risk levels of all deployed models. 作为替代方案，Anthropic 在新版政策中承诺其安全措施将「持平或超越」竞争对手，并引入了全新的信息披露机制。官方文件显示，公司未来将定期发布《前沿安全路线图》以详细规划未来的安全目标，并同步公开《风险报告》，量化评估所有已部署模型的实际风险水平。

Company Chief Scientist Jared Kaplan stated in an interview that with competitors rapidly advancing, unilaterally stopping AI model training "does no one any good." 公司首席科学家 Jared Kaplan 在接受采访时表示，在竞争对手快速推进的情况下，单方面停止训练 AI 模型「对任何人都没好处」。

It is understood that Anthropic launched its RSP policy in 2023, aiming to prevent systemic risks of large models by setting absolute safety thresholds (such as ASL risk levels). 据了解，Anthropic 于 2023 年推出 RSP 政策，旨在通过设立绝对的安全门槛（如 ASL 风险等级）来防范大模型的系统性风险。

However, according to reports, with the escalating global AI arms race and surging model training costs, Anthropic's adherence to unilateral stringent restrictions has led the company to face the risk of falling behind in R&D progress in its commercial competition with giants like OpenAI and Google. 但据报道，随着全球 AI 军备竞赛加剧与模型训练成本激增，Anthropic 坚持单方面的严苛限制已导致公司在与 OpenAI、Google 等巨头的商业博弈中面临研发进度落后的风险。

The entire speaker has a minimalist pure white appearance, with a high-quality Bluetooth speaker built into the lid. It charges via a USB-C interface, and as long as it has power, the music can play continuously. 整个音箱为极简纯白的外观，盖子内置高音质蓝牙音箱，通过 USB-C 接口充电，只要不断电，音乐就能一直播。

It also features a Spotify-customized "underworld algorithm": by answering a few questions about "afterlife vibes" and combining them with the user's personal account listening history data, it can generate a unique "Eternal Playlist." 它还有 Spotify 定制的「阴间算法」：通过回答几个关于「来世氛围感」的问题，配合用户的个人账户历史听歌数据就能生成独一无二的「永恒歌单」。

In terms of price, the Eternal Playlist Urn sells for $495 (approximately 3500 RMB). 价格方面，永恒歌单骨灰盒售价 495 美元（约合人民币 3500 元）。
