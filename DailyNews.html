iPhone 17 Pro 机身褪色上热搜，触屏版 MacBook Pro 再曝光

The released images show that the aluminum alloy back cover of the discolored iPhone 17 Pro has changed from orange to rose red, forming a strong contrast with the orange of other components. Users who encountered this emphasized that they did not post-process the images; the dark blue version of the iPhone 17 Pro, on the other hand, shows different colored marks. 从放出的图片显示，出现变色情况的 iPhone 17 Pro 的铝合金后盖从橙色变为了玫红色，与其他零部件的橙色形成了强烈对比，并且遇到的网友强调自己并没有对图片进行后期处理；深蓝色版本的 iPhone 17 Pro 则会出现不同色的印痕。

Regarding the cause of the fading, many affected users speculate it is related to ultraviolet light. They stated that after briefly placing their phones in sunlight, they observed obvious fading. Some users even joked that by simply exposing the "Starry Orange" iPhone 17 Pro to the sun for a while, they could get a unique "Rose Gold" phone for free. 而关于褪色原因，许多受影响的用户推测与紫外线有关。他们表示，在将手机短暂置于阳光下之后，便观察到了明显的褪色现象。部分用户甚至调侃称，只需将「星宇橙」 iPhone 17 Pro 放在太阳下晒一会儿，就能免费获得一台独特的「玫瑰金」手机。

法国卢浮宫被盗过程仅 7 分钟

According to Xinhua News Agency, a robbery occurred at the Louvre Museum in Paris, the capital of France, on the morning of October 19. The museum subsequently announced a temporary closure for one day. Later, the Louvre announced that the museum would be closed that day for special reasons. 据新华社报道，位于法国首都巴黎的卢浮宫博物馆 10 月 19 日上午发生抢劫事件，馆方随后宣布临时闭馆一天。随后卢浮宫宣布，出于特殊原因，博物馆当日关闭。

According to multiple media reports, at around 9:30 to 9:40 local time, several masked individuals arrived at the scene. They used a small electric saw to break a window in a maintenance area on the Seine River side of the museum, entered the target room, and stole 9 pieces of Napoleonic-era jewelry. 综合多家媒体消息，当地时间 9 点 30 分至 40 分左右，数名蒙面人员到达现场，从博物馆朝塞纳河一侧正在维修的区域，使用小型电锯破坏窗户，进入目标房间，劫走 9 件拿破仑时期珠宝。

French Interior Minister Laurent Nunez, in an interview with French media, said that this 'major robbery' was completed by 'an experienced team' in 'a very short time.' According to his description, the suspects used a lift on a truck to enter the museum, smashed glass to commit the crime, and the entire process took only 7 minutes . The value of the stolen artifacts is 'inestimable.' 法国内政部长洛朗·努内兹在接受法媒采访时说，这起「重大抢劫案」 由「一支经验丰富的团队」在「极短时间内」完成。 据其描述，嫌疑人利用卡车上的升降装置进入博物馆，砸碎玻璃作案， 整个过程仅 7 分钟 ，被抢走的文物价值「无法估量」。

The suspects are currently at large, but there are conflicting reports on how long the crime took. French Culture Minister Rachida Dati said in a media interview that the crime took less than 4 minutes. 目前嫌疑人在逃，但嫌疑人作案花了多少时间目前众说纷纭，法国文化部长拉茜达·达蒂在接受媒体采访时说，作案时间不到 4 分钟。

Nunez stated that the major armed robbery at the Louvre exposed 'significant security vulnerabilities' in the French museum system. 努内兹表示，卢浮宫发生的重大持械抢劫案暴露出法国博物馆体系「存在较大安全脆弱性」。

According to French media reports, the stolen artifacts include necklaces, brooches, crowns, etc., but the 'Regent Diamond,' weighing over 140 carats, was not stolen. 据法国媒体报道， 被抢走的文物包括项链、胸针、皇冠等，而重逾 140 克拉的「摄政王钻石」并未被抢走。

One piece of jewelry was dropped by the suspects during their escape and has since been recovered. According to Le Parisien, a crown found outside the Louvre Museum, possibly belonging to Empress Eugénie, wife of Napoleon III, was damaged when discovered. 其中，一件珠宝在嫌疑人逃跑过程中被遗落，现已被找回。按《巴黎人报》的说法，在卢浮宫博物馆外找到的是一顶皇冠，可能属于拿破仑三世的妻子欧仁妮皇后，被发现时已经破损。

Additionally, according to an interview with Louvre guide Alan by Red Star News, the robbers' target was the Apollo Gallery. The Apollo Gallery is described as the most luxurious gallery in the Louvre, with its construction history dating back to Henry IV, the first king of the Bourbon dynasty. 'The robbers were likely after the most valuable exhibits.' 另据红星新闻采访卢浮宫讲解员阿蓝获悉，匪徒的目标是阿波罗长廊。据介绍，阿波罗长廊是卢浮宫最奢华的长廊，长廊的修建历史可以追溯到波旁王朝第一位国王亨利四世。「劫匪应该是奔着最名贵展品去的。」

Alan speculated that if the aforementioned crown was stolen, then 'two other treasures in the same display case were probably also taken': including a pearl crown and a brooch made of over four thousand diamonds. Alan pessimistically predicted that the collections 'would likely be dismantled immediately,' especially the brooch, as the robbers would try to quickly fence the goods. 阿蓝推测，如果上述皇冠被盗，那么「同一个展柜里的另外两件至宝，估计也被抢走了」：包括一顶珍珠王冠，一件由四千多颗钻石制作的胸针。阿蓝悲观地预测，藏品「应该会被马上拆掉的」，尤其是那件胸针，因为劫匪会尝试迅速销赃。

我国生成式人工智能用户规模超 5 亿

According to Xinhua News Agency, on October 18, the China Internet Network Information Center released the "Generative AI Application Development Report (2025)" at the 2025 (6th) China Internet Basic Resources Conference. 据新华社报道，10 月 18 日，中国互联网络信息中心在 2025（第六届）中国互联网基础资源大会上发布《生成式人工智能应用发展报告（2025）》。

The report shows that as of June 2025, the number of generative AI users in China reached 515 million, an increase of 266 million from December 2024, doubling the user base in half a year; the penetration rate is 36.5%. 报告显示，截至 2025 年 6 月，我国生成式人工智能用户规模达 5.15 亿人，较 2024 年 12 月增长 2.66 亿人，用户规模半年翻番；普及率为 36.5%。

The report believes that generative AI is gradually integrating into the daily lives of various groups in China, with young and middle-aged, highly educated users being the core group. Among all generative AI users: 报告认为，生成式人工智能正逐渐融入我国各类群体的日常生活中，中青年、高学历用户是核心群体。在所有生成式人工智能用户中：

  • Users under 40, comprising young and middle-aged individuals, account for 74.6%; 40 岁以下中青年用户占比达到 74.6%；
  • Highly educated users with college degrees, bachelor's degrees, or higher account for 37.5%, the report points out; 大专、本科及以上高学历用户占比为 37.5%报告指出；
  • Domestic generative AI large models are widely favored by users and are driving intelligent transformation and upgrading in various application scenarios. 国产生成式人工智能大模型得到用户广泛青睐，并推动各种应用场景下的智能化改造升级。

The survey found that over 90% of users prefer domestic large models. Generative AI is widely applied in various scenarios such as intelligent search, content creation, office assistants, and smart hardware, and is also actively explored and practiced in fields like agricultural production, industrial manufacturing, and scientific research. 调查发现，超九成用户首选国产大模型。生成式人工智能被广泛应用于智能搜索、内容创作、办公助手、智能硬件等多种场景，还在农业生产、工业制造、科学研究等领域得到积极探索实践。

💡 智元机器人高级副总裁：人形机器人不会被一两家垄断

Recently, Wang Chuang, Senior Vice President of Zhiyuan Robotics, accepted an interview with Sina Finance. Regarding the competitive landscape of the domestic humanoid robot industry, Wang Chuang believes that this industry will eventually be larger than the automotive industry, and it is impossible for one or two leading companies to monopolize it. 日前，智元机器人高级副总裁王闯接受新浪财经的采访，对于如何看待国内人形机器人产业的竞争格局，王创认为，这个产业最终会比汽车产业还要大，不可能是一两家龙头垄断。

Wang Chuang pointed out that the humanoid robot industry has several characteristics: 王闯指出，人形机器人产业有几个特点：

  • The industry scale is large enough, with many different demands in segmented fields, and each company may have its own areas of expertise; 产业规模足够大，细分领域有非常多不同的需求，每一家公司可能都有一些擅长的点；
  • Every region globally has many differentiated demands. If a company can focus on these differentiated demands, it might perform even better than leading large companies. "For example, some mobile phone manufacturers thrive in Africa, and some car companies do very well in the off-road vehicle segment." 全球每一个区域都有很多差异化的需求，如果一个公司能够比较专注于做这些差异化需求，可能做得比头部的大公司还要更好。「比如有手机厂商在非洲就活得比较好，有些车企在越野车赛道做得很好。」

At the same time, Wang Chuang also stated in the interview that the difficulty of deploying humanoid robots is tiered, and working at home is actually the most challenging scenario because it involves many problems, such as safety issues, cost issues, high generalization for various complex tasks, and not damaging household items. These problems are very difficult. However, in some simpler scenarios, humanoid robots can already be deployed this year. 同时，王闯也在采访中表示，人形机器人落地的难度是分阶梯的，而到家里干活其实是最难的场景，因为它要解决的问题非常多，比如安全问题、成本问题、同时对各种复杂任务的泛化性要高，且不能破坏家里的东西，这些问题是非常难。但在一些简单的场景，人形机器人今年就已经可以落地了。

"Last year, humanoid robots couldn't even walk steadily; this year, everyone can see robots running around and even performing various fancy dances," Wang Chuang predicted. "The development speed this year has been incredibly fast. In our view, many problems we previously thought would take 3-5 years to solve have been resolved in the past year." 「去年人形机器人还走不稳，今年大家能看到机器人满地跑，甚至能够跳各种花里胡哨的舞蹈」，王闯预测，「这一年的发展速度已经非常快了，在我们看来很多过去我们认为可能 3-5 年后能解决的问题，过去一年都被解决掉了。」

Regarding the question of "robots replacing humans," Wang Chuang pointed out that the prerequisite for machines to replace humans is a very high success rate, but from a cost perspective, machines cannot yet replace manual labor. "It's too early to talk about replacing manual labor now; it would be great if they could replace it in the next five years." 对于如何看待「机器人替人」这一问题，王闯指出，想让机器代替人的前提条件是有非常好的成功率，但从成本角度讲，机器还不能替代人工。「现在谈替代人工还早得很，未来五年能替代就已经很不错了。」

In Wang Chuang's view, the reasonable path for the deployment of robots (including humanoids) will follow a sequence: first, avoid areas that involve direct contact with the physical world. Instead, focus on areas that can demonstrate their movement and interaction capabilities, easily integrate with large language models and multimodal models, provide excellent user experience, and bring practical value.
