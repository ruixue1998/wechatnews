微信灰度测试「折叠发图」功能：3 张以上图片可合并展示

Recently, some netizens posted on social platforms stating that WeChat is gray-testing a new 'fold image sending' feature. SK 集团会长：DRAM 短缺将持续到 2030 年，缺口超 20%

According to Reuters, SK Group Chairman Chey Tae-won stated in an interview with the media at the Nvidia GTC 2026 conference that the global DRAM chip shortage is expected to continue until 2030, with the shortage of basic DRAM wafers exceeding 20%. 据路透社报道，SK 集团会长崔泰源在英伟达 GTC 2026 大会上接受媒体采访时表示，全球 DRAM 芯片短缺的状况预计将持续至 2030 年，基础 DRAM 晶圆的短缺幅度将超过 20%。

He believes that the DRAM industry will need at least 'four to five years' to complete capacity expansion, stating, 'No matter where you go, the situation is the same; even establishing capacity outside of Korea will take the same amount of time.' 他认为，DRAM 产业至少需要「四到五年」来完成产能扩张，「不管到哪里，情况都是一样，即使在韩国之外建立产能，也需要同样的时间」。

Regarding capacity expansion strategy, Chey Tae-won stated that SK Hynix will currently prioritize building new wafer fabs in South Korea rather than expanding overseas. He explained that the core obstacles limiting overseas expansion are not funding or government subsidies, but rather the supply of energy and water resources, and the maturity of the local industrial ecosystem. 在产能扩张策略上，崔泰源表示，SK 海力士目前将优先在韩国本土新建晶圆厂，而非向海外扩张。他解释称，制约海外扩产的核心障碍并非资金或政府补贴，而是能源、水资源供应以及当地产业生态系统的成熟度。

Chey Tae-won also revealed that SK Hynix is preparing a plan to stabilize DRAM prices, with the specific details to be announced by the company's CEO. He also warned that if HBM production is overly focused, the shortage of general-purpose DRAM will further intensify, impacting consumer electronics markets such as smartphones and laptops. 崔泰源还透露，SK 海力士正在准备一项稳定 DRAM 价格的计划，具体方案将由公司 CEO 对外公布。他同时提醒，若过度聚焦 HBM 生产，通用 DRAM 的短缺将进一步加剧，并对智能手机、笔记本电脑等消费电子市场造成冲击。

Kimi 首披 K2.5 技术路线图：三大底层重构，马斯克点赞「令人印象深刻」

Yesterday, Yang Zhilin, founder of Moonshot AI Kimi, delivered a keynote speech titled 'How We Scaled Kimi K2.5' at the Nvidia GTC 2026 conference, systematically disclosing Kimi's complete technical roadmap for the first time, centered around three underlying architectural refactorings: 昨天，月之暗面 Kimi 创始人杨植麟在英伟达 GTC 2026 大会上发表主题演讲《How We Scaled Kimi K2.5》，首次系统性披露了 Kimi 的完整技术路线图，围绕三项底层架构重构展开：

  • MuonClip Optimizer : Addressing the Adam optimizer, which has been in use since 2014, the team introduced Newton-Schulz iteration and QK-Clip mechanism based on the Muon optimizer, solving the Logits explosion problem in trillion-parameter scale training and achieving 2 times the computational efficiency of traditional AdamW; MuonClip 优化器 ：针对沿用自 2014 年的 Adam 优化器，团队在 Muon 优化器基础上引入 Newton-Schulz 迭代与 QK-Clip 机制，解决了万亿参数规模训练中的 Logits 爆炸问题，实现了 2 倍于传统 AdamW 的计算效率；
  • Kimi Linear : A hybrid linear attention architecture based on the KDA architecture, challenging the convention that 'all layers must use full attention,' boosting decoding speed by 5 to 6 times in 128K and even 1M ultra-long context scenarios; Kimi Linear ：基于 KDA 架构的混合线性注意力架构，挑战「所有层必须使用全注意力」的惯例，在 128K 乃至 1M 超长上下文场景中，将解码速度提升 5 到 6 倍；
  • Attention Residuals : Addressing residual connections that have been used for a decade, it replaces traditional equal-weight superposition with cross-layer Softmax attention, allowing each layer to actively and selectively extract information from preceding layers. Attention Residuals ：针对沿用十年的残差连接，将传统的等权叠加替换为跨层的 Softmax 注意力，让每一层主动、有选择性地从前序层提取信息。

Among these, Attention Residuals garnered widespread attention in the industry after its release: Elon Musk commented that it was 'impressive' , former OpenAI co-founder Karpathy lamented, 'It seems we haven't fully understood 'Attention is All You Need' literally,' and Jerry Tworek, the main inventor of OpenAI o1, called it the beginning of 'Deep Learning 2.0'. 其中，Attention Residuals 发布后在业界引发广泛关注： 马斯克评论称「令人印象深刻」 ，前 OpenAI 联合创始人 Karpathy 感慨「看来我们还没把『Attention is All You Need』按字面意思理解透」，OpenAI o1 主要发明者 Jerry Tworek 称其为「深度学习 2.0」的开端。

Yang Zhilin stated that Kimi will adhere to the open-source path, contributing underlying innovations such as MuonClip, Kimi Linear, and Attention Residuals to the open-source community. 杨植麟表示，Kimi 将坚持开源路径，把 MuonClip、Kimi Linear 和 Attention Residuals 等底层创新贡献给开源社区。

小米深夜发布三款模型

Early this morning, Xiaomi centrally released three models in the MiMo V2 series: the flagship language foundation model MiMo-V2-Pro, the full-modal Agent foundation model MiMo-V2-Omni, and the speech synthesis model MiMo-V2-TTS. 今天凌晨，小米集中发布了 MiMo V2 系列三款模型：旗舰语言基座 MiMo-V2-Pro、全模态 Agent 基座 MiMo-V2-Omni，以及语音合成模型 MiMo-V2-TTS。

  • API pricing is approximately 1/5 of Claude's equivalent models: for contexts up to 256K, input is $1 / million tokens, output is $3 / million tokens; for contexts up to 1M, input is $2 / million tokens, output is $6 / million tokens. API 定价为 Claude 同级模型的约 1/5，256K 上下文以内输入 $1 / 百万 token，输出 $3 / 百万 token；1M 上下文以内输入 $2 / 百万 token，输出 $6 / 百万 token。

MiMo-V2-Omni 则是小米首个在基座层面统一感知与行动的全模态模型，融合文本、视觉与语音输入，支持 256K 上下文，定价为输入 $0.4 / 百万 token，输出 $2 / 百万 token。

苹果叫停多款 Vibe Coding 工具更新

According to The Information, Apple is quietly pressuring AI 'vibe coding' apps like Replit and Vibecode, demanding modifications before their updates are listed on the App Store, or their updates will be suspended. 据《The Information》报道，苹果正在悄然向 Replit、Vibecode 等 AI「氛围编程」（vibe coding）应用施压，要求它们在 App Store 上架更新前进行修改，否则将暂停其更新。

An Apple spokesperson stated that the policy is not specifically aimed at Vibe Coding apps, but rather a consistent enforcement of existing review guidelines: if an app undergoes significant functional changes, it must be resubmitted for review, and AI-generated code may introduce new features that have never been reviewed by Apple. 苹果发言人表示，该政策并非专门针对 Vibe Coding 类应用，而是对现行审核准则的一贯执行：应用若发生功能性重大变更，必须重新提交审核，而 AI 生成的代码可能引入从未经过苹果审查的新功能。

Specifically, Apple's review team cited App Store Review Guideline 2.5.2, which requires apps to 'be self-contained in their bundles' and not 'download, install, or execute code that introduces or changes app functionality.' 具体而言，苹果审核团队援引了 App Store 审核准则第 2.5.2 条，该条款要求应用「必须在其自身包内自足运行」，不得「下载、安装或执行引入或改变应用功能的代码」。

Replit appealed this, arguing that its AI-generated applications run in independent virtual machines and present previews via in-app Web views, which is no different in essence from how apps like X and Facebook embed web browsing. Replit 对此提出申诉，认为其 AI 生成的应用运行于独立虚拟机中，并通过应用内 Web 视图呈现预览，与 X、Facebook 等应用内置网页浏览的方式并无本质区别。

After months of appeal back-and-forth, Apple's review team is currently inclined to approve Replit's update, but requires that previews of AI-generated apps be opened in a separate browser rather than embedded within the app. Vibecode was told that if it removes the functionality for generating native Apple device apps, its update is highly likely to be approved. 经过数月的申诉拉锯，苹果审核团队目前倾向于批准 Replit 的更新，但要求其将 AI 生成应用的预览改为在独立浏览器中打开，而非嵌入应用内部。Vibecode 则被告知，若移除生成苹果设备原生应用的功能，其更新大概率将获批。

蔚来分拆芯片业务，寻求对外供货

According to 36Kr, Nio's chip subsidiary, Shenji Technology, is actively expanding its chip supply business to external car manufacturers. 据 36 氪报道，蔚来旗下芯片子公司神玑科技正积极向外部车企拓展芯片供应业务。

Insiders revealed that after the M97 chip, jointly developed by Shenji and Aixin Yuanzhi, completed tape-out, both parties have begun contacting car manufacturers such as Leapmotor and Geely. The M97 boasts computing power exceeding 700 TOPS, with performance comparable to Horizon Journey J6P (officially rated at 560 TOPS), and is expected to be officially released in the third quarter of this year. 知情人士透露，神玑与爱芯元智合作研发的芯片 M97 完成流片后，双方已开始接触零跑、吉利等车企。M97 算力超 700 TOPS，性能对标地平线征程 J6P（官方标称 560 TOPS），预计于今年三季度正式发布。

Nio independently spun off its chip business in June last year, establishing Anhui Shenji Technology Co., Ltd. 蔚来于去年 6 月将芯片业务独立拆分，成立安徽神玑技术公司。

Last November, Shenji, together with Aixin Yuanzhi and its parent company OmniVision Technologies, jointly invested to establish a joint venture company, "Chongqing Chuangyuan Zhihang Technology Co., Ltd." in Chongqing. The M97 chip will be shipped from this joint venture, marking NIO's first external supply of assisted driving chip technology. 去年 11 月，神玑与爱芯元智及其母公司豪威集成电路共同出资，在重庆成立合资公司「重庆创元智航科技有限公司」。M97 芯片将从该合资公司出货，标志着蔚来辅助驾驶芯片技术首次对外供应。

Industry insiders close to Aixin Yuanzhi described the uniqueness of this collaboration: "It's neither buying IP nor directly purchasing original chips. It can be understood as people from both sides joining forces to create a new chip, and then re-taping out." 接近爱芯元智的行业人士描述了这次合作的特殊性：「既不是买 IP，也不是直接采购原版芯片，可以理解为是两边的人联手做了一个新的芯片，然后重新流片。」

This new chip adopts the core modules and architecture of NIO's Shenji chip, but Aixin Yuanzhi reduced the specifications from over 1000 TOPS of the original NX9031 to about 700 TOPS to control costs and enhance market competitiveness. It is reported that NIO gained hundreds of millions of yuan in licensing fees through this technology licensing cooperation. 这颗新芯片沿用了蔚来神玑芯片的核心模块与架构，但爱芯元智将规格从原版 NX9031 的逾 1000 TOPS 削减至约 700 TOPS，以控制成本并提升市场竞争力。据悉，蔚来通过此次技术授权合作获得了数亿元授权费收入。

Beyond the M97, Shenji and Aixin Yuanzhi are also planning a low-end chip, codenamed 9031e, with performance benchmarked against Horizon Journey J6M (officially rated at 128 TOPS), aiming to further expand their external supply footprint. 在 M97 之外，神玑与爱芯元智还在规划一款低端芯片，项目代号为 9031e，性能对标地平线征程 J6M（官方标称 128 TOPS），旨在进一步拓展外供版图。

乐天「日本最强 AI」塌房：扒开代码全是 DeepSeek，还删了开源协议

On March 17, Japanese conglomerate Rakuten grandly launched its large language model, Rakuten AI 3.0.
