海力士市值突破 1 万亿美元

SK Hynix's stock price once rose 11% during trading, with its market capitalization reaching 1605.72 trillion Korean Won, equivalent to approximately 7 trillion Chinese Yuan at current exchange rates, breaking the $1 trillion USD threshold. Over the past year, SK Hynix's stock price has cumulatively increased by over 1019%, mainly due to the continuous surge in demand for HBM high-bandwidth memory. 海力士盘中股价一度上涨 11%，市值达到 1605.72 万亿韩元，按现汇率约合 7 万亿元人民币，突破 1 万亿美元门槛。过去一年，海力士股价累计上涨超过 1019%，主要来自 HBM 高带宽内存需求持续升温。

SK Hynix thus becomes another chip company in the global semiconductor/tech industry to reach the "trillion-dollar spotlight moment," following giants like Nvidia, Microsoft, TSMC, and Broadcom. 海力士由此成为继英伟达、微软、台积电、博通等巨头之后，全球半导体行业/科技界又一迎来「万亿美元高光时刻」的芯片企业。

阿里调整十三薪发放方式

Alibaba yesterday issued an all-staff letter, stating that starting from fiscal year 27, the original "thirteenth-month salary" will revert to its essence as an annual bonus, adjusted to "Moving Forward Together Annual Bonus," and will be distributed along with each fiscal year's performance annual bonus. The year-end bonus will consist of two parts, "Moving Forward Together Annual Bonus" and "Performance Annual Bonus," listed separately. 阿里巴巴昨日发布全员信，自 27 财年起，原十三薪将回归年奖本质，调整为「并肩前行年奖」，并与每财年的绩效年奖一起发放，年终奖由「并肩前行年奖」和「绩效年奖」两部分组成、单独列示。

Alibaba's original 13th-month salary was usually distributed from December to January of the following year. After being merged with the year-end bonus, the distribution time will be postponed to April to May. 阿里原十三薪通常在每年 12 月至次年 1 月发放，与年终奖合并后，发放时间顺延至 4 月至 5 月。

The all-staff letter also mentioned that because the original 13th-month salary cycle is a calendar year and the annual bonus cycle is a fiscal year, the first "Shoulder-to-Shoulder Progress Annual Bonus" will be distributed in the bonus month of 2027. If an employee is on duty from January 1, 2026, to March 31, 2027, they will receive a 1.25-month Shoulder-to-Shoulder Progress Annual Bonus. 全员信还提到，因原十三薪周期为自然年、年奖周期为财年，第一次「并肩前行年奖」将于 2027 年奖发放月发放。若员工 2026 年 1 月 1 日至 2027 年 3 月 31 日均在岗，将获得 1.25 个月的并肩前行年奖。

多家 AI 平台回应「高考期间答题受限」传闻

According to Red Star News, in response to online discussions about "AI tools being disabled during the college entrance examination," several AI platforms have responded. Doubao customer service stated that Doubao can be used normally during the college entrance examination, but similar functions such as photo-based problem solving and Q&A will be disabled, and corresponding restrictions will be displayed on the page during the exam. 据红星新闻报道，针对「高考期间 AI 工具将禁用」的网络讨论，多家 AI 平台给出了回应。豆包客服表示，高考期间豆包可正常使用，但拍题答疑等类似功能会被禁用，考试期间页面会显示对应限制。

Tencent staff said that during last year's college entrance examination, Yuanbao had explicitly stated that it would not answer questions during the exam and suspended service requests related to the college entrance examination. Baidu's relevant business head said that they have not yet received notification of restrictions on some functions of Baidu Wenxin during the college entrance examination; iFlytek staff said that there is no definite news yet, but as long as it is a large model, there should be restrictions. 腾讯工作人员称，去年高考期间元宝已经明确考试期间不答题，并暂停涉及高考的服务请求。百度相关业务负责人表示，暂未收到高考期间百度文心部分功能限制服务的通知；科大讯飞工作人员称，目前还没有确切消息，但只要是大模型，应该都会有限制。

The report reviewed similar settings during last year's college entrance examination: Tencent Yuanbao had prompted that image recognition and summarization could not be used during the college entrance examination period, Alibaba Tongyi App showed that the photo-based problem-solving service was temporarily closed, and tools like Quark, Doubao, and Kimi also imposed restrictions on exam-related images or questions. 报道回顾了去年高考期间的类似设置：腾讯元宝曾对识图总结提示高考时段无法使用，阿里通义 App 显示拍照讲题服务暂时关闭，夸克、豆包、Kimi 等工具也对考试相关图片或题目作出限制。

英伟达退役沿用约 20 年 GeForce 控制面板的游戏驱动支持

According to IT Home, Nvidia released the GeForce Game Ready 610.47 WHQL driver on May 26 local time, and announced that the classic NVIDIA Control Panel officially canceled Game Ready and Studio driver support. 据 IT 之家报道，英伟达当地时间 5 月 26 日发布 GeForce Game Ready 610.47 WHQL 驱动，并宣布经典 NVIDIA 控制面板正式取消 Game Ready 和 Studio 驱动支持。

Nvidia stated that after the NVIDIA App update, control panel functions for GeForce users have been migrated to the new client. The installed NVIDIA Control Panel can still be used, but new drivers no longer maintain corresponding functions for GeForce consumer cards. 英伟达称，NVIDIA App 更新后，面向 GeForce 用户的控制面板功能已经迁移到新客户端。已安装的 NVIDIA 控制面板仍可使用，但新驱动不再面向 GeForce 消费卡维护对应功能。

伯克利法学院收紧学生使用 AI 规则

According to Reuters, the University of California, Berkeley School of Law's new AI policy will take effect this summer, restricting students from using chatbots in coursework and exams. Related discussions have already sparked controversy in legal education and law firm internship settings. 据路透社报道，加州大学伯克利法学院的新 AI 政策将于今年夏季生效，限制学生在课程作业和考试中使用聊天机器人。相关讨论已经在法律教育和律所实习场景中引发争议。

Publicly leaked policy explanations show that Berkeley Law School focuses its restrictions on stages such as concept ideation, outlining, drafting, revision, editing, translation, and exam use, reasoning that legal education still requires students to independently complete reasoning and writing training. However, some writing refinement is still allowed to use AI. 公开流出的政策说明显示，伯克利法学院把限制重点放在概念构思、提纲、起草、修改、编辑、翻译和考试使用等环节，理由是法律教育仍需要学生独立完成推理和写作训练。不过，部分写作润色仍允许使用 AI。

YouTube 将 AI 标签移到视频主界面

According to The Verge, YouTube announced adjustments to its AI content disclosure method, moving the "AI" label from the video description area to a more prominent position. For regular long videos, the label will appear below the player and above the description area; for Shorts, it will be displayed as a video overlay. 据 The Verge 报道，YouTube 宣布调整 AI 内容披露方式，把「AI」标签从视频描述区移到更醒目的位置。普通长视频的标签会显示在播放器下方、描述区上方；Shorts 则会以视频叠层形式展示。

This adjustment targets video content that is "realistic" or "substantially modified by AI." In the past, viewers needed to expand the description area and check "How this content was made" to see disclosure information; the new label directly states AI and is accompanied by an information icon. YouTube stated that non-realistic, animated, or slightly modified content can still have disclosures viewed in the expanded description. 这次调整针对「逼真」或「有实质 AI 改动」的视频内容。过去，观众需要展开描述区并查看「这段内容如何制作」才能看到披露信息；新标签直接写明 AI，并配有信息图标。YouTube 表示，非写实、动画或轻微修改内容仍可在展开描述中查看披露。

YouTube will also launch new internal identification signals this month. If a creator does not actively disclose, but the system detects significant realistic AI use in a video, the platform will automatically add an AI label. Creators can update the disclosure status in YouTube Studio; YouTube 还将在本月推出新的内部识别信号。如果创作者没有主动披露，但系统检测到视频存在显著的写实 AI 使用，平台会自动加上 AI 标签。创作者可以在 YouTube Studio 更新披露状态；

However, if generated using YouTube's own AI tools such as Veo and Dream Screen, or if the file contains C2PA metadata proving it was entirely generated by AI, the label will be retained. 但使用 YouTube 自家 Veo、Dream Screen 等 AI 工具生成，或文件包含 C2PA 元数据证明完全由 AI 生成时，标签会保留。

💡 Spotify CEO：受控的 AI 音乐比盗版和无授权生成更可取

According to The Guardian, Spotify CEO Alex Norström yesterday defended the company's entry into AI-generated music. 据《卫报》报道，Spotify CEO Alex Norström 昨日为公司进入 AI 生成音乐辩护。

In an interview with the Financial Times, he stated that Spotify is trying to offer a "controlled" alternative, allowing musicians to consent to the use of their work and earn revenue, thereby avoiding piracy or unauthorized use by AI tools. 他在接受《金融时报》采访时表示，Spotify 试图提供一种「受控」替代方案，让音乐人能够同意作品被使用并获得收益，避免作品被盗版或被无授权 AI 工具使用。

Last week, Spotify reached a licensing agreement with Universal Music Group, which will allow subscribers to create AI-generated covers and remixes in the future. Norström stated that there have already been numerous unauthorized attempts externally, and the platform needs to incorporate licensing, consent, and payment mechanisms into product design in advance. 上周，Spotify 与环球音乐集团达成授权协议，未来将允许订阅用户用 AI 创作翻唱和混音版本。Norström 称，外部已经出现大量违规尝试，平台需要把许可、同意和付费机制提前放进产品设计中。

The solution he proposed is to integrate AI music into a licensing, consent, and payment framework, allowing generated content to leave an authorized trail within the platform. The report mentioned that related tools would charge additional fees and allow a song to be expanded into more cover or remix versions. 他给出的方案是把 AI 音乐纳入许可、同意和付费框架中，让生成内容在平台内留下授权链路。报道提到，相关工具会收取额外费用，并允许一首歌曲被扩展为更多翻唱或混音版本。
