数百 GB、超 20 万份 iPhone 18 Pro 机密文件流出

According to Reuters, the ransomware group World Leaks has uploaded sensitive files related to the iPhone 18 Pro series to the dark web after infiltrating Apple's Indian supplier, Tata Electronics. 据路透社报道，勒索软件组织 World Leaks 入侵苹果印度供应商塔塔电子后，已将涉及 iPhone 18 Pro 系列的敏感文件上传至暗网。

Sources claim that this leak involves hundreds of GBs, totaling over 200,000 files, covering component and supplier lists, drop test photos, and related drawings for the A20 chip and C2 baseband. 有消息称，此次泄露文件体量达数百 GB，共超 20 万份，涵盖零部件与供应商清单、跌落测试照片，以及 A20 芯片和 C2 基带的相关图纸。

Documents obtained by Reuters show that at least 6 files detail the suppliers corresponding to each component of the iPhone 18 Pro, involving main circuit board chips, batteries, and camera modules, collectively covering hundreds of components. 路透社获取的文件显示，至少 6 份文件详细列出了 iPhone 18 Pro 各零部件对应的供应商，涉及主电路板芯片、电池及摄像头组件，合计涵盖数百种零部件。

Multiple documents bear Apple's "Confidential" watermark, with internal codenames consistent with the iPhone 18 Pro product generation. Drop test photos taken earlier this year show a gray slab device being tested at a Tata Electronics factory, which sources say is the iPhone 18 Pro series. 多份文件带有苹果「机密」水印，内部代号与 iPhone 18 Pro 产品世代一致。跌落测试照片拍摄于今年初，显示一台灰色直板设备在塔塔电子工厂接受测试，知情人士称拍摄对象为 iPhone 18 Pro 系列。

In addition to the iPhone 18 Pro, the leaked content also involves design data for older iPhone components, some Tesla component files, and relevant documents from TSMC and Qualcomm. Apple has launched an investigation, while Tata Electronics has restricted employee access to sensitive systems and hired a consulting firm to conduct a forensic audit. 泄露内容除 iPhone 18 Pro 外，还涉及旧款 iPhone 零部件设计资料、特斯拉部分零部件文件，以及台积电和高通的相关文档。目前，苹果已启动调查，塔塔电子则限制员工访问敏感系统，并聘请咨询公司开展取证审计。

Claude Sonnet 5 正式上线，想把 AI Agent 运行成本打下来

On the 30th local time, Anthropic released its new generation mid-sized model, Claude Sonnet 5, with a core focus on strengthening AI agent capabilities. The company stated that the model's performance in autonomous planning, browser, and terminal tool invocation has reached the level of previous large and expensive models. 当地时间 30 日，Anthropic 发布新一代中型模型 Claude Sonnet 5，核心强化了 AI 智能体能力。官方表示，该模型在自主规划、浏览器及终端工具调用方面的表现，已达到此前大型昂贵模型的水平。

In terms of performance, Sonnet 5 scored 63.2% in programming benchmarks, approaching the top model Opus 4.8, and significantly outperforming its predecessor Sonnet 4.6. 性能方面，Sonnet 5 在编程基准测试中得分 63.2%，接近顶级模型 Opus 4.8，并显著优于前代 Sonnet 4.6。

Early partner feedback indicates that the model can autonomously complete multi-step, cross-platform tasks such as updating Salesforce accounts and sending announcements, and possesses stronger self-checking capabilities. In terms of security, the new model shows improvements in rejecting malicious requests, reducing hallucination rates, and resisting prompt injection. 早期合作伙伴反馈显示，该模型能自主完成更新 Salesforce 账户、发送公告等跨平台多步任务，且具备更强的自我检查能力。安全性上，新模型在拒绝恶意请求、降低幻觉率及抵御提示词注入方面均有提升。

Sonnet 5 is now the default model for both the free and Pro versions. In terms of pricing, the model's initial price on the Claude Platform is $2 per million input tokens and $10 per million output tokens (discounted until August 31st), after which it will be adjusted to $3 and $15 respectively. Sonnet 5 即日起成为免费版与 Pro 版的默认模型。价格方面，模型在 Claude Platform 的初始定价为每百万输入 Token 2 美元，每百万输出 Token 10 美元（优惠至 8 月 31 日），随后将调整为 3 美元和 15 美元。

Anthropic emphasizes that Sonnet 5's goal is to significantly reduce the cost for developers to run AI agents while maintaining high performance. Anthropic 强调，Sonnet 5 的目标是在维持高性能的同时，大幅降低开发者运行 AI 智能体的成本。

字节 CEO 梁汝波发全员信：要求 Leader 实质产出

According to Phoenix Network Technology, ByteDance CEO Liang Rubo sent an email to all employees on the evening of June 29, announcing an update to the company's leadership principles after four years. 据凤凰网科技报道，字节跳动 CEO 梁汝波于 6 月 29 日晚间向全员发布邮件，宣布时隔四年更新公司领导力原则。

Liang Rubo stated in the email that the company has distilled its operational thinking and principles into management philosophies, primarily comprising four parts: business strategy formulation principles, organizational management, talent strategy, and public affairs. 梁汝波在邮件中表示，公司将运作思考和原则沉淀为管理理念，主要包括业务战略制定原则、组织管理、人才策略和公共事务四个部分。

This updated set of leadership principles consists of 10 items; the previous version was released in 2022. Liang Rubo highlighted several key changes in the email: 此次更新的领导力原则共 10 条，上一版本发布于 2022 年。梁汝波在邮件中重点说明了几项主要变化：

  • Added two requirements: "Do things with vision" and "Dare to set high goals," urging managers to step out of daily routines, anchor long-term high-value directions, and avoid relying on resource inertia to maintain the status quo; 新增「做有高度的事」和「敢于设定高目标」两条要求，要求管理者跳出日常事务，锚定长期高价值方向，避免依靠资源惯性维持现状；
  • Separated "Maintain a sense of crisis and an external perspective" and "Go deep into the front line" as independent items, clearly requiring management to regularly descend to the front lines of business, products, and users; 将「有危机感，保持外部视角」和「深入一线」单独列为独立条目，明确要求管理层常态化下沉至业务、产品与用户一线；
  • Strengthened the "Context over Control" management philosophy, requiring a reduction in multi-level approvals, repetitive meetings, and ineffective reports, and focusing energy on solving actual business problems. 强化「Context over Control」管理理念，要求减少多层级审批、重复会议和无效报表，将精力集中于解决实际业务问题。

It is reported that the new leadership principles will be incorporated into managers' promotion and annual performance review standards. Managers who fail to deliver results or are detached from the business front lines will face adjustments to their positions and responsibilities. 据报道，新版领导力原则将纳入管理者的晋升和年度考核标准，无法落地产出、脱离业务一线的管理层将面临岗位权责调整。

WPS 回应 C 盘占用争议：7 月新版本将支持路径自选与一键清理

据《21世纪经济报道》消息，WPS 昨日针对近期引发热议的系统卡顿及 C 盘占用问题再次作出回应。公司表示，优化后的新版本将于 7 月正式上线，届时用户可前往官网下载更新。

针对用户反映强烈的缓存与备份默认存放 C 盘、占用情况不直观等痛点，新版本将围绕「安装有选择、使用可管理」进行以下重点优化：

  • 安装环节路径自选：在安装界面新增路径选择功能，将安装路径、文件备份路径、云文档存储路径三条路径分开设置，互不绑定；
  • 新增存储管理模块：在设置页集中展示缓存、文件备份、云文档缓存等各类文件的空间占用情况，支持一键清理并反馈清理结果；
  • 自主修改存储路径：支持在使用过程中随时变更存储路径，系统将在变更前自动校验目标盘的空间与权限。

WPS 此前曾解释称，本地保留云文档缓存副本是为了实现多设备同步而无需重复下载，是行业通行做法。但公司承认在产品设计上对用户知情权和选择权的重视程度仍有改进空间，未来将持续优化产品功能。

动力电池装车需求今年预计达 888.7 GWh，同比增长 15.8%

Yesterday, the China Automotive Power Battery Industry Innovation Alliance 2026 Annual Forum was held in Beijing. Ma Xiaoli, Deputy Director of the National Power Battery Innovation Center, disclosed at the forum that, affected by the slowdown in the growth of new energy vehicles, the domestic power battery industry has transitioned from a high-speed growth stage to a "steady growth, structural upgrading" stage, with overall growth rate decreasing compared to 2025. 昨日，中国汽车动力电池产业创新联盟 2026 年度论坛在北京召开。国家动力电池创新中心副主任马小利在论坛上披露，受新能源汽车增量放缓影响，国内动力电池行业已由高速增长阶段转入「稳健增长、结构升级」阶段，整体增速较 2025 年有所下降。

Ma Xiaoli expects that this year, the total domestic power battery installation demand will reach 888.7 GWh, a year-on-year increase of 15.8%. By vehicle type, the installation demand for new energy passenger vehicles is expected to reach 672.7 GWh, a year-on-year increase of 10.8%; the installation demand for new energy commercial vehicles is expected to reach 216.1 GWh, a year-on-year increase of 34.7%, with commercial vehicle growth significantly higher than passenger vehicles. 马小利预计，今年国内动力电池全年装车需求将达 888.7 GWh，同比增长 15.8%。分车型来看，新能源乘用车装车需求预计达 672.7 GWh，同比增长 10.8%；新能源商用车装车需求预计达 216.1 GWh，同比增长 34.7%，商用车增速明显高于乘用车。

💡 研究：把 AI 智能体叫做「员工」，会让人类犯更多错误

According to MIT Technology Review, the marketing approach of packaging AI agents as "digital employees" may be systematically undermining human employees' judgment and sense of responsibility. 据 MIT Technology Review 报道，将 AI 智能体包装成「数字员工」的营销方式，可能正在系统性地削弱人类员工的判断力与责任感。

Research by Emma Wiles, a researcher at Boston University's Questrom School of Business, found that when AI tools were defined as "AI employees" rather than ordinary chatbots, the ability of managers participating in the experiment to identify errors decreased by 18%, and 44% were more inclined to report problematic results to superiors rather than correcting them themselves. 波士顿大学商学院研究员 Emma Wiles 的研究发现，当 AI 工具被定义为「AI 员工」而非普通聊天机器人时，参与实验的管理者识别错误的能力下降了 18%，且有 44% 更倾向于将有问题的结果上报给上级，而非自行纠正。

Among the 1,261 managers participating in the study, nearly one-third stated that their companies classified AI agents as "employees," and even 23% of companies included them in their organizational charts. 参与研究的 1,261 名管理者中，已有近三分之一表示其所在公司将 AI 智能体定性为「员工」，甚至有 23% 的公司将其列入组织架构图。

MIT economist and 2024 Nobel laureate Daron Acemoglu also stated that current AI agents "are being marketed as tools to replace humans, which is a dead end," and believes they should be optimized as tools to augment human capabilities, not replacements. MIT 经济学家、2024 年诺贝尔奖得主 Daron Acemoglu 也表示，当前 AI 智能体「被当作替代人类的工具来营销，这是一条走不通的路」，并认为其应当被优化为增强人类能力的工具，而非替代品。

MIT Technology Review pointed out that the "digital employee" narrative is essentially a branding exercise that does not enhance the tool's capabilities but makes surrounding human employees perform worse. As AI agents accelerate their penetration into critical areas such as healthcare, education, and government affairs, how to avoid a responsibility vacuum in deployment has become a systemic risk that cannot be ignored. MIT Technology Review 指出，「数字员工」的叙事本质上是一种品牌包装，并不能提升工具本身的能力，却会让周围的人类员工表现更差。随着 AI 智能体加速渗透医疗、教育、政务等关键领域，如何在部署方式上避免责任真空，已成为不可忽视的系统性风险。

VitaBench 2.0 includes 56 simulated users, 819 complex tasks, over 2000 dynamic preferences, and 66 executable tools. Each user averages 2093 interaction events with an average time span of 1580 days. The system exposes history to the Agent along a timeline to test the model's ability to continuously extract, update, and utilize user preferences. VitaBench 2.0 包含 56 名拟真用户、819 个复杂任务、超过 2000 个动态偏好和 66 个可执行工具。每位用户平均包含 2093 个交互事件，平均时间跨度 1580 天，系统按时间线向 Agent 暴露历史，用来测试模型持续提取、更新并使用用户偏好的能力。

The Meituan team stated that experiments show even in modes where all historical records are visible, the strongest model's average score barely exceeded 0.5; after integrating Agentic Memory or RAG Memory, most models performed worse than directly using the full historical record. 美团团队称，实验显示即使在能看到全部历史记录的模式下，最强模型平均分也刚过 0.5；接入 Agentic Memory 或 RAG Memory 后，多数模型表现低于直接使用全历史记录。
