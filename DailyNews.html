🦞 国家互联网应急中心：养虾需谨慎

Yesterday, the National Computer Network Emergency Response Technical Team/Coordination Center of China (CNCERT/CC) issued a "Risk Alert Regarding OpenClaw Security Applications". 昨日，国家互联网应急中心发布「关于 OpenClaw 安全应用的风险提示」。

It is reported that OpenClaw (also known as "Little Lobster", formerly Clawdbot, Moltbot) applications are experiencing booming download and usage, with mainstream domestic cloud platforms offering one-click deployment services. However, to achieve its "autonomous task execution" capability, the application has been granted high system permissions. 据悉，OpenClaw（「小龙虾」，曾用名 Clawdbot、Moltbot）应用下载与使用情况火爆，国内主流云平台均提供了一键部署服务。但因其为实现「自主执行任务」的能力，该应用被授予了较高的系统权限。

The National Computer Network Emergency Response Technical Team/Coordination Center of China (CNCERT/CC) pointed out that due to the improper installation and use of OpenClaw agents, several serious security risks have emerged: 国家互联网应急中心对此指出，由于 OpenClaw 智能体的不当安装和使用，已经出现了一些严重的安全风险：

  • "Prompt Injection" Risk: Network attackers can construct hidden malicious instructions on a webpage, inducing OpenClaw to read the page, which may lead to it being tricked into leaking user system keys; 「提示词注入」风险：网络攻击者通过在网页中构造隐藏的恶意指令，诱导 OpenClaw 读取该网页，就可能导致其被诱导将用户系统密钥泄露；
  • "Misoperation" Risk: Due to incorrect understanding of user operation instructions and intentions, OpenClaw may permanently delete important information such as emails and core production data; 「误操作」风险：由于错误的理解用户操作指令和意图，OpenClaw 可能会将电子邮件、核心生产数据等重要信息彻底删除；
  • Function Plugin (Skills) Poisoning Risk: Multiple function plugins suitable for OpenClaw have been identified as malicious or having potential security risks. After installation, they can perform malicious operations such as stealing keys and deploying Trojan backdoor software, turning the device into a "botnet zombie"; 功能插件（skills）投毒风险：多个适用于 OpenClaw 的功能插件已被确认为恶意插件或存在潜在的安全风险，安装后可执行窃取密钥、部署木马后门软件等恶意操作，使得设备沦为「肉鸡」；
  • Security Vulnerability Risk: As of now, OpenClaw has publicly exposed multiple high and medium-risk vulnerabilities. If these vulnerabilities are maliciously exploited by network attackers, it could lead to severe consequences such as system compromise, and the leakage of private and sensitive data. 安全漏洞风险。截止目前，OpenClaw 已经公开曝出多个高中危漏洞，一旦这些漏洞被网络攻击者恶意利用，则可能导致系统被控、隐私信息和敏感数据泄露的严重后果。

Therefore, the National Computer Network Emergency Response Technical Team/Coordination Center of China (CNCERT/CC) recommends that relevant organizations and individual users take corresponding security measures when deploying and using OpenClaw, such as "not directly exposing OpenClaw's default management port to the public network," "strengthening credential management," and "strictly managing plugin sources." 因此，国家互联网应急中心建议相关单位和个人用户在部署和应用 OpenClaw 时，应采取相对应的安全措施，如「不将 OpenClaw 默认管理端口直接暴露在公网上」「加强凭证管理」「严格管理插件来源」等。

It is reported that WorldCompass is a reinforcement learning (RL) post-training framework specifically designed for long-sequence, interactive world models. 💡 黄仁勋：AI 与电力一样同等重要

Yesterday, Nvidia announced that CEO Jensen Huang published an article proposing the AI "Five-Layer Cake" architecture model, explicitly defining AI as a modern infrastructure as important as electricity. 昨天，英伟达公布了 CEO 黄仁勋发表署名文章提出 AI 「五层蛋糕」架构模型，并明确将 AI 定性为与电力同等重要的现代基础设施。

In the article, Jensen Huang pointed out that computing technology is transitioning from traditional pre-fabricated software to real-time generated intelligence, and this evolution of underlying logic requires a complete reconstruction of the entire computing architecture. He breaks down the AI architecture from an industrial perspective into five layers from bottom to top: energy, chips, infrastructure, models, and applications. 黄仁勋在文章中指出，计算技术正从传统的预制软件向实时生成的智能转变，这种底层逻辑的演进要求整个计算架构进行彻底重构。他将工业视角的 AI 架构自下而上拆解为五个层级：能源、芯片、基础设施、模型与应用。

The article emphasizes that energy is the primary bottleneck limiting the output scale of intelligent systems; the chip layer determines the speed and efficiency of AI expansion; the infrastructure layer manifests as AI factories designed to "manufacture intelligence"; the model layer is expanding from language to cutting-edge fields such as biochemistry and physical simulation; and the top application layer (e.g., autonomous driving, humanoid robots) is responsible for creating economic value. 文中强调，能源是制约智能系统产出规模的首要瓶颈；芯片层决定了 AI 的扩展速度与效率；基础设施层表现为旨在「制造智能」的 AI 工厂；模型层正从语言扩展至生物化学、物理模拟等前沿领域；顶层的应用层（如自动驾驶、人形机器人）则负责创造经济价值。

These five layers reinforce each other, and the success of any top-layer application will create strong demand for underlying equipment and energy. 这五个层级相互强化，任何顶层应用的成功都会对底层设备与能源产生强烈的需求拉动。

At the industry trend level, the article affirmed the critical role of open-source models in activating full-stack computing power demand. Jensen Huang pointed out, taking DeepSeek-R1 as an example, that the widespread availability of high-performance inference models directly accelerates the popularization of the application layer and, in turn, drives the scaled growth of underlying training, computing facilities, and energy. 在行业动向层面，该文章肯定了开源模型对激活全栈算力需求的关键作用。黄仁勋以 DeepSeek-R1 为例指出，高性能推理模型的广泛开放直接加速了应用层的普及，并逆向带动了底层训练、算力设施及能源的规模化增长。
