曝苹果用 Gemini 训练端侧 AI，部分 Siri 请求转向谷歌云

According to The Information, Apple plans to fully leverage on-device AI computing power at next month's Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC), while also introducing Nvidia's privacy technology to ensure the security of some cloud-based AI tasks. 据《The Information》报道，苹果计划在下月的全球开发者大会（WWDC）上全面发力端侧 AI 算力，同时将引入英伟达的隐私技术以保障部分云端 AI 任务的安全性。

Sources revealed that Apple is using Google's large language model Gemini to train lightweight models that can run locally (i.e., model distillation). 知情人士透露，苹果正利用谷歌大语言模型 Gemini 训练可本地运行的轻量化模型（即模型蒸馏）。

To advance model miniaturization, Apple is seeking to acquire relevant startups, with Liquid AI, an edge AI company in Cambridge, Massachusetts, being one of its potential acquisition targets. 为推进模型小型化，苹果正在寻求收购相关初创公司，马萨诸塞州剑桥市的边缘 AI 企业 Liquid AI 曾是其潜在的收购目标之一。

To address complex cloud-based demands that edge devices cannot handle, Apple has approved the use of Nvidia's Confidential Compute privacy protection technology within the Google Cloud environment. 针对端侧设备无法处理的复杂云端需求，苹果已批准在谷歌云（Google Cloud）环境中使用英伟达的机密计算（Confidential Compute）隐私保护技术。

This technology ensures the security of cloud AI requests by processing data and models with hardware-level encryption. This also means that while Apple maintains its 'Private Cloud Compute' brand promise, the underlying processing still relies on Nvidia GPUs and Google's cloud infrastructure. 该技术通过硬件级加密处理中的数据与模型，确保云端 AI 请求的安全性。这也意味着苹果在维持其「私有云计算（Private Cloud Compute）」品牌承诺的同时，底层处理过程仍需依赖英伟达 GPU 及谷歌的云基础设施。

Regarding AI usage costs, Jensen Huang consistently advocates for employees to use AI extensively. 刘强东提出「3 个 12」原则，京东规定员工每日工作不超 12 小时

According to TechWeb, JD.com recently introduced a new work hour management rule internally called '3 x 12'. This principle was proposed by founder Richard Liu last week and is being gradually implemented across multiple departments within the group starting this week. Relevant notifications have now been cascaded down from C1 and C2 levels, and frontline R&D teams have received specific arrangements. 据 TechWeb 报道，京东集团近日在内部推出了一项名为「3 个 12」的工作时间管理新规。该原则由创始人刘强东于上周提出，并于本周起在集团内多个部门逐步推进落实。目前相关通知已从 C1、C2 层级向下传达，一线研发团队已收到具体安排。

The '3 x 12' principle revolves around three quantitative indicators: 「3 个 12」原则围绕三项量化指标展开：

  • First, employees' daily working hours should generally not exceed 12 hours, with exceptions only during major promotional periods or special projects; 其一，员工每日工作时长原则上不超过 12 小时，仅大促期间或特殊项目情况下可作例外；
  • Second, employees are encouraged to leave the company no later than 12 AM, and workplaces should guide employees on duty to leave for rest and arrange lights out at midnight 24:00; 其二，倡导员工不晚于夜间 12 点离开公司，各职场在午夜 24 点时应引导在岗员工离岗休息并安排熄灯；
  • Third, the number of consecutive working days for employees shall not exceed 12 days, to prevent long-term overtime without rest. 其三，员工连续在岗工作天数不得超过 12 天，以防止长期无休加班。

JD.com stated that the introduction of this new regulation is a proactive adjustment made in response to the excessive workload of some internal teams, aiming to explicitly incorporate employee physical and mental health into management guidance and standardize daily office work and overtime management mechanisms through quantified working hour standards. 京东方面表示，此次新规的出台，是针对内部部分团队工作强度过大问题所作的主动调整，旨在将员工身心健康明确纳入管理导向，通过量化的工作时间标准规范日常办公与加班管理机制。

工信部：动力电池进入规模化退役阶段

On May 28, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology held the second meeting of the national special working group on the recycling and utilization of new energy vehicle power batteries, stating that China's new energy vehicle power batteries have entered a large-scale retirement phase, and accelerating the improvement of the waste power battery recycling and utilization system has become a key task for 2026. 工业和信息化部 5 月 28 日召开全国新能源汽车动力电池回收利用工作专班第二次会议，称我国新能源汽车动力电池已进入规模化退役阶段，加快健全废旧动力电池回收利用体系已经成为 2026 年重点工作。

The meeting called for strengthening collaboration around the construction of laws, policies, and standard systems, as well as research on major issues, and launching a special joint law enforcement action to regulate the recycling and utilization of waste power batteries. The focus will be on investigating and prosecuting violations such as illegal delivery of waste power batteries, using waste power batteries to produce unqualified products, failing to fulfill information traceability responsibilities, illegal dismantling causing environmental pollution, and operating without a license. 会议要求围绕法规、政策、标准体系建设和重大问题研究加强协同，并开展规范废旧动力电池回收利用联合执法专项行动。重点查处违规交售废旧动力电池、用废旧动力电池生产不合格产品、不履行信息溯源责任、非法拆解污染环境、无照经营等行为。

The Ministry of Industry and Information Technology also required enterprises to strengthen their primary responsibility, use digital technology to enhance monitoring of power battery flow, and guide industry associations, key enterprises, and research institutions to cooperate in exploring advanced technical equipment and new business models. 工信部还要求压实企业主体责任，运用数字化技术加强动力电池流向监测，并引导行业协会、重点企业和研究机构合作，探索先进技术装备和新型商业模式。

哈啰明确顺风车高速费规则

Hello Inc. yesterday released a guide for carpooling highway toll rules. The core rule is that the method of bearing highway tolls is subject to the passenger's remarks, and the driver's acceptance of the order indicates acceptance of this method. 哈啰昨日发布顺风车高速费规则指南，核心规则是高速费承担方式以乘客备注为准，车主接单即表示接受该承担方式。

  • If the passenger chooses "willing to negotiate fees," the agreed-upon result via the platform chat box and platform phone call shall prevail; the driver should complete the negotiation before accepting the order or within 2 hours after accepting the order, and before the passenger gets in the car. If overdue, highway tolls will not be charged by default; 乘客如果选择「愿意协商费用」，双方以平台聊天框和平台电话协商一致的结果为准；车主应在接单前或接单后 2 小时内、且乘客上车前完成协商，逾期默认不收取高速费；
  • If the passenger chooses "I will bear all costs," they shall solely bear the highway toll fees corresponding to the start and end points of their journey; 乘客如果选择「我承担全部费用」，则独自承担其行程起终点对应高速路段费用；
  • If "I will not bear the cost" is chosen, the passenger does not bear the highway toll, and the driver can decide whether to take the highway. 若选择「我不承担费用」，乘客不承担高速费，车主可以决定是否走高速。

The rules also list violation scenarios, including drivers charging passengers who have refused to bear costs, repeatedly charging highway tolls, charging tolls when none were incurred, charging tolls inconsistent with the actual journey, and passengers refusing to pay after reaching an agreement. 规则还列出违规场景，包括车主向已拒绝承担费用的乘客收费、重复收取高速费、未产生高速费却收费、收取与实际行程不符的高速费，以及协商一致后乘客拒不支付。

For violations, drivers who overcharge must refund the excess amount and may have their growth value and trust score deducted, be banned for 3 to 7 days, or permanently banned for severe cases; passengers who fail to pay highway tolls as agreed will not be able to continue booking rides until payment is completed. Hello Inc. also reminds that highway tolls should be paid through the carpooling platform, not via offline transfers. 违规处理上，车主多收费用需退回，并可能被扣成长值、信任分，封禁 3 至 7 天，严重者永久封禁；乘客未按约支付高速费，将在支付完成前不能继续叫车。哈啰同时提醒，高速费应通过顺风车平台支付，不要线下转账。

💡 莫言：AI 创作本质是二手货，取代不了作家的原创能力

According to an interview with Xinhua News Agency, Mo Yan recently stated when discussing AI and literary creation that he believes AI cannot replace a writer's creation. 据新华社采访，莫言近日谈到 AI 与文学创作时表示，他认为 AI 无法取代作家的创作。

I believe that a writer's most valuable asset is originality—the ability to write novels or poems that they themselves have not written and others have not written, to create typical character images that have never appeared in others' works. This is the reason and value for a writer's existence. 我觉得作家最宝贵的就是原创能力，就是写自己没有写过的、别人也没有写过的小说或者诗歌，创造在别人的作品里面从来没有出现过的、典型的人物形象，这才是一个作家能够存在的理由和价值。

Mo Yan further analyzed that current AI-generated content does not possess originality. 莫言进一步分析，当下 AI 生成内容并不具备原创能力。

AI is fed with content written by generations of writers. Based on a massive volume of works, it can recombine them to produce creations that seem as if no one has ever seen them before. The program mentioned that the product's outer packaging prominently stated "100% Juice" and "NFC Not From Concentrate," but the first two ingredients in the list were water and concentrated juice, indicating that the label information did not match the actual ingredients. 据界面新闻报道，央视新闻曝光果汁标签乱象后，好想来旗下一款系列果汁饮料引发关注。节目提到，该产品外包装显著标注「100% 果汁」和「NFC 非浓缩还原」，但配料表前两位为水和浓缩果汁，标签信息与实际成分不符。

The PR representative of Wanchen Group, to which Haoxianglai belongs, responded that the relevant products have label flaws, which consumers might interpret as pure NFC juice. The company has already delisted the products and will redesign the packaging in the future to avoid such ambiguities. The report also listed previous cases involving Beibingyang, Master Kong, and others that sparked controversy over juice content and packaging labels. 好想来所属万辰集团公关负责人回应称，相关产品涉及标签瑕疵，消费者可能理解为纯 NFC 果汁，公司已先将产品下架，未来会重新制作包装，避免相关歧义。报道还列举了北冰洋、康师傅等此前因果汁含量和包装标识引发争议的案例。

买书不读指南｜《大厂病：从个体困境到组织表演》

Xi Fanjun, author of 'Big Tech Sickness,' has over ten years of experience working in large technology companies, having long engaged in strategic communication and technology governance, and now focuses on organizational structure and public narrative research. 《大厂病》作者席凡君拥有十余年大型科技企业从业经历，长期从事战略传播与技术治理工作，现专注组织结构与公共叙事研究。

This book approaches the topic from a sociological perspective, drawing on theoretical resources from Weber, Foucault, Bauman, Bourdieu, and others, to systematically dissect the structural roots behind contemporary workplace phenomena such as 'involution,' 'lying flat,' and the '35-year-old crisis'. 本书以社会学视角切入，援引韦伯、福柯、鲍曼、布尔迪厄等理论资源，系统解剖「内卷」「躺平」「35 岁危机」等当代职场现象背后的结构性根源。

The book's core argument is that individual anxiety and burnout do not stem from insufficient effort, but are the inevitable products of the alienation of modern organizational structures. Xi Fanjun extracts a series of original concepts such as 'liquid person,' 'safe person,' 'performative governance,' and 'the cage of language,' dissecting how big tech companies reshape human behavior and hollow out people's sense of meaning through processes, performance, and rhetoric. 全书核心论点在于：个体的焦虑与倦怠，并非源于努力不足，而是现代组织结构异化的必然产物。席凡君提炼出「液态人」「安全人」「表演性治理」「语言的牢笼」等一系列原创概念，逐一拆解大厂如何以流程、绩效与话术重塑人的行为、掏空人的意义感。

The book points out that when companies evolve into 'self-sustaining systems,' middle managers become 'loyal hostages,' and senior employees face a higher risk of marginalization—'correct process, no one survives' is the core metaphor running through the entire book. 书中指出，当公司进化为「自我维持的系统」，中层管理者沦为「忠诚人质」，资深员工反而面临更高的边缘化风险——「流程正确，无人生还」是贯穿全书的核心隐喻。
