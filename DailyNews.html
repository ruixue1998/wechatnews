曝折叠屏 iPhone 采用单挖孔外屏，支持指纹识别

According to Bloomberg, Apple's first foldable iPhone will feature an internal screen close to the size of an iPad mini, and will introduce an iPad-like interface layout on the iPhone for the first time, including a sidebar and dual-app side-by-side display functionality, with a planned release in the fall of this year. 据彭博社报道，苹果首款折叠屏 iPhone 将配备接近 iPad mini 尺寸的内屏，并首次在 iPhone 上引入类似 iPad 的界面布局，包括侧边栏与双应用并排显示功能，计划在今年秋季发布。

In terms of design, the new device will feature a single punch-hole front camera , instead of the current Dynamic Island form. As the front panel thickness is insufficient to accommodate Face ID, Apple will switch to a side-mounted Touch ID. A similar punch-hole solution will also be used in the touch-enabled MacBook Pro to be released later this year. 外观设计方面，新机将采用 单挖孔前摄 ，而非目前的灵动岛形态。由于前面板厚度不足以容纳 Face ID，苹果将改用侧边 Touch ID。类似的挖孔方案也将用于今年稍晚推出的触控版 MacBook Pro。

The report indicates that the new device's external screen size will be similar to a smaller iPhone, while the internal screen will adopt a "wider aspect ratio" to address the common issue of "internal screens being too narrow" in current foldable phones. Apple is also using new display technology to reduce creases, but they cannot be completely eliminated. 报道指出，新机外屏尺寸接近小尺寸 iPhone，内屏采用「更宽的屏幕比例」，旨在改善当前折叠屏手机普遍存在的「内屏过窄」问题。苹果同时采用新型显示技术以减轻折痕，但折痕仍无法完全消除。

It is worth noting that although the foldable iPhone can run two apps side-by-side in the unfolded state, it still cannot open multiple windows like an iPad, nor can it directly run existing iPad apps. 值得注意的是，尽管折叠屏 iPhone 可在展开状态下并排运行两个应用，但仍无法像 iPad 那样开启多窗口，也无法直接运行现有 iPad 应用。

In terms of the imaging system, the foldable iPhone has two rear cameras, one less than the current iPhone Pro; the inner screen's front camera was tested with an under-display solution, but it was abandoned due to poor image quality. 影像系统方面，折叠屏 iPhone 后置两颗摄像头，比当前的 iPhone Pro 少一颗；内屏前摄曾测试过屏下方案，但因画质不佳被放弃。

The report states that durability is Apple's main selling point this time. Apple hopes to improve the opening and closing lifespan of the hinge and screen to address the common reliability issues of foldable devices. 报道称，耐用性是苹果本次的主打卖点。苹果希望提升铰链与屏幕的开合寿命，以解决折叠设备普遍存在的可靠性问题。

In terms of price, the new device is expected to be priced at around US$2,000 (approximately RMB 14,000). 价格方面，新机预计定价约 2000 美元（约合人民币 1.4 万元）。

英伟达计划 5 年内投 260 亿美元开发开放权重 AI 模型

According to Wired, Nvidia plans to invest $26 billion over the next five years to develop open-weight AI models and build a complete system covering model research and development, computing infrastructure, talent, and ecosystem. 据《连线》报道，英伟达计划在未来 5 年内投入 260 亿美元，用于开发开放权重 AI 模型，并构建覆盖模型研发、算力基础设施、人才与生态的完整体系。

The report points out that Nvidia's chosen path is not fully open source, but rather an "open-weight" strategy, meaning model parameters are disclosed, but full adherence to open-source protocols is not required. This model sits between OpenAI's closed system and Meta Llama's fully open source approach, better aligning with enterprises' needs for transparency and customizability. 报道指出，英伟达选择的路线并非完全开源，而是采用「开放权重」策略，即公开模型参数，但不必完全遵循开源协议。这一模式介于 OpenAI 的封闭体系与 Meta Llama 的完全开源之间，更贴合企业对透明度与可定制性的需求。

Regarding specific progress, Nvidia has released its strongest open-weight model to date, Nemotron 3 Super, with 128 billion parameters, claiming to surpass OpenAI's GPT-OSS in multiple benchmark tests. The company also revealed that it has completed pre-training a 550 billion-parameter ultra-large model to validate the limits of its next-generation hardware architecture. 在具体进展上，英伟达已发布其迄今最强的开放权重模型 Nemotron 3 Super，参数规模达 1280 亿，并在多个基准测试中声称超越 OpenAI 的 GPT-OSS。公司还透露已完成一个 5500 亿参数超大模型的预训练，用于验证下一代硬件架构的极限。

In contrast, the core models of OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google remain closed-source, offering only cloud access; Meta has also hinted at potentially tightening its open-source strategy in the future. 相比之下，OpenAI、Anthropic 与 Google 的核心模型仍保持闭源，仅提供云端访问；Meta 也暗示未来可能收紧开源策略。
