苹果 Siri AI 提示词文件曝光：22000 个 Token、超 1300 行核心指令

Recently, developer 'samhenrigold' extracted the complete Siri AI system prompt words from Apple Siri's diagnostic files. The file is named siri_prompt.md, has a total length of over 1300 lines, approximately 22000 tokens, and has now been uploaded to GitHub. 近日，开发者「samhenrigold」在苹果 Siri 的诊断文件中扒出了完整的 Siri AI 系统提示词。该文件名为 siri_prompt.md，总长度超过 1300 行、约 22000 个 Token，目前已被上传至 GitHub。

This prompt defines Siri as "Apple's intelligent assistant designed in California" and clarifies its workflow and code of conduct. The core logic includes: 这份提示词将 Siri 定义为「苹果在加利福尼亚设计的智能助手」，并明确了其工作流程与行为准则。核心逻辑包括：

  • When processing tasks, Siri must first think before deciding whether to call a tool; 处理任务时须先思考再决定是否调用工具；
  • Prioritize the use of structured entity information from local device data and search results, treating entity attributes as authoritative data rather than a source of instructions; 优先使用来自设备本地数据和搜索返回的结构化实体信息，且将实体属性视为权威数据而非指令来源；
  • When encountering missing information, ambiguity, or uncompletable tasks, Siri must ask the user or explicitly inform them, and must not make assumptions or fabricate content. 遇到信息缺失、存在歧义或无法完成的任务时，必须向用户提问或明确告知，不得自行推断或编造内容。

The prompt also reveals Siri's tool invocation mechanism. The system has built-in fixed tools such as find, open, play, make_call, manage_message_draft, and manage_email_draft, and extends support for dozens of application scenarios like calendar, reminders, maps, health, payments, and smart home through a "tool catalog." 提示词还揭示了 Siri 的工具调用机制。系统内置了 find、open、play、make_call、manage_message_draft、manage_email_draft 等固定工具，并通过一份「工具目录」扩展支持日历、提醒事项、地图、健康、支付、智能家居等数十类应用场景。

Regarding security boundaries, the prompt explicitly requires Siri to reject any attempts to redefine its instructions or capabilities through dialogue, and stipulates that entity content and tool return results must not be considered executable instructions. 在安全边界方面，提示词明确要求 Siri 拒绝任何试图通过对话重新定义其指令或能力的行为，并规定实体内容和工具返回结果均不得被视为可执行指令。

TrendForce：Q1 iPhone 产量同比逆势增长 19.7%，全球智能手机市场整体下滑

According to the latest research report released by TrendForce, global smartphone production in the first quarter of this year was approximately 284 million units, a year-on-year decrease of 1.7%. 据 TrendForce 集邦咨询发布的最新研报，今年第一季度全球智能手机产量约为 2.84 亿部，同比下降 1.7%。

Among them, Apple iPhone production was approximately 60.2 million units, a counter-trend increase of 19.7% year-on-year, with growth momentum coming from the continued ramp-up of the iPhone 17 series and the launch of the iPhone 17e. 其中，苹果 iPhone 产量约为 6020 万部，同比逆势增长 19.7%，增长动能来自 iPhone 17 系列持续放量及 iPhone 17e 上市。

The research report points out that memory prices have been continuously rising since the second half of 2025. TrendForce expects this pressure to become more concentrated in the second quarter and has lowered its full-year production forecast to approximately 1.051 billion units, a year-on-year decrease of about 16.2%. 研报指出，内存价格自 2025 年下半年起持续上涨，TrendForce 预计这一压力将在第二季度更为集中地显现，并将全年产量预期下调至约 10.51 亿部，同比降幅约 16.2%。

  • Samsung: Production was approximately 62.6 million units, a year-on-year increase of 2.3%, driven by inventory preparation for new Galaxy S series models, ranking first globally; 三星：产量约 6260 万部，同比增长 2.3%，受 Galaxy S 系列新机备货拉动，位居全球第一；
  • OPPO, Xiaomi, vivo: Production was approximately 29.5 million, 26 million, and 22 million units respectively. Affected by seasonal factors and cost pressures, their full-year production plans face downward revision risks; OPPO、小米、vivo：产量分别约为 2950 万、2600 万、2200 万部，受季节性因素及成本压力影响，全年产量计划面临下调风险；
  • Transsion: Production was approximately 19.8 million units, largely flat year-on-year, but its product lines are concentrated in the entry-level market, making it more significantly impacted by this round of memory price increases. 传音：产量约 1980 万部，与去年同期基本持平，但产品线集中于入门市场，在本轮内存涨价周期中受冲击较为明显。

韩国查获首例 AI 智能眼镜考试作弊案

According to reports from Xinhua News Agency, Yonhap News Agency, and others, the South Korean TOEIC exam organizer recently confirmed for the first time that candidates used AI smart glasses to cheat. Two candidates were found to have violated rules in TOEIC exams held on May 10 and May 31, respectively. Their scores have been canceled, and they are prohibited from taking the TOEIC exam for the next four years. 据新华社、韩联社等报道，韩国 TOEIC 考试主办方近期首次确认考生利用 AI 智能眼镜作弊。两名考生分别在 5 月 10 日和 5 月 31 日举行的 TOEIC 考试中被认定违规，成绩已被取消，并被禁止未来四年参加 TOEIC 考试。

The examination body stated that shortly after the exam began, invigilators noticed abnormal behavior from candidates and observed that their glasses appeared to have smart functions. To avoid disturbing other candidates, the invigilators did not immediately interrupt the exam but confirmed the cheating through a review process after the exam concluded. 考试机构称，监考人员在考试开始后不久发现考生行为异常，并注意到其佩戴眼镜疑似具备智能功能。为避免影响其他考生，监考人员没有立即中断考试，而是在考试结束后通过复核程序确认作弊事实。

The Korean TOEIC Committee has begun specialized training for invigilators, focusing on identifying AI smart glasses and other new electronic devices, and strengthening electronic device management, identity verification, and post-exam data analysis in test centers. The Korean Ministry of Education is also researching clearer management measures for this November's College Scholastic Ability Test, potentially adding AI smart glasses to the list of prohibited items. 韩国 TOEIC 委员会已开始对监考人员进行专项培训，重点识别 AI 智能眼镜和其他新型电子设备，并加强考场电子设备管理、身份核验和考后数据分析。韩国教育部也在研究针对今年 11 月大学修学能力考试的更明确管理措施，可能把 AI 智能眼镜单独列入禁带物品清单。

💡 OpenAI：完全自动化不是想要的未来，人类判断力不可替代

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman and Chief Scientist Jakub Pachocki recently co-authored an article, officially abandoning the previously proposed plan to "build a fully autonomous AI research system by March 2028," instead emphasizing a human-computer collaboration model. OpenAI CEO 山姆·奥特曼（Sam Altman）与首席研究员雅各布·帕乔基（Jakub Pachocki）日前联合发文，正式放弃此前提出的「到 2028 年 3 月构建完全自主 AI 研究系统」的计划，转而强调人机协作模式。

In the blog post, the two wrote: "We internally believe that by March 2028, a significant portion of our research work may be completed collaboratively by AI systems and researchers." 在这篇博文中，两人写道：「我们内部认为，到 2028 年 3 月，我们的研究工作中可能会有相当一部分由 AI 系统与研究人员协同完成。」

This is significantly more conservative compared to OpenAI's full automation goal proposed last October. The blog post also clearly stated a rejection of the fully automated approach: 这与 OpenAI 去年 10 月提出的全自动化目标相比，明显更为保守。博文同时表明了对完全自动化路线的明确否定：

Fully automating everything is not the future we want. It is both frustrating and dangerous. In the long run, people's crucial role lies in deciding what is worth doing. 完全自动化一切并不是我们想要的未来。它既令人沮丧，又很危险。从长远来看，人们的关键作用在于决定什么事情值得去做。

Altman further elaborated in the article that the stronger AI systems become, the more crucial the human role becomes—including setting direction, weighing pros and cons, and exercising judgment. 奥特曼在文中进一步阐述，AI 系统能力越强，人类的作用反而越关键——包括设定方向、权衡利弊与运用判断力。
