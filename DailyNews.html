OpenAI 发布 GPT-5.5 Instant

Early this morning, OpenAI officially released GPT-5.5 Instant, making it the default model for ChatGPT, replacing the previous GPT-5.3 Instant. 今天凌晨，OpenAI 正式发布 GPT-5.5 Instant，将其设为 ChatGPT 的默认模型，取代此前的 GPT-5.3 Instant。

This update focuses on three directions: accuracy, response style, and personalization capabilities. 此次更新聚焦三个方向：准确性、回复风格和个性化能力。

  • In terms of accuracy, GPT-5.5 Instant's hallucination rate in high-risk areas such as healthcare, law, and finance has decreased by 52.5% compared to the previous version, and the error rate in incorrect conversations has reduced by 37.3%; 在准确性方面，GPT-5.5 Instant 在医疗、法律、金融等高风险领域的幻觉率较上一版本下降 52.5%，错误对话的错误率减少 37.3%；
  • Math and science test scores have also improved, with AIME 2025 scores rising from 65.4 to 81.2, GPQA from 78.5 to 85.6, MMMU-Pro from 69.2 to 76, and CharXiv from 75 to 81.6; 数学与科学测试成绩同步提升，AIME 2025 得分从 65.4 升至 81.2，GPQA 从 78.5 升至 85.6，MMMU-Pro 从 69.2 升至 76，CharXiv 从 75 升至 81.6；
  • In terms of response style, the new model is more concise, reducing unnecessary formatting, emojis, and follow-up questions; 在回复风格上，新模型更为简洁，减少了不必要的格式堆砌、表情符号和追问；
  • Regarding personalization capabilities, Plus and Pro users can have the model retrieve historical conversations, uploaded files, and associated Gmail content to receive answers that are more tailored to their personal situations. 个性化能力方面，Plus 和 Pro 用户可让模型调取历史对话、上传文件及关联的 Gmail 内容，以获得更贴合个人情况的回答。

At the same time, the "Memory sources" feature has been launched. When an answer uses personal background information, users can view which historical conversations or saved memory entries were specifically accessed, and can delete or correct them at any time. Users who do not wish to be recorded can choose temporary conversation mode. 与此同时，「记忆来源（Memory sources）」功能同步上线，当回答用到个人背景信息时，用户可查看具体调用了哪些历史对话或已保存的记忆条目，并可随时删除或修正。不想被记录的用户可选择临时对话模式。

iPhone 17 包揽 2026 年 Q1 全球最畅销智能手机前三

According to the latest "Global Handset Model Sales Tracker" report released yesterday by Counterpoint Research, Apple iPhone 17 became the world's best-selling smartphone in Q1 2026, accounting for 6% of global shipments; the top ten models collectively contributed 25% of global shipments, setting a record for the highest concentration in a single quarter. 据 Counterpoint Research 昨日发布的最新《全球手机型号销售追踪报告》，苹果 iPhone 17 成为 2026 年第一季度全球最畅销智能手机，占全球出货量的 6%；前十名机型合计贡献了全球出货量的 25%，创下一季度最高集中度纪录。

The iPhone 17 series took the top three spots, while Samsung's Galaxy A series occupied five positions in the top ten, and Xiaomi Redmi A5 ranked tenth. Data shows that the iPhone 17 achieved double-digit year-on-year growth in key markets such as China and the United States this quarter, and even tripled its growth in South Korea. iPhone 17 系列包揽前三名，三星 Galaxy A 系列则在前十中占据五席，小米 Redmi A5 位列第十。数据显示，iPhone 17 本季度在中国、美国等关键市场实现了两位数的同比增长，在韩国更实现了三倍增长。

As for Samsung, the Galaxy A series covers different price segments with multiple models, among which the Galaxy A07 4G was the best-selling Android smartphone this quarter, performing exceptionally well in emerging regions such as the Middle East, Africa, and Latin America; the flagship model Galaxy S26 Ultra narrowly missed the top ten, but its initial sales have already surpassed its predecessor. 三星方面，Galaxy A 系列以多款机型覆盖不同价位段，其中 Galaxy A07 4G 是本季度最畅销的安卓智能手机，在中东、非洲和拉丁美洲等新兴地区销量表现突出；旗舰机型 Galaxy S26 Ultra 以微弱差距未能进入前十，但初期销量已超越前代机型。

Karn Chauhan, a senior analyst at Counterpoint, pointed out that the share of the top ten models in global sales is expected to further expand this year. Persistent memory shortages are driving up component costs, putting greater pressure on Android manufacturers in the mass market. The market downturn is expected to have a more significant impact on the mid-to-low price segments, while the share of high-end models continues to grow. Counterpoint 高级分析师 Karn Chauhan 指出，前十名机型在全球销量中的份额预计将在今年进一步扩大。持续的内存短缺推高零部件成本，给安卓厂商在大众市场带来更大压力，市场下滑预计对中低价位段影响更为显著，高端机型份额则持续增长。

曝苹果寻求英特尔、三星代工芯片

According to Bloomberg, Apple is exploring bringing in Intel and Samsung to manufacture its main processors, seeking alternatives to TSMC. 据彭博社报道，苹果正在研究引入英特尔和三星代工其主要处理器，以在台积电之外寻求备选。

Reporter Mark Gurman, citing informed sources, stated that Apple has held early-stage discussions with Intel regarding wafer foundry services, and Apple executives have also visited Samsung's advanced chip factory under construction in Texas. The cooperation is currently still in the exploratory phase. 记者 Mark Gurman 援引知情人士表示，苹果已与英特尔就晶圆代工服务进行早期阶段磋商，同时有苹果高管赴访三星位于德克萨斯州、尚在建设中的先进芯片工厂，目前合作仍处于探索阶段。

Supply shortages are the direct cause of these discussions. During the previous Q2 2026 earnings call, Apple CEO Tim Cook stated that the shortage of chips required for iPhones and Macs is constraining the company's growth, and "supply chain flexibility is below normal levels." 供应短缺是此次探讨的直接诱因。在此前的 2026 财年第二季度业绩电话会议上，苹果 CEO Tim Cook 表示，iPhone 和 Mac 所需芯片的短缺正在制约公司增长，「供应链的灵活性低于正常水平」。

Apple is currently collaborating with TSMC to expand local production capacity in Phoenix, Arizona, and is expected to receive 100 million chips from there this year, but this only covers a small portion of Apple's annual device shipments. Cook has previously warned multiple times about the risks of over-reliance on a single region for supply. 苹果目前正与台积电合作，在亚利桑那州凤凰城扩大本土产能，今年预计将从该地获得 1 亿颗芯片，但仅覆盖苹果年度设备出货量的一小部分。库克此前曾多次提示过度依赖单一地区供应的风险。

郭明錤：OpenAI 首款 AI 手机提速，联发科定制芯片有望明年上半年量产

Yesterday, Tianfeng International Securities analyst Ming-Chi Kuo updated his forecast for OpenAI's first AI phone, stating that the device's mass production schedule has been significantly advanced, and it is expected to enter the mass production phase as early as the first half of next year. 昨天，天风国际证券分析师郭明錤更新了对 OpenAI 首款 AI 手机的预测，称该设备的量产时间表已大幅提前，预计最早将于明年上半年进入量产阶段。

Regarding chip suppliers, MediaTek has currently become the strongest candidate, while Qualcomm was also previously considered. Ming-Chi Kuo revealed that the OpenAI phone will be equipped with a customized MediaTek chip, based on a modified Dimensity 9600, using TSMC's next-generation N2P process, and is expected to officially debut in the second half of this year. 在芯片供应商方面，联发科目前已成为最有力的候选方，高通此前也曾被列为考虑对象。郭明錤透露，OpenAI 手机将搭载联发科定制版芯片，基于天玑 9600 改款，采用台积电下一代 N2P 制程，预计于今年下半年正式亮相。

In terms of chip design, this customized chip will focus on AI capabilities rather than solely pursuing performance benchmarks. Among them, the ISP (Image Signal Processor) is listed as a "key selling point," enhancing the HDR processing pipeline and improving the device's visual understanding of its surroundings to fit the usage scenarios of AI phones that continuously perceive and analyze the real world. 在芯片设计上，该定制芯片将以 AI 能力为核心，而非单纯追求性能跑分。其中，ISP（图像信号处理器）被列为「主打卖点」，增强 HDR 处理管线，提升设备对周围环境的视觉理解能力，以契合 AI 手机持续感知与分析现实世界的使用场景。

Additionally, the chip is equipped with a dual NPU architecture for processing different types of AI tasks and supports LPDDR6 memory and UFS 5.0 storage. Ming-Chi Kuo estimates that if the project progresses smoothly, the combined shipments of this device in the coming year and 2028 are expected to reach approximately 30 million units. 此外，芯片还配备双 NPU 架构，用于处理不同类型的 AI 任务，并支持 LPDDR6 内存与 UFS 5.0 存储。郭明錤预计，若项目进展顺利，该设备明年与 2028 年的合计出货量有望达到约 3000 万部。
