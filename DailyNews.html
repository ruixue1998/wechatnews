曝苹果寻求收购 AI 芯片企业，补强 AI 服务器算力

According to The Information, Apple is seeking to acquire AI chip companies to enhance its own data centers' ability to handle high-intensity AI workloads and reduce its reliance on Nvidia chips. 据《The Information》报道，苹果正在寻求收购 AI 芯片企业，以提升自有数据中心处理高强度 AI 工作负载的能力，并降低对英伟达芯片的依赖。

Currently, Apple's data centers use self-developed M2 Ultra chips to handle some AI tasks; for the demand of running larger models like Google Gemini, it relies on Nvidia chips in Google Cloud. 目前，苹果数据中心使用自研 M2 Ultra 芯片处理部分 AI 任务；对于运行 Google Gemini 等更大型模型的需求，则需要依托 Google 云中的英伟达芯片。

Sources say that Apple's next-generation AI server chip, codenamed Baltra, was originally planned for release this year, but the project has been delayed, with the specific reasons not yet disclosed. 知情人士称，苹果内部代号为 Baltra 的下一代 AI 服务器芯片原计划于今年推出，但项目已经推迟，具体原因尚未披露。

The report mentioned that Apple has recently discussed potential deals with bankers and has also contacted some semiconductor startups to understand their willingness to be acquired. 报道提到，苹果近期已与银行家讨论潜在交易，也接触过部分半导体初创企业，了解其被收购意愿。

Apple is also advancing AI server chip development through other means. In 2024, Apple was reported to be collaborating with Broadcom to develop AI server chips; Broadcom stated in a securities filing last week that the two parties would extend their long-term technology cooperation until 2031, but did not disclose the specific content of the cooperation. 苹果还在通过其他方式推进 AI 服务器芯片研发。2024 年，苹果曾被报道与博通合作开发 AI 服务器芯片；博通上周在证券文件中表示，双方将把长期技术合作延续至 2031 年，但没有披露具体合作内容。

Codex 团队回应 GPT-5.6 误删文件风险：建议谨慎使用完全访问模式

Yesterday, Tibo, technical manager of the Codex team, posted that the team has investigated multiple reports of GPT-5.6 accidentally deleting files. Such issues are most common when users enable full access mode, do not enable sandbox protection, and do not use automatic review features. 昨天，Codex 团队技术经理 Tibo 发文称，团队已调查多起 GPT-5.6 意外删除文件的报告。相关问题最常见于用户开启完全访问模式、未启用沙箱保护，且没有使用自动审查功能的情况。

Tibo stated that the model sometimes attempts to overwrite the HOME environment variable to set up a temporary directory; if the model makes an incorrect judgment as a result, it may accidentally delete the user's HOME directory. In response, the Codex team plans to update developer messages to guide more users to use safer permission modes and add additional harness protection measures. Tibo 表示，模型有时会尝试覆盖 HOME 环境变量，以便设置临时目录；如果模型因此发生误判，就可能误删用户的 HOME 目录。对此，Codex 团队计划更新开发者消息，引导更多用户使用更安全的权限模式，并增加额外的 harness 保护措施。

He also stated that even if users run the model in full access mode without sandbox protection or automatic review, the system should not delete the user's home directory. The team will release a detailed post-mortem analysis report in the coming days, further explaining the cause of the incident and measures to mitigate risks. 他同时表示，即使用户在没有沙箱保护或自动审查的完全访问模式下运行模型，系统也不应出现删除用户主目录的行为。团队将在未来几天发布详细的事后分析报告，进一步说明事件原因以及降低风险的措施。

华为撤回「世界」商标申请

The 1991st trademark announcement issued by the National Intellectual Property Administration shows that Huawei's "世界" (World) trademark application has been approved for withdrawal. 国家知识产权局发布的第 1991 期商标公告显示，华为「世界」商标申请已获准撤回。

The trademark is approved for use in Class 12, covering core automotive and vehicle-related goods such as electric vehicles, hybrid vehicles, vehicle interiors, and photographic drones. It entered the preliminary examination announcement phase on May 13, 2026, with the opposition period originally planned to last until August 13, 2026. 该商标核定使用在第 12 类，涵盖电动汽车、混合动力汽车、运载工具内饰、摄影无人机等核心汽车及运载工具相关商品，于 2026 年 5 月 13 日进入初审公告阶段，异议期原计划持续至 2026 年 8 月 13 日。

On June 23 this year, Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. submitted a trademark withdrawal application to the National Intellectual Property Administration. Currently, the announcement only indicates that the applicant has withdrawn the trademark application, without disclosing the specific reasons for the withdrawal. 今年 6 月 23 日，华为公司向国家知识产权局提交了商标撤回申请。目前，公告内容仅表明申请人撤回了该商标申请，未披露具体撤回原因。

Previously, this trademark sparked widespread discussion during the preliminary examination and announcement phase. Some netizens questioned whether the word 'world' could be registered as a trademark by a single enterprise, while others joked that the World Cup would be forced to change its name. 此前，这枚商标在初审公告阶段曾引发广泛讨论，有网友质疑「世界」一词是否可以被单一企业注册为商标，也有网友调侃称世界杯将被迫改名。

In addition to 'World', Huawei has also applied for over 170 trademarks containing the character '界' (jie), including Tianjie (Heavenly Realm), Xianjie (Immortal Realm), Zunjie (Venerable Realm), Longjie (Dragon Realm), Xinjie (New Realm), and Jingjie (Capital Realm). 除「世界」外，华为还申请了包括天界、仙界、尊界、龙界、新界、京界等在内的 170 多个「界」字商标。

丰田、宝马联合测试可再生汽油，二氧化碳排放有望减少 70%

According to InsideEVs, Toyota and BMW launched a pilot project for renewable gasoline (Renewable Gas) in Spain yesterday. The project, a collaboration between Toyota, BMW, Spanish energy company Repsol, and Bosch, aims to explore emission reduction pathways beyond electrification. 据 InsideEVs 报道，丰田与 BMW 昨日起在西班牙启动了一项可再生汽油（Renewable Gas）的实测项目。该项目由丰田、BMW、西班牙能源公司 Repsol 以及博世（Bosch）合作开展，旨在探索电动化之外的减排路径。

The pilot project involves approximately 20 vehicles and will run for 6 months. The test vehicles will exclusively use Nexa 95 renewable fuel produced by Repsol, while Bosch will provide the digital fuel tracking system. Repsol stated that Nexa 95 fuel is made from renewable raw materials such as organic waste, and its CO2 emissions can be reduced by over 70% compared to traditional gasoline. 此次实测规模约为 20 辆汽车，测试周期为 6 个月。测试车辆将完全使用 Repsol 生产的 Nexa 95 可再生燃料，博世则负责提供数字燃料跟踪系统。Repsol 方面表示，Nexa 95 燃料由有机废料等可再生原料制成，相比传统汽油，其二氧化碳排放量可降低 70% 以上。

Unlike e-fuels, which require CO2 capture and synthesis with hydrogen, renewable fuels are primarily derived from organic waste. Repsol pointed out that the amount of CO2 released by this fuel is essentially equal to the amount absorbed by its raw materials during their growth cycle. 与需要捕获二氧化碳并结合氢气合成的电子燃料（e-fuels）不同，可再生燃料主要源自有机废物。Repsol 指出，这种燃料释放的二氧化碳量与其原材料在生长周期内吸收的量基本相等。

Furthermore, renewable fuels can serve as a "direct substitute" for existing internal combustion engine vehicles, requiring no extensive modifications to vehicle hardware. 此外，可再生燃料可作为现有内燃机车辆的「直接替代品」，无需对车辆硬件进行大规模改装。

共享单车集体涨价，美团青桔哈啰起步价迈入 2 元时代

According to Yicai, Meituan Bike, Didi Qingju, and Hello Bike recently adjusted their billing rules in Beijing and several other cities. The three major shared bike platforms collectively adopted a combined strategy of "raising the starting price and extending the basic riding duration" for this price adjustment. 据第一财经报道，美团单车、滴滴青桔、哈啰单车于近期在北京市等多个城市上调计费规则。三大共享单车平台此次集体调价普遍采取了「提高起步定价、拉长基础骑行时长」的组合策略。

Specific rules show that Meituan Bike's starting price in the urban six districts for weekdays and weekends has been adjusted to 1.88 yuan, an increase of 0.38 yuan from before, and the initial riding duration has been extended from 30 minutes to 60 minutes; other areas maintain a starting price of 1.5 yuan / 30 minutes. Additionally, Meituan's holiday prices across all regions uniformly implement the 1.88 yuan / 60 minutes standard. 具体规则显示，美团单车在城六区的工作日及周末起步价调整为 1.88 元，较此前上涨 0.38 元，起步骑行时长由 30 分钟延长至 60 分钟；其他区域维持 1.5 元 / 30 分钟的起步价。此外，美团全区域节假日价格统一执行 1 88 元 / 60 分钟标准。

Didi Qingju and Hello Bike's billing standards have also been adjusted simultaneously. Didi Qingju's starting price for weekdays and weekends has been raised to 1.99 yuan, and the initial duration expanded from 30 minutes to 60 minutes. Hello Bike's weekday pricing standard is on par with Qingju, with a starting price of 1.99 yuan and a 60-minute ride. 滴滴青桔与哈啰单车的计费标准也进行了同步调整。滴滴青桔工作日及周末起步价上调至 1.99 元，起步时长由 30 分钟扩容至 60 分钟。哈啰单车的工作日资费标准与青桔持平，起步价同为 1.99 元并可骑行 60 分钟。

In response to this price change, all three platforms have simultaneously launched various riding membership products, including time-limited unlimited ride cards, fixed-quantity ride cards, and monthly unlimited ride cards. The platforms stated that after users activate a membership, the cost per ride can return to pre-increase levels, with some packages even offering better value for money. 针对此次价格变动，三家平台均同步推出了多种骑行会员产品，包括限时不限次卡、定量骑行次卡及月度畅骑卡等。平台方表示，用户开通会员后，单次骑行成本可回落至涨价前水平，部分套餐甚至更具性价比。
