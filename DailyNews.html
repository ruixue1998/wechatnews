GPT Image 2 全量上线，中文渲染能力大升级

OpenAI's image generation model, GPT Image 2, was officially rolled out yesterday to all ChatGPT users, including free accounts. OpenAI 旗下图像生成模型 GPT Image 2 昨日正式全量推送，面向所有 ChatGPT 用户开放，包括免费账号。

GPT Image 2 has made significant breakthroughs in Chinese text rendering capabilities. According to APPSO's testing of the model , GPT Image 2 can accurately reproduce the Song/Kai font typesetting style of elementary school math test papers, including details like fill-in-the-blank underlines and geometric annotations; GPT Image 2 中文文字渲染能力有了极大突破。据 APPSO 对该模型的测试 ，GPT Image 2 已能精准还原小学数学试卷的宋体/楷体排版风格，包括填空题下划线、几何图形标注等细节；

Furthermore, Image 2 can fully replicate the Douyin live streaming interface, including the hierarchical logic of interactive elements such as the comment section, like and share buttons, viewer count, and scrolling marquee; the reproduction of League of Legends team fight scenes also includes details like health bars, skill effects, and mini-map UI frames. 此外，Image 2 能够完整复刻抖音直播界面，包括评论区、点赞与分享按钮、观众人数及跑马灯等交互元素的层级逻辑；对英雄联盟团战画面的还原同样包含血条、技能特效及小地图 UI 框等细节。

At the commercial application level, GPT Image 2 can directly generate finished images for business proposals and e-commerce placements, covering scenarios such as product exploded views, high-end commercial advertisements, movie posters, and comic book panels. Work that previously took 3D modelers or professional designers several days to complete can now generate high-quality prototype references in seconds. 商业应用层面，GPT Image 2 已能直接生成可用于商业提案和电商投放的成品图，涵盖产品分解图、高端商业广告、电影海报及漫画分镜等场景，过去需要 3D 建模师或专业设计师数天完成的工作，现在可在数秒内生成高品质原型参考。

分析师：苹果评估 CEO 更看重多元化

Yesterday, Apple officially announced that Tim Cook will step down as CEO on September 1st, transitioning to Executive Chairman; John Ternus, current Senior Vice President of Hardware Engineering, will officially take over. In response, Tianfeng International Securities analyst Ming-Chi Kuo published an article analyzing this leadership change. 昨天，苹果官宣 Tim Cook 将于 9 月 1 日卸任 CEO，转任执行董事长；现任硬件工程高级副总裁 John Ternus 正式接棒。对此，天风国际证券分析师郭明錤发文，就这一换帅决定进行了分析。

Kuo believes that Ternus's most representative achievement was leading the smooth migration of Mac from x86 to ARM (Apple Silicon). This system-level platform transition encompassed software and hardware integration and the reconstruction of the developer ecosystem. It is precisely this "brain transplant" experience that makes him the right person to lead Apple into the era of on-device AI. 郭明錤认为，Ternus 最具代表性的成就是主导 Mac 从 x86 平稳迁移至 ARM（Apple Silicon）。这场系统级平台转换涵盖软硬件整合与开发者生态重建，正是这段「换脑手术」经验，使他成为带领苹果迈向设备端 AI 时代的合适人选。

Regarding the selection logic, Kuo pointed out that Ternus did not come from the iPhone system, reflecting that the board's evaluation criteria for CEO have become more diverse. He also mentioned that the Asian supply chain circle had previously widely bet on Jeff Williams taking over, and this judgment only changed after his retirement news was announced. 在人选逻辑上，郭明錤指出，Ternus 并非出身 iPhone 体系，反映出董事会对 CEO 的评估标准已趋向多元。他还提到，此前亚洲供应链圈普遍押注 Jeff Williams 接任，直至其退休消息公布后判断才随之改变。

Kuo expects Ternus to continue the supply chain management mechanism established by Tim Cook and deepen technical cooperation with suppliers. 郭明錤预计，Ternus 将延续 Tim Cook 建立的供应链管理机制，并加深与供应商的技术合作。

According to Bloomberg, Ternus is believed to be able to recreate the decisive and tough decision-making style of the Jobs era. As the core head of Apple's hardware business, Ternus has long been deeply involved in the R&D and decision-making of core product lines such as iPhone and Mac, and holds high prestige within Apple. 另据彭博社报道，Ternus 被认为能够重现乔布斯时代那种果断、强硬的决策风格。作为苹果硬件业务的核心负责人，Ternus 长期深度参与 iPhone、Mac 等核心产品线的研发与决策，在苹果内部具有较高威望。

QuestMobile：今年一季度 AI 原生 App 月活破 4.4 亿、腾讯 WorkBuddy 周活大增 72.2%

Yesterday, market research firm QuestMobile released its Q1 2026 AI Application Insights, showing that the overall user scale and stickiness of the domestic AI-native App industry achieved dual-driven growth, with industry competition officially entering the "second half". 昨天，市场调研机构 QuestMobile 发布 2026 年一季度 AI 应用洞察，国内 AI 原生 App 行业整体用户规模与粘性实现双轮驱动增长，行业竞争正式进入「下半场」。

The research report shows that as of March this year, the monthly active user (MAU) scale of AI-native Apps reached 446 million, with Doubao, Qianwen, and DeepSeek ranking in the top three, with MAUs of 345 million, 166 million, and 127 million respectively. 研报显示，截至今年 3 月，AI 原生 App 月活用户规模达 4.46 亿，豆包、千问、DeepSeek 位居前三，月活分别为 3.45 亿、1.66 亿和 1.27 亿。

In the first quarter, the industry's average monthly usage frequency reached 87.1 times, and the average monthly usage duration reached 173.3 minutes, representing year-on-year increases of 55.3% and 41.4% respectively. The industry is shifting from last year's "surging user numbers" to this year's "simultaneous growth in user numbers and user stickiness". 一季度行业月人均使用次数达 87.1 次、月人均使用时长达 173.3 分钟，同比分别提升 55.3% 和 41.4%，行业正从去年的「用户量飙涨」转向今年的「用户量与用户粘性齐涨」。

The report points out that this year's Spring Festival "red packet war" was the main reason for the growth. After the holiday, Doubao's DAU remained at approximately 140 million, while Qianwen and Yuanbao stabilized at approximately 30 million and 9 million respectively. 研报指出，今年春节「红包大战」是增长的主因。节后豆包 DAU 维持在约 1.4 亿，千问和元宝则分别稳定在约 3000 万和 900 万。

At the "Claw" platform ecosystem level, Tencent's WorkBuddy saw its weekly active users increase by 72.2%, surpassing 200,000, leading the market; Alibaba's QoderWork and QwenPaw grew slightly by 10.1% and 8.4% respectively, with both scales remaining below 100,000; 在「龙虾」Claw 平台生态层面，腾讯旗下 WorkBuddy 周活跃用户增幅达 72.2%，规模突破 20 万，领跑市场；阿里旗下 QoderWork 和 QwenPaw 分别小幅增长 10.1% 和 8.4%，规模均不足 10 万；

Zhipu's AutoClaw declined by 14.1%; Alibaba's JVSClaw saw the largest drop, reaching 57.5%, with its scale falling below 20,000. QuestMobile points out that the Claw ecosystem is currently still in the scale expansion phase, network effects are accumulating, and there is still a long exploration period before true value realization. 智谱旗下 AutoClaw 下滑 14.1%；阿里旗下 JVSClaw 降幅最大，达 57.5%，规模跌至不足 2 万。QuestMobile 指出，Claw 生态目前仍处于规模扩张阶段，网络效应正在积累，距离真正的价值兑现仍有较长探索周期。

员工故意删除 AI 训练数据被起诉，北京市检察机关深化知识产权保护

According to 21st Century Business Herald, the Beijing Municipal People's Procuratorate yesterday released the "Beijing Procuratorial Organs Intellectual Property Procuratorial White Paper (2025)". 据 21 世纪经济报道，北京市人民检察院昨日发布《北京市检察机关知识产权检察白皮书（2025 年）》。

Last year, Beijing's procuratorial organs handled a total of 1,195 intellectual property cases, a year-on-year increase of 10.34%.
