Claude Opus 4.7 深夜上线，编程能力大幅跃升

Last night, Anthropic officially released its flagship model, Claude Opus 4.7 . The pricing remains consistent with the previous generation Opus 4.6: $5 per million input tokens and $25 per million output tokens. Developers can access it via claude-opus-4-7. 昨晚， Anthropic 正式发布 Claude Opus 4.7 旗舰模型 ，定价与上一代 Opus 4.6 保持一致：每百万输入 token 5 美元，每百万输出 token 25 美元，开发者可通过 claude-opus-4-7 调用。

According to reports, Opus 4.7 can maintain rigor and consistency in complex, long-running tasks and has the ability to self-verify its output before returning results. In Cursor's internal evaluation, Opus 4.7 achieved a 70% pass rate on CursorBench, compared to 58% for Opus 4.6. 据介绍，Opus 4.7 能够在长时间运行的复杂任务中保持严谨与一致，并具备在回报结果前自我验证输出的能力。Cursor 的内部评测中，Opus 4.7 在 CursorBench 上的通过率达 70%，而 Opus 4.6 为 58%。

In terms of multimodal capabilities, Opus 4.7 supports high-resolution image input with a longest side of up to 2576 pixels (approximately 3.75 million pixels), which is more than 3 times that of previous Claude models, opening up possibilities for fine-grained visual scenarios such as computer-controlled agents and complex chart data extraction. 多模态能力方面，Opus 4.7 支持最长边达 2576 像素（约 375 万像素）的高分辨率图像输入，是此前 Claude 模型的逾 3 倍，为计算机操控智能体、复杂图表数据提取等精细视觉场景打开了空间。

In terms of safety and alignment, Opus 4.7 strengthens the interception of high-risk cybersecurity requests by limiting relevant capabilities during training and incorporating built-in protective layers. To this end, Anthropic has launched a "Cybersecurity Verification Program," which is only open to professionals for compliant use. 在安全与对齐方面，Opus 4.7 通过在训练中限制相关能力并内置防护层，强化了对高风险网络安全请求的拦截。为此，Anthropic 推出了「网络安全核查计划」，仅限专业人员申请合规使用。

Anthropic also admits that although Opus 4.7 has made progress in honesty and resistance to prompt injection attacks, it has slightly regressed in providing harmful advice on controlled substances, and its overall alignment performance is currently still inferior to Mythos Preview. Anthropic 也承认，尽管 Opus 4.7 在诚实性和抗提示注入攻击上有所进步，但在管控物质的危害建议方面略有退步，其整体对齐表现目前仍次于 Mythos Preview。

曝 OLED 版 iPad Air 明年到来

According to ETNews, Apple plans to launch a new generation of iPad Air equipped with an OLED display in the first half of next year. 据 ETNews 报道，苹果计划于明年上半年推出新一代配备 OLED 显示屏的 iPad Air。

To support this plan, Samsung Display is expected to begin mass production of OLED panels specifically for the iPad Air by the end of this year or January next year. Industry sources predict that the new iPad Air will be released in March or May next year. 为配合这一计划，三星显示（Samsung Display）预计将于今年年底或明年 1 月启动 iPad Air 专用 OLED 面板的量产工作。业界预测，新款 iPad Air 的发布时间为明年 3 月或 5 月。

It is worth noting that the OLED in the new iPad Air is not the same high-brightness, two-stack tandem solution found in the iPad Pro. Instead, it features a single-layer emissive stack structure with reduced specifications and cost, combined with low-temperature polycrystalline silicon (LTPS) thin-film transistors (TFT) and a hybrid substrate design. 值得注意的是，新款 iPad Air 所搭载的 OLED 并非 iPad Pro 同款的高亮度双层叠加方案，而是规格与成本均有所下调的单层发光堆叠结构，配合低温多晶硅（LTPS）薄膜晶体管（TFT）及混合基板设计。

The report disclosed specific progress across multiple dimensions: 报告围绕多个维度披露了具体进展：

  • Recycled Materials : Self-developed batteries fully utilize 100% recycled cobalt, all magnets switch to 100% recycled rare earth elements, and self-developed printed circuit boards use 100% recycled gold plating and recycled solder. 再生材料 ：自研电池全面采用 100% 再生钴，所有磁体切换至 100% 再生稀土元素，自研印刷电路板使用 100% 再生金镀层与再生焊锡
  • Packaging : Completed a full transition to 100% fiber-based packaging, completely eliminating plastic from product packaging; over the past five years, accumulated avoidance of over 15,000 tons of plastic, equivalent to approximately 500 million plastic water bottles. 包装 ：完成向 100% 纤维基包装的全面过渡，彻底去除产品包装中的塑料；过去五年累计避免使用超过 15,000 吨塑料，相当于约 5 亿个塑料水瓶
  • Renewable Energy : Direct suppliers purchased over 20 gigawatts of renewable energy last year, generating over 38 billion kilowatt-hours; Apple's own offices, retail stores, and data centers maintain 100% renewable electricity supply. 可再生能源 ：直接供应商去年采购超过 20 吉瓦再生能源，发电量超过 380 亿千瓦时；苹果自身办公室、零售店与数据中心维持 100% 可再生电力供电
  • Water Resources : Apple and its suppliers saved a total of 17 billion gallons of fresh water last year, and by 2025, through contract projects, replenished over half of the total fresh water consumed by global facilities; all eight of its own data centers worldwide have obtained Water Stewardship Alliance standard certification. 水资源 ：苹果及其供应商去年共节约 170 亿加仑淡水，2025 年通过合约项目补给了全球设施所耗淡水总量的一半以上，全球八家自有数据中心均已获得水资源管理联盟标准认证
  • Waste : Waste diversion rate reached 75%, with the supply chain diverting over 600,000 tons of waste from landfills throughout the year, and 400 supplier factories participating in the Zero Waste program; Apple's Fifth Avenue retail store in New York City became the first Apple retail store to achieve TRUE Zero Waste certification. 废弃物 ：废弃物转化率达到 75%，供应链全年共转移超过 60 万吨废弃物使其免于填埋，400 家供应商工厂参与零废弃计划；纽约市第五大道零售店成为苹果首家获得 TRUE 零废弃认证的零售店
  • Carbon Emissions : Overall greenhouse gas emissions decreased by over 60% compared to 2015. 碳排放 ：与 2015 年相比，温室气体排放量整体下降超过 60%

L2 辅助驾驶强制国标开始征求意见：脱手脱眼超时将自动接管并禁用系统

Yesterday, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT) released the draft for approval of the "Safety Requirements for Intelligent Connected Vehicle Combined Driving Assistance Systems," which is the first mandatory national standard in China for L2-level assisted driving, proposed to be officially implemented from January 1, 2027. 工信部昨日发布《智能网联汽车 组合驾驶辅助系统安全要求》报批稿，这是国内首部针对 L2 级辅助驾驶的强制性国标，建议于 2027 年 1 月 1 日起正式实施。

Currently, the adoption rate of L2 systems in new energy vehicles has exceeded 50%, but accidents caused by insufficient system capabilities and driver misuse continue to increase. The core purpose of this national standard is precisely to draw a mandatory red line for this. 目前新能源汽车 L2 系统搭载率已超 50%，但因系统能力不足及驾驶员误用引发的事故持续增多，此次国标的核心目的正是为此划定强制红线。

  • If hands leave the steering wheel, an alarm must be triggered within a maximum of 10 seconds; if eye-gaze deviation is also detected, this is shortened to 5 seconds; 双手离开方向盘，最多 10 秒须报警；若同时检测到视线脱离，缩短至 5 秒；
  • If eyes leave the road, an alarm must be triggered within a maximum of 5 seconds, escalating after another 3 seconds, followed by a mandatory takeover warning within 5 seconds; 眼睛离开路面，最多 5 秒须报警，再过 3 秒升级，之后 5 秒内发出强制接管警告；
  • If the driver continues to be unresponsive, the system will eventually automatically take over the vehicle and pull over; 驾驶员持续不响应，系统最终将自动接管车辆并靠边停车；
  • If an automatic takeover is triggered once, or if there are 3 cumulative hands-off/eyes-off warnings within 30 minutes, assisted driving will be forcibly disabled for at least 30 minutes. 触发一次自动接管、或在 30 分钟内累计 3 次脱手/脱眼警告，辅助驾驶将被强制禁用至少 30 分钟。

Furthermore, the standard explicitly prohibits the system from changing lanes over solid lines, exceeding the current road speed limit, and mandates that vehicles be equipped with Automatic Emergency Braking Systems (AEBS) and Lane Keeping Assist. 此外，标准还明确禁止系统在实线处变道，禁止超过当前道路限速行驶，并要求车辆强制配备自动紧急制动系统（AEBS）和车道保持辅助。

This model only requires a common RGB camera to estimate camera pose and reconstruct the 3D structure of a scene in real-time during video acquisition, providing continuous and stable spatial perception capabilities for applications such as robotics, autonomous driving, and AR glasses. 蚂蚁灵波科技昨日宣布开源流式三维重建模型 LingBot-Map。该模型仅需一个普通 RGB 摄像头，即可在视频采集过程中实时估计相机位姿、重建场景三维结构，为机器人、自动驾驶、AR 眼镜等应用提供连续、稳定的空间感知能力。

LingBot-Map adopts a streaming architecture, simultaneously receiving video frames and outputting localization results and 3D structures in real-time. It supports real-time inference at approximately 20 FPS and can run continuously in long videos exceeding ten thousand frames with virtually no loss in accuracy, balancing the three key metrics of accuracy, speed, and long-term stability. LingBot-Map 采用流式架构，一边接收视频帧、一边实时输出定位结果与三维结构，支持约 20 FPS 的实时推理，并可在超万帧的长视频中连续运行而精度几乎不衰减，兼顾了精度、速度与长时稳定性三项关键指标。

LingBot-Map comprehensively outperforms existing streaming methods on multiple international mainstream evaluation benchmarks. 在多个国际主流评测基准上，LingBot-Map 全面领先现有流式方法。

阶跃星辰发布 StepAudio 2.5 TTS

Step Audio yesterday released its next-generation speech generation model, StepAudio 2.5 TTS, reconstructing the control methods for speech synthesis around three core capabilities: 阶跃星辰昨日发布新一代语音生成模型 StepAudio 2.5 TTS，围绕三项核心能力重构语音合成的控制方式：

  • Global Context Control: Using natural language to set the emotional tone, character state, and scene atmosphere for an entire segment of speech, replacing traditional tag combinations for more unified and precise expression; 全局语境控制：以自然语言为整段语音设定情绪基调、角色状态与场景氛围，取代传统标签组合，表达更统一、更精准；
  • In-text Context Control: Intervening in every detail of the speech, covering emotional intensity, rhythm, emphasis, pauses, breathiness, and even character psychological activities and subtext flow; 文中语境控制：对语音每一处细节进行干预，涵盖情绪强度、节奏、重音、停顿、呼吸感乃至角色心理活动与潜台词流动；
  • Zero-shot Replication and Full Timbre Control: Any timbre can be used directly without retraining, and a complete "voice character profile" can be built for each timbre, enabling systematic customization from voiceprint to personality. 零样本复刻与全音色控制：任意音色无需重新训练即可直接使用，并可为每个音色构建完整的「声音角色档案」，实现从声纹到人格的系统化定制。

Step Audio summarizes the positioning of this model as "not just reproducing sound, but creating expression." The new model covers various application scenarios such as audiobooks, film and television dubbing, and intelligent voice interaction. 阶跃星辰将这一模型的定位概括为「不止于复现声音，更在于创造表达」。新模型覆盖有声书、影视配音、智能语音交互等多类应用场景。

交管部门：新能源绿牌「变白」违法

According to CCTV News, recently, there have been instances in many parts of the country where new energy vehicle license plates have been artificially discolored. Some car owners have used methods such as chemical solution immersion or special light exposure to fade the green gradient background of new energy license plates into white background with black characters, and have spread related 'license plate fading tutorials' on social media platforms. 据央视新闻报道，近日国内多地出现新能源汽车号牌被人为改色的现象。有车主通过化学溶液浸泡或特殊灯光照射等方式，将绿色渐变底新能源号牌褪色为白底黑字，并在社交平台上传播相关「车牌褪色教程」。

In response, the public security traffic management department clearly stated yesterday that China has not issued any new type of new energy vehicle license plates, and the aforementioned behavior is illegal. 对此，公安交管部门昨天明确表示，目前我国并未发布任何新式新能源汽车号牌，上述行为属于违法。

Zhu Jian'an, Deputy Director of the Publicity and Education Department of the Road Traffic Safety Research Center of the Ministry of Public Security, pointed out that Article 95 of the 'Road Traffic Safety Law of the People's Republic of China' clearly stipulates that intentionally covering, defacing, or failing to install license plates as required shall be subject to a warning or a fine of not less than 20 yuan but not more than 200 yuan, in accordance with Article 90. 公安部道路交通安全研究中心宣教部副主任朱建安指出，《中华人民共和国道路交通安全法》第 95 条明确规定，故意遮挡、污损或不按规定安装号牌的，将依据第 90 条处以警告或 20 元以上 200 元以下罚款。

For more serious offenses, according to Article 9 of the 'Measures for the Administration of Points for Road Traffic Safety Violations,' driving a vehicle without a license plate or intentionally covering or defacing a license plate on the road will result in 9 points deducted from the driver's license at one time. 情节更重者，依据《道路交通安全违法行为记分管理办法》第 9 条，驾驶未悬挂号牌或故意遮挡、污损号牌车辆上路行驶的，驾驶证一次记 9 分。

The public security traffic management department emphasized that the style, color, and specifications of motor vehicle license plates are uniformly stipulated by the state. Each license plate corresponds to specific vehicle and owner information and is not a decorative item that can be arbitrarily changed. 公安交管部门强调，机动车号牌的样式、颜色与规格均由国家统一规定，每块号牌对应特定车辆及所有人信息，并非可随意更改的装饰品。
